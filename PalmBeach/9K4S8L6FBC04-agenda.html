Budget Retreat will include a brief Legislative update as it relates to projected revenues and District budget forecast for FY2011.  After a brief overview of the District's current budget, the following departments will present a functional line-item review of their current FY2010 budgets:



Chief Operating Officer



Legislative Liaison

Accounting

FTE & Student Reporting



Labor Relations



School Police



Risk & Benefits Management



Purchasing



Business Diversity



Transportation



Food Service



The functional line-item budgets for each of the departments listed have been consolidated into one PDF file and attached to the agenda.



Also attached is a draft presentation for the Legislative update and budget overview based on the information available as of April 23, 2010.  This presentation may be amended prior to Wednesday's meeting to reflect Legislative developments that occur over the next few days (up to April 28). The 2010 Legislature is expected to adjourn on April 30, 2010.
