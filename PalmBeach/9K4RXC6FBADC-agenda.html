Subject
Personnel Disciplinary Action - Grenisha Williams
Meeting
May 7, 2008 - Spec Mtg re Disciplinary Action
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Action

On or about February 23, 2007, Grenisha Williams, Bus Attendant, became the subject of an Employee Relations investigation based upon allegations of unprofessional conduct, failure to exercise best professional judgment, and misconduct, careless disregard for policy, directive, and/or rule.  Furthermore, the allegations included potential violations of School Board Policy 1.013, Responsibilities of the School District Personnel and Staff, Section XV of the School Bus Drivers and Bus Attendants Handbook entitled Grounds for Discharge of School Bus Drivers and Bus Attendants, and Section II (A) of the School Bus Drivers and Bus Attendants Handbook entitled Duties and Responsibilities of Bus Drivers, General.



On or about January 16, 2007, Ms. Williams was involved in an incident on her bus route.  While she was supervising the students, a high school student committed sexual battery on a Pre-Kindergarten student.  Ms. Williams witnessed the incident but did not take appropriate action to separate the students.  She and the bus driver discussed what occurred and what they would report.  Ms. Williams reported the incident but intentionally omitted many relevant details.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve Grenisha Williams suspension without pay, effective May 8, 2008 and Termination, effective fifteen (15) days after the May 7, 2008, Board Meeting, subject only to appeal under Section 120.68, Florida Statutes, unless a timely request for an administrative hearing is made within the fifteen (15) days stated herein pursuant to payment of salary and benefits until final School Board disposition is entered to the administrative hearing proceedings.

Contact:

Ann Killets; Darron Davis, Angelette Green

Financial Impact:

There is no financial impact to the District.
