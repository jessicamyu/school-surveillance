Executive Summary



 Wellness Promotion Policy Annual Report



(July 1, 2011 – June 30, 2012)



The district-wide initiative will offer a comprehensive presentation to the School Board and the District Leadership as requires by Section 204 of the Healthy, Hunger-Free Kids Act of 2010, Public Law 111-296.  This is a collaboration effort between the Operational and the Academic areas of the District - with the partnership of many internal/external stakeholders.  Many of these partners will be present for support.



 



This School Board workshop will provide a detailed review of the 6th edition of the Wellness Promotion Policy Annual Report (July 1, 2011 - June 30, 2012).



 



 



Please view this annual report at the following link:







  • www.2012wellnessannualreport.com


Note:  This annual report is in draft form and still requires editing.  The final version will be completed in its entirety prior to the Winter Break.

Description



The purpose of the Workshop is to provide a summary of the Advisory Boundary Committee (ABC) recommendations for the 2013-2014 Boundary Cycle, pursuant to Policy 5.01.



The ABC presented the following Boundary Change Proposals to the Superintendent (Dr. Janis Andrews – designee) on November 15, 2012:



1. PR1 - Boundary Study S-1 (Sunset Palms Elementary to Coral Reef Elementary) was voted on by the ABC to be recommended to the Superintendent on November 1, 2012 with a 6-2 vote and with an opt-in provision for students who are currently attending Sunset Palms from the proposed change area.



The proposed change provides relief to Sunset Palms Elementary and moves SACs 216B and 227A to Coral Reef Elementary.



2. PR2 - Boundary Study H-1 (Hagen Road Elementary to Crystal Lakes Elementary) was voted on by the ABC to be recommended to the Superintendent on October 4, 2012 with a 12-1 vote.



The proposed change provides current and projected relief from crowding to Hagen Road Elementary and moves SACs 252 and 253 to Crystal Lakes Elementary.



The full proposals and Support Documentation are provided as attachments.
