Subject
Personnel Disciplinary Action - Rutha Scott
Meeting
Jun 4, 2008 - Spec Mtg re Disciplinary Actions
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Discussion

On or about March 17, 2008, Rutha Scott, a Teacher, became the subject of an Employee Relations investigation based upon allegations of  unprofessional conduct, failure to exercise the best professional judgment and failure to supervise students.  Furthermore, the allegations included potential violations of School Board Policy 1.013, Responsibilities of the School District Personnel and Staff, and Florida Administrative Code 6B-1.006, (3) (a) (e), and 6B-1.001(2) (3) Principals of Professional Conduct for the Education Profession in Florida.



On or about February 1, 2008, Rutha Scott did direct a student to enter a dark storage room inside a classroom at Palm Beach Central High School as a means of discipline.  The student remained inside the storage room for a duration of five to fifteen minutes.  the storage room is located in between two classrooms with a wooden door on each side and no windows.  The storage room measurements are 16 x 6.  It has no windows, but does receive air ventilation by means of an air duct.  Due to the storage room being utilized as an area for storage with boxes and shelving, it posed a potentially unsafe situation for the student being in this room in darkness.  Rutha Scott's actions were an improper method of disciplining the student.  Additionally, during this incident, Rutha Scott sent other students outside in the hallway unsupervised, which is also an improper discipline method for classroom management.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve Rutha Scott's suspension without pay, effective June 5, 2008 and Termination, effective fifteen (15) days after the June 4, 2008, Board Meeting, subject only to appeal under Section 120.68, Florida Statutes, unless a timely request for an administrative hearing is made within the fifteen (15) days stated herein pursuant to payment of salary and benefits until final School Board disposition is entered to the administrative hearing proceedings.

Contact:

Ann Killets; Chief James P. Kelly; Angelette Green

Financial Impact:

There is no financial impact to the School District.
