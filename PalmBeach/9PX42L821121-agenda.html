DESCRIPTION:

This November 4, Palm Beach County voters will be asked whether or not to renew an existing special property levy of 25 cents per $1,000 of home valuation to pay for arts, music and physical education teachers and choice and magnet programs. This revenue source generates approximately $36 million annually to support operational needs of District-operated schools.
