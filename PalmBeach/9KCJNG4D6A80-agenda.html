Recommended Action
I recommend the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp authorize the issuance of COP series 2014B to refund or restructure the 2011B COP and terminate the associated interest rate swap.

DESCRIPTION:

  • This resolution for the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp authorizes the School Board to move forward with the issuance of COP Series 2014B, which will refund the 2011B COP.
  • As part of the transaction, the District may terminate the associated interest rate swap. 
  • The School Board's Finance Committee will consider this transaction at their meeting on May 30, 2014.  
  • The School Board will consider the approval of the matter on June 4, 2014 with agenda item FMT-1.
  • A summary of the Leasing Corp is also attached as a reference.

CONTACT:

Michael J. Burke, Chief Operating Officer (mike.burke@palmbeachschools.org)
