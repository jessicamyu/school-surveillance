Subject
Career Pathways Update - CTE and Vocational Programs of Study
Meeting
Dec 7, 2016 - 2. WORKSHOP following Special Meeting re Superintendent Contract in the Board Room
Category
2. Workshop Items
Type
Information

DESCRIPTION:

The Department of Choice and Career Options within the School District of Palm Beach County has developed Career Pathways System to prepare students for the demands of today’s global market, as well as our local economy. Technological advances and global competition continues to transform the nature of work at an ever growing pace. As a result, jobs today and those in the future will require more knowledge, better skills and more flexibility than ever before. This system encompasses rigorous programs of study providing students the academic preparation, direction, and career-related knowledge and skills to help them plan studies that are in line with their interests, abilities, and career goals.  Our constant goal is to improve, expand, and develop Career & Technical Education programs of study through a solid Career Pathways System that allows our students to transition from secondary to post-secondary education as smoothly as possible to eventually earn a degree, certificate, and/or industry credential.
