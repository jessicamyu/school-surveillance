Cooperative Agreement between SDPBC and College for Kids, Inc. D/B/A Take Stock in Children (TSIC) of PBC

Category

The mission of TSIC is to prepare Palm Beach County middle and high school students to graduate career and college ready.  Mentors from the Agency meet with the student and family and discuss pre-graduating needs such as:  career goals, selection of courses, communication skills, anger management, writing college application, interviewing skills, keys to success, and turning obstacles into opportunities.  The mentors follow the progress of the students throughout the year, and upon graduation offer scholarship opportunities.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the amendment to the existing Interagency Agreement with College for Kids, Inc. D/B/A Take Stock in Children and authorize the Superintendent or designee to sign all documents and contracts related to the agreement which are required for implementation of the agreement activities.

G2 Rescind the amended motion taken on August 25, 2010 regarding the Teaching American History Grant



 



On August 25, 2010, the School Board approved the amended motion to open the Teaching American History Grant to all elementary and middle American History teachers to participate with substitutes financially covered and to include a stipend for weekends.



 



The response from the Teaching American History Grant office at the U.S. Federal Government Office of Innovation and Improvement is:



 



The original application is the defining set of activities and perspectives for the entire grant.  A change to the teacher cohort or reallocation of monies cannot be sanctioned as it would alter the parameters of the original grant awarded.  The review panel rated the application of the School District of Palm Beach County at a high level among highly competitive applications based largely on the proposed plan of professional development that gave teachers from lower performing schools priority. 



 

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board rescind their vote from the August 25, 2010 meeting.  I recommend that the School Board accept the Teaching American History Grant as submitted.

Contact:

Ann Killets, Chief Learning Officer (akillets@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)



Constance Tuman-Rugg, (tuman@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)

Financial Impact:

The financial impact to the Special Revenue-Other Funds Budget is an increase of $951,782.00.
