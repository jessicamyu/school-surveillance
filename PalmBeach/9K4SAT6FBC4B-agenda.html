Board Workshop on the Negotiated Settlement Agreement
Meeting
Oct 26, 2011 - Workshops
Category
Workshop Item(s)
Type
Action

On February 4, 2009, the School Board approved a Negotiated Settlement Agreement between students represented by Legal Aid Society, Southern Poverty Law Center, and Southern Legal Counsel. 



In part, the Negotiated Settlement Agreement provides that the School District would implement Positive Behavior Intervention Supports (PBIS) district wide.  



The purpose of this workshop is to advise the School Board on the background leading up to this Settlement Agreement and provide information on the status of the terms set forth within the Negotiated Settlement Agreement.
