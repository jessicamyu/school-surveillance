Special Meeting re: Cheryl McMaster Settlement
Meeting
May 5, 2010 - Legal Sessions
Category
Special Meetings
Type
Action
On or about October 14, 2009, the Board voted to terminate Ms. McMaster’s employment as a teacher due to a second offense of off-the-job use or involvement with alcohol. Ms. McMaster’s appealed her termination. Settlement is recommended as being in the best interest of the District due to the anticipated costs of the litigation and due to the desire to prevent Ms. McMaster from returning to the classroom. The settlement includes back pay from October 15, 2009 through December 31, 2009 and, in return, the District receives a resignation from Ms. McMaster as of December 31, 2009 and a guarantee that she will never reapply for a job within the School District of Palm Beach County.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Settlement Agreement between Cheryl McMaster and the School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to execute all related documents.

Contact:

Gerald A. Williams, Esq.; Chief Counsel



Vicki L. Evans-Pare, Esq.; Associate Counsel

Financial Impact:

The financial impact is $13,753.91 minus any lawful deductions from Payroll.
