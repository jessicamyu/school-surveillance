5. New Business

Action
Recommended Action
I recommend that the Board, effective immediately, reinstate the suspended protocols in Paragraph 24(a) (iii), (vi), and (ix) and 25 relating to required facial coverings but that unmasked persons will be escorted to a separate room or area with the ability for public comment. Paragraphs 24(a) and 25 of Policy 1.03 would be suspended if facial coverings requirements are suspended under Policy 2.065 relating to visitors or until a modified Policy 1.03 is adopted, and may be reinstated if the facial coverings requirements are reinstated under Policy 2.065 or the School Board votes to reinstate these Standing Rules applicable to the health-safety protocols.

DESCRIPTION:
