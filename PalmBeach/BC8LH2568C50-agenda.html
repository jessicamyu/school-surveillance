Recommended Action
I recommend the Board approve the Renewal Charter of ten (10) years for Believers Academy, and authorize the Superintendent and/or his designee to sign all related contracts and documents.

DESCRIPTION:

On July 1, 2009, Believers Academy was granted a ten (10) year Charter Contract to serve ESE students ages 14-21.

 

Pursuant to F.S. 1002.33(7)(b)1, Believers Academy meets the criteria for renewal and does not fall under any grounds for termination.  The School is in compliance with its current charter. The Renewal Charter was reviewed and agreed upon by the appropriate District staff.

 

The renewal charter will become effective upon signing by both parties, and shall cover a term of ten (10) years, commencing July 1, 2019, and ending June 30, 2029, except as provided in the charter.

 

The mission statement of the school states that “Believers Academy is dedicated to ESE students aged fourteen to twenty-one who are at risk of dropping out of their schools due to persistent lack of success in that environment.”

I recommend the Board approve the Renewal Charter of ten (10) years for Renaissance Charter School at Wellington, and authorize the Superintendent and/or his designee to sign all related contracts and documents.

DESCRIPTION:

On July 1, 2014, Renaissance Charter School at Wellington was granted a five (5) year Charter Contract to serve students in grades K-8.

 

Pursuant to F.S. 1002.33(7)(b)1, Renaissance Charter School at Wellington meets the criteria for renewal and does not fall under any grounds for termination.  The School is in compliance with its current charter. The Renewal Charter was reviewed and agreed upon by the appropriate District staff.

 

The renewal charter will become effective upon signing by both parties, and shall cover a term of ten (10) years, commencing July 1, 2019, and ending June 30, 2029, except as provided in the charter.

 

The mission of the school is “to create a learning environment that integrates a research-based curriculum, a culture of student safety and success, and concentration on academic rigor while focusing on citizenship and experimental learning through student engagement.”

I recommend the Board approve the Third Amendment to the Charter Contract with Imagine Schools – Chancellor Campus, and authorize the Superintendent and/or his designee to sign all related contracts and documents.

DESCRIPTION:

On November 5, 2014, Imagine Schools – Chancellor Campus was granted an Amended Renewal Charter Contract that amended the Renewal Charter School Agreement to expand its term to fifteen (15) years from its inception in 2009.  The term of the 2014 Renewal Charter Contract commenced on July 1, 2014 and ends on June 30, 2024.

 

Imagine Schools – Chancellor Campus desires to further amend the Charter Contract in order to extend the term of its Charter for a total of fifteen (15) years pursuant to § 1002.331(2)(e), Florida Statutes. The School has been designated as a high performing charter school in accordance with § 1002.33, Florida Statutes.

 

The School and Sponsor have agreed to propose to the School Board to amend the existing Renewal Charter School Agreement to expand its term to fifteen (15) years to facilitate access to long-term financial resources for charter school facility acquisition purposes.

 

The Amended Charter Contract will become effective upon signing by both parties, and shall cover a term of fifteen (15) years, commencing July 1, 2014, and ending June 30, 2029, except as provided in the charter.

 

The mission of Imagine Schools – Chancellor Campus is “To provide each child with an opportunity to realize his or her full potential in a setting that is safe, orderly and small enough so that students feel recognized and cared for.”

DESCRIPTION:

A Tentative Agreement has been reached on Modifications to the 2018-2020 Collective Bargaining Agreement between The School Board of Palm Beach County and The Palm Beach County Police Benevolent Association (PBA). This Tentative Agreement reflects the following negotiated changes to the Agreement:

Pay increases will be awarded to each PBA bargaining unit employee who was an employee of the District on the date the School Board approves these modifications, retroactive to January 1, 2019, based upon the following:

    1. Officers with less than 1 year of service in the District will receive a 1% increase.
       
    2. Officers with 1 year to less than 5 year of service in the District will receive a 3% increase.
       
    3. Officers with 5 years to less than 10 year of in the District service will receive a 3.5% increase.
       
    4. Officers with 10 years to less than 15 year of in the District service will receive a 4% increase.
       
    5. Officers with 15 years or more of service in the District will receive a 6% increase.
