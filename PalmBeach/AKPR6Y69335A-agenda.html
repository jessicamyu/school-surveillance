2.c. Board Discussion - ROBINSON - Cursive Writing
Meeting
Apr 5, 2017 - 3. WORKSHOP immediately following Special Meeting for Expulsions in the Board Room
Category
2. Workshop Items
Type
Discussion

Dr. Robinson will request staff to provide an analysis of the benefits of teaching penmanship and cursive writing, a review of how these skills managed to drop from the curriculum.  It is her understanding that once learned, cursive is faster than printing and that Florida Writes is handwritten.  If true, cursive handwriting would benefit a student completing this exam.

Subject
2.d. Board Discussion - ROBINSON - Field Trips
Meeting
Apr 5, 2017 - 3. WORKSHOP immediately following Special Meeting for Expulsions in the Board Room
Category
2. Workshop Items
Type
Discussion

Dr. Robinson will request that the Board discuss and consider giving direction to the Superintendent and staff to outline a comprehensive plan for students to participate in out-of-school learning experiences as a regular and frequent part of their education program.  These field trips should be in the scope and sequencing of lessons.  As has been done, pre- and post-lessons can be created to maximize the learning experience.  Priority should be given to students in high poverty schools.
