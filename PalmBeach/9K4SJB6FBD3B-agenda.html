·        On January 30, 2013, the Administrative Law Judge issued a Recommended Order upholding the termination of Paula Prudente.

Sunset Palms Elementary Relief



Sunset Palms Elementary to Coral Reef Elementary

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve PR-1 Sunset Palms Elementary Relief, with current 4th grade students attending Sunset Palms Elementary School having the option to stay for 5th grade and currently enrolled K-3 siblings of 4th grade students having the option to stay for the remainder of their elementary years.  Siblings will be reviewed and processed through the Choice Department (reassignments). Transportation is the responsibility of parents.
