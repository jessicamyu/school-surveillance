Subject
AC1 - Cancelation of the 3rd year of a 3-year contract with Palm Beach County Convention Center and confirmation of the South Florida Expo Center at Palm Beach County Fairgrounds for Spring 2012 Graduations
Meeting
May 25, 2011 - Special Meeting
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Discussion
There are two actions in this item: 1. Agree to notify the Palm Beach County Convention Center of the District's intention to cancel the 3rd year of the 3-year contract for high school graduations for 2012. * A comparison estimate was completed which shows that there is a significant cost savings to switch 2012 graduation site to the County Fairgrounds * Included in the attachments are the original convention center contract with legal review checklist and a Comparison Estimate Graph of the savings 2. Approve the confirmation of the South florida Expo Center at the Palm Beach County Fairgrounds for Spring 2012 Graduation. * The attached County Fairgrounds contract includes: - Rental of building space - Audio Visual - Personnel charges - Facility equipment and fees - Emergency medical, Sheriff or Police - Ticketing fees

Recommendation:

1. I recommend that the Board agrees to cancel the 3rd year of the 3-year contract with the Palm Beach County Convention Center and authorize the superintendent or designee to sign all documents and contracts required. 2. I recommend that the School Board approve the confirmation of the South Florida Expo Center at the Palm Beach County Fairgrounds to hold the 2012 spring high school graduation ceremonies and authorize the superintendent or designee to sign all documents and contracts required.

Contact:

Judith Klinek,(klinek@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)



Dr. Contance Tuman-Rugg

Financial Impact:

The financial impact of the Palm Beach County Fairgrounds contract is $122,609.20 from the general fund; however, after additional costs for the services highlighted in the comparison chart, the total cost of 2012 graduations will be $150,126.20.
