Subject
Strategic Plan Workshop #2 (Student Input - Priority Student Outcomes, Themes, Objectives)
Meeting
Apr 13, 2022 - 3. WORKSHOP immediately following Closed Session in the Board Room
Category
3. Workshop Items
Type
Discussion, Presentation

DESCRIPTION:

The Office of the Chief of Staff will present the students' input for the District's next Strategic Plan. The input is based on findings from the student engagement activities which took place on March 24 and March 25.
 
This workshop will provide members of the School Board with an opportunity to review the findings and provide additional feedback.  We will use feedback generated from the Board workshop and student engagement to refine the proposed priority student outcomes, themes, and objectives. The anticipated School Board adoption will take place in May.
