Recommended Action
I recommend the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp authorize the resolution to issue Certificates of Participation Series 2023A and B with an aggregate principal amount not to exceed $225 million.

DESCRIPTION:

  • The FY 2023 Capital Plan includes the issuance of debt to fund the construction of several projects.  Based on the construction schedules, six projects will be financed this year.
    • North Tech/Riviera Beach Prep Modernization
    • Pine Grove ES Modernization
    • Roosevelt Full Service Modernization
    • Timber Trace Core Expansion
    • West Riviera ES Modernization
    • Wynnebrook ES Modernization
  • The District traditionally issues tax-exempt debt, which allows for construction of public schools. However, tax-exempt debt is not appropriate for funding projects that will generate revenues so taxable debt will be used to finance the construction at Roosevelt Full Service.  The taxable transaction will mature first to reduce the additional interest cost.
    • Tax-exempt COP series 2023A will finance the North Tech/Riviera Beach Prep, Pine Grove, Timber Trace, West Riviera, and Wynnebrook projects with a par amount not to exceed $200 million.
    • Taxable COP series 2023B will finance the Roosevelt Full Service project with a par amount not to exceed $25 million.
  • Both transactions will mature no later than 8/1/2048.
  • The estimated costs associated with the issuance of the debt is not to exceed $1.94 million.  A detailed list of the estimated costs is attached.
  • B of A Securities, Inc will serve as the Senior Underwriter and J.P. Morgan will serve as the Co-Senior Underwriter.  RBC Capital Markets, LLC, Siebert Williams Shank & Co. LLC and Stifel, Nicolaus & Company, Inc will serve as Co-Underwriters on the transactions.  The underwriter team is based on the contract approved by the School Board in January 2018.
  • The Finance Committee will review the transactions at their meeting on October 28, 2022.  The meeting will be recorded and available online shortly after the meeting via this link:
    https://www.palmbeachschools.org/school_board/board_advisory_committees/board_advisory_committee_meetings_on_you_tube/ 
  • The School Board will also consider this item on November 2, 2022 (agenda item FMT1).

 

CONTACT:

FINANCIAL IMPACT:

The issuance of COP series 2023A and B will provide the funding needed to finance a portion of the FY 2023 capital budget.  Revenues of approximately $202 million will be used to finance the construction projects listed above and costs of issuance not to exceed $1.94 million.
