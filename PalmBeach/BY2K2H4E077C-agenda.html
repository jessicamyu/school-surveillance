Recommended Action
I recommend the Board authorize the resolution to issue COP Series 2021A and 2021B with an aggregate principal amount not to exceed $148 million.

DESCRIPTION:

  • The FY 2021 Capital Plan includes the issuance of debt to fund the construction of several projects.  Based on the construction schedules, construction will begin on three projects this year.  
    • Lake Worth Area HS (03-OOO)
    • Boca Raton Area ES (05-C)
    • Delray Full Service
  • The District traditionally issues tax-exempt debt, which allows for construction of public schools.  However, tax-exempt debt is not appropriate for funding projects that will generate revenues so taxable debt will be used to finance the construction at Delray Full Service.  The taxable transaction will mature first to reduce the additional interest cost. In the current low interest rate environment, the net interest rate for the transactions is estimated at 3.26% but the actual rate will be determined based on market conditions when the transaction is sold. 
    • Tax-exempt COP series 2021A, will finance 03-OOO and 05-C with a par amount not to exceed $136 million.
    • Taxable COP series 2021B will finance Delray Full Service with a par amount not to exceed $12 million.
  • Both transactions will mature no later than 8/1/2040.
  • The estimated costs associated with the issuance of the debt is not to exceed $1.27 million.  The details of the costs of issuance are attached.
  • The Finance Committee reviewed the transactions at their meeting on February 5, 2021 and unanimously recommended the transaction to the School Board for approval.
  • The Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp will also consider this item on March 3, 2021 (agenda item LC2).

 

CONTACT:

Michael J. Burke, CFO (Mike.Burke@palmbeachschools.org)

Leanne Evans, Treasurer (Leanne.Evans@palmbeachschools.org)

 

FINANCIAL IMPACT:

The issuance of COP series 2021A and 2021B will provide the funding needed to finance a portion of the FY 2021 capital budget.  Revenues of approximately $145 million will be used t ofinance the construction projects listed above and costs of issuance not to exceed $1.27 million.

Type

Recommended Action
I recommend the Board approve the Data Sharing Agreement with Tindale-Oliver & Associates, Inc., and authorize the Board Chair and Superintendent to sign all necessary documents.

DESCRIPTION:

  • Palm Beach County's Impact Fee Ordinance requires that an update be prepared every two years. The County has Impact Fees for seven public service areas, including Public Schools.
     
  • In order to calculate the appropriate School Impact Fees, the County's consultant, Tindale-Oliver & Associates, Inc., must prepare a Student Generation Rate (SGR) using the Geographic Information System Geo-code file that is semiannually prepared by the School District's GIS Manager. 
     
  • School District staff also uses the SGR prepared by Tindale-Oliver as part of its School Capacity Availability Determination ("SCAD") reviews.
     
  • This Data Sharing Agreement has been developed to protect the integrity and confidentiality of the data being provided.
