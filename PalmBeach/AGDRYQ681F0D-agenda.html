Recommended Action
I recommend the Board postpone previous time-certain dates to discuss and vote on the Superintendent's contract to the new dates of February 1, 2017 for discussion and February 15, 2017 for vote.

DESCRIPTION:

At the November 16, 2016 Regular Board Meeting, the Board tabled item BRD1 Second Amendment to Superintendent Employment Contract with a time-certain dates of December 7, 2016 for discussion and December 14, 2016 for vote.  To allow more time for the gathering of requested information, it is being recommended to change the time-certain dates to February 1, 2017 for discussion and Februrary 15, 2017 to the vote.
