Recommended Action
I recommend the Board authorize the settlement of Palm Beach County School Board v. Jacinta Larson, in accordance with the terms set forth in the Settlement Agreement, and authorize Board Chair, Superintendent, and General Counsel to sign all necessary documents.

DESCRIPTION:

On December 20, 2018, Ms. Larson, while a teacher at Turning Points Academy, picked up and threw a chair at two (2) students who were misbehaving in class. The chair hit a desk causing it to hit another student’s leg.  Ms. Larson was suspended without pay and terminated. Ms. Larson then requested a hearing conducted by an ALJ appointed by DOAH.

 

The final hearing was on September 21 and 22, 2020, before Administrative Law Judge (“ALJ”), Robert E. Meale. On December 2, 2020, ALJ Robert E. Meale filed his Recommended Order finding Ms. Larson not guilty of the charges set forth in the Administrative Complaint and reinstating her with full back pay.  His primary reasoning was that the Board only focused on Ms. Larson’s throwing of the chair and not on her mental state that drove her to throw the chair.  However, no information was presented to the Board prior to the Board voting to terminate her because she did not provide any information regarding her mental state until she was defending the termination.

 

On February 8, 2021, the Superintendent filed Exceptions to the Recommended Order. The District scheduled the Exceptions hearing for May 19, 2021.  On May 14, 2021, The District and Ms. Larson entered into an agreement to settle this matter. Dr. Fennoy subsequently filed a Notice of Withdrawal and Cancellation of the Exceptions Hearing.
