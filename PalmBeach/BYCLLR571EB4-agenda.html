DESCRIPTION:

This is the first of several Board Workshops dedicated solely to the topic of equity. These workshops were intentionally scheduled to precede development of the District's next strategic plan.

 

During Equity Workshop 1, Mary Fertakis (Equity Consultant) will lead Board Members through a Root Cause Analysis that will consist of analyzing critical data points in response to the following prompt: Why do inequities exist in Palm Beach County Schools? The learnings gleaned from this first workshop will provide the framework for Equity Workshop 2, which is designed to focus the Board on drafting a working definition of equity for the School District of Palm Beach County (Equity WS 2 is scheduled for March 31, 2021).
