DESCRIPTION:

Commencing for the 2017-2018 school year: Changes in student attendance boundaries for six (6) Elementary Schools – Calusa, Addison Mizner, Boca Raton, J.C. Mitchell, Sunrise Park and Whispering Pines and adding SAC 296C as a Choice Priority SAC area for Morikami Park.

 

The purpose of the Workshop is to provide a summary of the Advisory Boundary Committee (ABC) recommendation by a 7-4 vote for the 2017-2018 Boundary Cycle for Capacity Relief for Calusa Elementary, pursuant to Policy 5.01 on Student Assignment.

 

The ABC Committee voted to present the following Boundary Change Proposal to the Superintendent:

 

PIR-1 - Boundary Study C-1-Hybrid (Calusa to Whispering Pines Elementary, Whispering Pines to Sunrise Park, Calusa to J.C. Mitchell, J.C. Mitchell to Addison Mizner, and J.C. Mitchell to Boca Raton Elementary) was voted on December 13, 2016 by the ABC to be recommended to the Superintendent.

 

The study approved by the ABC specified that current 4th grade students and their current K-3 grade siblings would be permitted to opt-in to remain at their current SY16-17 schools.  

 

The proposed change provides relief to Calusa Elementary, provides additional enrollment at other schools, and moves the following SAC’s:

 

SACs 296C and 317 (Portion) from Calusa Elementary to Whispering Pines Elementary

 

SAC 311D from Whispering Pines Elementary to Sunrise Park Elementary

 

SACs 315 and 316A from Calusa Elementary to J.C. Mitchell Elementary

 

SAC 335 from J.C. Mitchell Elementary to Addison Mizner Elementary

 

SAC 336A from J.C. Mitchell Elementary to Boca Raton Elementary

 

The four dissenting ABC votes we due to concerns that the study does not provide substantial enough enrollment relief to Calusa Elementary.

 

On 12/16/16,  the C-1 Hybrid study and other studies in this boundary cycle were shared with the Diversity and Equity Committee. The Committee had no specific comments regarding the proposed studies. However, there was general discussion about concerns that parents in disadvantaged socioeconomic groups may not have the time and means to participate in the ABC and Community Input Meetings.


The Superintendent would recommend to the Board this study, as well as adding SAC 296C as a Choice Priority SAC area for Morikami Park Elementary.  Within SAC 296C are the Casa Bella and Addison Reserve communities, which did not exist when nearby SACs were created as Choice Priority SAC areas for Morikami Park. SACs 296B, 306A & 306B are already priority SACs for Morikami. These are SACs surrounding SAC 296C. At the time these other SACs were deemed priority for Morikami there was no development in SAC 296C, which is why that SAC was not included in the priority designation at that time.

 

The boundary study proposal and supporting documentation, including the study, maps and the legal descriptions, are provided as attachments.
