A new contract agreement between the Education Foundation of Palm Beach County and the Volunteer USA Foundation has been established to provide Family Literacy Services from July 1, 2007 - June 30, 2008 in the amount of $75,000.



The funds will be disbursed in increments of $20,000 to the Education Foundation of Palm Beach County by the Florida Family Literacy Initiative Program in accordance to the Volunteer U.S.A. Foundation disbursement schedule.



The Education Foundation will disburse funds to the Department of Adult and Community Education in the amount not to exceed $63,933 to pay salaries and benefits to the staff at Freedom Shores Elementary School's Family Literacy Initiative Program.



Freedom Shores Elementary School was chosen based on a history of providing an effective Family Literacy Program to those families most in need.



Funds will be used to provide all four components of a Family Literacy Program:



*Adult Education



*Parent Education



*Early Childhood Education



*Parents and Children Together (PACT) Time





The program will be evaluated internally to ensure compliance with all State and Federal requirements.  In addition, a representative from the Volunteer USA Foundation will conduct an evaluation/monitoring process.  The evaluation will consist of a review of student records, including student performance and completion rates.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the new contract/agreement in the amount of $75,000 between the Education Foundation of Palm Beach County and the Volunteer USA Foundation effective July 1, 2007 to June 30, 2008; approve the disbursement of funds to the Department of Adult and Community Education in the amount not to exceed $63,933 to pay salaries and benefits to the staff at Freedom Shores Elementary School's Family Literacy Initiative Program; and authorize the Superintendent or designee to sign all documents and amendments related to the agreement which are required for implementation.

Contact:

Ann Killets (akillets@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)



Brenda Magee, Ed.D.

Financial Impact:

The financial impact to the FY 2008 Special Revenue-Other Funds Budget is an increase of $63,933.00.  These Education Foundation of Palm Beach County related grant funds are not part of K-12 FTE funding.  There is no capital impact.  The funds will be allocated within the Division of Learning Support, Department of Adult and Community Education budget.
