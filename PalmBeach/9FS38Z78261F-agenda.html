Subject
FMPA1 Additional Funds for the Current Term of Bid 07C-916CH and Renewal of Bid 07C-916CH for the Purchase of Library Materials
Meeting
Feb 5, 2014 - 3. SPECIAL MEETING following Workshop in the Board Room
Category
11. Consent Agenda
Type
Action (Consent)
Recommended Action
I recommend approval of additional funds for the current term of Bid 07C-916CH, a Piggyback Bid of the State of Florida Term Contract 715-001-07-1 in the amount of $300,000 and, the renewal of Bid 07C-916CH for the period March 1, 2014 through February 27, 2015. The current term of this bid expires February 28, 2014.

DESCRIPTION:

This is a request for additional funds for the current term of Bid 07C-916CH, a Piggyback Bid of the State of Florida Term Contract 715-001-07-1 in the amount of $300,000 and the renewal of Bid 07C-916CH for the period March 1, 2014 through February 27, 2015 for the purchase of library materials.

 

The request for the additional funds for the current term of the bid is for the purchase of library materials comprised of categorical books and materials which must be purchased and circulated through the School Media Centers no later than March 28, 2014.  The District uses this alternate source contract for the purchase of library materials to obtain the favorable  discounts ranging  up to 40%  by the vendors awarded on this contract.  Items to be purchased from this bid include all types of library materials, both domestic and foreign, including, but not limited to in print, out-of-print books, subscriptions, audio books, periodicals, pre-processed books, maps, charts, globes, CD Rom’s, DVD’s slides, films, phonographic and tape recordings, musical scores, sheet music, and other materials in this category.

 

Some of the awarded vendors on this bid may exceed the $250,000 threshold for the remaining period of the current term of this bid and the term of the renewal of the bid.

Michael J. Burke, Chief Operating Officer (mike.burke@palmbeachschools.org)

Keith Oswald, Assistant Superintendent, Curriculum and Learning Support

 

FINANCIAL IMPACT:

The revised annual financial impact to District’s budget during the current term of Bid 07C-916CH is not to exceed $2,300,000, an increase of $300,000 and the annual spend for the renewal period 3/1/2014 through 2/27/2015 is not to exceed $2,300,000.  The source of funds is the Categorical Funds from the State of Florida for Library Media Materials and the various school and department budgets.
