Charter Application for Gardens School of Technology Arts

The Department of Charter Schools received an application, on August 3, 2009, from Gardens School of Technology Arts ("Gardens SOTA") to open a charter school in the School District of Palm Beach County in August, 2010.



The application was evaluated using the "Florida Charter School Application Evaluation Instrument" (see Exhibit "A"). 



The instrument and the application process were explained to the applicants during the New Applicants' Workshop that was held on May 28, 2009 (see Exhibit "B").



The District Reviewers were trained on the Florida Charter School Application Evaluation Instrument and Application Process on May 15, 2009 (see Exhibit "C").



The Reviewers evaluated the 21 sections of the written application and identified deficiencies. One of the 21 sections was rated "Partially Meets the Standard," meaning the responses addressed most of the criteria, but lacked meaningful detail and required important additional information (see Exhibit "D").  The Facilities section which partially meets the standard will be cured during Contract Negotiations.



Based on the written application and the interview, this application has fulfilled the "Application Process and Review" outlined in F. S. 1002.33(6)(a).  Step 6, second paragraph in the Overview of the Florida Charter School Application Process states, "The sponsoring board will only approve applicants that demonstrate quality in all components of the application process.  An application cannot have significant weaknesses in some components of the application and still be judged to have met the standard for approval," (see Exhibit "E"). 



Pursuant to F. S. 1002.33 (5) (c), "An applicant may appeal any denial of that applicant's application or failure to act on an application to the State Board of Education no later than 30 calendar days, after receipt of the sponsor's decision or failure to act and shall notify the sponsor of its appeal..."



A copy of the Charter Application  for Gardens SOTA is on file in the Board Office for review.




Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Charter Application for Gardens School of Technology Arts.
