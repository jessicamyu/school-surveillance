Dr. Robinson would like the Board to discuss directing staff to re-evaluate school start times.  This is a request to bring back an analysis of the pros and cons of altering school start times and the impact on students, parents, district operations, etc.  This analysis should also include the pros and cons of having some schools choose to alter their start time, possibly as a recruitment tool.
