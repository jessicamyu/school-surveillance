Subject
PS1 - Personnel Disciplinary Action - Samantha Major
Meeting
Mar 1, 2017 - 3. SPECIAL MEETING immediately following Workshop in the Board Room
Category
3. Superintendent Withdraws Items
Type
Information

DESCRIPTION:

On September 7, 2016, Samantha Major became the subject of a Professional Standards investigation based upon allegations of policy violations related to Failure to Report Child Abuse, Ethical Misconduct, Inappropriate Interaction with Student(s), and Failure to Follow Policy, Rule, Directive, or Statute.  At the conclusion of the investigation, the allegations were substantiated.  

 

Therefore, these actions violate the following:  School Board Policies 0.01(2)(3), Commitment to the Student, Principle I; 1.013(1), Responsibilities of School District Personnel and Staff; 2.122(D), Freedom of Religion; 3.02(4)(a), (4)(b), and (4)(i), Code of Ethics; 3.02(5)(i), Code of Ethics; 5.30, Reporting Child Abuse, Abandonment or Neglect; 6A-10.081(1)(b), and (1)(c), F.A.C., Principles of Professional Conduct for the Education Profession in Florida; 6A-10.081(2)(a)(1), F.A.C., Principles of Professional Conduct for the Education Profession in Florida. 

 

There is "just cause" to support Samantha Major's termination of employment.

 

On February 14, 2017, the Superintendent's "Notice of Recommendation for Termination of Employment" was hand-delivered to Samantha Major, a copy of which is attached hereto.

I recommend the Board approve the non-renewal of the Charter for Belle Glade Excel, and authorize the Superintendent and/or his designee to sign all related contracts and documents.

DESCRIPTION:

On June 20, 2012, Belle Glade Excel, Inc. was granted a five (5) year Charter Contract to serve K-5 students.

 

The mission of the school is “to incorporate active parental involvement, community resources, and to utilize the best curriculum to prepare students to be successful in their continuing education. Students will hold themselves to the highest standards of achievement and academic excellence as they have an equal opportunity to receive a quality education and gain and educational foundation on which they can learn and grow.”

 

Pursuant to F.S. 1002.33(8)(a), determination has been made that the charter for Belle Glade Excel will not be renewed.  The good cause to not renew includes the failure of the charter school to meet student academic achievement and progress as demonstrated by school grades of “F” in school years 2014-15 and 2015-16.  Current formatives assessments results indicate that student academic achievement demonstrates a high probability that the charter school will receive a school grade of “F” for the current school year.
