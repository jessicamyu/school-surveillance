Subject
Equity, Mission/Vision, and Strategic Plan (Workshop 2)
Meeting
Dec 16, 2020 - 2. WORKSHOPS at immediately following closed session in the Board Room
Category
4. Workshop Items
Type
Discussion

DESCRIPTION:

This workshop is a follow up to a previous November 10, 2020 workshop focused on equity, mission/vision, and the strategic plan as well as an initial equity deep dive that occurred during the most recent School Board Retreat on December 4, 2020. Next steps will be discussed during this Board Workshop, including:

  • Recommendations for keeping equity at the forefront of the District's work moving forward
  • Potential timelines for revising the District's current mission and vision 
  • Overall framework for approaching development of the District's next strategic plan

This discussion is intended to give Board Members the opportunity to provide District staff with feedback on how to best proceed with all efforts related to equity, mission/vision, and the future strategic plan. The presentation for this workshop will be attached no later than Monday, December 14, 2020.
