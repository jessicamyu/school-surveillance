DESCRIPTION:

The purpose of the Board Workshop is to provide an overview regarding the critical issue of community-wide housing and how the goal for “Attainable Housing” can be achieved. According to data published by the Florida Housing Data Clearinghouse, in 2015 approximately 45 percent of Palm Beach County households paid more than 30 percent of income for housing and about 23 percent paid more than 50 percent of income for housing.
