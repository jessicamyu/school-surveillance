Subject
OA1 - Adoption of the 2012-2013 School Calendar
Meeting
Jan 25, 2012 - Special Meeting
Category
New Business
Type
Action (Consent)

The School Calendar Committee elected to submit two 2012-2013 School Calendar options (Draft A and Draft B) to the Superintendent and the School Board for consideration with the Committee recommending Option “Draft A”.



 



Option Draft A:



·         Begins on August 20, 2012 (14 days before Labor Day).



·         Ends school on June 6, 2013.



·         Completes 1st semester exams prior to Winter break.



·         Has 82 instructional days in the first semester and 98 instructional days in the second semester.



·         Contains a 3-day Thanksgiving Holiday for students, beginning on November 21, 2012 through November 23, 2012.



·         Contains a 10-day Winter break for students, beginning on Monday, December 24, 2012 through Friday, January 4, 2013.



·         Contains a 5-day Spring break for students, beginning on Monday, March 25, 2013 through Friday, March 29, 2013.



·         Continues one “floating” holiday for 12-month employees provided such holiday is not used on any student attendance day.



 



Option Draft B:



·         Begins on August 13, 2012 (assumes the District is designated an Academically High-Performing School District).



·         Ends school on May 30, 2013.



·         Completes 1st semester exams prior to Winter break



·         Has 87 instructional days in the first semester and 93 instructional days in the second semester



·         Contains a 3-day Thanksgiving Holiday for students, beginning on November 21, 2012 through November 23, 2012.



·         Contains a 10-day Winter break for students, beginning on Monday, December 24, 2012 through Friday, January 4, 2013.



·         Contains a 5-day Spring break for students, beginning on Monday, March 25, 2013 through Friday, March 29, 2013.



·         Continues one “floating” holiday for 12-month employees provided such holiday is not used on any student attendance day.



 

Recommendation:



CHAIRMAN READS:  Good cause exists for adding this item to the agenda.  This recommendation is important to the district so a timely setting of the district calendar can be available to stakeholders.



SUPERINTENDENT READS:  I recommend the adoption of Option Draft A as the 2012-2013 School Calendar.
