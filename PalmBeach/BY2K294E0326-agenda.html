Subject
2016-2021 5-Year Strategic Plan Update
Meeting
Mar 3, 2021 - 3. WORKSHOP immediately following Closed Sessions in the Board Room
Category
3. Workshop Items
Type
Discussion, Information

DESCRIPTION:

At the start of FY16, the District embarked on creating a new Strategic Plan after soliciting input from our stakeholders, an unprecedented 18,000 points of engagement. What emerged was a robust Strategic Plan with 4 Long Term Outcomes, 4 Themes, 11 Objectives, and 19 evidence-based Initiatives.  As the District closes out this strategic plan and begins the process of a new one, it is vital to review the work accomplished and its impact as well as lessons learned.

Subject
Strategic Plan Workshop 2
Meeting
Mar 3, 2021 - 3. WORKSHOP immediately following Closed Sessions in the Board Room
Category
3. Workshop Items
Type
Discussion

DESCRIPTION:

Based on feedback received during Strategic Plan Workshop 1 on January 20, 2021, the Chief of Staff's Office will present an adjusted timeline for development of the District's next strategic plan. Critical structures to ensure a successful development process will also be reviewed in addition to details surrounding the Phase 1 Stakeholder Engagement Plan. The majority of workshop time will be utilized to engage Board Members in a working session meant to capture their reflections on the current strategic plan. These reflections will inform development efforts moving forward along with other internal and external stakeholder feedback collected as part of Phase 1 engagement efforts.
