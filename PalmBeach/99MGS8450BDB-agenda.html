1. Discussion Item - Robinson - Informing Parents of Needed Information
Meeting
Jul 24, 2013 - 1) WORKSHOP at 2:00 p.m. - Boardroom
Category
3. Workshop Items
Type
Discussion

Dr. Robinson would like to discuss with the Board ways of informing parents of needed information on a regular basis.

 

In a meeting with a group of 10 future administrators, the need to share information with parents for the purpose of empowering them to become active partners in their child's education was discussed. The blast phone messages are great but many phone numbers are incorrect.

 

Text messaging appears to be a great option to explore. In the future, cell phone number and permission to text can be added to the new/returning student registration form.

 

For the upcoming school year, we might ask teachers to send home notes granting permission to text. Possibly this information can be sorted by class and grade so that class/grade specific texts can be sent.

 

We can consider an online suggestion box for educators and the community to list "What Every Parent Needs To Know".  Possibly a committee can outline the messages...possibly a message every week.
