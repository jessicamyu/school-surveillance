CS1 Toussaint L’Ouverture High School for Arts & Social Justice – Proposed Third Charter Amendment to Extend with Corrective Action Plan (CAP)

Recommended Action
I recommend thE Board approve the proposed Third Amendment to extend the charter with Corrective Action Plan for Toussaint L’Ouverture High School for Arts and Social Justice, and authorize the Superintendent and/or his designee to sign all related contracts and documents.

DESCRIPTION:

On June 22, 2011, Toussaint L’Ouverture High School for Arts and Social Justice was granted a five (5) year Charter Contract to serve students in grades 9-12.  This Contract expired on June 30, 2016. On June 1, 2016, a First Amendment to the charter was granted to extend the charter one (1) year to June 30, 2017 with a Corrective Action Plan (CAP). On June 21, 2017, a Second Amendment to the charter was granted to extend the charter one (1) year to June 30, 2018 with a Corrective Action Plan (CAP).

 

The mission of the school is to, “To provide at-risk students of diverse ability levels a unique secondary school experience with particular emphasis on ESOL education for newcomer students from the Caribbean and other countries as well as for native English speakers of all proficiency levels who were born and raised in the U.S.  Using arts as a vehicle for social justice and individual change, our educational team utilizes the latest research-based methods of instruction within a small-school environment that maximizes each student’s potential and abilities and prepares students to participate in their communities and in the local and world economics.”

There may be a loss of revenue to the operating fund of $725,000 annually.
