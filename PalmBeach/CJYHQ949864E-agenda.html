DESCRIPTION:

 

A workshop on Policy 5.32: Student Illness or Accident; Policy 5.321: Administration of Student Medication/Treatment; Policy 5.3212: Blood Glucose Monitoring; Policy 5.324: Students with Life Threatening Health Conditions; Policy 5.325: Pediculosis (Head Lice) Prevention and Control.  The presentation will review changes to legislation, and modifications to these policies recommended. The updated policies provide guidance to schools as they navigate student health and wellness considerations while ensuring parental rights are honored.
