DESCRIPTION:

This is the third of several Board Workshops dedicated solely to the topic of equity. These workshops were intentionally scheduled to precede development of the District's next strategic plan.

 

Last December, the Board began initial conversations around revising the District's current mission and vision statements in anticipation of the next strategic plan development process and the need to ensure an emphasis on equity. Board Members decided at the workshop (December 16, 2020) that revisions could not occur until the Board had additional time to unpack the root causes of inequities in our educational system and come to a consensus on what equity must look like in our context.

 

Between February and March 2021, the Board engaged in one-on-one conversations with Equity Consultant Mary Fertakis and completed several pre-work activities to prepare for meaningful and robust discussions as a collective governing body. These discussions occurred as part of Equity Workshop 1 (March 9) and Equity Workshop 2 (March 31) and were facilitated by Ms. Fertakis in order to develop a shared knowledge and understanding of equity and related concepts across all Board Members. 

 

Equity Workshop 3 will focus on incorporating the Board's equity-related knowledge and understanding into the District's mission and vision statements, which will be used to ensure the District's next strategic plan emphasizes equity as a central tenet. The workshop will be structured so that Board Members can reach a consensus on necessary revisions by the conclusion of the meeting. Lastly, Board Members will begin the workshop by briefly revisiting the draft equity statement created during Equity Workshop 2 on March 31 (see attached).
