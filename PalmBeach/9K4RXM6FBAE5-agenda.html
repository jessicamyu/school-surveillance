This meeting involves the grievance filed by former employee, Learna Ramsey, against the Superintendent.



 



On November 7, 2007 the Board directed the Chairman to work with legal counsel and to bring a recommendation authorizing the scope of an investigation into Ms. Ramsey’s allegations back to the Board.  Frederick Ford, Esq. on behalf of Ms. Ramsey and Bruce Harris, Esq. on behalf of the Superintendent were directed to draft a scope of the investigation for further consideration by the Board.  However, Mr. Ford and Mr. Harris were unable to reach a consensus on the scope.  Mr. Ford’s recommended scope is attached as “Ramsey Scope Recommendation”.  Mr. Harris’ recommended scope is attached as “Superintendent Scope Recommendation”.



 



After meeting with Chairman Graham and reviewing Messrs. Ford and Harris’ recommendations, the following is the Chairman’s recommended investigation scope:



 



Whether the Superintendent followed the proper procedures, under School Board Policy 3.31, relating to Learna Ramsey’s February 25, 2006 grievance addressed to Board Chairman Tom Lynch?



 



Specifically:



 



1.      Did the Superintendent improperly influence the scope or outcome of the August 30, 2005 Grievance Committee hearing or the Grievance Committee’s recommendations?



 



2.      Was Ms. Ramsey subjected to retaliatory action by the Superintendent in her assignment as an Assistant Principal at Palm Beach Lakes HS in February, 2006?



 



3.      Did the Superintendent improperly influence how Ms. Ramsey’s February 25, 2006 grievance was handled?



 



Messrs. Ford and Harris both agreed to use Annette Szorosy, Esq. as the investigator for this matter.  However, it appears Ms. Szorosy will not be able to serve as the investigator.  Therefore, it is recommended that the Board direct Messrs. Ford and Harris to agree on an investigator and that the individual agreed upon be hired to conduct the investigation.  In the event that Messrs. Ford and Harris are unable to agree on an investigator by December 19, 2007, legal counsel for the Board will make a recommendation to the Board at the January 9, 2008 meeting.

Recommendation:

(Both Motions should be made)



A.                I move that the Board conduct an investigation relating to Learna Ramsey’s February 25, 2006 grievance addressed to Board Chairman Tom Lynch, the scope of which is as follows:



 



Whether the Superintendent followed the proper procedures, under School Board Policy 3.31, relating to Learna Ramsey’s February 25, 2006 grievance addressed to Board Chairman Tom Lynch?



 



Specifically:



 



1.      Did the Superintendent improperly influence the scope or outcome of the August 30, 2005 Grievance Committee hearing or the Grievance Committee’s recommendations?



 



2.      Was Ms. Ramsey subjected to retaliatory action by the Superintendent in her assignment as an Assistant Principal at Palm Beach Lakes HS in February, 2006?



 



3.      Did the Superintendent improperly influence how Ms. Ramsey’s February 25, 2006 grievance was handled?



 

I move that the Board hire an individual agreed to by Frederick Ford, Esq. and Bruce Harris, Esq. to serve as the investigator and task him/her with conducting an investigation according to the scope set forth above and providing a written report as to his/her findings. In the event that Messrs. Ford and Harris are unable to agree on an investigator by December 19, 2007, legal counsel for the Board is directed to bring back a recommendation to the Board at the January 9, 2008 meeting.
