The purpose of the Restructuring Workshop is to provide Board Members with an up-to-date summary of the implementation of the restructuring teams.  Information shared with them will be roles, responsibilities, outcomes and specific examples of the implementation in our schools that are restructured or are planning for restructuring.



Board Members will also be given an overview of academic yearly progress, it's academic targets and the impact it is having on our schools.
