Recommended Action
I recommend the Board approve the Renewal Charter of fifteen (15) years for G-Star School of the Arts for Motion Pictures & Television, Inc.; and authorize the Superintendent and/or his designee to sign all related contracts and documents.

DESCRIPTION:

On July 1, 2007, G-Star School of the Arts for Motion Pictures & Television was granted a ten (10) year Charter Contract to serve students in grades 9-12.  This Contract expires on July 30, 2017.  The Florida Department of Education has identified G-Star School of the Arts for Motion Pictures & Television, Inc. as a high performing charter school.
 

Pursuant to F.S. 1002.33(7)(b)1, G-Star School of the Arts for Motion Pictures & Television, Inc. meets the criteria for renewal and does not fall under any grounds for termination.  The School is in compliance with its current charter.  The Renewal Charter was reviewed and agreed upon by the appropriate District staff.
 

The renewal charter will become effective upon signing by both parties, and shall cover a term of fifteen (15) years, commencing August 1, 2017, and ending June 30, 2032, except as provided in the charter.
 

The mission of G-Star School of the Arts for Motion Pictures & Television, Inc. is to prepare and educate students to demonstrate mastery in the arts and academics; to achieve physical, social, emotional, and intellectual wellness; and to implement career goals and strategies

There may be a loss of revenue to the operating fund of $6.4 million.
