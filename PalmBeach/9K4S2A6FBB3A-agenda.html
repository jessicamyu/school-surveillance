  • The School Board and Audit Committee requested a credit card program to be used as a payment option for school fees.


  • The program will be rolled out in phases over the course of the school year.


  • The goals of the program is to




    • Lessen cash handling at schools


    • Ease the payment process for parents and employees


  • The contract includes a five-year evergreen contract for the accounting software and a two-year term contract for the online credit card facility.

Recommendation:

I recomend approval of the expenditure for the negotiated agreement with KEV Group for the accounting software and online credit card services and authorize the Superintendent and Board Chairman to sign all necessary documents.
