AC1 - Thirteenth Amendment to the Agreement with the Health Care District of Palm Beach County
Meeting
Mar 11, 2009 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Academics
Category
Consent
Type
Action (Consent)

The original Agreement was approved in 2001 to pay for basic health supplies for all schools and nursing services in schools built after 2001.



This Amendment addresses the following funding items:



 



 





  • To provide funding for full-time nurse coverage at Pine Jog Elementary School and Sunset Palms Elementary School opened in August 2008.



  •  To provide for the student supply fee of $.50 per student.




  •  To provide $75,000 per nurse for the 32 schools opened since 2001.




  • The Health Care District is responsible to pay for background checks for all of the employees participating in the school nurse program.


 



The Thirteenth Amendment with the Health Care District will be effective retroactive to July 1, 2008.  This action does not prejudice the School Board.  The reason this item is coming to the Board at this time is because of legal negotiations. 

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Thirteenth Amendment with the Health Care District for the provision of basic school health services.

Contact:

Ann Killets   akillets@palmbeach.k12.fl.us       



Alison Adler



Ann Faraone

Financial Impact:

The financial impact to the Operating Budget is estimated at $2,481,000 including nursing services and supply fees.

AC2 - FY2009 Graduation Agreements with PBC Convention Center and The Kravis Center
Meeting
Mar 11, 2009 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Academics
Category
New Business
Type
Discussion

The Palm Beach County Convention Center and The Raymond F. Kravis Center for the Performing Arts, Inc. have been reserved for graduation ceremonies for the Adult Education Center, summer school, and 22 high schools which include the following:



° Alexander Dreyfoos School of the Arts
° Atlantic Community High
° Boca Raton Community High
° Boynton Beach Community High
° Forest Hill Community High
° John I. Leonard High
° Jupiter Community High
° Lake Worth Community  High
° Palm Beach Central High
° Palm Beach Gardens High
° Palm Beach Lakes Community High
° Park Vista Community High
° Roosevelt Full Service Center
° Royal Palm Beach Community High
° Santaluces Community High
° Seminole Ridge Community High
° South Tech Academy
° Spanish River Community High
° Suncoast High
° Wellington Community High
° West Boca Community High
° William T. Dwyer High



Schools not mentioned above will facilitate graduation ceremonies at their school sites.



The graduation cost includes rehearsals, room rental fees and deposits, custodial services, and security.
Funds are included in the District operating budget for the Department of K-12 Curriculum.



The Palm Beach County Convention Center Agreement is for annual renewal and provides for multi-year use of facilities, pending availability with annual cost, dates and specific needs to be determined.  The Kravis Center Contract is a new agreement which will have to be reissued every year.



The projected cost is $360,483.10 including rental and applicable parking fees.

Recommendation:

I recommend approval of the graduation agreements with The Palm Beach County Convention Center  and The Raymond F. Kravis Center for the Performing Arts, Inc. for the FY 2009 high school graduation ceremonies, effective March 11, 2009 in the amount of $360,483.10 and authorize the Superintendent to sign all related documents and contracts.

The financial impact is  $360,483.10 .  The source of  funds is the operating budget of the Department of K-12 Curriculum.
