CS1 University Preparatory Academy - School Improvement Plan
Meeting
Nov 8, 2017 - 4. SPECIAL MEETING immediately following Workshop in the Board Room
Category
12. New Business
Type
Action
Recommended Action
I recommend the Board approve the School Improvement Plan for University Preparatory Academy, and authorize the Superintendent
and/or his designee to sign all related correspondence and documents.

DESCRIPTION:

On July 1, 2014, University Preparatory Academy was granted a five (5) year Charter Contract to serve students in grades K-8. The Charter became effective upon signing by both parties, and covers a term of five (5) years commencing July 1, 2014, and ending June 30, 2019, unless amended or terminated as provided in the Charter.

University Preparatory Academy received a grade of “F” for the fiscal year 2017.

Florida Statute 1002.33, Section 9(n)(1) states: “The director and a representative of the governing board of a charter school that has earned a grade of “D” or “F” pursuant to s. 1008.34(2) shall appear before the sponsor to present information concerning each contract component having noted deficiencies. The director and a representative of the governing board shall submit to the sponsor for approval a school improvement plan to raise student achievement. Upon approval by the sponsor, the charter school shall begin implementation of the school improvement plan. The department shall offer technical assistance for developing, submitting, and approving such plans.” (emphasis added)

The Department of Charter Schools staff provided technical assistance to University Preparatory Academy in the drafting of the School Improvement Plan attached.
