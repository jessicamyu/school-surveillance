The Leasing Corporation is a Florida not-for-profit corporation that has as its sole member the School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida.  The corporation facilitates the School Districtâ€™s lease purchase financing of school facilities, commonly referred to as COPs (or Certificates of Participation).
