Subject
Special Meeting re: Fees and Costs re: 05-C Elementary Eminent Domain
Meeting
Nov 4, 2009 - Legal Sessions
Category
Special Meetings
Type
Action (Consent)


In September 2007, School Board authorized eminent domain on a 20 acre ± parcel in unincorporated western Boca Raton.  This parcel was identified as the site for the new 05-C Elementary School. 



 



The eminent domain suit was filed in June, 2008. 



 



On April 8, 2009, the Board directed the Chief Counsel’s office to dismiss the eminent domain suit.



 



Pursuant to §73.091 and §73.092, Florida Statutes, the Board is responsible for the attorney’s fees and expert witness costs incurred by the landowner.  The landowner’s attorney initially requested attorney’s fees and costs in the amount of approximately $300,000. 



 



Through negotiation with the landowner’s attorney, an agreement on attorney’s fees and two of the expert witnesses was reached.  A second agreement with regard to three more of the experts and the landowner is pending.  All totaled, the attorney’s fees and expert witness costs covered by the two agreements are $168,107.18. 



 



Still unresolved and currently scheduled for a hearing, are the fees due to the last expert in the case, the landowner’s surveyor/engineer.  The landowner’s attorney is also entitled to a supplemental fee for his work with regard to the establishment of the fees and costs due.  These remaining fees and costs will be brought back to the Board on a subsequent agenda for approval.



 

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the payments pursuant to §73.091 and §73.092, Florida Statutes related to the lawsuit styled School Board of Palm Beach County v. E.F. Johns, Ltd., et al, Case Number 502008CA019824XXXXMBAA and authorize the School Board attorney to execute all necessary documents.
