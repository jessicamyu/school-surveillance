The following is a brief history of the events leading up to the Settlement, Full and Final Release, Indemnity, and Hold Harmless Agreement.



 





  • This matter involves the Academy Building at Boynton Beach Community High School (Academy).

  • The South Florida Water Management District regulates the management and storage of surface waters in, among other areas, Palm Beach County, Florida.  Chapter 373, Fla. Stat., and Rule 40E-4.011, Florida Administrative Code.  On June 15, 2006, the School Board was issued a permit by the South Florida Water Management District for the Academy.  The School Board is in compliance with the South Florida Water Management District.

  • On May 8, 2007, the Quantum Community Development District (QCDD) served its lawsuit against the School District of Palm Beach County, Florida, more properly known as The School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida (School Board), Case No. 50 2007 007046 XXXX MB AN (Lawsuit) seeking a temporary and permanent injunction to enjoin the School Board from constructing the Academy building at Boynton Beach Community High School in Quantum Corporate Park alleging that such construction will have a material impact on the QCDD facilities and that the School Board failed to submit plans for the building to the QCDD for approval as set out in a 1999 Settlement Agreement between the QCDD and the School District, and as such is a violation of the Settlement Agreement.  The parties to the 1999 Settlement Agreement are as follows: The QCDD, the Quantum Property Owners Association, Inc., and the Quantum Limited Partners, Ltd., and the School Board.

  • On May 15, 2007, the School Board filed a Motion to Dismiss the Lawsuit on the following grounds: 1) The Complaint fails to allege all of the elements of temporary and permanent injunction, hence lacks a proper legal basis for a claim against the SCHOOL DISTRICT, 2) The Complaint fails to attach a document upon which the action is based, 3) Failure to join indispensable party, 4) Multifarious Pleading, and 5) Naming wrong party.

  • On May 29, 2007, the School Board filed a Motion to Abate the lawsuit pursuant to the Florida Governmental Conflict Resolution Act, Chapter 164, Fla. Stat.

  • On June 7, 2007, the Court granted the School Boardâ€™s Motion to Abate and currently the Lawsuit is abated by an Agreed Order pending the outcome of the Florida Governmental Conflict Resolution Act process pursuant to Chapter 164, Fla. Stat.

  • On June 28, 2007, the QCDD passed a Resolution to initiate conflict resolution procedures under the Florida Governmental Conflict Resolution Act.

  • On July 11, 2007, the School Board passed a Resolution to initiate conflict resolution procedures under the Florida Governmental Conflict Resolution Act.

  • On August 2, 2007, the School Board and the QCDD participated in a duly noticed Conflict Assessment Meeting pursuant to Chapter 164, Fla. Stat.

  • On March 20, 2008, the School Board, the QCDD, and a representative of both the Quantum Property Owners Association, Inc., and the Quantum Limited Partners, Ltd. participated in a duly noticed Joint Public Meeting pursuant to Chapter 164, Fla. Stat.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve the terms of the Settlement, Full and Final release, Indemnity, and Hold Harmless Agreement with the Quantum Community Development District, the Quantum Property Owners Association, the Quantum Limited Partners, Ltd., and Pavarini Construction Co. (SE), Inc. contingent upon execution of the Settlement document by all parties and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to execute the Settlement document, and all related documents.
