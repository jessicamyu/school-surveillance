Payment To Contract between Palm Beach County, FL and Arbor E&T, LLC for FY07

Jan 30, 2008 - Spec Mtg re Excel
Category
Consent Agenda

Discussion

The School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida approved a contract (and two amendments thereto) with Arbor E&T, LLC for FY07.  The services provided by Arbor E&T, LLC at four Excel Alternative Sites included counseling, social skills training, and substance abuse awareness training for Middle and High School aged students.  Several invoices for services provided under that contract remain outstanding.  This request will enable the School Board to compensate Arbor E&T, LLC (Excel Alternatives) for educational services provided at the West Area and North Area Sites in fiscal year 07.



 



The amount of $340,845.00 is for the following uncontested invoices:



 



         PBWest01     35,408.00



         PBWest02     42,047.00



         PBWest03     46,473.00



         PBWest04     33,195.00



         PBWest05     48,686.00



         PBNorth05  135,036.00



 



The contract with Arbor E&T, LLC (Excel Alternatives) was not renewed for fiscal year 08.



 

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the payment to Arbor E&T, LLC (Excel Alternatives) for monies due from services provided January 07 through May 07.

Contact:

akillets@palmbeach.k12.fl.us



 



Janice Cover



 



Alexandra Deveroux

Financial Impact:

The financial impact to the  FY08 General Fund Operating budget is $340,845.00.  The source of funds is from the Alternative Education operating budget.
