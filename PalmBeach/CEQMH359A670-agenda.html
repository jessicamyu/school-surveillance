Recommended Action
I recommend the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp authorize the resolution to issue COP series 2022B with an aggregate principal amount not to exceed $230 million.

DESCRIPTION:

  • The FY 2022 Capital Plan includes the issuance of debt to fund the construction of several projects.  Based on the construction schedules, construction will begin on three projects this year.  
    • Sunset Palms Area MS (17-PP)
    • Grove Park ES Modernization
    • Melaleuca ES Modernization
    • Lake Worth HS - Facility Renewal
    • Olympic Heights HS - Facility Renewal
    • Santaluces HS - Facility Renewal
    • William T Dwyer HS - Facility Renewal

 

  • The four facility renewal projects were originally funded with sales tax.  These projects will now be funded with the debt proceeds, freeing the sales tax dollars to be used to cover rising construction costs.
  • The transaction will mature no later than 8/1/2047.
  • The estimated costs associated with the issuance of the debt is not to exceed $1.5 million.  The details of the costs of issuance are attached.
  • The Finance Committee reviewed the transactions at their meeting on May 20, 2022 and unanimously recommended the transaction to the School Board for approval.
  • The School Board will also consider this item on June 15, 2022 (agenda item FMT1).
  • A summary of the Leasing Corporation is also attached as a reference.

 

Original Recommendation:  I recommend the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp authorize the resolution to issue COP series 2022B with an aggregate principal amount not to exceed $225 million.
