Special Meeting re: Ricky Woods Final Order
Meeting
Jul 7, 2010 - Legal Sessions
Category
Special Meetings
Type
Action




  • The

    Superintendent made a recommendation that Ricky Woods be terminated  for employment for     failure to exercise

    best professional judgment, engaged in inappropriate physical force on a

    student, and engaged in inappropriate interaction with students in violation of

    School District Policies 1.013, as well as School Board Administrative

    Directive 3.27; and State Board of Education Rules 6B-1.001 and 6B-1.006.    
  • Mr. Woods timely

    asserted his right to an appeal before the Division of Administrative Hearings

    (DOAH).
  • On January

    6, 2010 an Administrative Hearing was held before Judge Patricia Hart regarding

    the termination of Ricky Woods.
  • On April

    23, 2010, Judge Hart issued a Recommended Order that the Board enter a Final

    Order dismissing the charges against Mr. Woods.
  • This Final Order is in the best interest of the

    School Board.
















Recommendation:

I

recommend that the School Board approve the Final Order in the matter of School

Board of Palm Beach County v. Ricky Woods, DOAH case No. 09-4238, a copy of

which is attached hereto, and authorize the execution of all related documents.

Contact:

Bruce A. Harris, Esq.; Senior Counsel
Vicki L. Evans-Par, Esq.; Associate Counsel

Financial Impact:





The financial impact is $32,811.60 in

gross back wages to be paid from the salary account.
