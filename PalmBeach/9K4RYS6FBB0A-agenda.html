This litigation was brought by a school bus driver and other similarly situated employees who were seeking back wages for allegedly unpaid overtime wages.

Settlement is recommended as being in the best financial interest of the District due to the anticipated costs of the litigation and the method of payment to employees and the Fair Labor Standards Act.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Settlement Agreement between Evangeline Paterson and other similarly situated Plaintiffs and the School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to execute all related documents.
