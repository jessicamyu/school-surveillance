Action

Berger Singerman



Legal Advisor performed the investigation of the grievances filed by former employee, Learna Ramsey.  In the course of finalizing the investigation report, Legal Advisor exceeded the approved total under the contract, however in the interest of time and in good faith, Legal Advisor completed and submitted his report.  Legal Advisor voluntarily offered to cut the overage by 30% resulting in a shortfall of $5,600.07.



 



Edwards Angell Palmer & Dodge, LLP



Legal Advisor shall provide legal services associated with acquisition by eminent domain of properties required for the New Suncoast High School as assigned Legal Advisor by the Chief Legal Counsel from time to time, including, but not limited to preparation and filing of pleadings, attendance at hearings, mediation, trial and appeals. 



 



This amendment is to increase the maximum payment amount an additional $50,000 for the term of this contract.



 



Acquisition of the property necessary New Suncoast High School has been underway for the last several years.  The four day eminent domain trial in April depleted the remaining balance of the funds.



 

There are approximately eight parcels owned by four different owners that are currently subject to a pending eminent domain suit.  These parcels, if not settled, will need to be tried on the issue of valuation.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the Board amend the contract with Berger Singerman to increase the authorized amount by an additional $2,600.07.  I also recommend that the Board amend the contract with Edwards Angell Palmer & Dodge, LLP for $50,000, through December 31, 2008, and authorize the Superintendent and Board Chairman to sign all necessary documents.
