Special Meeting to Dismiss Lawsuit
Meeting
Apr 8, 2009 - Legal Sessions
Category

Type
Action

In September 2007, School Board authorized eminent domain on a 20 acre ± parcel in unincorporated western Boca Raton.  This parcel was identified as the site for the new 05-C Elementary School. 



 



The eminent domain suit was filed in June, 2008. 



 



An Order of Taking Hearing is currently scheduled for May 7, 2009. 



 



Based upon the discussion at the March 25, 2009 Board Workshop, staff is recommending that the suit be dismissed prior to the Order of Taking Hearing. 



 

Under Florida law, the Board is responsible for the attorney’s fees and expert costs incurred by the landowner.  Following the dismissal of this suit, the fees and costs incurred by the landowner will be brought back to the Board for approval.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board direct Chief Counsel to dismiss the lawsuit styled School Board of Palm Beach County v. E.F. Johns, Ltd., et al, Case Number 502008CA019824XXXXMBAA and authorize the School Board attorney to execute all necessary documents.

Contact:

Gerald A. Williams, Esq; Chief Counsel williamsg@palmbeach.k12.fl.us



Blair R. LittleJohn, Esq.; Senior Counsel

Financial Impact:

The financial impact is not to exceed $250,000.
