Recommended Action
I recommend the Board expel student 21/22-X-008, from November 12, 2021, through August 1, 2022. If the student successfully completes this school year at the Alternative School Placement and demonstrates regular attendance, passing grades, and completes all counseling support services required, the student will be allowed to enroll at a comprehensive campus for the 2022/2023 School Year. In the event the student does not meet these requirements, the student will not be allowed to re-enroll in a comprehensive campus until November 12, 2022.

DESCRIPTION:

Recommendation for Expulsion of student 21/22-X-008.  On November 12, 2021, Respondent was in possession of a taser.  I further recommend the attached Final Order be adopted and entered by the School Board.
