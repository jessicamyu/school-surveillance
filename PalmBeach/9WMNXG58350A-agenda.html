6. NEW BUSINESS
Subject
LC3 Authorize Issuance of COP 2015G to Refund COP 2003B and Terminate Associated Interest Rate Swap
Meeting
May 27, 2015 - 4. SPECIAL MEETING for PALM BEACH SCHOOL BOARD LEASING CORP immediately following Regular Special Meeting in the Board Room
Category
6. NEW BUSINESS
Type
Action
Recommended Action
I recommend the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp authorize the issuance of COP series 2015G to refund or restructure 2003B COP and terminate the associated interest rate swap.

DESCRIPTION:

  • This resolution for the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp authorizes the School Board to move forward with the issuance of COP series 2015G, which will refund the 2003B COP.
  • As part of the transaction, the District will terminate the associated interest rate swap.
  • The School Board's Finance Committee met on April 23rd and unanimously recommended moving the transaction forward to the School Board and Leasing Corp for consideration.  Minutes of the meeting are available online at http://www.palmbeachschools.org/treasury/FinComm2015.asp
  • The School Board will consider the approval of this matter on May 27, 2015 with agenda item FMT-3.
  • A summary of the Leasing Corp is also attached for reference.

CONTACT:

Michael J. Burke, Chief Operating Officer (mike.burke@palmbeachschools.org)

Leanne Evans, Treasurer (leanne.evans@palmbeachschools.org)
