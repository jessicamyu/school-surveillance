I recommend the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp approve the slate of officers.

DESCRIPTION:

The Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp is a Florida not-for-profit corporation that has as its sole member the School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida.  The corporation facilitates the School District’s lease purchase financing of school facilities, commonly referred to as COPs (or Certificates of Participation).  

 

A summary of the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp is attached.

 

The bylaws of the Leasing Corp require an annual meeting of the Board of Directors to elect corporate officers.  That meeting was held on January 24, 2018.  Another election is required to add Dr. Fennoy as the new secretary of the corporation.  Palm Beach School Board Members or School District Staff may be elected to serve as officers.

 

The Current Officers are as follows:

 

President           Chuck Shaw, School Board Chairman

Vice President    Debra Robinson, MD, School Board Vice-Chairwoman

Secretary           Dr. Robert Avossa, Superintendent
