Meeting

Category

Type
Information

DESCRIPTION:

School Board Members will discuss their legislative priorities for the upcoming 2022 Florida Legislative Session, which is scheduled to begin in early March.  Feedback generated during this workshop will be used by internal staff to draft the SDPBC Legislative Platform and Issues of Continuing Concern documents for FY22.  In addition, the School Board will discuss priorities to include as part of a federal-level legislative platform.
