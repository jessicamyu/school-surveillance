Subject
LD1 Possible Challenge to Portions of House Bill 7069
Meeting
Aug 9, 2017 - 4. SPECIAL MEETING immediately following Workshop in the Board Room
Category
10. Consent Agenda
Type
Action (Consent)
Recommended Action
I recommend the Board authorize the General Counsel to initiate or join litigation challenging only those specific provisions of HB 7069 that raise constitutional and legal issues as outlined in this item as soon as practicable, subject to the Board's final review and approval of an interlocal or other agreement setting forth costs of litigation, engagement of law firm, and case management process. I further recommend authorizing an initial expenditure of up to $25,000 for attorneys fees, utilizing funds from the BP Oil Spill recovery.

DESCRIPTION:

The School Board will discuss the possibility of challenging certain portions of House Bill 7069, whether it would want to explore joining legal challenges with other Florida school boards, hiring of outside counsel related to this matter.  The Board may take any action, if needed.

 

House Bill 7069 was recently enacted by the Florida legislature, signed by the Governor, and became effective July 1, 2017.  Portions of this legislation has a major impact on the School District and local control of public education in the County.

 

At the July 19, 2017 Board meeting the Board’s motion asked for the General Counsel to consult with an outside law firm in order to provide the Board with options to consider relating to a legal challenge to this legislation.

 

CONTACT:

JulieAnn Rico, Esq., General Counsel (julieann.rico@palmbeachschools.org)

 

FINANCIAL IMPACT:

Attorneys’ fees and costs up to $25,000 at this time if the Board decides to challenge the legislation, funded from the BP Oil Spill recovery.
