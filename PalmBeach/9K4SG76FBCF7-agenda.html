Subject
School Board Approve Sale of Certificates of Participation Series 2008A
Meeting
Sep 24, 2008 - Spec Mtg re School Board and PBC Leasing Corp
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Action

This Resolution delegates authority to the Superintendent to negotiate the terms of the transaction with the underwriters within the parameters set forth in the resolution.  The Resolution also authorizes the execution and delivery of all instruments and documents necessary to complete the transaction.



 



The COP issuance is scheduled as part of the Five Year Capital Plan that was adopted in September 2008.



 



The issue will be sized to net the School District sufficient funds to finance the named projects.



 



The Finance Committee reviewed the transaction at their meeting on September 5, 2008. 



 



Note: Some of the attachments to the authorizing resolution are forthcoming from bond and underwriting counsel.  These documents will be available in the Board Office.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board adopt a Resolution authorizing execution of Schedule 2008A to the Master Lease and approve the sale of Certificates of Participation, Series 2008A, in an aggregate principal amount not to exceed $110,000,000. 

Contact:

Joseph M. Moore (moorej@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)



Michael J. Burke (burkem@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)



Leanne Evans (evansl@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)

Financial Impact:

The issuance of the 2008A COPs are undertaken to provide the funding needed to finance a portion of the FY2009 Capital Budget.  Revenue source of approximately $106,000,000.
