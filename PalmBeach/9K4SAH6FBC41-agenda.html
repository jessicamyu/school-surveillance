Subject
Workshop on Prone Restraints
Meeting
May 11, 2011 - Workshops
Category
Workshop Items
Type
Action
This workshop is designed to provide School Board members with information regarding the use of prone restraints in schools. This workshop is provided at the request of Mrs. Brill prior to the formal adoption of School Board Policy 5.181: Policy for the Use of Physical Restraint with Students with Disabilities.

Two experts will present information to the School Board. Dr. Merrill Winston from Professional Crisis Management Association (PCMA)will demonstrate a prone restraint and share his view on why the prone restraint associated with PCMA constitutes safe and effective crisis management. Ms. Kevin Ann Huckshorn, the Director of the Delaware Division of Substance Abuse and Mental Health and the Acting Director of the Delaware State Hospital System will share her concerns about the dangers of prone restraint. It is her professional opinion that prone restraint should be eliminated in public schools. The goal of the workshop is to provide the Board with information on both sides of this controversial issue.

Glades Schools Update
Meeting
May 11, 2011 - Workshops
Category
Workshop Items

Discussion

Over the past two years, staff has been working with the residents, schools, SACs, parents and elected officials  in the Glades area to develop a plan that best meets the needs of the students.



On October 6, 2010 the School Board provided a directive to staff for the modernization of schools.



The directive however, did not address grade configuration and academic benefits.



This discussion item is to present academic information, current and projected school utilization and address changed conditions in order  to obtain Board directive on planning for the students needs and schools in the Glades area.
