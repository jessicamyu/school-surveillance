I recommend the School Board adopt the proposed millage levies of 7.586 mills for fiscal year 2014. This millage levy is made up of: 5.076 mills for required local effort; 0.012 mills for prior period RLE adjustment; 0.727 mills for discretionary operating; 1.500 mills for local capital improvement; 0.021 mills for discretionary capital improvement; and 0.250 mills for additional operating.

Motion & Voting

I recommend the School Board adopt the proposed millage levies of 7.586 mills for fiscal year 2014. This millage levy is made up of: 5.076 mills for required local effort; 0.012 mills for prior period RLE adjustment; 0.727 mills for discretionary operating; 1.500 mills for local capital improvement; 0.021 mills for discretionary capital improvement; and 0.250 mills for additional operating.

Motion by Karen Brill, second by Frank Barbieri.
Final Resolution: Motion Passes
