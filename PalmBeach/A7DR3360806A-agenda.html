Category
2. Workshop Items
Type
Presentation

DESCRIPTION:

On December 17, 2014, the School Board approved the Master Agreement between the School Board of Palm Beach County and the Metropolitan Center for Urban Education at New York University to conduct an Educational Equity Audit of the Palm Beach County School District.  On February 18, 2015, the School Board approved the Project Plan for the Equity Audit.  Dr. Roey Ahram and Ms. Madelyn Boesen of the Metropolitan Center will present the Second Interim Report of the progress of the audit.
