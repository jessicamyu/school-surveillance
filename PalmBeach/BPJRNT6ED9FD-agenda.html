DESCRIPTION:

Policy 5.1817 - Severe Incident Assignment - Adoption 

(previously named “Student Expulsion”)


The following changes were made to the proposed policy after the Board meeting on April 29, 2020:

  • Lines 9-12: New language: ”Any disciplinary action taken pursuant to this policy shall be in accordance with the provisions of the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act, 20 U.S.C. § 1400 et seq.; Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, 34 C.F.R. Part 104 et seq., and School Board Policy 5.725”.
  • Line 42: Removed repetitive language: “Upon being charged”
  • Policy has been updated to provide disciplinary consequences for students who engage in behavior that has an imminent adverse impact on the safety and welfare of a school campus.
