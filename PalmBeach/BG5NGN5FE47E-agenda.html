DESCRIPTION:

The District School Safety Specialist will share a summary of findings related to the Florida Safe Schools Assessment Tool (FSSAT) conducted for all public schools. Recommendations to improve school safety and security will also be discussed to identify strategies and activities that the District School Board should implement in order to address the findings. This public meeting is being held in accordance with SB 7030.
