RE 1 Northboro ES/City of West Palm Beach - Purchase and Sale Agreement
Meeting
Mar 25, 2009 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Operations
Category
Consent
Type
Action


  1. The FY 2009-2013 Five Year Plan includes the modernization of the Northboro Elementary School campus.


  2. Staff ordered updated title work for the Northboro Elementary School campus.  During this process it was discovered that the City of West Palm Beach hold's title to +/- 0.052 acres and +/- 0.089 acres of the School site.


  3. The subject parcels are situated on the Northboro Elementary School campus between building 2 and building 5, and an underlying portion of building 3.  (See Attached Aerial Exhibit "A")


  4. The School Board requested conveyance of the +/- 0.052 acres and the +/- 0.089 acres to rectify the title discrepancy.


  5. The total appraised value for both parcels is $150,000.00.  The contract price is $150,000.00.


  6. The Real Estate Purchase and Sale Agreement is subject to the City's final approval, April 6, 2009.


  7. Approval of this acquisition is needed in order to meet construction deadlines.


  8. Closing costs: $2,000.00.


 

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve the Purchase and Sale Agreement between the City of West Palm Beach and the School Board of Palm Beach County for +/- 0.052 acres and +/- 0.089 acres, part of the Northboro Elementary School campus; and authorize the Superintendent and Chairman to sign all necessary documents.

The financial impact to the Capital Project's Fund Budget is $152,000.00.

Certification of Facilities Data

  • WHEREAS, §1013.03(3) F.S., states in part that the Department of Education must, "Require boards to submit other educational plant inventories data…."






  • WHEREAS, §1013.31(1)(d) F.S., states in part, "...School districts shall periodically update their inventory of educational facilities…."






  • WHEREAS, State Requirements for Educational Facilities (SREF) §6.1(7)(c) requires that, "Prior to April 1 of each year, each district shall review the Florida Inventory of School Houses (FISH) and shall certify to the Office of Educational Facilities that the inventory is current and accurate."






  • THEREFORE, the School District of Palm Beach County hereby certifies that the educational facilities inventory data for the district contained in the Florida Inventory of School Houses (FISH) is current and accurate pursuant to applicable statutes and rules.
