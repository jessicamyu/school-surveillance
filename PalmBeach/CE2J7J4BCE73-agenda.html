DESCRIPTION:

The settlement of this General Liability Claim No. 6769510 was recommended by District counsel and claim adjusters and has been approved by the General Counsel, Chief Financial Officer, and the Director of Risk & Benefits Management as required by District Policy 6.09.  The bulk of the claim, $152,500, will be paid by the District's active shooter insurance company.

 

INCIDENT: 

55-year-old Claimant at the  time of the incident, broke a hip when she fell while abruptly leaving PB Central HS football stadium following criminal activity involving gun shots outside the stadium.

 

DATE OF LOSS:  08/17/2018

 

INJURIES:  Broken hip, which required a right total hip arthroplasty.

 

MEDICAL COSTS:  $185,227.68

 

CONTACT:

The financial impact to the District for this settlement is $10,000.  The source of funds is the Workers' Compensation and Liability Claims Internal Service Fund.

  • Increase the Automotive Service Excellence (ASE) Incentive from $150 to $250 per certification and expand eligibility to employees working as a Transportation Foreperson.
  • Attendance Supplement for Bus Drivers and Bus Attendants will not be removed when absent for bereavement leave or for any vehicle violations or accidents while operating a school bus provided a summons was not issued at the scene.
  • Increase the annual Tools Allowance from $300 to $600.
  • Provide Transportation Helpers with an annual Tools Allowance of $300.
  • Provide additional supplements to School Food Service Managers who supervise two sites.
  • Provide designated School Food Service Manager supplements to employees who are working in the capacity of a School Food Service Manager.
  • Additional language modifications per the Tentative Agreement
  • Mutual agreement to meet to discuss the following areas:
    • Article 14, Section 1B - Bus Attendant Route Bidding

    • Article 5, Section 6 - Dues Deductions
