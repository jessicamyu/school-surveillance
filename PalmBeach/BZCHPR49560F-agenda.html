BD1 Board Discussion - ANDREWS - In Honor of U.S. Congressman Alcee Hastings
Meeting
Apr 7, 2021 - 3. SPECIAL MEETING immediately following Workshop in the Board Room
Category
13. Board Discussion Items
Type
Discussion
School Board Member Marcia Andrews will discuss the following:
 
U.S. Congressman Alcee Hastings....A Legend. 
 
We honor his service as a lawyer, federal judge, and member of the United States Congress. His outstanding work at the local, state, and federal level is appreciated. 
 
I request to have in his honor:
  • A Proclamation.
  • To have a school, building, etc. named after him.
  • The Department of Curriculum should incorporate lessons into all history classes educating students about US Congressman Alcee Hastings and his many contributions.  It is important that students fully understand how their civic duty plays an important role within this society.
