I recommend the Board accept the General Counsel's recommendation to settle the federal lawsuit styled U.S.A. v. School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida, and execute all related documents.

DESCRIPTION:

This matter involves a lawsuit by the U.S. Department of Justice ("DOJ"). The lawsuit alleges discrimination by the District against Ms. Dorsey based on her sex (pregnancy) and retaliation for reporting alleged sexual harrassment.

 

Mediation in this case was held on November 15, 2016. The DOJ has presented a global settlement demand which includes entering into consent decree with no admission of liability. Ms. Dorsey will release the Board from any and all claims.
