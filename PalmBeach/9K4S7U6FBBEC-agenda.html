This Project Modification contains the following item:







  • Change Order Proposal Request #1 for non-compensable time extensions for Substantial and Final Completion, and a credit in the amount of $3,281,679.88.




  • Description:  Time extensions and final contract reconciliation. Extend the Substantial Completion date six (6) calendar days from December 23, 2008 to December 29, 2008. Extend the Final Completion date 277 calendar days from April 8, 2009 to January 12, 2010.




    • Direct Material Purchase (DMP) Deduct ($2,656,510.22)

    • Unused Construction Contingency ($4,684.01)

    • Sales Tax in Excess of Guaranteed Amount  ($53,849.61)

    • Contract Buy-Out Savings  ($117,557.04)

    • Unused Allowances ($258,775.00)

    • Master Control Insurance Program (MCIP) Deduct  ($190,304.00)


  • Cause: School District Requested



  • Justification:  Funds are deducted from the Construction Manager's (CM) Agreement that the School District uses for purchasing materials and equipment directly from suppliers.  These purchases are tax free.  The CM's Agreement is also adjusted to return unused contingency, sales tax savings in excess of the guaranteed amount, buyout savings and unused allowances to the School District. Insurance payments to the MCIP by the CM and its subcontractors are also deducted from the CM Agreement amount.  The Substantial Completion date was extended 6 days so that final systems testing could be completed over winter break.  The Final Completion date was extended because the school requested that occupancy occur after FCAT testing and work added after Substantial Completion.  The additional work included an ESE playground and fencing around the water meters and backflow preventers.

RE-2 Approve Special Use Agreement with PBC/Park Vista HS
Meeting
Apr 28, 2010 - Spec Mtgs and Workshop re Operations
Category
Consent
Type
Action (Consent)

1.  On May 18, 2005, the School Board approved the Special Use Agreement between Palm Beach County and the School Board of Palm Beach County for use of recreational facilities at the West Boynton District Park ("Park") and facilities and parking at Park Vista Community High School. (2005 Board Report, Exhibit "A")



2.  The existing Agreement will expire May 3, 2010.  A new Agreement has been negotiated that will permit both parties continued use of facilities.



3.  The following provisions in the new Agreement are the same as contained in 2005 Agreement:



     a.  The School Board will continue to have first priority use of  the major league baseball field at the Park for boy's baseball practice and games and the softball field at the Park for girl's softball practice and games during the Florida High School Activities Association seasons.
     b.  The School Board will continue to operate and maintain a County owned concession trailer at the Park, during the baseball and softball seasons.
     c.  The School Board shall be allowed to situate and maintain a portable shed and ice machine at the Park.
     d.  Public use of the tennis courts, multipurpose courts and general parking at Park Vista High School is allowed during normal park operating hours, after normal school hours and other times that do not  conflict with the School Board's use.  
      e. The County will continue to utilize approximately .25 acres of School Board owned land solely for vehicular parking for County employees.
      f.  The County shall have continued access to modular classrooms and the gymnasium for summer camp and activities.



4.  Major changes in the new Agreement are as follows:
 
      a.  The County shall no longer utilize the auditorium and art rooms for summer camp events, activities and special events.
      b.  Modular units have been placed on Park Vista High School's practice fields and therefore are no longer available for School and County use.



5.  Park Vista High School does not have practice fields, softball fields and baseball fields.



6.  Approval of this Agreement is needed to support the athletic program at Park Vista High School and maintain a cooperative working relationship with the County and Community.



7.  The initial term of this Agreement shall run until June 30, 2011 and shall be automatically renewed up to four (4) additional consecutive one (1) year terms.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve the Special Use Agreement between Palm Beach County and the School Board of Palm Beach County for the use of recreational facilities at the West Boynton District Park and facilities and parking at Park Vista Community High School; and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to sign all necessary documents.

Contact:

Joseph M. Moore (moorej@palmbeachschools.org)
Joseph Sanches (sanchesj@palmbeachschool.org)
