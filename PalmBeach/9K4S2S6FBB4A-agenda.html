Financial Impact:

The financial impact to the General Fund is an increase of $18,445,435.  The financial impact to the Debt Service Funds is a decrease of $26,778,802.  The financial impact to the Capital Projects Funds is an increase of $46,611,005.  The financial impact to the Special Revenue Funds - Food Service is an increase of $132,940.  The financial impact to the Special Revenue Funds - Other Federal Programs is a decrease of $2,269,087.  The financial impact to the Internal Service Funds (Self Insurance - Health) is an increase of $154,710,649.  These funds have been appropriated in reserve, school, and department budgets.  Appropriations in all funds have been amended to direct resources where needed.
