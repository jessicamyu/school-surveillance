This Project Modification contains the following items:



Item #1







  • CCD #1 in the amount of $44,282.00 and the addition of two hundred ten (210) non compensable calendar days.




  • Description: Extend the project schedule by an additional two hundred ten (210) non compensable (210) calendar days.   Revise the final completion date from January 22, 2007 to August 20, 2007.




  • Cause: School District and Contractor Requested




  • Justification: Work scope was increased by Change Proposals CP-4, -5, -8, -9, -10, -11, -12R, -15, -21, -22, -23, -24, -25, -26 and -27. Contract days (non compensable) were added to accomplish the punchlist work. School building openings during this phased project were not impacted. 


Item #2







  • CCD #2 credit in the amount of ($11,818.60) and the addition of eighty-seven (87) non compensable calendar days.




  • Description: Extend the project schedule by an additional eighty-seven (87) non compensable calendar days.   Revise the final completion date from August 20, 2007 to November 15, 2007.




  • Cause: School District and Contractor Requested




  • Justification: Work scope was reduced by input from the school administration and Program Management. Credits were negotiated and applied to the contract amount. Revision on the plans for chiller yard work and Test and Balance Punchlist work delayed the Final Completion date. Occupancy of school campus was not affected and has remained so since original scheduled Final Completion of January 22, 2007.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board ratify Project Modifications in the amount of $32,463.40 and  the addition of two hundred ninety-seven (297) calendar days to Hill York Corporation for Wellington Landings Middle School,  HVAC Project #1701 8222 and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to execute all related contract documents.

RE-1 Pahokee H.S. Stadium Agreement
Meeting
Jan 30, 2008 - Spec Mtg re Construction Projects
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Action (Consent)


  • The 2008-2012 Five Year Capital Plan includes a new Stadium for Pahokee High School.

  • Midway Foundation Inc. has agreed to sell 14.87 acres located on Larrimore Road across the street from Pahokee Middle/High School for the new Pahokee High School Stadium.

  • The School Board owns approximately 10.34 acres on Rardin Avenue., Pahokee, formerly known as the School of Choice.

  • The School Board will exchange the 10.34 acres for the 14.87 acres to reduce the cost of the land purchase for the stadium. (See Exhibit "A")

  • The appraised value of Midway's 14.87 acres is $423,000.00.

  • The appraised value of the School Board's 10.34 acres is $300,000.00.

  • A Phase I and Phase II Environmental Audit conducted on the 14.87 acre new Pahokee High School Stadium site has revealed the presence of muck and soil contamination.  As part of the agreement, Midway will demuck and fill the site according to Program Management's requirements for a cost of $4,051,636.00 within 90 days from the effective date.

  • The Deeds, Purchase Price and Demucking Fee will be held in Escrow as security for the timely and satisfactory completion of Midway's obligation to demuck and fill the Midway Property.

  • The Chief of Facilities Management will execute the Escrow Agreement's Exhibit "A" and Exhibit "B" per paragraph 3(c) and paragraph 4b ii (2).

  • Closing Costs: $5,500.00


 



 



 

Recommendation:

(1)       I recommend the School Board approve the Agreement for Purchase, Sale and Exchange and the Escrow Agreement between the School Board of Palm Beach County and Midway Foundation, Inc., providing for the exchange of properties by the parties, at appraised value; approve the Resolution declaring, only for the purposes associated with the subject Agreement for Purchase, Sale and Exchange, the School of Choice School site to be unnecessary for educational purposes and surplus; authorize the Chief of Facilities Management or his designee to execute instruments required in the Escrow Agreement; and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to sign all other necessary documents.



 



(2)       I recommend the School Board approve the Agreement for Purchase, Sale and Exchange and the Escrow Agreement between the School Board of Palm Beach County and Midway Foundation, Inc., providing for the demucking and filling of the 14.87 acre site to be acquired by the School Board.
