Recommended Action
I recommend the Board adopt the resolution determining revenues and millages levied for fiscal year 2019. This resolution by the School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida, levies a total millage rate of 6.572 mils which raises $1,264,966,687 in local revenue. The millage is comprised of 4.062 mills for required local effort, 0.012 mills for prior period required local effort adjustment, .748 mills for discretionary operating, 1.5 mills for local capital improvement, and 0.2500 mills for additional operating funds. This millage is 1.43% more than the rolled back rate.

DESCRIPTION:

Chapter 200.065(2)(f)3 of the Florida Statutes requires that the School District hold a public hearing to adopt a millage rate within 80 days of certification of value, but not earlier than 65 days after certification. The total millage rate being levied is 6.5720 mills which raises $1,264,966,687 in local revenues.
