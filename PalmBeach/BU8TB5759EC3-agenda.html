1. Call To Order

Superintendent Opens Meeting
Meeting
Nov 17, 2020 - 1. SPECIAL MEETING FOR BOARD REORGANIZATION at 4 p.m. in the Board Room
Category
1. Call To Order
Type
Information, Procedural

Superintendent Dr. Donald Fennoy will preside over this meeting as Secretary of the School Board as stipulated in Florida Statute.  Dr. Fennoy will present the newly-elected and re-elected School Board Members, introduce the Oath Ceremony, and preside over the organizational portion of the meeting, which includes the selection of the School Board Chair and Vice Chair.  After which, he will turn the meeting over to the School Board Chair.

- Close nominations

 

- Vote on nominations

 

 

Roll call voting will take place per School Board Policy 1.03.  Per Robert's Rules, in the event a single candidate does not receive four or more votes, the balloting will continue as many times as necessary until one obtains a majority vote.  A nomination is not removed unless a candidate voluntarily withdraws his or her name.
