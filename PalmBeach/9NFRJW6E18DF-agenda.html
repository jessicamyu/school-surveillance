Recommended Action
I recommend the School Board adopt the Resolution determining revenues and millages levied for FY15.

DESCRIPTION: 

Chapter 200.065(2)(f)3 of the Florida Statutes requires that the school district hold a public hearing to adopt a millage rate within 80 days of certification of value, but not earlier than 65 days after certification.

The total millage rate 7.594 mills which raises $1,094,286,905 in local tax revenues for FY15.
