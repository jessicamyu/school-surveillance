Motion & Voting

I recommend the Board adopt the proposed millage levies of 6.8750 mills for fiscal year 2022. The total millage rate is 2.52 percent greater than the roll back rate and is made up of 3.6210 mills for Required Local Effort, 0.0060 mills for Prior Period RLE Adjustment, 0.748 mills for Discretionary Operating Funds, 1.500 mills for Local Capital Improvement, and 1.000 mills for Additional Operating Funds.

Motion by Erica Whitfield, second by Debra Robinson.
Final Resolution: Motion Passes
Yea: Marcia Andrews, Frank Barbieri, Karen Brill, Barbara McQuinn, Debra Robinson, Erica Whitfield
