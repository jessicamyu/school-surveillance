Subject
Royal Palm School Drainage Interlocal Agreement for Lawrence Road
Meeting
Nov 11, 2009 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Operations
Category
Consent
Type
Action (Consent)


  • The School District of Palm Beach County has committed to modernize the existing Royal Palm School in order to provide a more up-to-date and comprehensive facility to meet the special needs of its students.


 





  • The modernization of the school facility requires that improvements be made to the adjacent Lawrence Road as well as modifications to a portion of the school’s existing drainage system.  This modification to the drainage system will provide legal positive outfall for surface water from a segment of Lawrence Road.


 







  •  The drainage pipe crosses School District property and discharges into an existing retention area.  In the absence of a drainage easement, the County wants to be assured that the School District will allow the uninterrupted flow of storm water discharge from the road through the District’s drainage system.  Additionally, the County wants a guarantee that the School District will not make any changes or modifications to the portion of the drainage system used for road drainage without prior written approval from the County.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve the Interlocal Agreement (ILA) between Palm Beach County and the School Board of Palm Beach County pertaining to the drainage of a portion of Lawrence Road adjacent to the Royal Palm School facility and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to sign all necessary documents.
