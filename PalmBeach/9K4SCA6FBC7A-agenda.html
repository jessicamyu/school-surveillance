Subject
RE 1 Palm Beach Lakes High School Turning Easement
Meeting
Jan 14, 2009 - Spec Mtgs and Wkshops re Operations
Category
Consent
Type
Action


  • Palm Beach Lakes High School's campus is being expanded to accommodate a new addition and practicing field.


  • An existing Turning Easement containing 1,330 SF located on the north end of the new practice field needs to have the legal description corrected to reflect the actual land area in use.


  • This reconfiguration of the Turning Easement will contain 1,728 SF and provide a more functional design for the new practice field.


  • The additional area covered by the new Turning Easement has a diminumus value but will provide a mutual benefit to both parties.


  • The amended easement also deletes a Utility Easement in favor of the Board and a drainage in favor of Sorrento.


  • By amending and restating the rights and obligations of the existing easement the School Board will not be required to indemnify Sorrento Buildings, LTD. nor carry liability insurance.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve the Amended And Restated Reciprocal Grants of Easement between Sorrento Buildings LTD. and the School Board of Palm Beach County for a perpetual non-exclusive turning easement; and authorize the Superintendent and Chairman to sign all necessary documents.

Contact:

Joseph M. Moore (moorej@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)



Joseph M. Sanches (sanchesj@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)

Subject
PL2 - Agreement with the City of West Palm Beach for the expansion of Palm Beach Lakes High School's campus
Meeting
Jan 14, 2009 - Spec Mtgs and Wkshops re Operations
Category
Consent
Type
Action (Consent)


  • In accordance with the approved Capital Plan, Palm Beach Lakes High School’s campus has to be expanded to accommodate the new academy and addition.


 





  • The School Board has contracted to acquire real property owned by U.S. Truss, Inc. located north of the School Property to accommodate a new practice field at the School.


 





  • The property is encumbered by a Unity of Title agreement that requires the Mayor of the City to execute a document to release.


 





  • The School Board has requested that the City release the Unity of Title, which the City has agreed to do providing the School Board takes certain actions following the acquisition of the property.


 





  • These actions include the filing of an application for a comprehensive land use amendment, rezoning and major amendment to the planned development to include the newly acquired property.


 







  •  The Agreement sets out the conditions and time frames for filing the application.


 







  • There shall be no fees charged to the School Board for filing the application.


 



 

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve the Agreement between the School Board of Palm Beach County and the City of West Palm Beach regarding the expansion of Palm Beach Lakes High School’s campus.

moorej@palmbeach.k12.fl.us; sanchesj@palmbeach.k12.fl.us

No cost.

PL1 - Interlocal Agreement between the Greater Boca Raton Beach and Park District and the School Board of PBC for fudning of Athletic Field Lighting at Boca Raton Middle School.
Meeting
Jan 14, 2009 - Spec Mtgs and Wkshops re Operations
Category
New Business
Type
Action (Consent)


  • Boca Raton Middle schools has recreational amenities inclusive of Athletic Field Facilities, which are not lit.


 





  • The Greater Boca Raton Beach and Park District (District) has agreed to reimburse the School Board for the actual costs of construction of the lighting of the Athletic Field facilities estimated at $250,000.


 





  • The District will reimburse the School Board for the actual costs of maintenance of the Athletic Field Facilities lighting, including normal repairs.


 





  • The District will pay all power bills associated with the Athletic Field Facilities’ lighting.


 





  • The School Board will plan, design and construct the Athletic Field Facilities improvements.


 





  • The School Board agrees that the District, its residents and the District’s approved uses groups shall have exclusive uses of the Athletic Fields after 6:00 P.M. on weekdays.


 





  • During the holidays, the School and the District shall mutually agree on a schedule of use, with the general intent that the District shall have priority use during weekends and holidays and the School shall have use of the Athletic Field Facilities for special School activities and programs. 


 

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve the Interlocal Agreement between the Greater Boca Raton Beach and Park District and the School Board of Palm Beach County for funding of the Boca Raton Middle School Athletic Field Lighting Facilities.

Contact:

moorej@palmbeach.k12.fl.us; sanchesj@palmbeach.k12.fl.us

Financial Impact:

District to reimburse School Board for improvements to Athletic Fields estimated at $250,000.
