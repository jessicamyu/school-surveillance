Subject
CLS2 - 2010-2011 Differentiated Accountability
Meeting
Jul 28, 2010 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Academics
Category
New Business
Type
Action (Consent)

This is a workshop designed to inform the Board about the State Differentiated Accountability System.  Differentiated Accountability (DA) is Florida's accountability system for public schools.  Based on school grade and AYP criteria met, schools are placed into the appropriate DA category.  Each category has requirements for the District and schools to follow for state accountability.  Various levels of support are provided to DA schools from both the District and the State.



Dr. Deedara Hicks is the State Regional Executive Director for Palm Beach County.  Her team will provide guidance, monitoring, and academic support throughout the year for DA schools, with a particular focus on the needs of the schools in the most intensive categories.
