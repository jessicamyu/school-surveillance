CLS1 - Response to Intervention (RtI) Workshop
Meeting
Feb 3, 2010 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Academics
Category
Consent
Type
Action (Consent)

Response to Intervention (RtI) is a Federal and State mandate whereby scientific research-based interventions are provided to students in the general education environment.



 



Palm Beach County has been implementing behavioral interventions pursuant to RtI since August 2007 and is now beginning to train all schools on Positive Behavior Intervention Supports (PBIS). Palm Beach County began implementing academic interventions pursuant to RtI in August 2008. 



 



When academic and/or behavioral interventions are successful, it alleviates the need to identify students as having a Specific Learning Disability, Emotional/Behavioral Disability or Language Impairment.
