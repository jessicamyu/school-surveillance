Energy Conservation, Recycling & Sustainability Initiatives
Meeting
Oct 31, 2012 - Workshop 10-31-12
Category
Workshop Item(s)
Type
Action (Consent)

This workshop will highlight the School District of Palm Beach County's programs, initiatives and successess in reducing energy consumption, increasing recycling and saving money.  It will also address some next steps the District can take to increase our efforts towards becoming a more sustainable school district.



Some highlights include:





  • $11 million savings in electric costs from FY08 to FY12

  • Significant savings through utility bill auditing

  • Leadership in Energy & Environmental Design (LEED) Schools

  • Energy Conservation Incentive Program for Schools

  • 100% of schools recycling cardboard and batteries

  • Nearly 6,000 tons of paper and cardboard recycled in FY11

  • $1.5 million in waste reduction and recycling
