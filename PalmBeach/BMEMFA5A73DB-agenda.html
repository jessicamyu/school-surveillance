I recommend the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp approve the slate of officers.

DESCRIPTION:

The Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp is a Florida not-for-profit corporation that has as its sole member the School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida.  The corporation facilitates the School District’s lease purchase financing of school facilities, commonly referred to as COPs (or Certificates of Participation).  

 

A summary of the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp is attached.

 

The bylaws of the Leasing Corp require an annual meeting of the Board of Directors to elect corporate officers.  Palm Beach School Board Members or School District Staff may be elected to serve as officers.

Recommended Action
I recommend the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp authorize the issuance of COP series 2020A.

DESCRIPTION:

  • The 2020A COP issue is a long-term borrowing which will generate up to $155 million for the construction of projects approved in the FY 2020 Capital Plan.
    • Verde K-8 Modernization
    • Addison Mizner K-8 Modernization
    • Washington ES Modernization
    • Plumosa 6-8 Addition
    • Citrus Cove ES Core Addition
       
  • Prior debt issues were structured with payments over 25 years.  As all the existing debt matures by 2032, there is capacity to shorten the life of this and the debt issues projected in the capital plan.  This debt issue is planned to mature in 14 years or 2034.  This will significantly reduce the total interest that will be paid and increase borrowing flexibility in the future.  
     
  • The Finance Committee reviewed the transaction at their meeting on December 6, 2019.
     
  • The School Board will also consider this item on March 18, 2020 Agenda item FMT1.
