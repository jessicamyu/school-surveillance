Subject
Reallocation of Capital Class Size Reduction Funds
Meeting
Jan 28, 2009 - Spec Mtg re Reallocation of Capt CSR Funds
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Action


  1. The Department of Education requires that all capital class size reduction funds be encumbered within 31 months.  Unencumbered funds will revert to the State of Florida to be reallocated to other projects.


  2. DOE requires School Board approval of any reallocations of these funds before they will authorize any adjustments.


  3. Most of the Class Size Reduction projects are nearing completion.  As purchase orders are closed, funds become available but will not be eligible for reallocation once the 31 month period has ended.


  4. To prevent the possible reversion of the funds, this action authorizes the reallocation of all remaining funds in all the CSR projects to the Northboro Elementary Modernization project. 


  5. An equal amount of local funding will be transferred from the Northboro project so there will be no change in that project budget.


  6. This action will allow all the remaining CSR funds to be immediately encumbered and expended in the most expeditious manner.


  7. As the CSR projects are completed, any unused funds will be transferred to capital contingency.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve revised encumbrance authorizations for the Class Size Reduction funds and authorize the Superintendent and the School Board Chairman to execute all necessary documents to access State funding.

Contact:

moorej@palmbeach.k12.fl.us; sanchesj@palmbeach.k12.fl.us; evansl@palmbeach.k12.fl.us

Financial Impact:

None.  This action reallocates the residual balance of Class Size Reduction funds between projects.  If not approved, the monies will revert to the State of Florida.
