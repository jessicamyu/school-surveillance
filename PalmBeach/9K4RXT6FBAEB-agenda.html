PM1 West Central Communities ES (03-W) Project Modifications
Meeting
Feb 27, 2008 - Spec Mtg re Construction Projects
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Action

This project modification contains the following item:







  • Additional Architectural Services in the amount of $218,000.00




  • Description: Design school to meet USGBC LEEDS (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design) Silver Certification requirements.




  • Cause:  School District Requested




  • Justification:  Some of the benefits of LEED "Green" designs are sustainable sites/buildings, water savings, energy efficiency, and better indoor environmental quality. 

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve Project Modifications in the amount of $218,000.00 to Song & Associates for West Central Communities Elementary School (03-W), New School Project #0061-8363, and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to execute all related contract documents.

Contact:

Joseph M. Moore (moorej@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)
Joseph M. Sanches

Financial Impact:

The financial impact to the Project's Budget is $218,000.00.
 
The source of funds is the Project Budget in the FY08-FY12 Five-Year Plan.
