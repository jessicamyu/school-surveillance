Meeting

Category

Type
Action

The Superintendent made a recommendation that Rebecca Sorenson be suspended for ten (10) days without pay for failure to report child abuse, failure to safeguard students, and misconduct in office in violation of School District Policies, Florida Administrative Code Rules, and School Board Bulletins.



Ms. Sorenson timely asserted her right to an appeal before the Division of Administrative Hearings (DOAH).



On September 9, 2009, an Administrative Hearing was held before Judge Claude Arrington regarding the ten (10) day suspension without pay of Rebecca Sorenson.



On November 18, 2009, Judge Arrington issued a Recommended Order that the Board enter a Final Order suspending Rebecca Sorenson for ten (10) days without pay.

This Final Order is in the best interest of the School Board.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Final Order in the matter of School Board of Palm Beach County v. Rebecca Sorenson, DOAH case No. 09-2749, a copy of which is attached hereto, and authorize the execution of all related documents.
