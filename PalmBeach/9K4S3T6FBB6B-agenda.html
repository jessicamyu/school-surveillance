Acceptance of the Grant for $536,609 from the U.S. Department of Justice for Youth Gang Violence Prevention
Meeting
Oct 22, 2008 - Workshops and Spec Mtgs re Operations
Category
Consent
Type
Discussion

Gangs are a growing problem in Palm Beach County and surrounding counties.



Most gang strategies are reactionary.



The School District Police Department believes that true gang prevention requires intelligence gathering and communication so that gang activity may be prevented.



This grant provides the software and personnel to allow all police officers in Palm Beach County to input and share information that would allow a proactive approach to gang prevention and intervention.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board accept the grant funds for $536,609 for youth gang violence prevention.

Contact:

Joseph M. Moore, James P. Kelly (e-mail: Jim Kelly (Chief of Police))

Financial Impact:

There is no financial impact to the School District.
