I recommend the Board approve the application of Connections Education Center of the Palm Beaches for any and/or all of the reasons stated by the evaluation instruments and the overall assessment, and authorize the Superintendent and/or his designee to sign all related correspondence and documents.

DESCRIPTION:

On August 3, 2015, the Department of Charter Schools received an application from Connections Education Center of the Palm Beaches (CECPB), Inc. to open Connection Education Center of the Palm Beaches charter school in the School District of Palm Beach County in August, 2016. 

 

A review of the application was conducted using the required Florida Charter School Application Evaluation Instrument.

 

Connections Education Center of the Palm Beaches meets “Meets the Standard” in 18 of 19 sections.

 

One section received a rating of “Partially Meets the Standard:”

  • Section 17 - Budget

Projected Enrollment

 

          First Year                PreK-3          45 Projected Enrollment

          Second Year           PreK-6          55 Projected Enrollment

          Third Year               PreK-6          65 Projected Enrollment

          Fourth Year            PreK-8          75 Projected Enrollment

          Fifth Year                PreK-8          85 Projected Enrollment

 

Education Program Design

The Educational Model is implemented within a framework of continuous improvement to ensure its validity and the development of supporting tools to meet the needs of each individual student.  The development of this Educational Model enables administrators and teachers to better understand, better explain, and make better predictions regarding the elements needed to facilitate each student’s mastery of the Florida Standards and the Florida Standards Access Points.  Educational best practices, technology, communication, and documentation tools are created and revised to support the implementation of the model and ensure that teachers have the support necessary to meet the needs of each student. 

 

Location

CECPB will be renting space from the Jewish Children and Families Services which is located in the center of Palm Beach County just west of the Palm Beach International Airport.  The facility was previously rented by Renaissance Learning Center, another Charter School for children with Autism.  The facility layout will accommodate a logical separation of the elementary (K-5) and the middle (6-8) school grades.

 

Applicant’s Founding Governing Board

The Governing Board members consist of an attorney certified in the areas of business litigation as well as civil trial; a doctor well known through the Autism community for her education and knowledge; and a family of well known philanthropists very committed to many causes include Autism Spectrum Disorder and National Association of Mental Illness.

 

Ratings of Connections Education Center of the Palm Beaches Charter School Application

Per the Overall Assessment Summary/Checklist, the ratings indicated that their application did meet the statutory requirement set forth in F.S. 1002.33(6) in 18 of 19 sections.  (See Overall Assessment/Checklist)

 

“If an application is denied, the sponsor shall, within 10 calendar days after such denial, articulate in writing the specific reasons, based upon good cause, supporting its denial of the charter application and shall provide the letter of denial and supporting documentation to the applicant and to the Department of Education.”
