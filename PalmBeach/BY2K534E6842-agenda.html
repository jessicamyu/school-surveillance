I recommend the Board approve the Memorandum of Agreement, effective March 25, 2021, through September 30, 2021, with Prevention Partnerships for Children, Inc.; and authorize the Superintendent or his designee to sign all related documents and contracts.

DESCRIPTION:

This Memorandum of Agreement with Prevention Partnerships for Children, Inc. provides $17,630.00 to the Department of Early Childhood Education for the replacement of computer equipment assigned to specialists funded by the Strong Minds Network Technical Assistance (TA) Grant. These TA grant-funded specialists provide on-site coaching and support to private child care centers and school-based programs that participate in the countywide Strong Minds Network, which was initiated and funded by the Children’s Services Council.

The District is both a partner and participant in the Strong Minds Network, and the TA Specialists provide supports in the areas of teacher-child interactions (Classroom Assessment Scoring System); assessment and documentation (Teaching Strategies GOLD); curriculum and instruction; behavior management; accreditation; and foundational practices.
