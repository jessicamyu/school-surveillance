DESCRIPTION: 

On December 17, 2014, the School Board approved the Master Agreement between the School Board of Palm Beach County and the Metropolitan Center for Urban Education (NYU) for the purchase of Education Equity Audit Services. 

 

NYU has conducted a combination of data collection methods and analysis techniques to examine the extent to which the District's culture, practices, and policies promote equitable opportunities for all students and staff members.  The final report will be presented and their findings which will include root causes and contributing factors to any identified disparities found and suggested remedies to correct those disparities.
