Pursuant to Florida Statutes §194.015, the membership of the county value adjustment board shall include a citizen member who “must be appointed by the school board and must own a business occupying commercial space located within the school district.”  As the owner of a business established within Palm Beach County, Mr. Norem meets these statutory requirements.  This appointment is retroactive to September 1, 2008.

Recommendation:

I recommend the Board approve the appointment of Stormet Norem as the Palm Beach County School Board representative to the Palm Beach County Value Adjustment Board.

Gerald A. Williams, Esq.; Chief Counsel williamsg@palmbeach.k12.fl.us

None
