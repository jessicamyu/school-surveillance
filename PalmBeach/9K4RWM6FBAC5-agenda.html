Special Meeting re: PBCSB v. Jack Williams, et al Settlement

Feb 13, 2008 - Spec Mtg re Williams
Category
Consent Agenda

Discussion


  • This eminent domain suit was filed to acquire a parcel necessary for the New Suncoast HS in Riviera Beach.


  • The 1.000 acre parcel is improved with a church and day-care facility (See Location Map).


  • The School Board has already taken title to the parcel via the "Quick Take" process provided for under the Florida Statutes.


  • Settlement is recommended as being in the best financial interest of the District due to the anticipated costs of the litigation and litigation risk inherent in such an eminent domain proceeding.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Stipulated Final Judgment and Instructions to the Clerk of the Court between Jack Williams and the School Board of Palm Beach County in the case of School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida v. Jack Williams regarding the acquisition of certain real property consisting of 1.000 acres, more or less and authorize the School Board attorney to execute all related documents.
