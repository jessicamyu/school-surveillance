Action

 

 




     
  •  
    The Superintendent made a recommendation that Ms. Gadson be suspended and terminated due to insubordination as it related to her attendance at Boca High School where she was a part-time custodian. 



    •  
  •  
    Ms. Gadson timely asserted her right to an appeal before the Division of Administrative Hearings (DOAH).



    •  
  •  
    On February 27, 2009, an Administrative Hearing was held before Judge Stuart Lerner regarding the termination of employment of Ms. Gadson.



    •  
  •  
    On April 13, 2009, Judge Lerner issued a Recommended Order that the Board enter a Final Order upholding the Superintendent’s recommendation for termination.



    •  
  •  
    This Final Order is in the best interest of the School Board.

 

 

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Final Order in the matter of School Board of Palm Beach County v. Karen Gadson a copy of which is attached hereto, and authorize the execution of all related documents.
