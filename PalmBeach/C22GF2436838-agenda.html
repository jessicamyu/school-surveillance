DESCRIPTION:

The Chief of Staff's Office will present a summary of findings from the Phase 1 Feedback Survey, which closed on April 9, 2021. A summary of feedback collected during Board Member-led Community Meetings conducted in late April will also be reviewed during the May 5 Board Workshop. These findings will be shared with the Strategic Plan Steering Committee to inform upcoming drafts of proposed goals and associated measures for the next strategic plan. A draft of proposed goals will be shared with the School Board and Members of the public on May 26, 2021.

DESCRIPTION:

This Budget Workshop will provide an overview of the state education revenue forecast and District FY22 budget development process, along with an update on Federal ESSER funds including the American Rescue Plan. With the Legislature in session and budget deliberations evolving daily, this presentation may be updated prior to the workshop.  This workshop is part of a series of Budget Workshops and Budget Advisory Committee meetings leading up to budget adoption September 14, 2021.
