PLEASE NOTE THAT THIS WORKSHOP IS BEING MOVED TO DECEMBER 9, 2020.

 

 

The School District's current strategic plan is set to sunset in Summer 2021, so efforts to begin developing the next strategic plan must begin shortly. The goal for this workshop will be to provide the School Board and community with an overview/timeline for strategic plan creation as well as proposed plans for refreshing the School District's mission and vision. Please note this will be the first of several workshops focused on the new strategic plan. PDF of presentation forthcoming.
