Recommended Action
I recommend the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp approve a resolution authorizing the issuance of certificates of participation series 2015C to refund all or a portion of COP series 2011A.

DESCRIPTION:

  • The School District, in conjunction with the Finance Committee, actively manages the debt of the District, always seeking to reduce capital costs and manage risk.
  • Due to continued low interest rates in the current market, the District has the opportunity to refund, or refinance, COP Series 2011A and obtain savings for the District which will reduce future debt service payments.
  • The 2011A is scheduled to revert to variable rate in August 2016 if no action is taken.
  • Based on the recommendation of the Finance Committee, the District would refund the certificates, locking in fixed rates until maturity, when the net present value savings is at least 5%, including all asociated fees. 
  • This transaction would result in net present value savings of more than $3 million and eliminate future interest rate risk
  • On November 14, 2014, the Finance Committee unanimously recommended the District move forward with the transaction as soon as possible.
