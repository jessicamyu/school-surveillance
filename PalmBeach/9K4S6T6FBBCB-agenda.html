Subject
Special Meeting re: Edwards Angell Contract
Meeting
Dec 2, 2009 - Legal Sessions
Category
Special Meetings
Type
Action

Legal Advisor shall provide legal services associated with acquisition by eminent domain of properties required for the New Suncoast High School as assigned Legal Advisor by the Chief Legal Counsel from time to time, including, but not limited to preparation and filing of pleadings, attendance at hearings, mediation, trial and appeals. 



 



This contract will run through June 30, 2010.



 

Acquisition of the property necessary New Suncoast High School has been underway for the last several years.  Final valuation of several parcels previously acquired through the “quick take” process is still pending, as is a claim for damages associated with an alleged breach of a settlement agreement.  Legal advisor is counsel of record in these pending proceedings and is intimately familiar with the nuances of the issues that are outstanding.   Additionally in order to complete the roadway improvements along W. 13th Street and Congress Avenue, additional land interests will need to be acquired and may require the use of eminent domain.  Staff counsel will continue to handle the majority of the day to day work associated with these matters, however when complex issues arise and/or workload dictates, Legal Advisor’s services will be used.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the Board amend the contract with Edwards Angell, Palmer & Dodge, LLP and authorize the Superintendent and Board Chairman to sign all necessary documents.
