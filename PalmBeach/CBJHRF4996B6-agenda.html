I recommend that the School Board approve the Program Renewal for Palm Beach Preparatory Academy aka Northstar and authorize the Superintendent and/or his designee to sign all related contracts and documents .

DESCRIPTION:

On May 19, 2021, Palm Beach Preparatory Charter Academy a.k.a. Northstar, was granted a one (1) year contract extension in order to allow for a comprehensive renewal review that was unable to be conducted due to the COVID-19 pandemic.  

 

Pursuant to F.S. 1002.33(7)(b)1, Palm Beach Preparatory Charter Academy a.k.a. Northstar, meets the criteria for renewal and does not fall under any grounds for termination.

Recommended Action
I recommend the Board Approve the First Amendment and Renewal of RFP 17C-020W for Program Management Support Services through June 21, 2023, and authorize the Superintendent and Board Chair to finalize and sign all necessary documents.

DESCRIPTION:

First Amendment and Renewal of RFP 17C-020W for Program Management Support Services to AECOM Technical Services, Inc.

 

The framework of the First Amendment was reviewed by the CORC Committee with no objections.

 

AECOM Technical Services, Inc. will continue to serve as the Program Manager to act on behalf of the Board for the delivery of a Capital Improvement Program, which may be composed of new schools, school replacements, new additions, existing building modifications, renovations, ancillary facility modification or renovations, facility expansions or consolidation, capital renewal, and technology improvements, as well as other capital needs. This First Amendment and Renewal run through June 21, 2023.

 

Due to District staffing shortages in conjunction with high volumes of projects, AECOM Technical Services, Inc. will also provide the District additional project-specific support staffing in accordance with rates defined in attached Schedule C-1 to supplement the administrative team’s design and construction efforts.  There are limitations embedded in the contract language to control the project-specific fund source, amount and duration of services, as well as the exclusion of these services from final reconciliation tabulations.

 

AECOM Technical Services, Inc. will continue to serve as the Program Manager to act on the Board's behalf for the delivery of a Capital Improvement Program, which may be composed of new schools, school replacements, new additions, existing building modifications, renovations, ancillary facility modification or renovations, facility expansions or consolidation, capital renewal, and technology improvements as well as other capital needs.

 

CONTACTS:

Joseph Sanches, Chief Operations Officer (joseph.sanches@palmbeachschools.org)

Heather Frederick, CPA, Chief Financial Officer (heather.frederick@palmbeachschools.org)

David Dolan, Deputy Chief of Facilities Management, (david.dolan@palmbeachschools.org)

 

FINANCIAL IMPACT:

The financial impact to the District budget for based Program Manager Services is not to exceed 2.2 percent of the various project cost assigned to the Program Manager. The financial impact for support-staffing services in the First Amendment is already allocated within the various individual project budgets established by District staff, with a not-to-exceed value of $3,000,000.  The sources of funds are the Sales Tax Referendum Proceeds and the Capital Projects budget.
