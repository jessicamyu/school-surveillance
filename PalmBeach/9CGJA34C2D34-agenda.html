2. Board Discussion - ROBINSON - Changes to Policy 7.18
Meeting
Oct 23, 2013 - 2. WORKSHOP at 2 p.m. at Gove Elementary School
Category
3. Workshop Items
Type
Discussion

Dr. Robinson would like the Board to discuss changes to Policy 7.18 (Community Use of School Facilities) such that we partner with nonprofit organizations that provide high quality support programs to children and families in our high needs schools.

 

Through this partnership, leasing fees would be waived (or possibly a sliding scale based on percent of children on FRL that participate in the program).  

 

This would require that we:

1.  Identify criteria for determining which schools are high needs.

2.  Identify criteria for determining which programs are high quality.

 

This would have a negative impact on our budget but would help us to achieve our primary mission.

 

The School District of Palm Beach County is committed to providing a world-class education with excellence and equity to empower each student to reach his or her highest potential with the most effective staff to foster the knowledge, skills, and ethics required for responsible citizenship and productive careers.
