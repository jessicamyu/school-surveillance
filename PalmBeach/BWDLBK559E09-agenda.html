Recommended Action
I recommend the Board approve the five (5) year Initial Charter with Covenant Arts Academy Charter, Inc., and authorize the Superintendent and/or his designee to sign all related contracts and documents.

DESCRIPTION:

Pursuant to Florida Statute 1002.33(7), the terms and conditions for the operation of the charter school shall be set forth by the School District and the applicant in a written contractual agreement called a charter.

 

The Department of Charter Schools received an application from Covenant Arts Academy Charter, Inc. in March 2018. The application for Covenant Arts Academy Charter, Inc. was approved on October 8, 2018, as Item CS5 during a Special Board Meeting.

 

The Charter Contract for this charter school was reviewed and agreed upon by the appropriate District staff and negotiated by the General Counsel’s office.

 

The charter will become effective upon signing by both parties, and shall cover a term of five (5) years, commencing on July 1, 2020 and ending on June 30, 2025, except as provided in the charter.

Discussion

School Board Member Marcia Andrews will discuss the following:  

 

Glades Career Readiness Roundtable is requesting the School District act as the fiscal agent for West Technical Education Center Legislative  Appropriation Request for $426,857 for the continuation of the Adult Commercial Vehicle Driving Program (CDL). The program was established with $250.000 from the Governor's Contingency funds for the school year 2019-2020. The funding would upgrade the technological infrastructure which is needed in the era of virtual learning, instructional personnel, and to purchase needed programmatic equipment such as tractors and trailers. There have been 30 graduates from the Glades Region who received their Commercial Driver's License.
