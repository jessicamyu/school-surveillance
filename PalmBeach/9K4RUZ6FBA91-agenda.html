•            The 2006-2010 Five Year Plan includes the modernization of Indian Ridge Learning Center.



 



•            Due to campus safety and security concerns, the School Administration wished to close off the platted easement for all use except school related traffic.



 



•            During the modernization, some of the structures were constructed over a platted easement.



 



•            Upon notification by the adjacent owners who were entitled to use the easement, attempts were made to acquire the easement interest.



 



•            Staff was unable to reach an agreement with the owners.



 



•            The owners have insisted that acquisition of title to an 11,713 sf out-parcel by the School Board be included in any settlement of this dispute. (See Exhibit “A”)



 



•            Staff believes that the use of eminent domain is the best way to resolve all of the outstanding issues amongst the parties



 

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve a Resolution authorizing the acquisition of certain real property consisting of third-party rights to use a 50’ Ingress-Egress Easement (60,810 sf, more or less) and a 11,713 sf out-parcel located at the southwest corner of Okeechobee and Golden Lakes Boulevard, through eminent domain proceedings for Indian Ridge Learning Center; authorize the Legal Department to hire an eminent domain attorney; and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to execute all related documents.

Contact:

Joseph M. Moore (moorej@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)
Joseph M. Sanches

Financial Impact:

The financial impact to the Capital Project's Fund Budget is not to exceed $700,000.00
