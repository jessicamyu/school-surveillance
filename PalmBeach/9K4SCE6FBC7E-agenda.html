Renewal of Florida Virtual School Franchise Agreement
Meeting
May 13, 2009 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Academics
Category
Consent
Type
Action

Florida Virtual School (FLVS) is an established leader in developing and providing virtual K-12 education solutions to Florida students, using a nationally recognized e-learning model.



 



In response to the growth in online education, Florida Virtual School developed the FLVS franchise program. As a franchise, Districts take advantage of the courses and learning management system developed by Florida Virtual School and implements the successful instructional model already in use, using local educators.



In May, 2008, the Board approved the District's continued participation in the FLVS franchise program for FY09. The franchise is implemented through the District's virtual school, Palm Beach Virtual.



Palm Beach Virtual is used for students who need flexibility in their scheduling, to take courses for graduation, acceleration, grade forgiveness or to raise their GPA. They are enrolled in the virtual program after consultation with and approval by their school and the virtual school  guidance counselors and their parent/guardian.



The renewal of the FLVS franchise for FY10 will allow the District to continue the franchise and broaden the opportunities for students taking virtual education courses through Palm Beach Virtual.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the purchase of a Florida Virtual School Franchise License effective from July 1, 2009 through June 30, 2010 and authorize the Superintendent or designee to sign all related documents.

$50.00 per half-credit enrollment with no minimum number of enrollments



The total contract value will include only the per half-credit enrollment fees and will be based on all enrollments outside the 28 day Grace Period.



Payment of the contract will be quarterly with payment for enrollments served as of October 31, February 28, April 30 and June 30.
