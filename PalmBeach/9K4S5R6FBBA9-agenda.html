Special Meeting re: Angela Jones Settlement
Meeting
Jul 29, 2009 - Legal Sessions
Category
Special Meetings
Type
Action (Consent)


On December 10, 2008, the Superintendent recommended a 5-day suspension without pay for employee Angela Jones who is a data processor at Independence Middle School.  After reaching a settlement with Ms. Jones, it is recommended that the 5-day suspension without pay be rescinded.  This settlement is in the best interest of the District.  The attached Settlement Agreement is subject to School Board approval.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the attached Settlement Agreement & Release in the matter of Angela Jones v. Palm Beach County School District, a copy of which is attached hereto, and authorize the execution of all related documents.
