Recommended Action
I recommend the Board adopt the Resolution determining revenues and millages levied for FY 2017.

DESCRIPTION:

Chapter 200.065(2)(f)3 of the Florida Statutes requires that the School District hold a public hearing to adopt a millage rate within 80 days of certification of value, but not earlier than 65 days after certification. The total millage rate being levied is 7.0700 mills which raises $1,212,288,445 in local revenues.  

 

CONTACT:

Michael J. Burke, Chief Financial Officer (Mike.Burke@palmbeachschools.org)

Heather Knust, C.P.A., Budget Director (Heather.Knust@palmbeachschools.org)

 

FINANCIAL IMPACT:

The financial impact to the District is $1,212,288,445 in local tax revenues for FY 2017.
