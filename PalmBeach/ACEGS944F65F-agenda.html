DESCRIPTION:

This workshop will provide the Board with an overview of current student performance status and achievement gaps between subgroups on the Florida Standards Assessment (FSA) English Language Arts (ELA) assessment, the alignment of school improvement plans to the strategic plan as well as opportunities for improvement.  In addition, the results of elementary, middle school master board scheduling practices that was completed between December and June will be shared.
