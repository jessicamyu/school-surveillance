Subject
Interlocal Agreement between the Board of County Commissioners of Palm Beach County and the School Board of Palm Beach County for Roadway Improvements to Accommodate New Elementary School 06 D.
Meeting
Oct 29, 2008 - Workshops and Spec Mtgs re Operations
Category
Consent
Type
Action


  • This is an Agreement for funding assistance and right of way acquisition if necessary from Palm Beach County for the improvement of Stacy Street.


 





  • The improvements will provide access to 06 D elementary school.


 





  • The County agreed to contribute up to $2.5 million for roadway, sidewalk, traffic signal and drainage improvements to Stacy Street.


 





  • The County will supervise and administer construction including disbursement of funds for the Project.


 





  • The School Board will fund the construction of the right turn lane at the north approach at Haverhill Road and Stacy Street at an estimated cost of $151,000.


 





  • The School Board will fund, design and construct a storm water retention area to collect the Project storm water and to provide approximately 575 lineal feet of exfiltration trench and pipe size increase at an estimated cost of $131,260.00.


 



·    The School Board will provide approximately 2.24 acres for the detention area to accommodate the storm water for the school as well as up to 1950 lineal feet of the Stacy Street roadway system.



 



·    The Board of County Commissioners approved the subject Agreement at its October 21, 2008 meeting.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve the Interlocal Agreement between the Board of County Commissioners of Palm Beach County and the School Board of Palm Beach County for improvements to Haverhill Road and Stacy Street to accommodate new elementary school 06D.

moorej@palmbeach.k12.fl.us; sanchesj@palmbeach.k12.fl.us

Financial Impact:

Up to $2.5 million from Palm Beach County

An estimated $282,260 from School’s Capital Budget

P1 Investment Advisor to the School District
Meeting
Oct 29, 2008 - Workshops and Spec Mtgs re Operations
Category
Consent
Type
Action (Consent)
P1 This contract is for the Investment Advisor to the School District.  The purpose of this contract is to advise staff regarding the management of the District's investments.  PFM Asset Management manages specific securities purchased, provides advice on investments in money market funds or investment pools, assists with any investment matter such as banking contracts, provides daily reports regarding the performance of the investments, and reports performance to the Finance Committee on a quarterly basis.  The volume of investments and the interaction with staff has increased dramatically since November 2007 when the District stopped using the SBA Local Government Investment Pool for short term investments.  Given the instability of the financial markets and the superior services received over the term of the last contract, this contract was negotiated with the assistance of the Finance Committee.

Recommendation:

I recommend award of the Investment Advisor Contract with PFM Asset Management and authorize the Superintendent and Board Chairman to sign all necessary doucments.

Contact:

Joseph Moore, MooreJ@palmbeach.k12.fl.us



Leanne Evans, Treasurer

Financial Impact:

The financial impact is estimated at $330,000 per year based on the investment activity of the last year or $990,000 over three years.  Roughly $160,000 is for the general fund and $170,000 is for the capital funds.  The actual fees assessed will be based on the amount of funds under management.
