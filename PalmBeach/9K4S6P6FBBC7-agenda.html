Action




  • Jerold Self is employed by the School District of Palm Beach County as a teacher at Independence Middle School.




  • The Superintendent recommends to amend Mr. Self’s fifteen (15) day suspension that was approved by the School Board on July 22, 2009.




  • The amendment is a recommendation for a five (5) work day unpaid suspension with a ten (10) work day unpaid suspension held in abeyance pending completion of workplace therapy as determined and administered by Corporate Care Works. 




  • This Settlement is in the best interest of the District.


  • The attached Settlement Agreement is subject to School Board approval.

Recommendation:



I recommend that the School Board approve the attached Settlement Agreement & Release in the matter of  Palm Beach County School Board v. Jeold Self, a copy of which is attached hereto, and authorize the execution of all related documents.
