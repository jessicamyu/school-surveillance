WITHDRAWN - 2.a. Board Discussion - ROBINSON - Pro-Rate Leasing Fees - Old Plumosa - WITHDRAWN
Meeting

Category
3. Workshop Items
Type
Discussion

THIS ITEM HAS BEEN WITHDRAWN BY DR. ROBINSON.

 

Dr. Robinson will discuss directing the Superintendent to pro-rate the leasing fees for a church that wishes to park in the parking lot of old Plumosa for less than 3 hours each Sunday morning.  We lease other spaces by the hour, but I am being told that the lease, in this case, is by the day and can not be calculated for a portion of the day without board action. This small church cannot afford to pay for parking space-time that it is not using.

 

Additionally, I request that the Board direct the Superintendent to evaluate converting all daily lease rates to hourly rates.
