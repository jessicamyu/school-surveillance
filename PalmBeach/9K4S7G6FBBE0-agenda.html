Subject
Special Meeting re: Noreen Ikonen Settlement Agreement
Meeting
Feb 10, 2010 - Legal Sessions
Category
Special Meetings
Type
Action






  • On or about May 19, 2005, the Superintendent made a recommendation that Noreen Ikonen be terminated for incompetence and failure to correct the performance deficiencies which rendered her evaluations unsatisfactory overall in violation of School District Policies, Florida Administrative Code Rules, and Florida Statutes. 




  • Ms. Ikonen timely asserted her right to an appeal and a hearing was held before the Division of Administrative Hearings (DOAH) on or about August 22, 2006.




  • In November 2006, the Fourth District Court of Appeals rendered two opinions against the School District which negatively affected the outcome of the Ikonen case.




  • To mitigate damages, Ms. Ikonen was returned to work in August 2008; however, a settlement agreement was not reached between the parties until January 2010.




  • The Settlement Agreement includes back wages for approximately three years in the amount of $139,971.59 and for $15,969.87 in damages.




  • This Settlement Agreement is in the best interest of the School Board.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Settlement Agreement in the matter of School Board of Palm Beach County v. Noreen Ikonen, DOAH case No. 06-000023, a copy of which is attached hereto, and authorize the execution of all related documents.

Contact:

Gerald A. Williams, Esq.; Chief Counsel



Vicki L. Evans-Pare, Esq.; Associate Counsel

Financial Impact:

The financial impact is $139,971.59 in back wages and $15,969.87 in damages. The  $139,971.59 is to be paid from the salary account and they $15,969.87 is to be paid from the District Legal Settlement Fund.
