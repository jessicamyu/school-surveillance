Category

  • The Facility Lease Agreement permits Construction Specifications Institute (CSI) to use Board Rooms A and
    B at the School District's Administrative Building for a one (1) day continuing education seminar.


  • CSI's seminar will provide continuing education credits for Architects, Interior Designers and Contractors.


  • The seminar shall take place on May 21, 2008.


  • The Board Room will be utilized from 7:30 a.m. to     4:30 p.m.


  • The rental rate shall be $15.00 per hour for each room, which will be a total of $270.00.


  • In lieu of the rental payment, CSI will provide fourteen (14) one hour continuing education seminar sessions
    which shall be available at no charge exclusively to School District Employees.


 

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve the Lease Agreement  between the School Board of Palm Beach County and Construction Specifications Institute for use of the Board Rooms A and B at the School District's Administrative Building; and authorize the Chairman and the Superintendent to sign all necessary documents.
