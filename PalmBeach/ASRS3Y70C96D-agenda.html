DESCRIPTION:

Award of ITN 18C-010J for Underwriter services.

 

On September 25, 2017, Invitation to Negotiate, ITN 18C-010J Underwriter Services was solicited.  The Evaluation Committee, consisting of District staff and a member of the Finance Committee, convened and reviewed the 16 responses that were received.  It was the recommendation of the Evaluation Committee to begin negotiations with the highest ranked responders and, if negotiations were successful, recommend award to three (3) Sr. Managers and seven (7) Co. Managers.

 

Citigroup Global Markets Inc., J.P. Morgan Securities LLC and Merrill Lynch, Pierce, Fenner & Smith Incorporated are being recommended as Sr. Managers.

 

Morgan Stanley & Co. LLC, PNC Capital Markets LLC, Raymond James & Associates, Inc., RBC Capital Markets, LLC, Siebert Cisneros Shank & Co., L.L.C., Stifel, Nicolaus & Company, Incorporated, and Wells Fargo Bank, N.A. are being recommended as Co-Managers.  Siebert Cisneros Shank & Co., L.L.C. is a self-reporting M/WBE vendor.

 

The capital plan includes $463 million of borrowing to finance construction projects as well as $89 million of short term financing to expedite sales tax revenues.  Other transactions will be recommended as needed.  Each transaction will be individually reviewed by the Finance Committee and subsequently presented to the School Board for approval.  Four refinancing transactions totaling $455 million were approved by the School Board on September 6, 2017, and will be executed when market conditions are favorable.

 

Fees paid to underwriters are incorporated in each debt issue and are netted from the proceeds received by the School District.  The fees for each transaction are negotiated during the day of the sale with the assistance of the School Board’s Financial Advisor.

 

The Finance Committee reviewed the ITN prior to its release, and will consider the recommendation of the Evaluation Committee at their December 8, 2017 meeting.
