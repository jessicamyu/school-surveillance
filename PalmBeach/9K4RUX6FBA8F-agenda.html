§         The following College Board Pre Advanced Placement Workshops will be provided by this Agreement:



o        Topics for AP Vertical Teams in Social Studies – Developing Reading Habits



o        Interdisciplinary Strategies – Argumentation and the Writing Process



o        Strategies in Math – Developing Algebraic Thinking



o        Strategies in Science – Inquiry Based Laboratories for Middle Schools



o        Advanced Topics for AP Vertical Teams in English – Grammar



o        Strategies in Mathematics – Rate



o        Strategies in Science – Energy Systems



 



§         Funding for this Agreement is provided by The Picower Foundation Grant, which was approved by the school board in May 2007. 



 



§         Participating schools include:  Bear Lakes, H.L. Watkins, Lake Worth, Tradewinds, and Watson B. Duncan Middle Schools, and Lake Worth, Palm Beach Gardens, Palm Beach Lakes, Santaluces, and William T. Dwyer High Schools.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the College Board Professional Development Agreement, effective September 20, 2007 through January 30, 2008.

Contact:

Ann Killets (akillets@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)



Alison Adler

Financial Impact:

The financial impact to the Special Revenue-Other Funds budget is $29,400.  The source of funds is The Picower Foundation Grant.
