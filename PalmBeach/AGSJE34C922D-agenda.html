I recommend the Board accept the Miller3 Disparity Study findings and recommendations.

DESCRIPTION:

Franklin M. Lee, Esq. will present his review of the Miller3 Disparity Study, discuss policy options available to the District, and outline next steps.  Formal acceptance of the Disparity Study by the Board will facilitate implementation of programs responsive to the study findings.
