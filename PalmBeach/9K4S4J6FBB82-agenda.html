LA1 - 2009-2011 Labor Contract between the District and SEIU/Florida Public Services Union
Meeting
Mar 11, 2009 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Operations
Category
Consent
Type
Action (Consent)

The Tentative Agreement provides for:



 



Ø      A new three-year successor contract effective January 1, 2009, through December 31, 2011, with limited reopener provisions on salary and a few additional items for year two (2010) and year three (2011);



Ø      Continuing the January 1, 2008 Salary Schedules through December 31, 2009, and the base salaries of bargaining unit employees in effect December 31, 2008, through December 31, 2009;



Ø      Requiring the Union to certify in writing the names of non-District employed Union representatives and prohibiting such representatives from contacting employees during regular working hours;



Ø      Granting the Union the right to hold meetings at District work locations subject to a three (3) day advance notice to the supervising administrator or principal, and prohibiting such meeting(s) during the work day;



Ø      Providing the Union, upon written notice, complete employee contact information twice annually;



Ø      Granting the Union the right to designate nine (9) Union Days annually on which Union members may wear Union Shirts, subject to advanced review by the Chief Operating Officer;



Ø      Expanding the basis for employee requests for reimbursement for damage to personal vehicle while on District property to include damage caused by an act of nature;



Ø      Prohibiting the use of Annual Leave during an employee’s initial Probational Period;



Ø      Limiting the continuation of a 10-month employee’s regular year rate of pay for summer employment to instances where employee is employed in the same classification or classification series during the summer;



Ø      Extending the promotional Probationary Period from ninety (90) calendar days to ninety (90) working days;



Ø      Rescinding provisions that grant employees their higher salary amount when voluntarily returning to a previously occupied lower level position;



Ø      Modifying Shift Assignment provision to establish a definition for “day shift” and “evening shift” for regular contract employees;



Ø      Continuing the Attendance Incentives for School Bus Drivers, Bus Attendants, and Custodial personnel at the levels authorized for 2008;



Ø      Limiting an employee’s right to bid on new or vacated routes created subsequent to the annual initial bid process to once per semester;



Ø      Establishing Uniform provisions pertaining specifically to School Bus Drivers and Bus Attendants which stipulate that Uniforms shall be provided at no cost to employee; employee is responsible for maintenance of Uniforms; setting conditions for uniform replacement; employee’s responsibilities when separating service with the District; and consequences for failing to comply with Uniform Policies;



Ø      Reducing the number of contracts the District is obligated to print to 400 copies;



Ø      Recognizing the District’s right to require the electronic deposit of all employee paychecks upon ninety (90) days advanced written notice to the bargaining unit, and pledging assistance to employees to establish a checking account.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the 2009-2011 labor contract between the School District of Palm Beach County and the SEIU/Florida Public Services Union.  SEIU/FPSU ratified this agreement on February 26-27, 2009.

Contact:

Joseph M. Moore (Joseph Moore); James Hayes, Jr. (James Hayes, Jr.); Van V. Ludy (Van Ludy)

Financial Impact:

The financial impact  for FY09 is $45,000.
