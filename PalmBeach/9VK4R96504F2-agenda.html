Subject
Joint Meeting with Advisory Boundary Committee - Boundary Change Review
Meeting
Apr 22, 2015 - 2. WORKSHOP immediately following Executive Session in the Board Room.
Category
2. Workshop Items
Type
Information

DESCRIPTION: 

 

  • The purpose of the Workshop is to provide a summary of the Advisory Boundary Committee (ABC) recommendation for the 2015-2016 Boundary Cycle, pursuant to Policy 5.01.
  • The ABC presented the following boundary change proposal to the Superintendent, (Keith Oswald, CAO-designee), on December 4, 2014:  

     

                    PIR-1 - "Boundary Study S-4 - Revised" (Sunset Palms Elementary to Crystal Lakes Elementary) was voted on by the ABC to be recommended to

                    the Superintendent on December 4, 2014 with a  9-2 vote.        

  • The proposal relieves future crowding at Sunset Palms Elementary by moving the future residential development named "Palm Meadows" (288 single family homes) from Sunset Palms Elementary to Crystal Lakes Elementary.  There are currently no homes constructed, nor students residing in the proposed change area.
  • The full proposal and support documentation are provided as attachments.
