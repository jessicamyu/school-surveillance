Recommended Action
I recommend the Board accept the School Safety and Security - FSSAT Findings Summary, and authorize the District to take action to implement the proposed recommendations.

DESCRIPTION:

In this publicly-noticed meeting the District School Safety Specialist will share a summary of findings related to the Florida Safe Schools Assessment Tool (FSSAT) that was conducted for all public schools. Recommendations to improve school safety and security will be discussed identifying strategies and activities that the District should implement in order to address the findings.  This public meeting is being held in accordance with SB7030.

 

This item serves as the Board's acceptance of those findings and recommendations.
