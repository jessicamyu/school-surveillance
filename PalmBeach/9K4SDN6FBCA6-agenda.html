During the 2009 legislative session, the Legislature added subsection (4) to Fla. Stat.ยง 1011.09 which prohibits the use of public funds for out-of state travel, cellular phones, cellular phone service, personal digital assistants, or any other mobile wireless communication device or service including text messaging, whether through purchasing, leasing, contracting, or any other method ,unless specifically approved by the District School Board.



This resolution specifically approves the expenditure of public funds for out-of-state travel, cellular phones, cellular phone service, personal digital assistants, or any other mobile communication device including text messaging.  The School District will continue to restrict travel and minimize the cost of mobile communication devices and services.

FM3 - Budget Resolution - School Board Members Salaries
Meeting
Jul 1, 2009 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Operations
Category
Consent
Type
Action (Consent)

During the 2009 legislative session, the Legislature added subsection (3) to Fla. Stat.ยง 1001.395 that the salary of District School Board Members for FY2010 be the amount calculated by the Florida Legislative Committee on Intergovernmental Relations (LCIR) or the District's beginning salary for teachers who hold baccalaureate degrees, whichever is less.



This resolution specifically approves reducing the School Board Member salaries from the current LCIR calculated salary of $40,932 to $36,822 for FY2010, effective July 1, 2009.  The proposed salary reflects the District's current beginning salary for teachers who hold baccalaureate degrees.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve the attached resolution to set each School Board Member's salary as that of the District's beginning salary for teachers, who hold baccalaureate degrees, which is currently $36,822, effective July 1, 2009.

Contact:

Joseph M. Moore (moorej@palmbeach.k12.flus)



Michael J. Burke (burkem@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)



 

Financial Impact:

The financial impact of this resolution is a $34,811 reduction to the District's FY2010 General Fund Operating Budget.  This reflects a $4,110 salary reduction per Board Member plus the associated reduction in variable benefit costs (FRS, FICA, Medicare).  This reduction will be reflected in the FY2010 District budget submitted for Board adoption on September 9, 2009.
