Category
Consent Agenda

Action (Consent)





  1. The annual update of the Generic Educational Specifications is based upon new technology and innovations.






  1. The Educational Specifications include the decrease in the size of the classrooms for grades 1 thru 8.  The Educational Specifications includes classrooms of 900 s.f. for kindergarten; 850 s.f. for grades 1 thru 8 and 810 s.f. for grades 9 thru 12.






  1. The Educational Specifications include the prototype classroom, clinic and group restroom layouts.






  1. The Educational Specification and DOE Facility List for Middle School includes the decrease of career spaces and the increase of general classrooms.






  1. For modernizations of existing school and other schools with a high concentration of special needs, additional teaching spaces may be provided to meet program capacity per the approval on January 21, 2004 of the Charter District Waiver.






  1. The existing facilities will be analyzed by the project architect to determine appropriate usage of the site and buildings for necessary renovations and remodeling to meet SREF size standards, ADA requirements and other code issues.

Recommendation:

                  I recommend the School Board approve the Generic Educational Specifications for Elementary, Middle and High schools, along with the DOE Facility List for Middle School and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to execute all related contract documents.

PM2 Belle Glade Elementary School Educational Specification
Meeting
Oct 24, 2007 - Spec Mtg re Ed Specs
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Action (Consent)





  1. The construction of the addition to Belle Glade Elementary School is scheduled to commence SY 2008-09 and be completed by SY 2009-10 according to the draft FY 2008-2012 Five-Year Plan.






  1. The Educational Specification for Belle Glade Elementary School is consistent with the Class Size Reduction (CSR) Facility Lists and CSR generic educational specification proposed to the Board on September 19, 2007.






  1. The “CSR” FISH Capacity of this school design will be 978 for students in grades Pre-Kindergarten to 5.  The school will be designed for a Program Capacity of 681 (Kindergarten-5).  The use of the “Program Capacity” for the construction of the school is consistent with the Charter School District Waiver granted by the Florida Board of Education on January 21, 2003. 






  1. The Educational Specification includes additional spaces for the existing ESE, ESOL and Pre-Kindergarten programs.  These new instructional spaces shall accommodate existing classrooms in relocatables.






  1. Although, Belle Glade Elementary does have three (3) existing and it is proposed for two (2) additional new PreK units, the administration has decided to house all the units within the existing building and move other classroom functions to the new addition.  Therefore, the Educational Specification does not include any PreK classrooms.  The Educational Specification does include a new playground area including play equipment for the PreK program.






  1. The Educational Specification reflects the use of grade level grouping to enable implementation of the “school-within-a-school” concepts.






  1. The Educational Specification was reviewed and approved by the Principal.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve the Educational Specification for construction of the addition to Belle Glade Elementary School, amend the project budget to $6,011,559.00, and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to execute all related contract documents.
