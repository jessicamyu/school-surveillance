Meeting
Jun 6, 2018 - 6. WORKSHOP immediately following Executive Session in the Board Room
Category
2. Workshop Items
Type
Information

DESCRIPTION:

SB 7026 is a comprehensive legislation that focuses on public safety and school safety.  An important  component  of  the  legislation  is providing students and their families access to mental health services.  This presentation will provide a broad overview of the legislation focusing on the mental health provisions and requirements for school districts.

Dr. Robinson requests the Board discuss directing the Superintendent to plan and implement a training initiative to ensure that school-based staff are made aware that Florida is No. 3 in the nation and Palm Beach County is No. 3 in the state in documented cases of human trafficking.  The training should include risk factors, signs of human trafficking, and the appropriate response if suspicion is raised.
