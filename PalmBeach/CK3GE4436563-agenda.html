Dermatology d/b/a Jupiter Dermatology and Hair Restoration, effective December 1, 2022, through December 31, 2026; and authorize the Superintendent or designee to sign all documents and contracts related to the Agreement which are required for implementation.

DESCRIPTION:

This Agreement is necessary to meet clinical requirements of the Health Science Education Programs, pursuant to the Florida Department of Education Curriculum Frameworks. This clinical component is also required by the various Boards regulating specific professions such as the Florida Board of Nursing, Florida Board of Pharmacy, Emergency Medical Services, etc.

 

In order to expand our students’ opportunities for clinical experiences we are adding the above referenced agreement.  This agreement originated with Jupiter High School, but is available to all schools and students in the Medical Sciences Academies.

 

Health Science Education programs are located in the following high schools: Atlantic, Boca Raton, Boynton Beach, Forest Hill, Glades Central, Inlet Grove, John I. Leonard, Jupiter, Lake Worth, Pahokee, Palm Beach Gardens, Palm Beach Lakes, Palm Beach Prep, Park Vista, Royal Palm Beach, Santaluces, Somerset Academy, South Tech Academy, SLAM Boca, SLAM Palm Beach, Wellington, and West Boca Raton High School. In addition, the following schools have Adult Education Health Science Programs: Chuck Shaw Technical, Delray Full Service, Forest Hill, and West Tech Education Center. The Health Science Education Programs impact an estimated 7000 students.
 
The programs include Allied Health Assisting, Behavioral Health Technician, Biomedical Sciences, Electrocardiograph Technician, Electrocardiograph Technology, Emergency Medical Responder, Emergency Medical Technician, Exercise Science, Health Unit Coordinator, Home Health Aide, Medical Laboratory Assisting, Medical Assisting, Medical Skills & Services, Nursing Assistant, Patient Care Assistant, Patient Care Technician, Pharmacy Technician, Phlebotomy, Practical Nursing, and Health Science Education Directed Study which prepares students with entry level job skills and exposes them to career opportunities. The Health Science Education programs also provide the frameworks, certifications, and articulations for matriculation to post-secondary education in health occupations and related programs of study.

  • Head Start COLA: $69,651.76 
  • Early Head Start COLA: $3,300.64
