Subject
Equity, Mission/Vision, and Strategic Plan
Meeting
Nov 10, 2020 - 4. WORKSHOP immediately following Special Meeting in the Board Room
Category
2. Workshop Items
Type
Discussion

DESCRIPTION:

With the District's current strategic plan scheduled to sunset in Summer 2021, Board Members have shared feedback around the possibility of revising certain aspects of the District's mission and vision, particularly as it relates to equity. District staff have been developing a timeline to address the Board's feedback, since engaging in these discussions now will ensure the District has a strong foundation on which to build its next strategic plan. This workshop will give Board Members a clearer picture of proposed next steps as well as create opportunities for the Board to provide District staff with direction on how to best proceed with future planning efforts.

 

The workshop presentation will be attached no later than Friday, October 30.
