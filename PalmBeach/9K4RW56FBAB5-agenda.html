  • The agreement allows Bus Radio to provide an interactive radio show for students to listen to as they travel to and from school.




  • The Free Program is designed specifically to improve bus safety while providing the students with age-appropriate content and an entertaining ride.




  • The Pilot Program will allow Bus Radio to install hardware on approximately 200 buses.




  • The School District of Palm Beach County will never incur a charge for the hardware, installation or maintenance of the Bus Radio system.




  • The School District of Palm Beach County has the right to reject the Content at any time if it deems such Content age inappropriate.




  • Bus radio agrees to include public service and safety messages as part of the Content.




  • Bus Radio will indemnify the School District of Palm Beach County against all liabilities regarding hardware, installation and usage of equipment.




  • The School District will run the Pilot Program for the 07/08 school calendar year and then make a decision whether to continue.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve the Pilot Program between Bus Radio and the School District of Palm Beach County for students who ride public school buses.

Contact:

Joseph Moore (moorej@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)

Financial Impact:

The financial impact will be to the general operating budget of Transportation in the amount of $0.
