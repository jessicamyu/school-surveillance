DESCRIPTION:

On December 17, 2014 the School Board approved the Master Agreement between the School Board of Palm Beach County and the Metropolitan Center for Urban Education at New York University to conduct an Educational Equity Audit of the Palm Beach County School District.  On February 18, 2015, the School Board approved the Project Plan for the Equity Audit.  Dr. Roey Ahram and Ms. Madelyn Boesen of the Metropolican Center will present the First Interim Report of the Audit.

Subject
Proposed 2016 Legislative Issues
Meeting
Jul 29, 2015 - 1. WORKSHOP at 2 p.m. in the Board Room
Category
2. Workshop Items
Type
Information

DESCRIPTION:

  • The attached list of critical issues has been developed based on a review of the 2015 legislation and recommendations by District staff.  They are presented for Board discussion and consensus.
  • Results from this workshop will be brought back for Board approval at the August 19, 2015 Board Meeting and will be submitted subsequently to the Greater Florida School Boards Consortium (GFSCB) and the Florida School Boards Association (FSBA).
  • These recommendations may be modified or augmented at any future time as desired by the School Board up to and during the 2016 regular legislative session which begins January 12, 2016, and runs 60 calendar days through March 11, 2016.  These issues can also be continued or modified in the event of any subsequent special session.
  • Proposals adopted on August 19, 2015 will be submitted to the Palm Beach County Legislative Delegation and serve as the basis for discussion at a joint meeting scheduled for October 28, 2015.  These proposals will also be sent to the Governor, House Speaker, Senate President, and legislators serving on committees having a direct bearing on PreK-12 education for 2016, and will be part of the District's community outreach efforts.
  • As was done this past year, a tri-fold color brochure containing these critical issues will be printed (not at District expense) to assist with individual legislative efforts of the School Board, Superintendent, and staff.
