Special Meeting re: Budget Advisory Committee Resolution
Meeting
Feb 2, 2011 - Workshops
Category
Workshop Items
Type
Action (Consent)

The proposed resolution:



 



      Provides for a seven (7) member committee, with each Board member appointing a committee member.  See Section 3.







  • Establishes the duties and responsibilities for the Committee.   See Section 2.




  • Provides for a staff liaison and staff support to the Committee.   See Section 6.




  • Establishes that all meetings are public subject to the Sunshine Law; requires a quorum of no less than four (4) members; and provides for meetings to be conducted in accordance with Robert’s Rules.   See Section 4.




  • Provides guidelines for Committee members’ communications outside of the School District.  See Section 7.




  • Gives the effective date of the resolution to be the date of passage.  See Section 10.


  • Sunsets the Committee upon the School Board’s adoption of the 2011-2012 budget for the School District.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the attached resolution establishing an ad hoc Budget Advisory Committee (Committee) to the School Board for the purpose of making recommendations to the School Board regarding the School District’s budget for the 2011-2012 fiscal year.
