Special Meeting re: Jerry Smith v. PBCSB Settlement
Meeting
Jan 16, 2008 - Spec Mtg re Jerry Smith
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Action

This litigation was brought by an employee, Mr. Jerry Smith, who claimed that the School Board and Mr. James Kelly failed to provide him with a “Name Clearing Hearing” as a result of allegations made against him; which allegations were placed in his personal file.  This case went to trial and a verdict and judgment was entered in favor of Mr. Smith.  The judgment for compensatory damages was $60,320.00.  The Judge entered a separate order on the plaintiff’s entitlement and amount of attorney fees and costs.  While the Plaintiff accepted the judgment on the compensatory damages, which has been satisfied, the plaintiff elected to appeal the Court’s ruling on the issue of attorney fees and costs.



 



The appellate court recently rendered a decision which reversed the trial court on both the fee and cost issues.  The appellate court directed the trial judge to increase the hourly rate used in calculating the fees by one hundred dollars ($100.00) per hour. The appellate court also granted the plaintiff’s motion for attorney fees at the appellate level.  Finally, the appellate decision also provided for an increase in the costs awarded at the trial level and costs associated with the appeal.



 



Settlement of the remaining issue of fees and costs is recommended as being in the best financial interest of the District due to the anticipated cost for continuing with the litigation over fees and costs and the possibility that additional fees which were previously denied could be awarded as a result of a renewed effort by the plaintiff’s attorney.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the settlement agreement between Jerry Smith and the School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida and James P. Kelly and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to execute all related documents.
