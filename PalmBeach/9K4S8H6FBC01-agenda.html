AmeriCorps Agreement
Meeting
Nov 5, 2008 - Wkshps and Spec Mtgs re Academics
Category
Consent
Type
Action (Consent)


  • The District partnered with the Palm Beach County Literacy Coalition and other partners to write a grant for AmeriCorps members

  • The grant provides the School District with six (6) AmeriCorps members who act as graduation coaches at the following high schools:  Boynton Beach High School, Forest Hill High School, John I. Leonard High School, Olympic Heights High School, Palm Beach Central High School and Royal Palm Beach High School.  The matching funds that the district is required to pay for the AmeriCorps members is paid by a grant from the Education Foundation in the amount of $25,000 for this year.

  • The AmeriCorps members are college graduates.  They were trained by our district to assist in breaking down barriers for at-risk students at the school to which they are assigned.

  • These six (6) coaches work with an additional six (6) coaches obtained through a Small Learning Communities (SLC) Grant.  The SLC coaches are at the following schools:  Atlantic High School, Glades Central High School, Lake Worth High School, Palm Beach Gardens High School, Palm Beach Lakes High School and Santaluces High School.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the cooperative agreement between the Literacy AmeriCorps Palm Beach County and the School Board of Palm Beach County, effective August 24, 2008 through July 24, 2009, and authorize the Superintendent or designee to sign all related documents.

Ann Killets  akillets@palmbeach.k12.fl.us
