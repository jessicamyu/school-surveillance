Special Meeting re: Sun Art Final Order
Meeting
Aug 11, 2010 - Legal Sessions
Category
Special Meetings
Type
Action




  • School District Staff, from the Purchasing Department issued a competitive invitation to bid for exterior painting of Item 1:  The Exterior Painting of Greenacres Elementary School Project, Item 2: The Exterior Painting of South Olive Elementary School Project, and Item 3:  The Exterior Painting of Belvedere Elementary School Project.




  • Ultimately, appropriate District Staff issued an intended decision to reject all bids in Items 1 & 2 due to an ambiguity that affected fair competition.  Sun Art Corporation filed a rejection of all bids protest.  In its protest, Sun Art requested a Division of Administrative Hearings (DOAH) hearing and alleged, among other things, that the School Board’s intended decision of rejecting all bids in Items 1 & 2 was arbitrary.




  • Subsequent to the DOAH hearing, the Administrative Law Judge, for his own reasons, found that the School Board’s intended decision was arbitrary and, therefore, the School Board should not reject all bids on Items 1 & 2, but instead should, with respect to each of these items, award a contract to the lowest responsive and responsible bidder.  Additionally, in determining the lowest responsive and responsible bidder, the School Board should find that a bidder’s failure to have signed its Revised Bid Summary Sheet constituted a minor irregularity not vitiating the bidder’s responsiveness.




  • Upon review of the entire record and full hearing on the Amended Exceptions from Sun Art Painting Corporation, and the Amended Exception of the Superintenden, the Board voted to reject the Amended Exceptions of Sun Art Painting Corporation and accept the Amended Exception of the Superintendent.




  • The Board, therefore rejected the recommendation in the Recommended Order and will issue a Final Order upholding the recommendation to reject all bids on Item 1 and Item 2 of the Exterior Painting Projects.




  • This Final Order is in the best Interest of the School Board.




  • The Final Order will be attached Monday due to the short turnaround time from the August 4, 2010 meeting.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Final Order in the matter of Sun Art Painting Corporation v. The School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida, DOAH case No. 10-0976BID, and authorize the Superintendent and Board Chairman to finalize and sign all required documents.
