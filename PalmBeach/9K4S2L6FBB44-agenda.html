Interlocal Agreement with the School Board of Palm Beach County and Palm Beach County
Meeting
Aug 27, 2008 - Spec Mtg re Interlocal Agreement with County
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Discussion


  • Because of technical limitations, the County is currently unable to transmit Channel 20 programming to Receive Sites in the western sector of Palm Beach County.


  • The School Board currently broadcasts six channels of digital programming within the 2608 to 2614 MHz band, which cover the Palm Beach County, including the western sector.  Through this agreement, the School Board will broadcast Channel 20 programming with the 2608 to 2614 band.


  • The County will provide the School Board payment of Fifty Two Thousand Five Hundred ($52,500.00) (”Broadcast Fee”) for the term commencing on the date of execution of the agreement by the County and shall terminate on December 31, 2009.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve the Interlocal Agreement between the School Board of Palm Beach County and Palm Beach County to transmit Palm Beach County’s Channel 20 programming over the School Board’s 2608 to 2614 band and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to sign all necessary documents.
