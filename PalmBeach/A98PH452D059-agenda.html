BRD1 Date Change for November, 2016 Board Meetings

May 4, 2016 - 3. SPECIAL MEETING immediately following Workshop in the Board Room

Recommended Action
I recommend the Board approve changing the November, 2016 Regular Board Meeting date to Wednesday, November 16, 2016 and the Reorganization Meeting to Tuesday, November 22, 2016.

DESCRIPTION:

At the 2015 Reorganization meeting, the Board voted that both the Reorganization and Board Meeting for 2016 would occur on the third Tuesday: November 15, 2016. 

 

Per Fla. Statute §§ 100.041(3)(a)…”The term of office of a school board member…shall begin on the second Tuesday following the general election in which such member…is elected.”  In this case, the Reorganization Meeting should be Tuesday, November 22, 2016.  This meeting would be exclusively for the purpose of:  swearing in Board members, choosing a Chair and Vice Chair, voting on dates and times of the 2017 Regular Board Meetings, approving the Organization Resolution, and making School Board committee assignments.  No other meetings would be scheduled for that day.  The suggested start time is 4 p.m.

 

It is recommended that the Regular Board Meeting follow our normal schedule of occurring on the third Wednesday of the month.  In that case, the Regular Board Meeting would be on Wednesday, November 16, 2016 at 5 p.m.  This will provide for only one meeting to occur on November 22, 2016, as it is two days prior to Thanksgiving.

Recommended Action
I recommend the Board approve the five (5) year Charter with Connections Education Center of the Palm Beaches, and authorize the Superintendent and/or his designee to sign all related contracts and documents.

DESCRIPTION:

Pursuant to Florida Statute 1002.33(7), the terms and conditions for the operation of the charter school shall be set forth by the School District and the applicant in a written contractual agreement called a charter.

 

The Department of Charter Schools received an application from Connections Education Center of the Palm Beaches in August, 2015.  The application for Connections Education Center of the Palm Beaches was approved on November 4, 2015, as Item CS6 during a Special Board Meeting.  The Charter Contract for this charter school was reviewed and agreed upon by the appropriate District staff and negotiated by the General Counsel’s office.  The charter will become effective upon signing by both parties, and shall cover a term of five (5) years, commencing July 1, 2016, and ending June 30, 2021, except as provided in the charter. 

 

Connections Education Center of the Palm Beaches will serve ESE students in grades PreK-8, ages 3-21.  Their mission is, “To serve students with autism spectrum disorder and their families by providing high-quality education and therapeutic services through collaboration, communication, and integration of evidence-based best practices in a unique learning environment.  Connections Education Center intends to prepare its students to be as independent, self-sufficient and socially interactive as possible.”

LAC1 - Amendment to the 2017-2018 School Calendar

May 4, 2016 - 3. SPECIAL MEETING immediately following Workshop in the Board Room

Recommended Action
I recommend the Board approve the amended 2017-2018 School Calendar as attached.

DESCRIPTION:

A revision to the 2017-18 School Calendar previously approved by the School Board on December 16, 2015 (Agenda Item LAC1) is necessary to provide 180 student attendance days.  The cause for revision was discovered through planning and implementation work subsequent to Board approval.  One student attendance day must be removed from the initial calendar to provide 180 student attendance days for the 2017-2018 school year.  To affect this change, the following amendments to the 2017-2018 School Calendar are required:

 

1.       The end of the 2nd nine weeks grading period is moved up one day from Thursday, December 21 to Wednesday, December 20, 2017.

2.       The “Teacher Workday/In-Service Day” is moved up one day from Friday, December 22, to Thursday, December 21, 2017.

3.       The first day of Winter Break is moved up one weekday from Monday, December 25 to Friday, December 22, 2017.

4.       Because Winter Break begins one weekday earlier, 12-month employees return to work from their Winter Break on Wednesday, January 3, instead of Thursday, January 4, 2018.

5.       The “HOLIDAY” dates are amended to reflect the above changes.

6.       The number of Student Attendance Days in each of the three (3) trimesters are changed to 58, 57 and 65.

7.       The number of Student Attendance Days in each of the four (4) secondary grading periods are changed to 47, 39, 47 and 47.

8.       The number of Student Attendance days in each of the two (2) semesters are changed to 86 and 94.

 

The beginning and ending dates for students (August 14, 2017 and May 31, 2018) and the beginning and ending dates for teachers (August 8, 2017 and June 1, 2018) are unchanged.
