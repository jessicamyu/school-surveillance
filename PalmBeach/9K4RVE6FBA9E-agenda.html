This meeting involves a grievance filed by former employee, Learna Ramsey, against the Superintendent as set forth in her February 25, 2007 letter (Exhibit 8 hereto). 



 



 



§         School Board Policy 3.31(7) (Exhibit 1 hereto) provides that if a grievance is filed against a direct Board report the “Board shall determine if the complaint appears to merit formal investigation.”  If it makes that determination, “the Board shall set the parameters for the investigation…[and] [u]pon receiving the report, the Board shall determine whether any action is required.”



 



§         The current grievance concerns whether the proper procedures under Policy 3.31 were followed in relation to Ms. Ramsey’s February 25, 2006 grievance. Among the procedural issues Ms. Ramsey has raised is that the Board should have heard her earlier grievance and that the investigative report was not forwarded to her attorney as provided by the policy. (Exhibit 8) The District’s position was that a direct appeal of her grievance to the School Board was not available because the three circumstances allowing a direct appeal under the Policy did not exist, particularly since the grievances were not against a direct report to the Board. (Exhibit 11) Further, the investigative report had been forwarded to counsel for Ms. Ramsey. (Exhibit 9)



 



§         Attached to this Board report are a Table of Exhibits, School Board Policies 3.31 and 3.28, and correspondence, marked as Exhibits, between Ms. Ramsey and the District.



 



§         Set forth within School Board Policy 3.31(7) are the two (2) options available to the Board at this time.  These are stated below as alternative motions to be made by a Board member.



 



§         If the Board votes for the second option, the Board needs to vote to set the parameters of the investigation under Policy 3.31(7), such as whether to retain outside counsel.



 

Recommendation:

1)  I move that the Board determine that the February 27. 2007 grievance filed by Learna Ramsey does not merit formal investigation and her grievance is denied and authorize the Board Chairman to so notify the grievant and execute all necessary documents.



                                       [OR]



2)  I move that the Board determine that the February 27. 2007 grievance filed by Learna Ramsey does merit formal investigation and authorize the Board Chairman to so notify the grievant and execute all necessary documents.
