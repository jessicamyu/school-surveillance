Category
3. Workshop Items
Type
Information, Presentation

DESCRIPTION:

This workshop is to share a comprehensive overview plan for District schools to re-engage into fall interscholastic sports and extracurricular activities. This plan shares timelines for re-engagement as well as operational pieces necessary to provide safety and security as we return to athletics and extracurricular activities amid COVID-19.

 

CONTACT: 

Keith Oswald, Deputy Superintendent/Chief of Schools (keith.oswald@palmbeachschools.org)

Glenda Sheffield, Ed.D., Chief Academic Officer

John-Anthony Boggess, Assistant Superintendent for Choice & Innovation

Valerie Miyares, District Athletic Manager 

 

FINANCIAL IMPACT:

Due to COVID-19, schools are anticipating a decrease in gate revenue, coupled with the structure of regional play, the District's impact is estimated at $650,000.
