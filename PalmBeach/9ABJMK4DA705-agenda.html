The need to create adults with job skills is imperative.

As I understand Palm Beach State College is not able to meet the need alone, I request that we explore renegotiating

our agreement with PBSC for adult "vocational" programs.  We could use some of our facilities to provide night

programming for drop outs, change in career adults and others.
