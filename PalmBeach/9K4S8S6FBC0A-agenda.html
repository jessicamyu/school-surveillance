FM6 - Qualified School Construction Bonds
Meeting
Sep 7, 2010 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Operations
Category
New Business
Type
Action (Consent)


  • Approve a resolution authorizing the sale of Certificates of Participation Series 2010A (Qualified School Construction Bonds).

  • The resolution allows for a competitive sale of a principal amount not to exceed $67,665,000.  The resolution also allows the sale to be negotiated through a public sale or as a private placement if necessary due to market conditions.

  • The resolution delegates the authority to the Superintendent to negotiate the terms of the transaction within the parameters set forth in the resolution.

  • The sale of the Qualified School Construction Bonds is included in the FY 2011 - 2015 Five Year Capital Plan.

  • The Finance Committee reviewed the transaction at their meetings held on May 21, 2010 and August 11, 2010.  The Committee approved the transaction on August 11, 2010.

  • Other documents related to the sale are under development.  Drafts are available in the Board Minutes office.


 

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board adopt a Resolution authorizing the execution of Schedule 2010A to the Master Lease and approve the sale of Certificates of Participation (Qualified School Construction Bonds - Federally Taxable - Interest Subsidy) Series 2010A in an aggregate principal amount not to exceed $67,665,000 and authorize the Superintendent to negotiate and sign all necessary documents.

Contact:

Joseph M. Moore (moorej@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)



Michael J. Burke (burkem@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)



Leanne Evans (evansl@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)

Financial Impact:

The sale of the Qualified School Construction Bonds will bring in approximately $67.6 million for the construction of two schools as included in the FY 2011 - FY 2015 Five Year Capital Plan.
