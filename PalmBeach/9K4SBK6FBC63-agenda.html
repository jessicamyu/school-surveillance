Closed Session -



The reason for calling this emergency special meeting in the Buccini case is because last Thursday the judge continued the case for one week--until April 25--and ordered mediation before this Wednesday April 20. There is an immediate danger to the welfare of the district by the need to determine whether settlement is prudent and possible



before this case goes to court ordered mediation and trial and the district incurs significant expenditures at this time.



 



Notice for the public portion of this session is sufficient  and it is posted on the District's website.This meeting was advertised in the newspaper on April 16, 2011.
