Special Meeting re: Corebridge Settlement
Meeting
Jul 30, 2008 - Spec Mtg re Corebridge
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Action




  • In 2004, Corebridge operated a charter school under the auspices of the Palm Beach County School District and operated said school on properties leased from Boca Fed, L.L.C.  Subsequent thereto, Boca Fed brought suit against Corebridge in order to obtain the termination of Corebridge’s lease agreement in the Circuit Court of the Fifteenth Judicial Circuit in and for Palm Beach County, Florida, in an action styled BOCA FED, L.L.C. V. COREBRIDGE EDUCATIONAL ACADEMY SCHOOL, INC., Case  502004CA011762XXXXAF (the “Trial Court Action.”). Boca Fed and Corebridge entered a settlement.  Once Boca Fed tendered the settlement monies to the Court, an Order of Dismissal with prejudice was entered by the Court, and Boca Fed was, in effect, no longer a party to the trial court action.




  • The School Board intervened in the above-styled action for, among other matters, delivery of financial documents, for payment to the IRS and to determine whether it was entitled to any unencumbered public funds from the settlement proceeds.  Over $100,000 was paid to the US Department of Treasury for IRS liens.  The settlement proceeds at issue now total $28,761.72, which are currently being held in escrow by the law firm of Gerstin & Associates, counsel for Corebridge and the Tetreaults and escrow counsel.




  • Verne and Constance Tetreault are creditors of Corebridge and sought re-compensation in the trial court action for monies loaned to the school.




  • To determine Corebridge’s immediate right to possession of the settlement proceeds, an interlocutory appeal was taken to the Fourth District Court of Appeal by Corebridge of the Circuit Court’s Order on Corebridge’s Motion to Disburse and a supersedeas bond was posted. This appeal is presently pending before the Fourth District and is styled COREBRIDGE EDUCATIONAL ACADEMY CHARTER SCHOOL, INC., ETC. V. SCHOOL BOARD OF PALM BEACH COUNTY, FLOIRDA, Case 4D08-270. (the “Appellate Court Action”).




  • This settlement agreement resolves all issues of the trial court action and appellate court action.

Recommendation:

I recommend the Board approve the settlement in the matters of  BOCA FED, L.L.C. V. COREBRIDGE EDUCATIONAL ACADEMY SCHOOL, INC., Case  502004CA011762XXXXAF  and COREBRIDGE EDUCATIONAL ACADEMY CHARTER SCHOOL, INC., ETC. V. SCHOOL BOARD OF PALM BEACH COUNTY, FLOIRDA, Case 4D08-270  and authorize the Chariman and Superintendent to execute all related documents.

Contact:

Gerald A. Williams, Esq.; Chief Counsel williamsg@palmbeach.k12.fl.us



Bruce A. Harris, Esq.; Senior Counsel

Financial Impact:

From the $28,761.72, Corebridge will receive $16,000 and the School Board will receive $12,761.72.
