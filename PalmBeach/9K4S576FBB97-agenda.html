Amendment to Charter Agreement for Life Skills Center of Palm Beach County
Meeting
Oct 1, 2008 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Curriculum
Category
New Business

Action

Life Skills Center of Palm Beach County's Charter Agreement expired June 30, 2008.  The Renewal Application was approved by the School Board on May 21, 2008. 



An amendment to extend the current Charter to September 30, 2008 was approved by the School Board on June 4, 2008.



Contract negotiations are ongoing but in order to effectively complete a mutually agreed upon contract that clearly articulates the rights and responsibilities of each party, an extension is needed.



The Governing Board has signed the amendment to extend the current "Contract" until December 30, 2008 (see Exhibit "A").



Data Panels are attached (see Exhibit "B").

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the amendment to extend the Charter for Life Skills Center of Palm Beach Charter School.
