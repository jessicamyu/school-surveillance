  1. Delegation Member Discussion Items and General discussion (as submitted)


 



Vice-Chair (Sen. Bogdanoff) - status of district policy to implement cyber-bullying legislation



 



Sen. Benacquisto - status of implementation of SB736 (Student Success Act)



- status of implementation of virtual education (HB7197)



- thoughts on benefits of extended school year



 



Sen. Sachs - Global languages in the classroom;



- Free tuition for teachers studying mathematics and science (her legislation, HB723, 2010)



- High school apprenticeship programs at Biotech companies for students interested in the life sciences



- High school apprenticeship programs at various government offices and private sector institutions



 



Rep. Bernard - General update on student achievement;



- Update on renovation of Glades area schools;



- Update on superintendent's search;



- Status of school board member redistricting;



- Update on charter schools



 



Rep. Hager - Board/district governance and organization to ensure high school graduation for college/career readiness



 



Rep. Moraitis - school choice, including an evaluation of the performance of charter schools in their area; ideally, some data and analysis broken down by school grade, test scores and per pupil spending; also a comparison between charter and traditional schools serving similar groups



 



Rep. Rooney - status of leadership training, preparation and selection for school-based administrators (principals in particular)



 



Rep. Slosberg - support for sale of advertising on school buses to raise revenues for school districts (His legislation, HB19, 2012 prefiled)
