1.  The FY 2009-2013 Five Year Plan includes the construction of a New Suncoast High School.
2.  Due to the construction of the new Suncoast High School certain road improvements on Congress Avenue, West 13th Street and Jake Lane are required to meet Palm Beach County's and the City of Riviera Beach's Traffic Standards.
3.  The School District has successfully acquired the rights-of-way needed to proceed with the required road improvements.  (See Exhibit "A"")
4.  Ownership of the three parcels needs to be conveyed to the City of Riviera Beach.

RE-2 Interlocal Agreement/City of Riviera Beach
Meeting
Jul 7, 2010 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Operations
Category
Consent
Type
Action (Consent)

1.  The Agreement provides for the mutual use of School District Facilities by the City of Riviera Beach and its affiliated recreation groups and organizations; and
2.  In return, the City agrees to make the City's Facilities available to Suncoast Community High School, New Riviera Beach Area High School 02-MMM (when constructed), John F. Kennedy Middle School, West Riviera Beach  Elementary School, Dr. Mary McLeod Bethune Elementary School, Washington Elementary School and Lincoln Elementary School.
3.  The following language has been added to address issues not included in standard Mutual Use of Facilities Agreements:
      A.  The City's annual six (6) week youth summer camp program will be allowed to utilize John F. Kennedy Middle School's classroom space, computer lab and cafeteria, from mid-April to mid-May.
      B.  John F. Kennedy Middle School's students will receive free swimming lessons from the City.  The number of lifeguards required for each swimming lesson session will also be provided by the City at no charge to the School District.
4.  The term of this Agreement is for one year with four (4) consecutive one year renewals. 
5.  Each party agrees to provide proper supervision of its own activities.



 

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve the Interlocal Agreement between the City of Riviera Beach and the School Board of Palm Beach County for the Mutual Use of Facilities; and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to sign all necessary documents.
