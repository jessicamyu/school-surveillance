CS1 Day Star Academy of Excellence, Inc. - Voluntary Termination of Charter Contract
Meeting
Aug 26, 2015 - 5. SPECIAL MEETING immediately following Workshop in the Board Room
Category
11. Consent Agenda
Type
Action (Consent)
Recommended Action
I recommend the Board accept the voluntary termination of Day Star Academy of Excellence, Inc. Charter School effective August 3, 2015.

DESCRIPTION:

The School District of Palm Beach County, (“Sponsor”), entered into a renewal charter agreement with Day Star Academy of Excellence, Inc. commencing July 1, 2010, and the term of the Agreement was for five (5) years, ending on June 30, 2015. 

 

The Governing Board of Day Star Academy of Excellence, Inc. unanimously elected to terminate the charter on August 3, 2015, due to being unsuccessful in securing a leased location for the FY16 school year and financial challenges.  (See Voluntary Termination Letter attached.)

 

Day Star Academy of Excellence, Inc. served students in grades K-5.  The school will not reopen in August, 2015.  There is no negative impact to any student.  There were 55 students enrolled at the school.  The parents were notified by the School and the District that Day Star Academy of Excellence, Inc. would not reopen in August, 2015.

 

Attached are copies of the Contract, Voluntary Termination Letter from Day Star's Board, Board Notification, and Matrix for August 26, 2015, Agenda Items.
