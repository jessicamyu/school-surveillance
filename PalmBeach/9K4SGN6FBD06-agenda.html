Subject
Reconsideration and Appeal Hearing of School Board action to reject renewal application for prequalification status
Meeting
Aug 17, 2011 - Administrative Hearing re Built Right
Category
Special Meetings
Type
Discussion

On July 20, 2011, the School Board rejected an application for renewal of prequalification status submitted by Built Right Construction Inc. due to:





-failure to provide adequate management of projects, and



-failure to satisfactorily resolve claims by a sub-contractor.



Such failures were contrary to the criteria for the prequalification of contractors pursuant to School Board Policy 7.08 and State Requirements for Educational Facilities (SREF) 4.1(1)(a)(3), and (4). Back up documentation to support the above referenced failures was attached to the July 20, 2011 Board Report. A link to the July 20, 2011, School Board Report with back up documentation is as follows:



http://www.palmbeach.k12.fl.us/agenda/Wednesday,%20July%2020,%202011%20School%20Board%20Meeting/F7AF65C5-5496-48FA-B263-76E1A7AE04EB-4936A910-CDF2-4FE3-BBB8-0AD2AAF09932.HTM



Pursuant to SFREF 4.1 (h), a contractor whose application has been rejected shall be given the benefit of reconsideration and appeal by submitting a written request for reconsideration, along with any additional information, within 10 days after receiving notification of the action by the School Board. On July 21, 2011, District Staff provided Built Right Construction Inc. with notification of the School Board’s action. Built Right Construction Inc. received such notification on July 22, 2011. On August 1, 2011, Built Right Construction Inc. timely appealed the School Board’s July 20th action by submitting its written request for reconsideration and attached additional information.



SFREF 4.1 (h), also, states that the School Board shall act upon the contractor’s request within thirty (30) calendar days after the filing. In compliance with SFREF 4.1 (h), on August 17, 2010, the School Board has scheduled a Reconsideration and Appeal Hearing to review Built Right Construction’s written request for reconsideration and attached additional information for the purpose of acting upon Built Right Construction’s written request.



Finally, SFREF 4.1 (h) states that the School Board can require additional information to justify the reconsideration.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board adhere to its July 20, 2011 action to approve the non-renewal of Built Right Construction Inc.’s prequalification status, thereby rejecting its application for renewal of its prequalification status, and authorize the Superintendent and the Board Chairman to finalize and sign all required documents.
