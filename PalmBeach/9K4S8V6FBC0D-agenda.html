Special Meeting re: PBCSB v. Jill Shadoff Final Order
Meeting
Sep 29, 2010 - Legal Sessions
Category
Special Meetings
Type
Action






  • Jill Shadoff was employed by the School District of Palm Beach County as an ESE teacher at Tradewinds Middle School.




  • On December 2, 2009, the Superintendent made a recommendation that Ms. Shadoff be terminated based upon her arrest, on March 6, 2009, for trafficking of Oxycontin.




  • Ms. Shadoff timely asserted her right to an appeal and a hearing was held before the Division of Administrative Hearings on April 26, 2010.  A Recommended Order was entered by the Administrative Law Judge on June 28, 2010, recommending the School Board enter a final order dismissing any and all charges, reinstating Ms. Shadoff, and awarding her back pay for the period of suspension.




  • Petitioner filed exceptions to certain Conclusions of Law of the Recommended Order.  Respondent did not file any exceptions or object to Petitionerâ€™s exceptions.




  • To mitigate damages, Ms. Shadoff was returned to work at the beginning of this school year.




  • This Final Order is in the best interest of the School Board.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Settlement & Release in the matter of Palm Beach County School Board v. Jill Shadoff, DOAH Case No. 10-0372, a copy of which is attached hereto, and authorize the execution of all related documents.

Contact:

Bruce A.Harris, Esq.; Interim Chief Counsel



Elizabeth T. McBride, Esq.; Counsel

Financial Impact:

The financial impact to the District is $40,569.95 from the general fund.
