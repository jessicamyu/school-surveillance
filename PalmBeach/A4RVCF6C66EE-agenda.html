I recommend the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp consider the annual election of officers.

DESCRIPTION:

The Leasing Corporation is a Florida not-for-profit corporation that has as its sole member the School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida.  The corporation facilitates the School District's lease purchase financing of school facilities, commonly referred to as COPs (or Certificates of Participation).  A summary of the corporation is attached.

 

The bylaws of the Leasing Corp require an annual meeting of the Board of Directors to elect corporate officers.  Palm Beach School Board members or School District staff may be elected to serve as officers.

 

The current officers are as follows:
