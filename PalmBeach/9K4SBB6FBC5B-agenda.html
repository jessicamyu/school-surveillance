SUMMARY OF Advisory Boundary Committee (ABC)



2010-2011 Cycle



BOUNDARY CHANGE PROPOSALS



Presentation to Superintendent on December 3, 2009



 



1.  PL-1  Boundary Study P-1 (Palm Beach Public Elementary)



·         Study P-1 was recommended to the Superintendent on October 29, 2009 with a 12-3 vote.



·         Proposed changes provides relief to Palm Beach Public Elementary School/and adjustments to Westward and Palmetto elementary schools:



 



                                          i.    Moves SACs 154, 155 (part), 400 (part) from Palm Beach Public Elementary to Palmetto Elementary.    



                                         ii.    Moves SAC 142 from Palm Beach Public Elementary to Roosevelt Elementary.



                                        iii.    Moves SAC 382 from Westward Elementary to Roosevelt Elementary.



                                        iv.    Moves SAC 185 from Palmetto Elementary to Meadow Park Elementary.



 



Current 4th grade students would have the option to remain at Palm Beach Public, Palmetto, or Westward as 5th graders without transportation; current gifted or strings students in proposed change areas have the option to remain.



 



2.  PL-2  Boundary Study C-2 amended (Calusa Elementary School)



·         Study C-2 amended was recommended to the Superintendent on October 15, 2009 with a unanimous vote (14-0).



·         This proposed boundary change provides relief to Calusa Elementary School.



 



                                          i.    Moves SACs 310, 316 (part) and 376 from Calusa Elementary to J.C. Mitchell Elementary.



 



Note: Current 4th grade students would have the option to remain at their current school as 5th graders without transportation.



 



3.  PL-3  Boundary Study W-1 (New school 03-W) West Central Communities Elementary School.



·         Boundary Study W-1 was recommended to the Superintendent on October 29, 2009 with a unanimous vote (14-0).



·         This proposed change establishes the boundary for the new West Central Communities Elementary School (03-W).



 



                                          i.    Moves students in SAC 157 from Cholee Lake Elementary to the new school 03-W.



                                         ii.    Moves students in SACs 106G and 106B (part) from Cypress Trails Elementary to the new school 03-W.



                                        iii.    Moves students in SACs 156R and 158 (part) from Equestrian Trails Elementary to the new school 03-W.



 



Note: Current 4th grade students would have the option to remain at their current school as 5th graders without transportation.



                                          



4.  PL- 4  Boundary Study PL-1 (Plumosa School of the Arts Elementary)



·         Study PL-1 was recommended to the Superintendent on October 29, 2009 with a 10-4 vote.



·         This proposed change provides future relief to Plumosa School of the Arts Elementary School:



 



                                          i.    Moves SACs 291, 300C, 374, and 375 from Plumosa School of the Arts Elementary to Pine Grove Elementary. 



 



Current 4th grade students would have the option to remain at Plumosa School of the Arts Elementary as 5th graders without transportation. Current Visual, Performing and Communication Arts students in proposed change areas have the option to remain.



5.  PL-5  Boundary Study G-1 (Gove Elementary)



·         Study G-1 was recommended to the Superintendent on October 29, 2009 with a unanimous vote (14-0).



·         This proposal changes the grade level at Gove Elementary School from K-6 to K-5 effective August 2010.



 



                                          i.    Changes grade level at Gove Elementary from K-6 to K-5 by moving students entering 6th grade to Lake Shore Middle School in August 2010.



 



6.  PL-6  Boundary Study MM-1 (New school 03-MM) Pahokee Area Middle School.



·         Boundary Study MM-1 was recommended to the Superintendent on October 29, 2009 with a unanimous vote (14-0).



·         This proposal establishes the boundary for the new Pahokee Area Middle School



 



                                          i.    Changes grade level at Pahokee Elementary and KEC/Canal Point Elementary from K-6 to K-5 by moving students entering 6th grade to the new Pahokee Area Middle 03-MM in August 2010.



                                         ii.    Moves students entering grades 6, 7, and 8 in August 2010 from Pahokee Middle/Senior High School to the new Pahokee Area Middle school 03-MM in August 2010.



 



7.  PL-7  Boundary Study PBC-3 (Palm Beach Central High)



·         Study PBC-3 was recommended to the Superintendent on October 15, 2009 with a unanimous vote (14-0).



·         This proposed change provides relief to Palm Beach Central High School.



 



                                          i.    Moves SACs 156B and 179A (part) from Palm Beach Central High to Wellington High.



                                         ii.    Moves SAC 180B from Palm Beach Central High to John I. Leonard High.



 



Current 9 -11 grades students in proposed change areas that attend Palm Beach Central have the option to remain without transportation.

Recommendation:

The Advisory Boundary Committee (ABC) meeting with the Board to review 2010-11 boundary cycle recommendations of boundary and grade level changes for schools in the numbered and entitled description.

This Project Modification contains the following items:



Item #1



CCD #3 in the amount of $78,131.00.
Description:  CCD #3 includes three items:
   1.  Change underside of bleacher finish from powder-coated to  galvanized steel ($43,000.00 credit).
   2.  Add roof hatch, stairs and handrails to create viewing area on top on press box and divide the press box into three compartments and add angled glass ($13,764.00.)
   3.  Add middle aisle and third vomitory to home side bleachers ($107,367.00).
Cause:  School District Requested
Justification:  Item #1 was requested by the SDPBC to reduce first-time costs with little impact on long - term maintenance costs.  Item #2 was requested by the school to provide additional space and better viewing for the press, scouts, and coaches.  Item #4 is safety driven.  It provides significantly improved egress for the  home-side bleachers.  There is an additional mid-aisle exit and the size of the exit is increased



 

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board ratify Project Modifications in the amount of $78,131.00 to W.G. Mills, Inc. for the Pahokee High School Stadium Project #1771-8275, and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to finalize and execute all related contract documents.

Contact:

Joseph Sanches (sanchesj@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)

Financial Impact:

The financial impact is $78,131.00.



The source of funds is the project budget as approved by the Board at the time of the Construction Contract Award.
