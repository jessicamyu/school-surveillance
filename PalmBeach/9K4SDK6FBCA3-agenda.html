This Project Modification contains the following items:



Item #1







  • CCD #4 for the addition of 253 calendar days.




  • Description:  Revise the substantial completion date from June 15, 2006 to February 23, 2007 and the final completion date from March 30, 2007 to December 8, 2007.  Student occupancy of the school, excluding the auditorium, was in August 2006 per the original schedule.  This change revises the substantial completion date to when the auditorium was completed.




  • Cause:  Unforeseen Conditions/Outside Agency/School District Requested.




  • Justification:  To minimize the impacts of hurricane Wilma, Florida Power & Light (FP&L) providing power to the school late, and the School District's request to complete Building 15 early, the School District approved James B. Pirtle's plan to delay work in the auditorium so that they could complete all other buildings in time for the August 2006 occupancy date.


Item #2







  • CCD #5 for the addition of 73 calendar days.




  • Description:  Revise the final completion date from December 8, 2007 to February 19, 2008.




  • Cause:  Outside Agency




  • Justification:  Late approval from the State of Florida Department of Community Affairs of the design documents delayed completion of the press box at the football stadium.


Item #3







  • CCD #6 credit in the amount of ($12,279,164.17).




  • Description:  Deduct Direct Material Purchases (DMP), Sales Tax Savings in excess of the guaranteed amount, and unused Construction Contingency from the contract with James B. Pirtle Construction Company, Inc.






    • DMP resulting in a deduct in the amount of ($11,892,447.15)




    • Additional Sales Tax Savings resulting in a deduct in the amount of ($213,547.81)




    • Construction Contingency deduct in the amount of ($173,169.21)




  • Cause:  School District Requested




  • Justification:  Final reconciliation of contract amount prior to project closeout.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board ratify Project Modifications for the addition of 326 calendar days and a credit of ($12,279,164.17) from James B. Pirtle Construction Company, Inc. for the John I. Leonard High School Modernization Project #1361-8218, and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to finalize and execute all contract related documents.

Contact:

Joseph M. Moore (moorej@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)
Joseph M. Sanches

Financial Impact:

The financial impact to the Project Budget is a credit of ($386,717.02).  The funds will be returned to Capital Contingency.
