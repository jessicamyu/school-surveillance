Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Amendment to the 2007-2008 High School Student Progression Plan



 





  • The purpose of the Amendment is to delineate what must be considered for a second semester examination exemption because a student showed significant progress on FCAT.


 





  • Non-retained and retained students will have the opportunity for an examination exemption.


 





  • Semester examination exemptions are listed by course.
