Choice Policy Workshop
Meeting
Apr 4, 2018 - 6. WORKSHOP immediately following Executive Session in the Board Room
Category
2. Workshop Items
Type
Discussion

DESCRIPTION:

Policy 5.016 for Choice Schools and Programs is necessary to meet requirements of Florida Statute 1001.41 stating that standards and policies must integrate career awareness, career exploration, and career and technical education. The purpose of the policy is to set forth the PBCSD Choice schools and programs that may be available to the School District's eligible students; the procedures for applying to these programs; and the eligibility requirements, Choice programs, magnet schools, special programs, and career academies. The Choice programs contracts and forms are included and are cited within the Choice Programs Procedures Manual and will be implemented along with the updated policy and procedures manual so that there is consistency among all District Choice and Career programs.

DESCRIPTION:

PUBLIC HEARING regarding proposed adoption of Instructional Materials. 

 

Pursuant to School Board Policy 8.122(3), a district instructional materials adoption committee was convened to evaluate and recommend new materials for sixteen elementary schools to adopt in the area of literacy in grade 3.  

 

The sixteen elementary schools are:

Banyan Creek

Barton

Del Prado

Diamond View

Indian Pines

Jupiter Farms

Pine Grove

South Olive

Starlight Cove

Sunrise Park

Verde

Waters Edge

Whispering Pines

 

The recommended materials are:
 

Grade 3 Core Knowledge Language Arts by Amplify Education

Unit 1 – Classic Tales

Unit 2 – Animal Classification

Unit 3 – The Human Body: Systems & Senses

Unit 5 – Light and Sound

Unit 8 – Native Americans: Regions & Cultures

Unit 9 – Early Explorations of North America

Unit 10 – Colonial America

Unit 11 – Ecology

 

Materials:  Each domain contains a teacher guide, activity books, and student reader (one copy for every student).
