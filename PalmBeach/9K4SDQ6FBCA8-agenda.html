  • K.S. filed a complaint in September of 2008 with the U.S. District Court, Southern District of Florida requesting reimbursement for private school placement based on an action against the School District brought under the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act.  The Administrative Law Judge in a prior Division of Administrative Hearings (DOAH) action made factual and legal findings that $137,789.29 was appropriate reimbursement for a violation of the Child Find Provision of IDEA (2004).




  • The Parties participated in mediation August 14, 2009, at which time K.S. sought reimbursement of $137,789.29 and requested an additional amount of $80,000.00 in attorneyâ€™s fees and costs.  The total amount sought by plaintiff at mediation in this action was $217,798.29.  The parties were able to reach an agreement for full settlement and release of all claims in this case for the total sum of $100,000.

Recommendation:

I recommend the Board approve the settlement and release in the matter of K.S. v. Palm Beach County School Board,  08-CV-80925-Hurley/Hopkins.
