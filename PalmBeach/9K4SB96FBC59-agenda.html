CS1 - Merger of Gulfstream Goodwill LIFE and Gulfstream Goodwill Transition Academies
Meeting
Apr 29, 2009 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Academics
Category
Consent
Type
Action (Consent)

The Governing Board for Gulfstream Goodwill Academies, Inc. submitted a letter to merge Gulfstream Goodwill LIFE and Gulfstream Goodwill Transition to become Gulfstream Goodwill LIFE (see Exhibit "A").



The applications for both Gulfstream Goodwill LIFE and Gulfstream Goodwill Transition were simultaneously renewed effective July 1, 2008 and the terms and the conditions in the two charter contracts are substantially similar.



Gulfstream Goodwill LIFE and Gulfstream Goodwill Transition are in compliance with their current charter.



Both schools serve students ages 14-22 Trainable Mentally Handicapped ("TMH") or Emotionally Handicapped ("EMH").  Gulfstream Goodwill LIFE will serve up to 400 youths and young adults with primary exceptionalities of TMH or Specific Learning Disabilities.



Gulfstream Goodwill Transition will be closed effective June 30, 2009.  The termination of the contract for Gulfstream Goodwill Transition and the merger will be effective July 1, 2009.  The students will have the option to: a) transfer to Gulfstream Goodwill LIFE; b) transfer to a different Charter School; or c) transfer to their home school. 



There are no Data Panels attached as both schools serve special needs students.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the merging of Gulfstream Goodwill LIFE Academy with Gulfstream Goodwill Transition Academy to become Gulfstream Goodwill LIFE Academy.

The School Board of Palm Beach County and Renaissance Learning Charter, Inc. ("RLC") entered into a ten (10) year Charter Agreement on July 1, 2002.  The Charter School currently serves Pre-K through age14, Exceptional Student Education ("ESE") /Autism.



On January 12, 2009, The Governing Board Chair of Renaissance Learning Center, Inc. submitted a letter to the School District requesting an increase in the cap of the number of students from 60 to 65 students over the life of the school (see Exhibit "A").



The addition of students will not compromise the current educational goals Renaissance Learning Center, Inc. offers its students and will enable the school to retain its valued staff.



RLC is fulfilling its mission and vision as outlined in its Charter.



See Fifth Amendment to Charter Agreement for Renaissance Learning Center, Inc. attached as Exhibit "B".



No data panels are attached.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Fifth Amendment for Renaissance Learning Center, Inc. Charter School.

The subscription agreement with Educational Options, Inc. is for the implementation of the Novel/Stars Course Curriculum as online courseware to assist the District meeting in the District goals of:



1. Student Credit Recovery
2. Student Credit Acceleration
3. Student Grade Forgiveness
4. Raising Student GPA
5. FCAT Preparation and /or Remediation
6. Academic Skill Remediation
7. Middle School to High School Transition



Educational Options, Inc. agrees to furnish the following:



- Subscription to the Novel/Stars course curriculua with all curricula upgrades and additions during the term of the contract for up to 1400 reusable slots or seats.



-Online textbooks if needed for a course



-Hosting of the service and Learning Management System
Technical Support



The District will continue to provide the following:



A contact person for the District
A site administrator
Online instructors



The subscription is for up to 1400 reusable slots using the Educational Options system at a cost of $55 per student slot, for a total cost of up to $77,000.



The amended subscription agreement period is February 21, 2009 through August 15, 2009.



 

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the amended subscription agreement with Educational Options, Inc. for the implementation of the Novel/Stars Course Curriculum effective February 21, 2009 through August 15, 2009, and authorize the Superintendent or designee to sign all related documents.
