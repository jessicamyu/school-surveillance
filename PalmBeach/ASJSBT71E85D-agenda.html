DESCRIPTION:

In FY2018, the District will renew our Accreditation with AdvancED for all schools, the District and our Early Learning programs.  The external review team will be in the District January 28-31 with Board Member interviews scheduled for Monday, January 29, 2018.  The purpose of the workshop is to provide an overview of the purpose, process and timeline for AdvancED Accreditation.
