Recommended Action
I recommend the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp. adopt a resolution amending Schedule 2007A and 2007B of the Master Lease and authorize the Superintendent and Board Chairman to finalize and execute all related documents.

DESCRIPTION:

  • This resolution authorizes the addition of the Gladeview Elementary Modernization and Rosenwald Elementary Modernization projects to the schedule 2007A-1, the ground lease for associated with the 2007A COP issue.
  • The resolution also authorizes an amendment to the 2007B-2 schedule to include the Gladeview Elementary Modernization project.  
  • The projects are under construction and scheduled to open in the fall of 2015.
  • Approval of this resolution is required for full compliance with our master lease agreement (COP funding program).
