Aug 4, 2010 - Legal Sessions
Category
Exceptions Hearings

The case is before the School Board for review of the Recommended Order, the Amended Exceptions of Sun Art, and the Amended Exception of the Superintendent.  Sun Art takes exception to a portion of the Recommended Order that states, in determining the lowest responsive and responsible bidder the School Board should find that failure of bidder to have signed its Revised Bid Summary Sheet constituted a minor irregularity not vitiating the bidder’s responsiveness.  Sun Art claims that this portion of the Administrative Law Judge’s recommendation is not supported by facts or by applicable law.  The Superintendent takes exception of the Recommended Order in that the decision of the Administrative Law Judge was neither supported by competent substantial evidence nor supported by Florida law.

Subject
Special Meeting re: Augustus Chappelle Final Order
Meeting
Aug 4, 2010 - Legal Sessions
Category
Special Meetings
Type
Action




  • The Superintendent made a recommendation that Augustus Chappelle be terminated for falsification of documents and insubordination.    




  • Mr. Chappelle timely asserted his right to an appeal before the Division of Administrative Hearings (DOAH).




  • On February 9, 2010 an Administrative Hearing was held before Judge Errol Powell regarding the termination of Augustus Chappelle.




  • On May 10, 2010, Judge Powell issued a Recommended Order that the Board enter a Final Order suspending Respondent without pay for fifteen (15) days and reinstating his employment as of the date of the expiration of his employment.




  • The Superintendent filed an Exception to the Recommended Order.




  • Upon review of the entire record and full hearing on the Exception, the Board voted to reject the Exception.




  • The Board, therefore, approved and adopted the Recommended Order of Judge Powell as the Final Order.




  • This Final Order is in the best interest of the School Board.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Final Order in the matter of School Board of Palm Beach County v. Augustus Chappelle, DOAH case No. 09-2926, a copy of which is attached hereto, and authorize the execution of all related documents.

Contact:

Bruce A. Harris, Esq.; Interim Chief Counsel



Vicki L. Evans-Pare, Esq.; Associate Counsel

Financial Impact:

The financial impact is $36,567.00 in gross back wages to be paid from the salary account.
