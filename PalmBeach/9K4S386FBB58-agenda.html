•            On January 23, 2008, the School Board approved the Post Closing Occupancy Agreement for the Riviera Beach Community Chapel located at 3031 Avenue “I”, Riviera Beach, that will be a part of the New Riviera Beach Area High School (02-MMM) on the future expanded site of Suncoast High School.  (See Exhibit "A")



 



•            The New Riviera Beach Area High School (02-MMM) is scheduled to open for the School Year 2014/2015.



 



•            Riviera Beach Community Chapel is a strong and important part of the Community.  The chapel provides free day care and after school care for the single parents and needy families in this community.  Without the Chapel’s assistance many of the parents would not be able to work.  The Chapel provides transportation for the sick, school supplies and clothing to those in need, food and financial help to the poor and spiritual and physical assistance to those that need help.



 



•            The Chapel has acquired another property in the area that is presently being renovated.



 



•            The Chapel would like to retain the use of the existing property through June 30, 2009, in order to provide continuous service to the neighborhood until the renovation of their replacement facility has been completed.



 



•            The Post Closing Occupancy Agreement required the Chapel to pay a monthly rental amount of $4,000.00.  The Chapel has paid the required amount through January 31, 2009.



 



 



 



•            As of December 1, 2009, the Riviera Beach Community Chapel shall permit the School District’s “ATOSS” program to exclusively occupy one classroom which can be partitioned off into three (3) classrooms, one small orientation room and one storage room, Monday through Friday, and to use on a non-exclusive basis the kitchen, playground, two bathrooms, parking lot and other common facilities on the property.



 



•            The Chapel will provide for “ATOSS” utilities, a phone and fax line up to $35.00 per month, maintenance and daily custodial services to the facilities including trash pick-up, stocking of soap and paper products in the restrooms.



 



•            The Chapel, as its sole expense, will be required to maintain commercial general liability insurance for the entire period of possession.



 



•            In exchange, the required monthly rental beginning December 1, 2008, will be $2,500.00 creating a $3,000.00 excess payment already received for December 2008 and January 2009.  Therefore, the $3,000.00 pre-paid excess will cover the Chapel’s February payment and $500.00 of the March payment.



 













 



 

Recommendation:











I recommend the School Board approve an Amendment to the Post Closing Occupancy Agreement for Riviera Beach Community Chapel located at 3031 Avenue “I”, Riviera Beach, adjacent to the existing Suncoast High School Campus as herein described; and authorize the Superintendent and Chairman to sign all necessary documents.
