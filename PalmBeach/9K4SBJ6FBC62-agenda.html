CLS1 - Polo Park Middle School Trip to Costa Rica
Meeting
Mar 9, 2011 - Special Meeting
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Action (Consent)

Six Science students will be chaperoned by a teacher.  The group will travel by air to San Jose, Costa Rica departing from Miami International Airport on May 3, 2011 and returning to Miami International Airport on May 10, 2011.  The cost of the trip is $1,716.00 per student which includes round trip airfare, hotel, meals, full time tour director and tours.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the school board approve an educational field trip for the Polo Park Science Department to travel to San Jose, Costa Rica.  The primary purpose of this trip is an educational one which will provide the students with an opportunity to enhance their knowledge about another country, its culture, traditions, history, natural biomes and use of the Spanish language.

Contact:

Judith Klinek, Chief Academic Officer (klinek@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)



Dr. Janis Andrews, West Area Superintendent



William Latson, Principal, Polo Park Middle School

Financial Impact:

The financial impact of $10,296.00 will be covered by the parents' of the students that will be attending the trip.  All funds will be paid directly to EF Tours by the parents.  There is no cost to the District.

Subject
Approval of settlement in Elfers v. School District
Meeting
Mar 9, 2011 - Special Meeting
Category
New Business
Type
Action (Consent)

This litigation was brought by a former employee, Thomas Elfers, who claimed that the School Board violated his statutory and constitutional rights to free speech resulting in his constructive discharge and/or the failure to hire him for two subsequent positions.  The case is currently pending in the 15th judicial circuit under case number CA03-0390AJ.



Settlement is recommended as being in the best financial interest of the district based upon the potential exposure and the uncertainty of the outcome of the litigation.  This agreement was reached after extended negotiations which began at a mediation conference on September 13, 2010, and culminated with this agreement which

represents the lowest amount the plaintiff would take to avoid the trial which was specially set to begin on March 29, 2011.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board  approve the settlement agreement between Thomas Elfers and the School District/Board of Palm Beach County, Florida and authorize the Chairman and Acting Superintendent to execute all related documents.

Contact:

Bruce A. Harris, Esq.; Chief Counsel



Robert A. Glassman, Esq.; Senior Counsel

Financial Impact:

The total amount of the Settlement to be paid during four fiscal years is $490,000.00.  This year’s payment is $115,000.00, and the balance will be paid in equal installments of $125,000.00, on October 1, of each year until the balance owed is paid.  This payment will come out of the general fund.
