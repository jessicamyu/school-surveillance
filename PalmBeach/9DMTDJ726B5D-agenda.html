I recommend the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp consider the annual election of officers.

DESCRIPTION:

The Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp is a Florida not-for-profit corporation that has as its sole member the School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida.  The corporation facilitates the School District’s lease purchase financing of school facilities, commonly referred to as COPs (or Certificates of Participation).  

 

A summary of the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp is attached.

 

The bylaws of the Leasing Corp require an annual meeting of the Board of Directors to elect corporate officers.  Palm Beach School Board Members or School District Staff may be elected to serve as officers.

Recommended Action
I recommend the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp adopt the resolution authorizing the conversion of interest rates modes with respect to the $115,350,000 aggregate principal amount of Certificates of Participation, Series 2002B and authorize execution of related documents.

DESCRIPTION:

  • This resolution authorizes the restructure of the 2002B COP issue from an Index Rate mode that will expire on 2/1/14 to another Index Rate mode or other mode such as a 7-day  VRDO (Variable Rate Demand Obligation).
  • The resolution also delegates authority to the Superintendent to negotiate the terms of the transaction with the underwriters or COP Purchasers within the parameters set forth in the resolution.  The resolution also authorizes the execution and delivery of all instruments and documents necessary to complete the transaction.
  • The 2002B COP issue was originally completed in 2002 as a variable rate transaction, specifically in a 7-day VRDO (Variable Rate Demand Obligation) mode. 
  • On August 1, 2011, the transaction was converted to an index rate mode.  The conversion eliminated credit risk and reduced the interest cost of the transaction.  The transaction was locked for a period of 30 months and would need to be restructured prior to February 1, 2014. 
  • The Finance Committee will review the transaction at the meeting on December 6, 2013.   A draft of the meeting minutes will be available online at: http://www.palmbeachschools.org/treasury/2013FinanceCommittee.asp

 

CONTACT:

Michael J. Burke, Chief Operating Officer (mike.burke@palmbeachschools.org)

FINANCIAL IMPACT:

There is no cost for the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp.  The cost of the conversion for the School Board is estimated not to exceed $257,000.  The cost of the transaction will included in the debt issue.  The costs are detailed in agenda item FMT4 to be considered by the School Board on December 18, 2013.
