DESCRIPTION:

The Office of the Chief of Staff will present the community input, revised Priority Student Outcomes, Themes, and Objectives for the District's next Strategic Plan. The suggested revisions are based on findings from the community input and the Board recommendations.
 
This workshop will provide members of the School Board with an opportunity to review the suggested revisions and provide their endorsement.  

 

*Attached is Appendix Strategic Plan Workshop #3.  Although not discussed in the presentation, community input/thoughts around the four strategic themes are shared.
