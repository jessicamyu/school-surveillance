COM1 Kravis Center for the Performing Arts 25th Anniversary
Meeting
Dec 7, 2016 - 5. SPECIAL MEETING for PRESENTATIONS at 5 p.m. Time Certain in the Board Room
Category
4. New Business
Type
Recognition

DESCRIPTION:

The Kravis Center for the Performing Arts is an excellent example of a community working together to achieve an ambitious goal. Twenty-five years after the opening, the Center continues to be a shining star, reflecting the commitment of Palm Beach County residents to the world of performing arts. The Center is also an outstanding partner to the District, serving as a venue for myriad District events, providing a wide cross-section of opportunities to District students, including access each season to nearly 50,000 students to a variety of Center performances.
