FM1 - COBI Bond Sale
Meeting
May 5, 2010 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Operations
Category
New Business
Type
Action (Consent)


  • COBI (Capital Outlay Bond Issue) bonds are issued by the State of Florida on behalf of schools and community colleges within the State of Florida.

  • The District last authorized a COBI in 2002 when the State of Florida required School District participation in order to receive Classrooms First funding.

  • The principal and interest will be paid by the State of Florida from motor vehicle license revenues.

  • The debt issue does not impact the District's capacity to issue or pay for COPs.

  • This was included in the current 5 year capital plan for FY11 with proceeds estimated at $11 million.

  • The funds can only be used for projects in the current ed plant survey and on the District's Project Priority List (PPL).

  • A revised PPL is attached.  The list includes district-wide projects that will be included in the new ed plant survey.

  • Based on the current 5 year plan and the current ed plant survey, the funds will be used for the construction of covered walkways.  Those projects are currently funded with a COP issue.  

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board authorize a resolution requesting the issuance by the State Board of Eduction of Florida of $9,700,000 of Capital Outlay Bonds and approve a revised Project Priority List. I further recomment the School Board authorize the Superintendent to sign all necessary documents.
