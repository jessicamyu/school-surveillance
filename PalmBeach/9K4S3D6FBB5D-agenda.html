I recommend that the School Board select a name for the new elementary school "03-X" from the list of submitted recommendations: 



1. Marsh Pointe Elementary School



Our school is situated on the northern most "pointe" of land off the Jog Road extension. The land is located on the edge of the Loxahatchee Slough (marsh) and neighbors in the Eastpointe and Old Marsh neighborhoods speak of the beautiful wildlife that surrounds them.





2. Sunshine Pointe Elementary School



Many students took the "sun" theme from the Spanish word "sol" in Mirasol because the school has been called the "school at Mirasol" since its inception.



(There were a lot of submissions with "sunset" and "sunrise" but there is already a "Sunrise Park Elementary" and the new 03Z has requested a first choice of "Sunset Palms Elementary" so we steered away from those choices.)





3. Sunny Ridge Elementary



Again, a "sol" based name that does not appear on any other school at this time.



The selection committee was comprised of the following:  Principal Maureen Werner, newly hired teachers, students and business partner Michele Jacobs, the Corporate Director of Marketing of the Forbes Company (owner of the Gardens Mall).

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board select a name for Elementary School "03-X" in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida from the list of submitted recommendations by the selection committee in order of preference.
