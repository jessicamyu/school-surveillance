CL1 - Discussion and Development of District Technology Policies - Technology Workshop
Meeting
Apr 14, 2010 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Academics
Category
New Business
Type
Action (Consent)

This workshop will focus on District Technology and the related policies that align and steer technology activities and actions to reach our District's Mission, goals, and Key Results.  The District's Instructional and Operational sectors heavily rely on technology for numerous functions including classroom instruction, human resource and financial management, communication, collaboration, research, and sharing with students, teachers, staff, parents, and the community.  However, the guiding tenets (policies) are outdated for today's district technologies which are the indispensable underpinnings for a multitude of daily instructional, operational, and tactical activities.  To steer this essential District investment, existing policies must be modernized, and a new technology manual and policies must be created.  This can only be accomplished with the crucial Board input and insight which will be gained by this workshop.



It is requested that the Board Members review the attached documents.  In reviewing the fourteen Questions and Staff Recommended Answers, the Board should note those which they want to pull for discussion at the workshop. Additionally, if these questions generate other thoughts, concerns, or matters for discussion they should be noted too.
