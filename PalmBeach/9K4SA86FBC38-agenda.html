P-1 Whispering Pines Elementary School Project Modification
Meeting
Apr 7, 2010 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Operations
Category
New Business
Type
Action

This Project Modification contains the following items:



Item #1





  • Architect Additional Services in the amount of $19,274.00.


  • Description: Relocate covered play area from east side of 5-classroom addition to the south side of the campus.


  • Cause: School District Requested


  • Justification: The School Board decided to cancel elementary school 05-C and to expand Whispering Pines ES.  The covered play was relocated to provide space for the future expansion.


Item #2





  • Architect Additional Services in the amount of $7,045.00


  • Description: Design a lift station for the sanitary sewer discharge from the covered play area.


  • Cause: School District Requested


  • Justification: The elevation of the covered play area along with the distance to the nearest tie-in point in the sanitary sewer system did not allow for a gravity discharge line.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve Project Modifications in the amount of $26,319.00 to Pierce Goodwin Alexander & Linville, Inc. for the Whispering Pines Elementary School Classroom Addition Project #1781-8307, and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to finalize and execute all related contract documents.

Contact:

Joseph Sanches (sanchesj@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)

Financial Impact:

The financial impact to the Project's Budget is $26,319.00.



The source of funds is the project budget as approved by the Board in the FY 2010-2014 5-Year Plan.

T1 - Discussion and Development of District Technology Policies
Meeting
Apr 7, 2010 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Operations
Category
New Business
Type
Action (Consent)

This workshop will focus on District Technology and the related policies that align and steer technology activities and actions to reach our District's Mission, goals, and Key Results.  The District's Instructional and Operational sectors heavily rely on technology for numerous functions including classroom instruction, human resource and financial management, communication, collaboration, research, and sharing with students, teachers, staff, parents, and the community.  However, the guiding tenets (policies) are outdated for today's district technologies which are the indispensable underpinnings for a multitude of daily instructional, operational, and tactical activities.  To steer this essential District investment, existing policies must be modernized, and a new technology manual and policies must be created.  This can only be accomplished with the crucial Board input and insight which will be gained by this workshop.



It is requested that the Board Members review the attached documents.  In reviewing the fourteen Questions and Staff Recommended Answers, the Board should note those which they want to pull for discussion at the workshop. Additionally, if these questions generate other thoughts, concerns, or matters for discussion they should be noted too.

SUMMARY OF Advisory Boundary Committee (ABC)



2010-2011 Cycle



BOUNDARY CHANGE PROPOSAL



Presentation to Superintendent on December 3, 2009



First Reading and Tentative Adoption by Board on January 13, 2010



 



PL-1  Boundary Study P-1 Amended (Palm Beach Public Elementary)







  • Study P-1 was recommended to the Superintendent on October 29, 2009 with a 12-3 vote by the Advisory Boundary Committee.




  • Study P-1 Amended was approved by the Board on January 13, 2010 at the first reading and tentative adoption.




  • Proposed changes provide relief to Palm Beach Public Elementary School and includes adjustments to Meadow Park, Palmetto, Roosevelt, and  Westward elementary schools:




                                          i.    Moves SACs 154, 155 (part), 400 (part) from Palm Beach Public Elementary to Palmetto Elementary.  (Study P-1 Amended removes the neighborhoods of Prospect Park and Southland Park from the original Study P-1.  These neighborhoods will remain at Palm Beach Public School.)   



                                         ii.    Moves SAC 142 from Palm Beach Public Elementary to Roosevelt Elementary.



                                        iii.    Moves SAC 382 from Westward Elementary to Roosevelt Elementary.



                                        iv.    Moves SAC 185 from Palmetto Elementary to Meadow Park Elementary.



 



Current 4th grade students would have the option to remain at Palm Beach Public, Palmetto, or Westward as 5th graders without transportation;  

Current gifted or strings students in proposed change areas have the option to remain.
