DESCRIPTION:

The retention of the law firms of Wagstaff & Cartmell, LLP and The Maher Law Firm (“Law Firms”) will be discussed in order to evaluate the viability of and potential prosecution of a civil action against the manufacturers and/or distributors and/or sellers of electronic cigarettes and vaping products (“Potential Defendants”), including filing suit on the Board’s behalf seeking recovery for damages arising out of the marketing, distribution and sale of electronic cigarettes and vaping products to students.
