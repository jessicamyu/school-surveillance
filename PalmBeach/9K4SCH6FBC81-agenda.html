Tentative Agreement between the School District and the Palm Beach County Police Benevolent Association (PBA)
Meeting
Jul 29, 2009 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Operations
Category
Consent
Type
Action

The Tentative Agreement provides for:



·         A new three-year successor contract effective January 1, 2009, through December 31, 2011, with limited reopener provisions for salary and a stipulated number of additional items for year two (2010), and year three (2011);



·         Continuing the January 1, 2008 Salary Schedules through December 31, 2009, and the base salaries of bargaining unit employees in effect December 31, 2008, through December 31, 2009.



·         Granting employees the option of electing to accumulate compensatory time in lieu of overtime pay, up to an annual non-reoccurring maximum of twenty (20) hours.



·         Clarifying the definition of a grievance and transferring from the Director of Employee Relations to the Department of Labor Relations, the responsibility of responding to grievances at Level II of the Grievance Procedure, after appeal to the Chief of Police is unsuccessful.  All arbitration proceedings shall be conducted in accordance with rules established by the Federal mediation & Conciliation Service.



·         Increasing the number of Union Representatives from two to three, in any one instance, and to use on-duty time for the purpose of conducting PBA business with the School District, at the discretion of the Chief of Police.



·         Clarifying the procedure for Involuntary Transfer in that employees shall be provided advanced written notice before the effective date of the involuntary transfer



·         Implementing the procedure for timely payment of out-of-county travel reimbursements.



·         Clarifying that employees not provided with a Department-issued vehicle will not be dispatched during the work day to another school or non-school site.



·         Granting a full-time, non-probationary employee who does not utilize any paid or unpaid leave during the calendar year, a One Thousand ($1,000) dollar Attendance Incentive paid in the following year over the number of pay periods during that calendar year.



·         Granting an employee who is permanently assigned, full-time to the Glades Area, an annual Glades Area Supplement:      0 - 2 Years of Service        $3,000



                          2 – 8 Years of Service       $4,500



                          9+ Years of Service           $5,500





  • Granting the transfer of Sick Leave to another regular full-time employee in the same bargaining unit, in whole day increments.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Tentative Agreement for a new three-year successor contract (January 1, 2009 – December 31, 2011) between the Palm Beach County Police Benevolent Association and the School District of Palm Beach County.  PBA ratified the Tentative Agreement on July 10, 2009, with a vote of 35 for, and 12 against.

Contact:

Joseph M. Moore (Joseph Moore); James Hayes, Jr. (James Hayes, Jr.); Van V. Ludy (Van Ludy)

Financial Impact:

The financial impact to the District for FY10 is approximately $29,000.
