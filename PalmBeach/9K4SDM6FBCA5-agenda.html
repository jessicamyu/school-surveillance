  • Lake Worth Drainage District

    (LWDD) is requesting the School Board grant an exclusive easement for +/-

    .043 acres, a portion of West

    Palm Beach     Area

    Elementary School

    (O6-D), for the purpose of accessing and maintaining Canal E-3 (See

    Exhibit A), (1).




 





  • LWDD is also requesting the

    School Board grant an exclusive easement for +/- .28 acres, a portion

    of West Palm Beach

    Area Elementary

    School     (O6-D), for the purpose of accessing

    and maintaining Canal L-2 (See Exhibit A), (2).




 





  • LWDD will issue a permit to

    connect the 06-D surface water management system to the E-3 and L-2 canals

    upon receipt of easement.




 





  • Granting of an exclusive easement

    and confirmation of ownership requires Board approval (See Exhibit B).




 





Recommendation:





 





I recommend

the School Board approve the Easement Deed between Lake Worth Drainage District

and the School Board of Palm Beach County located on Stacy Street for the West Palm Beach Area

ES (O6-D), and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to sign all necessary

documents.
