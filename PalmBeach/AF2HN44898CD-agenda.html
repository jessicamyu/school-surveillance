Recommended Action
I recommend the Board approve November 14-18, 2016 as School Psychology Awareness Week.

DESCRIPTION:

The School District of Palm Beach recognizes the vital role that school psychologists play in the personal and academic development of Palm Beach County’s children. They facilitate collaboration and help parents and educators identify and reduce risk factors; create effective, caring schools; access needed community resources; and implement research-driven prevention and intervention strategies. This proclamation recognizes these contributions and acknowledge the positive impact our professionals play in the preparation and success of our children. Employees will also be recognized for earning the Petway Award, Early Career Award, and Outstanding Employee Award.
