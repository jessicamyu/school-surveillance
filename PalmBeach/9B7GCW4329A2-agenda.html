Recommended Action
I recommend the School Board adopt the Resolution determining revenues and millages levied for FY 2014.

DESCRIPTION:  Chapter 200.065(2)(f)3 of the Florida Statutes requires that the School District hold a public hearing to adopt a millage rate within 80 days of certification of value, but earlier than 65 days after certification.

 

The total millage rate 7.586 which raises $1,009,809,565 in local tax revenues for FY 2014.

 

 

CONTACT:

Shirley Knox (Shirley.Knox@palmbeachschools.org)

 

 

FINANCIAL IMPACT:  The financial impact to the District is $1,009,809,565 in local tax revenues for FY 2014.
