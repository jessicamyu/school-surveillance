Special Meeting re: Nguzo Saba Corrective Action and/or Termination
Meeting
Jul 28, 2010 - Legal Sessions
Category
Special Meetings
Type
Discussion






  • On June 9.2010 the Board held an informal hearing regarding the Superintendent’s recommendation to terminate the JLNS Charter School charter contract.




  • The Board unanimously voted to allow the school to enter into a CAP with the District to remedy the issues presented by staff.




  • Staff has developed the attached proposed CAP.




  • Staff has met with the school’s representatives to review the proposed CAP.




  • The School has generally agreed to all but three of the provisions proposed in the CAP.




  • The School requested further time to discuss the CAP with the Charter School Board.




  • Staff has scheduled a meeting to finalize the CAP for Monday, July26, 2010 with the schools’ representatives.




  • The results of that final meeting and the final CAP will be forwarded to the Board at the conclusion of that meeting.


 

Recommendation:

I recommend that the Board stay the termination of the charter pending final negotiation of a corrective action plan, if agreed to by the parties.
