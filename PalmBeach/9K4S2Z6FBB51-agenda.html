Changes made to the Consultant Contract are as follows:



 



 



The Consultant shall perform the following services: Legal Advisor shall provide FCC regulatory and transactional advice to the School District with respect to Educational Broadband Service Spectrum, television and radio.



 



This amendment is to increase the maximum payment amount an additional $235,000 and extend the term of the contract through June 30, 2009.



 



Staff and Chief Counsel need an attorney with expertise in FCC regulatory matters, spectrum use and valuation issues to assist staff in the evaluation of the alternatives available to the School Board.



 



The firm of Fish & Richardson and Attorney Ed Lavergne have provided excellent service since being hired in March 2005. Approval of the funds requested in this amendment will allow Mr. Lavergne to provide staff with a sound basis for making future decisions regarding the acquisition, disposal and/or leasing of FCC licenses held by the School Board.

Recommendation:

I recommend award of Amendment No. 7 for the Consultant Contract with Fish & Richardson for $235,000, and extend the term of the contract through June 30, 2009, and authorize the Superintendent and Board Chairman to sign all needed documents.

Contact:

Gerald A. Williams, Esq.; Chief Counsel

Financial Impact:



Authorized Amount this Request: $235,000



Previously Authorized Amount Year-to-Date: $75,000



Total Authorized Amount if Request Approval: $310,000
