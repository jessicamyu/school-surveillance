Subject
FINAL ORDER DISMISSING GRACE & NAEEM UDDIN, INC.’S PETITION AND REQUEST FOR FORMAL HEARING (FORMAL WRITTEN PROTEST OF DECISION TO REJECT ALL BIDS) WITH PREJUDICE - EMERGENCY ITEM
Meeting
Feb 24, 2010 - Legal Sessions
Category
Special Meetings
Type
Action

On February 3, 2010, the School District posted its recommendation to reject all bids submitted in response to the Invitation to Bid for the Spanish River HS Fire Alarm Replacement project, Project No.:  1681-9503.



 



The Notice of rejection of all bids regarding the Spanish River HS Fire Alarm Replacement project, Project No. 1681-9503 contained the following statement:  “Failure to file a protest within the time prescribed in Section 120.57(3), Florida Statues, or failure to post the bond or other security required by law within the time allowed for filing a bond shall constitute a waiver of proceedings under Chapter 120, Florida Statutes.



 



On February 8, 2010, Grace & Naeem Uddin, Inc. (Petitioner) filed a Notice of Protest of the School Board’s rejection of all bids for the Spanish River HS Fire Alarm Replacement project, Project No.:  1681-9503 with the Director of the Purchasing Department of the School District of Palm Beach County.



 



On February 12, 2010, the Petitioner faxed a petition entitled Grace & Naeem Uddin, Inc.’s Petition and Request for Formal Hearing (Formal Written Protest of Decision to Reject All Bids), without exhibits, to the attorney for the School Board. 



 



On February 15, 2010, the Petitioner filed a petition entitled Grace & Naeem Uddin, Inc.’s Petition and Request for Formal Hearing (Formal Written Protest of Decision to Reject All Bids) (the Petition or formal protest) with the Director of the Purchasing Department of the School District of Palm Beach County.



 



Inserted in between Exhibit F and Exhibit G of the Exhibits of Petitioner’s Petition is a Bid Protest Bond No.: SU1039375.  Bid Protest Bond No.: SU1039375 is a bond that was originally submitted in a separate and pending proceeding with the State of Florida Division of Administrative Hearings, Case No. 10-0326BID.



 



The Petitioner failed to post a valid bond or other security required by law with its February 15, 2010 formal protest.

Recommendation:

 



I recommend the School Board issue the Final Order Dismissing Grace & Naeem Uddin, Inc.’s Petition and Request for Formal Hearing (Formal Written Protest of Decision to Reject all Bids) with Prejudice with regard to the Spanish River HS Fire Alarm Replacement project, Project No.:  1681-9503 and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to finalize and sign all necessary documents.



 



Approval of this Agenda Item is necessary at this time in order to comply with the statutory provisions of Chapter 120, Florida Statutes, which sets forth the requirements for dismissal of a petition.

Contact:

Gerald Williams, Chief Counsel
