Recommended Action
I recommend the Board adopt the resolution determining revenues and millages levied for fiscal year 2022. This resolution by the School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida, levies a total millage rate of 6.875 mils which raises $1,550,213,494 in local revenue. The millage is comprised of 3.621 mills for required local effort, 0.006 mills for prior period required local effort adjustment, .748 mills for discretionary operating, 1.500 mills for local capital improvement, and 1.000 mills for additional operating funds. This millage is 2.52 percent more than the rolled back rate.

DESCRIPTION:

Chapter 200.065(2)(f)3 of the Florida Statutes requires that the School District hold a public hearing to adopt a millage rate within 80 days of certification of value, but not earlier than 65 days after certification. The total millage rate being levied is 6.875 mills which raises $1,550,213,494 in local revenues.
