Special Meeting re: C.F. v. Cynthia Alexandre, et. al Settlement
Meeting
Mar 24, 2010 - Legal Sessions
Category
Special Meetings
Type
Action

The School Board had previously agreed to resolve this matter by the payment of $32,500.00, contingent on the Statute (Pledge of Allegiance) in question being found unconstitutional.  By settling earlier, the School Board limited its exposure to attorneyâ€™s fees and the cost of defending the case on appeal.  The trial court found the statute unconstitutional, and the State of Florida appealed.  The appellate court affirmed in part and reversed in part, resulting in the case being returned to the trial court for further action.  This action will conclude the pending litigation between the plaintiff and the School Board for the amount it had previously agreed to pay, but which had not been paid pending the appeal.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve the Settlement Agreement in the case of C.F. v. Cynthia Alexandre, et. al (05-81142 CIV-RYSKAMP/VITUNAC) and authorize the payment of $32,500 to the plaintiff in exchange for a release and agreement to have the Court enter an Order of Dismissal with prejudice.

Financial Impact:
