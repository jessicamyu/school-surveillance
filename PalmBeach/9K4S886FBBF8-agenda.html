SM2 - Special Meeting re: Settlement Agreement Capital Business Credit v. SBPBC
Meeting
Jul 14, 2010 - Legal Sessions
Category
Special Meetings
Type
Action (Consent)




  • Glades Central HS ordered 120 uniforms from FG Athletics. 




  • The uniforms were delivered, and then subsequently returned to the vendor.  In the meantime, the vendor sold the accounts receivable to Capital Business Credit.




  • The case commenced on or about February 19, 2009, when Capital Business Credit filed a Complaint against the School District asserting that the High School breached an Acknowledgement Agreement by failing to pay for the uniforms.




  • The Parties to the litigation, and the vendor FG Athletics, expressly understand and agree to a compromise of disputed claims and their Settlement Agreement shall not be construed as, or deemed to be, evidence of an admission or concession of any fault or liability whatsoever on the part of any Party.




  • The attached Settlement Agreement is submitted for School Board approval.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approves the attached Settlement Agreement in the matter of  Capital Business Credit, LLC v. The School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida, Case No. 502009CC002621XXXXMB, a copy of which is attached hereto, and authorize the execution of all related documents.

Contact:

Bruce A. Harris, Esq.; Senior Counsel



Kalinthia R. Dillard, Esq.; Associate Counsel

Financial Impact:

The total settlement is $11,000.00.
