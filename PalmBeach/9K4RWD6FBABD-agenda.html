On December 12, 2007 at a special School Board Meeting on Survivors Charter Schools, the Board voted to conduct an evidentiary hearing in the matter based on the remand from the Fourth District Court of Appeal, also voted that Board member Dr. Monroe Benaim would hold the hearing on the Board’s behalf, and further voted to retain outside counsel to advise the Board concerning the hearing. 



 



The Chief Counsel has selected the law firm of Weiss, Handler, Angelos & Cornwell, P.A. to handle this case.  Henry B. Handler, Esq., would be the principal attorney at that law firm providing the legal services.  Based upon an estimated budget, $30,000 is being requested at this time.

Recommendation:

I recommend award of the consultant contract with the Law Firm of Weiss, Handler, Angelos & Cornwell, P.A. in relation to the evidentiary hearing concerning Survivors Charter Schools, Inc. and authorize the Superintendent and Board Chairman to sign all needed documents.

Contact:

Gerald A. Williams, Esq. ( williamsg@palmbeach.k12.fl.us )

Financial Impact:

$30,000 from the Legal Services Budget
