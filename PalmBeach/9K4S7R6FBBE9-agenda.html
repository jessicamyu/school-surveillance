Informal Hearing re: Joseph Littles Nguzo Saba Charter School Termination
Meeting
Apr 7, 2010 - Legal Sessions
Category
Special Meetings
Type
Action
The Joseph Littles Nguzo Saba Charter School will be recommended for termination of its charter after having numerous compliance and financial issues which have gone unresolved.  The school was notified in writing by the District of the proposed action on February 26, 2010.  Under Florida Statutes, a charter school is entitled to an informal hearing before the sponsor prior to a final vote on the recommendation.  The school timely requested such a hearing.  This hearing is designed to ensure the administration of due process.

Recommendation:

I hereby recommend that the Board commence an informal hearing regarding the proposed termination of the Joseph Littles Nguzo Saba Charter School.

Dr. Janice Cover, Assistant Superintendent Quality Assurance
