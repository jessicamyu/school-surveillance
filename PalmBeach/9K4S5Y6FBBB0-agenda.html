P1   The current banking services contracts expire on November 22, 2008. 



Due to the unusual conditions in the banking industry, staff recommended that we negotiate an additional year with an optional one-year renewal. 



The District’s current banks as well as other competing banks have agreed that it is in the best interest of the School District to extend current banking contracts at this time.  The District’s Investment Advisor has also agreed that an RFP would not be an effective tool in this environment. 



The Finance Committee approved this recommendation with a unanimous vote at their meeting on October 17, 2008.



Once the financial markets have stabilized, an RFP will be issued to ensure the District is receiving the required services at the most competitive prices possible.



There are two portions of the banking services contracts. 



1.      The general banking services are handled by Bank of America.  Lee County Schools issued an RFP and received responses in April 2008.  Bank of America has agreed to honor the existing contract with Palm Beach Schools with pricing reduced to match those offered to Lee County Schools.  This will reduce the annual fees for general banking services by approximately $19,000 per year.



2.      The custody services were originally awarded to First Union/Wachovia Bank.  Wachovia subsequently sold that portion of their business to US Bank.  US Bank has agreed to honor the original terms of the contract as awarded in 2002.



The contract with Bank of America, for general banking services, includes pricing for our school’s internal accounts.  The contract also includes special programs available to all District Employee such as free or discounted banking, teacher loan programs and special mortgage programs.



 



 



 

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Banking Contracts for general banking and custody services with Bank of America and US Bank.   

Contact:

Joseph Moore, MooreJ@palmbeach.k12.fl.us



Leanne Evans, Treasurer

Financial Impact:

The estimated cost of general banking services is $100,000 per year.  This is funded from the treasury and school food service department budgets. 



The estimated cost of custody services is $25,000 per year.  This is funded from the treasury department budget.
