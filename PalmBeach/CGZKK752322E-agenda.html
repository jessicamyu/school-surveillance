Recommended Action
I recommend the Board adopt the resolution determining revenues and millages levied for fiscal year 2023. This resolution by the School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida, levies a total millage rate of 6.519 mils which raises $1,797,821,227 in local revenue. The millage is comprised of 3.265 mills for Required Local Effort, 0.0060 mills for Prior Period RLE Adjustment, 0.748 mills for Discretionary Operating Funds, 1.500 mills for Local Capital Improvement, and 1.000 mills for Additional Operating Funds. This millage is 14.38 percent more than the rolled back rate.

DESCRIPTION:

Chapter 200.065(2)(f)3 of the Florida Statutes requires that the School District hold a public hearing to adopt a millage rate within 80 days of certification of value, but not earlier than 65 days after certification. The total millage rate being levied is 6.519 mills which raises $1,797,821,227 in local revenues. 

 

CONTACT:

Heather Frederick, C.P.A., Chief Financial Officer (Heather.Frederick@palmbeachschools.org)

 

FINANCIAL IMPACT:

The financial impact to the District is $1,797,821,227 in local tax revenues for FY 2023.
