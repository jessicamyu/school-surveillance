Recommended Action
I recommend the Board approve the Amended FY21 Equitable Sharing Agreement with modifications from the document approved at the September 22, 2021, Board meeting, and authorize the Superintendent and Board Chair to execute all necessary documents.

DESCRIPTION:

The Equitable Sharing Agreement and Certification is the annual financial report required for the Palm Beach County School District Police participation in the US Department of Justice Equitable Sharing Program (USDJESP).  Signatures are required from the Law Enforcement Agency Head and the Governing Body Head.  Once signed, the report was filed online electronically by the Budget Department.  However, $2,361.26 originally deemed to be forfeiture funds were removed at the request of the USDJESP.  Once signed, the amended report will be filed online electronically by the Budget Department.

Recommended Action
I recommend the Board approve and adopt Resolution No. 2021-12-08 PIR-1 changing the boundaries of the School Board Member Residence Areas ("Scenario 2021 - Revised"), and authorize the Board Chair and Superintendent to sign all necessary documents.

DESCRIPTION:

  • After four public meetings, the School Board approved "Scenario 2021 - Revised" (maps attached) on November 3, 2021, and directed staff to bring back a Resolution for adoption on December 8, 2021. Links to those School Board meetings are provided below:

    • May 5, 2021 - 1st School Board Workshop - 2020 Census Data Review & Redistricting Process

    • August 18, 2021 - Regular School Board Meeting - Adoption of Redistricting Criteria

    • October 6, 2021 - 2nd School Board Redistricting Workshop - Base Scenario 2021

    • November 3, 2021 - Special Meeting - Scenario 2021 Revised
       

  • All meetings and related documents have been posted on the District's website: www.palmbeachschools.org/school_board/school_board_member_redistricting_information
     
  • In preparing the Legal Descriptions for the resolution, staff refined the population and demographic data resulting in de minimus changes to the data tables. The overall population deviation between the seven Board Member Districts in "Scenario 2021- Revised" is 1.22 percent.
     
  • The approved and executed Resolution will be recorded with the Clerk of the Circuit Court, transmitted to the Secretary of State, and advertised in the local newspaper, as required by F.S.§1001.36.
