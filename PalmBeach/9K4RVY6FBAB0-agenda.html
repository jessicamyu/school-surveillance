Subject
Conversion of Interest Rate Modes for Certificates of Participation, Series 2007B
Meeting
Jan 30, 2008 - Spec Mtg re COPS Restructuring
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Discussion


  • This resolution authorizes the conversion of the 2007B COP issue from 7-day Auction Rate mode to another mode, such as a 7-day VRDO (Variable Rate Demand Obligation) mode or a term mode not to exceed 5 years.

  • The resolution also delegates authority to the Superintendent to negotiate the terms of the transaction with the underwriters within the parameters set forth in the resolution.  The resolution also authorizes the execution and delivery of all instruments and documents necessary to complete the transaction.

  • The 2007B COP issue was originally completed in March 2007 as a variable rate transaction, specifically in an "Auction Rate" mode.  That market is no longer efficient, causing the cost of borrowing to increase. The original documents contemplated and provided for conversion among interest rate modes.

  • The costs to date are still within the amount budgeted, but all indications are that interest costs will continue to be higher than expected.  This action will transition the 2007B COP issue to a more efficient market.

  • The Finance Committee reviewed the transaction at meetings on January 7 and January 30, 2008.  Drafts of the meeting minutes as well as the presentations on this topic are available online at: http://www.palmbeach.k12.fl.us/FinMan/FINANCIALDIVISIONS/Treasury/nTreasury%20FinCom.htm

  • Some of the attachments to the authorizing resolution are forthcoming from bond and underwriter's counsel and will be available in the Board Minutes Office prior to this meeting.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board adopt a resolution authorizing the conversion of interest rates modes with respect to the $119,400,000 aggregate principal amount of Certificates of Participation, Series 2007B and authorize execution of related documents.

Contact:

Joseph M. Moore (moorej@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)



Michael J. Burke (burkem@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)



Leanne Evans (evansl@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)



 

Financial Impact:

The cost of the conversion is estimated not to exceed $790,000.  Most of the parties involved in the transaction are reducing their fees below our contracted amounts to complete this transaction.  The cost of the transaction should be offset by a reduction of the borrowing costs during the next 6-8 months.  The cost of the transaction will be paid from the COPs lease payment budget as approved in the capital budget.

Subject
Leasing Corp - Annual Election of Officers
Meeting
Jan 30, 2008 - Spec Mtg re COPS Restructuring
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Action (Consent)


  • The Leasing Corporation is a Florida not-for-profit corporation that has as its sole member the School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida.  The corporation facilitates the School District's lease purchase financing of school facilities, commonly referred to as COPs (Certificates of Participation).

  • The bylaws of the Leasing Corp require an annual meeting of the Board of Directors to elect corporate officers.  Palm Beach School Board Members or School District Staff may be elected to serve as officers.

  • The current officers are as follows:



    • President - William Graham, School Board Chair

    • Vice President - Dr. Monroe Benaim, School Board Vice-Chair

    • Secretary - Dr. Art Johnson, Superintendent

    • Assistant Secretary - Joseph Moore, Chief Operating Officer

    • Treasurer - Leanne Evans, Treasurer


  • The Proposed Officers are as follows:



    • President - William Graham, School Board Chair

    • Vice President - Paulette Burdick, School Board Vice Chair

    • Secretary - Dr. Art Johnson, Superintendent

    • Assistant Secretary - Joseph Moore, Chief Operating Officer

    • Treasurer - Leanne Evans, Treasurer

Recommendation:

I recommend the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp consider the annual election of officers.
