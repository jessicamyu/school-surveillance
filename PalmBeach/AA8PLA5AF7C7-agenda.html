Jun 1, 2016 - 4. SPECIAL MEETING immediately following Workshop in Board Room

Recommended Action
I recommend the Board approve the proposed one-year renewal of the Charter Contact for Lakeside Academy and authorize the Superintendent and/or his designee to sign all related contracts and documents.

DESCRIPTION:

On June 22, 2011, Lakeside Academy was granted a five (5) year Charter Contract to serve students in grades K-6.  This Contract expires on June 30, 2016.

 

The mission of the school is, “To provide a thorough and efficient education for at-risk elementary students in rural western Palm Beach County.  In order for the students to reach their fullest potential, the vision is to offer opportunities for academic, social and emotional growth so that students develop into productive and positive members of society.”

 

Lakeside Academy received a school grade of “F” for the school year 2014-15, and therefore must revise the school’s School Improvement Plan to address deficiencies resulting in the school grade.  The Annual Review of Lakeside Academy was conducted by the Charter Department on October 27, 2015, and significant deficiencies were noted in several areas including Curriculum, Instruction and Finance; with lesser deficiencies noted in ELL, ESE, and Personnel. Lakeside has taken corrective actions to cure some of the deficiencies of the Annual Review and made progress as demonstrated in the Program Renewal Review conducted by the Charter Department on February 8, 2016.

 

A recommendation could not be made to extend the Charter Renewal beyond one (1) year with deficiencies remaining on the Program Renewal Review.   It will be necessary for Lakeside Academy to develop and submit a school improvement plan, for approval by the District, to improve student performance.

I recommend the Board approve adoption of the forthcoming State Board of Education Final Order in Palm Beach Collegiate, Inc. vs. School Board of Palm Beach County, per the Charter School Statute specifically sec. 1002.33(6)(d) F.S. regarding the Denial of Application of Palm Beach Collegiate, Inc., authorize the Superintendent and/or designee to sign all related documents, and authorize the General Counsel to defend any appeal to the Fourth District Court of Appeal that the Applicant may file.

DESCRIPTION:

On November 4, 2015, the School Board voted unanimously to deny the application of Palm Beach Collegiate, Inc. and the Charter Applicant filed its appeal on December 11, 2015. On February 24, 2016, the Charter School Appeals Commission met and heard the Appeal and the Commission voted  4-0 to recommend the State Board of Education deny the Appeal of the Charter Applicant. 

 

The State Board of Education Appeal Hearing regarding the School Board's denial of the application of Palm Beach Collegiate, Inc.,was held on May 20, 2016. The ruling at the State Board of Education at the Appeal Hearing was to deny the Appeal and uphold the School Board's decision to deny the application. The Final Order from the State Board of Education is forthcoming and per the Charter School Statute, specifically sec. 1002.33(6)(d),F.S., the School Board is required to act upon the decision of the State Board of Education within thirty (30) calendar days after it is received. Therefore, I recommend that the School Board adopt the forthcoming State Board of Education Final Order and authorize the Superintendent and/or designee to sign all related documents. In the event, the Applicants, Palm Beach Collegiate, Inc. exercise their right to seek Judicial Review of the Final Order pursuant to Florida Rules of Appellate Procedure 9.030(b)(1) (c) and 9.110 [The Appeal must be filed within "thirty (30) days of rendition of the Order to be reviewed"], to authorize the General Counsel to defend the Appeal of the Final Order with the Fourth District of Appeals.
