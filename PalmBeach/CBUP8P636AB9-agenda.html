DESCRIPTION:

In partnership with Greenway Strategy Group, the Chief of Staff's Office will present proposed student priority outcomes, themes, and objectives for the District's next Strategic Plan. The proposed student priority outcomes, themes, and objectives were drafted based on findings from the Phase 1 Feedback Survey, Phase 2 Focus Groups, direction provided by the new Superintendent and his Executive Cabinet, and the District's Mission, Vision, and Equity Statement.
 
This workshop will provide members of the School Board with an opportunity to review the proposed student priority outcomes, themes, and objectives. Students and the community will have the opportunity to provide input, scheduled to take place from March 24 - April 6. We will use feedback generated from the Board workshop and student and community engagement to refine the proposed student priority outcomes, themes, and objectives. The anticipated School Board adoption will take place in April.
