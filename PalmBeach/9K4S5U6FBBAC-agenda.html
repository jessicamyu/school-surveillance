CP-1 Construction Management at Risk Agreement for Plumosa Elementary School Modernization at Seacrest
Meeting
Mar 26, 2008 - Spec Mtg re Construction Projects
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Discussion

Construction Management at Risk Services required for Preconstruction Phase Services including design review and recommendation to establish a Guaranteed Maximum Price for construction of a 87,403 GSF, 670 new student stations plus remodeling 14,129 GSF cafeteria. The design requirements will also incorporate the demolition of the existing school to accommodate construction of the new facility. Construction will be in accordance with the permitted design documents for student occupancy January 2010. 



 



Estimated Budget for Construction: $22,475,056.



 



Construction Type: To be determined during Preconstruction Phase



No. of Stories: To be determined during Preconstruction Phase.



 



Gross Square Feet: 87,403 new and 14,129 remodeled



Budgeted Construction Cost/Gross Square Foot: $221.



 



Total New Student Stations: 670



Budgeted Construction Cost/Student Station: $33,545.



 



Preconstruction Phase Services Fee: $121,808.



Reimbursable Expenses:                     $54,250. (not-to-exceed)



Total:                                                  $176,058.



 



Thirteen (13) firms responded to the RFP of which three (3) were shortlisted. The final ranking of



the Selection Committee is as follows:



 



(1) Suffolk Construction Company, Inc.



(2) Hedrick Brothers Construction Company, Inc.



(3) W.G. Mills, Inc.



 



Minority Sub-consultant Participation: 15%

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve an Agreement for Construction Management at Risk Services and Preconstruction Phase Services Fee in the amount of $121,808. and Reimbursable Expenses not to exceed $54,250. to Suffolk Construction Company, Inc., and authorize the Superintendent and Board Chairman to sign all required documents for Pre-Construction fees for Plumosa Elementary School Modernization Project No. 0862-7507.

Contact:

Joseph M. Moore (moorej@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)



Joseph M. Sanches (sanchesj@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)

Financial Impact:

The financial impact is $176,058. The source of funds is the corresponding Capital Projects



Budget.

PM1 Crystal Lakes ES Project Modifications

Category
Consent Agenda

Discussion

This Project Modification contains the following items:



Item #1







  • CCD #6 in the amount of $145,352.00




  • Description:  Furnish and install all material related to the repair and reconstruction of areas damaged by mold and the mold remediation necessary to provide a clean and mold free environment Phases 1, 3 and 4. Mechanical Room #7 and catalogue and reinstall casework in Buildings #2 and #3.




  • Cause: School District Requested.




  • Justification:   Mold discovered during the HVAC retrofit requiring remediation and reconstruction


Item #2







  • CCD #7 in the amount of $227,787.00




  • Description:  Furnish and install all material related to the repair and reconstruction of areas damaged by mold and the mold remediation necessary to provide a clean and mold free environment Buildings #2 and #3. Replace cubbies damaged by mold, added scope in Buildings #1, #6, #7, and #8 due to damages from the mold remediation. 




  • Cause: School District Requested




  • Justification:   Mold discovered during the HVAC retrofit requiring remediation and reconstruction.


Item #3







  • CCD #8 in the amount of $254,782.00.




  • Description:  Furnish and install all material related to the repair and replacement of cabinets, floors, wood molding, acoustic panels and shelving damaged by mold and the mold remediation necessary to provide a clean and mold free environment Buildings #1 through Building #8.




  • Cause: School District Requested.




  • Justification:   Mold discovered during the HVAC retrofit requiring remediation and reconstruction.


Item #4







  • CCD #9 in the amount of $227,227.00 and the addition of one hundred forty (140) days.




  • Description:  Extend the project schedule by an additional one hundred forty (140) calendar days.




  • Cause:  School District Requested




  • Justification:   General Conditions and construction phase fee for an additional 140 days due to the impact of the schedule for the mold remediation and repairs.


Item #5







  • CCD #10 in the amount of $128,000.00




  • Description:  Additional General Conditions for Hill York due to schedule impacts caused by the mold, mold remediation and repair during the project duration.




  • Cause:  School District Requested.




  • Justification:   Mold.


Item #6







  • CCD #11 in the Not-to-Exceed amount of $58,648.00




  • Description:  Relocate the installation of a new play court that was to be located where the portables were situated which was 25 feet from Building #5. The new location of the play court place the play court more than 60 feet away from the building.




  • Cause:  School District Requested




  • Justification:   Noise from play court would interfere with classroom activities.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board revise the project budget to $15,665,449.00, and ratify Project Modifications in the amount of $1,041,796.00 and the addition of one hundred forty (140) calendar days to Suffolk Construction, for the Crystal Lakes Elementary School Class Size Reduction & HVAC Project #2121 8356, and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to execute all related contract documents.

Contact:

Joseph M. Moore (moore@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)
Joseph M. Sanches

Financial Impact:

The financial impact is an increase of $790,341.00 to the Project's Budget and a commitment of $1,041,796.00 to the Construction Management contract.



The source of funds is Capital Contingency.

PM2 Spanish River HS Biotech Academy Project Modifications

Mar 26, 2008 - Spec Mtg re Construction Projects
Category

This Project Modification contains the following items:



Item #1







  • CCD #1 in the amount of $107,200.00




  • Description:  The purchase and installation of plastic laminate casework, shelves and countertops.




  • Cause:  School District Requested




  • Justification: Although the casework was shown on the drawings, it was mistakenly thought to be furnished as part of the Academy FF&E and was not included in the GMP.


Item #2







  • CCD #2 in the amount of $185,290.00




  • Description:  The purchase and installation of wood science casework and fume hoods.




  • Cause:  School District Requested




  • Justification: Although the casework was shown on the drawings, it was mistakenly thought to be furnished as part of the Academy FF&E and was not included in the GMP.


Item #3







  • CCD #3 in the amount of $60,446.00 and for the addition of eighty (80) calendar days of which Forty one (41) are compensable. 




  • Description: Extend substantial completion from March 10, 2008 to May 29, 2008. Extend final completion from June 10, 2008 to August 27, 2008.




  • Cause: Unforeseen Condition.




  • Justification:  Time extension due to need to add shoring during the demolition phase because actual conditions differed from the record drawings and the impact of adding the casework to Construction Manager's scope of work.


Item #4







  • CCD #4 in the amount of $93,383.00. 




  • Description: Various electrical construction changes not included in the original design due to incomplete scope of the Distance Learning Laboratory being known at the time of establishing the project GMP. There were extensive requirements for conduit runs for data, power and communications. There were also numerous miscellaneous electrical circuits required as the project was progressing.




  • Cause: School District Requested




  • Justification:  Completion of project requirements to meet the needs of the Distance Learning Lab which were not known at project commencement.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board revise the project budget to $4,520,545.00, and ratify Project Modifications in the amount of $446,319.00 and the addition of eighty (80) calendar days to Horus Construction, for the Spanish River High School Biotech Academy Project #1681-8228, and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to execute all related contract documents.

Contact:

Joseph M. Moore (moore@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)
Joseph M. Sanches

Financial Impact:

The financial impact is an increase of $405,206.00 to the project budget and a commitment of $446,319 to the Construction Management contract.



The source of funds is the Five-Year Plan budget for Academies at Existing Schools.
