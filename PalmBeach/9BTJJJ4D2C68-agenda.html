Category
13. NEW BUSINESS
Type
Action
Recommended Action
I recommend the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp. adopt a Resolution authorizing the addition of Pioneer Park Roof Replacement Project and Belle Glade Elementary Roof Replacement project to the Schedule 2010A to the Master Lease and authorize the Superintendent and Board Chairman to finalize and execute all related documents.

DESCRIPTION:

  • This Resolution authorizes the addition of the roofing projects at Belle Glade Elementary and Pioneer Park Elementary to the Qualified School Construction Bonds.

Michael J. Burke (mike.burke@palmbeachschools.org)
