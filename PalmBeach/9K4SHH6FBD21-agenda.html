Subject
LR 8 Falcon Waterfree Technologies, LLC - Settlement Agreement
Meeting
Aug 8, 2012 - Special Meeting 8-08-12
Category
New Business
Type
Action (Consent)




  • The SCHOOL BOARD purchased waterless urinals from Falcon Waterfree Technologies, LLC (Falcon) to replace standard urinals, as a retrofitted item, in many schools throughout Palm Beach County.




  • While the District has installed and continues to use thousands of water free urinals in newly constructed buildings without incident resulting in substantial savings, problems were discovered in incidences where the water free urinals were installed in older facilities with copper pipe.




  • Initially, District Staff had to replace and repair a total of 111 water free urinal connections (87 of which were actively leaking) at the following schools:  Palm Beach Lakes High School, Spanish River High School, Pahokee Middle/Senior High School, South Tech Academy, and Santaluces High School.




  • A total of 220 water free urinal connections currently require replacement and repair (59 of which have some leakage) at the following schools:  Glades Central High School, Lake Worth High School, Santaluces High School, South Tech Academy, Spanish River High School, and Wellington High School.




  • District staff requested that Falcon take responsibility for the replacement and repair of all affected water free urinal connections at all schools.




  • The Settlement Agreement document resolves the issues between the School Board and Falcon as related to the replacement and repair of the water free urinals at all schools.


 

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Settlement Agreement between the School Board and Falcon Waterfree Technologies, LLC which involves the replacement and repair of water free urinal connections at eight schools throughout the District, and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent/designee to finalize and execute and all necessary and related settlement documents.

Sheryl G. Wood, Esq.; General Counsel



Kathelyn Jacques-Adams, Esq.; Associate Counsel
