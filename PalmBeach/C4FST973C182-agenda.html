Recommended Action
I recommend the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp authorize a resolution to issue COP series 2022A and 2024A to refinance the 2012C and 2014C COP issues.

DESCRIPTION:

  • The District’s Financial Advisor identified an opportunity to refund (or refinance) the 2012C COP Issue.  As advance refundings are no longer allowed, an RFP was circulated to banks and members of the District’s underwriting team to find the best option.
     
  • Twelve proposals were submitted.  The recommended proposal, from Bank of America, is a forward refunding which will lock in a rate for the 2012C and the 2014C.  The estimated savings are 12.09 percent and 15.93 percent respectively or a total of approximately $10 million.  The savings will be realized by reducing future debt service payments.  The actual savings amount will be determined when the rate is locked on July 19, 2021.  The transaction will close in May 2022 and May 2024.
     
  • The resolution authorizes issuance of the refunding COP issues in 2022 and 2024.  The School Board will also authorize a related resolution on July 21, 2021 (FMT-1).
     
  • The estimated costs of issuance for the two transactions are estimated at $293,601.  Some costs will be paid once the rate is locked, since most of the work will be completed at that time.  The rest of the costs will be paid after the transactions close in 2022 and 2024.  A list of the firms to be included in the transaction as well as the maximum fees expected for each and when payment will be made is attached.  
     
  • The Finance Committee reviewed the transaction at their meeting on June 11, 2021.  The Committee unanimously recommended the transaction be presented to the School Board for approval.  The meeting was recorded and can be watched online at https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JMc2E_XZS2o&list=PLOQqH0OHtbmPHNJsh2mbTobJaHHbJ2tUv&index=3 . The minutes from the meeting as well as the presentation are available on Board Docs and the Finance Committee website:  https://www.palmbeachschools.org/school_board/board_advisory_committees/finance_committee/2021_finance_committee_meetings

CONTACT:

Michael J. Burke, CFO (mike.burke@palmbeachschools.org)

Leanne Evans, Treasurer (leanne.evans@palmbeachschools.org)

 

FINANCIAL IMPACT:

The net financial impact of this transaction is a net present value decrease in the debt service budget of approximately $10 million.  This will be incorporated into future debt service budgets and the capital plan.
