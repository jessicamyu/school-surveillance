There is probable cause sufficient  to support the termination of Augustus Chappelle.

PS2 - Personnel Disciplinary Action - Curtis McRae
Meeting
May 6, 2009 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Academics
Category
Consent
Type
Action (Consent)

On January 11, 2006 Mr. Curtis McRae applied for a non-instructional position with the Palm Beach County School District.  He was eventually hired on April 19, 2006.  During his initial employment processing, the normal screening procedures were not followed.  Mr. McRae did not file a complete set of fingerprints.  As part of a state mandated reprinting of all employees of the School district, Mr. McRae was fingerprinted on September 28, 2008.  It was after analysis by the Florida Department of Law Enforcement that the District discovered that Mr. McRae was a convicted felon and had not been fingerprinted back in 2006 when he was initially hired.  Mr. McRae's felony convictions were for crimes of moral turpitude which did not comply with standards of good moral character.  Mr. McRae's failure to follow the normal hiring process by not submitting a complete set of fingerprints is a violation of school district policy 3.12 Criminal Background Checks and school district policy 1.013, Paragraph 1, Responsibilities of the School District Personnel and Staff.



There is probable cause sufficient to support the termination of Curtis McRae. 

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve Curtis McRae's suspension without pay, effective May 7, 2009.  I further recommend that the School Board approve the termination of Curtis McRae's employment effective May 21, 2009 subject only to appeal under Section 120.569, Florida Statutes.
