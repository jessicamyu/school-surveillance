PD2 - Personnel Disciplinary Action - Gwendolyn Johnson
Meeting
Jul 30, 2008 - Spec Mtg re Personnel and Disc Actions
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Discussion

Dr. Gwendolyn Johnson, Principal, became the subject of an Employee Relations investigation regarding allegations of falsification of district documents, misconduct and unprofessional conduct.  Specifically, these allegations include potential violations of School Board Policies, Procedures, Bulletin Directives and the Code of Ethics of the Education Profession in Florida as follows:



Violation of Florida Administrative Codes 6B-1.001, (3), Code of Ethics of the Education Profession in Florida "Aware of the importance of maintaining the respect and confidence of one's colleagues, of students, of parents, and of other members of the community, the educator strives to achieve and sustain the highest degree of ethical conduct"; 6B-1.006, (4) (b), Principles of Professional Conduct for the Education Profession in Florida "Obligation to the public requires that the individual:  shall not intentionally distort or misrepresent facts concerning an education matter in direct or indirect public expression"; 6B-1.006, (5) (a), Principles of Professional Conduct for the Education Profession in Florida "Obligation to the profession of education requires that the individual:  shall maintain honesty in all professional dealings"; 6B-1.006, (5) (h), Principles of Professional Conduct for the Education Profession in Florida "Obligation to the profession education requires that the individual:  shall not submit fraudulent information on any document in connection with professional activities"



Violation of School Board Policies 1.014, Responsibilities of Principals and 1.013, Responsibilities of the School District Personnel and Staff



Violation of School District of Palm Beach County Bulletins P-12542-CAO/COO-Count Day and Class Size Reduction Review and P-12519-CAO/COO-Florida Department of Education Student Enrollment Procedures

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve Dr. Gwendolyn Johnson's twenty (20) day suspension without pay, effective July 31, 2008, subject only to appeal under Section 120.68, Florida Statutes, unless a timely request for an administrative hearing is made within the fifteen (15) days stated herein pursuant to payment of salary and benefits until final School Board disposition is entered to the administrative hearing procedures.

Contact:

Ann Killets; Chief James P. Kelly; Angelette Green

Financial Impact:

There is no financial impact to the School District.
