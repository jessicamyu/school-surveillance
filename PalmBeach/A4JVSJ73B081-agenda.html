A petition seeking waiver of School Board Policy 1.09, Section 5(b) has been received in the Board Office.  This advertisement is to provide notice of petition per § 120.542(6) F.S. providing 14 days’ notice for any interested party to provide written comments to the Board Clerk.  This matter is expected to be considered by the School Board at their Special Meeting on December 9, 2015.  A copy of the petition can be found at http://www.boarddocs.com/fl/palmbeach/Board.nsf/goto?open&id=A4JVT573D63F.
