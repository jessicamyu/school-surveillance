PL1 ILA Between the School District of Palm Beach County and Palm Beach County for Wastewater Improvements at 03W
Meeting
Apr 14, 2010 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Operations
Category
Consent
Type
Action (Consent)


  • The School District of Palm Beach County has committed to construct a new school located north of 7th Street, south of Pioneer Road, east of Lyons Road and west of Marginal Road.  The new school will serve as relief for other elementary schools located in the Greater Central Western Communities.


 





  • The construction of this new school facility requires the School District to connect to the County’s existing wastewater system.  The County currently has very limited wastewater transmission capacities in the force main adjacent to the new school which requires installation of a 30” diameter force main to be located in Lyons Road and to run approximately 750 feet south of the school’s property.


 





  • The County agrees to install the proposed force main by June 30, 2010, maintain and share the total costs of $330,000.00.


 





  • The School Board will pay for the design and permitting of the force main and also contribute $89,000.00 to the cost of installation.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve the Interlocal Agreement (ILA) between Palm Beach County and the School Board of Palm Beach County for the installation, dedication and maintenance of a Force Main along Lyons Road to accommodate the New Western Communities Elementary School (03W).

Contact:

moorej@palmbeach.k12.fl.us; sanchesj@palmbeach.k12.fl.us

Financial Impact:

$89,500 for the School’s capital budget.

PM-3 Plumosa Elementary School Project Modification

Apr 14, 2010 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Operations
Category

This Project Modification contains the following item:







  • Change Order Proposal Request (COPR) #1 for the addition of twenty-two (22) non-compensable calendar days.




  • Description: Extend the Substantial Completion date from May 28, 2010 to June 19, 2010 and the Final Completion Date from September 10, 2010 to October 2, 2010.  This change does not impact the school occupancy date of August 2010.




  • Cause: Contractor Suggested




  • Justification: The use of precast sills was approved as a value engineering item as part of the GMP.  The precast sill shop drawings were approved by the Architect and the Construction Manager proceeded with the required mock-up window.  During the review of the mock-up window installation by the commissioning team, it was noted that the profile of the window sill did not match the profile in our specification.  The installation of the remaining window sills was put on hold until the Construction Manager submitted window sill shop drawings to the Building Department for review and approval.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve Project Modifications for the addition of twenty-two (22) non-compensable calendar days to Suffolk Construction Company, Inc. for the Plumosa Elementary School Modernization Project #0871-7507 and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to finalize and execute all related contract documents.

Contact:

Joseph Sanches (sanchesj@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)

Financial Impact:

There is no financial impact to the Project Budget.

This Project Modification contains the following item:





  • Change Order Proposal Request (COPR) #2 for the addition of six hundred ninety-four (694) non-compensable calendar days.


  • Description:




    • Phase 2a (Educational Facilities):  Extend the Substantial Completion date 39 calendar days from May 31, 2009 to July 9, 2009.


    • Phase 2b (Football Field and Track):  Extend the Substantial Completion date 624 calendar days from July 14, 2008 to May 28, 2010.


    • Phase 2c (Concession  Building and Play Fields):  Extend the Substantial Completion date 98 calendar days from February 19, 2010 to May 28, 2010. Extend the project Final Completion to July 19, 2010.


  • Cause: School District Requested


  • Justification: 




    • Phase 2a (Educational Facilities):  The change in Architect had a 39 day impact.


    • Phase 2b (Football Field and Track):  The football field and track were put on hold for almost 21 months to allow the school time to raise funds for an artificial turf field.  The school was unable to raise the funds during this tough economic time and we are now proceeding with a Bermuda sod field.


    • Phase 2c (Concession Building and Play Fields):  The addition of the "safe refuge' rooms impacted the concession building completion by approximately 3 months.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve Project Modifications for non-compensable time extensions for James B. Pirtle Construction Company, Inc. for the Palm Beach Gardens High School Modernization Project #1371-7506, and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to finalize and execute all related contract documents.

  • Contract with FP&L to install a small solar panel generation system.


  • FP&L will install the solar panel generation system at no cost to the district.  The system will be used as a teaching tool.  FP&L will provide training on this system to the Suncoast HS staff.  The School District will benefit from the energy produced and these savings will offset anticipated maintenance expenses.
