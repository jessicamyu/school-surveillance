Subject
Interlocal Agreement with the Village of Palm Springs for Lift Station Improvements for the Clifford O. Taylor/Kirklane Elementary School
Meeting
Aug 6, 2008 - Spec Mtg re Interlocal Agreement with Palm Springs
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Discussion


  • As scheduled in the 5-Year Capital Plan, the School District of Palm Beach County is modernizing the existing Clifford O. Taylor/Kirklane Elementary School.


 





  • The plan to modernize the Clifford O. Taylor/Kirklane Elementary School facility was approved by the Palm Springs Village Council on April 26, 2007.


 





  • The increased size of the new school (177,000 square feet) requires making improvements to an existing Village lift station that serves the facility.


 





  • The Village agrees to upgrade the current lift station with new pumps and an electrical panel by June 1, 2009 in order to provide the School District of Palm Beach County the additional lift station capacity required by the improvements made to the property.


 





  • The School District of Palm Beach County will compensate the Village the actual cost, not to exceed $35,000, to upgrade the lift station with new pumps and an electrical panel within 90 days after receipt of invoice from the Village.


 





  • This Agreement will assure that the lift station servicing the modernized Clifford O. Taylor/Kirklane Elementary School has the ability to handle the additional effluence from the school when it opens in August 2009.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve the Interlocal Agreement between the Village of Palm Springs and the School District of Palm Beach County for lift station improvements to accommodate the modernization of Clifford O. Taylor/Kirklane Elementary School and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to sign all necessary documents.

Contact:

moorej@palmbeach.k12.fl.us; sanchesj@palmbeach.k12.fl.us

Financial Impact:

$35,000.00 from the Capital Budget for the Clifford O. Taylor/Kirklane Elementary School modernization.
