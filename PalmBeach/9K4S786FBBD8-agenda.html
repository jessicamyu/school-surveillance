PL1 - Amendment to the Adopted Five-Year Plan: Replace Southwest Boca Raton Elementary School (05-C) with an addition at Whispering Pines Elementary
Meeting
Apr 29, 2009 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Operations
Category
Consent
Type
Action (Consent)
Funding for the construction project of Southwest Boca Raton Elementary (05-C) is not available.  An alternative construction project is needed at the Whispering Pines Elementary School campus to relieve current and projected overcrowding at Del Prado, Sunrise Park, and Waters Edge Elementary Schools. Both projects are non-Referendum construction projects, and future funding will be contingent upon the coming FY10-14 Five-Year Plan and Capital Budget process.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve an amendment to the adopted FY09-FY13 Five-Year Plan that replaces Southwest Boca Raton Elementary (05-C) with a new school addition to the Whispering Pines Elementary School campus.

Contact:

Joseph M. Moore Moorej@palmbeach.k12.fl.us



Joseph M. Sanches Sanchesj@palmbeach.k12.fl.us

Financial Impact:

This amendment to the current FY09-13 Five-Year Plan will save the District approximately 23.6 million in short-term capital dollars.  The financial impact does not include operating budget savings.

Motion & Voting

I recommend the School Board approve an amendment to the adopted FY09-FY13 Five-Year Plan that replaces Southwest Boca Raton Elementary (05-C) with a new school addition to the Whispering Pines Elementary School campus.

Description:



·         There is a Class action against the manufacturer of OxyContin, Purdue Pharmacy for improperly marketing Oxycontin resulting in an increased market share at the expense of those who paid for the drug.



·         The School District through its Third party administrator, United HealthCare, paid $164,345.51 in 2007 for OxyContin claims.



·         United HealthCare brought this case to our attention



·         “We expect to recover approximately $13,000 as a member of this action.  The actual amount will be a proration based upon the total amount of the claims to the amount of the net settlement proceeds”



·         Legal has reviewed and concurs

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve the execution of a claims form to participate in a proposed nationwide OxyContin Third Party Pay or Class Settlement.
