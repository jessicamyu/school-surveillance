Procedural

Dr. Robert Avossa, Superintendent of Schools, will preside over this meeting as Secretary of the Board as stipulated in Florida Statute.  Dr. Avossa will present the newly-elected and re-elected Board Members, introduce the Oath Ceremony, and preside over the organizational portion of the meeting, which includes the selection of the School Board Chairman and Vice Chairman.  After which, he will turn the meeting over to the Board Chairman.
