Dr. Robinson's comments:

 

Though poorly attended, the community input meeting at Palm Beach Lakes High School led to vibrant post-meeting discussions. I am honored to bring forward a recommendation from Ambar, a recent Palm Beach County high school graduate. She expressed dismay that so many students do not know about the high school options available to them. She also commended us for the HBSU/HSI college fair though she criticized her school's narrow selection of students to attend a few years ago.

I ask the Board to discuss directing staff to make plans for every 8th grade student to participate in the Career and Choice Fair that is held each fall. This could be holding the Fair for two full days and transporting all 8th graders to the Fairgrounds from school to learn about all of our various options.
