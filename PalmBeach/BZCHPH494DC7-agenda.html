DESCRIPTION:

The Chief of Staff's Office will present an update on stakeholder engagement efforts related to the Phase 1 Feedback Survey that launched on Monday, March 22. Highlights from the first Strategic Plan Steering Committee meeting will also be shared. In addition, given the connection between the Board's ongoing Equity Workshops and the next Strategic Plan, Board Members will review a proposed timeline for vetting the equity statement drafted on March 31 with specific stakeholder groups in the coming months.

 

A copy of the workshop presentation will be posted no later than Monday, April 5.

DESCRIPTION:

This Budget Workshop will provide an overview of the state education revenue forecast and District FY22 budget development process, along with an update on recently approved Federal ESSER funds including the American Rescue Plan. With the Legislature in session and budget deliberations evolving daily, this presentation may be updated prior to the workshop.  This workshop is part of a series of Budget Workshops and Budget Advisory Committee meetings leading up to budget adoption September 14, 2021.
