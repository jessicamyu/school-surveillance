Continuation of October 21, 2020 Regular School Board Meeting at 9 a.m. in the Board Room
This meeting is open to the public.
PUBLIC COMMENT CAN BE MADE IN ONE OF TWO WAYS:
1. IN PERSON:
The public can attend the meeting in a room or rooms at the Fulton-Holland Educational Services Center. A room or rooms will be provided for the public to view, listen, and/or provide public comment. Speakers must be signed up in advance of the meeting by calling the Board Office at: 561-434-8136, 561-434-8139, 561-434-7481, OR 561-434-8131. THE DEADLINE TO SIGN UP TO SPEAK IS FRIDAY, OCTOBER 30, 2020 AT 5 P.M. Blue cards will be offered to sign up in person on the day of the meeting and up to the meeting being called to order. Once the meeting is called to order, no additional speakers will be accepted.
2. BY RECORDED MESSAGE:
Public comment can also be received by leaving a voice-recorded message that will be played in the meeting by calling: 561-684-5103. Comments are limited to 1 minute for comments on the items listed above only. ONLY NEW COMMENTS WILL BE ACCEPTED. IF COMMENTS WERE PREVISOULY LEFT FOR THE 10/21/20 MEETING, DUPLICATE COMMENTS FROM THE SAME CALLERS WILL NOT BE HEARD NOR ACCEPTED FOR THIS CONTINUATION MEETING. Please be sure to state name in message as no anonymous comments will be accepted. DEADLINE TO BE INCLUDED IN THIS MEETING IS FRIDAY, OCTOBER 30, 2020 AT 5 P.M.
