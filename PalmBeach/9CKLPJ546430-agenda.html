RECOMMENDATION:

It is my pleasure as Superintendent of Schools to preside over this meeting as Secretary of the Board as stipulated in Florida Statutes.  As we have no newly-elected Board Members to swear in, we will move directly to the organizational portion of the meeting which will include the selection of the new School Board Chairman and Vice Chairman. At that time, I will turn over the meeting to the new Board Chairman, who will preside over the organizational decisions that will be made with regards to the new meeting dates.

 

It is now my duty and honor to start the organization portion of the meeting.  We will now move to New Business and begin by calling for the nominations for School Board Chairman and Vice Chairman.
