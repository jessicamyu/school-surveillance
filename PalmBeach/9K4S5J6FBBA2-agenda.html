AC1 - WORKFORCE ALLIANCE SUMMER YOUTH EMPLOYMENT AND TRAINING PROGRAM 2009
Meeting
Jul 1, 2009 - Spec Mtgs and Wkshps re Academics
Category
Consent
Type
Action (Consent)


As a result of the passage of the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009 (ARRA), Workforce Alliance will operate a Summer Jobs Program for youths and young adults ages 18-24. Workforce Alliance will initiate the program with a work readiness segment during the last two weeks of June. This will be followed by the employment segment at various Palm Beach County employer worksites from July 6 through August 14, 2009



Workforce Alliance is seeking businesses and governmental / municipal agencies to host worksites for the participants, and to provide supervised, safe and meaningful employment opportunities. Workforce Alliance will pay the participants' wages and provide workersâ€™ compensation coverage.



Various Palm Beach County School District departments such as Facilities and IT has expressed interest in offering summer internship opportunities for participants in this program. Offerings will be based upon departmental needs for assistance and qualified, available participants in the program. This program is for summer internships only and participants will not displace current employees or be placed in vacant positions as stated within the agreement.

Recommendation:



I recommend that the School Board approve the Agreement between Workforce Alliance, Inc. and The School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida effective from July 1, 2009 through June 30, 2012 and authorize the Superintendent or designee to sign all related documents.
