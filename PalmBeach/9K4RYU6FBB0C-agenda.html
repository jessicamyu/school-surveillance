This project modification contains the followings items:



 



Item #1





  • Additional Architectural Services in the amount of $283,766.00.

  • Description:  The Architect shall provide all contract documents, bid support, and contract administration to add four (4) Pre-K classrooms at Benoist Farms Elementary School and additional parking at Benoist Farms Elementary School, Cholee Lake Elementary School, and Indian Pines Elementary.  The scope of work includes the redesign and permitting of site storm water systems as result of the additional classrooms and parking lots.

  • Cause:  School District Requested

  • Justification:  Additional parking is required to allow parents to park their cars and walk their kids to the Pre-K classrooms.  The additional classrooms at Benoist Farms were added to meet both voluntary and ESE Pre-K needs.




Item #2





  • Cancel Architectural Services in the amount of ($46,774.00).

  • Description:  Cancel remaining site specific Pre-K design, bid support and contract administration services at Gove Elementary School, North Grade Elementary School, South Olive Elementary School, and Dr. Mary McLeod Bethune Elementary School.

  • Cause:  School District Requested

  • Justification:  Pre-K centers will be provided at Belle Glades Elementary School, Barton Elementary School, Berkshire Elementary School and Lincoln Elementary School where space is already available instead of building new spaces at Gove Elementary School, North Grade Elementary School, South Olive Elementary School, and Dr. Mary McLeod Bethune Elementary, respectively. 

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board revise project budgets to $6,146,152.00 and approve Project Modifications in the amount of $236,992.00 for the Pre-K Classroom Addition Projects and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to execute all related contract documents.

PM3 Palm Beach Gardens High Artificial Turf
Meeting
Dec 5, 2007 - Spec Mtg re Construction Projects
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Discussion





  • Palm Beach Gardens High School is currently undergoing a complete modernization.  The school is also receiving a new stadium.  The District’s standard specification for high school stadiums is natural turf.






  • The Principal and Community Representatives have requested that the school be allowed to upgrade the stadium with artificial turf.  They have pledged to fund raise to cover the majority of costs to install the artificial turf.  They have requested the District contribute up to $250,000 on a dollar for dollar matching basis with their fund raising efforts.






  • Staff has analyzed the costs associated with installing natural turf in the new stadium and estimates the costs to be $60,000.  Staff also acknowledges that there will be reduced grounds maintenance costs associated with the artificial turf when comparing industry standard maintenance cost (see attached comparison).  Staff estimates the cost of the installation of the artificial turf to be up to $1,000,000 of which the district would limit its contribution to $250,000. Research staff has completed on other artificial turf fields currently installed indicates more usability and a lower incidence of injury rate than natural turf with average maintenance.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the Board approve matching funds in an amount Not-to-Exceed $250,000 for Palm Beach Gardens High School towards installing artificial turf in their new stadium.
