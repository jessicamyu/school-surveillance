4. Superintendent Withdraws Items
Subject
BCS1 Educational Plant Survey
Meeting
May 9, 2018 - 1. REGULAR BOARD MEETING at 5 p.m. in the Board Room
Category
4. Superintendent Withdraws Items
Type
Information

DESCRIPTION:

  1. A formal and complete Educational Plant Survey (Survey) is required by the Florida Department of Education (DOE) to be conducted every five (5) years. The last Survey was approved on May 12, 2015 (Agenda Item PIR-7). 
     
  2. An out-of-cycle Survey was prepared and submitted for review to DOE to reflect the FY 2018-2027 Capital Plan that the Board approved on September 6, 2017.  DOE has reviewed and commented on the draft Survey and has released staff to obtain Board approval.
     
  3. Authorization for the Survey is specified in Article IX and Article XII of the Florida Constitution; Chapters 1001, 1011, and 1013, Florida Statutes (F.S.); State Requirements for Educational Facilities (SREF); and the Florida Building Code.
     
  4. The purpose of the Survey is to assist District staff with recommendations for housing the educational activities of the students and staff of the School District in the future. It is a needs assessment which compares what exists at a facility, as listed on the Florida Inventory of School Houses (FISH), to what an educational or ancillary plant is allowed to have based on the latest Facilities Lists (DOE Facilities Lists for generic elementary, K-8, middle and high schools are attached).
     
  5. The Survey is a planning tool to assist in identifying future projects in the Capital Plan based on District needs.
     
  6. DOE Table II (attached) is the summary of the individual evaluations of each facility with respect to student capacity, projected student capacity and a recommendation of the final student capacity at each site within the next five years.
     
  7. DOE Table III (attached) is the financial data of all the facility recommendations within the first five years of the Survey based on State formulas and requirements.
     
  8. The Ten Year Capacity, Infrastructure and Maintenance tables and the Twenty Year Capacity, Infrastructure and Maintenance tables are also included as part of the long range planning of the Survey and are based on the draft District Capital Facilities Plan.
     
  9. The Survey can be amended at any time based on need.
