This litigation was brought by an employee, Ms. Eaton, who claimed that she was unlawfully denied a promotion due to her age and gender.  Settlement is recommended as being in the best financial interest of the District due to the anticipated costs of the litigation and the unavailability of a key witness. 

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Settlement Agreement between Lori Eaton and the School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to execute all related documents.
