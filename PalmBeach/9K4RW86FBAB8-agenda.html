Subject
Special Meeting re: Indian Ridge School/Turning Points Academy – Golden Lakes Property Owners Association Eminent Domain Settlement
Meeting
Oct 10, 2007 - Spec Mtg re Indian Ridge
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Action




  • This eminent domain suit was filed to extinguish third-party rights to use a platted ingress-egress easement crossing the Indian Ridge School/Turning Points Academy site and to acquire fee title to a parcel adjacent to the Indian Ridge School campus.




  • The platted ingress-egress easement encumbers approximately 1.4 acres of the 11.74 acre campus shared by the Indian Ridge School and Turning Points Academy.




  • The owner has claimed that certain actions taken by the School Board resulted in a taking of their rights to use the easement.  Extinguishment of these third-party rights will result in improved campus security.




  • The owners also own a 0.27 acre parcel located at the northeast corner of the School Board ownership that they desire to include as a part of the overall settlement of the ingress-egress easement taking.




  • The owners submitted an appraisal valuing the easement interest at $821,000 and Parcel 5 at $425,000, for a total of $1,246,000.  The average of the School Board’s appraisals on the value of the easement interest is $157,500 and Parcel 5 is $242,500, for a total of $400,000.




  • Settlement negotiations were held and the parties were able to arrive at a negotiated amount of $600,000 plus statutory attorney’s fees and expert witness costs.




  • Settlement is recommended as being in the best financial interest of the District due to the anticipated costs of the litigation and litigation risk inherent in an eminent domain proceeding.


  • In the event that the Board rejects the proposed settlement, the Board and owner will prepare this matter for a jury trial.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Stipulated Final Judgment between the Golden Lakes Property Owner’s Association, Inc. and the School Board of Palm Beach County in the case of School Board of Palm Beach County, Florida v. Golden Lakes Property Owner’s Association, Inc. et al. regarding the acquisition of certain real property consisting of 1.67 acres, more or less and authorize the School Board attorney to execute all related documents.
