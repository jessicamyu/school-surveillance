Recommended Action
I recommend the Palm Beach School Board Leasing Corp authorize a resolution allowing the issuance of COP Series 2014C to extend the call date on certain bonds.

DESCRIPTION:

  • Citi owns $33,280,000 or 35.6% of the 2007A COP issue.  They have offered to pay the District to extend the call option date on those certificates from 2017 to 2024.
  • Citi has offered to pay the District $2,063,360 to extend the call date.  In addition, Citi has agreed to pay $144,710 for the costs associated with the transaction.
  • The transaction would not change the original maturity or par amount of the bonds outstanding.
  • The Finance Committee reviewed the transaction during it's meeting on May 30, 2014 and has unanimously recommended moving forward with the transaction.  They also recommended allowing other investors the same opportunity and this possibility has been included in the resolution.
  • The proceeds will be used for debt service payments in FY 2015 freeing capital dollars to be used for any capital purpose.
