Discussion

The recent storm activity resulted in there being a disruption to the supply of fuel that we use for our buses.  Therefore our bid awarded vendor, BV Oil, was unable to obtain enough fuel to respond to all of our needs.  As a result, we declared an emergency and then contacted several vendors.  We were successful in obtaining the needed fuel from two of those vendors.  Locally, Palmdale Oil, was able to supply us with 8,700 gallons of low sulfur diesel fuel at an average price of $3.7571/gallon.  In addition, Glover Oil out of Melbourne was able to supply us with 19,321 gallons at an average price of $3.7586/gallon after obtaining the majority of this supply from Georgia. 



We appreciate the assistance these two vendors provided to us during this shortage.



Pricing included delivery and although this purchase was not on bid, it was in line with our current fuel costs.




 

Recommendation:

I recommend ratification of the purchase of fuel off bid as an emergency purchase from Palmdale Oil and Glover Oil as identified above.

Contact:

Joseph M Moore, (moorej@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)



Sharon Swan, Director of Purchasing



 

Financial Impact:

The financial impact is $105,307 from the operating budget - fuel.
