G1 Grove Park Elementary School - Mary and Robert Pew Public Education Fund Grant
Meeting
May 14, 2008 - Spec Mtg re Grants
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Action (Consent)
This grant is to support staff development for teachers in Readers and Writers Workshop and classroom libraries required for successful implementation of the workshop model at Grove Park Elementary.  The Fund reserves the right to rescind this grant if the original leadership needed for successful implementation of this initiative leaves or transfers from the school.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the Board approve this grant in the amount of $29,000 for the improvement of Reading and Writing scores in grades 3-5 at Grove Park Elementary School.

May 14, 2008 - Spec Mtg re Grants
Category
Consent Agenda

Action (Consent)

The grant will be assigned to the following schools in the indicated amounts:



Belle Glade Elementary:   $30,567.40



Canal Point Elementary:   $27,775.00



Glade View Elementary:   $22,000.00



Gove Elementary:             $48,730.00



Pahokee Elementary:         $28,050.00



Pioneer Park Elementary:   $25,300.00



Rosenwald Elementary:      $16,005.00



Funds will be used to support admission and transportation fees for field trips designed to enhance instruction of the Florida Sunshine State Standards.



The principal of each school will furnish the Pew Fund with a final report that contains a statement and a narrative account of program outcomes.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Mary and Robert Pew Public Education Grant in the amount of $198,427.40 effective July 1, 2008 through June 30, 2009 and authorize the Superintendent or designee to sign all related documents and contracts.

The grant will be assigned to the West Area Office for 87,000.00 to be distributed as needed to KEC/Canal Point Elementary, Pahokee Elementary and Rosenwald Elementary.



Funds will be used to allow above referenced schools to participate as affiliate schools in the Columbia University's Teachers College Writers Workshop project.



The principal of each school will furnish the Pew Fund with a final report that contains a statement and a narrative account of program outcomes.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Mary and Robert Pew Public Education Grant - Lucy Calkins Writers Workshop Affiliate School Project in the amount of $87,000.00 effective June 1, 2008 through May 30, 2009 and authorize the Superintendent or designee to sign all related documents and contracts.

Financial Impact:

The financial impact to the district is $87,000.00.  These funds will be received at the West Area Office to be distributed to the subject schools as needed.
