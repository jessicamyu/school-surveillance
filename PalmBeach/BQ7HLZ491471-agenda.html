Meeting
Jun 24, 2020 - 1. VIRTUAL SPECIAL MEETING at 2 p.m. using Google Meet Technology

Type
Action (Consent)
Recommended Action
I recommend the Board approve the updated Cafeteria Plan Documents and Summary Plan Documents (SPD), as well as allow certain relief for deadlines in 2020 as permitted in the 2020 Coronavirus Aid, Relief & Economic Relief Act (CARES Act).

DESCRIPTION:

The CARES Act allows certain changes to Cafeteria plans as long as they are formally adopted and communicated to employees.  In addition to a general update, the following changes will be included in this update to the District’s Cafeteria Plan:

  • Allows over the counter medicine to be run through health care Flexible Savings Accounts (FSAs) without a prescription.
     
  • Allows a one-time mid-year enrollment, cancellation, or changes to an FSA in 2020 for any reason. Cancelations or decreases in contributions will be allowed, less any amount already claimed.  
     
  • Allows a one-time extension for 2019 reimbursement requests for timely incurred healthcare FSAs.  The deadline of March 30, 2020 will be extended to August 15, 2020.
     
  • These changes will be advertised to all employees.
