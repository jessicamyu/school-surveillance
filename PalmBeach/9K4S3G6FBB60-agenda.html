2008 Amendment to the Association of Educational Secretaries and Office Professionals

Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Discussion

The Tentative Agreement between the School District and the Association of Educational Secretaries and Office Professionals (AESOP) provides the following:





  • Granting all eligible bargaining unit members a two (2) percent salary increase, effective January 1, 2008.


  • Increasing the Attendance Incentive from thirty-five cents ($.35) per hour to one dollar ($1.00) per hour, effective January 1, 2008.


  • Authorizing the upgrade of the Media Clerk position from Pay Level 7 to pay Level 8, and the Attendant-PBX Operator position from Pay Level 7 to Pay Level 9.


  • Compensating AESOP bargaining unit members the same as instructional personnel for assuming supplemented work duties that are declined by instructional personnel.


  • Rescinding the provisions that define District designated holidays as working duty days for purposes of overtime compensation.


  • Establishes a joint committee to conduct a classification and pay study of select bargaining unit positions, and review the District's practices for designating positions Confidential Exempt.


  • Creates an additional Education Incentive Award Level, effective for the 2008-2009 school year.

Recommendation:

I recommend the Board approve the Tentative Agreement between the School District and the Association of Educational Secretaries and Office Professionals (AESOP) as submitted.
