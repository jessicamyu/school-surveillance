Amendment of the Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) between the Classroom Teachers Association (CTA) and the School District
Meeting
Oct 1, 2008 - Workshops and Spec Mtgs re Operations
Category
Consent
Type
Action (Consent)
The MOU was initially approved by the School Board on May 17, 2006.  This amendment changes the date from February 1 to March 15 of each year for discussions between the District and CTA regarding the critical needs area(s), the number of tiers and the amount of the incentive, if any for each tier for the following school year, and extends the Tuition Reimbursement Program from summer 2007 to summer 2009.  All other criteria remain unchanged.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Amendment to the Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) between the School District of Palm Beach County and the Palm Beach County Classroom Teachers Association (CTA) that authorizes the mutually agreed upon date for discussion of the critical needs area(s), the number of tiers and the amount of the incentive(s) to be paid for the following school year, and Tuition Reimbursement for teachers meeting the criteria.

Contact:

Joseph M. Moore (Joseph Moore)/James Hayes, Jr. (James Hayes, Jr.)/Van V. Ludy (Van Ludy)

Financial Impact:

The estimated financial impact to the Districts budget is $662,000 (Federal Title II Grant).
