Subject
TL1 State of Florida Extension of the 2019-20 Statewide School Readiness Provider Contract with the Early Learning Coalition of Palm Beach County
Meeting
Jul 1, 2020 - 2. VIRTUAL SPECIAL MEETING immediately following Virtual Workshop using Google Meet Technology
Category
10. Consent Agenda
Type
Action (Consent)
Recommended Action
I recommend the Board approve the Extension of the 2019-2020 Statewide School Readiness Provider Contract, effective July 1, 2020, through June 30, 2021, with the Early Learning Coalition of Palm Beach County; and authorize the Superintendent or his designee to sign all related documents and contracts.

DESCRIPTION:

To continue the provision of School Readiness services after June 30, 2020, the District must execute the Extension of the 2019-2020 Statewide School Readiness (SR) Provider Contract with the Early Learning Coalition of Palm Beach County, effective July 1, 2020. The District may provide SR services under this Extension for a period up to 12 months, during which time a 2020-2021 SR contract may be fully executed.

 

The Extension of the 2019-2020 SR contract is being offered statewide by FLDOE to accommodate the impacts of the COVID-19 pandemic on providers’ and Coalitions’ ability to fulfill certain requirements necessary to execute a 2020-2021 SR contract prior to June 30, 2020. Currently, the statewide provider portal cannot accept the 2020-2021 SR contracts approved by the Board on June 17, 2020. In addition, not all providers have had an opportunity to meet contract eligibility criteria related to program assessment scores and/or Quality Improvement Plan completions due in part to the order issued by DOE that suspended program assessments beginning March 23, 2020.

 

The execution of the Extension of the 2019-2020 Statewide (SR) Provider Contract will enable the District to continue to provide subsidized child care services for students in Extended Learning and Early Childhood Education programs in FY21. 

 

CONTACT:

Keith Oswald, Deputy Superintendent keith.oswald@palmbeachschools.org

Glenda Sheffield, Ed.D., Chief Academic Officer 

Diana Fedderman, Assistant Superintendent of Teaching and Learning 

 

FINANCIAL IMPACT:

There will be an increase to the Special Revenue-Other Funds of up to $5,221,000 for Early Childhood Education and up to $5,292,232 for Extended Learning programming, dependent on the number of families that apply and qualify for subsidy. The funds will be allocated to the Department of Early Childhood Education ($5,221,000) and Department of Extended Learning ($5,292,232).
