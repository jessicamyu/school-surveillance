Consent Agenda
Subject
AC1 - Cooperative Agreement with Columbia Hospital (Palm Beaches) Limited Partnership dba Columbia Hospital
Meeting
Mar 30, 2011 - Special Meeting
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Discussion

The purpose of this confirming cooperative agreement is to provide a highly restrictive, therapeutic educational/residential facility for a student with a disability whose depression and impulsive behaviors have escalated to the point that the student requires crisis stabilization and a diagnostic evaluation in order to determine the appropriate educational services and placement.



Although backdating agreements is not our standard practice, it was necessary in this particular case due to the time it takes to secure a bed in a residential facility.  It does not prejudice the rights of the School Board.



Prior to placement in the residential facility, the student was receiving ESE services at Palm Beach Gardens High School.



The student has been identified as Emotionally/Behaviorally Disabled by the School District of Palm Beach County in compliance with state and local statutes and regulations.



The cooperative agreement is in compliance with State Board Rule 6A.6-0361, "Special Programs Contractual Agreements with Non-Public Schools".

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve this confirming cooperative agreement with Columbia Hospital (Palm Beaches) Limited Partnership dba Columbia Hospital in the amount of $19,500 for the residential costs for a student with a disability from March 1, 2011 through March 30, 2011, and authorize the Superintendent or his designee to sign all documents related to this agreement.

Contact:

Judith Klinek (klinek@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)
Constance Tuman-Rugg
Laura E. Pincus

Financial Impact:

The financial impact to the School District's general operating fund is $19,500.  The source of the funds is the Exceptional Student Education Department budget.
