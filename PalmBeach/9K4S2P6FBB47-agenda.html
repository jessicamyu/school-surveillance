Section 1008.25, Florida Statutes: Public school student progression, remedial instruction, promotion, and reporting requirements require that each district school board shall establish a comprehensive program for student progression.



 



The attached revision is to the 2008-2009 Elementary Student Progression Plan regarding Grades K-5 Student Performance Level Charts. These charts provide student identification in regards to promotion, retention and/or remediation.



 



Key To Revisions:



Ø  "Blue" font indicates new language



Ø  "Red strike-out" font indicates deletion of language



Ø  "Green" font indicates existing language that was moved to another section (for clarity or relevance)



 



Revisions Include the Following:



Ø  The Grades K-5 Student Performance Level Charts on pages E/20-E/25 have been revised to reflect the Literacy Assessment System Reading Running Records Guided Reading Leveling System from the Fountas and Pinnell Assessment.



Ø  All references to the NRT in the charts have been removed.



 



 



BOARD GOAL(S): 1,2,4,7                                       KEY RESULT(S): 1,2,3,4,5,8,10

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the amendment, attached herein as pages E/20-E/25, to the 2008-09 Elementary Student Progression Plan in compliance with Florida State Statutes 1008.25 effective September 3, 2008.
