Subject
WITHDRAWN - Strategic Plan Workshop 5 (Review of Draft Goals and Measures)
Meeting
May 26, 2021 - 1. WORKSHOP at 1 p.m. in the Board Room
Category
3. Workshop Items
Type
Discussion

DESCRIPTION:

This workshop is withdrawn for 5/26/2021.  It will be rescheduled for a later date.

 

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 

The Chief of Staff's Office, in partnership with Insight Education Group and on behalf of the District's Strategic Plan Steering Committee, will present proposed goals and measures for the District's next Strategic Plan. The proposed goals and measures were drafted based on findings from the Phase 1 Feedback Survey (which gathered community input) as well as progress monitoring data related to the current Strategic Plan (long-term outcomes focused on 3rd grade reading, high school readiness, high school graduation rates, and post-secondary readiness) and direction provided by the School Board's recently adopted Mission, Vision, and Equity Statement.

 

This workshop is intended to provide members of the School Board and public with an opportunity to share feedback on the Steering Committee's proposed goals and measures (to be posted no later than Monday, May 24). Additional feedback will also be solicited during Phase 2 Focus Groups scheduled to take place May 27 - June 6, 2021. Feedback generated during the workshop and Phase 2 Focus Groups will be used by the Steering Committee to make refinements to the proposed goals and measures prior to final School Board adoption (anticipated in late July).
