Literacy AmeriCorps Palm Beach County
Meeting
Sep 23, 2009 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Academics
Category
Consent
Type
Action (Consent)

The District partnered with the Palm Beach County Literacy Coalition to write a grant for AmeriCorps members.







  • The grant provides the School District with (6) AmeriCorps members who act as graduation coaches at the following high schools:  Boynton Beach High School, Forest Hill High School, John I Leonard High School, Olympic Heights High School, Palm Beach Central High School and Royal Palm Beach High School.




  • The funding match for each AmeriCorps member is paid through a grant with the Educational Foundation for $24,750 for this year and with Palm Beach Community College for $4,950.




  • The AmeriCorps members are college graduates.  They are trained by our district to assist in breaking down barriers for at-risk students at the school to which they are assigned.




  • These six (6) coaches work with an additional six (6) coaches obtained through a Small Learning Communities (SLC) Grant.  The SLC coaches are at the following school:  Atlantic High School, Glades Central High School, Lake Worth High School, Palm Beach Gardens High School, Palm Beach Lakes High School and Santaluces High School.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the cooperative agreement between the Literacy Coalition of Palm Beach County and the School Board of Palm Beach County, effective September 24, 2009 through July 2010 and authorize the Superintendent or designee to sign all related documents.

Contact:

Jeffrey J. Hernandez - Jeffrey.Hernandez@palmbeach.k12.fl.us



Judith Klinek - klinek@palmbeach.k12.fl.us



 

Financial Impact:

There will be no financial impact to the district as all employees are grant funded.

Kravis Center and Palm Beach County Convention Center Confirmation of 2010 Graduation Ceremonies Sites

Sep 23, 2009 - Spec Mtgs and Workshops re Academics
Category
Consent
Type
Action

The Palm Beach County Convention Center and The Raymond F. Kravis Center for the Performing Arts, Inc. have been reserved for graduation ceremonies for the Adult Education Center, summer school, and 23 high schools which include the following:



° Alexander Dreyfoos  School  of the Arts
° Atlantic Community High
° Boca Raton Community High
° Boynton Beach Community High
° Forest Hill Community High
° John I. Leonard High
° Jupiter Community High
° Lake Worth Community  High
° Olympic Heights High
° Palm Beach Central High
° Palm Beach Gardens High
° Palm Beach Lakes Community High
° Park Vista Community High
° Roosevelt Full Service Center
° Royal Palm Beach Community High
° Santaluces Community High
° Seminole Ridge Community High
° South Tech Academy
° Spanish River Community High
° Suncoast High
° Wellington Community High
° West Boca Community High
° William T. Dwyer High



Schools not mentioned above will facilitate graduation ceremonies at their school sites. 



The graduation cost includes rehearsals, room rental fees and deposits, custodial services, and security.
Funds are included in the District operating budget for Secondary Curriculum.



The Palm Beach County Convention Center Agreement is for annual renewal and provides for multi-year use of facilities, pending availability with annual cost, dates and specific needs to be determined. The Kravis Center Contract is a new agreement which will have to be reissued every year.



The projected cost is $395,375.80 including rental and applicable parking fees.

Recommendation:

I recommend approval of the confirmation of The Palm Beach County Convention Center and The Raymond F. Kravis Center for the Performing Arts, Inc. to hold FY 2010 high school graduation ceremonies, effective        August 27, 2009 in the amount of $395,375.80 and authorize the Superintendent to sign all related documents and contracts.

Contact:

Jeffrey J. Hernandez (Hernandezj3@palmbeach.k12.fl.us)



Dr. Constance Tuman-Rugg, Ed.D.



Peter Licata

Financial Impact:

The financial impact is  $395,375.80.  The source of  funds is the operating budget of Secondary Curriculum.
