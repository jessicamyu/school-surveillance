The purpose of the Workshop is to provide a summary of  the Advisory Boundary Committee (ABC) recommendations for the 2012-2013 Boundary Cycle, pursuant to Policy 5.01. 


The ABC presented the following Boundary Change Proposals to the Superintendent (Judith Klinek-designee) on November

17, 2011:





 





1. PR-1

Boundary Study SP-1 (Sunrise Park Elementary to Whispering Pines Elementary)

was voted on by the ABC to be

recommended to the Superintendent on October 20, 2011 with a 7-4 vote.





 





The

proposed changes provide relief and ease core facility crowding at Sunrise

Park Elementary and moves SACs

295A, 295B, 295D and 311K to Whispering Pines Elementary.





 





2.

PR-2 Boundary Study DV-1 (Diamond View Elementary to Starlight Cove Elementary)

was voted on by the ABC to be recommended to the Superintendent on October 6,

2011 with a 10-0 vote.





 





The

proposed changes provide relief and ease core facility crowding at Diamond

View Elementary and moves SAC 210E and SAC 210H to Starlight Cove Elementary.





 





3.

PR-3 Another Boundary Study ST-2 (Seminole Trails Elementary to Hope Centennial

Elementary, Egret Lake Elementary to Hope Centennial Elementary and Seminole

Trails Elementary to Egret lake Elementary) was voted on by the ABC to be recommended

to the Superintendent on November 3, 2011 with a 13-0 vote; however, a partial/revised Boundary Study ST-2 is being recommended which moves fewer students

into Hope Centennial Elementary to allow the integration of these students with

current students. The changes from Egret Lake Elementary to Hope Centennial and

from Seminole Trails Elementary to Egret Lake Elementary are not recommended at this time, but could be considered again in the future.





 





The proposed changes provide relief to

Seminole Trails Elementary and move SAC 118 and SAC 125 to Hope Centennial

Elementary.





 





4.

PR-4 Boundary Study N-1 (Northboro Elementary to Northmore

Elementary and to Pleasant City Elementary) and designation of SAC areas 099B,

105A, 105B, 115A, 115B, 389A and 389B as priority SAC areas effective in 2013

was voted by the ABC to be recommended to the Superintendent on November 17,

2011 with a 13-0 vote.





 





The proposed changes align school attendance

areas with Policy 5.01, Student Assignment and moves SAC 099B, 105A, 105B,

115A, and 115B to Northmore Elementary and SAC 389A and 389B to Pleasant City

Elementary and designates SAC areas 099B, 105A, 105B, 115A, 115B, 389A and 389B

as priority SAC areas effective in 2013.





 





5.

PR-5 Boundary Study Omni-1 (Omni Middle to Boca Raton Middle and

Boca Raton Middle to Carver Middle) was voted on by the ABC to be recommended

to the Superintendent on October 6, 2011 with a 10-0 vote.





 





The proposed changes provide relief to

Omni Middle and move SAC 341B, SAC 341C, SAC 341D, SAC 341E and SAC 342A into

Boca Raton Middle and moves SAC 298B from Boca Raton Middle into Carver Middle.


The full proposals and Support Documentation are provided as attachments.
