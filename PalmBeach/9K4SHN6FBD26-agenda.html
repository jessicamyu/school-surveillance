SUPERINTENDENT PRESIDES and opens the meeting.



 

Recommendation:

My pleasure as Superintendent of Schools to preside over this meeting as Secretary of Board as stipulated in Florida Statutes.  Next, we will have the Oath Ceremony which will include our newly elected Board Members Frank Barbieri, Michael Murgio, and Chuck Shaw.  Following that, the newly elected Board Members will have an opportunity to say their remarks about this important occasion.  After that, we will have the organizational portion of the meeting which will include the selection of the new School Board Chairman and Vice Chairman, then I will turn over the meeting to the new Board Chairman who will preside over the organizational decisions that will be made with regards to the new meeting dates.



Mr. Barbieri, Mr. Murgio, and Mr. Shaw please come down to the floor for the Oath Ceremony by Judge Alvarez.



I will now invite each of the newly elected and sworn in Board Members for their individual comments. 



Please join me in recognizing our new School Board Team.



It is now my duty and honor to start the organizational portion of the meeting.  We will begin by calling the nominations for School Board Chairman and Vice Chairman.

Contact:

E. Wayne Gent, Superintendent
