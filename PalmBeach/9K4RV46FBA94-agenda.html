  • The Lease Agreement permits Toussaint L'Ouverture High School for Arts and Social Justice Charter School to use buildings 24, 25, 26, and 31 of the Seacrest Campus and the designated parking area depicted in Exhibit "A".




  • The term of the Lease shall commence on November 1, 2007 and extend to June 15, 2008 (7 1/2 months).




  •  The Charter School will maintain the improvements on the Leased Premises in good condition and repair at all times.




  • Annual rent for the term of the Lease shall be $1.00 per year.


 

Recommendation:











I recommend the School Board approve the Lease Agreement between the School District of Palm Beach County and Toussaint L'Ouverture High School for Arts and Social Justice Charter School for use of building and classroom space at the Seacrest Campus; and authorize the Chairman and the Superintendent to sign all necessary documents.
