DESCRIPTION:

Given the importance of the upcoming 2020 Census, District Leadership has established its own Complete Count Committee (CCC) to help ensure a complete count of residents of Palm Beach County, especially children. The purpose of this workshop is to share the CCC’s current and future efforts.
