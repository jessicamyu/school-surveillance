PL1 Interlocal Agreement with the Town of Haverhill for new elementary school 06D
Meeting
Mar 5, 2008 - Spec Mtg re Construction Projects
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Action (Consent)


  • On December 11, 2006, the School District staff notified the Town of Haverhill of the development of new elementary school 06 D at its subject location. 06D elementary school is scheduled to open in August 2009.


 





  • Approximately 2 acres of the school’s site is located in the Town of Haverhill, with the majority of the school site located in unincorporated Palm Beach County. 


 





  • The approximate 2 acre site in the Town is located at the north end of Trailaway Lanes, a private roadway serving single family homes.


 





  • The Town has stated that they are supportive of the new elementary school, however are concerned about the impacts the school may create on pedestrians, vehicles and other activities on the low density residential development around the School Board owned 2 acre parcel with the Town limits.


 





  • The purpose of this agreement is to facilitate a means whereby the new school can be developed in a timely manner while still protecting the health, safety and welfare of the Town residents.


 





  • The Agreement requires that the School District applies for a Special exception to allow recreational use as an accessory use to the school on the 2 acre parcel in the Town and limits its use to those identified on the site plan.  The Agreement restricts access to the site from Stacy street and not through the Town’s current limits, it requires that an 8 foot wall be installed around the three sides of the 2 acre parcel located in the Town, it provides for mitigated impacts of lights and mechanical equipment on the residential properties and it provides for the Town to continued to be exempt from school siting criteria as defined per FS 163.3177(6).


 





  • The subject Interlocal Agreement was presented to the Town of Haverhill’s Council on February 28, 2008 and was approved on a 4 to 0 vote.

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve an Interlocal Agreement between the Town of Haverhill and the School Board of Palm Beach County pertaining to the construction of new elementary school 06D fronting on Stacy Street and authorize the Chairman and Superintendent to sign all necessary documents.
