Special Meeting re: Rayburn White Final Order
Meeting
Apr 14, 2010 - Legal Sessions
Category
Special Meetings
Type
Action




  • The Superintendent made a recommendation that Rayburn White be terminated  for employment for ethical misconduct, failure to exercise best professional judgment in violation of School District Policies 1.013, as well as School Board Administrative Directive 3.27; and State Board of Education Rules 6B-1.001   




  • Mr. White timely asserted his right to an appeal before the Division of Administrative Hearings (DOAH).




  • On August 19, 2009 an Administrative Hearing was held before Judge Claude Arrington regarding the termination of Rayburn White.




  • On February 10, 2010, Judge Arrington issued a Recommended Order that the Board enter a Final Order dismissing the charges against Mr. White.




  • This Final Order is in the best interest of the School Board.

Recommendation:

I recommend that the School Board approve the Final Order in the matter of School Board of Palm Beach County v. Rayburn White, DOAH case No. 09-0387, a copy of which is attached hereto, and authorize the execution of all related documents.
