Subject
Student Academic Support Plan Workshop
Meeting
Feb 3, 2021 - 1. WORKSHOP at 2:30 p.m. in the Board Room
Category
2. Workshop Items
Type
Discussion

DESCRIPTION:

The negative effects of COVID-19 on student learning have been well documented by school districts across the nation. In response, the School District of Palm Beach County has been codifying its approach to mitigating learning loss by developing the Student Academic Support Plan (SASP). An overview of the SASP will be presented during this workshop to provide an update on work to-date as well as targeted investments made in service of closing instructional gaps in the short term.
