Superintendent Opens Meeting

Category
1. Call To Order
Type
Procedural

Dr. Donald Fennoy, Superintendent of Schools, will preside over this meeting as Secretary of the School Board as stipulated in Florida Statute.  Dr. Fennoy will present the re-elected School Board Members, introduce the Oath Ceremony, and preside over the organizational portion of the meeting, which includes the selection of the School Board Chair and Vice Chair.  After which, he will turn the meeting over to the School Board Chair.

Vote on Nominations

 

 

Roll call voting will take place per School Board Policy 1.03.  Per Robert's Rules, in the event a single candidate does not receive four or more votes, the balloting will continue as many times as necessary until one obtains a majority vote.  A nomination is not removed unless a candidate voluntarily withdraws his or her name.
