Subject
Informal Hearing re: Joseph Littles Nguzo Saba Charter School Termination
Meeting
May 12, 2010 - Legal Sessions
Category
Special Meetings
Type
Action

The Joseph Littles Nguzo Saba Charter School will be recommended for termination of its charter after having numerous compliance and financial issues which have gone unresolved including:



 



·         Failure of the governing board to properly monitor and oversee the financial status of the school;



·         Failure to meet the generally accepted standards of fiscal management under Florida law;



·         Failure to meet the academic standards as set out in its charter.



 

The school was notified in writing by the District of the proposed action on February 26, 2010 and timely requested an informal hearing .  Under Florida law, a charter school is entitled to an informal hearing before the sponsor prior to a final vote on the recommended termination.  An hearing was originally scheduled for April 7, 2010 however was continued to May 12, 2010.  The hearing is designed to ensure the administration of due process.

Recommendation:

I hereby recommend that the School Board terminate the charter contract of the Joseph Littles Nguzo Saba Charter School at the conclusion of the informal hearing.

Dr. Janice Cover, Assistant Superintendent Quality Assurance
