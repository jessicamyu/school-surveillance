Lease Agreement Belle Glade Boys & Girls Club (Alternative to Out of School Suspension
Meeting
Apr 30, 2008 - Spec Mtg re Interlocal Agreement for ATOSS Site
Category
Consent Agenda
Type
Action

To provide students recommended for out of school suspension to attend school at an Alternative to Suspension site where they receive interim educational services in a safe, structured environment that reinforces traditional academic concepts as well as character education and career exploration skills.



Program benefits include:



* The opportunity for students to continue their academic momentum  without being penalized.



* Offers social and emotional skill building opportunities in addition to job seeking and interviewing skills.



* Allows students to receive full credit for attendance.



* Academic curriculum includes FCAT preparation for strategic reading, mathematics and writing.



* Parents are required to attend an Orientation session for registration and program rules and requirements.



* The term of this lease shall be from the effective date until June 15, 2009 with five (5) automatic one (1) year renewals.  The term shall be automatically renewed unless either Party provides to the other of it's election not to renew at least 90 days notice prior to the  next renewal date.




 

Recommendation:

I recommend the School Board approve the Lease Agreement between the School Board of Palm Beach County and Belle Glade Teen Center Boys & Girls Club for use of classroom space at the  building located at 350 S. W. 10th Street Belle Glade, FL 33430. And authorize the Chairman and the Superintendent to sign all necessary documents.

akillets@palmbeach.k12.fl.us

Financial Impact:

There will be a positive impact of $1.00 per year.
