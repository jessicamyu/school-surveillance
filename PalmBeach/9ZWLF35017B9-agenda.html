Type
Action (Consent)
Recommended Action
I recommend the Board adopt the Resolution determining revenues and millages levied for FY 2016.

DESCRIPTION:

Chapter 200.065(2)(f)3 of the Florida Statutes requires that the School District hold a public hearing to adopt a millage rate within 80 days of certification of value, but not earlier than 65 days after certification. The total millage rate being levied is 7.512 mills which raises $1,191,282,414 in local revenues.

FINANCIAL IMPACT:

The financial impact to the District is $1,191,282,414 in local tax revenues for FY 2016.
