Recommendation for Expulsion of Student 07/08-X-094

Recommendation:

I recommend the Board adopt the Out of County Expulsion from Brevard County School District and expel student 07/08-X-094 from the regular public school program of the School District of Palm Beach County for one (1) calendar year.  On November 29, 2007, student 07/08-X-094 was charged with committing disruption of school/disorderly conduction while on the campus of Southwest Middle School.
