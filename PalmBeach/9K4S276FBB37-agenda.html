1.  Outside Counsel Contract with Weiss Serota Helfman Pastoriza Cole & Boniske, P.L.



 



The outside counsel shall perform the following services: Legal advisor shall provide legal services associated with acquisition by eminent domain of property required for 05-C Elementary School as assigned Legal Advisor by the Chief Legal Counsel from time to time, including but not limited to, preparation and filing of pleadings, attendance at hearings, mediation, trial and appeals.



 



This Amendment will extend the term of the contract until June 30, 2009.



 



No additional funds are being requested at this time.



 



2.  Consultant Contract with Liberty Diagnostics, LLC d/b/a LBFG



Review, analyze, and evaluate any and all appropriate documents and other items for purposes of providing expert testimony and other services in preparation for mediation and/or trial in the case of The School Board of Palm Beach County v. The Morganti Group, Inc., as successor in interest to Morganti Florida, Inc., Morganti Florida, Inc. and M.P.A. Architects , Inc. f/k/a Marion, Puluga & Associates, Case No 50 2005CA 006997 XXXXMB..  Perform site visits as necessary.



 

Recommendation:

1.  Outside Counsel Contract with Weiss Serota Helfman Pastoriza Cole & Boniske, P.L.



I recommend an extension to the outside counsel contract with the law firm of Weiss, Serota, Helfman, Pastoriza, Cole & Boniske, P.L., and authorize the Superintendent and Board Chairman to execute all necessary documents.



 



2.  Consultant Contract with Liberty Diagnostics, LLC d/b/a LBFG



I recommend award of the Consultant Agreement in the amount of $25,000.00 to Liberty Diagnostics, LLC d/b/a LBFG in the case of The School Board of Palm Beach County v. The Morganti Group, Inc., as successor in interest to Morganti Florida, Inc., Morganti Florida, Inc. and M.P.A. Architects , Inc. f/k/a Marion, Puluga & Associates. in an amount not-to-exceed $25,000.00 to Liberty Diagnostics, LLC d/b/a LBFG and authorize the Superintendent and Board Chairman to sign all required documents.

Contact:

Gerald A. Williams, Esq.; Chief Counsel williamsg@palmbeach.k12.fl.us



Blair R. LittleJohn, III, Esq.; Senior Counsel



Kathelyn M. Jacques-Adams, Esq.; Associate Counsel

Financial Impact:

1.  Outside Counsel Contract with Weiss Serota Helfman Pastoriza Cole & Boniske, P.L.



There is no financial impact with regard to the Weiss Serota Helfman Pastoriza Cole & Boniske contract extension.



 



2.  Consultant Contract with Liberty Diagnostics, LLC d/b/a LBFG



The financial impact for the Liberty Diagnostics contract is $25,000.00 from the Legal Services Operational Budget.
