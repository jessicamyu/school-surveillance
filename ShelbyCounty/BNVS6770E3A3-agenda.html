7.1 Contract: Book Systems, Inc. for Integrated Library and Textbook Automation System
Meeting
May 19, 2020 - May Work Session
Category
7. Staff Action Items
Type
Action (Consent)
Dollar Amount
$288,931.50
Budgeted
Yes
Budget Source
01-General Funds
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the contract to Book Systems, Inc. in the amount of $288,931.50 for an initial term of three (3) years to provide an automated, integrated and centralized library and textbook system. The total amount of $288,931.50 includes an annual cost of $87,555.00 for software licenses for a total of $262,665.00 for the three-year term and a 10% Board contingency of $26,266.50. The Board contingency will be used to cover the replacement of District’s barcode scanners, textbook smart barcodes and barcode label protectors. The agreement includes the option to renew for two (2) additional one (1) year periods at a cost of $87,555.00 each, provided that the maximum duration of the agreement does not exceed five (5) years.
Goals
Priority 1: Strengthening Early Literacy
Priority 2: College and Career Readiness

Responsible Party: Dr. Antonio Burt, Chief Academic Officer
