Category

$250,000.00
Budgeted

Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education accepts the year two funding of the multi-year contract with Cognia, Inc. to ensure continued support in implementing the school turnaround plans for Hawkins Mill Elementary School and Trezevant High School. The individual turnaround plans for Hawkins Mill Elementary and Trezevant High have been developed with input from school faculty, students, parents/guardians, community partners, district personnel, and Cognia, Inc. school turnaround leaders. Each plan was carefully crafted as a result of the discovery process which required a thorough analysis of current and pertinent data gathered from interviews, a tour of the surrounding community, classroom observations, focus groups, and surveys. The school turnaround plans are grounded in strategies and action steps designed to address the priority needs identified during the discovery process.

Responsible Party: Dr. Angela Whitelaw, Deputy Superintendent of Schools & Academic Support
