The implementation started in January 2016, and is estimated to go live by no later than September 2017, at a two-year cost of $1,894,799.75 for software and hosting fees, and migration, implementation, and training services. This scope of work will increase the total cost of the project to $2,020,799.75.

Master Clean for the provisions of custodial cleaning services for the Shelby County School District. The District’s
Zones 1 and 3 will be awarded to Service Master Clean at a cost of $12,744,643.90 annually. The District’s Zone 2 will

services will be $19,636,427.90. The initial service term is for the 2017-2018 school year with the option of extending
the terms of the agreements for four (4) additional terms of one (1) year each, provided that the maximum duration of
the agreements shall not exceed five (5) years.
