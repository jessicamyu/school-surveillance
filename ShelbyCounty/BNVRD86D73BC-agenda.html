6. Public Comment
Subject
6.1 Public Comments to the Board
Meeting
Apr 28, 2020 - April Board Business Meeting
Category
6. Public Comment
Type
Information, Procedural

As practiced at all SCS Board Business Meetings, a Public Comment Submission period will open prior to the start of the meeting. Any citizen interested in providing public comment for the April Business Meeting must submit their comment through email at BoardPublicComments@scsk12.org, between 4:00 p.m. and 5:15 p.m. on Tuesday, April 28, 2020. *

 

Please include your name, home address, organization represented, if any, and subject of your comment in the email. Comments will not be read unless these items are included.

 

*Comments will be read in the order in which they are received, and in accordance with Board Policy 0000 School Board Meetings: IV Public Comment. Public comments will share the 30-minute time slot, in equal increments of time, not to exceed three (3) minutes each. In the event that a public comment is not read in its entirety within the individual allotted time, a full copy will be accessible to the Board.
