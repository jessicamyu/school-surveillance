Budget Source
Fund 07- Capital Projects fund - Facilities Mangement
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the bid from Martin and White Mechanical, Inc. in the amount of $2,688,759.00 to provide and install roof top units and split systems at Germantown High School, Buildings A, B & V. The total amount of $2,688,759.00 consists of the base bid of $2,413,472.00, performance bond cost of $30,855.00, and a 10% Board contingency of $244,432.00. The Board contingency will be used, if needed, to cover unforeseen conditions encountered while this project is in progress.

Responsible Department:Beth Phalen, Business Operations
