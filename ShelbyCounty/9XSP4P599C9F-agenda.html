Dollar Amount
$30,755.04
Budgeted
Yes
Budget Source
Fund 8- Special Revenue Fund- TLE Gates Fund
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the proposal from International Business Machines (IBM) in the amount of $30,755.04 annually to provide a Compensation Administration Software System that will automate the market analysis process as well as provide tools necessary to conduct market evaluations and salary harmonization across the district. Market data available within the software will guarantee SCS is paying employees a fair and competitive salary as well as highlight any positions/employees that are currently overpaid. The cost of the Compensation Administration Software System includes ongoing licensing, training, maintenance and support. This project is funded through the Gates Foundation through the first three years and will not be charged toward district dollars.
The Compensation Administration Software System will also include a job description database that will house all district job descriptions in one centrally located place and maintain all descriptions in the same template. The system will allow for future budgeting as the district hopes to move toward a pay for performance model in the future. The agreement shall continue for a term of three (3) years and reserve SCS the unilateral option of extending the term of the agreement for two (2) additional terms of one (1) year each provided that the maximum duration of the agreement will not exceed five (5) years.
Goals

Responsible Department:   Human Resources, Trinette Small
