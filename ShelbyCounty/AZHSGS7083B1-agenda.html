Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the purchase of cameras, license, servers, card access, and Airphone equipment from ADI/Honeywell, Cyber Technology Security, and System Integrations Incorporated in the amount of $815,837.00. This award will allow the District to continue with the standardization of a stable surveillance system that will provide each location with adequate camera coverage and data storage capacity. The initial term of this bid award is one year with the option to extend the terms for (2) additional one (1) year terms for a total of three (3) years.
