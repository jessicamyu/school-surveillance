11.2 Plan Approval: Return of 3 Achievement School District (ASD) Schools to Memphis-Shelby County Schools (MSCS)
Meeting
Apr 26, 2022 - April Board Business Meeting
Category
11. Consent Agenda
Type
Action (Consent)
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the following actions to aid with the transition of Frayser Achievement Elementary School, Corning Achievement Elementary School, Georgian Hills Achievement Elementary, and Whitney Achievement School from the Achievement School District (ASD) to Memphis-Shelby County Schools (MSCS) for the 2022-2023 school year: (1) MSCS will apply to open three schools with these names: Frayser-Corning Elementary; Georgian Hills Elementary; and Whitney Elementary. (2) MSCS will maintain the attendance zones already established by the ASD for these three schools. (3) The Board waives requirements of Policy 1007 and 6009 regarding community meetings and public input for the purposes of naming and attendance zones although community meetings will still be held to assist parents with the transition.
Goals
Initiative 2: Recruit and retain the best district leaders and teachers in the nation, immerse them in professional development to embrace and teach foundational literacy skill concepts, and entrench them in the community and classroom;
Initiative 3: Create relevant and equitable academic choices and learning environments to ensure scholars are prepared for the global workforce

Responsible Party: Dr. Angela Whitelaw, Deputy Superintendent of Schools and Academic Support

Category

Type
Action (Consent)
Dollar Amount

Budgeted

Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the award to Thomas Consultants in the amount of $269,140.00, and Broadway Typewriter Company Inc. (DBA Arey Jones Educational Solutions) in the amount of $6,090.00, for a total amount of $275,230.00 for the purchase of a laptop cart with twenty laptops and deployment of each for twenty, K-5 ESL M-SCS classrooms. The purchase from Thomas Consultants includes 400 HP ProBook 440 G8 laptops, in the amount of $672.85 per laptop, and 20 carts for the unit cost of $469.00. The Broadway Typewriter Company Inc. (DBA Arey Jones Educational Solutions) amount of $6,090.00 includes 400 laptops with a deployment cost of $14.00 each, and 20 cart deployments, at a cost of $24.50 each. The ESL classroom laptop carts will provide access to enhanced digital differentiated language and content area instruction to schools with high English learner populations. The schools consist of the following: Belle Forest ES, Berclair ES, Brewster ES, Chimneyrock ES, Crump ES, Dexter ES, Egypt ES, Germanshire ES, Hickory Ridge ES, Jackson ES, Kingsbury ES, Lowrance School, Macon Hall ES, Oakhaven ES, Parkway Village School, Shelby Oaks ES, Sherwood ES, Snowden School, Treadwell ES, and White Station ES.
