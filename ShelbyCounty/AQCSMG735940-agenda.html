7.9 Group Short Term –Long Term Disability Insurance
Meeting
Sep 19, 2017 - Work Session
Category
7. Staff Action Items
Type
Action (Consent)
Dollar Amount
$3,520,000.00
Budgeted
Yes
Budget Source
Employee Funded
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the proposal from MetLife in the amount up to $3,520,000.00 to provide insured group insurance plan for short term disability (STD) 30 day elimination period and long term disability (LTD) insurance. This is the least expensive combination plan for both STD and LTD insurance plan. Two short term disability options and one long term disability insurance option will be offered to approximately 11,084 active employees. The insurance will be available to Shelby County Schools (SCS) active employees will be effective January 1, 2018. The insurance premiums are 100% paid by employees. The term of the agreement is for one (1) year with the option of unilateral option of extending term of the agreement for two (2) additional terms of one (1) year each, provided that the maximum duration of the agreement will not exceed three (3) years.

Responsible Department: Trinette Small, Chief of Human Resources
