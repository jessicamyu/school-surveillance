CALL FOR 2022 LEGISLATIVE PROPOSALS

We want to hear from you! Legislative advocacy is a high priority for Shelby County Schools and as we approach the upcoming legislative session, the District has an opportunity to promote legislation at the state-level to support the needs of our students, families, staff and community. In order to effectively develop our legislative priorities and positions, we are soliciting input regarding issues relevant to your area of work that you believe should be addressed during the upcoming legislative session. If you have a proposal(s) that you would like to be considered for the District’s 2022 Legislative Agenda, please submit a completed Legislative Proposal Form by the close of business, August 20, 2021.

 

If you have questions and/or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact Maria Stewart at stewartma1@scsk12.org.
