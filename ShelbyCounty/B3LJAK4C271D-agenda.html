It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approve a 1-year renewable $200,000 (annually) professional services contract to Educational Epiphany for teachers, teacher leaders, school based teams (ILTs), assistant principals, principals, district content leads, and principal supervisors (ILDs) i n Shelby County Schools.
