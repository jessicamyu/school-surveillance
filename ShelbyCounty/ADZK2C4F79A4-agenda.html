Budget Source
Fund 10 - Nutrition Services
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approve the proposals of LEAN Frog Business Solutions, Inc. in the amount of $136,672.04 to provide services to assess Nutrition Services’ food service management processes and systems, including interfaces with District Financial Accounting, Accounts Payable and Procurement; assist in preparing a request for proposals (RFP) to enhance or replace the existing food services management system and assist in evaluating vendor RFP responses; and provide an assessment of organizational readiness to implement the recommended solution.

The agreement shall continue for a term of six months and reserves SCBE the unilateral option of extending the term of the agreement for an additional six month term provided that the maximum duration of the agreement will not exceed one (1) year.

Priority 3: Develop Teacher, Leaders, and Central Office to Drive Student Success

Budget Source
Fund 7 - Capital Improvement Projects
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the award to Medford Roofing and Repair in the amount of $161,437.50 for the Brookmeade Elementary School Roof Replacement Project. The total amount of $161,437.50 consists of the base bid of $150,000.00, performance bond cost of $3,750.00 and a 5% Board contingency of $7,687.50. The Board contingency will be used, if needed, to cover unforeseen conditions encountered while this project is in progress.
