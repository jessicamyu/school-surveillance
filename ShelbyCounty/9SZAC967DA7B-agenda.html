Budget Source
Fund 07 – Building Improvements – Facilities Management
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the award to Watson Roofing & Associates, LLC in the amount of $101,272.50 for the Roof Replacement at Delano Elementary School. The total amount of $101,272.50 consists of the base bid of $94,038.75, performance bond cost $2,411.25 and a 5% board contingency of $4,822.50. The board contingency will be used, if needed, to cover unforeseen conditions encountered while this project is in progress.
