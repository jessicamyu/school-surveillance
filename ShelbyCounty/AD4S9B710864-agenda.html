Fund 12 – Head Start Fund – Other Capital Outlay
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the bid from Wilco Services LLC Contractors, Inc. in the amount of $103,805.10 for construction and installation of playgrounds for Early Childhood District Locations. The total amount of $103,805.10 consists of the base bid of $ 96,450.00, performance bond cost of $2,412.00 and a 5% Board contingency of $4,943.10. The Board contingency will be used, if needed, to cover unforeseen conditions encountered while this project is in progress.
The playgrounds will meet Federal Mandated Guidelines and are SCS/ADA Early Childhood approved for ages two through twelve to help enhance physical fitness, coordination skills and the overall educational process. The playgrounds will be constructed and installed for the following Early Childhood District Locations: Sheffield Elementary School and Ross Elementary School.

Responsible Department:  Dr. Heidi Ramirez, Academics
