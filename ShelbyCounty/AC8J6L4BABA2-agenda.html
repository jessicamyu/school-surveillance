Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the resolution to accept the administration’s recommendations for the approval of five (5) amended charter applications: Crosstown High School, Inc.; Gateway University Schools of Applied Sciences, Inc.; Legacy Leadership Academy; Kaleidoscope Schools; and Artesian Schools, Inc.; and the denial of three (3) amended charter applications: Green Dot Public Schools Tennessee; Pathways in Education and The LeFlore Foundation.

Responsible Department: Brad Leon, Strategy and Innovation

 

Amended Charter Applications for Approval:

  • Crosstown High School, Inc.
  • Gateway University Schools of Applied Sciences, Inc.
  • Legacy Leadership Academy
  • Kaleidoscope Schools
  • Artesian Schools

 

Amended Charter Applications for Denial:

  • Green Dot Public Schools Tennessee
  • Pathways in Education 
  • The LeFlore Foundation
