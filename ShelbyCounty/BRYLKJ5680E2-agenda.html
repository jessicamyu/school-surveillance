Aug 5, 2020 - Procurement Committee Meeting
Category
2. Discussion Items
Type

This item was moved to the August Procurement Committee Meeting by vote of the Shelby County Board of Education during the July Board Business Meeting.

 

 

Original recommendation:

 
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the contract with RCM Healthcare Services in the amount not to exceed $1,816,446.00 to provide thirty-six (36) registered nurses for the District’s in-school nursing services for the 2020-2021 school year. The hourly billing rate is $36.50 for 7.5 hours per day. This agreement shall continue for a term of one (1) year with the option to renew the contract for two (2) additional, one (1) year terms provided that the maximum duration of the Agreement shall not exceed three (3) years.
 
Original Dollar Amount:
1,816,446.00
 
 
*To be discussed at a later time per General Counsel.

Subject
2.06 Lakeshore Distance Learning Box
Meeting
Aug 5, 2020 - Procurement Committee Meeting
Category
2. Discussion Items
Type

All students, including Pre-K, will begin the school year virtually.

 

• The Pre-K experience is typically designed to be hands on and engaging with adults and peers.

 

• Many families do not have the resources to provide developmentally appropriate materials for students during this virtual learning period.

 

• In efforts to ensure our students and parents have what they need to be success in the virtual learning space we are providing access to developmentally appropriate instruction, supplies, and materials.

 

• In collaboration with Lakeshore we have created a custom remote learning toolkit. Boxes will include pencils, scissors, glue sticks, crayons, markers, erasers, paint, paintbrushes, playdough, tactile alphabet match-ups, counting match-ups, unifix cubes, small vehicles, ball, sidewalk chalk, journal, beanbags and activity cards, color paddles, magnifier, magnetic wand, egg shapers, and table blocks. Total Cost: $570,000
