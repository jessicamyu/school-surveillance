Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the proposal from iCIMS in the amount of $134,000.00 to provide a cloud based Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) Applicant Tracking System for the Shelby County School District. The total amount of $134,000 consists of an annual licensing fee of $84,000 and a one-time setup fee of $50,000. The Applicant Tracking System will provide Human Resources with a solution for tracking applications and will reduce the manual labor hours currently required for applicant screening. The cost of the Applicant Tracking System includes licensing, training, maintenance and support. This project is funded through the Gates Foundation Grant.
The agreement shall continue for a term of one (1) year and reserve SCS the unilateral option of extending the term of the agreement for four (4) additional terms of one (1) year each provided that the maximum duration of the agreement will not exceed five (5) years.

Human Resources, Chief Small
