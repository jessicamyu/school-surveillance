6. Public Comment
Subject
6.1 Public Comments to the Board
Meeting
Aug 31, 2021 - August Board Business Meeting
Category
6. Public Comment
Type
Information, Procedural

The Citizens’ Sign in for Public Comment will begin at 5pm on the day of the Board Meeting. The Citizens’ sign in sheet will be located in the Board Auditorium on the podium in the middle of the room. Citizens’ sign in will end no later than the “Approval of the Agenda” during the Board Meeting.

 

Please include your name, home address, and the topic of your comment on the form.

 

During the Public Comment Portion, the Chair will call your name to inform you that your turn to speak is approaching. She will then call your name for you to approach the microphone. After you have completed your comment or the timer ends, please return to your seat.  

 

The Board Chair will make the following statement at the beginning of the Public Comment portion of the meeting as a reminder:

 

Tennessee law makes no specific provisions for the participation of the public at meetings of local boards of education. However, Board Policy provides that not more than, thirty (30) minutes of each Regular Business and Special Called Meeting shall be devoted to public comment.

 

Speakers must state their name, home address, organization he/she is representing, if any, and subject of the presentation before they are permitted to proceed. Speakers may offer objective comments about school operations and programs that concern them. Speakers are asked to refrain from using names of personnel or names of persons connected with the school system, particularly when lodging a complaint.

Speakers will not be permitted to engage in gossip, make defamatory comments, or use abusive or vulgar language. The Chairman shall have the authority to terminate the remarks of any individual who is disruptive or does not adhere to Board rules.

 

Thank you for your interest in and support of the Shelby County Board of Education and Shelby County Schools. We are 901!
