Dollar Amount
$19,636,427.90
Budget Source
Fund 01- General Fund
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the proposals from Aramark and Service
Master Clean for the provisions of custodial cleaning services for the Shelby County School District. The District’s
Zones 1 and 3 will be awarded to Service Master Clean at a cost of $12,744,643.90 annually. The District’s Zone 2 will
be awarded to Aramark at a cost of $6,891,784.00 annually. The District’s total annual cost for custodial cleaning
services will be $19,636,427.90. The initial service term is for the 2017-2018 school year with the option of extending
the terms of the agreements for four (4) additional terms of one (1) year each, provided that the maximum duration of
the agreements shall not exceed five (5) years.
Goals
Priority 3: Develop Teacher, Leaders, and Central Office to Drive Student Success

Responsible Department:Cerita Butler, Business Operations

Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the contract with PowerSchool Group , LLC in the amount of $126,000 for historical data migration services related to the upgrade of the Shelby County Schools PowerSchool license to Release 9.x, and migration of SCS’ student information system from PowerSchool SMS to PowerSchool. The implementation started in January 2016, and is estimated to go live by no later than September 2017, at a two-year cost of $1,894,799.75 for software and hosting fees, and migration, implementation, and training services. This scope of work will increase the total cost of the project to $2,020,799.75.
