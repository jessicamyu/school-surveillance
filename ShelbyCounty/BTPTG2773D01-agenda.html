Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the contract to Rethink Ed, Inc. in the amount of $330,000 for year one, and $330,000 for each option to renew years for a total of $1,320,000.00 if the three options are exercised. The purchase will be used to provide a Social Emotional Learning (SEL) curricula digital platform that is evidence-based program developed for educators and K-12 students. The program is designed for easy implementation and is flexible with a scalable solution that empowers educators to successfully integrate and build a culture around Social Emotional Learning competencies to students. The agreement includes three (3) additional one (1) year periods, provided that the maximum duration of the agreement does not exceed four (4) year
Goals
Priority 1: Strengthening Early Literacy
Priority 2: College and Career Readiness
