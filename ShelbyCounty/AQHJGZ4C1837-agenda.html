Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves a contract with Tripod Education Partners, Inc. to conduct a comprehensive review of all district CTE programs to (i) assess the impact of CTE on student performance, graduation and preparation for college and a career, (ii) examine the strength of the relationships between high school career preparation programs and the regional workforce needs of Shelby County businesses and industries, and (iii) redesign the technical centers and ensure high-quality career pathway programs in all comprehensive high schools that offer CTE, from August 1, 2017 to June 30, 2018, at a cost of $163,242.50.
Goals
Priority 2: College and Career Readiness
Priority 3: Develop Teachers, Leaders, & Central Office
Priority 4: High-Quality School Options

Responsible Department: Academics (Career and Technical Education)

Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the bid from JD Ventures LLC dba JDRoof.com in the amount of $191,056.44 to provide emergency roof replacement services for Georgian Hills Elementary School. The total amount of $191,056.44 consists of the base bid of $177,672.80, performance bond cost of $4,500.00, and a 5% Board contingency of $8,883.64. The Board contingency will be used, if needed, to cover unforeseen conditions encountered while this project is in progress.
