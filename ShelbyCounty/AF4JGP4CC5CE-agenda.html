Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the bid from Dunbar Armored Inc. in the amount of $335,865.20 for District wide bank deposit pick-up services. The total cost of $335,865.20 consists of the $285,865.20 first year cost for the regularly scheduled school year pick-up services and an estimated cost of $50,000 for summer school unscheduled pick-up service. If the District decides to exercise the additional option years, the cost per pick up for year two (2) is $10.69 for a total year two cost of $344,402.60, and a year three (3) cost per pick up of $11.01 with a total year three (3) cost of $353.215.40.
