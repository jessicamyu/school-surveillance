Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the listed amounts as indicated as the best proposals from Thomas Consultants Inc., Virtucom Inc., CDW Inc., and Unistar-Sparco Computers, Inc., for PC computer equipment and related services. The estimated annual spend for these purchase is not to exceed $15,000,000.00. Services include: installation, configuration, custom imaging, installing software (Microsoft Office), on-site delivery and setup, and order and invoice management. The term of the agreement is for one (1) year with the option of extending the agreement for two (4) additional terms of one (1) year each not to exceed (5) years.

Responsible Department: John Williams, Information Technology
