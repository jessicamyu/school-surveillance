Meeting
Dec 15, 2015 - Board Business Meeting
Category
11. Consent Agenda
Type
Action (Consent)
Fiscal Impact
Yes
Dollar Amount
$200,000.00
Budgeted
Yes
Budget Source
Fund 08 - Discretionary Fund Teacher Effectiveness Grant
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the contract with Margarita Calderón and Associates to provide a cohort of schools with high English Language Learner (ELL) populations professional learning focused on building College and Career Ready and Comprehensive Literacy Improvement Plan aligned student vocabulary and comprehension. Margarita Calderón and Associates will provide professional development through the Expediting Comprehension for English Language Learners (ExC-ELL™) Institute, and ExC-ELL™ support and coaching following the Institute. ExC-ELL is an empirically tested model that focuses on teaching academic language, reading comprehension, and text-based writing integrated into the content instruction. The cost through June 30, 2016 should not exceed $200,000.00.
