Budgeted

Budget Source
Fund 82- Health Fund
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the proposal from Cigna in the amount of $24,140,000.00 (annual cost) be approved to provide self-funded group medical plan administrative services for Shelby County Schools (SCS) active employees and pre-65 retirees effective September 1, 2015 at a cost of $1,920,000.00; and a fully-insured Medicare Supplement Plan for post-65 retirees effective January 1, 2016 at a cost of $22,220,000.00. This cost is for full service Healthcare Plan administration, and a Medicare Supplement Plan for one (1) year for a total population of approximately 14,000 active employees/pre-65 retirees, and 5,500 post-65 retirees. The agreement shall reserve for SCS the unilateral option of extending the initial term of the agreement for two (2) additional terms of one (1) year each, provided that the maximum duration of the agreement will not exceed three (3) years. The annual administrative services cost is exclusive of active and pre-65 retiree claims and other fixed costs.
Cigna has proposed an administrative services fee of $13.00 per employee per month (PEPM) for administering the self-funded group medical insurance plan. The $13.00 PEPM fee is fixed for the term of the agreement with no annual rate increases.

Responsible Department:  Trinette Small, Human Resources
