7.15 Security Cameras

Category
7. Staff Action Items
Type
Action (Consent)
Dollar Amount
$1,000,000.00
Budget Source
Fund-01 Safety and Security & Fund-01 Safe School Grant
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the purchase of Security Cameras from Shoney’s Electronic Repairs, Thomas Consultants Inc., and Oteka Technologies for the estimated award amount of $1,000,000.00. This amount will be for the purchase of security cameras and licenses to upgrade the current security camera system infrastructure for multiple Shelby County School buildings throughout the District. This award will allow the District to continue with the standardization of a stable surveillance system that will provide each location with adequate camera coverage and data storage capacity. The initial term of this bid award is one (1) year. This bid award has the option of extending the terms of the agreement for four (4) additional one (1) year terms. Estimated total cost for the 2017-2018 school year is $1,000,000.00
