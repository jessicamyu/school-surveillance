6. Public Comment
Subject
6.1 Public Comments to the Board
Meeting
Feb 23, 2021 - February Board Business Meeting
Category
6. Public Comment
Type
Information, Procedural

As practiced at all SCS Board Business/Special Called Meetings, a Public Comment Submission period will open prior to the start of the meeting. Any citizen interested in providing public comment for this Meeting must submit their comment through email at BoardPublicComments@scsk12.org, between 4:00 p.m. and 5:15 p.m. on day of meeting.

 

Please include your name, home address, organization represented, if any, and subject of your comment in the email. Comments will not be read unless these items are included. Please see time limits below.

 

*Comments will be read in the order in which they are received, and in accordance with Board Policy 0000 School Board Meetings: IV Public Comment. Public comments will share the 30-minute time slot, in equal increments of time, not to exceed three (3) minutes each. In the event that a public comment is not read in its entirety within the individual allotted time, a full copy will be accessible to the Board.

Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the contract to Educational Epiphany in the amount not to exceed $5,575,000.00 for a one-year term to provide literacy instructional materials and professional learning for Shelby County Schools’ staff and parents. Staff includes instructional paraprofessionals, teachers, teacher leaders, assistant principals, principals, principal supervisors (ILDs), content specialists, assistant superintendents, and key central office personnel. The scope of work is for a multi-tiered approach to district-wide professional learning which has a primary focus in literacy. The agreement includes four (4) additional one (1) year optional renewal periods provided that the maximum duration of the agreement does not exceed five (5) years: not to exceed $2,306,309.00 for option to renew year two, not to exceed $2,259,996.00 for option to renew year three, not to exceed $2,215,999.00 for option to renew year four, and not to exceed $2,174,202.00 for option to renew year five.
