Absolute Date

Budgeted

Budget Source
Fund 01- General Funds - Other Contracted Services
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the award to Book Systems Incorporated in the amount of $354,810.00 to provide an automated, integrated and centralized library and textbook system. The cost of the centralized library and textbook system includes $242,239.00 for software, $9,950.00 for professional development, $75,801.00 for supplies and $26,820.00 for scanning equipment for year one (1). Implementation of this automated system will allow the schools to use the same platform, providing the district with library collection numbers, circulation statistics and inventory, knowledge of textbook quantities, increased material accountability, unlimited user access, immediate feedback, increased student accountability through circulation records and instant access to textbook data.
The agreement shall continue for a term of three (3) years and SCS reserves the unilateral option of extending the term of the agreement for two (2) additional terms of one (1) year each.
Goals

Responsible Department:   Academics, Heidi A. Ramirez
