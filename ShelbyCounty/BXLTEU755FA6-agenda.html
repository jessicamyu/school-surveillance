7.1 Contract: Educational Epiphany for Literacy Materials and Professional Development
Meeting
Feb 16, 2021 - February Work Session
Category
7. Staff Action Items
Type
Action (Consent)
Dollar Amount
$5,575,000.00
Budgeted
Yes
Budget Source
Various Budgets
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the contract to Educational Epiphany in the amount not to exceed $5,575,000.00 for a one-year term to provide literacy instructional materials and professional learning for Shelby County Schools’ staff and parents. Staff includes instructional paraprofessionals, teachers, teacher leaders, assistant principals, principals, principal supervisors (ILDs), content specialists, assistant superintendents, and key central office personnel. The scope of work is for a multi-tiered approach to district-wide professional learning which has a primary focus in literacy. The agreement includes four (4) additional one (1) year optional renewal periods provided that the maximum duration of the agreement does not exceed five (5) years: not to exceed $2,306,309.00 for option to renew year two, not to exceed $2,259,996.00 for option to renew year three, not to exceed $2,215,999.00 for option to renew year four, and not to exceed $2,174,202.00 for option to renew year five.
Goals
Priority 1: Strengthening Early Literacy
Priority 2: College and Career Readiness
Priority 4: High-Quality School Options

Responsible Party: Dr. Angela Whitelaw, Deputy Superintendent of Schools and Academic Support
