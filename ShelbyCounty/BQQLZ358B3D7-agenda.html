Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves a contract with the Tennessee Book Company for the use of the Thrivist Platform for Shelby County Schools. The scope of the services to be provided includes ClassGather Learning Object Repository (LOR). The ClassGather is an online repository for managing and deploying publisher licenses and learning resources into the Canvas LMS.
