11.7 Office of Innovation: Virtual Schools- Learning Management System
Meeting
Aug 25, 2015 - Board Business Meeting
Category
11. Consent Agenda
Type
Action (Consent)
Dollar Amount
$179,500.00
Budget Source
Fund 1 - General Fund
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the proposal from Instructure Inc. in the amount of $179,500.00 to provide a Learning Management System (LMS) that will house online courses for student access and archive records of student performance for courses and virtual summer programs for the 2015-2016 school year.
The cost of the Learning Management System includes the purchase of licenses for 10,000 concurrent users, training and maintenance. The LMS allows the Virtual Learning office to monitor and record analytics associated with student grades and time logged into specific courses for grades 6-12.
The agreement shall continue for a term of one (1) year and reserve SCS the unilateral option of extending the term of the agreement for two (2) additional terms of one (1) year each provided that the maximum duration of the agreement will not exceed three (3) years.

Responsible Department: Brad Leon, Innovation
