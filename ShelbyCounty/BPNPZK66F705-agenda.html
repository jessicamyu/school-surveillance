Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves amendment requests for Freedom Preparatory Charter Schools (#8355) and Freedom Preparatory Academy (#8234).

The Item above is unfinished Business from the April Board Business Meeting and May 12, 2020 Special Called Meeting. The item was originally brought as one item recommending both Charter Approvals and Denials. Several Board Actions arose from the original, published item. The original recommendation is published below. All other actions may be found in the agenda from the April Business Meeting. Recommendations for Charter Approvals were approved during the business meeting. Recommendations for denials were approved during the May 12, 2020 Special Called Meeting. The Freedom Prep Schools were removed from the Denial List during the Special Called Meeting and are presented during this meeting as Unfinished Business.

 

 

Original Published Recommendation from the April Board Business Meeting: It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education denies amendment requests for Compass Midtown; Freedom Preparatory Academy Charter Schools (#8355); Freedom Preparatory Academy (#8234); Memphis Business Academy Elementary; Memphis STEM Academy; and approves amendment requests for Aurora Collegiate Academy; Arrow Academy School of Excellence; Leadership Preparatory Charter School; Dubois Elementary School of Entrepreneurship.

Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the contract to Book Systems, Inc. in the amount of $288,931.50 for an initial term of three (3) years to provide an automated, integrated and centralized library and textbook system. The total amount of $288,931.50 includes an annual cost of $87,555.00 for software licenses for a total of $262,665.00 for the three-year term and a 10% Board contingency of $26,266.50. The Board contingency will be used to cover the replacement of District’s barcode scanners, textbook smart barcodes and barcode label protectors. The agreement includes the option to renew for two (2) additional one (1) year periods at a cost of $87,555.00 each, provided that the maximum duration of the agreement does not exceed five (5) years.
Goals
Priority 1: Strengthening Early Literacy
Priority 2: College and Career Readiness
