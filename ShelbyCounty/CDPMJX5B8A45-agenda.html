7.7 Approval: Memphis Adult Virtual High School
Meeting
May 24, 2022 - CANCELLED- May Work Session
Category
7. Staff Action Items
Type
Action (Consent)
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the opening of Memphis Virtual Adult High School, effective in the 2022-23 SY. As a result of the legislative agenda item submitted by our district's school board’s Community Engagement Committee, House Bill No. 2553/PC 838 now allows Tennessee school districts to establish virtual adult high schools. Opening a Virtual Adult High School within MSCS emphasizes our district’s commitment to strengthening our community and workforce while maximizing the agency that families have in their K-12 students’ academic performance. Consistent with Key Performance Initiative #3, an adult high school will prepare adult learners with 21st-Century skills. The virtual component of this high school will eliminate the barriers that unequal access to brick-and-mortar school options may present.
Goals
Initiative 3: Create relevant and equitable academic choices and learning environments to ensure scholars are prepared for the global workforce

Responsible Party: Jaron Carson, Chief Academic Officer
