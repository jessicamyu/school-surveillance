7.1 ERP System
Meeting
May 23, 2017 - Work Session
Category
7. Staff Action Items
Type
Action (Consent)
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the proposal from Cherry Road Technologies Inc. to provide implementation of an Oracle Cloud based Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system for the Shelby County School District. The District’s total implementation cost for an ERP Cloud Based System will be $19,111,386 which consist of: CherryRoad Technologies Inc. contract in the amount of $7,240,638, Apriant Inc. for professional service fees in the not to exceed amount of $3,711,950, Oracle software licensing fees of $2,729,152 (2 year cost), local consultant costs in the not to exceed amount of $2,212,975, ADP Licensing Fee of $31,440, and a 20% board contingency cost of $3,185,231. The board contingency will be used, if needed, to cover unforeseen conditions and to cover support services required for a successful ERP implementation. The Oracle software licensing fees will not exceed $1,615,406 annually.

Responsible Department:John Williams: Information Technology
