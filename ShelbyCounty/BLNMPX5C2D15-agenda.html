Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the request for entering into a one-year partnership with Carnegie Learning for professional services in the amount of $356,404.69. Carnegie Learning will provide Additional Targeted Support and Improvement (ATSI) at five high schools (Craigmont HS, Kingsbury HS, Kirby HS, Overton HS, and Southwind HS) with innovative strategies and systems, professional development, on-site and virtual coaching, and planning support designed to increase teacher instructional capacities for teaching mathematics (Algebra, Geometry, and Algebra II) and increase student growth and achievement in mathematics. Students will benefit through collaborative, student-centered mathematics classrooms led by knowledgeable teachers.
