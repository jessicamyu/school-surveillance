Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the contract to The New Teacher Project (TNTP,) in the amount of $100,000, to fulfill the early literacy work outlined in the TDOE Early Literacy Networks Grant for FY22 and FY23, at $50,000 per year. This will help ensure K-2 teachers receive the professional learning needed to engage students in high-quality foundational literacy instruction using the district-adopted Wonders Curriculum. Additionally, the partnership with TNTP will yield a K-2 ELA implementation plan that documents the expectations for early literacy teaching and learning.
Goals
Initiative 1: Strengthen Early (K-2) and Continuing Literacy (3-12)

Responsible Party: Jaron Carson, Chief Academic Officer
