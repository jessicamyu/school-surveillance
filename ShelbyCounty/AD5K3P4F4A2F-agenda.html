Dollar Amount
$119,320.00
Budget Source
Fund 1 - General Fund
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the contract with Achieve 3000 for a reading program designed to ensure every student, regardless of reading level, attains college and career benchmarks through a proven methodology of differentiation. Achieve 3000 focuses specifically on developing reading and writing for nonfiction text, by making sure all students are delivered grade-appropriate content, differentiated to their specific Lexile level. The program, including professional development for teachers at the schools, will be available to an additional 1357 students at thirteen schools throughout the district, from September 1, 2016 to August 31, 2017, at a total cost not to exceed $119,320.00.

Responsible Department: Dr. Heidi Ramirez, Academics
