Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the request for Pomeroy to complete the Data Center network infrastructure Refresh in the amount of $139,831.29.

Responsible Department: John Williams, IT

 

The Data Center Refresh, is to upgrade the network infrastructure in the Data Center.  Major components of the network infrastructure are at end of life and out of warranty.  These upgrades are necessary to ensure that the district is able to conduct it primary business efficiently and with minimum risk.  Options that were considered included the upgrading of all components at a cost of $1M; upgrade with components from comparable vendors at a cost of $180K; use selected vendor with components from same manufacturer.
