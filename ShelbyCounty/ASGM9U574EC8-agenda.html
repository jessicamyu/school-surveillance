Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the Architect Services of John Pruett Architects for the design and construction project management of the six classroom addition at Delano Elementary School, in the amount of $100,000.00 plus a 10% reimbursable expense of $10,000.00 for a total amount of $110,000.00. Reimbursable expenses are in addition to the compensation for Basic and Additional Services and include actual expenditures made by the Architect/Engineer and its employees, associate Architect/Engineer, and consultants in the interest of the Project. The Architect/Engineer understands and agrees that $100,000.00 has been established as the maximum amount to be paid for all reimbursable expenses.
