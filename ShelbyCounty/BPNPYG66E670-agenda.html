7.24 Discussion: Digital Device Ancillary Purchases in Process Requiring Board Approval During June 30th Board Meeting
Meeting
Jun 23, 2020 - June Work Session
Category
7. Staff Action Items
Type
Discussion

 

 

Student Internet Connectivity

A recommendation will be submitted on June 30, 2020 for the Shelby County Board of Education to approve Internet Access providers to provide HotSpots - wireless internet access services for the District’s leased computer devices. The vendor(s) will provide a quantity of 24,000 mifi hotspots and the monthly internet connection services per device.  The term of the services will be for two (2) years. SCS will piggyback on existing State of Tennessee Agreements for Cellular Devices, Services, and Business Solutions.

Computer Tracking Software

A recommendation will be submitted on June 30, 2020 for the Shelby County Board of Education to approve the recommended vendor for the purchase of a quantity of 95,000 Computer Tracking software license subscriptions for the District’s leased computer devices. The subscription for the software will be for four (4) years based on the lease term agreement for the computer devices. The off-the-shelf software provides visibility and control features, plus remote scripting, self-healing for critical apps, investigation and recovery services, and sensitive data identification. The recommended award will be finalized the week of June 22, 2020. 

 

Rugged Cases for Devices

A recommendation will be submitted on June 30, 2020 for the Shelby County Board of Education to approve the recommended vendor for the purchase of a quantity of 70,300 rugged laptop cases for the District’s Microsoft leased computer devices. The rugged laptop case will be compatible for the Microsoft Surface Go P 4/64 computer device. The recommended award will be finalized the week of June 22, 2020.

 

Expansion of Online Curriculum for K-5 Virtual School

A recommendation will be submitted on June 30, 2020 for the Shelby County Board of Education to approve the recommended vendor for online courses for K-5 students consistent with the online curriculum supports currently provided to grades 6-12th.  The recommended award will be finalized the week of June 22, 2020.
