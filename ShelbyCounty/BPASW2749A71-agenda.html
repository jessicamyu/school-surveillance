6.8 Motion to Suspend Rule: Committee Reports and Adhering Amendments for Charter School Amendments-Denials
Meeting
May 11, 2020 - Special Called Meeting
Category
6. Request for Action
Type
Action
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education denies amendment requests for Compass Midtown; Freedom Preparatory Academy Charter Schools (#8355); Freedom Preparatory Academy (#8234); and Memphis STEM Academy.

Responsible Party: Dr. Angela Whitelaw, Deputy Superintendent of Schools and Academic Support

 

The Item above is unfinished Business from the April Board Business Meeting. The item was originally brought as one item recommending both Charter Approvals and Denials. Several Board Actions arose from the original, published item. The original recommendation is published below. All other actions may be found in the agenda from the April Business Meeting. Recommendations for Charter Approvals were approved during the business meeting. Recommendations for denials were remanded to the Academic Performance Committee.

 

 

Original Published Recommendation from the April Board Business Meeting: It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education denies amendment requests for Compass Midtown; Freedom Preparatory Academy Charter Schools (#8355); Freedom Preparatory Academy (#8234); Memphis Business Academy Elementary; Memphis STEM Academy; and approves amendment requests for Aurora Collegiate Academy; Arrow Academy School of Excellence; Leadership Preparatory Charter School; Dubois Elementary School of Entrepreneurship.
