Budgeted

Budget Source
Fund 8 - Special Revenue Fund- Gates Fund, Fund 12 - Federal Funds
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the proposal from Truenorthlogic in the amount of $974,000.00 to provide Professional Learning Management System for the Shelby County School District. The total amount of $974,000.00 consists of a base contract award annual licensing fee of $842,325.00, a one-time setup fee of $49,000.00 and a training contingency amount of $82,675.00. The Professional Learning Management System software will provide both non-instructional and instructional professional access to a comprehensive, role based talent management system that enables each user the ability to take a personalized approach to professional development growth. The system also includes evaluation and calibration systems that support observations, training, and multiple measures. As part of this solution, Truenorthlogic will partner with School Improvement Network to provide video content which includes access to a library of teaching videos, courses, and other aligned resources.
The cost of the Professional Learning Management System includes licensing, training, maintenance, and support. This project is funded through the Gates Foundation Grant and Federal Funds.
The agreement shall continue for a term of one (1) year and reserves SCS the unilateral option of extending the term of the agreement for three (3) additional terms of one (1) year each provided that the maximum duration of the agreement will not exceed four (4) years.

Responsible Department:

 

Chief Ramirez, Academics
