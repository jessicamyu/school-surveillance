12.4 CNC Optimization Study - Savills Studley, Inc.
Meeting
Oct 25, 2016 - Board Business Meeting
Category
12. Requests for Action
Type
Action
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approve the proposal from Savills Studley, Inc. in the amount of $237,540.00 to provide professional services to optimize Shelby County Schools Nutrition Services’ product distribution and central production operation in the following phases:

· Phase I: Review current categories of products being procured and produced and determine which channel is optimal for each for the long-run. (This includes determining the optimal use, if any, of a warehouse and central production facility.)
· Phase II: Provide the specifications for the required warehouse facility (if any), plus the specifications for administrative offices and the required central production facility (if any).
· Phase III: As the District’s real estate broker identifies existing facilities and “green field” sites for purchase or lease, Savills Studley will determine whether the best path to a new facility is to purchase a “green field site” and build a new facility, purchase an existing facility and renovate it to meet the specifications developed in Phase II, or lease a facility that the owner and/or SCBE will improve to meet the specifications.
The agreement shall continue for a term of six months and reserves SCBE the unilateral option of extending the term of the agreement for an additional six month term provided that the maximum duration of the agreement will not exceed one (1) year.

Responsible Department: Cerita Butler, Business Operations
