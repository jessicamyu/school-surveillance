Motion & Voting

It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the Superintendent's recommendation for the approval of the charter school applications for Believe Memphis and Freedom Preparatory Charter School.

Motion by Teresa Jones, second by Kevin Woods.
Final Resolution: Motion Passes
Affirmative: Shante Avant, Miska Clay Bibbs, Chris Caldwell, Teresa Jones, Mike Kernell, Scott McCormick, Billy Orgel, Kevin Woods

It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education upholds the Superintendent's recommendation for the denial of charter school applications with the exception of Perea Elementary School.

Motion by Kevin Woods, second by Scott McCormick.
Final Resolution: Motion Passes
Affirmative: Shante Avant, Miska Clay Bibbs, Chris Caldwell, Teresa Jones, Mike Kernell, Stephanie P Love, Scott McCormick, Billy Orgel, Kevin Woods
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approves the charter application for Perea Elementary School.

Motion by Chris Caldwell, second by Kevin Woods.
Final Resolution: Motion Passes
