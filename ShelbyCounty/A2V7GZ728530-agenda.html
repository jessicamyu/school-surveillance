Budget Source
Fund 1-General Fund-Regular Instruction Support Budget
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Shelby County Board of Education approve the proposal of NCS Pearson, Inc. in the amount of $225,500.00 to provide a Credit Recovery & Intervention web-based curriculum system that will provide students in grades 9-12 with an integrated learning approach that is tailored to meet their individual instructional needs for the 2015-2016 school year. The system will allow students who have failed one or more courses with an opportunity to recoup credit needed for graduation. The cost of the Credit Recovery & Intervention System includes the purchase of District site licenses for (38) SCS locations, training and maintenance.
The agreement shall continue for a term of one (1) year and reserve SCS the unilateral option of extending the term of the agreement for two (2) additional terms of one (1) year each provided that the maximum duration of the agreement will not exceed three (3) years.

Responsible Department:

Dr. Heidi Ramirez, Chief Academic Office
