18. Athletics
Subject
A. Consider and Take Possible Action to Ratify and Approve the Interlocal Cooperative Agreement with the City of Dallas for the Use of the District's Natatoriums for Aquatics Programming (No Funding Required)
Meeting
Mar 8, 2018 - Board Briefing Agenda and Notice
Category
18. Athletics
Type
Information
Goals
Goal 6: All students will participate in at least one extracurricular or co-curricular activity each year.

The Interlocal Agreement provides the City of Dallas, Park and Recreation Department, with use of the district's natatoriums for aquatics programs that will benefit the district and the community-at-large. The proposed programs include swim lessons, water exercises, middle and high school swim training, and coaching clinics. The Park and Recreation Department will be responsible for the implementation and funding of the programs. The district would be responsible for the maintenance and operation of the natatoriums, and for providing assistance in the marketing of the programs to district students and local communities.
