Only members of the public who are properly registered will be allowed to speak during the meeting. No substitutions will be allowed.

Persons behaving in a manner inappropriate for a school setting or posing a substantial risk of harm to either themselves or to others, to include but not limited, to crossing the roped off barriers will be asked to leave the premises. Failure to leave the premises peacefully, upon request, will result in the person being ejected from the premises by law enforcement officials. (See Board Policy GKA (Legal)(Local)).
