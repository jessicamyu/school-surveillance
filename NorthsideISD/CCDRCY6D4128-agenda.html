Tuesday, April 14, 2020
Called Meeting of the Board of Trustees
Pursuant to Governor Abbott's Temporary Suspension of Open Meetings Laws issued on March 16, 2020, this meeting will be conducted via videoconference, which will be audible to the Board and public and allow for two-way communication. The Board President, presiding officer or trustees will not be physically present at one location but a quorum will be required for the meeting on the videoconference. The public may access this meeting at https://nisd.webex.com/nisd/onstage/g.php?MTID=ec3edc68570ac8d197c4f687fe86aae47 An electronic copy of the agenda may be viewed at www.nisd.net/board/agendas A recording of the meeting will be available for viewing at www.nisd.net/board A public comments form must be completed prior to the beginning of the meeting if you wish to address the Board of Trustees on an agenda or non-agenda topic for regular meetings or an agenda item at called or special meetings. The form will be available online at the links identified below one hour prior: English: https://forms.gle/i3ijRLtfsZEr8zAK6 Spanish: https://forms.gle/ziLJBeFBJkNtDVoC7
Northside facilities are wheelchair accessible. Accessible parking spaces are available and designated by signage. Interpreters for the deaf must be requested at least 24 hours prior to the meeting by calling 397-8770.
