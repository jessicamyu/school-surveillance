3. Public Hearings
Subject
A. 2020-2021 Texas Academic Performance Report (TAPR)
Meeting
Feb 22, 2022 - Regular Meeting of the Board of Trustees
Category
3. Public Hearings
Type
Discussion

Texas Education Code Chapter 39 requires each district's board of trustees to publish an annual report that includes the Texas Academic Performance Report (TAPR), district accreditation status, student enrollment and academic performance, PEIMS financial standards report, a report of violent or criminal incidents, and information received from the Texas Higher Education Coordinating Board (THECB) for each high school campus.  The Board of Trustees shall hold a hearing for public discussion of the report and they shall widely disseminate the report within the district.

10. Curriculum and Instruction
Subject
A. First Reading and Approval of EIAA (Local)
Meeting
Feb 22, 2022 - Regular Meeting of the Board of Trustees
Category
10. Curriculum and Instruction
Type
Approval

EIAA (Local) outlines guidelines for grading and progress reports to parents including semester exams, exemptions, and exam weight.  Changes to this policy were brought in November 2020 to address the weight of the semester exam,  in February 2021 to address absences in the second semester and final average of the spring semester, and in October 2021 to change the final average required for the spring semester for exemptions.   Due to a COVID uptick which resulted in higher student absences, we are recommending that the requirement to have three or fewer absences to qualify for exemptions start on March 1, 2022 as opposed to the beginning of the second semester.  Revisions to this policy have been made accordingly and approval is requested.
