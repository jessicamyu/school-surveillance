O. 22-23 Staff Development Waiver
Meeting
Jun 28, 2022 - Regular Meeting of the Board of Trustees
Category
9. Consent Agenda
Type
Action (Consent)

In the 22-23 school year, TEA will offer a Staff Development Minutes Waiver that provides for a maximum of 2,100 total waiver minutes to use for professional development for districts.  The waiver is for staff development in place of student instruction; therefore, the waiver minutes are only applicable to staff development provided instead of student instruction during the school year.  The waiver may not be used prior to the first day of student instruction or after the last day of student instruction.  It is recommended that the Board adopt this waiver for 1,680 minutes for the November 8 and February 20 professional learning days and the November 21 and 22 teacher choice days.
