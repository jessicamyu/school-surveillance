Subject
A. Recognition of Health Careers HS Academic Decathlon Team, 2022 National Champions
Meeting
May 24, 2022 - Regular Meeting of the Board of Trustees
Category
6. Recognitions
Type
Information

The Academic Decathlon Team at Health Careers High School brought home top honors after competing in the USAD Nationals, placing first in Division IV. This is the second year in a row for Health Careers High School.

The team competed virtually this year as well, meaning the Speech and Interview portions were via Zoom, unlike the regional and state competitions that were held face-to-face. The team also received the Rookie of the Year award since it was their first time competing in this division at the national level.
