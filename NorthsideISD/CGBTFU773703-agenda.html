12. Business and Finance
Subject
A. Notice of Public Meeting to Discuss Budget and Proposed Tax Rate (Rene Barajas)
Meeting
Aug 2, 2022 - Called Meeting of the Board of Trustees
Category
12. Business and Finance
Type
Action (Consent), Approval

Presenter: RENE BARAJAS


Budget Provisions: N/A


Committee: N/A


Recommended Action:  N/A

 

The Board of Trustees is required by Section 44.004 of the Texas Education Code to provide for the publication of Notice of Public Meeting to Discuss Budget and Proposed Tax Rate ("Notice").  The Notice must contain the District's proposed tax rate for the year 2022, presented in two separate components:  the Maintenance & Operation (M&O) Rate and the Interest & Sinking (Debt) Rate.  The tax rate that is ultimately adopted for the year 2022 may not exceed the proposed rate shown in the Notice unless the District publishes a higher proposed tax rate in a revised notice and holds another public meeting to discuss the second notice.

The Board will vote on adoption of the final tax rate for the year 2022 at a later date.

It is recommended that the Board call a public meeting for August 23, 2022, to discuss the budget and proposed tax rate.
