Twelve Northside ISD Fine Arts students from eight high schools were selected to the Texas Music Educators’ Association (TMEA) All-State Band, Choir, Orchestra and Jazz bands.

Selection to Texas All-State is the highest public school award for individual achievement in music that a student can receive. It represents the top one percent of student musicians in Texas.

10. Curriculum and Instruction
Subject
A. HB 3 Reading Academies Proposal
Meeting
Mar 22, 2022 - Regular Meeting of the Board of Trustees
Category
10. Curriculum and Instruction
Type
Action
Recommended Action
APPROVAL

The HB 3 Reading Academies are research-based professional development courses passed by the 86th legislature in June 2019 requiring all K-4 teachers, including special education teachers and principals, to be enrolled in the HB 3 academics by the 22-23 school year.  Coursework includes 12 modules that require participants to dedicate a minimum 60 hours of work time.  A proposal will be brought to the Board regarding compensation using ESSER funds to the teachers and literacy leaders required to complete these academies.
