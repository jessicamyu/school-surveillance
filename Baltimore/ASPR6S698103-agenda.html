Meeting
Nov 21, 2017 - WORK SESSION OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION
Category
I. WORK SESSION REPORT - FY 17 COMPREHENSIVE ANNUAL FINANCIAL REPORT AND SINGLE AUDIT (Mr. Sarris) (7:35 - 7:45 p.m.)
Type
Report

The Comprehensive Annual Financial Report (CAFR) is prepared annually in compliance with the public school laws of the state of Maryland. All funds and accounts of the Board are included in this CAFR. While the Board is an entity created and governed by state law, it has been defined as a component unit of the Baltimore County government for financial reporting purposes. The financial statements for fiscal year 2017 have been audited by CliftonLarsonAllen LLP in accordance with state law. The independent auditor’s report is included in the financial section of the CAFR. As required as a condition of receipt of federal funds, CliftonLarsonAllen LLP also conducts an annual audit of federal funds. The fiscal year 2017 Single Audit of federal funds is also provided.

Subject
1. Report on the Proposed Boundary for Lansdowne Elementary School
Meeting
Nov 21, 2017 - WORK SESSION OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION
Category
K. WORK SESSION REPORT ON THE PROPOSED BOUNDARY FOR LANSDOWNE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL (first reading) (Dr. Brown/Dr. Martin-Knox) (7:55 - 8:25 p.m.)
Type
Report

On November 21, 2017, the Board of Education of Baltimore County (Board) will receive, for consideration and approval, a report from the Lansdowne Elementary School Community Boundary Study Committee recommending boundary changes to schools in the region to help relieve overcrowding.  The recommendation, known as Option A, affects the boundaries of Baltimore Highlands, Lansdowne, and Riverview elementary schools.  The Board's public hearing on the recommended boundary change is scheduled for Wednesday, December 6, 2017, at Lansdowne High School at 6:30 p.m.  The Board is scheduled to vote on the recommendation at its regular meeting on December 19, 2017.
