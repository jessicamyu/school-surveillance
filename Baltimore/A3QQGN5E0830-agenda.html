The Board of Education publishes the Comprehensive Annual Financial Report (CAFR).  Each year, student artwork is included in the CAFR. The fiscal year 2015 CAFR includes the work of middle school students, and these students are recognized at the Board meeting when the CAFR is presented to the Board.  Each participating student receives a gift certificate to Barnes & Noble book store.  The following students’ artwork was selected:

M. WORK SESSION REPORT - REPORT ON FY15 CAFR AND SINGLE AUDIT (Mr. Sarris)
Subject
1. Report on FY15 Comprehensive Annual Financial Report and Single Audit
Meeting
Nov 17, 2015 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
M. WORK SESSION REPORT - REPORT ON FY15 CAFR AND SINGLE AUDIT (Mr. Sarris)
Type
Report

The Comprehensive Annual Financial Report (CAFR) is prepared annually in compliance with the public school laws of the state of Maryland.  All funds and accounts of the Board are included in this CAFR.  While the Board is an entity created and governed by state law, it has been defined as a component unit of the Baltimore County government for financial reporting purposes.  The financial statements for fiscal year 2015 have been audited by CliftonLarsonAllen LLP in accordance with state law.  The independent auditor’s report is included in the financial section of the CAFR.  As required as a condition of receipt of federal funds, CliftonLarsonAllen LLP also conducts an annual audit of federal funds.  The fiscal year 2015 Single Audit of federal funds is also provided.
