J. WORK SESSION REPORTS--REPORT ON BOARD OF EDUCATION POLICIES--first reading (Ms. Williams)
Subject
1. Technical Amendments to Board Policies Required by Creation of the General Provisions Article of the Annotated Code of Maryland
Meeting
Sep 23, 2014 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
J. WORK SESSION REPORTS--REPORT ON BOARD OF EDUCATION POLICIES--first reading (Ms. Williams)
Type
Action (Consent)

During the 2014 Legislative Session, the Maryland General Assembly created a new article in the Annotated Code of Maryland.  This “General Provisions Article” goes into effect on October 1, 2014.  The Open Meetings Act, Public Information Act, and Public Ethics Law, which were previously a part of the State Government Article, have been moved to the General Provisions Article.

 

As a result, Board policies affected by this change have been edited to reflect the new legal reference.

 

The Superintendent asks that the Board of Education waive second and third reader and approve the technical amendments presented on the attached policies with an effective date of October 1, 2014.
