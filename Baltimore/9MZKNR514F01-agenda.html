F. SPECIAL ORDER OF BUSINESS - MABE (Mr. Smith)
Subject
1. Presentation of Maryland Association of Boards of Education (MABE) FY 2015 Pool Grant Award
Meeting
Sep 9, 2014 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
F. SPECIAL ORDER OF BUSINESS - MABE (Mr. Smith)
Type
Presentation

Through its Pool Grant Award, The Maryland Association of Boards of Education (MABE) supports and encourages the application of effective risk management strategies.  MABE awarded a $100,000 grant to fund installation of a swipe card reader system at eight elementary schools and one special center:

 

Baltimore Highlands Elementary School

Bear Creek Elementary School

Chesapeake Terrace Elementary School

Church Lane Elementary School

Deer Park Elementary School

Dogwood Elementary School

Dundalk Elementary School

Edmondson Heights Elementary School

Maiden Choice School

 

Mr. Steve James, Deputy Director, MABE, and Ms. Lisa Spencer, Risk Management Officer, MABE, will present the award to the Board president and discuss its significance.
