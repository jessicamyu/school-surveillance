P. NEW BUSINESS - FY 2019 CAPITAL BUDGET SUPPLEMENT (Mr. Smith, Mr. Sarris) (9:20 - 9:30 p.m.) - REMOVED FROM AGENDA
Subject
1. Consideration of the FY2019 Capital Budget Supplemental Appropriation
Meeting
Apr 9, 2019 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
P. NEW BUSINESS - FY 2019 CAPITAL BUDGET SUPPLEMENT (Mr. Smith, Mr. Sarris) (9:20 - 9:30 p.m.) - REMOVED FROM AGENDA
Type
Report

That the Board of Education approves the FY2019 supplemental appropriation to the capital budget of $137,354,000 from Baltimore County government.

 

The November 6, 2018, bond referendum was approved by the electorate for the construction of elementary and middle schools, to continue Baltimore County's aggressive Schools for Our Future program. The funding represents the County’s capital contribution for approved projects and allows BCPS to issue the necessary purchase orders to construction contractors prior to July 2019. The specific capital project and schools impacted by the supplemental appropriation include the construction of a new middle school on the Nottingham Site; the Pine Grove Middle School addition/renovation project; the replacement of Berkshire, Chadwick, Colgate, and Dundalk elementary schools; and the construction of a new elementary school on the Ridge Road site.

 

The Baltimore County Council approved this supplemental appropriation on November 19, 2018, in Bill No. 83-18.
