Subject
1. Consideration of a New Kiln at Prettyboy Elementary School
Meeting
May 10, 2016 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
N. NEW BUSINESS - SPECIAL PROJECT REQUEST - PRETTYBOY ELEMENTARY SCHOOL (Dr. Wheatley-Phillip)
Type
Action

Prettyboy Elementary School has been given a surplus kiln from the Office of Visual Arts within Baltimore County Public Schools.  The kiln at Prettyboy Elementary School is over 30 years old.  It is currently in a location in the art room that is accessible to all students.  The Prettyboy PTA and Hereford Recreation and Parks have donated a total of $8,292 in order to have the new kiln installed.

8. Students Count 2015 Update
Meeting
May 10, 2016 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
Q. INFORMATION
Type
Information

The Students Count 2015 Report was presented to the Board of Education as an information item on March 1, 2016.  With the approval of the boundary change for the Southwest area schools, the Students Count 2015 report must be updated to reflect the redistribution of projected enrollment among schools affected by the boundary change.

 

Also included in the update is the discontinuation of the Deep Creek Elementary kindergarten classes annexed to Hawthorne Elementary.  These classes will be returned to Deep Creek, their home school, starting school year 2016-2017.
