M. OLD BUSINESS - Naming of BCPS K - 8 School (Renaming of Imagine Discovery) (Dr. Allie/Ms. Thomas)
Subject
1. Consideration of the Proposed Name of the BCPS K - 8 School (Renaming of Imagine Discovery)
Meeting
May 6, 2014 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
M. OLD BUSINESS - Naming of BCPS K - 8 School (Renaming of Imagine Discovery) (Dr. Allie/Ms. Thomas)
Type
Action (Consent)

Dr. Catherine Allie, Assistant Superintendent, and Ms. Catherine Thomas, Principal, reported that in 2008, Imagine Discovery Charter School opened as the first charter school in Baltimore County.  Currently, Imagine Discovery operates as a charter school in Baltimore County as a K - 8 school with an enrollment of 550 students.  At the November 5, 2013, Board meeting, the Board of Education voted not to renew Imagine Discovery Charter School's contract.  As a result, Baltimore County Public Schools committed to assuming the operations of the school in its present location for two years to assist the families during this transition.  Since the school will now be a Baltimore County public school, parent meetings were held to determine the new name for the school.  The proposed recommended name for the school is “Scholars K – 8.”   This is the third reading.
