Subject
12. PULLED - Consideration of Disposal of Modular Classroom Structure
Meeting
Aug 12, 2014 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
O. NEW BUSINESS - CONTRACT AWARDS (Mr. Dixit/Ms. Burnopp)
Type
Action (Consent)

Since October of 2010, the Board of Education has owned a 2-bathroom, 16-classroom modular building located at Milford Mill Academy.  The modular building was utilized as swing space during the renovation of Milford Mill Academy.  The renovation project was completed in late 2012 and the modular building has remained unused since that time. The structure is now at the Pikesville High School site to be used as swing space for the major renovation and additions project at that school until 2017.
 

This modular building will no longer be needed at the completion of the Pikesville High School renovation and additions project.  Staff believes that the building will be, by that time, worn beyond any economically feasible use.  The practice of property disposal is consistent with Board of Education Policy 3640, Disposal of Surplus or Excess Property. Staff is recommending approval by the board to declare this structure surplus so that it can be demolished and remove at the conclusion of the Pikesville High School project.
