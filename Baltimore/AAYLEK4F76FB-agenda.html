M. NEW BUSINESS - TRANSFER AND SUPPLEMENTS (Mr. Sarris)
Subject
1. Consideration of the Transfer of Fiscal Year 2017 General Fund Balance
Meeting
Jul 12, 2016 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
M. NEW BUSINESS - TRANSFER AND SUPPLEMENTS (Mr. Sarris)
Type
Action

On May 17, 2016, the superintendent proposed that $20 million from the Baltimore County Public Schools’ general fund balance be transferred to Baltimore County Government in support of an $83 million accelerated plan to complete the air conditioning of Baltimore County schools by 2017.

 

When last reported on June 30, 2015, Baltimore County Public Schools’ general fund balance was approximately $47.5 million.  The general fund balance is projected to be approximately $62 million on June 30, 2016.  After allowing for $23.17 million appropriated from the general fund balance for FY2017 operating revenue and for the recommended transfer of $20 million to Baltimore County Government, the remaining general fund balance of $18.8 million is adequate for FY2017 operations.

 

It is anticipated that the Baltimore County Council will approve a supplemental capital appropriation of approximately $83 million for school air conditioning and that the FY2017 Baltimore County Public Schools’ capital budget will be amended accordingly.
