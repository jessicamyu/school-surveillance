L. NEW BUSINESS - SPECIAL PROJECT REQUESTS (Dr. Roberts and Ms. Byers) (8:35 - 8:45 p.m.)
Subject
1. Consideration of a Privately Funded Capital Project for Gunpowder Elementary School - Freestanding Sign Display
Meeting
Sep 14, 2021 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
L. NEW BUSINESS - SPECIAL PROJECT REQUESTS (Dr. Roberts and Ms. Byers) (8:35 - 8:45 p.m.)
Type
Action

The Gunpowder Elementary School PTA is donating $3,732 to cover the cost of the sign design preparation; fabrication and installation of one 3'-6' high x 4'-0" wide double faced non-illuminated painted aluminum freestanding sign display. This sign will be at the entrance of the newly revitalized playground area at Gunpowder Elementary School. 

 

This sign will be the final part of the Officer Caprio Playground project. The PTA at Gunpowder Elementary School began The Officer Caprio Playground Project in November 2019.

2. Consideration of a Privately Funded Capital Project for Timonium Elementary School - New Playground and Playground Improvements
Meeting
Sep 14, 2021 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
L. NEW BUSINESS - SPECIAL PROJECT REQUESTS (Dr. Roberts and Ms. Byers) (8:35 - 8:45 p.m.)
Type
Action

The Timonium Elementary PTA is proposing renovation of the existing playground and building of a new primary playground. The existing playground is currently used by kindergarten through fifth graders. Updates are needed to ensure that equipment is accessible and appropriate for students in all grades. The primary playground is needed for kindergarten through second grade students to ensure that the equipment is accessible and appropriate for primary students. The installation/renovation of these playgrounds will provide spaces that foster social and emotional skills through dramatic play including more engagement for students with special needs.
