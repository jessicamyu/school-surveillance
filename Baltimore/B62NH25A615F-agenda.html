Subject
1. Contingent Approval of the Watershed Public Charter School, Inc.
Meeting
Nov 20, 2018 - WORK SESSION OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION
Category
J. NEW BUSINESS - WATERSHED PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL, INC. (Dr. Adams) (7:55 - 8:15 p.m.)
Type

On September 26, 2018, the General Counsel notified members of the local board that the State Board had issued a decision in the matter of Watershed Public Charter School, Inc. v. Baltimore County Board of Education, MSBE Op. No. 18-31 (2018), overturning the local board’s denial of the charter school application.  

 

In its decision, the State Board directed the local board to grant contingent approval of the charter.  Additionally, the State Board ordered the school system and Watershed to establish a reasonable timeline--not to exceed 18-24 months--for Watershed to secure a facility and (if necessary) to renovate it. 

 

The Order being provided for the local board members’ signatures is consistent with the State Board’s directive and with the guidance received from the counsel to the State Board.  The Order grants contingent approval of the application, subject to Watershed’s ability to meet the agreed-upon timeline and subject to final approval by the local board regarding the suitability of that facility pursuant to Md. Code. Ann., Educ. Art. §9-104(a)(6).

K. WORK SESSION REPORT - FY2018 COMPREHENSIVE FINANCIAL REPORT AND SINGLE AUDIT (Mr. Sarris) (8:15 - 8:45 p.m.)
Type
Report

The Comprehensive Annual Financial Report (CAFR) is prepared annually in compliance with the public school laws of the state of Maryland.  All funds and accounts of the Board are included in this CAFR.  While the Board is an entity created and governed by state law, it has been defined as a component unit of the Baltimore County government for financial reporting purposes.  The financial statements for fiscal year 2018 have been audited by CliftonLarsonAllen LLP in accordance with state law.  The independent auditor’s report is included in the financial section of the CAFR.  As required as a condition of receipt of federal funds, CliftonLarsonAllen LLP also conducts an annual audit of federal funds.  The fiscal year 2018 Single Audit of federal funds is also provided.
