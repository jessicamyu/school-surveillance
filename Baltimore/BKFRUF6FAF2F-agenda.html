Subject
1. Report on the Pleasant Plains Elementary School Capacity Relief Boundary Recommendation
Meeting
Feb 11, 2020 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
N. NEW BUSINESS - REPORT ON THE PLEASANT PLAINS ELEMENTARY CAPACITY RELIEF BOUNDARY RECOMMENDATION (Dr. Wheatley-Phillip) (8:20 - 8:50 p.m.)
Type
Report

On February 11, 2020, the Board of Education of Baltimore County (Board) receives for consideration a recommendation for a boundary change from the Pleasant Plains Elementary Capacity Relief Boundary Study Committee.

 

The committee recommended Option B, which affects the boundaries for Hampton and Pleasant Plains elementary schools.

 

The Board's public hearing on the recommended boundary changes is scheduled for February 26, 2020, at Loch Raven High School (Snow Date:  February 27, 2020).  The Board will vote on the recommendations at the March 10, 2020, Board meeting.
