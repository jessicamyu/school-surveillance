May 5, 2020 - VIRTUAL MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
L. NEW BUSINESS - CONTRACT AWARDS (Dr. Scriven, Mr. Sarris, Mr. Dixit) (7:50 - 8:05 p.m.)
Type
Action (Consent)

An eight-year contract for 554 school buses was approved by the Board on December 1, 2015. Under this contract, BCPS will order 44 buses costing a total of $4,712,802.24 that are scheduled for replacement. On April 14, 2020, contracts to replace 43 general purpose fleet vehicles costing a total of $2,790,623 were approved by the Board. Under contract LKO-400-17, Financing of Various Vehicles, approved by the Board on April 17, 2017, BCPS will finance the purchase of 87 buses and vehicles totaling $7,503,425.24 over the term of five years. The settlement of the financing agreement is scheduled for May and the vehicles will be delivered after July 1, 2020. This annual authorization is related to the future issuance of the tax-exempt debt under IRS regulations.
