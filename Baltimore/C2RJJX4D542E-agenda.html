O. NEW BUSINESS - SPECIAL PROJECT REQUESTS (Ms. Byers) (8:45 - 8:55 p.m.)
Subject
1. Consideration of a Privately Funded Capital Project - Hereford High School Stadium Pavilion
Meeting
Jun 8, 2021 - HYBRID MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
O. NEW BUSINESS - SPECIAL PROJECT REQUESTS (Ms. Byers) (8:45 - 8:55 p.m.)
Type
Action

Taylor's Tribe, Inc., founded by Matthew Cunningham in memory of this son Taylor who died in a traffic accident while he was a senior at Hereford High School, is proposing to build a 30' x 40' pavilion in the Hereford High School Stadium complex. The installation of the pavilion will serve many purposes for the school and the community.  It can be used as an instructional space for physical education, science, and art classes, as well as being used as a gathering space for community groups, such as scouts and athletic teams. The structure will also provide shelter for those using the stadium and visiting teams. This project, including the maintenance, is being funded by Taylor's Tribe, Inc.

On Tuesday, July 14, 2020, the Board of Education of Baltimore County unanimously approved The Compass: Our Pathway to Excellence.  The Compass is our eight-year strategic plan designed to prepare each child in Baltimore County Public Schools to graduate ready to enter their chosen career, career training, military training, or credit-bearing college coursework.
