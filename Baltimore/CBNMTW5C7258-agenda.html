On February 8, 2022, the Board of Education of Baltimore County (Board) received for consideration and approval a report from the Northeast Area Elementary Boundary Study Committee recommending boundary changes to schools in the northeast region to help relieve overcrowding. The recommendation, known as Option 2, affects the boundaries of Elmwood, Fullerton, Joppa View, McCormick, Perry Hall, Red House Run, Shady Spring, and Vincent Farm elementary schools. The Board public hearing on the recommended boundary change was held on February 16, 2022. The Board will vote on the recommendation at the March 8, 2022, Board meeting.

Board Public Hearing - Board Policies 5550 and 5560 - School Climate and Student Discipline – Virtual

The Board of Education will hold a virtual public hearing on Board Policies 5550 and 5560 and school climate and student discipline on Wednesday, March 10, 2022, at 7:30 p.m. Registration to participate in public comment will be through the online registration form, which is now open and will close at 3 p.m. on the day before the hearing, Wednesday, March 9, 2022. Registrants will receive confirmation of their registration. Comments are limited to 3 minutes per speaker. No speaker substitutions will be allowed.
