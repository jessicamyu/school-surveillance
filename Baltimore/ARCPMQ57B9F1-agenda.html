1. 2017 Pupil Yield Study
Meeting
Oct 10, 2017 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
I. INFORMATION (9:05 p.m.)
Type
Information

Pupil yield factors are used across the country to help indicate the expected number of students a new residential development will likely generate over time. In accordance with the Baltimore County Adequate Public Facilities Ordinance (APFO) first adopted in 1996, Baltimore County Public Schools (BCPS) provides pupil yield factors to the Baltimore County Office of Planning. Baltimore County uses pupil yield factors to help determine the impact of proposed residential development on school overcrowding. This collaboration is further supported by Baltimore County Board of Education Policy and Superintendent’s Rule 7110 – Determining Needs, last revised in 2016.

 

In June of 2016 BCPS partnered with CropperGIS to conduct an update of the Pupil Yield Study. BCPS will use the revised pupil yield factors in the 2018-2027 projections analysis and will share the final results of the 2017 Pupil Yield Study in early fall 2017 for future incorporation in the APFO.
