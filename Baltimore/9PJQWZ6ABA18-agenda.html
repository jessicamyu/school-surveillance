M. NEW BUSINESS - REPORT ON ACADEMICS UPDATE (Ms. White)
Subject
1. Blueprint 2.0 Goal 1: Academics Update Accessing Tomorrow
Meeting
Nov 5, 2014 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
M. NEW BUSINESS - REPORT ON ACADEMICS UPDATE (Ms. White)
Type
Report

The Board of Education has requested a report on Accessing Tomorrow as an academics update.  For this report, Gus Schmedlen, Vice President of Worldwide Education for Hewlett Packard, will provide a global perspective of what schools and governments are facing as challenges in the 21st Century.  He will speak to the intersection of access and learning and the impact digital conversions, like S.T.A.T., are having across the globe.  He will also share his insights on the critical impact of digital conversions that ensure alignment with policy, readiness, deployment, and sustainability, and how education and econometrics are tied together.
