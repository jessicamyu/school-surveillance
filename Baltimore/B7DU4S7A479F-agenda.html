1. Consideration of the Privately Funded Capital Project Request for the Installation of New Playground Equipment at Essex Elementary School
Meeting
Jan 8, 2019 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY
Category
M. NEW BUSINESS - PRIVATELY FUNDED CAPITAL PROJECTS (Mr. Roberts) (8:30 - 8:40 p.m.)
Type
Action

This privately funded project to install new playground equipment at Essex Elementary School is fully funded by the Essex Elementary School PTA.  Existing playground equipment will be removed, and 36 pieces of new equipment will be installed.  This new playground will provide recess and physical activity equipment that is safer and more appropriate for younger students at school and in the community.

2. Consideration of the Privately Funded Capital Project Request for the Installation of a Playground at Edgemere Elementary School
Meeting
Jan 8, 2019 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY
Category
M. NEW BUSINESS - PRIVATELY FUNDED CAPITAL PROJECTS (Mr. Roberts) (8:30 - 8:40 p.m.)
Type
Action

This privately funded special project request is for the purchase and installation of playground equipment for PreKindergarten and Kindergarten students at Edgemere Elementary School.  Existing asphalt pads will be removed and replaced by new developmentally appropriate equipment. The project will beautify the playground area, facilitate student physical development and social skills during recess time, and has been fully funded by the Edgemere Elementary School PTA.
