2. Third Party Billing Annual Report 2014-2015

Type
Information

Annually, the Department of Fiscal Services prepares a summary of the activities completed by the Office of Third Party Billing.  This office is responsible for collecting reimbursements as authorized by the Medicare Catastrophic Act passed by the federal government in 1988.  The Office of Third Party Billing is also responsible for reimbursements connected with the Medicaid Home and Community-Based Autism Waiver program and the Out of County Living Arrangement program.  Attached is the Third Party Billing Annual Report for 2014-2015.
