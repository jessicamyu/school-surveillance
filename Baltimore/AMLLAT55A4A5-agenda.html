Subject
1. Consideration of the Victory Villa Elementary School Boundary
Meeting
Jun 13, 2017 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
O. UNFINISHED BUSINESS - VICTORY VILLA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL BOUNDARY (Dr. Brown/Dr. Martin-Knox/Mr. Roberts) (8:20 - 8:40 p.m.)
Type
Action

On May 9, 2017, the Board of Education of Baltimore County (Board) received, for consideration and approval, a report from the Victory Villa Community Boundary Study Committee recommending boundary changes to schools in the region to help relieve overcrowding.  The recommendation, known as Option D1, affects the boundaries of Glenmar, Hawthorne, Martin Boulevard, Middlesex, Orems, Shady Spring, Victory Villa, and Vincent Farm elementary schools. The Board public hearing on the recommended boundary change was held on May 15, 2017, at Eastern Technical High School.  The Board is scheduled to vote on the recommendation at the June 13, 2017, Board meeting.
