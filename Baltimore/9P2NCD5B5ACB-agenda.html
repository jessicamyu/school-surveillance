N. NEW BUSINESS - PROPOSED PLAYGROUND FOR WEST TOWSON ELEMENTARY SCHOOL (Ms. Lichter)
Subject
1. Consideration of the Proposed Playground for West Towson Elementary School
Meeting
Oct 7, 2014 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
N. NEW BUSINESS - PROPOSED PLAYGROUND FOR WEST TOWSON ELEMENTARY SCHOOL (Ms. Lichter)
Type
Action

The parents, students, and staff of West Towson Elementary School feel strongly that there is a need for an additional playground on the school campus.  The playground that was built as part of the new school construction is limited in the number of students that the space and equipment can accommodate.  In addition, there are limited opportunities for imaginative play on the existing playground equipment.

 

The West Towson Playground Committee invited representatives from the surrounding community associations to the planning meetings.  In addition, the principal of West Towson Elementary School has collaborated with the principal of Ridge Ruxton School, since the two schools share a campus.  The new playground will be accessible for students at both schools.
