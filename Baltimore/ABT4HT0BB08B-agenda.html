N. NEW BUSINESS - IB MAGNET PROGRAM AT STEMMERS RUN MIDDLE SCHOOL (Ms. White)
Subject
1. Consideration of the IB Magnet Program at Stemmers Run Middle School
Meeting
Aug 9, 2016 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
N. NEW BUSINESS - IB MAGNET PROGRAM AT STEMMERS RUN MIDDLE SCHOOL (Ms. White)
Type
Action

As a part of the approved findings of the Magnet Task Force, it is being requested that Stemmers Run Middle School be approved as a new middle school magnet offering site.  The magnet program will begin as an International Studies program for the 2017-18 school year as the school undergoes the International Baccalaureate (IB) certification process. The IB program at Stemmers Run Middle School will eventually provide a pathway for students, who choose to do so, to Kenwood High School.
