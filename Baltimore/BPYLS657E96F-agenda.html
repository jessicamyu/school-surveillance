H. UNFINISHED BUSINESS - CONSIDERATION OF THE EMERGENCY BOUNDARY RECOMMENDATION (Ms. Byers, Mr. Dixit, and Ms. Appler) (6:10 - 6:30 p.m.)
Type
Action

On May 19, 2020, the Board of Education of Baltimore County (Board) received, for consideration and approval, a boundary change recommendation for the Village at Gunpowder Falls development.  This boundary study recommends a boundary change to align school attendance areas for this new development.  The recommendation affects the boundaries of Pine Grove and Seven Oaks elementary schools and Perry Hall and Pine Grove middle schools.  Due to public health concerns, instead of a public hearing, the public were invited to provide comments to the Board about the proposal from May 11, 2020, through June 13, 2020.  The Board will vote on the recommendation at the June 23, 2020, Board meeting.
