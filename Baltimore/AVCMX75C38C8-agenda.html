Subject
1. Report on the Proposed Naming of the new Northeast Elementary School
Meeting
Feb 20, 2018 - WORK SESSION OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION
Category
H. WORK SESSION REPORT ON THE PROPOSED NAMING OF THE NEW NORTHEAST ELEMENTARY SCHOOL (Mr. Roberts) (7:20 - 7:30 p.m.)
Type
Report

The new northeast elementary school near the intersection of Joppa Road and Honeygo Boulevard in Perry Hall is scheduled to open with the beginning of the 2018-2019 school year.  In order to survey the community for the purpose of receiving suggestions for naming the new school, a preliminary multi-media press campaign was launched in early October 2017 announcing an online survey for community input which would run from October 16 through November 3, 2017.  A final online survey conducted from December 4 through December 22 solicited input on the two finalist names. As a result, the name being recommended for this new school is "Honeygo Elementary School."
