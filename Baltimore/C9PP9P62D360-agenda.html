Public Hearing on the Superintendent’s Proposed FY2023 Operating Budget – Virtual Only

 

A public hearing on the Superintendent’s Proposed FY2023 Operating Budget will be held virtually on Tuesday, January 18, 2022, at 6:30 p.m. Registration to participate in public comment will be through the online registration form, which will open on January 11, 2022, one week prior to the hearing date, and close at 3 p.m. on the day before the hearing, Monday, January 17, 2022. Registrants will receive confirmation of their registration. Comments are limited to 3 minutes per speaker. No speaker substitutions will be allowed.

To register to speak during the January 18, 2022, Public Hearing on the Superintendent’s Proposed FY2023 Operating Budget, click on the link PUBLIC HEARING ON THE FY2023 OPERATING BUDGET – Registration Form.
