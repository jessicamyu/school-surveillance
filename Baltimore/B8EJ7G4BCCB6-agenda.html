On January 8, 2019, the interim superintendent introduced her proposed FY2020 operating budget, which ties to Blueprint 2.0. A public hearing on the operating budget was held on January 15, 2019. A work session on the revised FY2020 operating budget is scheduled for February 5, 2019, with a vote scheduled for February 19, 2019.
