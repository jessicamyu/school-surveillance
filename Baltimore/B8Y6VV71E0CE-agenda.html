Category
F. UNFINISHED BUSINESS - PROPOSED FY2020 OPERATING BUDGET (Mr. Smith and Mr. Sarris) (7:10 - 8:00 p.m.)
Type
Action

On January 8, 2019, the interim superintendent introduced her proposed FY2020 operating budget.  A public hearing on the operating budget was held on January 15, 2019.  As requested, the interim superintendent presented a revised FY2020 operating budget at the MOE effort level for the Board's consideration on January 22, 2019.  A work session on the revised FY2020 operating budget was held on February 5, 2019.  An additional work session and public hearing was held on February 13, 2019, on the original January 8, 2019, proposed FY2020 Operating Budget and is being recommended by the interim superintendent for approval.

 

 

Once approved by the Board, the budget will be forwarded to the county executive on or before March 1, 2019, for his consideration and approval.

K. WORK SESSION REPORT - PROPOSED CASTANEA COMMUNITY-FOCUSED BOUNDARY CHANGE (Dr. Brown) (8:50 - 9:10 p.m.)
Subject
1. Report on the Proposed Castanea Community-Focused Boundary Change
Meeting
Feb 19, 2019 - WORK SESSION OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION
Category
K. WORK SESSION REPORT - PROPOSED CASTANEA COMMUNITY-FOCUSED BOUNDARY CHANGE (Dr. Brown) (8:50 - 9:10 p.m.)
Type
Presentation, Report

On February 5, 2019, the Board of Education of Baltimore County (Board) received for consideration and approval a report from the Castanea Estates Community Boundary Study Committee recommending boundary changes to schools to align attendance areas for this development. The recommendation, known as Scenario A, affects the boundaries of Mays Chapel and Fort Garrison elementary schools, Pikesville and Ridgley middle schools, and Dulaney and Pikesville high schools. The Board public hearing on the recommended boundary change is scheduled for February 20, 2019, at Mays Chapel Elementary School (snow date February 21, 2019).  The Board will vote on the recommendation at its March 5, 2019, Board meeting.
