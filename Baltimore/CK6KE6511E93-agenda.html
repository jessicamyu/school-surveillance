The Board of Education publishes the Annual Comprehensive Financial Report (ACFR, formerly the CAFR).  Each year, student artwork is included in the ACFR. The fiscal year 2022 ACFR includes the work of students, and these students are recognized at the Board meeting when the ACFR is presented to the Board.  Each participating student receives a gift card.  The following students’ artwork was selected:

 

  • Leah Abramov, Grade 8, Sudbrook Magnet Middle School
  • Kaden Brown, Grade 8, Deer Park Middle Magnet School

  • Scarleth Alvarado Puerto, Grade 7, Deep Creek Middle School
  • Sylvia Schmidt, Grade 6, Loch Raven Technical Academy
