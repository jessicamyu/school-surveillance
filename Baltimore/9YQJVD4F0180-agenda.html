K. NEW BUSINESS - SPECIAL PROJECT REQUEST for PRETTYBOY ELEMENTARY SCHOOL (Dr. Wheatley-Phillip)
Subject
1. Consideration of Installation of 6 Short-Throw Projectors at Prettyboy Elementary School
Meeting
Aug 18, 2015 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
K. NEW BUSINESS - SPECIAL PROJECT REQUEST for PRETTYBOY ELEMENTARY SCHOOL (Dr. Wheatley-Phillip)
Type
Action (Consent)

The installation of interactive projections will upgrade the delivery of instruction and enable interactive software to be accessible to students.  The technology will have a positive impact on students because they will have daily access to use the interactive technology as the curriculum is being taught in reading, math, and science.  The added technology has the potential to impact student achievement by providing an engaging and high interest mode of instructional delivery.  It will offer students another mode of accessing digital content and opportunities to collaborate around this content.
