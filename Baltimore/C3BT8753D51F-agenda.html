N. NEW BUSINESS - WATERSHED PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL (Dr. McComas and Dr. Jones) (8:05 - 8:25 p.m.)
Subject
1. Consideration of the Watershed Public Charter School Contract Extension
Meeting
Jul 13, 2021 - HYBRID MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
N. NEW BUSINESS - WATERSHED PUBLIC CHARTER SCHOOL (Dr. McComas and Dr. Jones) (8:05 - 8:25 p.m.)
Type
Action

The Board of Education of Baltimore County Public Schools (BCPS) and Watershed Public Charter School (WPCS) entered a four-year contract on August 20, 2019.  Subsequently, in the first year of the contract, the global COVID-19 pandemic erupted, resulting in significant interruption to traditional instruction and a sudden shift from in-person learning to forms of remote learning starting March 16, 2020.  As a result of the sudden interruption to schools, the Maryland State Department of Education (MSDE) waived conducting annual state assessments for the 2019-2020 academic year.  Additionally, the pandemic continued to have impact on the following 2020-2021 school year, thereby resulting in both BCPS and Watershed proposing a 1-year extension on the initial 4-year contact.  Staff will provide information to the Board of Education on a proposed extension, thereby extending the initial contract from July 1, 2019 - June 30, 2023, to be July 1, 2019 - June 30, 2024.  This proposal is reflective of MSDE guidance and how other LEAs have responded to Charter Schools regarding the impact of the global pandemic.

2. 2021-2022 Special Education Staff Plan
Meeting
Jul 13, 2021 - HYBRID MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
T. INFORMATION (10:20 p.m.)
Type
Information

In Maryland, each LEA is required to complete and submit a comprehensive staffing plan, which is developed consistent with the procedures provided by the Maryland State Department of Education (MSDE) for the purpose of ensuring that personnel and other resources are available to provide a free appropriate public education (FAPE) to each student with a disability in the least restrictive environment (LRE) as determined by an Individualized Education Program (IEP) team. It also is in accordance with the Code of Maryland Regulations (COMAR) 13A.05.02.13D.  The proposed staffing plan was developed in collaboration with multiple stakeholders.  The Department of Special Education elicited input through surveys.  School-based staff, parents, and Special Education Citizens Advisory Committee members (SECAC) provided feedback and recommendations. The Department of Special Education timeline will be implemented at the start of the 2021 academic school year.

3. Baltimore County Public Schools - Public Charter Schools Renewal Process
Meeting
Jul 13, 2021 - HYBRID MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
T. INFORMATION (10:20 p.m.)
Type
Information

Baltimore County Public Schools (BCPS) and Watershed Public Charter School (WPCS), in accordance with section 15.1 of the contract, worked together to establish criteria to be utilized by the BOE in making its determination to renew the contract at the end of the term.  The collaboration between BCPS and WPCS began in November 2019 and was delayed as a function of the interruptions created by the global pandemic.

 

BCPS and WPCS developed a comprehensive process of review, building upon best practices of local school systems and the Maryland Alliance of Public Charter Schools.  The mutually developed charter school review process is documented in the BCPS Charter School Renewal Booklet.  This is the process that will be utilized by BCPS in reviewing WPCS during the 2022-2023 school year to develop a report with recommendations regarding renewal for the BCPS BOE. Furthermore, this process of renewal will be utilized for any future BCPS charter schools.
