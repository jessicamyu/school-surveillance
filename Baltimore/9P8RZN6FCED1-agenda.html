L. INFORMATION
Subject
1. Additional Pre-Kindergarten Conference Day; School Year 2014-2015 and 2015-2016
Meeting
Oct 21, 2014 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
L. INFORMATION
Type
Information

As a result of the 2013-2014 negotiations with TABCO, it was agreed to include an additional Pre-K conference day in the second semester of the school year.  Monday, May 11, 2015, and Monday, May 16, 2016, are currently scheduled as Pre-K and Kindergarten Conference Days. Therefore, the calendars for the 2014-2015 and the 2015-2016 school year need to be amended to reflect Tuesday, May 12, 2015, and Tuesday, May 17, 2016, as additional Pre-K Conference Days. Only Preschool-3 and Pre-K students would not be in school on these days.  The scheduling of an additional Pre-K conference day has no impact on the MSDE mandated minimum student days.
