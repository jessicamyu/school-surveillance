O. NEW BUSINESS - SPECIAL PROJECT REQUEST - PLAYGROUND AT HAMPTON ELEMENTARY SCHOOL (DR. MARTIN-KNOX)
Subject
1. Consideration of the Installation of a New Playground at Hampton Elementary School
Meeting
Dec 6, 2016 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
O. NEW BUSINESS - SPECIAL PROJECT REQUEST - PLAYGROUND AT HAMPTON ELEMENTARY SCHOOL (DR. MARTIN-KNOX)
Type
Action

Hampton Elementary School is in need of a playground for the IGE3 program, which started last year, and the Prekindergarten program, which started this school year.  The existing playgrounds are not age appropriate due to the size of the equipment and height from the ground to the equipment and the steps within the equipment.  The proposed playground will meet the needs of the IGE 3's, Prekindergartens, and Kindergarteners.  Funding will come from the school's discretionary account and the school's Education Foundation account, Dolphin Dreams.

7. Third Party Billing Annual Report 2015-2016
Meeting
Dec 6, 2016 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
S. INFORMATION
Type
Information

Annually, the Department of Fiscal Services prepares a summary of the activities completed by the Office of Third Party Billing. This office is responsible for collecting reimbursements as authorized by the Medicare Catastrophic Act passed by the federal government in 1988. The Office of Third Party Billing is also responsible for reimbursements connected with the Medicaid Home and Community-Based Autism Waiver program and the Out of County Living Arrangement program. Attached is the Third Party Billing Annual Report for 2015-2016.
