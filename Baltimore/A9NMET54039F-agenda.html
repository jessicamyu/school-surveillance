4. 2016 Legislative Session - Final Summary
Meeting
May 24, 2016 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
N. INFORMATION
Type
Information

The 435th Session of the Maryland General Assembly began on January 11, 2016, and continued for 90 days until its Adjournment SINE DIE on April 13, 2016.  A total of 2,817 bills and 15 Joint Resolutions were introduced in the House of Delegates and the Senate during the session. Thirty-four bills that BCPS has been tracking were either passed by the House, Senate, or signed by the governor.  The governor can sign a bill into law, veto a bill (precluding a bill becoming law, unless the veto is overridden by the General Assembly), or allow a bill to become law if he does not sign it prior to June 2, 2016.
