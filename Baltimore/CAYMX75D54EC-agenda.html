Public Hearing on the New Northeast Area Elementary School Boundary – Virtual 

 

A public hearing on the New Northeast Area Elementary School Boundary will be held virtually on Wednesday, February 16, 2022, at 6:30 p.m. Registration to participate in public comment will be through the online registration form, which will open on February 9, 2022, one week prior to the hearing date, and close at 3 p.m. on the day before the hearing, Tuesday, February 15, 2022. Registrants will receive confirmation of their registration. Comments are limited to 3 minutes per speaker. No speaker substitutions will be allowed.
 

To register to speak during the February 16, 2022, Public Hearing on the new Northeast Area Elementary School boundary, click on the link PUBLIC HEARING ON THE NEW NORTHEAST AREA ES BOUNDARY – Registration Form.
