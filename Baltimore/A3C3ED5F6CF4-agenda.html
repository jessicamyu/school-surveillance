M. OLD BUSINESS - PROPOSED 2016-2017 SCHOOL CALENDAR (Mr. Duque)
Subject
1. Consideration of the Proposed 2016-2017 School Calendar
Meeting
Nov 3, 2015 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
M. OLD BUSINESS - PROPOSED 2016-2017 SCHOOL CALENDAR (Mr. Duque)
Type
Action

On October 26, 2015, BCPS received notification that the Maryland State Department of Education was in the process of adjusting the dates of the Islamic holidays on the Maryland Student Testing Calendar for 2015-2016 through 2017-2018.  Therefore, BCPS has amended its 2016-2017 School Calendar to reflect this change and has annotated September 11, 2016, as Eid al-Adha in accordance with the MSDE change.  Additionally, the professional development day originally scheduled for September 12, 2016, has been moved to January 17, 2017, and coincides with the Martin Luther King, Jr. holiday and the end of the first semester. 

 

The Board is committed to implementing the Policy Review Committee’s calendar recommendations and in the future will make every effort to, where appropriate and whenever the calendar permits, designating Eid al-Fitr or Eid al-Adha as a professional development day.

R. NEW BUSINESS - SPECIAL PROJECT REQUEST - LOCH RAVEN HIGH SCHOOL (Ms. King)
Subject
1. Consideration of the Installation of a Solar Powered Field Hockey/Girls Lacrosse Scoreboard
Meeting
Nov 3, 2015 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
R. NEW BUSINESS - SPECIAL PROJECT REQUEST - LOCH RAVEN HIGH SCHOOL (Ms. King)
Type
Action

The project consists of the installation of a solar powered field hockey/girls lacrosse scoreboard on the field closest to the school.

 

The scoreboard will be an environmentally friendly and aesthetically pleasing scoreboard to help enhance the successes of the Loch Raven athletic department, and help to satisfy the requirements of Title IX regarding equity among genders, as the baseball team already has a scoreboard on their field.
