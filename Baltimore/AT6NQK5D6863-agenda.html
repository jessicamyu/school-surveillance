Category
G. UNFINISHED BUSINESS - PROPOSED BOUNDARY FOR LANSDOWNE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL (Dr. Brown/Dr. Martin-Knox) (7:10 - 7:20 p.m.)
Type
Action (Consent)

On November 21, 2017, the Board of Education of Baltimore County (Board) received, for consideration and approval, a report from the Lansdowne Elementary Community Boundary Study Committee recommending boundary changes to schools in the region to help relieve overcrowding. The recommendation, known as Option A, affects the boundaries of Baltimore Highlands, Lansdowne and Riverview elementary schools. The Board’s public hearing on the recommended boundary change was held on Wednesday, December 6, 2017, at Lansdowne High School at 6:30 p.m. The Board will vote on the recommendation at its regular meeting on Tuesday, December 19, 2017.

Annually, the Department of Fiscal Services prepares a summary of the activities completed by the Office of Third Party Billing. This office is responsible for collecting reimbursements as authorized by the Medicare Catastrophic Act passed by the federal government in 1988. The Office of Third Party Billing is also responsible for reimbursements connected with the Medicaid Home and Community-Based Autism Waiver program and the Out of County Living Arrangement program.
