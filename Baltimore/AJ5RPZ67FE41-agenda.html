The superintendent’s proposed FY 2018 operating budget was introduced to the Board on January 10, 2017.  A public hearing was held on January 17, 2017.  A work session of the Board was held on January 24, 2017.  A public work session was held on January 31, 2017.  The Board amended and approved the budget on February 7, 2017.  The FY 2018 Board Proposed Budget will be forwarded to the county executive on or before March 1, 2017.
