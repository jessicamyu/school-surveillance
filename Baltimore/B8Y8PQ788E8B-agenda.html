On January 8, 2019, the interim superintendent introduced her proposed FY2020 operating budget, which ties to Blueprint 2.0. A public hearing on the operating budget was held on January 15, 2019.  On January 22, 2019, at the request of the Board, the interim superintendent presented a revised FY2020 operating budget. A work session on the revised FY2020 operating budget was held on February 5, 2019.  This additional work session and public hearing was scheduled for the Interim Superintendent's January 8, 2019, Proposed FY2020 Operating Budget.  

 

A vote is scheduled for February 19, 2019.  Once approved by the Board, the budget will be forwarded to the county executive on or before March 1, 2019.
