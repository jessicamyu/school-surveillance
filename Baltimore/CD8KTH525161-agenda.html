The Education Foundation of Baltimore County Public Schools, Inc., is an independent organization created under Section 501 (c) (3) of the Internal Revenue Code in support of a quality education for all children in Baltimore County Public Schools.  The foundation celebrates its 30th anniversary this year with continuous growth and measurable success impacting the equitable access of opportunities and experiences necessary for a bright and successful future for our students and their families, educators, and schools.
