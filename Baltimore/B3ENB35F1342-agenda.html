Meeting
Sep 11, 2018 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
M. NEW BUSINESS - PRIVATELY-FUNDED CAPITAL PROJECT (Dr. Jones) (8:30 - 8:40 p.m.)
Type
Action (Consent)

Best Buy of Owings Mills staff won the 2017 Score with Intel Core Award-2nd Prize.  As such, they had the opportunity to select a local school to which the prize would be donated.  Best Buy selected Owings Mills High School. The existing digital signage is in need of an upgrade as it is no longer functional.  The donation will allow Owings Mills High School to both upgrade and expand digitial signage throughout the building.
