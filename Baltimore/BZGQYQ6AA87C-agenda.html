J. NEW BUSINESS - FY2021 BUDGET APPROPRIATION TRANSFER (Dr. Scriven, Mr. Sarris, and Mr. Tantleff) (7:35 - 7:50 p.m.)
Subject
1. Consideration of the FY2021 Budget Appropriation Transfer
Meeting
Apr 20, 2021 - HYBRID WORK SESSION OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
J. NEW BUSINESS - FY2021 BUDGET APPROPRIATION TRANSFER (Dr. Scriven, Mr. Sarris, and Mr. Tantleff) (7:35 - 7:50 p.m.)
Type
Action

This annual budget appropriation transfer will realign funds by category with planned and projected year-end expenses. Funds are available due to savings in salary and benefit expenditures from higher-than-expected turnover and the superintendent's mandated reductions. The proposed transfers are necessary to implement the FY2021 operating plan, transition ERP system to the cloud and provide enhanced network security, provide for FY2022 textbooks, provide for Out-of-County Living Arrangements, provide for increased parent reimbursements and nonpublic placements in special education, and provide for maintenance of plant. Expenditure decisions are subject to the availability of funds. With the Board’s approval, this transfer will be submitted to the county executive and then to the Baltimore County Council for approval in June.
