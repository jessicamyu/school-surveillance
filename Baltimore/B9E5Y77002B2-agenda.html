Subject
1. Consideration of the Proposed Castanea Community-Focused Boundary Change
Meeting
Mar 5, 2019 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY
Category
I. UNFINISHED BUSINESS - CASTANEA COMMUNITY-FOCUSED BOUNDARY CHANGE (Dr. Brown) (7:45 - 8:00 p.m.)
Type
Presentation

On February 5, 2019, the Board of Education of Baltimore County (Board) received for consideration and approval a report from the Castanea Estates Community Boundary Study Committee recommending boundary changes to schools to align attendance areas for this development. The recommendation, known as Scenario A, affects the boundaries of Mays Chapel and Fort Garrison elementary schools, Pikesville and Ridgley middle schools, and Dulaney and Pikesville high schools. The Board held a public hearing on the recommended boundary change on February 21, 2019, at Mays Chapel elementary school. No persons signed up to speak. All supporting documentation, meeting materials, and scenarios are available on the Castanea Boundary Study Web page.
