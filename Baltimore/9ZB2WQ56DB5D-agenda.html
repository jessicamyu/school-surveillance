P. NEW BUSINESS - REPORT ON SCHOLARS K-8 (Ms. White/ Ms. Blannard)
Subject
1. Report on Scholars K-8
Meeting
Sep 1, 2015 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
P. NEW BUSINESS - REPORT ON SCHOLARS K-8 (Ms. White/ Ms. Blannard)
Type
Report

That the Board of Education reviews the proposed superintendent's recommendation, in accordance with Board Policy 1290, to close Scholars K-8 at the conclusion of the 2015-2016 school year.  Scholars K-8 was opened as Imagine Discovery Public Charter School in 2008.  Imagine Discovery voluntarily ended its charter at the end of the 2013-2014 school year and continued as a BCPS school in 2014-2015 and was renamed Scholars K-8.  As a result of parental input and in order to permit the administration to continue to evaluate the program's viability, the Board also extended the current facility's lease for two years so that students would not be disrupted.  The lease of the current facility ends in June 2016.  As per COMAR, BOE policy, and superintendent's rule, the following factors were thoroughly reviewed prior to this recommendation:  age and condition of the school building, enrollment trends, transportation, student relocation, educational programs, impact on the community, financial considerations, and racial composition of the student body.  This is the first reading.
