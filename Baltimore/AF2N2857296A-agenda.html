M. NEW BUSINESS - REPORT ON FY16 CAFR AND SINGLE AUDIT (Mr. Sarris)
Subject
1. Report on FY2016 Comprehensive Annual Financial Report and Single Audit
Meeting
Nov 22, 2016 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
M. NEW BUSINESS - REPORT ON FY16 CAFR AND SINGLE AUDIT (Mr. Sarris)
Type
Report

The Comprehensive Annual Financial Report (CAFR) is prepared annually in compliance with the public school laws of the state of Maryland.  All funds and accounts of the Board are included in this CAFR.  While the Board is an entity created and governed by state law, it has been defined as a component unit of the Baltimore County government for financial reporting purposes.  The financial statements for fiscal year 2016 have been audited by CliftonLarsonAllen LLP in accordance with state law.  The independent auditor’s report is included in the financial section of the CAFR.  As required as a condition of receipt of federal funds, CliftonLarsonAllen LLP also conducts an annual audit of federal funds.  The fiscal year 2016 Single Audit of federal funds is also provided.
