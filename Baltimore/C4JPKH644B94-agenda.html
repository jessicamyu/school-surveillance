3. Consideration of a Privately Funded Capital Project - Seventh District Elementary School Storage Sheds
Meeting
Aug 10, 2021 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
N. NEW BUSINESS - SPECIAL PROJECT REQUESTS (Ms. Byers and Dr. Roberts) (9:25 - 9:35 p.m.)
Type
Action

The Seventh District Recreation Council is proposing replacing five dilapidated storage sheds behind the backstops of the 4 softball/baseball fields at Seventh District Elementary School with four 4x8x7 lean-to-style, more-secure sheds. Each field currently has one shed. Field #4 currently has 2 boxes, which would be replaced with one new shed, as two sheds are no longer needed for that field. The funding and organization of this project will be overseen by the Seventh District Recreation Council. The benefits of this project will have a positive impact on the school grounds and campus. Seventh District Recreation Council will incur all costs for removal of existing five sheds and the delivery, preparation, and installation of the four new lean to sheds. The council will also provide any future maintenance costs for these sheds.
