Beginning December 3, 2018, the Board of Education of Baltimore County becomes a hybrid Board.  We propose cancelling the previously scheduled 12/4/18 Board meeting as a quorum of Board members would need to be sworn-in on December 3 or 4 to allow a meeting to proceed, and materials could not be distributed before a Board member is sworn.  Therefore, the revised schedule would eliminate the December 4 meeting.
