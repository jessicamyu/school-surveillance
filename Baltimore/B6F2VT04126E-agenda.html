O. REPORT - HIGH SCHOOL CAPACITY STUDY (Dr. Brown) (8:45- 9:25 p.m.)
Subject
1. Report on the High School Capacity Study
Meeting
Dec 11, 2018 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY
Category
O. REPORT - HIGH SCHOOL CAPACITY STUDY (Dr. Brown) (8:45- 9:25 p.m.)
Type
Report

On December 11, 2018, the Sage Policy Group, Inc., will present the final report of the High School Capacity Study to the Board of Education of Baltimore County (Board). The purpose of the High School Capacity project was to study all BCPS high schools and identify potential options and strategies for providing enough seats for growing enrollment.

 

The report and presentation will provide:

  • An overview of the process and the goals of the study.
  • Feedback received from the community.
  • Options for providing enough seats to address growing enrollment at BCPS high schools.
  • Recommendations for the Board’s consideration (no one scenario will be recommended).
