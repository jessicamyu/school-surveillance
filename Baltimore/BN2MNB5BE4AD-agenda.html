K. NEW BUSINESS - REPORT ON EMERGENCY BOUNDARY (Ms. Byers, Mr. Dixit, and Ms. Appler) (7:30 - 7:50 p.m.)
Subject
1. Report on the Village of Gunpowder Falls Boundary Recommendation
Meeting
May 19, 2020 - WORK SESSION OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
K. NEW BUSINESS - REPORT ON EMERGENCY BOUNDARY (Ms. Byers, Mr. Dixit, and Ms. Appler) (7:30 - 7:50 p.m.)
Type
Report

On May 19, 2019, the Board of Education of Baltimore County (Board) received, for consideration and approval, a boundary change recommendation for the Village at Gunpowder Falls development.  This boundary study recommends a boundary change to align school attendance areas for this new development. The recommendation affects the boundaries of Pine Grove and Seven Oaks elementary schools and Perry Hall and Pine Grove middle schools. Due to public health concerns, in lieu of a public hearing, the Board will be accepting comments through midnight on May 29, 2020, via e-mail, online comment form, or standard USPS mail. The Board will vote on the recommendation at the June 9, 2020, Board meeting.
