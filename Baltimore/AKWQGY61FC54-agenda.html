M. NEW BUSINESS - PROPOSED BOUNDARY FOR VICTORY VILLA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL (Dr. Brown/Dr. Martin-Knox/Mr. Roberts/Mr. Cropper) (8:15 - 8:45 p.m.)
Subject
1. Report on the Proposed Boundary for Victory Villa Elementary School
Meeting
May 9, 2017 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
M. NEW BUSINESS - PROPOSED BOUNDARY FOR VICTORY VILLA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL (Dr. Brown/Dr. Martin-Knox/Mr. Roberts/Mr. Cropper) (8:15 - 8:45 p.m.)
Type
Presentation

On May 9, 2017, the Board of Education of Baltimore County (Board) will receive, for consideration and approval, a report from the Victory Villa Community Boundary Study Committee recommending boundary changes to schools in the region to help relieve overcrowding.  The recommendation, known as Option D1, affects the boundaries of Glenmar, Hawthorne, Martin Boulevard, Middlesex, Orems, Shady Spring, Victory Villa, and Vincent Farm elementary schools.  The Board's public hearing on the recommended boundary change is scheduled for Monday, May 15, 2017, at Eastern Technical High School at 6:30 p.m.  The Board is scheduled to vote on the recommendation at its regular meeting on June 13, 2017.

N. NEW BUSINESS - PERRY HALL MIDDLE SCHOOL SHORT-TERM RELIEF STRATEGIES (Dr. Brown) (8:45 - 9:05 p.m.)
Subject
1. Report on Perry Hall Middle School Short-Term Relief Strategies
Meeting
May 9, 2017 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
N. NEW BUSINESS - PERRY HALL MIDDLE SCHOOL SHORT-TERM RELIEF STRATEGIES (Dr. Brown) (8:45 - 9:05 p.m.)
Type
Presentation

Baltimore County government recently announced that it will provide funding for the planning and design of a new middle school and addition to Pine Grove Middle School to relieve overcrowding in the northeast and central areas.  However, Perry Hall Middle School will exceed capacity by nearly 500 students before the anticipated completion of these projects in fall of 2021.  The Board of Education will review options for short-term relief strategies to manage overcrowding at Perry Hall Middle School.
