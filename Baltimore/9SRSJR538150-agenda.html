L. OLD BUSINESS - PROPOSED FY16 OPERATING BUDGET (Ms. Burnopp)
Subject
1. Consideration of the Proposed FY16 Operating Budget
Meeting
Feb 3, 2015 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
L. OLD BUSINESS - PROPOSED FY16 OPERATING BUDGET (Ms. Burnopp)
Type
Action

The superintendent’s proposed FY2016 budget was introduced to the Board on January 7, 2015.  A public hearing was held on January 13, 2015.  The Board held a public work session on January 28, 2015.  Once approved, the budget will be forwarded to the county executive before March 1, 2015. 

 

On January 23, 2015, preliminary calculations for major state aid programs were released by the Maryland State Department of Education.  State aid to education is projected to increase $18.04 million over FY2015.  Baltimore County government also provided updated projected costs for employee benefits programs.  This information has been incorporated in the superintendent’s proposal and the general fund budget is now proposed to increase 4.2% over FY2015.  The proposed budget for all funds increases 10.4% over FY2015.  Local county revenues are proposed to increase 2.5% over Maintenance of Effort.

 

The total budget for all funds is $1,723,366,799.  It includes $909,701,261 in local funding from Baltimore County, $672,870,693 in state funds, $105,113,179 in federal grants and contributions, and $35,681,666 in funds from service charges and other sources.
