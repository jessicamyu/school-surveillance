L. NEW BUSINESS - FY2020 BUDGET APPROPRIATION TRANSFER (Dr. Scriven, Mr. Sarris, Mr. Tantleff) (7:20 - 7:40 p.m.)
Subject
1. Consideration of the FY2020 Budget Appropriation Transfer
Meeting
Apr 14, 2020 - VIRTUAL MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
L. NEW BUSINESS - FY2020 BUDGET APPROPRIATION TRANSFER (Dr. Scriven, Mr. Sarris, Mr. Tantleff) (7:20 - 7:40 p.m.)
Type
Action

This annual budget appropriation transfer will realign funds by category with planned and projected year-end expenses. Funds are available due to savings in salary and benefit expenditures from higher than expected turnover, reduced use of contract buses, and reduced use of instructional supplies. The proposed transfers are necessary to implement the FY2020 operating plan, realign expenditures in schools, provide for FY2021 textbooks, provide for Out-of-County Living Arrangements, provide for increased nonpublic placements in special education, provide for maintenance of plant, and provide for electronic document imaging in human resources and fiscal services. Expenditure decisions are subject to the availability of funds. With the Board's approval, this transfer will be submitted to the county executive and then to the Baltimore County Council for approval in June.
