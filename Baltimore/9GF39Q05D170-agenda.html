M. WORK SESSION REPORT - NAMING OF BCPS K - 8 SCHOOL (RENAMING OF IMAGINE DISCOVERY)
Subject
1. Report on the Naming of BCPS K - 8 School (Renaming of Imagine Discovery) First Reading (Dr. Allie/Ms. Thomas)
Meeting
Mar 25, 2014 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
M. WORK SESSION REPORT - NAMING OF BCPS K - 8 SCHOOL (RENAMING OF IMAGINE DISCOVERY)
Type
Report

In 2008, Imagine Discovery Charter School opened as the first charter school in Baltimore County.  Currently, Imagine Discovery operates as a K-8 school with an enrollment of 550 students.  At the November 5, 2013, Board meeting, the Board of Education voted not to renew Imagine Discovery Charter School’s contract.  As a result, Baltimore County Public Schools committed to assuming the operations of the school in its present location for two years to assist the families during this transition.  Since the school will now be a Baltimore County Public School, parent meetings were held to determine the new name for the school.  The recommendation for the new school is “Scholars K – 8.”   This is the first reading.
