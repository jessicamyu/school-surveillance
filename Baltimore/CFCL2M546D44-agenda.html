Subject
1. Consideration of the FY2023 Mid-Year Supplemental Budget Appropriation
Meeting
Aug 9, 2022 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
L. NEW BUSINESS - FY2023 MID-YEAR SUPPLEMENTAL BUDGET APPROPRIATION (Mr. Hartlove) (8:15 - 8:30 p.m.)
Type
Action

This supplemental appropriation is being requested to provide enhanced compensation to each of the BCPS bargaining units and non-represented employees. The fund balance from the general fund is being used as the source for this funding. Until a permanent funding source is identified, future appropriation of fund balance will be required to cover ongoing salary expenditures. This additional funding should boost retention and provide strong support towards recruiting external candidates to BCPS. In the case of AFSCME, the scale was revised to create a minimum $15 entry wage. Please see the attached schedule which indicates the details for each unit.
