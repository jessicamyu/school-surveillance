P. NEW BUSINESS - SPECIAL PROJECT REQUEST - WEST TOWSON ELEMENTARY SCHOOL (Ms. Byers) (9:05 - 9:10 p.m.)
Subject
1. Consideration of the Privately Funded Capital Project – West Towson Elementary School Buddy Benches
Meeting
May 7, 2019 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
P. NEW BUSINESS - SPECIAL PROJECT REQUEST - WEST TOWSON ELEMENTARY SCHOOL (Ms. Byers) (9:05 - 9:10 p.m.)
Type
Action

Greater Baltimore Medical Center (GBMC) donated funds for two buddy benches to be placed near the West Towson Elementary School playgrounds in memory of Trish Dorroh, a former staff member at the school.  One bench will be anchored to the concrete next to an existing bench.  The second bench will be anchored into a concrete pad to be installed between the playground and multi-purpose court.  The benches will be inscribed with the following: “In memory of Trish Dorroh.  Donated by GBMC, your friend and neighbor in West Towson”.  The benches will help beautify the playground area and provide a place for students to establish and build friendships.

Q. NEW BUSINESS - REPORT ON THE PROPOSED DOGWOOD AND JOHNNYCAKE ELEMENTARY CAPACITY RELIEF STUDIES (Dr. Brown) (9:10 - 9:40 p.m.)
Subject
1. Report on the Proposed Dogwood and Johnnycake Elementary Capacity Relief
Meeting
May 7, 2019 - MEETING OF THE BOARD OF EDUCATION OF BALTIMORE COUNTY, MARYLAND
Category
Q. NEW BUSINESS - REPORT ON THE PROPOSED DOGWOOD AND JOHNNYCAKE ELEMENTARY CAPACITY RELIEF STUDIES (Dr. Brown) (9:10 - 9:40 p.m.)
Type
Report

On May 7, 2019, the Board of Education of Baltimore County (Board) received for consideration recommendations from the Dogwood Relief Study and Johnnycake Relief Study Committees for boundary changes at four southwest elementary schools. 

 

The Dogwood Relief Study Committee recommended Option 2, which affects the boundaries for Dogwood and Featherbed Lane elementary schools.

 

The Johnnycake Relief Study Committee recommended Option 4, which affects the boundaries for Johnnycake and Edmondson Heights elementary schools.

 

The Board's public hearing on the recommended boundary changes is scheduled for May 15, 2019, at Woodlawn High School.  The Board will vote on the recommendations at its June 11, 2019, Board meeting.
