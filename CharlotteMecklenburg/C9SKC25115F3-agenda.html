A. Student Assignment Overview for Schools Opening in Fall 2023 and Fall 2024
Meeting
Jan 11, 2022 - Regular Board Meeting - 6:00pm
Category
2. REPORTS
Type
Reports

In July 2022, the superintendent will make student assignment recommendations to the Board for schools opening in fall 2023 and fall 2024. Among these schools are three new elementary relief schools, one new high school relief school, and 2 repurposed schools – one elementary and one high school. The report to the Board kicks off a public engagement period to gather community input related to these schools and their attendance areas.
