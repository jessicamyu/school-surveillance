Contract Total: $104,198.10

 

The Myers Park High School Exterior Patio Reconstruction Project was bid on April 17, 2020. Staff recommends the contract to the lowest responsible responsive bidder; Hostetter and Son, Inc. in the amount of $104,198.10. The original contract total was $94,700 which did not require board approval. Unforeseen conditions have increased this project by an additional $9,498.10, which requires Board approval.

The Board of Education is asked to approve use of a facsimile signature of Sheila W. Shirley, Chief Financial Officer on all bank accounts of the Board of Education, in signing checks and drafts and in signing the preaudit certificate on contracts or purchase orders. Also, the Board is requested to approve the principals and school treasurers as signers for all individual school activity funds bank accounts (names are available by school on file in the Chief Financial Officer’s office).

 

Additionally, the board is requested to approve the official depositories for the Charlotte-Mecklenburg Board of Education. Depositories are listed in the attached recommendations.

Board approval of facsimile signatures, signers on individual school funds, and bank depositories is addressed in Sections 115C-439, 115C-448, and 115C-444, respectively, of the North Carolina School Budget and Fiscal Control Act.
