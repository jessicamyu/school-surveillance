Subject
D. Report on Calendar Development Process for 2020-2021 Academic Calendar
Meeting
Mar 12, 2019 - Regular Board Meeting
Category
5. REPORTS/INFORMATION ITEMS
Type
Information

The CMS Calendar committee, consisting of parents, community members, teachers, principals, assistant principals, students, staff from various administrative departments and a Board of Education member, began meeting in February and developed two recommendations for the 2020-2021 school year. A preference poll for staff and the community was conducted online from February 20-27, 2019. Over 8,300 employees, parents, students and community members expressed their preference for Calendar Option A and Calendar Option B during the online poll for the 2020-2021 academic calendar.

 

Both calendar options satisfy NC law which for 2020-2021 requires 185 instructional days or 1,025 instructional hours, 11 holidays, 10 annual leave days and the number of teacher workdays necessary to constitute an overall calendar of 215 days.
