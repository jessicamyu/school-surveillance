Meeting
Feb 8, 2022 - Regular Board Meeting - 6:00pm
Category
3. PUBLIC ENGAGEMENT
Type
Action
  1. Retire GBEBB
    • Rationale – Duplicate of JBA. We keep JBA because it has an extra paragraph at the bottom that is not in GBEBB, otherwise they are identical.  
  2. Retire JICA and Modify JICAA (attached)
    • Rationale – Policies deal with same overarching issue, so we combine both into one policy. We use JICAA as the continuing policy due to the fact that it has a corresponding regulation.
  3. Modify GCMBA (attached)
    • Rationale – This is the longstanding inclement weather policy that allows the Board to close schools due to inclement weather. Functionally this has been delegated to the superintendent, so we make that change in the policy.
  4. Modify ICA (attached)
    • Rationale – ICA does not clarify that the superintendent’s ability to waive four days is limited to have available days to waive. It is the regulation language, but a request was made to include that exact language from regulation and add to the policy.
  5. Modify IL (attached)
    • Rationale – The policy has a reference to the old mission language. Our suggested change is to remove that language.
