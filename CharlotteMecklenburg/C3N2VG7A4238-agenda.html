Special Meeting (2:30pm): Joint Meeting with Mecklenburg Board of County Commissioners
At this special meeting, the Charlotte-Mecklenburg Board of Education will hold a joint meeting with the Mecklenburg Board of County Commissioners pursuant to NCGS 115C-431. The meeting will be held at the Valerie C. Woodard Center and is open to the public. The public may also listen and view the meeting beginning at 2:30 p.m. on the CMS Facebook outlets and the CMS YouTube Channel. The links to those outlets are:
