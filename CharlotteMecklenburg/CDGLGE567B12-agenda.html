Emergency Meeting of the Board of Education - 12:30pm - CMGC Chamber
The Charlotte-Mecklenburg Board of Education will hold an Emergency Meeting on Tuesday April 19th at 12:30pm in the Chamber Room of the CMGC. At this emergency meeting, the Charlotte-Mecklenburg Board of Education will consider the following:
.
• Matters related to Superintendent Contracts and Attendant Personnel Items
.
The Board will move into Closed Session during the meeting before returning to complete its agenda. The meeting is open to the public and will be livestreamed on the Board’s Facebook page at www.facebook.com/cmsboe.
