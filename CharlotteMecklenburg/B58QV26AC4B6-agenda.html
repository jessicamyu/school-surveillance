Pledge Leader is Arjan Darjee from CELA, and presented by Elyse Dashew- Board Member At-Large

 

 

Arjan Darjee is a native of Nepal. He speaks three languages and is learning Spanish at Charlotte East Language Academy. Arjan is a bright energetic student whose enthusiasm is contagious to all he encounters. He is always pleasant and eager to learn. He meets no strangers, he is a friend to all. When he is at school he likes to play and talk to his friends a lot when he is not focusing on his studies. His teachers speak highly about his character and his genuinely mind nature. CELA is proud of him and looking forward to taking this educational journey with him. He is Joined by his Brother Sarjan Darjee, Father, Ammer Darjee,Teacher, ​​​​​​​Simona Waters, Principal, Carmen Concepcion, Local Superintendent, Kondra Rattley and Comminity Executive Director, Tracey Hayes.
