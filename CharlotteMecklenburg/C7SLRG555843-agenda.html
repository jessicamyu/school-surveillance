Subject - Recommend Approval of the Phase One Construction for the New Elementary School (Elon, Hawk Ridge, Polo Ridge relief) Project

 

Dollar Amount - $5,264,373

 

Budget Source - 2017 Bond Program

 

Recommended Action - Recommend Approval of the Phase One Construction for the New Elementary School (Elon, Hawk Ridge, Polo Ridge relief) Project

 

Public Content The phase one construction pricing for the New Elementary School (Elon, Hawk Ridge, Polo Ridge relief) was received on October 12, 2021.  Staff recommends approval of the phase one construction to Balfour Beatty Construction in the amount of $5,264,373.  The phase one construction provides the steel and roofing required for the construction of the new 45-classroom elementary school facility. This phase one package allows for early procurement due to the long lead times of these products. The remaining construction packages will bid as phase two in early 2022. The new school is scheduled for occupancy in August 2023.
