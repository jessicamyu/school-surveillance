Subject
D. Report on Calendar Development Process for 2019-2020 Academic Calendar
Meeting
May 8, 2018 - Regular Board Meeting
Category
6. REPORTS/INFORMATION ITEMS
Type
Information

Staff Present

Dr. Clayton Wilcox, Superintendent

Angie Bartles, Calendar Committee Co-Chair

Stephanie McKinney, Calendar Committee Co-Chair

 

Main Points:

​​The CMS Calendar committee, consisting of parents, community members, teachers, principals, assistant principals, students, staff from various administrative departments and a Board of Education member, began meeting in February and developed two recommendations for the 2019-2020 school year. A preference poll for staff and the community was conducted online from April 11-17, 2018. Over 7,400 employees, parents, students and community members expressed their preference for Calendar Option A and Calendar Option B during the online poll for the 2019-2020 academic calendar.

Both calendar options satisfies NC law which for 2019-2020 requires 185 instructional days or 1,025 instructional hours, 11 holidays, 10 annual leave days and the number of teacher workdays necessary to constitute an overall calendar of 215 days.
