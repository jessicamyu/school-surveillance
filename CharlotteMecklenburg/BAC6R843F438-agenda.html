H. Recommended Approval of the Appointment by Sean Strain of Adrianne Johnson to the Bond Oversight Committee
Meeting
Mar 26, 2019 - Regular Board Meeting
Category
3. CONSENT ITEMS
Type
Action (Consent)

Adrianne and her family moved to Charlotte in 2010. She and her husband, Jay, have 3 children ages 14, 12 and 9 all currently attending Waddell Language Academy, a CMS magnet school. Adrianne's undergraduate degree was in Economics, as well as Japanese and Spanish language studies. After graduating in 1994, she moved to Tokyo and taught English at a middle school in rural Nagano prefecture. She then moved to Tokyo and began working at a large Japanese trading company in the precious metals financial trading division. Her work experience also includes sales and marketing for an American telecommunications company in Tokyo. She lived a total of 15 years in Japan, with the last 5 years together with her husband and children. After having children, Adrianne was a stay at home mom. While in Charlotte, she has done private English tutoring for Japanese families and currently works as a Japanese speaking substitute teacher at Waddell Language Academy.
