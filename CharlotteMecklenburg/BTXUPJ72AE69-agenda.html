Thursday, October 1, 2020
Emergency Meeting of the Board of Education - 6:00pm
The Board of Education will hold a Emergency Meeting to consider modifications to the In-Person and Remote Learning Plan for the 2020-2021 school year.
.
The Board will be meeting in-person with very limited staff in room 267 at the Charlotte-Mecklenburg Government Center. However, due to the continuing COVID-19 pandemic and the associated orders from Mecklenburg County and the governor limiting the number of individuals in an enclosed space, this meeting will be conducted virtually for the public. The public may listen and view the meeting beginning at 6:00pm on the CMS Facebook outlets, the CMS YouTube Channel, and CMS Channel 3 on Spectrum TV outlets in the Charlotte area.
