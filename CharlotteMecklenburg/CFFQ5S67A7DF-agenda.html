Agenda Item Title

Cell tower lease extension at Quail Hollow MS

 

Main Points

  • Extend the lease for 3 years with two 3-year options. Expand space by 432 SF.
  • Starting August 15, 2022 rent will be $3,680.39 per month.
  • Rent increases 7% every three years.
  • Each subtenant will pay, additionally, $1,911 per month

None
