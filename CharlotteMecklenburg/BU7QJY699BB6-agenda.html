Main Points

  • City proposes to build a sidewalk on the north side of Highway 51 (Pineville-Matthews Rd.) from Echo Forest Drive (our east entrance) east to Alexander Road
  • Access to CMS campus will not be impacted as only curb and gutter and sidewalk are being constructed
  • No road impact
  • Total easement area: 2,162 square feet
