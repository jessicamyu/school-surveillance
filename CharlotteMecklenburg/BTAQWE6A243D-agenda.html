Special Meeting - 6:00pm
The Board of Education will hold a Special Meeting to consider plans for transitioning to in-person instruction as allowed under Plan B and to consider authorizing furloughs if required.
.
The Board will be meeting in-person with very limited staff in room 267 at the Charlotte-Mecklenburg Government Center. However, due to the continuing COVID-19 pandemic and the associated orders from Mecklenburg County and the governor limiting the number of individuals in an enclosed space, this meeting will be conducted virtually for the public. The public may listen and view the meeting beginning at 6:00pm on the CMS Facebook outlets.
