The Board of Education is asked to approve use of a facsimile signature of Sheila W. Shirley, Chief Financial Officer on all bank accounts of the Board of Education, in signing checks and drafts and in signing the preaudit certificate on contracts or purchase orders. Also, the Board is requested to approve the principals and school treasurers as signers for all individual school activity funds bank accounts (names are available by school on file in the Chief Financial Officer’s office).
