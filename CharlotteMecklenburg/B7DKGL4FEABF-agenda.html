Pledge leaders are Kaleem Tacuma Moats and Kelsey Esquivel Bonilla.

Kaleem Tacuma Moats was born June 27, 2009 in Brooklyn, NY. Kaleem comes from a Haitian/ Trinidadian American lineage. He migrated to Charlotte with his mom, dad, and younger brother; Diana, Kobla, and Kaydin Moats. Kaleem is an A/B scholar that attends Briarwood Academy. He enjoys playing sports, creating an array of art and is currently exploring how to create graphic animation. Kaleem aspires to be an engineer with hopes of being an entrepreneur. Kaleem looks forward to the journey of becoming a life long learner.

 

 

Kelsey Esquivel Bonilla was born January 8th 2009. Kelsey is a fourth grader at Briarwood Academy. Her favorite subject is math. Kelsey scored a level 5 on her math EOG’s last school year. There is no doubt in her mind that she will score a level 5 this school year. Kelsey’s reading teacher Mrs. McCoulough has helped Kelsey become a better reader and writer. With the strategies Kelsey is learning in reading, she feels strong that she will score a level 5 on the EOG’s this school year. Last year she scored a level 4. Kelsey has set high goals for herself.

Kelsey’s hobbies are going to school and going to the beach. She enjoys school because this will enable her to one day become a teacher. She likes going to the beach because it’s a place for peace, quiet and relaxation. She enjoys hanging out with her brother and sister.

When Kelsey finishes high school she would like to attend college at UNCC. Kelsey wants to be a second grade teacher when she completes college. Kelsey wants to become a teacher because of the great teachers that have influenced her as a student.
