Currently unoccupied parcels 16307133, 16307134, 16307135, 16307136, 16307137, 16307131, 16306501 along Rama Road are assigned to the Myers Park High School attendance area.  The parcels are proximate to Rama Road Elementary and McClintock Middle School, both of which are located on Rama Road, and to East Mecklenburg High School which is less than one mile away from the intersection of Rama and Monroe roads.  The parcels, currently unoccupied, are assigned to Billingsville - Cotswold Elementary, Alexander Graham Middle and Myers Park High schools. 

 

The recommended assignments are in much closer proximity to the parcels than the current assignments.
