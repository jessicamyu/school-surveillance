A. Recommend Approval to Name the Gymnasium at South Mecklenburg High School
Meeting
Jan 28, 2020 - Regular Board Meeting
Category
3. ACTION ITEMS
Type

According to Board Policy FF Naming Facilities, the Board of Education is responsible for naming schools and all other facilities or parts of facilities.

 

The superintendent recommends to the Board of Education that the South Mecklenburg High gymnasium be named the Coach Dave Price Gymnasium.

 

In 1959, Mr. Price joined the South Mecklenburg High staff. He worked at South Mecklenburg High for 26 years and won many honors and recognitions as a top athletic coach and mentor. Coach Price was a physical education teacher and coached basketball, football, golf, track and field, cross country, tennis and softball. He was a legendary basketball coach, accumulating 538 wins. Coach Price died in 2015 but his legacy lives on -- many people he coached are still in Charlotte today.

4. REPORT ON STUDENT ASSIGNMENT CHANGES
Subject
A. Elizabeth Lane Elementary Relief
Meeting
Jan 28, 2020 - Regular Board Meeting
Category
4. REPORT ON STUDENT ASSIGNMENT CHANGES
Type
Reports

A new Lansdowne elementary school with 45 classrooms will be delivered in August 2021.

 

Lansdowne currently uses 36 classrooms (32 classrooms + 4 mobile classrooms), leaving a delta of approximately 9 classrooms. 

 

Elizabeth Lane Elementary is overutilized and cannot accommodate additional mobile units.  The Lansdowne expansion creates the opportunity to relieve Elizabeth Lane of up to 180 students.

 

The recommended elementary assignment change will not be accompanied by recommended middle or high school assignment changes. 

 

Two community engagement sessions were held in October and November 2019.  

 

The report to the Board provides a summary of two boundary change scenarios informed by community input and the associated community feedback for each scenario.
