Per Board Bylaw BDB...

 

Board Officers

A Chairperson and a Vice Chairperson shall be elected at the first regular Board meeting in December and shall assume office immediately upon election.  Each shall serve for a term of one year or until a successor shall have been elected.

 

Board Chairperson

The Chairperson shall preside at all meetings, appoint the chairperson and members of all Board committees and perform all other duties prescribed by law or the Board.

D. Election of the Vice Chairperson of the Board of Education
Meeting
Dec 14, 2021 - Regular Board Meeting - 6:00pm

1. CALL TO ORDER
Type

Per Board Bylaw BDB...

 

Board Officers

A Chairperson and a Vice Chairperson shall be elected at the first regular Board meeting in December and shall assume office immediately upon election.  Each shall serve for a term of one year or until a successor shall have been elected

 

Vice-Chairperson

The Vice-Chairperson shall perform the duties of the Chairperson in case of absence or disability which prevents him/her from performing his/her duties.  If the Chairperson resigns or otherwise becomes disqualified from serving out his/her term, the Vice-Chairperson shall assume the office of Chairperson for the balance of the term and a new Vice-Chairperson shall be elected for the balance of the term.

 

 

Date of Adoption: 2/27/68, 2/27/68, 2/27/68
