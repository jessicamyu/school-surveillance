Subject - Recommend Approval of the Phase One Construction for the Bruns Elementary Replacement School Project

Dollar Amount - $24,195,036.00

Budgeted -Yes

Budget Source - 2017 Bond Program

Public Content - The phase one construction pricing for the Bruns Elementary Replacement School Project was received on October 21, 2022.  Staff recommends approval of the phase one construction to Edifice, Inc. in the amount of $24,195,036.  The phase one construction provides the sitework, steel, roofing, doors and frames, HVAC, and electrical scope packages required for the construction of the new 45-classroom elementary replacement facility. This phase one package allows for early procurement due to the long lead times of these products. The remaining construction packages will bid as phase two in early 2023. The project is scheduled for occupancy in August 2024.
