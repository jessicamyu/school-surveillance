F. Request approval of the transfer of ownership of the modular at the Levine Campus from CMS to CPCC
Meeting
May 10, 2022 - Regular Board Meeting - 6:00pm - (Closed Session at 4:30pm in CMGC Room CH-14)
Category
4. CONSENT AGENDA
Type
Action (Consent)

Agenda Item Title

Convey mobile classroom to CPCC

    

 

Main Points

  • CMS located a modular classroom building on the CPCC Levine Campus to support the Middle College operations
  • CPCC completed a project on the Levine Campus that eliminates the need for the modular classroom building
  • Per the agreement siting the modular, relocation of the modular from the Levine Campus is the sole responsibility of CMS
  • CMS has determined that the cost to relocate the modular is essentially the same as the cost to acquire and set a new modular classroom
  • Therefore, CMS has offered, and CPCC has agreed to, the conveyance of the modular; this avoids the expense associated with the relocation.

 

 

Fiscal Implications

None

 

Type of Item

Consent Item

 

Motion

Request approval of the transfer of ownership of the modular at the Levine Campus from CMS to CPCC.
