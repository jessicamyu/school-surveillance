Dec 8, 2020 - Regular Board Meeting (Electronic) - 6:00pm
Category
4. CONSENT ITEMS
Type

 

 

Fiscal Impact- Yes

Dollar Amount - $25,814,314

Budgeted -Yes

Budget Source - 2017 Bond Program

Recommended Action - Recommend Approval of the Construction Phase for the New Shamrock Elementary Replacement School Project

Public Content The construction phase was publicly bid and final pricing was received on November 18, 2020 for the New Shamrock Elementary Replacement School. Staff recommends approval of the construction phase to Rodgers-Holt Brothers III, in the amount of $25,814,314. The contract provides for the demolition of the existing building and construction of a new 45-classroom elementary school facility. The contract also includes site work, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, cabling for voice, video, data and all other required systems. The project is scheduled for occupancy in August 2022.

D. Recommend Approval to Lease a portion of land at Lakeview School to Lakewood Preschool, located at 3127 Kalynne St, Charlotte, NC 28202.
Meeting
Dec 8, 2020 - Regular Board Meeting (Electronic) - 6:00pm
Category
4. CONSENT ITEMS
Type

Main Points

  • Charlotte-Mecklenburg Board of Education proposes to lease to Lakewood Preschool.
  • Lakewood Preschool operates preschool programs that provide education and enrichment to children in preschool.
  • Lakewood Preschool has leased land and built a building on the land of the former Lakeview Elementary School property from CMBE since 1996.
  • CMBE may terminate lease with 365 days’ notice

 

Fiscal Implications

Minimal as $1.00 annual rent will continue.

 

Agenda Item Type

Consent Items

 

Motion:

Recommend approval to lease to Lakewood Preschool (“Tenant”) for a term of 5 years, from approval and executed documents for a portion of the property at 3127 Kalynne St on the campus of the former Lakeview Elementary School. Said lease term to be from approximately January 2021 to December 2025.
