At-Large Member - Jennifer De La Jara to Introduce

 

 

 

Our pledge leader tonight is Calli Robles, a junior at South Mecklenburg High School.

 

 

Calli has been active throughout her education in academic and extracurricular activities. She has completed an Outward Bound program and participated in an exchange program with a school in Costa Rica. She is also a Rotary-certified NewGen Peacebuilder. At South Meck, she is also a member of the National Spanish Honor Society and the Gender-Sexuality Alliance. Calli is a middle school tutor and also a student leader in the "One State, One Rate" equity campaign led by the Our Turn Organization.

 

Calli wants to continue her education by going to college. She is interested in Davidson College and New York University (NYU). She has not decided on her major yet, but she would like to work with social sciences and/or social justice issues. However, she knows one thing for sure: She wants to get a minor in Spanish language and culture.

 

We're delighted to have Calli join us tonight and I'll turn to her now to lead the Pledge of Allegiance.
