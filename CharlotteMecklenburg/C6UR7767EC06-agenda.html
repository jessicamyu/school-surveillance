Subject
D. Recommend Approval of Modifications to the 2021-2022 CMS Academic Calendar
Meeting
Sep 28, 2021 - Board Meeting - 6:00pm
Category
6. ACTION ITEM
Type

The recommended change does not alter any dates on the previously approved academic calendar. It only adds the reference that schools may transition to remote learning from in-person learning when schools are unable to open due to severe weather conditions, energy shortages, power failures, or other emergency situations. This authority is limited to five remote instruction days during the 2021-2022 school year. 

 

This authority comes from NC Session Law 2021-130 which can be found here: https://www.ncleg.gov/Sessions/2021/Bills/Senate/PDF/S654v7.pdf.
