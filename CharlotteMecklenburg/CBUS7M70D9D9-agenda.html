Public Content The phase one construction pricing for the Mountain Island Lake Academy Gym and Specialty Classrooms project was received on February 18, 2022.  Staff recommends approval of the phase one construction to The Whiting-Turner Contracting Company in the amount of $1,373,590.00.  The phase one construction provides the steel and roofing required for the construction of the new gym and specialty classroom building. This phase one package allows for early procurement due to the long lead times of these products. The remaining construction packages are scheduled for award as phase two in May 2022 allowing construction to commence in June. The classrooms are scheduled for occupancy in August 2023.
