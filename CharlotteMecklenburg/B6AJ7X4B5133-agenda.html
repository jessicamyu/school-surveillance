Recommend approval of a resolution authorizing the Director of Warehouse Operations to dispose of Surplus property by way of an online bid in auction format.

 

The Board will be asked to approve the adoption of a resolution for the disposal of surplus school property by way of an online bid in auction format.  The online auction time span will encompass December 13, 2018 -  January 8, 2019 period.  GovDeal, www.govdeals.com an experienced and proven online government surplus sales service provider will facilitate the process.

 

Recommend approval authorizing the Director of Warehouse Operations to dispose of Electronics/AV Surplus School property through "secured disposal" by way of private negotiation and sale - using Powerhouse Recycling, Inc. a certified recycling vendor.  The process will occur during the period of December 13, 2018 - January 8, 2019.

 

Action necessary, under N.C.G.S. 160-A266-270, to dispose of surplus property.

 

Action necessary, under 160A-274, sale, lease, exchange and joint governmental property.

 

These auctions usually generate $500.00 to $15,000.00 in revenue.

 

Recommend approval of the adoption of the resolution.
