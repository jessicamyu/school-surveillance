Character Trait: Courage

School: Myers Park High School

Student Pledge Leader: Makayla Gathers

Makayla Gathers is a standout junior at Myers Park High School. She is an extraordinarily gifted student and is enrolled in our International Baccalaureate Diploma Program. Makayla has excelled at participating in the full range of high school life, playing volleyball, leading in the Student Government Association, and working with the school’s administration as a student ambassador to promote diversifying our most rigorous academic offerings in IB, AP, and College Career Promise. Makayla has extended her advocacy work through leadership in the AXE club (Academic Excellence through Education) and by co-founding the Myers Park Black Student Union. Makayla will lead us in the pledge and then discuss this month’s character trait, which is courage.
