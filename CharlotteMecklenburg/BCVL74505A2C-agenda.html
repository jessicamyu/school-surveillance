Subject
I. Recommend Adoption of the 2019 Local Government Retention Schedule
Meeting
Jun 25, 2019 - Regular Board Meeting
Category
4. CONSENT ITEMS
Type
Action (Consent)

The North Carolina Department of Natural and Cultural Resources, Division of Archives and Records, released a new record retention schedule for local education agencies (LEAs) and other local government agencies. The new General Records Schedule for Local Government Agencies (General Schedule) is intended to unify retention schedules for records that are common among local government agencies, such as financial records, personnel records, IT records, etc. The retention standards in the new General Schedule supersede all retention standards in the 1999 Local Education Agencies Records Retention and Disposition Schedule, except Standard 7. Program Operational Records. Records created or received in the offices of the local education agency and used to manage and monitor all federal, state, and local school programs.

 

Each LEA must adopt the new General Schedule before it can destroy any records, except those covered by Standard 7. Each LEA must also establish retention times for items listed in the new General Schedule with the disposition instruction "destroy when reference value ends." Staff recommends records with the disposition instruction "destroy when reference value ends" be retained for one (1) year after the reference value ends.
