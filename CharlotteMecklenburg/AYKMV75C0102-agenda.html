5. REPORTS/INFORMATION ITEMS
Subject
A. Report to the Board from Community Task Force on Student Assignment Review and 2018-19 Student Assignment Plan
Meeting
May 22, 2018 - Regular Board Meeting
Category
5. REPORTS/INFORMATION ITEMS
Type
Information

In July 2017, a community task force was formed in response to parent feedback at the conclusion of the 2016 - 2017 student assignment review. The task force's purpose was multi-fold and included 

‒bringing together engaged parents from schools experiencing a student assignment change

‒reflect on the community’s experience during the student assignment review and codify lessons learned to inform future student assignment reviews 

‒inform the student assignment planning year and ultimate implementation of the plan in key areas such as communication, family engagement, impact of central office decisions on schools, etc.

 

The May 22 report will highlight the task force's findings as well as share its recommendations with the Board.
