Type

  • Subject: Student Program Safety Taskforce Recommendations

  • Type: Information

  • Presenters:

    • Amy Daniels, Community Partner, Director of Outreach for Christ Lutheran Church

    • Laura Francisco, Chief of Staff, CMS

    • Julia Baker Jones, Chair, Student Program Safety Taskforce

  • Summary: The Student Program Safety Taskforce met throughout the 18-19 school year to develop recommendations for how CMS can best protect students from visitors in our buildings. The core team of the task force has already followed through on some recommendations and the remainder will happen over the upcoming academic year.
