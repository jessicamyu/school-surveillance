F. Recommend Approval of 2019-2020 Early College Calendar
Meeting
Feb 12, 2019 - Regular Board Meeting
Category
4. CONSENT ITEMS
Type
Action (Consent)

Will Leach, principal of the Charlotte Engineering Early College and the Charlotte Teacher Early College at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte, is a member of the CMS Calendar Committee. Mr. Leach use the UNC Charlotte Master Academic Calendar to build the Early College Calendar, which was discussed at a School Leadership Team meeting in the fall. To meet state requirements and be aligned with UNC Charlotte, students in both programs must begin classes in early August and end in mid-May. This is typically two weeks before UNC Charlotte classes begin and one week after they end in May. Students in both programs are able to complete the first semester before winter break.

Based on survey data from stakeholders, the attached Continuous Learning Calendar is recommended for approval for 2019-2020 for the following four schools: Walter G. Byers School, Bruns Elementary School, Thomasboro Academy and Druid Hills Academy.

 

 

Budgetary Implications include the following:

 

Transportation July-August

$80,000

Buses for students from CLC start of school to CMS start of school

Intersession Camps

$250,000

About $62,000 per school; pays for teachers to work during intersession breaks to provide students with academic support and enrichment activities. Student participation in intersession camps are optional and are mostly attended by younger, K-3 students.

Compensation

$500,000

Pays for the following:

-Assistant principals additional month of employment

-Social workers and counselors to work an additional month to bolster outreach to parents for increased attendance

-CSA employment

-Employment for Project L.I.F.T. team (curriculum coordinator, Behavior Modification Technicians and others on a 10-month scale)

Total

$830,000
