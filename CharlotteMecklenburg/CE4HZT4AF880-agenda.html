Agenda Item Title

Capital Needs Assessment Update and Engagement Overview

 

Main Points

  • CMBE requires a regular update of the ten year capital needs of Charlotte-Mecklenburg Schools
  • CMS is initiating the final projects funded by the 2017 Capital Investment Plan
  • Efforts to address student assignment and programmatic offerings must be done in coordination with a capital plan
  • A draft Capital Needs Assessment, covering 2022-2031, will be introduced to CMBE and the community at this meeting
  • This draft Capital Needs Assessment, and related programmatic and boundary changes, will be part of a comprehensive community engagement strategy
  • The results of that community engagement, coupled with internal feedback; direction from CMBE; and new and updated data points for the 2022-2023 school year will be collated and a revised CNA will be presented in Spring 2023
  • The revised CNA will produce a Capital Investment Plan, which is intended to be placed before the voters of Mecklenburg County as a general obligations bond referendum in November 2023
