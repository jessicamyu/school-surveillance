Character Trait: Justice and Fairness

 

Student Pledge Leader: Ka'Juan Durer  12th  Grader,  West Mecklenburg High School.

 

 

 

Ka’Juan Durer is a senior at West Mecklenburg High School and led the Pledge of Allegiance at tonight’s CMS Board of Education meeting. Juan currently serves as the student body president and homecoming king. Serving as a student leader at West Meck, Ka’Juan has a desire to positively impact and inspire others. He consistently challenges his peers to not settle for the bare minimum but to work hard for what they truly want out of life. Ka’Juan is an AB honor roll student and works in his community and church; where he volunteers in various outreach ministries, voter registration campaigns and feeding the homeless initiatives. Ka’Juan plans to attend North Carolina A&T State University this fall, to pursue a degree in communications where he can achieve his dream of becoming a radio personality. With his leadership and unwavering faith, he aims to brighten the lives of all members of his community and to provide a promising future for his family. With God on his side, he knows he can accomplish all his dreams.
