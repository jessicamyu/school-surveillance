Staff recommends approval of easements, covenants, and/or right of ways to local utility departments, Charlotte and North Carolina Departments of Transportation and local municipalities related to Charlotte-Mecklenburg School projects at Sharon Elementary, Shamrock Gardens Elementary, New Elementary (Nations Ford, Sterling, Steel Creek relief), New Elementary (Bain, Lebanon relief), West Mecklenburg, East Mecklenburg, and Garinger High Schools. This recommendation is made to facilitate timely processing of documents that may otherwise cause design, operational, or construction delays.

In order to be prepared for opening of school next year, there are some instances in which the purchase orders must be issued prior to July 1 in order to have those items on hand at the start of school (i.e. textbook orders). Approval of this resolution authorizes the Chief Financial Officer to approve 2020-2021 purchase orders for such items. Any purchase order issued prior to July 1 will be charged to the proper appropriation in the 2020-2021 Operating Budget upon adoption.

The construction phase was publicly bid for the West Charlotte High School Replacement (17) project. Final pricing was received on April 15, 2020. Staff recommends approval of the construction phase to Rodgers | Holt Brothers, a Joint Venture, in the amount of $88,173,714.00. The contract includes site work, building & associated construction including HVAC, plumbing, electrical, cabling for voice, video, data and all other systems required to construct the new high school facility. The existing turf field and track will remain. The contract also includes demolition and replacement of the existing high school, athletic fields & field houses, pool and provide new bleacher seating at the football stadium. The school is scheduled for occupancy August 2022 and demolition and sitework will continue until August 2023.
