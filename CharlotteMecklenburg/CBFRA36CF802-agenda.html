Meeting

Category
3. PUBLIC ENGAGEMENT
Type

 

  1. Modify ACD - Nondiscrimination on the Basis of Religion/Religion in Schools (attached)
    • Rationale: Takes language from policy ACDA and includes it into #16 of Policy ACD

 

  1. Modify BIFA, BIFA-E, GBGF (DIRM Language)
    • Rationale: Changes language from “Division of Insurance and Risk Management” to the correct terminology of “Risk Management”. If the policies are approved, we will request Superintendent make corresponding change to KF-R.

 

  1. Modify JEB - Entrance Age Requirement (attached)
    • Rationale: Current policy doesn’t comply with state law. So we simply delete the existing policy and replace it with the exact language from NCGS 115C-364.

 

  1. Retire ACDA - Schools and Religious Speakers/Organizations
    • Rationale: Language is placed in ACD if New Business A (above) is approved

 

  1. Retire KGE, KGE-E - CMS Television Underwriting
    • Rationale: CMS TV was disbanded by BOE action in 2010, so items are now moot
