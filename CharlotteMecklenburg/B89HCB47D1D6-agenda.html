Recommended Action
Recommend Approval to Dispose of Surplus Property

Recommend approval of a resolution authorizing the Director of Warehouse Operations to dispose of Surplus property by way of an online bid in auction format.

 

 

The Board will be asked to approve the adoption of a resolution for the disposal of surplus school property by way of an online bid in an auction format.  The online auction period will encompass  January 24 - February 26, 2019 period. GovDeals, www.govdeals.com an experienced and proven online government surplus sales service provider will facilitate the process.

 

Recommend approval authorizing the Director of Warehouse Operations to dispose of Electronics/AV Surplus School property through "secured disposal" by way of private negotiation and sale - using Powerhouse Recycling, Inc. a certified recycling vendor.  The process will occur during the period of January 24 - February 26, 2019.

 

Action necessary, under N.C.G.S. 160-A226-270, to dispose of surplus property.

 

Action necessary, under 160A-274, sale, lease, exchange and joint governmental property.

 

These auctions usually generate $500 to $15,000 in revenue.

 

Recommend approval of the adoption of the resolution.

Recommend Approval of Easements Related to Charlotte-Mecklenburg Schools Construction Projects

Staff recommends approval of easements, covenants, and/or right of ways to local utility departments, Charlotte and North Carolina Departments of Transportation and local municipalities related to Charlotte-Mecklenburg Schools projects at Harding, Phillip O. Berry, West Charlotte, New HS (Olympic relief), New K-8 Language Immersion North, New K-8 Language Immersion South, Briarwood, Landsdowne, Montclaire, and Lincoln Heights. Additionally staff recommends approval at school sites where mobile classroom placement or relocations are required from January 2019 through December 2019. Requested easements, covenants, and/or right of ways will follow Charlotte-Mecklenburg Schools normal legal review and signature process. This recommendation is made to facilitate timely processing of documents that may otherwise cause design, operational, or construction delays.

Recommend Approval of the Construction Phase Work for the New STEAM K-8 School
