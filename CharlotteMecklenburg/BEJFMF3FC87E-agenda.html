The City of Charlotte has requested a permanent sanitary sewer easement at Myers Park High School. In the fall of 2018, The City of Charlotte (Charlotte Water) made an emergency repair to an existing sewer line located on Myers Park High School’s property. A new pipe was installed to replace the section of sewer pipe that failed, and the City is requesting a permanent sanitary sewer easement to allow for maintenance of the new pipe.
