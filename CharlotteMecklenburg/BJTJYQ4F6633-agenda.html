Subject
J. Recommend Approval of Local Paid Leave for Deputy General Counsel
Meeting
Jan 14, 2020 - Regular Board Meeting
Category
7. CONSENT ITEMS
Type
Action (Consent)

The Board recognizes that the deputy general counsel will provide services to the Board amounting to many hours of additional time above and beyond those contemplated in her job description. The Board also recognizes that the deputy general counsel's former rate of earning annual leave has been adversely impacted by becoming a CMS employee. Therefore, the deputy general counsel will be eligible to take seven (7) days of additional, local paid leave, effective this fiscal year and each fiscal year thereafter while employed with the Board. The deputy general counsel will take any such leave prior to using any accrued, unused annual leave days. Furthermore, any such additional local paid leave that is not used during the fiscal year in which it is earned will be automatically forfeited and may not be accumulated or carried over to the following fiscal year, nor will it be subject to any payment by the Board or otherwise at the time of the deputy general counsel's separation from employment with the Board for whatever the reason.

Subject
D. Recommend Approval of Modifications to the June 4, 2019 Motion Which Established the 2020-21 Attendance Areas for Polo Ridge, Hawk Ridge, Jay M. Robinson and Community House Schools
Meeting
Jan 14, 2020 - Regular Board Meeting
Category
8. ACTION ITEMS
Type
Action (Consent)
Fiscal Impact
No
Budgeted
No

As part of the June 4, 2019 motion establishing the boundary for the new Rea Farms K-8 school, the Board approved the following:

 

Assign to Polo Ridge with continuation to Jay M. Robinson, the portion of Hawk Ridge Elementary attendance area which is east of Rea Road between Bryant Farms Rd. and Ardrey Kell Rd., including east of the lot line behind Elrose Place.

 

The Raeburn community, which sits both east and west of Rea Road, was assigned to two elementary and two middle schools as a result. 

 

The recommended change would allow the entire Raeburn community to attend elementary school and middle school together.  The modified motion will now read:

 

Assign to Polo Ridge with continuation to Jay M. Robinson, the portion of Hawk Ridge Elementary attendance area which is east of Rea Road between Bryant Farms Rd. and Ardrey Kell Rd., including east of the lot line behind Elrose Place and the portion of the Raeburn community which is west of Rea Road.

 

The effect of the change is that the entire Raeburn neighborhood will be assigned to Polo Ridge Elementary with continuation to Jay M. Robinson Middle beginning with the 2020-21 school year.  There is no change in the high school assignment.  Students who will be in grades 5, 7 and 8 in the 2020-21 school year will be grandfathered to remain in their current schools as consistent with the original June 4, 2019 motion.  No other changes to the June 4, 2019 motion are recommended.
