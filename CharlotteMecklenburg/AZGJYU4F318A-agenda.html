B. Recommend Approval to Dispose of Surplus Property
Meeting

Category
2. CONSENT ITEMS
Type

Recommend approval of a resolution authorizing the Director of Warehouse Operations to dispose of Surplus property by way of an online bid in an auction format.

 

The Board will be asked to approve the adoption of a resolution for he disposal of surplus school property by way of an online bid in an auction format.  The online auction time span will encompass June 28 - July 14, 2018 period.  GovDeals, www.govdeals.com an experienced and proven online government surplus sales service provide will facilitate the process.

 

Recommend approval authorizing the Director of Warehouse Operations to dispose of Electronic/AV Surplus school property through "secured disposal" by way of private negotiation and sale - using Powerhouse Recycling, Inc. a certified recycling vendor.  The process will occur during the period of June 28 - July 14, 2018.

 

Action necessary, under N.C.G.S. 160-A266-270, to dispose of surplus property.

 

Action necessary, under 160A-274, sale, lease, exchange and joint governmental property.

 

These auctions usually generate $500 to $15,000 in revenue.

 

Recommend approval of the adoption of the resolution.
