Character Trait:  Citizenship

 

Student Pledge Leader:  Mason Lennon

 

 

Mason Lennon is a Senior at Hough HS. He has been a member of the Husky Runners track team since 9th grade, has been a state qualifier twice and served a captain of both Cross County and Track. He is President of National Honor Society and a member of the Spanish Honor Society.

Mason is an Eagle Scout Candidate in Troop 200- Huntersville. He served on the Lake Norman Chamber Junior Leadership Council and as a House Page in the NC General Assembly. He was appointed as a delegate to the 65th Annual Tar Heel Boy State last summer by American Legion Post 86- Cornelius. While at Boys State, he was elected Secretary of Commerce and he was selected by the adult leadership to be an alternate delegate to Boys Nation.

”There are many qualities that help Mason stand out as a student. He has a tremendous work ethic which sets him apart from other students. He has a very mature attitude that allows him to excel in the classroom. He has distinguished himself as a bright, inquisitive, energetic scholar by his classroom participation, completion of homework and other assignments, and his desire to learn. Mason is popular among students and teachers alike. He works well independently but is also someone everyone wants to collaborate with. He is able to keep projects on track while making sure everyone in his group is valued and included. Without a doubt, Mason will continue to prove successful in everything he puts his mind to.”

Vickie Waddell
AP US Government Teacher
William A. Hough High School

Mason plans to attend college and study Business and Finance.

 

Accompanied by His Mother Rhonda Cheek and Stepfather Brent Cheek as well as Hough Principal Dr. Laura Rosenbach, AP Government Teacher Vicki Waddell , and Learning Community Superintendent Acquanetta Brown.
