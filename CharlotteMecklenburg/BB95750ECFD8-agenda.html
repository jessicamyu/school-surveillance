The construction phase was publicly bid for the Montclaire Elementary School construction project and final pricing was received on April 10, 2019. Staff recommends approval of the construction phase to Messer Construction Co. in the amount of $24,382,663.00. The contract includes site work, building & associated construction including HVAC, plumbing, electrical, cabling for voice, video, data and all other systems required to construct the new 45 classroom elementary school facility. The project also includes the demolition of the existing facility. The project is scheduled for occupancy in August 2020.
