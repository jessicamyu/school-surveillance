Introduction by: Rhonda Cheek, Distrcit 1

Character Trait:  ​RESPECT
School:  ​Coulwood STEM Academy

 

Yaretzy Martinez is an exceptional 8th grade student at Coulwood STEM Academy. Throughout her time at Coulwood, she has gained the respect of her peers, administrators and teachers alike. Her Robotics Club sponsor, Mr. Robbins, says of Yaretzy, “​From the day I first met Yaretzy as a 6​th​ grader, it became clear she was someone special.  She wanted to be involved in everything STEM - and play the violin, too. She has demonstrated her drone flying skills for the media and taught coding skills to 3​rd​ and 5​th​ graders at Paw Creek Elementary. She is a leader in and out of the classroom. Yaretzy for President!​”  

 

Even as we have been learning remotely, Yaretzy has continued to stay motivated in the classroom. Her 8th grade ELA teacher, Mrs. Jarrell, says, “Yaretzy is one of those students who makes you happy to teach, even over a screen. She is one of the most positive people in the classroom, and she is not afraid to try something, even if she doesn’t get it right the first time. I am so impressed with the leadership and kindness that Yaretzy shows on a daily basis.” Yaretzy will only continue to achieve and pave the way for others; we cannot wait to see what she will accomplish now and in the future!

  1. Restoration of Storm Water System and Repair of Sinkhole Project was bid on June 23, 2020. Staff recommends the contract to the lowest responsible responsive bidder; JL Raper Corporation in the amount of $440,500.

NEED FOR CHANGE ORDER:

Contractor is experiencing unforeseen difficulties at the Wilkinson Sinkhole Repair Project. The contractor needs to re-route a storm water pipe to avoid the City of Charlotte's sewer manhole and associated pipe. In addition, the contractor will incur additional costs to replace a junction box and nearly 100% of soil excavated due to existing soil not being suitable for backfilling. Soil samples collected and analyzed during pre-bid investigations did not indicate poor conditions of the soil.
