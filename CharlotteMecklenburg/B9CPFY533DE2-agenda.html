Recommend approval of a resolution authorizing the Director of Warehouse Operations to dispose of Surplus property by way of an online bid in auction format.

 

The Board will be asked to approve the adoption of a resolution for the disposal of surplus school property by way of an online bid in auction format.  The online auction period will encompass February 28 - March 12, 2019 period.  GovDeals, www.govdeals.com an experienced and proven online government surplus sales service provider will facilitate the process.

 

Recommend approval authorizing the Director of Warehouse Operations to dispose of Electronics/AV Surplus School property through "secured disposal" by way of private negotiation and sale - using Powerhouse Recycling, Inc. a certified recycling vendor.  The process will occur during the period of February 28 - March 12, 2019.

 

Action necessary, under N.C.G.S. 160-A266-270, to dispose of surplus property.

 

Action necessary, under 160A-274, sale, lease, exchange and joint governmental property.

 

These auctions usually generate $500 to $15,000 in revenue.

 

Recommend approval of the adoption of the resolution.

Based on survey data from stakeholders, the attached Continuous Learning Calendar is recommended for approval for 2019-2020 for the following four schools: Walter G. Byers School, Bruns Elementary School, Thomasboro Academy and Druid Hills Academy.

 

 

Budgetary Implications include the following:

 

Transportation July-August

$80,000

Buses for students from CLC start of school to CMS start of school

Intersession Camps

$250,000

About $62,000 per school; pays for teachers to work during intersession breaks to provide students with academic support and enrichment activities. Student participation in intersession camps are optional and are mostly attended by younger, K-3 students.

Compensation

$500,000

Pays for the following:

-Assistant principals additional month of employment

-Social workers and counselors to work an additional month to bolster outreach to parents for increased attendance

-CSA employment

-Employment for Project L.I.F.T. team (curriculum coordinator, Behavior Modification Technicians and others on a 10-month scale)

Total

$830,000
