The construction phase for the Ashley Park PK-8 Conversion was publicly and final pricing was received on August 15, 2018. Staff recommends approval of construction phase to Turner Construction Company in the amount of $ 9,849,521. The contract will include sitework, parking, building construction, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, cabling for voice, video, data and all other systems required to construct the new building which includes a new gymnasium and four specialty classrooms. The contract will also include the renovation/expansion of the existing kitchen & cafeteria.  The project is scheduled for occupancy in the fall of 2019.
