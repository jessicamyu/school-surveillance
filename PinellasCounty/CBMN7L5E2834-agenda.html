2. Workshop Topics

2.1 Superintendent Search
Meeting
Mar 10, 2022 - School Board Work Session, Superintendent Search - 8:30 a.m.
Type
Discussion

Presentation and Discussion: Florida School Board Association (FSBA) representatives and the board will discuss the Superintendent search process.

 

Determine:

  • Minimum/preferred qualifications - Qualities and characteristics needed in the next superintendent
  • Salary range - Length of contact to be offered
  • Contents of application packet
  • Application process and advertisement venues

 

Discuss: 

  • Applicant screening process to determine semi-finalists and finalists as desired by the Board.

 

Initial Discussion:

  • Finalist’s interview schedule and associated activities

 

Update:

  • Timeline as needed, and other items as may be needed
