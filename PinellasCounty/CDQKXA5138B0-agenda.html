BACKGROUND:

Section 1001.371, Florida Statutes, addresses the date of the School Board’s organization meeting. The proceedings of organization at this meeting include setting a schedule for School Board meetings from this date until the date of the 2022 School Board Organization meeting.

 

RATIONALE:

Setting the schedule for regular School Board meetings from this date until the date of the 2022 School Board Organization meeting will serve the best interest of the public, the school board and district administration.
