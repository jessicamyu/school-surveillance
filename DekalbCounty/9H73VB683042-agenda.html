4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition of Middle School Wrestling Team
Meeting
Apr 15, 2014 - CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Board of Education will recognize our middle school wrestling teams for the following accomplishments:

 

Our district middle school wrestlers had a strong showing at the IESA State Championship on March 15th.  Clinton Rosette had 11 state qualifiers, with Jacob Lindemann placing 5th and Jacobe Schmerbach placing 3rd in state. 

 

Huntley Middle School had 8 state qualifiers, with Justin Hernandez placing 5th, Christian Komitas placing  6th, Milo Johnson placing  4th and Keonte McKinnie placing  5th. 

 

Clinton Rosette finished 21st in state, while Huntley finished 12th in the state.
