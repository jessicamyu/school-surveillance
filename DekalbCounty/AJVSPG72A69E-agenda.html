Recognition

Chess Team:  

DHS Chess team competed in the Illinois State Chess Tournament this year. Team members are Christian Cooper, Daniel Alcaraz, Henry Robinson, Robert Hanson, Thomas Matejka-Crome, Santana Juarez, Dayne Johnson, Cameron Zeiter, Jacob McCann, and Liberty Mildner. The team played chess for two full days with sportsmanship and skill. Although chess season is over, they are continuing to meet twice weekly to practice new moves and strategies.

 

Forensics:

The DHS Forensics team completed another very successful season with a team of over 50 individuals.  In addition to accumulating over 200 individual awards, the team won team awards an impressive 8 times over the course of the season including its 16th consecutive Regional Championship, and 22nd in the last 23 years.  The team advanced 10 individuals to Sectionals and four to the State tournament.  At State, seniors Molly Stawinoga and Michael Williams finished in the Top 12 in their individual events and senior Paul Un qualified for state for the fourth consecutive year; finishing in  9th place in Extemporaneous Speaking and making the State Finals in Impromptu – finishing 6th in the State. 

 

Dance Team:

After placing 4th in the IHSA sectionals competition, the dance team went on to the state competition. There, they placed 18th out of the 30 teams in the 2A division in the IHSA state finals. The team has been working hard since June on choreography and technique, and the hard work and long season definitely paid off. This has been the most successful competition season yet for the DHS dance team, and Coach Valkner is very proud of the girls and their accomplishments.

 

Boys Basketball:

 

The Barbs finished the season 19-11 after returning only one starter.  This group also won the Northern Illinois Big 12 Conference for the third consecutive year. Senior Michael Mitchell was named the NIB 12 Most Valuable Player. Mitchell, senior Cole Tucker and senior Darvon Sisson were all named First Team All-Conference. Junior Michael Vilet was named Special Mention All-Conference.


In addition, six members of the varsity team were named Academic All-Conference. This is for student athletes who maintain an overall G.P.A. of 3.5 or better.  They included juniors Michael Vilet and Daniel Baggett, seniors Michael Mitchell, Cole Tucker, Wess Kunz and Derek Kyler.  

 


Wrestling:

The DeKalb wrestling team was the Northern Illinois Big 12 East conference regular season champs and finished second in the conference tournament.  Wrestlers receiving all conference recognition were Tyler Scarbrough, Fabian Lopez, Jakob Norman, Jack Evans, Logan Roach, Jacobe Schmerbach, and Niels Lewerentz.  Ethan Engelson was all conference honorable mention.  Blah Dahnweih and Fabian Lopez qualified for the IHSA State Tournament.  Fabian Lopez reached the finals and ended his freshman season as the 120 lb state runner up becoming DeKalb's first AAA state finalist and becoming the first freshman to reach the finals in DeKalb history!

 

Gymnastics:

The DeKalb-Sycamore Coop Gymnastic Team finished the regular season with an undefeated record.  They won the Upstate Eight Conference Team Championship.  They were also IHSA Regional and IHSA Sectional Team Champions.  The Sparbs brought home the 2nd Place Team Trophy at the IHSA State Tournament.
