You are invited to an open public forum to discuss equity, justice, race, and systemic issues.

This forum will be an opportunity for students and members of the community to address the DeKalb CUSD #428 Board of Education and to work together to achieve positive change within our school district and community by sharing stories and experiences.  

The meeting will take place at the Education Center Boardroom, 901 South Fourth Street in DeKalb, IL from 12:00-2:00 p.m.  Those in attendance who wish to speak should complete a Comment Card, available at the front door.  Social distancing guidelines will be observed and masks will be required. Members of the public who wish to join remotely may do so by joining the meeting using the Google Meet link above.  Comments will be limited to 5 minutes and  criticism of individuals is not in order. The meeting will also be livestreamed and can be viewed by subscribing by here:  https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLRG84hK2AMfxoRB6h50T8zi40ocrPk2Fs
