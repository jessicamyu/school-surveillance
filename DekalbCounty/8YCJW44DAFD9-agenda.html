Subject
A. Recognition of Semi-Finalist Students in the 2013 National Merit Scholarship Program
Meeting
Oct 2, 2012 - October 2, 2012
Category
2. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

 

The Board of Education will recognize seniors Eliyah Baker and Allison Duffin for being named as semi-finalists in the National Merit Scholarship Competition.  The National Merit® Scholarship Program is an academic competition for recognition and scholarships that began in 1955. High school students enter the National Merit Program by taking the Preliminary SAT/National Merit Scholarship Qualifying Test and by meeting published program entry/participation requirements.

Angelica Garcia

Courtney Donnelly

Kristin Johnson

Ashley Lorenz

Kelsey Grubbs (Secretary)

 

DCP/SAFE stands for DeKalb County Partnership for Safe and Active Family Environment.  Their mission is to improve the environment of our school and community by encouraging healthy choices and lifestyles. Some of their focus in the past has been underage drinking, texting and driving, and discouraging the use of tobacco.This group meets every other week in the student services conference room.  This is a dynamic group of students who are dedicated to their mission.  Dennis Waymire, DHS School Counselor,  is the group facilitator.
