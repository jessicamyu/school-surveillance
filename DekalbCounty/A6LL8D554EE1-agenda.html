Subject
A. Recognition - Brooks Elementary PTA Reflections Contest Winners
Meeting
Feb 2, 2016 - CUSD #428 Workshop School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

Brooks Elementary School held its first PTA Reflections contest in which there were 3 winners:

 
Ellison Xu- Intermediate Visual Arts
Erra Sanderson-Haque- Primary Visual Arts
Gianna Gooden- Intermediate Literature
 
These 3 students went on to the Regional competition in their categories & divisions where they placed:
 
Ellison Xu- 4th overall
Erra Sanderson-Haque- 1st overall
Gianna Gooden- 1st overall
 
The Board of Education congratulates Ellison, Erra and Gianna who are moving on to compete in the State Reflections contest on April 30th at Millikin University.
