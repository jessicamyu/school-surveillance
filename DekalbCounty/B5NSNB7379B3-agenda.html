Subject
B. Recognition: Clinton Rosette Middle School 8th Grade Student, Riley Newport
Meeting
Nov 6, 2018 - CUSD #428 Workshop School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Board of Education congratulates Clinton Rosette Middle School 8th grader Riley Newport, who on Saturday, October 13, won the IESA Class 2A State Cross Country Championship at Parkside Middle School in Normal, IL. Riley covered the two-mile course in a time of 10:36.2, culminating in an individually undefeated 2018 season, winning every single race all season long. As a result, he was also North Central Junior Conference champion as well as Genoa-Kingston Sectional champion over the previous two weekends, where the CRMS team took 3rd place at both of those meets.
