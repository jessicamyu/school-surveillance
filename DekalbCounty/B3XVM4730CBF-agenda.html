4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition - Early Learning and Development Center Preschool for All Program
Meeting
Sep 4, 2018 - CUSD #428 Workshop School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Illinois State Board of Education recognized the Circle of Quality that the Early Learning and Development Center’s Preschool for All (PFA) program has achieved in ExceleRate Illinois, the state’s quality recognition and improvement system for early learning and development programs. ExceleRate unifies all of the state’s early learning programs under a common set of standards across multiple settings to provide families with important information about quality early learning programs in their community.

 

Based on the most recent monitoring results, the Early Learning and Development Center, has achieved the Gold Circle of Quality. A certificate to recognize this award will be sent from the Illinois Network of Child Care Resource & Referral Agencies.
