Subject
E. Chesebro Easement Agreement, Tammy Carson (I)
Meeting
Jul 20, 2021 - DeKalb CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
5. NEW BUSINESS
Type
Information

During construction at Chesebro Elementary, the civil engineers identified an issue with the property line to the west of the building near Garden Street. The home at that property line installed a driveway on the property to access a garage in the back of the property. It has been determined that the majority of the driveway is actually on District property but did not impact the new parking lot and sidewalks.

 

Administration discussed the infringement of the District property with the District attorney and determined the best course of action would be to discuss the infringement with the owner of the property and come to an agreement to allow them to use the driveway by executing an Easement Agreement. The District Attorney prepared the attached document as well as worked with the civil engineers to finalize the terms and legal description.

Subject
F. Data Wins Consulting Proposal / Philip Iem (I) (A)
Meeting
Jul 20, 2021 - DeKalb CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
5. NEW BUSINESS
Type
Action (Consent)

To address attendance, student discipline, and failure rates the Department of Improvement and Innovation (I&I) is developing an Early Warning and Response System (EWRS) to ensure early identification and timely intervention for struggling students. A critical component of the EWRS is On Track data and reporting. On track data is a report provided to administrators and teachers, at least every 5 weeks, detailing student performance across three predictive domains for academic success: 1) attendance, 2) behavior, and 3) course performance. 

 

Data Wins LLC is a consultant I&I would like to partner with to leverage their expertise with On Track data. The partnership with Data Wins LLC will include: 1) development of On Track metrics and reporting, 2) guidance and ongoing support for district and school teams, and 3) data strategy. Please see the attached proposal for additional details.
