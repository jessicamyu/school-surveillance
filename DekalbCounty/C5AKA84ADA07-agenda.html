Subject
E. Chesebro Easement Agreement
Meeting
Aug 3, 2021 - DeKalb CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
3. CONSENT AGENDA
Type
Action (Consent)
Recommended Action
Administration is recommending that the Board of Education approve the Easement Agreement between
the School District and the owner of the property located at 824 E. Garden Street in DeKalb, Illinois.

During construction at Chesebro Elementary, the civil engineers identified an issue with the property line to the west of the building near Garden Street. The home at that property line installed a driveway on the property to access a garage in the back of the property. It has been determined that the majority of the driveway is actually on District property but did not impact the new parking lot and sidewalks.

 

Administration discussed the infringement of the District property with the District attorney and determined the best course of action would be to discuss the infringement with the owner of the property and come to an agreement to allow them to use the driveway by executing an Easement Agreement. The District Attorney prepared the attached document as well as worked with the civil engineers to finalize the terms and legal description.
