Meeting
Aug 6, 2013 - DeKalb CUSD 428 Workshop Board Meeting
Category
5. NON-BUSINESS CONSENT AGENDA
Type
Action (Consent)
Recommended Action
"that the Board of Education approve the attached intergovernmental agreement with the City of DeKalb to paint the "Barbs" logo on the east side of the water tower by DeKalb High School."

Dr. Briscoe & Bryon Houy have been meeting with the City of DeKalb to develop an agreement to place the Barb logo on the water tower by DeKalb High School.  The city has agreed to contribute $5,000 toward the project and other funds will be raised to assist with the cost.  More details on a timeline for completion of the project will be forthcoming.
