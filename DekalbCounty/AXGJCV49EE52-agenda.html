Subject
B. Recognition of the DeKalb High School Art Students
Meeting
Apr 17, 2018 - CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Board of Education and the DeKalb High School Art Department would like to recognize the achievements of Grace Verbic, Darius Moore, and Maeve Wallace at the 2018 IHSAE Art Exhibitions in Chicago. 


Grace Verbic is one of 20 Illinois High School artists chosen to have their artwork displayed around Chicago and the Illinois Capitol Building for the next year. 
 
Darius Moore was awarded Best in Show Photography at the IHSAE General Exhibition. 
 
Maeve Wallace was awarded Best in Show at the IHSAE Senior Scholarship Exhibition. 
 
Congratulations to Grace, Darius and Maeve on their outstanding achievements!
