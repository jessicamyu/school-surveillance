4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Acceptance of Gifts and Donations
Meeting
Jun 16, 2015 - CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Action
Recommended Action
"that the Board of Education accept with gratitude the donation of de-inventoried durable medical equipment supplies to the DeKalb High School from Midwest Orthopaedic Institute, valued at approximately $3,500; the donation of a 1995 Toyota Avalon to the Large Engines class at DeKalb High School by Tammy Tuell; and the donation of $18,065.94 in grants from the DeKalb Education Foundation."
The DeKalb High School Athletic Department would like to thank Midwest Orthopaedic Institute, Sycamore, IL for its recent donation of de-inventoried Durable Medical Equipment (DME) supplies.  These items will help continue the success of our students as they learn, grow  and compete at DeKalb High School.  Several of these items have already found use with our young people who may not have had the financial means to purchase them outright.  Although a full inventory of items was not taken, our sports medicine staff estimates the donated value of the braces, splint materials, supports and other DME items at approximately $3500.00.
