4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition - DHS Fall Teams
Meeting
Dec 6, 2016 - CUSD #428 Workshop School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

Boys Golf Team:

The boys golf team took 2nd place at the Northern Illinois Big 12 Conference meet and the Boylan Invitational.  They finished in 1st place at the Freeport Invitational and their own Kishwaukee Invite.  The Barbs finished the year on a strong note by winning the IHSA Regional and qualifying 2 individuals to the IHSA State Meet.  Austin Freeman is a two time IHSA State qualifier.  He shot a 76 and 75 with a total of 151 at the state meet this year which earned him 11th place!  Emma Carpenter qualified for the IHSA State Tournament and shot a 75 on Friday and 74 on Saturday for a total of 149.  She earned All-State honors by finishing in 10th place at the IHSA State Meet. 

 

Volleyball:

The varsity volleyball team finished the year with a record of 21-16.  They finished in 3rd place in the Northern Illinois Big 12 Conference.  In the post-season, the team hit their stride and won the IHSA Regional and Sectional.  They finished their historical season by advancing to the IHSA Super-Sectional which is the best finish in program history!  Hannah Oehlberg and JC Longville received 1st team All-Northern Illinois Big 12 Honors. 

 

Football:

The DeKalb football team finished the 2016 season with a 10-3 record, a Northern Illinois Big 12 East Division Conference championship, a First National Bank Challenge win over Sycamore and an IHSA Semifinal finish. Along with the team accomplishments they also had many individual achievements. The Barbs had 4 all-conference honorable mention players and 9 all-conference players including the two co-Conference MVPs for the second year in a row, Cole Tucker and Derek Kyler. Derek and Cole were also placed on the all-state team for 6A in 2016.
