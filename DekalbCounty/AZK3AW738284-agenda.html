6. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition - Barb Food Mart's Fifth Year Anniversary
Meeting
Jun 19, 2018 - CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
6. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition
The DeKalb Board of Education would like to recognize Barb Food Mart for their fifth year anniversary.  During the past five years, Barb Food Mart has served 80 - 100 District families in need every Thursday.  The concept of Barb Food Mart started by caring and energetic retired District #428 teachers and has continued to thrive with the collaborative work of so many retired teachers, District staff and caring community members.  We'd like to thank the efforts of the Barb Food Mart Team for their service to so many DeKalb District #428 families.
