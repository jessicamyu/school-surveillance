5. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition: Cortland, Jefferson and Tyler Elementary School - Awarded the Illinois Association of Health, Physical Education, Recreation and Dance Blue Ribbon Status / Kelly Zerby and Melanie Bickley
Meeting
Oct 20, 2020 - DeKalb CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
5. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Illinois Association of Health, Physical Education, Recreation and Dance has recently awarded Blue Ribbon Status to three of our elementary schools: Cortland, Jefferson, and Tyler.  The award is given to schools with PE programs with exemplary quality and commitment to Illinois State Learning Standards in physical education.  The recognition period lasts for 5 years.  As a recipient of this award, the school and staff will be a model of excellence in the state and provide encouragement for all schools in their pursuits to develop rigorous and effective physical education programs.  This award is very exciting because the criteria is rigorous and reflective, and we will now be recognized historically in an elite group of schools and educators in the State of Illinois, which also sets a tone for the future of physical education in the state.  Congratulations to Bill McGuire, Shay Sticka, Emily Young, Kelly Zerby, Brandon Daub and Kimberly Tinsley for all their hard work.

Category
7. SUPERINTENDENT'S REPORT / PRESENTATIONS
Type
Discussion

We have a request to have three alternate logos trademarked for DeKalb High School. As promised last month, we secured cost estimates for this Board meeting.  The “all in” cost to seek Trademarks is approximately $22,000 per Logo.  We are seeking Board input on whether D428 should move forward with this at a total cost of $66,000.  If there is a general consensus of the Board, we will see formal Board approval in November.

 

$3,515            Trademark Searches

 

$18,850          Application Fees (10 Classes)

 

$22,365          Total per logo (10 Classes - may be lower)

Subject
B. Gymnastics Program Facility Usage / Ray Lechner (A)
Meeting
Oct 20, 2020 - DeKalb CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
7. SUPERINTENDENT'S REPORT / PRESENTATIONS
Type
Action
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Board of Education approve the approve contract with Energym Gymnastics, Inc., subject to attorney review and subject to approval by the administration.

Our gymnastics program has a long-standing tradition of swapping facility use for coaching services with no contract.  To comply with Board policy, we have been working with legal counsel on a contract.

 

As of the date of publication of this agenda, the contract is still being reviewed and revised by legal counsel. To avoid a pause in programming, the Board is advised to approve the contract subject to attorney review and administration approval.
