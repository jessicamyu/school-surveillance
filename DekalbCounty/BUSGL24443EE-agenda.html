Subject
H. Approval to Donate the Gift from the Estate of Dean C. Burgemeister to the DeKalb Education Foundation
Meeting
Nov 17, 2020 - DeKalb CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
5. CONSENT AGENDA
Type
Action (Consent)
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Board of Education donate the gift of $43,929.46 from the Estate of Dean C. Burgemeister to the DeKalb Education Foundation.

The District has been gifted $43,929.46 by the Estate of Dean C. Burgemeister.  Mr. Burgemeister and his wife Betty were frequent donors to many institutions throughout the country, and locally, including Kish Health Systems, Kishwaukee College where they formed the Dean and Betty Burgemeister Scholarship, and Kishwaukee College Foundation where they formed the Dean and Betty Burgemeister Endowment. 

 

At the August 18, 2020 School Board Meeting, the Interim Superintendents recommended that this gift be extended to the DeKalb Education Foundation to aid in their efforts to continue to provide valuable assistance to our District Teachers through grant offerings made available by that Foundation.
