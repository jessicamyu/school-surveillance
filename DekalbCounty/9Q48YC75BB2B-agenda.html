4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition - Middle School Cross Country Team
Meeting
Nov 4, 2014 - CUSD #428 Workshop School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition
The Board of Education will recognize Clinton Rosette students Christina Ryzhov, Katherine Olsen, and Jacob Trapp for qualifying for the IESA State 2A Meet on October 18th.  
 
Jacob and Katherine had a strong showing either equaling or beating previous records.  
 
Christina followed up her 2013 IESA 2A 1st place  finish with a repeat performance on Saturday, finishing the 2 mile course in 11 minutes and 16 seconds.    
 
The Board of Education will also recognize Huntley Middle School student Riley Marks  for his IESA Cross Country State meet accomplishment.  He took 16th out of 207 runners with a personal best time of 10:45 earning a medal in the 3A state meet.
