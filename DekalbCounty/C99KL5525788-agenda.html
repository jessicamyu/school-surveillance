Meeting
Dec 14, 2021 - DeKalb CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
8. NEW BUSINESS
Type
Action, Discussion
We have been selected to attend the Courageous Principals professional development facilitated by Deloitte. This nationally acclaimed learning is highly sought after and selection is tough. The Courageous Principals training programs deliver an immersive and dynamic curriculum to offer K-12 school leaders 2.5 day (Friday-Sunday) learning programs offering:
  • Accomplished facilitators experienced in leadership and influence techniques
  • Experiential and problem-based learning leveraging real-life principal challenges
  • Expanded professional networks and team-based learning to share ideas and work together to develop collaborative strategies.

 

 

  • My intent is to take the 12 principals, 2 Board Members, and 1 Cabinet member.

 

The administration requests action tonight to accept the Deloitte Courageous Principals scholarship and allow for up to 16 administrators (principals, cabinet, Board, and superintendent) attend the sessions in Dallas, TX from April 22-24th.
