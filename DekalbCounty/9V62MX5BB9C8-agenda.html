Procedural

The Board will hold a public hearing for the purpose of considering whether to submit a waiver of School Code (Section 5/27-23) from the Illinois State Board of Education to allow the district to charge a reasonable fee, not to exceed $400, for students participating in Driver's Education behind-the-wheel training.

Recommended Action
"that the Board of Education accept with gratitude the donation of $200.00 to Lincoln Elementary from the family of former Lincoln Principal Irwin Cox."

Former Lincoln Principal Irwin Cox’s daughter, Liz Botts, would like to provide a $200 donation in the name of her father Irwin to be used to buy supplies for students that cannot afford to purchase supplies of their own.  Irwin Cox was an employee in the district from Sept 1956 -- retirement June 1983. He became Principal of Lincoln Elementary in 1958, moving to Littlejohn from 1974-1983.
