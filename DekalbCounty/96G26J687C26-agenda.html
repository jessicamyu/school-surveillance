9. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition - Kelsey Faivre and Justene Jennings
Meeting
May 7, 2013 - May 7, 2013
Category
9. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

 

The Board of Education will recognize Kelsey Faivre and Justene Jennings for their very successful performances at the District II FFA Public Speaking Contest.  Kelsey placed first for her extemporaneous speech on determining the best time to sell agricultural products and Justene placed fourth for her recitation of the FFA Creed.  Due to Kelsey's first place achievement at the district level, she advanced to the state competition last weekend where she placed first out of 10 contestants in the extemporaneous category.  She was given a topic and 20 minutes to prepare before her speech.  She placed first and will move on to the national contest in October at the National FFA Convention. 

 

The FFA will also give the Board an end-of-the-year update on their program.
