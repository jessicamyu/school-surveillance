Subject
C. Recognition of D428's Business and Finance Department for earning the Pathway to the Meritorious Budget Award (MBA) for the 2020–2021 Budget Year
Meeting
Apr 20, 2021 - DeKalb CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Association of School Business Officials International (ASBO) is proud to recognize DeKalb Community Unit School District 428 for excellence in budget presentation with the Pathway to the Meritorious Budget Award (MBA) for the 2020–2021 budget year. Each year, the process expands to include additional information so that is why it is called the "pathway".  This is the first year we participated in this program and submitted our report for consideration.

 

ASBO International’s MBA and Pathway to the MBA promote and recognize best budget presentation practices in school districts. Participants submit their applications and budget documents to a panel of school financial professionals who review the materials for compliance with the MBA Criteria Checklist and other requirements and provide expert feedback that districts can use to improve their budget documents.

 

Armir Doka, Business and Finance Manager, did most of the legwork preparing the document on behalf of D428 and he did a great job! Also, thanks to all in the business office - the district would not be able to continue reaching these achievements without your efforts - it takes a team!
