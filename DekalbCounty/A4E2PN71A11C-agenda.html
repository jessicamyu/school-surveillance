5. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition of DeKalb High School Chess Team
Meeting
Dec 1, 2015 - CUSD #428 Workshop School Board Meeting
Category
5. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The DeKalb High School Chess Team went to their first tournament on November 17 at Freeport High School in Freeport, Illinois. There were eight players on a team who played eight players from an opposing team.  There were 65 possible points in a round. DeKalb played two rounds and scored 40 to 25 the first round, and 60 to 5 the second round. This gave DeKalb the highest total points for that match.  The Board of Education recognizes and congratulates Paul Cappaert, Sam Clay, Bruce Henry, Chris Sheehan-Jago, Daniel Alcaraz, Chris Cooper, Libby Mildner, Robbie Hanson and Micah Keller on their team's success.
