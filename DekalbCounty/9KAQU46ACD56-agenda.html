The Board of Education will recognize the CRMS Track & Field Team for the following accomplishments:

 

Eight Clinton Rosette Middle School athletes competed in the IESA track and field state meet on May 23rd and May 24th in East Peoria.  The 8th grade 4 x 100 meter relay finished 5th in state, and 7th grader Christina Ryzhov finished 9th in the 1600 meter run.  Members of the 4 x 100 relay team are K’Maris Kirkwood, Caitlin Ross, Ashley Allen, Kristina Robinson, and Brimonet Buggs.  Other students participating in the state track meet were Ashley Nelson in shot put and Jaylen Hobsen in high jump.
