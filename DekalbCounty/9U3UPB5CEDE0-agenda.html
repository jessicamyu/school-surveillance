4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition - Amanda Baum, Excellence in Education Award Winner
Meeting
Mar 17, 2015 - CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Board of Education will recognize Amanda Baum, a 7th grade teacher at Clinton Rosette Middle School, for receiving a 2015 Excellence in Education award from the DeKalb County Community Foundation. Amanda exemplifies DeKalb County's best teachers and the Board is pleased to celebrate her leadership, hard work and dedication to the students and staff of DeKalb School District.  Amanda has the respect and admiration of students, parents, and coworkers and inspires a love of learning in students of all backgrounds and abilities.
