That the Board of Education accepts with gratitude the donation of approximately $2000.00 in items and check donations from Enchanted Backpack’s Teacher Appreciation Program, for the Littlejohn Staff.

Date: Mon, Aug 1, 2022 at 10:42 AM Subject: Littlejohn Elementary | Teacher Appreciation Program To: Rick Davis Hi Rick- I’m happy to share that Littlejohn Elementary has been chosen to participate in Enchanted Backpack’s Teacher Appreciation Program for the upcoming school year! There will be two pick-ups of gifts and checks scheduled, one in August and then one in either December or January, depending on our warehouse schedule. You will receive a list of items that are being donated at each pick-up and ideas on how to distribute, however, the meaning behind the program is to really make it work for your school! The first pick-ups will be ready the week of August 8th… is there a date/time that week that you (or another representative from the school) could pick-up your donation from our warehouse at 1040 Entry Drive? We are staffed M-F from 8am-4pm. Please let me know as pick-ups are by appointment. Thank you! And more detail to come soon.
