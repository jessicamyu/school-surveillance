Category

Type
Action
Recommended Action
"that the Board of Education accept with gratitude the donation of $1509.51 in materials plus labor from the Lowe's Hero Project for the installation of doors in the classroom display windows at Lincoln Elementary School."

Lincoln Elementary reached out to Lowe's to look for a way to provide a safe environment in the classroom by reducing the view through the display windows. Lowe's installed doors on the display windows that allow teachers to still access the space. Lowe's employees designed and constructed the doors, painted and installed them.
