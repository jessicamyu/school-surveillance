C. Recognition - DeKalb County Health Department
Meeting
Nov 19, 2013 - DeKalb CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
5. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Board of Education will recognize our local health department for their help with the enormous task of immunizing our 6-12 grade students with the Tdap vaccine.  It was only with our partnership with them that we were able to complete this task with as minimal exclusions as possible.  They offered extra clinics, held onsite clinics at our schools, and even took walk-ins on the 15th and 16th. The walk-ins was a first for them and was so badly needed and appreciated by us and I am sure our parents and students.
