4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition - DeKalb High School Forensics Team
Meeting
Mar 15, 2016 - CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition
The DHS Forensics team had another successful season in 2015-16, with over 60 students competing at 15 different tournaments, and winning 188 individual medals in the process. The team won the Northern Illinois Big 12 Conference Championship, as well as their 15th consecutive Regional crown. They advanced 6 students in 7 events to the IHSA State Finals, and Senior Ariel Williams placed 2nd in the state in Oratorical Declamation. 
 
Members of the Regional Team:
Sophomores: Matt Angel, Summer McFalls, & Gabbie Melms
Juniors: Hannah Clark, Molly Stawinoga, & Paul Un
Seniors: Heather Bemis, Willis Brei, Mike Leopardo, Ariel Williams, & Andrew Yohanan
