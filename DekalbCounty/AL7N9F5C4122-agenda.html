Subject
B. Recognition of the DeKalb Middle School Wrestling Team
Meeting
Apr 18, 2017 - CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
6. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
The DeKalb middle school wrestling team started the year with extremely high expectations. Their goal was to go undefeated in duals, place in the top 3 in every tournament they entered, and to place in the top 4 in the state. They finished the season 23-0, won every tournament, won regionals and sectionals, qualified a record 15 wrestlers to the state tournament, and won the team state title by 32 points. They boasted seven total placers, with three of them being state champs: Ethan Schultz, 5th; Luke Schmerbach, 4th; Caleb Nadig, 6th; Michael Clayton, 4th; Ben Aranda, 1st; Blake McGee, 1st; and Kyler Klapprodt, 1st.
