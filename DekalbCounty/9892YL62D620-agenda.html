4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition of Jasmine Brown
Meeting
Jun 18, 2013 - DeKalb CUSD 428 Board of Education Regular Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Board of Education will recognize DHS senior, Jasmine Brown, for winning the triple jump in the 3A division with a jump of 39'11.75", just missing her goal of jumping 40 feet.  She is a very positive, hard-working young lady and has definitely left her mark on the girls track and field program as well as DeKalb High School.  She also placed third in the long jump down state.  Last weekend Jasmine competed at Nationals where she jumped 40'6" and placed 3rd, earning All-American honors.  She earned a full scholarship to Western Illinois University which unfortunately is why she could not attend the school board meeting- she had orientation today.

The Board of Education will recognize the Cortland Elementary Physical Education Program for receiving the 2013 Blue Ribbon Award for outstanding  physical education programs by the Illinois Association of Health, Physical Education, Recreation and Dance (IAHPERD).  Physical education teachers are Bill McGuire, Tim Strohecker and Shay Sticka.  Maureen Fournier from IAHPERD will attend to share more information about the award.
