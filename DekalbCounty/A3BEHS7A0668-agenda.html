4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. National Academy of Future Scientist and Technologist Leaders and National Academy of Future Physician and Medical Scientist Leaders Awards of Excellence / Tamra Ropeter
Meeting
Oct 20, 2015 - CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition
The Congress of Future Science and Technology Leaders and Congress of Future Medical Leaders:
 
In June, three students, Peyton Billips, Andrew Bocklund and Joseph Sauser represented DeKalb High School and the State of Illinois at the Congress of Future Science and Technology Leaders and Congress of Future Medical Leaders in Boston Massachusetts. Our students were surrounded by many fellow high school students who share the same passion, ability and potential.They were personally taught by some of the greatest living minds in science and technology-winners of world's most prestigious international prizes, such as:
  • David Wineland, Ph.D., Winner of the 2012 Nobel Prize in physics and the 2007 National medal of science; 
  • John Mather, Ph.D., Winner of 2006 Nobel Prize in physics;
  • Cherry Murray, Ph.D., Dean of Harvard University's school of the engineering and applied sciences and recipient of the National Medal of Technology and Innovation
  • Dean Kamen, Recipient of the National Medal of Technology and Innovation and legendary inventor of the segway and the first drug infusion pumo;
  • Dennis Crowley, CEO and Co-Founder of Foursquare; and
  • Bill Westenhofer, two time Academy Award winner for best visual effects for Chronicles of Narnia and Life of Pi and visual effects supervisor for the World of Warcraft movie. 
These students aspire to become physicians, scientists or technologists (including biomedical scientists, software and hardware technologists, engineers and mathematicians).
