4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition of Roger Morgan/Owner of Roger's Music
Meeting
Nov 13, 2012 - November 13, 2012
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Board of Education will recognize Roger Morgan, owner of Roger's Music, for his many years of support to DeKalb School District.  Roger is incredibly kind with his time & goes above & beyond to help our music students.  On many instances, Roger has repaired instruments for students at no charge, offered very reasonable prices, taken extra time to explain to students how to best care for their instruments and worked closely with our district to keep costs for our music program at a minimum.  Many students, who would not have otherwise had the opportunity to play an instrument, have participated due to the unselfish giving of Mr. Morgan.

File Attachments
Subject
B. Recognition of Huntley Middle School Football Teams
Meeting
Nov 13, 2012 - November 13, 2012
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Board of Education will recognize the 7th and 8th grade football teams for making Huntley Middle School history by being the first HMS football team to boast both a 7th and a 8th grade conference championship. The 7th grade beat CRMS 44-12 and finished with a 7-0 record. The 8th grade blanked Rosette 18-0 and finished the first back-to-back championship in school history. The 8th grade has completed their middle school football career by going 15-0.  The teams are coached by Mr. Patrick Kiley.

Recognition

The Board of Education will recognize Brooke Condon, Assistant Principal at Clinton Rosette, for her behind the scenes work this past year in the establishment of a Community Network for Professionals Supporting LGBT (lesbian, gay, bi-sexual, transgender) Youth in DeKalb. Brooke’s efforts led to the launching of the first two Gay/Straight Alliance groups in middle schools in the State of Illinois.  Brooke was also recognized by Youth Outlook for her work in this area.
