B. Recognition/Greg Solomon
Meeting
Sep 3, 2013 - DeKalb CUSD 428 Workshop Board Meeting
Category
3. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Board of Education will recognize Greg Solomon, DHS teacher, for being named the 2012-13 Outstanding Speech, Debate and Theatre Educator of the Year in Illinois. 

 

In 1990, Solomon began student teaching at DeKalb  High School as well as being the school’s speech coach, a position he still holds today.  The program has grown from eight members when he first started to consistently having more than 60 now.  He also served as DeKalb’s theatre director from 1997 to 2010.

 

Greg has led DeKalb to 18 regional titles (12 consecutive), nine sectional (eight consecutive) and more than 30 state finalists with three winning the state championship.

 

Mr. Solomon will receive his award at the 2013/2014 IHSA state speech finals.
