A. Recognition of the DeKalb Middle School Wrestling Team
Meeting
Mar 19, 2019 - CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The DeKalb Middle School Wrestling Team finished their season this past weekend. They brought 14 wrestlers to the state tournament, which was the most in the AA state this year. The grapplers ended the day on Saturday with 8 total placers, 2 in the finals, and a 4th place finish in the state. This finishes a run for the 8th graders that had them placing in state all 3 years!

Subject
B. Recognition of Clinton Rosette and DeKalb High School Choir Students
Meeting
Mar 19, 2019 - CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition
 

Four Clinton Rosette Middle School and eight DeKalb High School choir students were selected to participate in the 2019 American Choral Directors Association 60th Anniversary Jubilee National Conference in Kansas City, Missouri February 27-March 3.  Over 4,200 students from seven geographic regions/divisions had auditioned this year for this honor. Our students were selected through their individual auditions they submitted this past September.
