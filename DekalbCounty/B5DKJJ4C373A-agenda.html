Recommended Action
"that the Board of Education accept with gratitude the donation 44 Samsung Galaxy Tab 4 tablets with a value of $1804.00 to the Technology Department from American Marketing and Publishing (AMP) of DeKalb. The tablets will be used to support Google Expeditions and to control Sphero STEM devices in the District; the donation of 232 Chromebooks with a value of $8,816.00 to the Technology Department from Central School District 301 in Burlington that will be used to support the Curriculum and Technology Initiative and device loaner pools in the buildings; and the donation of $4,989.92 awarded to Clinton Rosette Middle School from the Toshiba America Foundation to purchase STEM resources that include a Classroom Bundle of 12 Vex Robotics Kits and a 3D printer. These resources will be implemented in the STEM Class for grades 6-8 and the 3D printer will be available to all students and staff in the building."
