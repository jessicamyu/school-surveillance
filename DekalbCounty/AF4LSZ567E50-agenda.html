4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition - Clinton Rosette 6th Grade Student, Riley Newport
Meeting
Nov 1, 2016 - CUSD #428 Workshop School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type

The Board of Education would like to congratulate Clinton Rosette 6th grader Riley Newport for finishing 7th in the IESA Sectional cross country meet at Winnebago on 10/8/16, and qualifying for the IESA State cross country meet at Normal Parkside Jr. High on 10/15. There, Riley ran his season-best time of 11:12 for the two-mile race and just missed earning a top-25 medal by finishing 27th out of 240 runners. This was an especially strong performance, considering he was also competing against 7th and 8th graders in those meets.
