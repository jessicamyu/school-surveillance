4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition - Jacob McArtor, National Merit Scholarship Finalist
Meeting
Mar 3, 2015 - CUSD #428 Workshop School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Board of Education will recognize senior, Jacob McArtor, for being named as a finalist in the 2015 National Merit Scholarship Competition.  The National Merit® Scholarship Program is an academic competition for recognition and scholarships that began in 1955. High school students enter the National Merit Program by taking the Preliminary SAT/National Merit Scholarship Qualifying Test and by meeting published program entry/participation requirements.  They are among 8,300 of the brightest students in the United States.
