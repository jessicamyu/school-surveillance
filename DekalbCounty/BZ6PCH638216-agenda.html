F. Approval of Utilities RFP and Auction Parameters
Meeting
Apr 6, 2021 - DeKalb CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
5. CONSENT AGENDA
Type
Action (Consent)
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Board of Education approve:
(1) Contract with Direct Energy on a 36-month rate not to exceed $.056/kWh (adding 2% to the rate to allow for market movement in between Board Agenda posting and actual Board meeting). We also request approval to move forward with the auction process for power with the provided price thresholds if we are unable to secure this rate with Direct Energy, and (2) a live auction for the procurement of natural gas and electricity. District Administration grants authorization to award contracts no more than 48-months in length and subject to rates received no more than 2% above the request for proposals received on 3/31/2021. Final determination of product and term to be made following auction results.
