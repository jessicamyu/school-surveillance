B. Recognition - Student Services Department
Meeting
May 21, 2019 - CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition
The Board of Education will recognize the Student Service Department for receiving an overall Illinois State Board of Education (ISBE) determination of Meets Requirements for the 2017-18 school year.  This designation is given to districts that have implemented all the requirements of I.D.E.A. with integrity.  ISBE reviews six indicators of performance, completes an IDEA grant audit and reviews any compliance corrections in determining our designation.  
 
Congratulations!
