6. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition - Huntley Middle School Orchestra
Meeting
May 17, 2016 - CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
6. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Huntley Middle School Chamber Orchestra qualified to compete at the Illinois Grade School Music Association State Competition on April 23 and received a Division I rating, which is the highest they could have achieved.  They competed with 16 other schools at Jackson Middle School in Villa Park.  They qualified for the state competition by receiving a Division I rating at the Illinois Grade School Music Association District 9 Organizational Contest held at Huntley Middle School in March.  They received a Gold Plaque (Superior rating -- Division I) and each student received a medal.  The Huntley Middle School Chamber Orchestra is directed by Ashley Miceli.

File Attachments
Subject
B. Recognition - Clinton Rosette Orchestra
Meeting
May 17, 2016 - CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
6. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

For the first time ever, Clinton Rosette Middle School had 2 orchestras qualify for  the Illinois Grade School Music Association State Organizational Competition. They proudly came home on April 23 from Jackson Middle School in Villa Park with a Gold Plaque (Superior rating -- Division I) for 6th Grade Orchestra and a Silver Plaque (Excellent rating -- Division II) for 8th Grade Orchestra.  They were invited to participate at the Illinois Grade School Music Association state competition with 16 other outstanding Middle School Orchestras because they received Division I ratings at the Illinois Grade School Music Association District 9 Organizational Contest held at Huntley Middle School in March. Each student also received a medal with a ribbon for their accomplishments. The Clinton Rosette Middle School Orchestras are directed by Sheila Felder.
