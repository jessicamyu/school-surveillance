5. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition of CRMS Students Anan Ghrayeb, Bryce Comer and Stephanie Vazquez
Meeting
May 21, 2013 - May 21, 2013
Category
5. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Board of Education will recognize 7th grade students Anan Ghrayeb, Bryce Comer and Stephanie Vazquez for winning 1st place in the Ecybermission National Science Competition this year for the State of Illinois.  This competition challenges students across the country to make their communities better places.  This group designed “pocket wind energy generators.”  They designed and tested different fan blades to see what size blade would be most efficient at generating electricity.  Their hope was that one day you could recharge small things, like cell phones, with the energy from the wind.  Each of these students received a $1,000 savings bond for their efforts!  The groups' sponsor is Kelli Hamilton. Congratulations!
