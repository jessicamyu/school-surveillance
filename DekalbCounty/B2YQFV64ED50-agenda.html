4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition - School Resource Officer Jose Jaques
Meeting
Aug 7, 2018 - CUSD #428 Workshop School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Board of Education will recognize and congratulate School Resource Officer Jose Jaques on his retirement and for his service to DeKalb Community School District 428. 

Officer Jaques has served as a school resource officer for District 428 for 12 years, and received his certification through the National Association of School Resource Officers in 2006.  He is also a certified A.L.I.C.E. instructor.  In addition to his role as a school resource officer, he has been a police officer for 22 years and also serves as a Lieutenant Colonel in the Illinois National Guard.
