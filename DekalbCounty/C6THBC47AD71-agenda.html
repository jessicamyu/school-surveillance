C. Strategic Plan Update
Meeting
Sep 21, 2021 - DeKalb CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
6. SUPERINTENDENTS REPORT/PRESENTATION
Type
  • Principal Evaluation Alignment - school improvement plan
    • Assistant Principal Evaluation Alignment to Principal Evaluation
  • Cabinet Evaluation Alignment - Strategic Plan, priorities/milestones
  • Updating the Parent/Student Handbook pending
  • Transportation
  • Pending Training on use of Technology, Ethics (district-wide)

 

All departmental strategies within the four strategic priorities remain unchanged. Any changes will be a result of feedback provided by community stakeholders at the next town hall.

Currently, departments are implementing the action items under their theory of actions. At the first board meeting in October, the Board will receive an update on action items in the form of a high-level presentation of action items that fall within the following categories: 1) not started, 2) off track, 3) on track, 4) completed. Developing and aligning KPIs is the next phase.

Moving forward, on a monthly basis (if data is available), the Board will get information on two items regarding the strategic plan: 1) action item updates (implementation monitoring) and 2) KPIs as they become available (due to data collection cycles and type of KPI - e.g. NWEA reading is BOY, MOY, EOY; attendance every 5 weeks; graduation rates annual).
