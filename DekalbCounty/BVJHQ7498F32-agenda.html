7. SUPERINTENDENT'S REPORT / PRESENTATIONS
Subject
A. Closed POD Agreement / Tammy Carson, Kyle Gerdes and Crity Meyer (A)
Meeting
Dec 15, 2020 - DeKalb CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
7. SUPERINTENDENT'S REPORT / PRESENTATIONS
Type
Action
Recommended Action
It is recommended that the Board of Education approve the Memorandum of Understanding with the DeKalb County Health Department to allow the District to serve as a closed point of dispensing in case of a large scale incident or condition.

The District is willing and ready to host a Closed POD for the dispensing of medications per the MOU with the DeKalb County Health Department.  Our Registered Nurses are aware of the Agreement and are willing to participate.  In fact, one of the nurses said she was glad to serve in this way!  Tammy Carson is working with DCHD to arrange the facility and logistical needs regarding the COVID19 vaccine plan and Student Services will arrange for the training and provide the vaccine count needed.  We are glad for this partnership because it allows for our staff and their families to get the vaccine in an organized and timely fashion.
