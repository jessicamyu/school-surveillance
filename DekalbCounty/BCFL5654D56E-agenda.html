Subject
B. Recognition: Riley Newport, Clinton Rosette Middle School 8th Grade Student
Meeting
Jun 4, 2019 - CUSD #428 Workshop School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition
DeKalb Clinton Rosette 8th grader Riley Newport won the boys 1600-meter run in a time of 4:42.33 at the IESA state finals in East Peoria on Friday, May 17. He then followed that up on Saturday by winning the 800-meter run, posting a time of 2:04.57.  Newport also won the state cross-country championship in October, completing the distance runner's trifecta for the 2018-19 school year. 
 
Newport will run for DeKalb High School in the upcoming school year.
