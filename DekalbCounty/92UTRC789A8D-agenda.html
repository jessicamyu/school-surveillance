4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition of DHS Marching Band
Meeting
Jan 22, 2013 - January 22, 2013
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Board of Education will recognize the DHS marching band for receiving the following accolades at the Sugar Bowl Marching Band Competition in New Orleans:   Outstanding Music, Outstanding Marching, Outstanding Color Guard, Outstanding Overall Effect, First place in class AA and Sugar Bowl Participant.  By virtue of receiving the top score of any band in all classes, the band was also named Grand Champion and was selected to participate in the opening ceremony, carrying the US flag, in the Sugar Bowl game on January 2, 2013.  The band is directed by Mr. Steve Lundin, music teacher at DHS.
