C. Approval of Bid Results - Tyler Renovations/Tammy Carson (A)
Meeting
Feb 4, 2014 - DeKalb CUSD 428 Workshop Board Meeting
Category
9. BUSINESS AND FINANCE
Type
Action
Recommended Action
Administration is recommending that the Board of Education award the bid for renovating Tyler Elementary School to Nicholas & Associates in the amount of $2,867,290 which includes the Base Bid along with Alternates #1B and #2 as well as approve administration to commit to Alternate Bid #4 and #5 contingent upon the moisture barrier results to be obtained within 60 days in the amount of $133,000. Administration is recommending rejecting Alternate Bid #3.
Administration is also recommending that the Board of Education award the bid for Asbestos Abatement for the flooring and windows for the Tyler Elementary School to Midwest Service Group in the amount of $47,525.
In addition to the bid amount, it is also recommended that the Board of Education approve an additional $390,038 for contingency dollars and architect/engineering fees.
