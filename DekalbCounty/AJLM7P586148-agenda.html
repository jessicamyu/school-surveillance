4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Acceptance of Gifts and Donations
Meeting
Feb 21, 2017 - CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Action
Recommended Action
"that the Board of Education accept with gratitude the generous donation of equipment from Mr. Gary McConeghy for the Performing Arts Center at DeKalb High School; the donation of health room supplies for Cortland Elementary School purchased by the manager and employees of Walgreens who were working on February 3, 2017; and the monetary donation from Resource Bank for the health room at Cortland Elementary which will be used for the purchase of water bottles."
Cortland Elementary would like to extend our sincere thanks to DeKalb Walgreens, especially their manager Doug and the employees working on Friday, February 3.  They all pooled their own money together to donate needed supplies for our health room at Cortland!  They bought our health room a supply of much-needed Chapstick.  We will be providing those students in need with their own individually marked Chapstick to keep in their desk at school! 
 
Cortland Elementary would like to thank Resource Bank for their generous monetary donation to the health room.  We will be using their donation to purchase water bottles!  We will be able to give students their own water bottles when they present to our office with bad coughs/colds, etc.  By teaching them to increase their fluids, we are hoping that they will get over their illnesses quicker!  Again, thanks to Resource Bank for supporting our students!
