4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition of DeKalb/Sycamore Co-op Swim Team
Meeting
Mar 18, 2014 - CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The boys swimming team sent the following 5 boys to the IHSA State Championships:  Jacob Bjork (100 Breast, 400 Free Relay); Daniel Hein (200 Medley Relay, 100 Fly, 100 Back, 400 Free Relay); Holden Mackey (200 Medley Relay, 400 Free Relay); Dylan Powers (200 Medley Relay, 100 Fly, 400 Free Relay); and Ryan Schultz (200 Medley Relay, 100 Breast).  The number of entries to the IHSA State Championships marked the most in program history!

 

The boys combined to place 12th as a team overall (a sport in which state is not separated by class).  This was the highest finish in program history!

 

In the 100 Backstroke, Daniel Hein placed 3rd and became the first in program history to achieve a Top-3 finish at State.  He also placed 7th in the 100 Butterfly.

 

Additionally, the 200 Medley Relay (comprised of Daniel Hein, Ryan Schultz, Dylan Powers, and Holden Mackey) became the first relay in program history to qualify for finals at the IHSA State Championships.  They placed 7th overall.

 

In the process, varsity records were achieved in the following events:  200 Medley Relay (Daniel Hein, Ryan Schultz, Dylan Powers, Holden Mackey); 100 Back (Daniel Hein); 100 Free (Dylan Powers); and 400 Free Relay (Dylan Powers, Holden Mackey, Jacob Bjork, Daniel Hein).
