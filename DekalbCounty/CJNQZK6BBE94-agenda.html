Action, Procedural
Recommended Action
That the Board of Education accepts with gratitude the donation of $$632.20 from NIU Athletics, the College Football Playoff Foundation, and Donors Choose, for Ms. Wind's (a teacher at DHS) project, Be Comfortable, Be Cozy, Be Engaged!
That the Board of Education accepts with gratitude the donation of a water filling station in the amount of $4,700 from Delta Dental of Illinois Foundation and its partnership with Illinois Children’s Healthcare Foundation on the H20 On the Go program, that was awarded to Lincoln Elementary.
We are excited to share that through the Delta Dental of Illinois Foundation, our 501(c)3 charitable arm, and its partnership with Illinois Children’s Healthcare Foundation on the H20 On the Go program, your school has been selected for the H20 On the Go water bottle-filling station program! As you know, through H2O on the Go, we install new water bottle-filling stations at selected Illinois elementary, middle and high schools. H20 on the Go makes it easier for students to access drinking water at school and choose water over sugary beverages like soda, juice and sports drinks. This program is part of Delta Dental of Illinois Foundation’s focus on improving the overall health and wellness of children and teenagers statewide, as drinking fluoridated water helps keep teeth strong and can also help reduce obesity, increase energy levels and improve students’ attention span and ability to learn.
