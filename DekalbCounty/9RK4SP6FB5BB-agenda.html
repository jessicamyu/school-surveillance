4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition - Huntley Middle School Basketball Team
Meeting
Jan 20, 2015 - CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition
The Board of Education will recognize the 8th grade HMS boys basketball team for completing their 2014 season with a perfect 16-0 record.  Some of the highlights to the season:
 
*Defeated Sycamore three times
*Defeated Kaneland in a non conference game
*Defeated defending champion Burlington Central three times, one of which was the championship game on their home court after trailing 15-0 after the 1st Quarter.
 
*Team Members: Coaches Jim Kitchen & Jon Colvin  Players: Blake Williams, Chris Grays, Cristian Ibarra, David Hammarberg, Jalen Motsinger Thomas, Jarrett Sykes, Jaquon Brown, Jordan Gandy, Nigel Dyson, Brandon Pizano, Brayden MacDonald, Caleb Ihm, Connor Tierney, Dejon Ray, Devyn Peppers, James Balli, Jordan Garcia, Quinn Rempfer and Tyler Burton.
