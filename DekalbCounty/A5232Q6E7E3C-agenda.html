3. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition - DHS Fall Teams
Meeting
Dec 15, 2015 - CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
3. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

Boys Golf

The boys golf team were Northern Illinois Big 12 East Division Conference Champs and Northern Illinois Big 12 Tournament Champs.  The team also advanced to the IHSA Sectional after finishing in 2nd place at the Regional.  Ben Melms and Connor Hoyle earned All-Conference Honors at the conference tournament. 

 

Austin Freeman finished in 3rd place at the IHSA Regional and finished in the top 10 individuals at the sectional to qualify for the IHSA State Tournament.   At the state tournament he finished in 13th place.  He also earned all-conference honors at the conference tournament. 

 

Girls Tennis

Mary Ellen Green earned all-conference honors after finishing 3rd at the NIB 12 Tournament.  Mary qualified for the IHSA State Tournament with a 2nd place tournament finish at the IHSA Sectional. 

 

Football

The football team won the Northern Illinois Big 12 East Division.  They finished the regular season with a 7-2 record.  The football team’s playoff run of the 3rd round (Quarterfinals) was the longest since 1980.  Cole Tucker, Derek Kyler, Hayden LaPointe, Willis Brei, Dwayne Lacey, Leif Williams, David Long, Tony Tate, Taylor Geiseman, and Jake Neidel were All-Conference NIB 12.  Derek Kyler and Cole Tucker received All-State Honorable Mention Honors.

 

Girls Swimming

Jensen Keck, Bailey Flemming, Kylie Olson and Sarah Hein qualified for the State Tournament in the 4x200 medley relay. 

Bailey Flemming earned NIB 12 All-Conference Honors in the 200 medley relay.

Jensen Keck qualified for the state tournament in the 200 Individual medley and the 100 breaststroke.  Jensen earned NIB 12 All-Conference recognition in the 100 breaststroke and 200 medley relay. 

Kylie Olson qualified for the state tournament in the 100 backstroke.  She earned NIB 12 All-Conference honors in the 100 backstroke and 200 medley relay.

Sarah Hein qualified for the state tournament in the 100 butterfly.  She also earned NIB 12 All-Conference honors in the 100 backstroke, 100 fly and 200 medley relay.
