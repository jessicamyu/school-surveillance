4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Subject
A. Recognition - DeKalb High School Student Izi Myers-Miller, Semifinalist in the 65th National Merit Scholarship Competition
Meeting
Oct 1, 2019 - CUSD #428 Workshop School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Board of Education will recognize DeKalb High School’s Izi Myers-Miller who has been named a semifinalist in the 65th National Merit Scholarship Competition. She is one of approximately 16,000 semifinalists nationwide. Approximately 1.5 million juniors entered the 2020 National Merit Scholarship Program by taking the 2018 Preliminary SAT/National Merit Scholarship Qualifying Test (PSAT/NMSQT). The nationwide pool of semifinalists, representing less than one percent of U.S. high school seniors, includes the highest-scoring entrants in each state. The number of semifinalists in a state is proportional to the state’s percentage of the national total of graduating seniors.

The Board will hold a public hearing for the purpose of considering whether to submit a waiver of School Code (Section 5/27-24.2) from the Illinois State Board of Education to allow the district to charge a reasonable fee, not to exceed $300, for students participating in Driver Education behind-the-wheel training.
