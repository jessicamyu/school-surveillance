Subject
A. Recognition - Huntley Middle School 8th Grade Concert Band
Meeting
Apr 7, 2015 - CUSD #428 Workshop School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Board of Education will recognize the Huntley Middle School 8th Grade Concert Band for earning a Division 1 (highest ranking) at the District IX IGSMA Organizational Contest, which we hosted here at Huntley.  This is the second year in a row they have earned a Division 1 ranking at this contest.  They will advance to state on Saturday, April 25th to compete at Troy Middle School.  The band is directed by Sean Potts.

Subject
B. Recognition - DHS Winter Teams
Meeting
Apr 7, 2015 - CUSD #428 Workshop School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition
The Board of Education will recognize the following winter teams for their accomplishments:
 
Swimming - Boys:  The 200 yard medley took 5th place.  Daniel Hein finished runner-up in the 100 yard butterfly and 100 yard backstroke.  Jacob Bjork finished in 9th place in the 100 yard breaststroke and Dylan Powers took 7th in the 100 yard Butterfly.  The 400 yard freestyle relay team (Daniel Hein, Jacob Bjork, Dylan Powers and Michael Carlson) also qualified for the state meet.  The team finished in 7th place overall.
 
Wrestling:  The team had Jackson Montgomery win conference in the 132 lbs class and Jackson and Brad Green qualified for the state meet.
 
Basketball - Boys:  The team won the Northern Illinois Big 12 Conference East Division.  Luke Davis was named unanimous East MVP honors while Jace Kitchen, Michael Pollack, and Luke were named unanimously to the East All-Conference Team.  All three student-athletes were named 1st Team All-Area.  Luke was named 4th Team All-State.
 
Dance:  The team qualified for the state meet for a 2nd year in a row and finished 28th place overall.
 

Gymnastics:  The team for qualified for the state meet and Aleah Leman earned state championship in the vault with an average score of 9.825.  Aleah also finished runner-up in the floor exercise event.  Alison Morreale, Aleah Leman, Emily Jovanavich and Katlyn Nelson were selected All Conference and Alison Morreale was selected for Nationals.  The team finished in 7th place overall.
