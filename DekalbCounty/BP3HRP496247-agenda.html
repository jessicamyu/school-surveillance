9. NEW BUSINESS/ADMINISTRATION
Subject
A. Class of 2020 Graduation Requirements Modification/Mr. Jamie Craven (A)
Meeting
May 5, 2020 - DeKalb CUSD #428 Workshop School Board Meeting
Category
9. NEW BUSINESS/ADMINISTRATION
Type
Action, Discussion
Recommended Action
"that the Board of Education suspend for the Class of 2020 any Board policies to the extent such policies impose graduation requirements beyond the minimum requirements established by the Illinois School Code as modified by the Governors Executive Order 2020-31 and the State Board of Education Emergency Rules implementing Executive Order 2020-31."

The Illinois State Board of Education has relaxed/waived all state requirements for senior students due to the stay-home E-Learning semester. An Example: ISBE requires four years of English Language Arts. Their updated guidance reduces that requirement to 3.5 years. ISBE is encouraging local boards to do the same with any local graduation requirement. The only requirement that this would impact at DeKalb High School is the .5 Tech requirement. The Speech requirement is a part of the 4-year English Language Arts requirement which ISBE reduced.

"that the Board of Education adjourn sine die."
