A. Recognition - Founders and Malta Elementary Physical Education Programs
Meeting
Oct 7, 2014 - CUSD #428 Workshop School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Board of Education will recognize the Founders and Malta Elementary Physical Education Programs for receiving the 2014 Blue Ribbon Award for outstanding  physical education programs by the Illinois Association of Health, Physical Education, Recreation and Dance (IAHPERD).  Physical education teachers at Malta are Lisa Boyle and Brandon Daub and at Founders are Steve Bell, Brandon Daub Shay Sticka and Kelly Zerby.  Maureen Fournier from IAHPERD will attend to share more information about the award.
