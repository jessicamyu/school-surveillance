Dec 3, 2013 - DeKalb CUSD 428 Workshop Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

The Board of Education will recognize First Student for their wonderful donation of 56 Halloween costumes to DeKalb School District homeless youth. Drivers and other staff came up with the idea to help our students who would not normally have the funds to purchase costumes and celebrate Halloween.  Thanks to their kind donations, 56 students were able to participate in the holiday.
