Subject
D. Recognition - Head Cooks - Level 1 Certificate in School Nutrition/Ms. Carol Warren
Meeting
Apr 19, 2016 - CUSD #428 Regular School Board Meeting
Category
4. PUBLIC PARTICIPATION/RECOGNITION
Type
Recognition

In recognition of the School Nutrition Association Level 1 Certificate Program, the following Head cooks have achieved the Level 1 Certificate in School Nutrition. This certificate aligns with the USDA Professional Standards that focus on ensuring the success of the National School Lunch and Breakfast program.
 

The Professional Standards include:
  • Assisting School Districts in recruiting, hiring, training and retaining qualified school nutrition staff
  • Enhancing the image of school Nutrition professionals and their influence in our community
  • Building skills and empowering staff to lead and efficiently operate school nutrition programs

Congratulations to Gena, Brenda and Vicki for a job well done! Thank you!

 
Gena Groves-Huntley Middle School
Brenda Tangeman-Clinton Rosette Middle School
Vicki Stanton-Cortland Elementary School
