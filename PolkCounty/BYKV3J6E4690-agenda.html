DESCRIPTION:

 

As required by §447.403(4)(c)-(d), Florida Statutes (2020), the School Board will consider the Special Magistrate’s Report and specific recommendations to resolve the 2020-2021 salary issues at Impasse between the Polk County School District and the Polk Education Association (PERC Case SM-2020-19).

 

2020-2021 Impasse Issues:

  • Teacher Salaries under HB641 Teacher Salary Increase Allocation (TSIA)
    • Part 1: 80% of the total allocation shall be used to increase the minimum base salary for full-time classroom teachers as defined in §1012.01(2)(a) plus certified pre-kindergarten teachers funded in the FEFP, to $47,500 or to the maximum amount achievable based on the allocation and as specified in the General Appropriations Act. The Superintendent and the Polk Education Association agree that $45,172 is the maximum amount achievable for the new minimum base salary.
    • Part 2: 20% of the total TSIA shall be used to increase the salary of specified instructional personnel who are not eligible to receive an increase in Part 1 above, or who received an increase of less than 2% in Part 1 above. 
    • Substitute teachers are not eligible for this statutory requirement. 
  • Paraeducator Salaries
  • Educational Support (clerical) Personnel Salaries

 

See attached Exhibit 1 for the Superintendent's Executive Summary and related Exhibits 2 through 8.
