DESCRIPTION:

 

We currently use FOCUS for our Student Information System, this proposal is to add FOCUS SSS (Special Student Services) to the program, the data integration should be relatively simple.  This system, if adopted, will replace our current Enrich system (that we use for IEPs and 504 plans) and will also be available for us to use for the following services. 

 

Threat Assessments - estimated launch - October 2021

Mental Health Tracking - estimate launch - October 2021

ESOL/ELL Management - estimate launch - October/November 2021

MTSS management - estimate launch - January 2022

IEP/504/Gifted - estimate launch July 1, 2022

 

This schedule will allow us to scale up services, the biggest lift will be exporting IEP data from the current system to be loaded into the new system if adopted.

DESCRIPTION:

 

According to Section (s.) 1008.22(7)(c), Florida Statutes (F.S.), and State Board of Education Rule 6A-1.094224, Florida Administrative Code (F.A.C), each school district must complete the uniform calendar with district-required assessment information, publish the calendar to the district website, and provide it to the Department by October 1 of each school year, beginning in 2016–17. 

 

This calendar is subject to change with updates from the state throughout the year.
