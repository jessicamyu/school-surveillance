In accordance with Florida Charter Law, a charter may be renewed provided that a program review demonstrates that the criteria in paragraph (a) have been successfully accomplished and that none of the grounds for nonrenewal established by paragraph (8)(a) has been documented pursuant to Section1002.33 (7)(19)(c)(1), F.S.

  • The contractual agreement between Achievement Academy and the School Board of Polk County will expire on June 30, 2022
  • The contractual agreement between Berkley Accelerated Middle School and the School Board of Polk County will expire on June 30, 2022

 

OBJECTIVE:


To conduct a comprehensive programmatic and organizational review by the Charter Review Team, evaluating the success of a charter school program using the proposed charter renewal application, evaluation instrument, site visits, and other relevant data under the following subject criteria:

  1. Academic performance
  2. Financial performance
  3. Organizational performance

 

 

 

RECOMMENDATION:


To review the recommendation and final evaluation instrument submitted by the Charter Review Team.
