Presentation of the 2020-2021 Internal Funds Audit report by Carr, Riggs & Ingram CPAs. F.A.C. 6A-1.001 and the incorporated Financial and Program Cost Accounting and Reporting for Florida Schools (Red Book) requires that the School Board provides for an annual audit of Internal Funds. The signed, written report of the audit shall include notations of any failure to comply with Florida Statute, Florida Administrative Code or district School Board policy.  The report, which may also provide commentary as to financial management and irregularities, shall be presented to the School Board while in session and filed as a part of the public record.

3. Reproductive Health Curriculum Review and Approval as Required by 1003.42 f.s.
Meeting
Dec 14, 2021 - Work Session 12:30PM
Category
A. Superintendent's Report
Type
Information

 

DESCRIPTION:

 

 

 

Legislative updates/amendments to s. 1002.20, F.S. and s.1003.42, F.S. require the annual board approval of the instructional materials used to teach reproductive health or any disease including HIV/AIDS, its symptoms, development, and treatment.  In addition, a process is required for public review of, public comment on, and the adoption of instructional materials, including instructional materials used to teach reproductive health or any disease, including HIV/AIDS, under ss. 1003.42(3) and 1003.46, which that satisfies the requirements of s. 1006.283(2)(b)8., 9., and 11.

 

This information contains an Executive Summary and overview of curriculum, referenced Florida Statutes, and Reproductive Health lessons by grade level and topic, which are taught from 4th grade through high school (HOPE course).
