2. 12:50PM-1:10PM Nearpod, Teaching & Learning, $860,625.00
Meeting
Jan 25, 2022 - Work Session 12:30PM
Category
A. Superintendent's Report
Type
Discussion

 

DESCRIPTION:

 

 

Student performance data shows a drop in overall proficiency in English language arts, mathematics, science and social studies in the 2020-2021 school year (post pandemic) when compared to the pre-pandemic performance data from the 2018-2019 school year. As a result of student learning loss as evidence by pre and post pandemic student performance data, Polk County Public Schools will implement a variety of interventions to specifically address the academic impact of lost instructional time.  

 

Academic supports include a variety of expanded and new supports and activities including supplemental academic programs specific to English language arts, mathematics, science, and social studies such as

NearPod.  NearPod is a digital learning tool that has content specific Florida based standards, formative assessments, and increases student engagement. The platform works across all digital devices and has integration with Schoology. Nearpod has research based success in raising scores with all students including student with disabilities and English Language Learners.. This acquisition will also be used to support a one-to-one device initiative to ensure all students are able to access course content, programs, and supports both in and out of school.
