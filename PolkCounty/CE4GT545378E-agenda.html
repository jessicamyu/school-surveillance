The Junior League of Greater Lakeland is an organization of women committed to promoting voluntarism, developing the potential of women and improving the community through the effective action and leadership of trained volunteers. On the cusp of celebrating its 90th anniversary of working to strengthen the community through service, partnerships and financial support, league representatives will provide a brief presentation on the League’s history, key legacy contributions to Greater Lakeland and Polk County, and details surrounding its partnership and impact with PCPS. Afterward, they will accept feedback and answer questions from board members, Supt. Heid and/or any staff for how the League can continue to be a supportive partner to PCPS schools, teachers and students.

DESCRIPTION:

 

This project is funded by a COVID Relief CRRSA Grant, and will provide tutoring and extended learning opportunities for students in Grades K-3. The target student populations are the lowest 15% at each elementary based on Star Early Lit, Star Reading and/or the Comprehensive Screening and Progress Monitoring System.

 

Students attending a Lowest 300 Performing Elementary School will be  prioritized over higher achieving schools with fewer than 15% of students earning a 3-5 on the FSA ELA. Schools will be prioritize K-1 students when targeting students to participate.

DESCRIPTION:

 

 

The Educational Equity Act Annual Update is a reporting tool that enables the Department of Education’s Office of Equal Educational Opportunity (OEEO) to monitor and ensure that provisions of the laws are adhered to and that educational resources are equitably distributed is presented for review. The report contains the 2020-2021 reporting requirements for the Polk County School District on Black, Hispanic, Black Males, Hispanic Males and ELL student access and enrollment in advanced level courses including Advanced Placement, Dual Enrollment, and all Level III courses (grades 9-12), each analyzed separately by race/category. This report also contains athletic compliance monitoring forms and the District compliance verification form under Title IX, Employment Equity, Single-Gender Class information, and Teen Parent Program information.
