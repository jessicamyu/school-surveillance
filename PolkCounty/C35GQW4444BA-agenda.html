Motion to authorize the execution and delivery by the Chairman or Vice-Chairman of the Board, with the advice of PFM Financial Advisors LLC of a rate lock letter agreement with PNC Bank, National Association in connection with the refunding of the Refunding Certificates of Participation (School Board of Polk County, Florida, Master Lease Program), Series 2017A, Evidencing Fractional Undivided Interests of the Owners thereof in Basic Rent Payments to be made under a Master Lease Purchase Agreement by The School Board of Polk County, Florida in connection with the proposed issuance of the Refunding Certificates of Participation (School Board of Polk County, Florida, Master Lease Program), Series 2021A, Evidencing Fractional Undivided Interests of the Owners thereof in Basic Rent Payments to be made under a Master Lease-Purchase Agreement by The School Board of Polk County, Florida provided that the net present value savings of the refunding based on the locked interest rate under the rate lock letter agreement shall be at least $200,000, and the members of the Board, the Secretary, Special Counsel and Counsel to the Board and the officers, attorneys and other agents or employees of the District and the Board are hereby authorized to do all acts and things required of them by this motion, or desirable or consistent with the requirements of this motion

 

DESCRIPTION:

 

This Rate Lock agreement will lock the interest rate for the refunding of the COPS 2017A.  This agreement takes away the risk of rates rising further and locks the savings to the District sooner. Final documents and amount of savings as a result of the refunding will be brought to the Board on December 14, 2021 for approval.
