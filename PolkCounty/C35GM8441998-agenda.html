2. 12:40PM-12:45PM Middle School at Bella Citta
Meeting
Oct 26, 2021 - Work Session 12:30PM
Category
A. Discussion Items
Type
Discussion

 

DESCRIPTION:

 

 

There is a need for middle school capacity in the northeast area of the County.  

There is space on Bella Citta's campus to convert the school from a K-5 to a K-8.  

The existing K-5 school has a capacity of 996.  The additional 6 though 8 grades will have an estimated capacity of 465.

The cost of construction is projected at $20 million to be paid from impact fees.

There will be approximately 21 classrooms, a gymnasium and a dining room.

 

This will be the second K-8 school in the County with Citrus Ridge being the first.  

If approved, the middle school will be scheduled to open in August 2023. 

The estimated cost is $19 million from impact fees.

3. 12:45PM-12:50PM Termination of School Use at Alturas Elementary
Meeting
Oct 26, 2021 - Work Session 12:30PM
Category
A. Discussion Items
Type
Discussion

 

DESCRIPTION:

 

Alturas Elementary was originally built in 1947

Its current enrollment is 326

There is a lack of adequate infrastructure and service to the School

There is a new school approved by the School Board to be located  at the Northwest Corner of CR653 and Old Bartow - Lake Wales Road

The Plan is for the school to accommodate the students from Alturas Elementary, provide some relief to Chain of Lakes Elementary and accommodate growth.  

The existing Alturas site may be able to be used to assist a non-profit in the interim

When there is adequate service to the site, the plan is to build another school on it.

6. 1:30PM-1:45PM Sick Leave Policies for Administrative and Support Staff
Meeting
Oct 26, 2021 - Work Session 12:30PM
Category
A. Discussion Items
Type
Discussion

 

DESCRIPTION:

 

Discuss changes made to policies 1430.03 – Sick Leave (Administrative Staff) and 4430.03 – Sick Leave (Support Staff) February 23, 2021 which updated language regarding changes to termination payouts. Years of service and termination payout percentages align with statute. Payouts are capped at 50% of salary.

 

These policies are not in consistent with 3430.03 – Sick Leave (Instructional Staff) for which years of service and termination payout percentages align with statute.
