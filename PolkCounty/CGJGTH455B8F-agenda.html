Subject
1. Election of Chairman
Meeting
Nov 22, 2022 - Board Meeting - Reorganization Meeting-Election Year 9:00AM
Category
C. Reorganization of the Board
Type
Action
Recommended Action
Motion to close nominations and name as Board Chair.

 

DESCRIPTION:

 

 

Superintendent Heid will open the floor for nominations. After all nominations for Chairman are made, she will ask for a motion to close nominations. A roll call vote, by district, will follow beginning with the first candidate nominated. If there is a majority for the first nominee, the election of a Board Chair will be complete. If there is not a majority, there will be a roll call vote for the second nominee. The process will continue until a Board Chair is elected by a majority vote. The same process will be used in the election of the Vice-Chair
