Superintendent's Report

 

DESCRIPTION:

 

School Board Policy 8210 - SCHOOL CALENDAR states, "The Superintendent shall annually appoint a calendar committee to develop a school calendar for presentation to the School Board
for approval no later than March 1st each year..."

 

The Calendar Committee met December 13, 2021 and January 13, 2022 to discuss details of the new 2022-2023 tentative instructional calendar. The attached proposed tentative calendar has been substantially revised based on the Calendar Committee’s recommendation. The goal is to submit the final version for the Board’s approval on February 22, 2022 to meet the March 1st deadline.

 

The Superintendent's Calendar Committee proposes the attached "Tentative 2022-2023 Instructional Calendar for Students, Teachers, and Paraeducators."
