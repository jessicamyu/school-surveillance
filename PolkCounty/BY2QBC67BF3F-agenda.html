Per 1002.331 (1), Florida Statutes, a high-performing charter school is authorized to expand grade levels within kindergarten through grade 12 to add grade levels not already served, not to exceed the School's enrollment capacity.

 

The Governing Board of Ridgeview Global Studies Academy has approved to increase grade levels to grades 9-12 at their January 27, 2020, board meeting.
