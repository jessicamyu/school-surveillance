1. 12:30PM-12:45PM Frostproof Middle-High School/Name Main Office Suite after Mrs. Claire Hatcher Bush and Kathleen High School/Naming the Freshman Building after Mr. Johnnie Jackson
Meeting
May 10, 2022 - Work Session 12:30PM
Category
A. Superintendent's Report
Type
Superintendent's Report

 

DESCRIPTION:  Recommendation to name the main office suite at Frostproof Middle/High School after Mrs. Claire Hatcher Bush. Mrs. Bush was involved at Frostproof Middle/High School during her career spanning for decades and served in several positions. Mrs. Bush served with a passion and is well-respected in the Frostproof community. Mrs. Bush is honored and gives her permission to name the office after her.

 

Recommendation to name the Freshman Building at Kathleen High School in honor and memory of now deceased former Principal after Mr. Johnnie Jackson. Mr. Jackson served Kathleen Senior High with dedication, honor, and distinction. He was much loved and respected by all who entered the campus grounds and by those who lived in the community at large. Mr. Jackson worked diligently towards students' success from freshman to seniors and included alumni in the overall success of Kathleen Senior High. We propose the name of Mr. Johnnie Jackson-Freshman Academy Building.

DESCRIPTION:

 

This is an information item regarding the placement recommendations for challenged library books. 

 

The committee recommends the district retain current placement for the following titles:

The Kite Runner by Khaled Hosseini: retain availability of this title in middle and high school libraries

The Vincent Boys by Abbi Gines: retain availability of this title in high school libraries

Beloved by Toni Morrison: retain availability of this title in high school libraries

Thirteen Reasons Why by Jay Asher: retain availability of this title in high school libraries

Real Life Boyfriends by E. Lockhart: retain availability of this title in high school libraries

Tricks by Ellen Hopkins: retain availability of this title in high school libraries

Almost Perfect by Brian Katcher: retain availability of this title in high school libraries

I Am Jazz by Jazz Jennings and Jessica Herthel: retain availability of this title at all levels

 

 

The committee recommends the district change the placement for the following titles:

Drama by Raina Telgemeier: change availability of this title from elementary, middle and high school libraries to middle and high school libraries

George by Alex Gino: change availability of this title from middle and high school libraries to elementary, middle and high school libraries

Nineteen Minutes by Jodi Picoult: change availability of this title from middle and high school libraries to high school libraries

It's Perfectly Normal by Robie H. Harris: change availability of this title from high school libraries to middle and high school libraries

Extremely Loud and Incredibly Close by Jonathan Safran Foer: change availability of this title from high school libraries to middle and high school libraries

The Bluest Eye by Tony Morrison: change availability of this title from middle and high school libraries to high school libraries

Two Boys Kissing by David Levithan: change availability of this title from high school libraries to middle and high school libraries

More Happy Than Not by Adam Silvera: change availability of this title from high school libraries to middle and high school libraries
