DESCRIPTION:

 

The Carl D. Perkins Secondary Grant will continue to support development of career academies in Polk County high schools and to further integrate academic achievement, career area certifications, and technical competencies. Activities to be completed include:

  • Curriculum mapping and curriculum development.
  • Expanding articulation agreements.
  • Additional Academies with National Recognition.
  • Academic tutoring. 
  • Recruitment for nontraditional careers to promote equity between genders.
  • Academic assessments and improvement plans.
  • Project-based learning at work sites.
  • Professional development for instructors.

2. Professional Learning Catalog [Formerly Master Inservice Plan
Meeting
Oct 12, 2021 - Work Session 9:00 AM
Category
B. Information Items
Type
Information

 

DESCRIPTION:

 

 

 

 

 

State Statute 6A-5.071 requires that the School Board Annually approve the Professional Learning Catalog [formerly Master Inservice Plan - MIP].  The Professional Learning Catalog outlines high-quality professional learning components for awarding inservice points and prescribes record maintenance and data reporting requirements. 

 

 

The Professional Learning Catalog {former MIP} went through the 5-year revision process in 2019.  The name of the Master Inservice Plan was changed to the Professional Learning Catalog. The description of the Professional Learning Catalog update provided with this summary does NOT include approximately 202 specific inservice components that are a major element of the catalog or various forms associated with the plan. Those items currently consist of more than one thousand one hundred (1100) pages. The Professional Learning Management Suite is housed in the NetConnect System in the HUB.  The Professional Development Department annually reviews the components in the spring to meet the district and statutory requirements of the FLDOE.
