DESCRIPTION: 

 

Turnaround School Supplemental Services Allocation (TSSSA)

 

To be eligible for 2022-23 TSSSA funding a school must meet one of the following two criteria:

 

1. The school is implementing a 2022-23 State Board of Education approved Turnaround plan, OR

2. The school exited Turnaround within the last two years with a school grade of "C or higher.

 

Funding amount and authority of each Local Educational Agency's (LEA) funding allocation is based on the unweighted FTE student enrollment at the eligible schools and a per-FTE funding amount of $500 or as provided by the General Appropriations Act. (section 1001.69(21), F.S.).

 

Ten of our schools fall under TSSSA for 2022-2023

 

  • Crystal Lake
  • Philip O'Brien 
  • Elbert 
  • Inwood 
  • Griffin 
  • Jesse Keen 
  • McLaughlin 
  • Spook Hill 
  • Palmetto 
  • James E. Stephens
