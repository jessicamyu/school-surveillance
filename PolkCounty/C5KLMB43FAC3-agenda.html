Motion to deny PEA Step III Grievance Hearing #463-Teacher Ambria Llauger-Torres, Lakeland High School

 

DESCRIPTION:

 

The Polk Education Association (PEA) filed a Step III Grievance on behalf of former Lakeland High School teacher Ambria Llauger-Torres. At issue is whether a March 11, 2021 Verbal Warning issued to the Grievant by her former 2020-2021 Principal, Arthur Martinez, for the Grievant’s refusal / failure to complete lesson plans should be withdrawn.

 

The “Statement of Grievance” submitted by the Grievant states:

“Mr. Martinez issued Ms. Torres a verbal warning without just cause in violation of Teacher CBA 4.4. Mr. Martinez has and continues to direct Ms. Torres in a manner that violates the Teacher CBA 6.9.”

 

Mr. Don Wilson of Boswell & Dunlap, LLP will present on behalf of Superintendent Frederick R. Heid.

 

Mr. Wilfredo Tellado, PEA Service Unit Director will present on behalf of the Grievant.
