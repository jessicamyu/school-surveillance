Per State Statute, the Reproductive Health curriculum must be voted on by the school board in a publicly noticed meeting. At the direction of Superintendent Fred Heid, a committee was convened to make recommendations for improving the Reproductive Health Curriculum. The following link provides access to all updated curriculum in Grades 4 - the High School HOPE course for review.  Attached is a summary of the committee process and a summary of the changes made based on committee recommendations. In addition, an Executive Summary and a PowerPoint overview are attached. 

 

These changes would take place during the 22-23 school year if approved by the Board.
