Superintendent Jacqueline M. Byrd recommends denial of the grievance.

 

DESCRIPTION:

 

Teacher Mr. Matthew Pelletier is challenging his ineligibility for a $4,000 federal grant-funded retention bonus based on student learning growth under the Unified School Improvement Grant (UniSIG) for school year 2019-2020 (grant duration October 1, 2019 – September 30, 2020).  Management denied the grievance at Steps I and II. During the Step III January 26, 2021 hearing the Board directed the parties to reconvene the Step 2 hearing. The Step 2-B hearing occurred February 10, 2021 and was denied by the hearing officer.  PEA subsequently filed a Step III-B Board-level grievance for March 9, 2021 resolution by the Board.

 

Mr. Don Wilson of Boswell & Dunlap, LLP will represent the Superintendent's position.

 

Polk Education Association staff will represent the grievant's position.
