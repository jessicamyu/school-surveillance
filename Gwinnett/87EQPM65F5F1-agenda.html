Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on January 21, 2010, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

January 21, 2010, Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

GCPS Workforce Analysis & Projection - An analysis of the district's workforce and staffing projections for 2011-2015.

School Resource Officers' (SRO) Program Certification Update - An update of the SRO program.

Assessment Update: CRCT - An update of the CRCT assessment information.

2010 Legislative Update - An update on the legislative issues relevant to the 2010 session of the Georgia Legislative Assembly.





Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).
