Thursday, May 28, 2015
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on May 28, 2015, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

May 28, 2015 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

 

High School Academies Update - Staff updated the Board on the current status of the Academy model in GCPS, and provide information on the upcoming Academy Institute training teachers in the Academy program format.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Quarterly Discipline Report - Staff provided the 3rd Quarter Discipline report to the Board.

 

Legislative Update - Staff reviewed with the Board the status of education-related legislation in the current session of the Georgia General Assembly.
