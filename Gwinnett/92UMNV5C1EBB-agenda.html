Thursday, January 17, 2013
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on January 17, 2013, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

January 17, 2013 Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

Science Fair/STEM Update - Dr. Jonathon Wetherington, Director for Science, updated the Board on STEM, as well as the upcoming Science and Engineering Fair.

 

JROTC Update - Jody Reeves, Director of Tech Ed, Computer Science and Work-based Learning, updated the Board on the JROTC program.

 

2013 Dr. Martin Luther King Celebration - Gwinnett County Public Schools is pleased to participate and support the United Ebony Society's Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr., Celebration. Gwinnett students, staff, and families participate in this annual recognition of one of our nation's great leaders. Dr. Frances Davis, Chief Human Resources Officer, provided an overview of this year's activities.

 

Civics Instruction - Dr. Debbie Daniell, Director for Social Studies, gave an overview to the Board on Civics instruction.

 

 

Staff Reports:

 

Staffing Update - A monthly report on personnel matters was presented to the Board.

 

2013 Legislative Update - Staff updated the Board on the legislative issues relevant to the 2013 session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

School Safety Update - Staff provided an update to the Board regarding school safety.

 

Legislation in Regard to Discipline and Bullying - Staff updated the Board on legislation pending in regard to discipline and bullying.
