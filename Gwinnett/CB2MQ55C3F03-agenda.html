3.01 Adoption of Agenda

 

The agenda for the Monthly Business Meeting is tentative and subject to change until it is adopted by the Board at this point in the meeting. Before the agenda is adopted, the Superintendent may recommend to the Board that items be added, deleted, or revised. The Board will vote on the Superintendent's recommendation(s) in adopting the evening's official agenda.

 

Board members receive supporting documentation for items on the Monthly Business Meeting agenda seven days prior to the meeting. Staff is available during this time to answer questions or provide additional information, if needed.

6.07 City of Norcross: Mutual Cancellation of Land

 

Prepared By: Division of Facilities and Operations

 

Presented By: Walt Martin, Chief Operations Officer

 

Background: The City of Norcross is requesting to cancel the existing 10-year property lease with Gwinnett County Public Schools on a 9.49-acre tract of vacant land on Mitchell Road in Norcross. This vacant tract of land was leased to GCPS in 2014. As part of the same lease agreement the City of Norcross received a 10-year lease on a 2.68-acre GCPS parking lot at the intersection of Price Road and Mitchell Road. The cancellation of the lease would return this property to GCPS.

 

The leased properties of each party that were part of the agreement were scheduled to revert back to each party when the lease expires in 2024. 

 

Financial Impact: None 

 

Recommendation: That the Board of Education adopt a motion to approve the mutual cancellation of the existing lease with the properties reverting back to the City of Norcross and Gwinnett County Public Schools.

12.01 Meeting Adjourned OR Executive Session is Resumed

 

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session). If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and/or student discipline matters; and cybersecurity plans.
