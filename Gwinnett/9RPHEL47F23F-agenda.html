Thursday, January 22, 2015
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on January 22, 2015, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center, 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

January 22, 2015 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Curriculum and Instruction Update - Staff will present information on future adoptions.

 

Human Resources and Talent Management Updates - Staff will provide updates on Tiered Teacher Certification, Teacher Attendance Recognition, and Special Education Bachelor Degree Programs.

 

Update on Civic Engagement Initiative - Staff will update the Board on the work to review the district's vision, mission, and goals.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff updated the Board on legislative issues relevant to the 2015 session of the Georgia General Assembly.
