Thursday, April 18, 2013
Special Called Meeting
A Public Hearing on Gwinnett County Public Schools' Investing in Educational Excellence (IE2) Partnership Contract Amendment will be held on April 18, 2013, from 6:00 p.m. - 6:45 p.m. in the Board Room at the Instructional Support Center. The purpose of this forum is to provide information about the Investing in Educational Excellence (IE2) Partnership contract amendment and to provide time for public input.
