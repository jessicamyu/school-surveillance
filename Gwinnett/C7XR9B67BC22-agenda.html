Thursday, November 18, 2021
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on November 18, 2021, will begin at 2:00 p.m. at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

November 18, 2021, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Board Oversight Workshop - Human Resources and Talent Management - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing the management and operation of the school district, the Board held a Workshop on the Human Resources and Talent Management Division as part of its November 18, 2021, Monthly Work Session. Staff provided an overview of key issues and topics relevant to recruitment, employment, and development of Gwinnett County Public Schools' workforce. 

 

GEMS Oversight Committee Report and Recommendations - Staff presented results of the October 28, 2021, Gwinnett Educational Management System (GEMS) Oversight Committee review of Academic Knowledge and Skills curriculum revisions and additions, and recommendations of the committee for Board approval.

 

Monthly Discipline Report - Staff shared the monthly discipline report for the 2021-2022 school year.

 

Planning Department Process Update - Staff updated the Board on processes in the Planning Department.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

2022 Legislative Program - Staff presented a draft of the 2022 Legislative Program for the Board's consideration along with a recommendation for adoption in the Business Meeting.

 

2022-23 Calendar Update - Staff provided a calendar update for the 2022-2023 school year. 

 

Division Information - Staff shared timely and pertinent information regarding their division's work.

 

Board Discussion: After Action Review - The Board engaged in an after action review of the previous month's Board Meeting
