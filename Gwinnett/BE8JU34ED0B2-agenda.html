Thursday, August 15, 2019
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on August 15, 2019, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

August 15, 2019, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Back-to-School Update - Staff shared an update on Back-to-School for GCPS.

 

Secondary Curriculum Directors Update - Staff provided a preliminary update on the role of middle and high school curriculum directors and how they support teaching and learning. 

 

2018-2019 Georgia Milestones End-of-Grade and End-of-Course Assessments and Gateway Assessment Summary - Staff provided a summary of the 2018-2019 Georgia Milestones End-of-Grade and End-of-Course assessments and Gateway assessment results for Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Carl D. Perkins Career and Technical Education Act (Perkins V) - Staff provided an update on the amended Carl D. Perkins Career and Technical Education Act (Perkins V). 

 

2019 Summer Leadership Conference Report - Staff presented a report on the 2019 Summer Leadership Conference held June 5-7, 2019, at the Classic Center in Athens. The report included information on participants, distinguished speakers, and choice sessions.   

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Final Discipline Report for 2018-2019 - Staff presented the Final Discipline Report for the 2018-19 school year.

 

Discipline Code Update - Staff shared an update to the Student Discipline Code.

 

Division Information - Staff provided timely and pertinent information regarding division work.
