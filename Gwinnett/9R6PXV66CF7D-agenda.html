Thursday, December 18, 2014
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on December 18, 2014, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

Following the Work Session, the Board will hold a Public Hearing on Redistricting. The hearing will be held at 5:30 p.m. in the Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center, 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

December 18, 2014 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Business Oversight Workshop on School Nutrition - Staff provided the Board with information on the School Nutrition Program. This report is provided to keep the Board informed regarding the operations and financial status of the program.

 

Redistricting Overview - Staff provided the Board with an update on the redistricting process for the 2015-2016 school year.  

 

GEMS Report and Recommendation - Staff presented results of the recent GEMS Oversight Committee review of Academic Knowledge and Skills revisions and additions, and recommendations of the committee for Board approval.

 

Georgia High School Writing Test Results - Staff updated the Board on achievement results of the Georgia High School Writing Test.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Emergency Closings/Inclement Weather Procedures - Staff updated the Board on the process and procedures followed in regard to the cancellation of school or early dismissal due to inclement weather.
