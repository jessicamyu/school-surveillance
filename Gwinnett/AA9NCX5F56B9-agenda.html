Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on June 16, 2016, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

June 16, 2016 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Reading Recovery Program Summary - Staff updated the Board on the Reading Recovery program in GCPS. Reading Recovery is an approach to teaching and learning that focuses on short-term early interventions designed to support classroom instruction for students in first grade who are experiencing difficulty with learning to read and write. 

 

GCPS' Performance on End of Pathway Assessments - Staff briefed the board on GCPS' performance on End of Pathway assessments.

 

Update on the Administration of the Georgia Milestones End of Grade (EOG) and End of Course (EOC) Assessments - Staff provided an update on the administration of the Georgia Milestones End of Grade (EOG) and End of Course (EOC) assessments.

 

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

New Life Academy of Excellence Charter School Update - Staff presented the Board of Education an update on New Life Academy of Excellence Charter School.
