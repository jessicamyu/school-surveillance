Thursday, April 17, 2014
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on April 17, 2014, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

April 17, 2014 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Language Arts and Mathematics Curriculum Updates - Staff reported on the Common Core State Standards and the positive influence the standards have had on language arts and mathematics in GCPS.

 

XFAT Final Report: Basic Equipment List and Educational Specifications - Staff will present the findings of the Cross Functional Action Team's review of the current Basic Equipment List and Educational Specifications.

 

AdvancED Accreditation Interim Report - Staff shared an update of the AdvancED Interim Accreditation report. The report will summarize the actions taken following the Quality Assurance Review (QAR) visit.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff reviewed with the Board the status of education-related legislation in the current session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

IE2 Partnership Contract Amendment - Staff provided an overview of the need for an amendment requesting an extension to Gwinnett County Public Schools' IE2 Partnership Contract.
