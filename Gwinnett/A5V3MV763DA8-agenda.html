Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on January 21, 2016, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

January 21, 2016 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Board Business Oversight Workshop on Transportation - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board held a workshop on Transportation as part of its January 21, 2016 Monthly Work Session. Chief Operations Officer Danny Jardine provided an overview of key issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system's transportation department.

 

Redistricting Overview - Staff updated the Board on the redistricting process for the 2016-2017 school year.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff updated the Board on legislative issues relevant to the 2016 session of the Georgia General Assembly.
