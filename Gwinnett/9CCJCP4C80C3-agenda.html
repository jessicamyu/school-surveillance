Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on November 21, 2013, will begin at 2:00 p.m. with an observation of an eCLASS at North Gwinnett Middle School, 170 Peachtree Industrial Boulevard, Sugar Hill, followed by the regular Work Session at 3:30 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

 

The monthly Work Session on November 21, 2013, began at 2:00 p.m. with an observation of an eCLASS at North Gwinnett Middle School, 170 Peachtree Industrial Boulevard, Sugar Hill, followed by the regular Work Session at 3:30 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools' Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

 

November 21, 2013 Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

Human Resources Oversight Workshop - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing the management and operation of the school system, the Board held a workshop on Human Resources management. Staff provided an overview of the key issues and topics relevant to the recruitment, employment and development of the Gwinnett County Public Schools' workforce.

 

STEM Overview and Update - Staff presented an update on STEM opportunities and student achievement in GCPS. The presentation provided an overview of the STEM programs and how schools are engaging students through the application of STEM solutions and prepare them for careers in the STEM fields.

 

Naming New School - Peachtree Ridge Cluster Middle School - Staff presented the recommendation of the School Naming Committee for the name of the new Peachtree Ridge Cluster Middle School, slated to open in August 2014.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

2014 Legislative Program - Staff presented a draft of the 2014 Legislative Program for the Board's consideration.

 

1st Quarter Discipline Report - Staff shared the results of the 1st Quarter Discipline Report with the Board.
