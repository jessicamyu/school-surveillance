Monthly Work Session 4:30 p.m. (Time change for April only - Virtual Meeting)
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on April 16, 2020, will begin at 4:30 p.m. Due to restrictions regarding public gatherings during the COVID-19 pandemic, the April meeting will be conducted virtually.
To view the virtual meeting go to: https://gcpsk12.org/virtualboardmeeting
