Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to  student achievement and effective operations within the school system.  The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

 

The monthly Work Session on August 19, 2010, will begin at 1:00 p.m. with a Tour of Lanier HS and Burnette ES; followed by the regular Work Session at 3:30 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

 

 

 

August 19, 2010, Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:30 p.m.:

 

Opening of the New School Year - A report on the opening of the new school year.

 

Summer School Report - A report on the academic success of students at each level.

 

Gender Equity Report - A report on Title IX for the 2009-2010 school year.

 

AYP Report for 2009-10 - A report on GCPS schools AYP standings.

 

 

Staff Reports

 

Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (application, new hires, vacancies).

 

Final Discipline Report for SY2010 -  Staff will present the final discipline report for SY2010.

 

State Department of Education Sign-Off Process and Mandated Reporting - Staff will present a report on the State DOE Sign-Off Process and Mandated Reporting including information on how local mandates are monitored and managed.

 

Medical Services for Students - A report on medical services provided for GCPS students.
