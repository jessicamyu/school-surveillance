December 10, 2009, Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

Quality Plus Leader Academy (QPLA) Update and Graduation of 2009 Cohort III - A QPLA update will be given, followed by the 2009 Cohort III Graduates being presented to the Board.

Redistricting Update - An update on the redistricting process will be presented.

Academic Achievement Update - An academic achievement update will be presented.





Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

Emergency Closings/Inclement Weather Procedures - An update on the process and procedures followed due to cancellation of school or early dismissal.

Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on December 10, 2009, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.
