Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The Monthly Work Session on June 21, 2007, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA. During the first portion of the Work Session, the Board will meet in Executive (closed) Session to conduct the annual Elementary and Middle School Gateway Review Process. At the completion of the Gateway Review Process, which is anticipated to last until approximately 4:30 p.m., the Board will resume in open session for the remaining items on the Monthly Work Session agenda.

June 21, 2007 Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

2007 Legislative Update - A review on the status of education-related legislation acted on this session in the Georgia General Assembly was presented.
