6.05 Request for Proposals to Supply Non-Water Using Urinals
Meeting
Feb 14, 2008 - Monthly Business Meeting
Category
6. CONSENT AGENDA
Type
Action (Consent)

Prepared by: Division of Facilities and Operations

Presented by: L. J. Steele, Chief Operations Officer

Background: On Tuesday, January 8, 2008, at 3:00 p.m., proposals were received for the non-water using urinals for all school projects that are scheduled to bid by February 28, 2011, plus retrofits at all existing campuses. Proposals were received from five suppliers. The proposals were reviewed and evaluated based on price, technical data, and location of nearest distribution point. It is recommended that the urinal as manufactured by Zero Flush, Inc. of Kissimmee, Florida, and as distributed by Southeast Link of Doraville, Georgia, be used for Gwinnett County Public Schools construction and renovation projects.

This proposal includes, but is not limited to, providing non-water using urinals for all new schools, renovations, additions, and maintenance projects scheduled to bid prior to February 28, 2011.

Note: Estimated cost savings is $350,000.00/year and estimated water savings is 50,000,000 gallons. Payback is 2.5 to 3 years.

Financial impact: Within current budget of the 2007 Building Program.

Recommendation: That the Board of Education adopt a motion to award the contract to Zero Flush, Inc.

    Category
    11. ADJOURNMENT
    Type

    The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:30 p.m. Public Forum and 7:00 p.m. Open Session. If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

    Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and student discipline appeals.
