2.01 Adoption of Agenda

 

The agenda for the Monthly Business Meeting is tentative and subject to change until it is adopted by the Board at this point in the meeting.  Before the agenda is adopted, the Superintendent may recommend to the Board that items be added, deleted, or revised.  The Board will vote on the Superintendent's recommendation(s) in adopting the evening's official agenda.

 

Board members receive supporting documentation for items on the Monthly Business Meeting agenda seven days prior to the meeting.  Staff is available during this time to answer questions or provide additional information, if needed.

11.01 eCLASS Acceleration and Classroom Transformation PCR

 

 

Prepared by: Rick Cost, Chief Financial Officer

 

Presented by: J. Alvin Wilbanks, CEO/Superintendent

 

Background: The school district launched its five-year strategic initiative called eCLASS (digital Content, Learning, Assessment, and Support System) in 2011. To accelerate the implementation of eCLASS, the district is moving from a customized, jointly developed software package to an alternate approach that utilizes proven out-of-the-box and best-of-breed solutions. The district's strategic technology integrator, IBM, will serve as the lead system integrator and program manager for eCLASS moving forward. These changes will result in enhanced software functionality delivered to teachers and students more quickly, consolidation of all eCLASS program costs, and better collaboration and cost containment through more effective vendor partnerships.  

 

Financial impact: $27,020,497 currently budgeted over the next five years

 

Recommendation: That the Board approve the eCLASS Acceleration and Classroom Transformation Project Change Request 1317 as recommended by the Superintendent.

11.02 Naming of Chattahoochee ES Bus Lane

 

Prepared by:     Dr. Steve Flynt, Associate Superintendent

 

Presented by:   J. Alvin Wilbanks, CEO / Superintendent

 

Background:  The Local School Council of Chattahoochee ES has submitted a proposal to name the bus lane in front of the school “Coomer Cove” in honor of former principal Jane Coomer.  Ms. Coomer was the first principal of Chattahoochee ES and served for 14 years before retiring.  During her retirement, she has returned to the school to tutor students to help them succeed in reading, writing, and math.

 

Financial impact: n/a

 

Recommendation:   That the Board accept the request to name the bus lane at Chattahoochee ES “Coomer Cove”.

14.01 Meeting Adjourned

 

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:15 p.m. Public Forum (open session) and 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session).  If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and student discipline appeals.
