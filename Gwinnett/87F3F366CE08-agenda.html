Subject
7.04 Elementary and Middle School Gateway Review
Meeting
Jun 22, 2006 - Regular Meeting
Category
7. SUPERINTENDENT’S REPORTS
Type
Information
Prepared by: Linda M. Mitchell, Executive Director of Student Accountability, Assessment and Advisement

Presented by: Dr. Cindy Loe, Associate Superintendent for Organizational Advancement

Background: Of the 17,761 Gwinnett fourth and seventh graders required to pass the 2006 Gateway test administered in April, the vast majority earned passing scores on the first try and were promoted to the next grade level. For those students who did not do as well on the Gateway as would be expected based on their classroom grades, the school system’s Assessment Review Procedure was immediately initiated and acted upon by the Gwinnett County Board of Education.

According to the Assessment Review Procedure, any student whose report cards in 2005-06 included no grade below a “C” in Gateway subjects qualified for an automatic review if he or she failed any part of the Gateway test. Each review is conducted by an impartial committee of teachers, principals, and central office administrators. Two such committees operate simultaneously in order to complete the reviews before the June 22 School Board meeting.

In addition to reviewing each student’s preliminary Gateway scores, the committee examines each student’s classroom performance as documented by the
child’s teacher(s). Members also study each student’s standardized test data, other assessment information, and evidence provided by the parents of extenuating circumstances (illness, death in the family, etc.) that could have caused the student to perform poorly on the test. The committee then recommends one of the following three actions for each student:

- waive passage of this year’s Gateway and promote to the next grade
- waive passage of this year’s Gateway, promote to the next grade,
but recommend summer school
- do not waive passage of this year’s Gateway, recommend summer school,
and require Gateway retake and passage for promotion

The committee’s recommendations and the Board’s final decisions will be reviewed in this report.

Financial impact: None for this report

Recommendation: N/A
