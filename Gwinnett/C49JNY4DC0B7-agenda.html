Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on July 15, 2021, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

July 15, 2021, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Construction and Facilities Management Board Oversight Workshop - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board held a workshop on Construction and Facilities Management as part of its July 15, 2021, Monthly Work Session. CEO/Superintendent J. Alvin Wilbanks and Chief Operations Officer Walt Martin provided an overview of key issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system's construction and facilities management. 

 

2021 Summer Leadership Conference Overview and Participant Feedback - Staff presented information about this year's Summer Leadership Conference, which convened on June 9-10. The presentation included qualitative and quantitative data supporting the success of the district's signature learning event encompassing 78 choice sessions, distinguished speakers, and SHINE Talks.

 

Cross-Functional Action Teams (XFATS) Final Report - Decisions from Executive Cabinet on recommendations from all four XFATS were shared with the Board. The presentation was a follow-up to the recommendations that were presented to the Board in April and May from the following four XFATS: Addressing Prior Year Achievement Gaps, Social Emotional Learning Training and Implementation, Post Covid-19 Learning Models, and Human Resources and Talent Management Staffing.

 

Multi-Year Acceleration Plan - Staff provided information regarding the multi-acceleration plan for school year 2021-2022. 

 

Return to Learning Update for School Year 2021-2022 - Staff provided a return to learning update for the 2021-2022 school year. 


Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.
