Thursday, March 16, 2017
Monthly Work Session 2:30 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on November 21, 2013, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

March 16, 2017, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Business and Finance Oversight Workshop - Staff provided an overview of key financial reports and topics relevant to the sound fiscal operations of the school system. 

 

2017 Science and Engineering Fair - Staff provided a report on the Gwinnett Science and Engineering Fair that was held on February 24, 2017.

 

Board Members Meeting with Teachers' Advisory Council (TAC) - Board members met with the Teachers Advisory Council (TAC) representatives from the schools in their Board electoral districts. Following the meetings, Board members and the TAC representatives gathered in the Board Room where Board members shared what was heard in their conversations with the TAC representatives.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff reviewed the status of education-related legislation in the current session of the Georgia General Assembly.

In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board will hold a workshop on Business and Finance as part of its March 16, 2017, Monthly Work Session. Chief Financial Officer Joseph P. Heffron will provide an overview of key financial issues and topics relevant to the sound fiscal operations of the school system. The overview will be focused on key financial information included in the June 30, 2016, Comprehensive Annual Financial Report (CAFR), the FY2016 SPLOST IV Audit and Review Report, and the 2016 Annual Investment Oversight Committee Report.
