Thursday, June 19, 2014
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on June 19, 2014, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

June 19, 2014 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Academies Update - Staff updated the Board on the implementation of the Academies model of instruction within pilot high schools.

 

5th Grade Writing Assessment - Staff updated the Board on the latest 5th Grade Writing Assessment results.

 

IE2 Update - Staff updated the Board on the recent Year 4 Governor's Office Student Achievement (GOSA) evaluation of the system's IE2 contract.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Charter School Application - Staff updated the Board on the charter school application received for review.

 

Intergovernmental Agreement Between Gwinnett County Public Schools and Gwinnett County Government - Staff updated the Board on the status of the intergovernmental agreement between the school district and the county related to the use of stop-arm cameras to record vehicles that illegally pass school buses that are loading or unloading studnts.
