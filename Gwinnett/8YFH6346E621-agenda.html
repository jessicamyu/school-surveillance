Thursday, October 18, 2012
Monthly Work Session - 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on October 18, 2012, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

October 18, 2012, Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

Update on Leadership Certification in Georgia - Staff gave an update on the changes in obtaining Leadership Certification as documented by the Georgia Professional Standards Commission.

 

2013-2014 School Year Calendar Update  - Staff provided the Board with an update of the 2013-2014 school year calendar.

 

2012 SAT and ACT Report - Staff provided an update regarding student performance on the SAT and ACT Summary data.

 

Go STEM Grant Report - Staff provided a report regarding Go STEM, a grant funded through the Goizueta Foundation. This is a partnership between Georgia Institute of Technology and the Meadowcreek cluster schools.

 

Instructional Coach Endorsement Program - Staff provided an overview of the first year of the GCPS Coach Endorsement Program and plans for follow-up and sustainability.

 

Georgia Leadership Institute for School Improvement Report - Staff provided the Board with a report on the Georgia Leadership Institute for School Improvement (GLISI) program.

 

Memorandum of Agreement with City Schools of Decatur - Staff provided an overview of the Memorandum of Agreement with City Schools of Decatur that will assist in providing options of alternative education programs available.

 

 

 

Staff Reports:

 

Staffing Update - A monthly report on personnel matters was presented to the Board.

 

Employee Benefits Open Enrollment - Staff reviewed changes to the 2013 Open Enrollment Program currently in progress from October 9 until November 9, 2012.

 

2013 Legislative Program - Staff presented a draft of the 2013 Legislative Program for the Board's consideration.

 

Schott Report Analysis - Staff provided an overview of the Schott Foundation for Public Education that released its biennial report on outcomes for Black and Hispanic males in public education.

1.08 Memorandum of Agreement with City Schools of Decatur
Meeting
Oct 18, 2012 - Monthly Work Session - 3 p.m.
Category
1. Discussion Topics and Presentations
Type
Information

The City Schools of Decatur (CSD) has not operated its own alternative education program since 2009. Instead, they created partnerships with other school systems to serve their students who are placed on long-term suspension. Currently, Dekalb County Public Schools serves their students; however, CSD is seeking additional options for their students.
