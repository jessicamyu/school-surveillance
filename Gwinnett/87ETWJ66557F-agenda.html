10.10 Resolution to Participate in RGA Sustainability Program
Meeting
Apr 17, 2008 - Monthly Business Meeting
Category
10. NEW BUSINESS
Type
Action


Prepared by: Berney Kirkland, Chief of Staff

Presented by: J. Alvin Wilbanks, CEO/Superintendent

Background: Since July 2006, the Gwinnett County Board of Education has been participating in a two-year, invitation-only program called "Reform Governance in Action." RGA is conducted by The Center for Reform of School Systems (CRSS) and is funded by The Broad Foundation. Its focus is on improving large, urban school districts through effective governance by the Board-Superintendent team. The two-year program will conclude in July 2008.

CRSS has extended an invitation to the Gwinnett County Board of Education to continue its work with RGA through July 2010 as a participant in the RGA Sustainability Program. A resolution stating the Board's intent to participate in the Sustainability Program is required.

Financial impact: N/A

Recommendation: Staff will present for Board consideration a resolution stating the Board's desire and intent to participate in the Reform Governance in Action Sustainability Program from August 2008 through July 2010.

11.01 Meeting Adjourned
Meeting
Apr 17, 2008 - Monthly Business Meeting
Category
11. ADJOURNMENT
Type

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:30 p.m. Public Forum and 7:00 p.m. Open Session. If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and student discipline appeals.
