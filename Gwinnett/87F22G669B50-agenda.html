Prior to each monthly business meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a work session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The work session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled monthly business meeting.

The monthly work session on May 10, 2007, will begin at 3:15 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

May 10, 2007 Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon work session, which began at 3:15 p.m.:

Board Business Oversight Workshop on Transportation - An overview of key information and issues relevant to the sound operation of the school system's Transportation Department.

Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

Community Schools and Joint Use Agreement - Review of the annual agreement between the Gwinnett County Board of Education and the Gwinnett County Board of Commissioners regarding operation of the Community Schools Program and the use of GCPS facilities for activities conducted by the county's Parks and Recreation Department.

Calendars 2008-2009 and 2009-2010 - Final review of the proposed calendars for school years 2008-2009 and 2009-2010.

Quarterly Discipline Report - Report on student discipline statistics for the third quarter of the 2006-07 school year.

Review of 2007 Area Board Meetings - An overview of the 2007 Area Board Meetings, which were held in each of the five School Board districts during the month of April.

2.03 Calendars 2008-2009 and 2009-2010
Meeting
May 10, 2007 - Monthly Work Session
Category
2. Staff Reports
Type
Information

Dr. Cindy Loe, associate superintendent of Teaching and Learning, will present the proposed calendars for school years 2008-2009 and 2009-2010 that have been developed and shared at the April 12 Board Meeting, at Area Board Meetings during the month of April, and in employee publications. For the 2008-2009 calendar, input against a teacher workday on Friday, January 2, and for having a student/teacher holiday on Monday, February 16, was received. However, input also was received against making changes that would extend graduation or post-planning after Memorial Day. Taking all of the feedback gathered into consideration, no changes were recommended to the 2008-2009 and 2009-2010 calendars, and they are now ready to be communicated as final.
