Thursday, November 21, 2019
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on November 21, 2019, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

November 21, 2019, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

GCPS' College and Career Readiness Performance Index and ACT Performance - Staff shared GCPS' performance on the ACT and College and Career Readiness Performance Index (CCRPI). A summary of district and school level results on the CCRPI was also provided. 

 

GCPS' Early Learning and School Readiness Initiative - Staff provided an update on GCPS' Early Learning and School Readiness initiative

 

GEMS Oversight Committee Report and Recommendations - Staff presented results of the November 7, 2019, Gwinnett Educational Management System (GEMS) Oversight Committee review of Academic Knowledge and Skills revisions, additions, and recommendations for Board approval. 

 

Performance-Based Awards Update- Staff presented an update on the Performance-Based Awards, which will be issued for the first time in December based on data from the 2018-2019 school year.

 

GCPS' Equitable Resource Allocation for Schools - Staff presented the methodology used by GCPS for the equitable allocation of resources used to serve local schools.

 

Student Discipline Code Review - Staff provided a review of the student discipline code.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

1st Quarter Discipline Report for SY2020 - Staff presented the first quarter Discipline Report for the 2019-20 school year. 

 

2020 Legislative Program - Staff presented a draft of the 2020 Legislative Program for the Board's consideration.
