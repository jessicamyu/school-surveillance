Thursday, December 21, 2017
Monthly Work Session 2:30 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on December 21, 2017, will begin at 2:30 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

December 21, 2017, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Strategic Waivers Schools System (SWSS) Amendment #3 - Staff shared the Strategic Waivers School System (SWSS) contract amendment to add four additional flexibilities/waivers, revise three current flexibilities/waivers, and add two new schools.

 

GCPS' Performance on the College and Career Readiness Performance Index (CCRPI) - Staff shared GCPS' performance on the College and Career Readiness Performance Index (CCRPI).

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Emergency Closings and Inclement Weather Procedures - Staff updated the Board on the process and procedures followed in regard to the cancellation of school or early dismissal due to inclement weather.

 

Digital Learning Days - Staff shared information concerning digital learning days.

 

Legislative Report - Staff presented a legislative report to the Board.
