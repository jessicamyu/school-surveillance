Honorees: GCPS' 2009 Title I Distinguished Schools

Recognition: 2009 Georgia Title I Distinguished Schools

Awarded by: Georgia Department of Education

Introduced by: Carol Grady, Director of Federal and Special Programs

Background: The Title I Distinguished Schools Program, authorized in Section 1117 of the No Child Left Behind Act of 2001, recognizes Title I schools that have made Adequate Yearly Progress (AYP) for three or more consecutive years. The program was established by the Georgia Department of Education to recognize schools that meet or exceed the state’s academic content standards and student academic achievement standards. Schools making AYP for four or more consecutive years receive monetary awards. Schools making AYP for three consecutive years receive a certificate of recognition from the Georgia Department of Education.

11.01 Meeting Adjourned
Meeting
Dec 10, 2009 - Monthly Business Meeting
Category
11. ADJOURNMENT
Type

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:30 p.m. Public Forum and 7:00 p.m. Open Session. If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and student discipline appeals.
