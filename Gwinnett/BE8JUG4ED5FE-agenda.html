4.03  Brookwood High School Science Olympiad Team - 2019 State Champions

 

Honoree:   Brookwood High School Science Olympiad Team

 

Recognition:  2019 Science Olympiad State Championship

 

Awarded by:  Georgia Science Olympiad

 

Introduced by:   Jessica Holden,  Director of Science, Grades 6-12

 

Background:  Science Olympiad offers students the opportunity for interscholastic competition rooted in science. As one of the largest national science competitions, each year over 7,000 teams participate in Science Olympiad around the country. Science Olympiad tournaments are composed of several events which require students to apply various science disciplines such as biology, earth science, chemistry, and physics, along with technology, problem-solving, and teamwork.

     This past spring, the Brookwood Science Olympiad team competed in the State Science Olympiad at Emory University. In a competition that featured the 24 best teams in the state, Brookwood took home top honors, winning its 11th Science Olympiad championship. On its way to victory, Brookwood earned more medals than any other team in its history. This first place team finish allowed Brookwood to represent Georgia in the National Science Olympiad May 31 – June 1 at Cornell University, where the team was successful in many events featuring the nation’s top science talent.

14.01  Meeting Adjourned

 

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:15 p.m. Public Forum (open session) and 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session).  If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and student discipline appeals.
