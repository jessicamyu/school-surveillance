Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on September 19, 2019, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

Cross-Functional Action Teams, which are also known as XFAT teams, address issues of concern in the district which have been determined by the Superintendent, cabinet members, as well as other leaders within the district. XFAT teams meet for up to 90 days and are made up of a cross-section of district and school representatives. The team utilizes guiding concepts, principles, and practices as a way to create and sustain a culture committed to continuous improvement. This XFAT team will share findings on Encouraging Civic Knowledge and Participation.
