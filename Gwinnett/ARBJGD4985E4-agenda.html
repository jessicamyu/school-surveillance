Thursday, October 19, 2017
Monthly Work Session 2:30 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on October 19, 2017, will begin at 2:30 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

October 19, 2017, Work Session Topics

 

Division of Information Management and Technology Board Oversight Workshop - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board held a workshop on Information Management and Technology. Chief Information Officer Frank Elmore provided an overview of key issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system.

 

National Assessment Results - Staff shared a summary of GCPS performance during the 2016-17 school year on ACT, SAT, and Advanced Placement Exams.

 

School Nurses Report -  Staff shared a report on the work of school nurses in Gwinnett County Public Schools. 

 

Staffing Update -  Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

2018 Legislative Program -  Staff presented a draft of the 2018 Legislative Program for the Board's consideration.

 

Every Student Succeeds Act Update - Staff provided an update on the Every Student Succeeds Act.
