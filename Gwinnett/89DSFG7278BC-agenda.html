The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on October 21, 2010, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA. 


  

October 21, 2010, Work Session Topics:

 

Academic Achievement Update - EOCT, GHSGT, and AP - An update on these assessments will be provided. 

 

Adequate Yearly Progress (AYP) Update - An update on the District's AYP will be provided. 

  

Legislative Priorities - Staff will present a draft of the 2011 Legislative Program for the Board's consideration. 

 

Data Projects Update -An update of the District's data projects.  

 

 

Staff Reports:

 

Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

 

Partnership with Georgia Gwinnett College (GGC) - Staff will update the Board on GCPS' partnership with GGC.

 

Teacher Evaluation Program Update - Staff will update the Board on the Teacher Evaluation Program.

 

2011 Open Enrollment - Staff will review this year's open enrollment which concludes on November 10, 2010.
