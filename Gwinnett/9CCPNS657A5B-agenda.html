2.01 Adoption of Agenda

 

The agenda for the Monthly Business Meeting is tentative and subject to change until it is adopted by the Board at this point in the meeting.  Before the agenda is adopted, the Superintendent may recommend to the Board that items be added, deleted, or revised.  The Board will vote on the Superintendent's recommendation(s) in adopting the evening's official agenda.

The Gwen Hutcheson Outstanding Social Studies Educator Award is presented each year by the Georgia Council for the Social Studies. 8.05 Sports Medicine Services Agreement -- Gwinnett County Public Schools and Gwinnett Hospital System

 

Prepared by:  Division of School Improvement and Operations

 

Presented by:  J. Alvin Wilbanks

 

Background:  Gwinnett Hospital System (GHS) currently provides sports medicine services to the majority of high schools in Gwinnett County Public Schools (GCPS). GHS provides athletic training services without a formal agreement to 10 of the 18 high schools with athletic programs, and two additional schools are served by GHS sports medicine physicians. The hospital system provides concussion testing (ImPACT) to all high school athletic programs and serves all schools through its Concussion Institute. GHS also conducts semi-annual educational and CPR certification sessions for GCPS athletic directors and coaches. The proposed agreement will formalize and strengthen the partnership between the school district and GHS to extend and improve school sports medicine care in accordance with Georgia's statutory code and Georgia High School Association policies.

 

Financial impact:

 

Recommendation:  That the Board approve the proposed Sports Medicine Services Agreement between Gwinnett County Public Schools and Gwinnett Hospital System, effective January 1, 2014, with full implementation at the beginning of the 2014-2015 school year.

14.01 Meeting Adjourned

 

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:15 p.m. Public Forum (open session) and 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session).  If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and student discipline appeals.
