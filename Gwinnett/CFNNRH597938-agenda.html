Thursday, July 21, 2022
Monthly Work Session 12:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on July 21, 2022, will begin at 12:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

July 21, 2022, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

School Safety Measures and Improvements - Staff provided information on school safety measures and improvements.

 

Board's Theory of Action to Improve Student Behavior and Outcomes and Correlated Student Handbook Overview - Staff provided an update regarding the Board's Theory of Action to Improve Student Behavior and Outcomes (JCDI) and the coorelated student handbook, which would integrate the new JCDI Policy. 

 

COVID-19 Update - The Board received an update on current information related to COVID-19.

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

Financial Update - Staff provided a monthly financial update.

 

Strategic Plan Update - Staff provided an update on the Strategic Plan. 

 

Policy IKBB - Divisive Concepts Complaint Resolution - Staff updated the Board on this new policy that has been developed in accordance with policy and/or mandates in state and/or federal law.

 

Policy BBBB - New Board Member Orientation - Staff reviewed recommended changes. 

 

Policy GBQ - Employment of Retired Personnel - Staff updated the Board on changes that have been made in accordance with policy and/or mandates in state and/or federal law.

 

Policy IEDA - Unstructured Break Time - Staff updated the Board on changes that have been made in accordance with policy and/or mandates in state and/or federal law.

 

Policy KGA - Possesion of Weapons at Board Facilities - Staff updated the Board on changes that have been made in accordance with policy and/or mandates in state and/or federal law.

 

Policy JCD - Student Conduct Behavior Follow-Up - Staff continued the the review with the Board regarding the student conduct behavior code.
