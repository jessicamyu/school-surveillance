Thursday, August 16, 2018
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on August 16, 2018, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

August 16, 2018, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Meadowcreek Cluster Health Science High School Update - Staff shared an update on the Meadowcreek Cluster Health Science High School.

 

Back-to-School Update - Staff shared a report regarding back-to-school information.

 

2017-2018 Georgia Milestones End-of-Grade and End-of-Course Assessment Results - Staff provided a summary of the 2017-2018 Georgia Milestones End-of-Grade and End-of-Course assessment results for Gwinnett County Public Schools.　

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Title IX Gender Equity Report - Staff shared the Title IX Gender Equity Report for the 2017-18 school year.

 

Final Discipline Report for 2017-18 -  Staff presented the Final Discipline Report for the 2017-18 school year.

 

Division Information - Staff shared timely and pertinent information regarding their division's work.
