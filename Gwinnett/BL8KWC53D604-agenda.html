4.04  North Gwinnett High School National Art Honor Society - 2020 State Champions

Recognition: 2020 State Championship Team for the National Art Honor Society Conference and Competition

 

Awarded by:   Georgia Art Education Association

 

Introduced by:   David DuBose, Director of Fine Arts

 

Background: The Georgia Art Education Association hosts the National Art Honor Society state conference. Each year a different Georgia college hosts the yearly event. This year’s conference was held at the SCAD Atlanta campus and the High Museum of Art. This annual event brings together the National Art Honor Society Chapters from high schools from across the State of Georgia. During this two-day event, the chapters take part in many clinics and activities. Throughout the event, teams are faced with challenges in sculpture, design, drawing, portraits, fashion, mixed media, and photography. The chapter scoring the highest from all of the events and challenges is named the state champion. This is the sixth consecutive year for North Gwinnett High School to be named State Champions.

14.01  Meeting Adjourned

 

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:15 p.m. Public Forum (open session) and 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session). If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and/or student discipline matters.
