The Monthly Work Session on May 19, 2016, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Dr. Jeff Mathews, principal at Peachtree Ridge HS, and Dr. Jonathon Wetherington, Director of Science, will update the Board on innovations through project-based learning in GCPS. Project-Based Learning is an approach to teaching and learning that focuses on having students apply academic knowledge and skills to develop and communicate solutions and products. This helps students build background knowledge and experiences that provide context for the learning. To support this innovative and transformative approach, the Creativity, Innovation, and Entrepreneurship Transformation Team has developed a Framework for Project-Based Learning. The framework helps teachers engage students in a learning process that allows for frequent assessment and feedback that will positively impact student learning.
