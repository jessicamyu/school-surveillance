PUBLIC HEARINGS ON FY2017 MILLAGE RATE JULY 21 AND AUGUST 1, 2016
Subject
Public Hearings on FY2017 Millage Rate to be held July 21 and August 1, 2016
Meeting
Jul 21, 2016 - PUBLIC HEARINGS ON FY2017 MILLAGE RATE JULY 21 AND AUGUST 1, 2016
Type
Information, Special Agenda Type

The Gwinnett County Board of Education will hold Public Hearings on the Millage Rate required to support the Board's FY2017 adopted Budget on the following dates and times:
 

July 21, 2016 at 11:45 a.m. for the 1st Public Hearing
July 21, 2016 at 6:00 p.m. for the 2nd Public Hearing
August 1, 2016 at 8:00 a.m. for the 3rd Public Hearing (Vote/Adoption of Millage Rate as noted in below agenda)
 

All hearings will be held in the Board Room at the J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia 30024.


Agenda

 

I.    Call to Order and Opening Comments - Chairman Dr. Robert McClure


II.   Overview of FY2017 Budget and Tentatively adopted Millage Rate - CEO/Superintendent J. Alvin Wilbanks & Staff


III.  Comments and Questions - Public


IV.   Comments and Questions - Chairman McClure & Board Members

 

V.    Vote/Adoption - Chairman McClure & Board Members

       (at the August 1 meeting)


VI.   Closing Comments and Adjournment - Chairman McClure
