Thursday, July 16, 2015
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on July 16, 2015, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

July 16, 2015 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Board Business Oversight Workshop, Construction and Facilities Management - Staff from the Department of Construction and Facilities Management presented a Board Business Oversight Workshop in conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems.

 

K-12 Writing Initiative Update - Staff provided an update on GCPS K-12 writing initiative, including the 2014-15 High School Gateway results.

 

Creativity, Innovation, and Entrepreneurship Team (CIET) Update - Staff presented an update on how innovation is transforming student instruction and the role CIET has played in nurturing that transformation.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

E-SPLOST Renewal - CEO/Superintendent Wilbanks reviewed with the Board the final documents related to the proposed election to authorize continuation of the current one-percent education special purpose local option sales tax or E-SPLOST, which is on the evening's Business Meeting agenda for action.
