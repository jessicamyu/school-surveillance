Gwinnett County Board of Education
Special Board Meeting
7:15 a.m.


Purpose: This Special Board Meeting is for the final adoption of the millage rate. As part of the budget process, the Board of Education is required to adopt a millage rate to support the budget. The tentative adoption of the millage rate was approved at the May 20, 2010 Monthly Business Meeting.

Recommendation: That the Board of Education adopt a millage rate of 19.25 mills for M&O and 1.30 mills for debt service to support the FY 2011 Budget.
