Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on December 17, 2015, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

December 17, 2015 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Business Oversight Workshop - Human Resources and Talent Management Division - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing the management and operation of the school district, the Board held a workshop on Human Resources and Talent Management Division as part of its December 17, 2015 Monthly Work Session. Staff provided an overview of key issues and topics relevant to recruitment, employment, and development of Gwinnett County Public Schools' workforce.

 

GED Program Partnership with Juvenile Court System - In August of 2015, Gwinnett Technical College Department of Adult Education, Gwinnett County Public Schools, and Gwinnett County   Juvenile Court entered into a collaborative partnership to provide classes for students serving long-term suspensions or expulsions from GCPS that are desirous of obtaining a GED credential. This partnership is coordinated by Freeman Davis, Lead Facilitator. Mr. Freeman and Judge Rodatus provided an update of this program to the Board.  

 

GEMS Report and Recommendations - Staff presented information from the November 17, 2015 GEMS Review, as well as recommended changes to the AKS from the GEMS Review Committee.  

 

Junior Achievement Update - Staff updated the Board on the Junior Achievement Program for Norcross High School.

 

Theme High School Update - Staff updated the Board on the planning progress for the new theme high school in Norcross.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Emergency Closings/Inclement Weather Procedures - Staff updated the Board on the process and procedures followed in regard to the cancellation of school or early dismissal due to inclement weather.
