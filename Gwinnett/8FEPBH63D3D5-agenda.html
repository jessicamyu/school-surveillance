Monthly Work Session
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on May 19, 2011, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

May 19, 2011, Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.

 

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Transportation - Staff will provide an overview of key issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system's transportation department.

 

Update on State Bullying Mandates - Staff will update the Board on the state mandates that have been incorporated into the District's Bullying Policy.

 

Staff Reports:

 

Staffing Update - A monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

 

Quarterly Discipline Report - Staff will present the third quarter discipline report for the 2010-11 school year.
