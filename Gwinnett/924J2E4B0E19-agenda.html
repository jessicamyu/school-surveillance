Thursday, December 13, 2012
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on December 13, 2012, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

December 13, 2012, Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

Swearing In of Newly Re-Elected Board Members - Judge Jim Clarke, Gwinnett Probate Court, performed the Swearing In of newly re-elected Board Members, Mrs. Louise Radloff, Dr. Mary Kay Murphy, and Mrs. Carole Boyce.

 

Business Oversight Workshop on School Nutrition - Staff provided the Board with information on the operations and financial status of the School Nutrition Program.

 

Assessment and Accountability Year-End Data Report - Staff updated the Board on year-end testing data.

 

eCLASS Update/Teacher Portal - Staff updated the Board on recent eCLASS developments, as well as reported on the rollout of the teacher portal element of eCLASS.

 

 

 

Staff Reports:

 

Staffing Update - A monthly report on personnel matters was presented to the Board.

 

Emergency Closings/Inclement Weather Procedures - Staff updated the Board on the process and procedures in regard to the cancellation of school or early dismissal due to inclement weather.

 

Summer School 2013 - Staff gave a report to the Board on the High School Summer School schedule for 2013.

 

School Resource Officer Recertification - Staff provided information to the Board on recertification for School Resource Officers.
