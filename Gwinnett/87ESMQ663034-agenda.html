Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on January 8, 2009 will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

January 8, 2009, Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

Bus Refurbishing Report - Staff will provide information regarding the refurbishing of school buses for extended use.

Curriculum Update: English Language Learners (ELL) - Staff will update the Board on the GCPS ELL Program.

Curriculum Update: Language Arts Program - Staff will update the Board on the GCPS Language Arts Program.

2009 Legislative Update - Staff will update the Board on legislative issues relevant to the 2009 session of the Georgia Legislative Assembly.



Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).
