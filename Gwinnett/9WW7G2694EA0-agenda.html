Thursday, June 25, 2015
Monthly Work Session 1:30 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on June 25, 2015, will begin at 1:30 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

June 25, 2015 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Report on New Schools - Staff reviewed with the Board possible options for the names of future schools that will reflect the discreet programs that will be offered at these locations.

 

IE2 Update - Staff updated the Board on the district's Investing in Educational Excellence (IE2) partnership with the Department of Education.

 

Dual Language Immersion: Year One - Staff updated the Board on the first year of the Dual Language Immersion program, and future plans for the program.

 

eCLASS in Action: School Site Visits - Staff updated the Board on the district's host schools who have exceptionally integrated technology into instruction.

 

Student Attendance Update - Staff updated the Board on student attendance, including best practices in place across the district to encourage good school attendance.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Charter School Applications - Staff updated the Board on the charter school applications received for review.
