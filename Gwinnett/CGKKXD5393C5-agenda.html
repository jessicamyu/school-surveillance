Thursday, August 18, 2022
Monthly Work Session 12:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on August 18, 2022, will begin at 12:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

August 18, 2022, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

2022 Summer Leadership Conference Overview - Staff presented information about this year’s Summer Leadership Conference, which convened on June 8-10.

 

Back to School Update - Staff shared a Back-to-School update with the Board of Education.

 

Discipline Response and Tribunal Updates - Staff provided an update regarding the Board's Theory of Action to Improve Student Behavior and Outcomes, the student handbook, and tribunal proceedings.

 

2023-2024 School Calendar Development Process - Staff provided an overview of the 2023-2024 school calendar development process.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

School Safety Update - Staff provided an update regarding school safety. 

 

Financial Update - Staff provided a monthly financial update. 

 

Policy JCDI - Board's Theory of Action for Change to Improve Student Behavior and Outcomes - Staff updated the Board on the Board's Theory of Action Policy.

 

Policy JCAC - Sexual Harassment - Staff updated the Board on Policy JCAC

 

Policy JCD - Student Conduct Behavior Code - Staff updated the Board on Policy JCD.

 

Policy JCDAG - Bullying - Staff updated the Board on Policy JCDAG.

 

Policy JDD - Procedural Protections for Students - Staff updated the Board on Policy JDD.

 

Policy JDF - Suspended and Expelled Students - Staff updated the Board on Policy JDF.
