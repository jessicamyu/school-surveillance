Thursday, November 16, 2017
Monthly Work Session 2:30 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on November 16, 2017, will begin at 2:30 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

November 16, 2017, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

XFAT Report: Effective Instructional Models for Diverse Student Populations - The Effective Instructional Models for Diverse Student Populations (Special Education, English Language Learners) Cross Functional Action Team reviewed professional development options as a way to create a rigorous support process to meet the instructional needs of all students.

 

Division of Human Resources and Talent Management Board Oversight Workshop - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board held a workshop on Human Resources and Talent Management as part of its November 16, 2017, Monthly Work Session.

 

GEMS Oversight Committee Report and Recommendations - Staff presented information from the November 2, 2017, Gwinnett Educational Management System (GEMS) Oversight review, as well as recommended changes to the Academic Knowledge and Skills from the GEMS Oversight Committee.

 

Saturday Intervention Program - Staff presented information on the 2017-18 Saturday Intervention Program for grades 3-5.

 

Teacher Evaluations Update -  Staff provided an update of the Gwinnett Teacher Effectiveness System, the GCPS evaluation system that provides teachers with feedback and support so they can achieve the goal of increasing academic learning and achievement for all students.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

1st Quarter Discipline Report for SY2018 - Staff presented the first quarter Discipline Report for the 2017-18 school year. 

 

School Calendars for 2018-2019 and 2019-2020 - Staff reviewed the 2018-19 and 2019-20 school calendars.

 

2018 Legislative Program - Staff presented a draft of the 2018 Legislative Program for the Board's consideration.
