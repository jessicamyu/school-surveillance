Monthly Work Session 4:00 p.m. (Time change for May only - Virtual Meeting)
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on May 14, 2020, will begin at 4:00 p.m. Due to restrictions regarding public gatherings during the COVID-19 pandemic, the May meeting will be conducted virtually.
To view the virtual meeting go to: https://gcpsk12.org/virtualboardmeeting
