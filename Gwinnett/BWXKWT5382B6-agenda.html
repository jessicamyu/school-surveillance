Thursday, January 21, 2021
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on January 21, 2021, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

January 21, 2021, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Annual Business Responsibilities - Staff reviewed the annual business responsibilities with the Board. 

 

Accountability and Assessment Office Overview - Staff provided an overview of the Office of Accountability and Assessment. 

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff updated the Board on legislative issues relevant to the 2021 session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

Staff Vaccinations - Staff provided an update on staff vaccinations.

 

Accreditation Update - Staff provided an accreditation update to the Board.

 

Financing and General Obligation Bonds - Staff provided an overview of the General Obligation Bond Debt. 

 

Division Information - Staff shared timely and pertinent information regarding their division's work.
