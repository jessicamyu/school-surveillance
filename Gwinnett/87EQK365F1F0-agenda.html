Information

Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on February 18, 2010, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

February 18, 2010, Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

Alternative Education Program Update - An update on this program will be provided.
Community-Based Mentoring Program - An update on this program will be provided.
Academic Achievement Update 2009: High School Gateway Assessment - An update on this program will be provided.
2010 Legislative Update - A review of education-related legislation being considered in the 2010 session of the Georgia General Assembly will be presented.





Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).
Quarterly Discipline Report - Discipline report for Second Quarter of the 2009-2010 School Year will be presented.
Redistricting Timeline - A report on the timeline for redistricting for the 2010-11 School Year.
Four-Day Work Week - Summer 2010 - Information concerning the four-day work week for the summer of 2010 will be presented.
