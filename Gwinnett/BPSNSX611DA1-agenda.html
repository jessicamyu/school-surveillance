Monthly Work Session 4:00 p.m. (Time Change for June - Virtual Meeting)
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on June 18, 2020, will begin at 4:00 p.m. Due to restrictions regarding public gatherings during the COVID-19 pandemic, the June meeting will be conducted virtually.
To view the virtual meeting go to: https://gcpsk12.org/virtualboardmeeting

June 18, 2020, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

GCPS' COVID-19 Pandemic Response Update - Staff provided an update on the district's continued response to the COVID-19 pandemic.

 

Review of GCPS' Survey from Digital Learning Days - Staff reviewed survey results from GCPS' Digital Learning Days.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff reviewed the status of education-related legislation in the current work session of the Georgia General Assembly. 

 

Resolution to call for Renewal of E-SPLOST - CEO/Superintendent Wilbanks reviewed final documents related to the proposed election to authorize continuation of the current one-percent education special purpose local option sales tax or E-SPLOST.

 

E-SPLOST Intergovernmental Agreement with Buford City Schools - Mr. Wilbanks reviewed the agreement between Gwinnett County Public Schools and Buford City Schools which specifies the method of distribution for the education special purpose local option sales tax (E-SPLOST).
