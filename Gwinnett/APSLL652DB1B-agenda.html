Monthly Work Session 2:30 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on August 17, 2017, will begin at 2:30 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

The Aspiring Principal Program (APP), implemented through the Quality-Plus Leader Academy, prepares assistant principals to move into principal positions. The 11 graduates of the eleventh cohort will be presented to the Board of Education for recognition of completion of a year-long course of study. To date, five members of this cohort have been appointed as principal. A total of 169 participants from the 11 cohorts of the Aspiring Principal Program have been appointed as principals in Gwinnett County Public Schools.
