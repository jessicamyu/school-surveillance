Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on September 13, 2018, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

September 13, 2018, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Information Management and Technology - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board held a workshop on Information Management and Technology as part of its September 13, 2018, Monthly Work Session. Chief Information Officer Frank Elmore provided a tour of Information Management and Technology to take a closer look at the Security Operation Center, Data Center, and to see the School Technology demo.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Division Information - Staff shared timely and pertinent information regarding their division's work.
