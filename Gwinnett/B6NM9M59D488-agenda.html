Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on December 20, 2018, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

1.05 Aspiring Principal Program Cohort 12 Graduation Recognition

Dec 20, 2018 - Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Category
1. Discussion Topics and Presentations
Type
Information

The Aspiring Principal Program (APP), implemented through the Quality-Plus Leader Academy, prepares assistant principals to move into principal positions.  The twelfth cohort of 21 graduates is presented to the Board of Education for recognition of completion of a year-long course of study. To date, eight members of this cohort have been appointed as principal. A total of 203 participants have been appointed to leadership positions from the 12 cohorts of the Aspiring Principal Program.
