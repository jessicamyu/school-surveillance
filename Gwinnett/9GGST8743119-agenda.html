Thursday, March 20, 2014
Monthly Work Session 2 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on March 20, 2014, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

March 20, 2014 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Business and Finance Workshop Overview - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board held a workshop on Finance. CEO/Superintendent J. Alvin Wilbanks and Chief Financial Officer Rick A. Cost provided an overview of key financial issues and topics relevant to the sound fiscal operations of the school system. The overview was focused on key financial information included in the June 30, 2013 Comprehensive Annual Fiscal Report (CAFR), the FY2013 SPLOST IV Audit and Review Report, and the 2013 Annual Investment Oversight Committee Report.

 

GEMS Oversight Committee Recommendations - Staff provided a summary of the February 27, 2014 GEMS Oversight Review and provided recommendations for action by the Board.

 

Board Members Meeting with Teachers' Advisory Council - Following their meetings with TAC representatives from schools in their district, Board members and TAC representatives gathered in the Board Room where Board members reported out on conversations just held with those representatives.

 

2014 Gwinnett Regional Science and Engineering Fair - Staff provided an overview of the 2014 Gwinnett Regional Science and Engineering Fair.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Start-Up Charter School Application - Staff updated the Board on the Charter School Application received for review.
