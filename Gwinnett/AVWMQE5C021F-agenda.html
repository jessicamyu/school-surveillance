Thursday, March 15, 2018
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on March 15, 2018, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

March 15, 2018, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Fine Arts Update - Staff provided an update on fine arts in GCPS.

 

Instructional Resources Adoption Process for SY2018 - Staff provided an update on the instructional resources adoption process for the current school year. This update included resources recommended for adoption in select science and career and technical education courses. 

 

Board Members Meeting with Teachers' Advisory Council - As they do every year, Board members met with the Teachers' Advisory Council (TAC) representatives from the schools in their Board electoral districts. Following the meetings, Board members and the TAC representatives gathered in the Board Room where Board members shared what was heard in their conversations with the TAC representatives.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff reviewed with the Board the status of education-related legislation in the current session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

Emergency Preparedness Update - Staff provided an update to the Board on emergency preparedness for Gwinnett County Public Schools
