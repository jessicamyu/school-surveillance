2.01 Adoption of Agenda

 

The agenda for the Monthly Business Meeting is tentative and subject to change until it is adopted by the Board at this point in the meeting.  Before the agenda is adopted, the Superintendent may recommend to the Board that items be added, deleted, or revised.  The Board will vote on the Superintendent's recommendation(s) in adopting the evening's official agenda.

 

Board members receive supporting documentation for items on the Monthly Business Meeting agenda seven days prior to the meeting.  Staff is available during this time to answer questions or provide additional information, if needed.

4.01 Cindy Quinlan, Brookwood HS - ACTE 2016 Teacher of the Year

 

Honoree:      Cindy Quinlan, Brookwood High School   

 

Recognition:     ACTE 2016 Excellence Award Winner – Teacher of the Year

 

Awarded by:     Association of Career and Technical Education (ACTE)

 

Introduced by:  Jody Reeves, Executive Director – Academies and Career & Technical Education

 

Background:  Since 1954, the Association of Career and Technical Education (ACTE) has recognized those individuals who have made extraordinary contributions to Career and Technical Education (CTE) programs that exemplify the highest standards and organizations conducting activities to promote and expand CTE programs. Award winners serve as inspirational leaders to ACTE - they embody the core values of serving their students and being committed to CTE.

 

The national award winners were announced during the ACTE Awards Banquet on Thursday, November 19, at ACTE’s CareerTech VISION Conference in New Orleans, Louisiana. Brookwood High School’s teacher, Cindy Quinlan, was chosen as the ACTE 2016 Excellence Award Winners – Teacher of the Year.

 

Cindy Quinlan is a dedicated teacher who has been teaching Marketing, Computer Science, Business Education and Work-Based Learning at Brookwood High School for the past 10 years. As the Entrepreneurship Program Specialist, Cindy assists teachers with improving curriculum within Career and Technical Education.  

 

 

Additional Staff Attending with Honoree:

 

William “Bo” Ford, Principal, Brookwood High School

14.01 Meeting Adjourned

 

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:15 p.m. Public Forum (open session) and 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session).  If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and student discipline appeals.
