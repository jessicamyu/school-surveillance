Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on January 24, 2019, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

January 24, 2019, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Cross-Functional Action Team Report: Aligning the Roles and Responsibilities of the Instructional Coach - Cross-Functional Action Teams, which are also known as XFAT teams, address issues of concern in the district which have been determined by the superintendent, cabinet members, as well as other leaders within the district. XFAT teams meet for up to 90 days and are made up of a cross-section of district and school representatives. The team utilizes guiding concepts, principles, and practices as a way to create and sustain a culture committed to continuous improvement. The XFAT team updated the Board on its findings in regard to aligning the roles and responsibilities of the instructional coach.

 

Kindergarten Readiness Entry Profile and Play to Learn - Staff reviewed the Kindergarten Readiness Entry Profile and Play to Learn program.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff updated the Board on legislative issues relevant to the 2019 session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

GCPS and FBI "Think Before Your Post" Campaign - Staff updated the Board on the "Think Before You Post" campaign.

 

Facility Naming - Staff updated the Board on information relevant to renaming a facility.
