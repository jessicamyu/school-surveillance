Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on April 19, 2018, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

April 19, 2018, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Business and Finance - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board held a workshop on Business and Finance as part of its April 19, 2018, Monthly Work Session. Chief Financial Officer Joseph P. Heffron provided an overview of key financial issues and topics relevant to the sound fiscal operations of the school system. 

 

Charter Schools and New Schools Update - Staff presented a report on charter schools which included New Life Academy of Excellence and North Metro Academy of Performing Arts. The report also included the following new schools: Paul Duke STEM High School, Meadowcreek Cluster Health Science High School, and Phoenix High School at Sugarloaf Mills.

 

Career and Technical Education Update - Staff provided an update on the implementation of college and career academies at seven GCPS high schools. The report also included the current status of career and technical education in middle and high schools, including JROTC and JLC.

 

2018 Science, Engineering, and Innovation Fair - Staff provided an update on the 2018 Science, Engineering, and Innovation Fair that was held on February 23, 2018.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff reviewed the status of education-related legislation in the current session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

School Nurses Report for SY2018 - Staff  presented a report on GCPS school nurses and their role in schools across the district.
