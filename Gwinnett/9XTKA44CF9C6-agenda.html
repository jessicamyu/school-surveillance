2.01 Adoption of Agenda

 

The agenda for the Monthly Business Meeting is tentative and subject to change until it is adopted by the Board at this point in the meeting.  Before the agenda is adopted, the Superintendent may recommend to the Board that items be added, deleted, or revised.  The Board will vote on the Superintendent's recommendation(s) in adopting the evening's official agenda.

 

Board members receive supporting documentation for items on the Monthly Business Meeting agenda seven days prior to the meeting.  Staff is available during this time to answer questions or provide additional information, if needed.

7.03 Duluth Cluster MS

 

Prepared by:  Division of Facilities and Operations      

                                                                                                                                                                                                

Presented by: Daniel W. Jardine, Chief Operations Officer      

 

Background:  On Tuesday, July 7, 2015, at 3:00 p.m., bids were received on the above-referenced project.  The $4,158,000.00 low bid was submitted by Amacher Brothers Construction Co. of Atlanta, Georgia, and is $4,702.60 lower than the next lowest bidder.  Amacher Brothers Construction Co. of Georgia has successfully completed projects for Gwinnett County Public Schools in the past.

 

Eight Alternates were also bid separately.  After reviewing the cost for the Alternates, it is recommended to select Alternates 1 through 8 to add to the base bid cost.  Alternate 1 is to install a new asphalt play court for $ 19,000.00,  Alternate 2 is to install new lighting in the Gymnasium Building for $25,000.00,  Alternate 3 is to install new roofing to the Gymnasium Building for $187,000.00,  Alternate 4 is to install new roofing to the 1957 original classroom building for $158,000.00, Alternate 5 is to install a new bus canopy for $255,000.00, Alternate 6 is to install new entrance canopy at the front of the 1977 classroom building addition for $94,000.00, Alternate 7 is to install a new canopy at the side of the 1977 classroom building addition for $76,000.00 and Alternate 8 is to install new porcelain tile flooring at corridors for $167,000.00.  These Alternates adjust the contract amount to $5,139,000.00.

 

This bid process began more than a month ago when seven contractors requested plans to bid this project.  Five contractors submitted bids on July 7, 2015.

  

Financial impact:  Within current budget of the 2012 Building Program.

 

Recommendation:  That the Board of Education adopt a motion to award the contract to Amacher Brothers Construction Co. in the amount of $5,139,000.00, which includes Alternates 1 through 8.

7.05 South Gwinnett HS: Right-of-Way Deed, Temporary Driveway Easement, Permanent Easement for the Construction and Maintenance of Slopes, Permanent Easement for the Construction and Maintenance of Drainage

Background:  Gwinnett County requires a Right Of Way Deed, Temporary Driveway Easement, Permanent Easement for the Construction and Maintenance of Slopes and Permanent Easement for the Construction and Maintenance of Drainage at South Gwinnett High School for road improvements.  These easements consist of Right Of Way 3,277.96 square feet (0.076 acres), Temporary Driveway Easement 405.63 square feet (0.009 acres), Permanent Easement for the Construction and Maintenance of Slopes 3,506.42 square feet (0.081 acres) and Permanent Easement for the Construction and Maintenance of Drainage 3,552.89 square feet (0.081 acres).

 

These easements and Right Of Way are required for Gwinnett County to construct a new sidewalk along Skyland Boulevard past the school to tie into the existing sidewalk near the crosswalk to the student parking lot.

               

Financial Impact:  None

 

Recommendation:  That the Board of Education adopt a motion to grant the Right Of Way Deed, Temporary Driveway Easement, Permanent Easement for the Construction and Maintenance of Slopes and Permanent Easement for the Construction and Maintenance of Drainage.

14.01 Meeting Adjourned

 

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:15 p.m. Public Forum (open session) and 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session).  If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and student discipline appeals.
