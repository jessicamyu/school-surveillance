Thursday, April 15, 2021
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on April 15, 2021, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

April 15, 2021, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Board Oversight Workshop, Data Governance - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board held a workshop on Data Governance as part of its April 15, 2020, Monthly Work Session. Chief Data Officer Dr. Debbie Durrence provided an overview of key issues and topics relevant to the governance of the district's data. 

 

Instructional Materials Adoption Process and Recommendations - Staff provided an overview of the proposed instructional resources for Language Arts (grades 9-11), as well as miscellaneous courses in Career and Technical Education (select 6-12), Social Studies (select 9-12), and Foreign Language/Dual Language Immersion (grade 6, select 9-12).

 

Georgia Milestone Update - Staff updated the Board on the Georgia Milestones Assessment. 

 

2021 Summer Acceleration and Enrichment Overview - Staff provided an overview of the 2021 Summer Acceleration and Enrichment for grades K-8.  

 

Equity Report - Staff presented a series of equity reports comprised of internal and external input to enhance or develop school district programs and initiatives to move forward the Board's Educational Equity Policy.

 

Cross-Functional Action Team Report - Social Emotional Learning Training and Implementation - The XFAT team shared findings from their research on Social Emotional Learning Training and Implementation. 

 

Cross-Functional Action Team (XFAT) Report - Addressing Prior Year Achievement - The XFAT team shared findings from their research on Addressing Prior Year Achievement. 

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update and Policy Development - Staff provided an update on the status of education-related legislation in the now closed session of the Georgia General Assembly, and gave an update on policy development. 

 

Student Conduct Behavior Code Review Committee Recommendations - Staff reviewed recommendations from the Student Conduct Behavior Code Review Committee. 

 

Policy JCD - Student Conduct Behavior Code Review - Staff reviewed the student conduct behavior code.

 

Return to Learning Update - Staff updated the Board on the district's 2021-2022 return to learning plan.

1.07 Cross-Functional Action Team Report - Social Emotional Learning Training and Implementation

Apr 15, 2021 - Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Category
1. Discussion Topics and Presentations
Type
Information

Cross-Functional Action Teams, which are also known as XFAT teams, address issues of concern in the district which have been determined by the Superintendent, cabinet members, as well as other leaders within the district. XFAT teams meet for up to 90 days and are made up of a cross-section of district and school representatives. The team utilizes guiding concepts, principles, and practices as a way to create and sustain a culture committed to continuous improvement. This XFAT team will share findings on Social Emotional Learning Training and Implementation.
