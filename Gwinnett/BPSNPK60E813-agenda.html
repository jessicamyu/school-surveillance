Board members receive supporting documentation for items on the Monthly Business Meeting agenda seven days prior to the meeting. Staff is available during this time to answer questions or provide additional information, if needed.

11.01 Spending Resolution for Month of July 2020

 

Prepared By: Joe Heffron, Chief Financial Officer

 

Presented by: Joe Heffron, Chief Financial Officer

 

Background: Normally, local school boards of education adopt budgets prior to the first day of the new fiscal year (July 1). However, there are instances when the school board is not able to adopt a budget by July 1. In these instances, there is no authorization for the district to spend any funds in the next fiscal year until the budget is adopted formally. Legally, the district cannot operate without an approved budget. In order to continue operating when the school board cannot adopt a budget prior to July 1, it must adopt a “spending resolution” which authorizes the superintendent to spend funds in the new fiscal year until the budget is adopted.

 

Financial Impact: 1/12 of annual expenses 

 

Recommendation: That the Board of Education adopt the motion to approve the spending resolution for the month of July 2020.

14.01 Meeting Adjourned OR Executive Session is Resumed

 

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:15 p.m. Public Forum (open session) and 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session).  If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and/or student discipline matters.
