Monthly Work Session - 3:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The Monthly Work Session on September 16, 2010, will begin at 3:30 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a work session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The work session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

 

The monthly work session on September 16, 2010, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools' Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, in Suwanee, Georgia.

 

 

September 16, 2010, Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

 

Aspiring Leader Program (ALP) - Staff will present the participant's of the 2010 Aspiring Leader Program. 

 

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Information Management - An overview of key technology issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system.

 

Academic Achievement Update - High School Gateway, SAT, and ACT - An update on the results of Gateway, ACT and SAT 2010.

 

Title I Program Update - Staff will provide an update on the Title I Program.

 

Race to the Top - Staff will update the Board on the RT3 grant and next steps for implementation.

 

 

Staff Reports

 

Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

 

Common Core Standards - An update on Common Core Standards.  

 

2011-2012 School Calendar - An update on the 2011-2012 School Calendar.

 

2011 Legislative Issues - An update on the timeline for the presentation of the 2011 Legislative Program.

1.06 Race to the Top
Meeting
Sep 16, 2010 - Monthly Work Session - 3:00 p.m.
Category
1. First Category
Type
Information

On August 24, Georgia received news that it was one of nine states, along with the District of Columbia, to be selected as grantees in the second round of the federal Race to the Top (RT3) competitive grant program. Gwinnett County Public Schools is one of 26 school districts in the state that signed on as partners with the state in its RT3 application. Staff will update the Board on the RT3 grant and the anticipated next steps for the local district partners as implementation of the grant moves forward.
