Thursday, December 16, 2021
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on December 16, 2021, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

December 16, 2021, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Early Learning and School Readiness Initiative - Staff provided an update on Early Learning and School Readiness initiative.

 

2022-2023 Final Calendar - Staff presented the final calendar for the 2022-2023 school year. 

 

COVID-19 Mitigation, Second Semester - Staff provided COVID-19 mitigation information for second semester. 

 

Monthly Discipline Report - Staff shared the monthly discipline report for the 2021-2022 school year.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Inclement Weather and Emergency Closing Procedures - Staff updated the Board on the process and procedures followed in regard to the cancellation of school or early dismissal due to inclement weather.

 

Redistricting Update - Staff updated the Board on redistricting. 

 

Division Information - Staff shared timely and pertinent information regarding their division's work.

 

Board Discussion: After Action Review - The Board engaged in an after action review of the previous month's Board Meeting.
