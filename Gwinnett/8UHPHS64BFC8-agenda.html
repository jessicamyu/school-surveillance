Thursday, June 21, 2012
Work Session - 3:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on June 21, 2012 will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

June 21, 2012 Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session which began at 3:00 p.m.

 

5th Grade Writing Results - Staff provided a report on students' performance on the State's 5th Grade Writing Assessment.

 

High School Gateway Results - Staff provided a report on students' performance on the High School Gateway Assessment.

 

Content Area Retest Report - Staff briefed the Board on pending changes to the Gateway Assessment Program.

 

Instructional Strategies Conference Preview - Staff previewed an upcoming workshop for special education teachers paraprofessionals and administrators.

 

Focus on Leadership and Advancement in Renewal Energy (FLARE) Competition - Staff reported on the annual competition involving students from all school levels.

 

Staff Reports: 

 

Staffing Update - A monthly report on personnel matters.
