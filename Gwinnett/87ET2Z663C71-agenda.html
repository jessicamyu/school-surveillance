Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on October 9, 2008, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

October 9, 2008, Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

Legislative Priorities - Staff will present the legislative priorities for 2009.

Curriculum Update - Science Program - Staff will present an update of Curriculum and Instruction's science program.

New Life Academy of Excellence Charter School Renewal Request - Staff will provide an update of the status of the New Life Academy of Excellence Charter School's renewal request.

Employee Benefits/Open Enrollment -Staff will present the results of the benefits audit conducted by MGT, Inc., and review the upcoming benefits open enrollment.

School Resource Officer (SRO) Program - Staff will present a report on the School Resource Officer (SRO) Program.




Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).
