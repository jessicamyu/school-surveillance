Monthly Work Session 2 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on March 19, 2015, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

March 19, 2015 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Business and Finance Workshop Overview - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board held a workshop on Finance as part of its March 19, 2015 Monthly Work Session. Chief Financial Officer Rick A. Cost provided an overview of key financial issues and topics relevant to the sound fiscal operations of the school system. The overview focused on key financial information included in the June 30, 2014 Comprehensive Annual Financial Report (CAFR), the FY2014 SPLOST IV Audit and Review Report, and the 2014 Annual Investment Oversight Committee Report.

 

Language Arts Instructional Materials Adoption - Staff presented high school language arts instructional resources that have been selected for adoption by the Board in the evening's meeting.

 

2015 Gwinnett Science and Engineering Fair - Staff updated the Board on the recent Science and Engineering Fair.

 

Board Members Meeting with Teachers' Advisory Council - As they do each year, Board members met with the TAC representatives from the schools in their Board districts. Following the meetings, Board members and TAC representatives gathered in the Board Room where Board members shared what was heard in their conversations with the TAC representatives.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff reviewed with the Board the status of education-related  legislation in the current session of the Georgia General Assembly.
