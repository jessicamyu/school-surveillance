Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on March 12, 2009, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

1.01 Board Business Oversight Workshop - Business and Finance

Category
1. Work Session Discussion Topics and Presentations
Type
Information

In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board will hold a workshop on Finance as part of its March 12, 2009 Monthly Work Session. CEO/Superintendent J. Alvin Wilbanks and Chief Financial Officer Rick Cost will provide an overview of key financial issues and topics relevant to the sound fiscal operations of the school system. The overview will be focused on key financial information included in the June 30, 2008 Comprehensive Annual Financial Report (CAFR), the Final SPLOST II Audit and Review Report and the Year 1 SPLOST III Audit and Review Report.

1.02 Board Members' Meeting with Teachers' Advisory Council
Meeting
Mar 12, 2009 - Monthly Work Session
Category
1. Work Session Discussion Topics and Presentations
Type
Information , Special Agenda Type

Each year the Board of Education members meet with members of the Teachers' Advisory Council (TAC) from the schools in their Board districts. The Board and TAC representatives will meet, by district, from 3:00 p.m. until 3:45 p.m. At 3:50 p.m. all TAC members and the Board will gather in the Board Room for a report-out by each Board member on the conversations with the TAC representatives.
