3.01 Adoption of Agenda 

 

The agenda for the Monthly Business Meeting is tentative and subject to change until it is adopted by the Board at this point in the meeting. Before the agenda is adopted, the Superintendent may recommend to the Board that items be added, deleted, or revised. The Board will vote on the Superintendent's recommendation(s) in adopting the evening's official agenda.

 

Board members receive supporting documentation for items on the Monthly Business Meeting agenda seven days prior to the meeting. Staff is available during this time to answer questions or provide additional information, if needed.

7.05 Strategic Waivers School System (SWSS) - Extension

 

Prepared by: Dr. Al Taylor, Associate Superintendent for School Improvement and Operations

 

Presented by: Dr. Calvin J. Watts, Superintendent

 

Background: Gwinnett’s Strategic Waivers School System (SWSS) Partnership Contract is set to expire on June 30, 2022. All SWSS contracts outline five-year CCRPI accountability measures and require schools to meet those goals for at least three of the five years. Due to CCRPI changes and COVID-19 disruptions, there are only two years of data available by which to evaluate contract performance measures. The State Board of Education approved a two-year extension for all SWSS contracts to allow districts to continue to support student achievement and growth, address operational needs, and ensure additional years of valid and reliable data by which to better assess the impact of flexibility in schools. The amendment to extend the contract to June 30, 2024, will be submitted to the Georgia Department of Education for approval by the State Board of Education.  

 

Financial impact: N/A

 

Recommendation: Staff will bring forward a recommendation for Board approval.

7.06 Naming of North Gwinnett Middle School's Dance Studio

 

Prepared by: Dr. Al Taylor, Associate Superintendent for School Improvement and Operations

 

Presented by: Dr. Calvin J. Watts, Superintendent

 

Background: The Local School Council of North Gwinnett Middle School (NGMS) has submitted a proposal to name the school's dance studio Wanda F. Law Dance Studio. Ms. Law has served as the principal of NGMS for 13 years, and has served GCPS for 33 years. 

 

Financial impact: n/a

 

Recommendation: That the Board of Education approve the request to name the dance studio Wanda F. Law Dance Studio.

11.01 Meeting Adjourned OR Executive Session is Resumed

 

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session). If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and/or student discipline matters; and cybersecurity plans.
