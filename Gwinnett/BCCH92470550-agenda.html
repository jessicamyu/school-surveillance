Thursday, June 20, 2019
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on June 20, 2019, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

June 20, 2019, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Charter Schools and Strategic Waivers School System Update - Staff provided an update on GCPS charter schools and the Strategic Waivers School System contract.

 

End-of-Pathway, Gateway, and Georgia Milestones Assessments Update - Staff provided an update on the End-of-Pathway, Gateway, and Georgia Milestones assessments.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Assessments - Staff discussed current assessments given to GCPS students.

 

Accomplishments and Improvements of Gwinnett County Public Schools: 1996-Present - Staff shared information about accomplishments and improvements achieved by the district from 1996 to present.
