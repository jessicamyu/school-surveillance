Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on April 14, 2022, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

April 14, 2022, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

MGT Consulting Report Out - MGT updated the Board on preliminary recommendations and findings associated with the GCPS Organizational eEffectiveness Review.

 

Balanced Assessment Update - Staff provide a balanced assessment update. 

 

Strategic Waivers School System (SWSS) - Extension Follow-Up - Staff provided additional information to the Board on the Strategic Waivers School System (SWSS) contract with the Georgia Department of Education that is set to expire on June 30, 2022.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff updated the Board on legislative issues relevant to the 2022 session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

Division Information -  Staff shared timely and pertinent information regarding their division's work.

 

Policy BCBI - Public Participation in Board Meetings - Staff updated the Board on changes that have been made in accordance with policy guidance and/or mandates in state and/or federal law.

 

Policy JCD - Student Conduct Behavior - The Board engaged in a discussion regarding Policy JCD - Student Conduct Behavior. The Board will be developing this policy through the month of June at which time a recommendation for action will be brought forth.
