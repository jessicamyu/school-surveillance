Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on May 8, 2008, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

May 8, 2008, Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

Opening New Schools - A report on the various tasks associated with opening a new school.

End-of-School Processes and Checklist - A report on the various processes and checklist items associated with the end of the school year.

Local School Councils Report - A report on local school councils.

Legislative Update - A review of education-related legislation resulting from the 2008 session of the Georgia General Assembly.




Staffing Update - A monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies) will be presented.

Quarterly Discipline Report - The discipline report for the third quarter of the 2007-08 school year will be presented.

Review of 2008 Area Board Meetings - An overview of the 2008 Area Board Meetings held during the month of April will be presented.

Community Schools and Joint Use Agreement - The GCPS Facilities Use Contract for the period July 1, 2008, through June 30, 2009 will be presented.
