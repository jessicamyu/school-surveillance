Thursday, June 15, 2017
Monthly Work Session 2:30 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

June 15, 2017, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Yi Hwang Academy of Philosophy, Art, and Science - Charter School Application - Staff updated the Board on the charter school application received for review.

 

Units of Study in Writing - Middle School Language Arts Pilot Summary - Staff provided a summary of the middle school language arts pilot for Units of Study in Writing.

 

End of Pathway Assessments Performance - Staff briefed the Board on GCPS' performance on the End of Pathway assessments.

 

Gateway Assessment Update - Staff provided an update on the administration of the Gateway assessment.

 

Georgia Department of Education STEM and STEAM Certification Update - Staff provided an update of the science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) and science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics (STEAM) instructional programs within Gwinnett County Public Schools, including the designation of STEM or STEAM certification as recognized by the Georgia Department of Education. 

 

Discipline Review Committee Update - Staff provided an update from the Discipline Review Committee.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Naming of the New Norcross Cluster STEM High School - Staff provided information on the proposed name for the new Norcross Cluster STEM High School. The proposed name is on the Business Meeting agenda for approval.
