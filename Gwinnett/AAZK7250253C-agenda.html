Monthly Work Session - 1:00 p.m. Tour of Baldwin Elementary School and Coleman Middle School; 3:00 p.m. Work Session
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.
The Monthly Work Session on July 21, 2016, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

July 21, 2016 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Partnership Gwinnett 3.0 Update - Partnership Gwinnett, Gwinnett's award-winning economic development organization, is about to kick off its next 5-year strategic plan. Nick Masino, Sr. Vice President of Economic Development & Partnership Gwinnett, updated the Board on Partnership Gwinnett's next phase.

 

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Construction and Facilities Management - Staff from the Department of Construction and Facilities Management presented a Board Business Oversight Workshop in conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems.

 

Update on the Development of the Curriculum Embedded Performance Tasks for 4th and 7th Grade - Staff provided an update to the Board on the development of the Curriculum Embedded Performance Tasks for 4th and 7th grade.

 

Student Information System (SIS) Update - Staff updated the Board on the Student Information System (SIS).

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.
