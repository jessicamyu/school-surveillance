2.01 Adoption of Agenda 

 

The agenda for the Monthly Business Meeting is tentative and subject to change until it is adopted by the Board at this point in the meeting. Before the agenda is adopted, the Superintendent may recommend to the Board that items be added, deleted, or revised. The Board will vote on the Superintendent's recommendation(s) in adopting the evening's official agenda.

 

Board members receive supporting documentation for items on the Monthly Business Meeting agenda seven days prior to the meeting. Staff is available during this time to answer questions or provide additional information, if needed.

The Consent Agenda allows the Board of Education to act on routine items of business as a series. If a Board member needs additional information or clarification, an item can remain on the Consent Agenda. If a Board member wants to discuss or debate an item, it should be removed from the Consent Agenda and placed at the proper place on the agenda.

8.05 Tribunal Training Course Outline

 

Prepared by: Dr. Al Taylor, Associate Superintendent for School Improvement and Operations

 

Presented by: Dr. Calvin J. Watts, Superintendent

 

Background: Due to the passage of Senate Bill 367, the Georgia Department of Education promulgated Rule 160-4-8-.15 which became effective August 3, 2016. This Rule requires the Local Education Agency to do the following:

  • Annually approve a Training Course Outline to be followed by the Tribunal Training provider(s).
  • This Outline requires a minimum of five (5) hours of instruction for all qualified Student Discipline Hearing Officers and/or Panel Members in the areas of 1) Student disciplinary provisions in Title 20, 2) Due process requirement under federal and state law, 3) Applicable rules of evidence, 4) Federal and state judicial decisions, and 5) Applicable standards and the role of the Hearing Officer as an independent, neutral arbitrator.

Financial impact: None

 

Recommendation: That the Board of Education approve the attached Tribunal Training Course Outline.

14.01 Meeting Adjourned OR Executive Session is Resumed

 

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session).  If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and/or student discipline matters.
