6:00 - 6:30 p.m. -- Public Hearing on Redistricting for Lovin, Patrick, and Mulberry Elementary Schools
Meeting
Dec 14, 2006 - 12/14/2006
Category
Public Hearing
Type
Special Agenda Type

The Board will receive public input on the proposed attendance boundaries for three new elementary schools opening in August 2007. The public hearing is part of the redistricting process that began on November 1, 2006. The Board is expected to approve the attendance boundaries at its January 11 meeting. The public hearing will be held in the Board Room at the school system's Instructional Support Center, 437 Old Peachtree Road NW, Suwanee, GA.
