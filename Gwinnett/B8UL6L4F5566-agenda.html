Thursday, February 21, 2019
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on February 21, 2019, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

February 21, 2019, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

College and Career Academies Update - Staff shared an update on the GCPS college and career academies.

 

GCPS Fine Arts Program Overview - Staff provided an overview of the GCPS fine arts program. 

 

Instructional Resources Adoption for SY2019 - Staff provided an overview of the proposed instructional resources for grades K-12 fine arts education, grades 9 -12 career and technical education, and science education.

 

McClure Health Science High School Update - Staff shared an update on McClure Health Science High School with the Board of Education.

 

Summer 2019 - Staff shared an update of activities scheduled during the 2019 summer.

 

Comprehensive Support and Improvement (CSI) / Targeted Support and Improvement (TSI) Schools Update - Staff  shared information about the district's CSI and TSI schools with the Board of Education.

 

2018 Financial Efficiency Star Rating - Staff shared an update on the Georgia Department of Education's release of the 2018 Financial Efficiency Star Rating.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff reviewed with the Board education-related legislation that is being considered in the 2019 session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

2nd Quarter Discipline Report for SY2019 - Staff shared the 2nd Quarter Discipline Report with the Board.
