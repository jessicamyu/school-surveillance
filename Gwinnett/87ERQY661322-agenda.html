The Committee received such a request on April 15, 2009. The procedures outlined in Article 13 of the GRS Plan Document call for the Committee to promptly notify the Board of Education of a request for a review. The Board then decides whether it or the Committee shall handle the review, and notifies the Committee and the claimant. Should the Board decide to allow the Committee to decide the claim, all such decisions are final, conclusive, and binding upon all parties. The claimant is advised of the disposition of the claim within 60 days after receipt of the request for review, or within 120 days if a hearing is held.
