Subject
5.08 Gwinnett Retirement System – Review of Claim
Meeting
Oct 20, 2011 - Monthly Business Meeting - 7 p.m.
Category
5. CONSENT AGENDA
Type
Action (Consent)

 

Prepared by:  Rick Cost, Chief Financial Officer

 

Presented by:  Rick Cost 

 

Background:  Within 60 days after notice of denial of a claim for Long Term Disability (LTD) benefits under the Gwinnett Retirement System (GRS) Plan, a participant may request a review of the denial by filing written notice with the Plan Administrative Committee.  The Committee received such a request on October 3, 2011. 

 

The claim involves a second denial of the participant’s application for LTD benefits by the Director, based on the information submitted with the original application and a subsequent review of the participant’s medical documentation by an independent physician.

 

The procedures outlined in Article 8 of the GRS Plan Document call for the Committee to promptly notify the Board of Education of a request for a review.  The Board then decides whether it or the Committee shall handle the review, and notifies the Committee and the claimant.  Should the Board decide to allow the Committee to decide the claim, all such decisions are final, conclusive, and binding upon all parties.  The claimant is advised of the disposition of the claim within 45 days after receipt of the request for review.

 

Financial impact:  None.

 

Recommendation:  That the Board of Education adopt a motion to allow the Gwinnett Retirement System Plan Administrative Committee to handle review of the request received by the Committee on October 3, 2011 regarding denial of LTD benefits claim.
