Gwinnett County Board of Education
Special Board Meeting - November 15, 2007

Purpose: A redistricting process is underway in Gwinnett County Public Schools to create attendance boundaries for new schools opening in 2008 and 2009. A public meeting will be held on this date to receive citizen comments regarding the proposed attendance boundaries for the following schools:

Mill Creek HS, Jones MS, Osborne MS, Duncan Creek ES, Fort Daniel ES, Freeman's Mill ES, Ivy Creek ES, Collins Hill HS, Creekland MS, Dyer ES, McKendree ES, Rock Springs ES, Taylor ES, Walnut Grove ES, Dacula ES, Magill ES, and Norton ES

The public meeting will be held from 7:00 - 9:00 p.m. in the Board Room at the Instructional Support Center, 437 Old Peachtree Road, Suwanee, Georgia 30024.

Agenda

I. Call to Order and Opening Comments – Louise Radloff, 2007 Chairman

II. Redistricting Process Overview - CEO/Superintendent J. Alvin Wilbanks

III. Comments from the Public

Citizen Comments by Cluster
Mill Creek Cluster – 20 minutes
Mountain View Cluster – 20 minutes
Rosebud ES – 20 minutes
Mill Creek Cluster – 20 minutes
Mountain View Cluster – 20 minutes
Redistricting Comments on Other Clusters – 15 minutes

IV. Closing Comments – Mrs. Radloff


Information for Speakers:
- Speakers will be called based on the order in which they signed up.
- All speakers should speak from the podium.
- Each speaker will be limited to three minutes.
- Large groups are encouraged to designate a spokesman to deliver their comments.
