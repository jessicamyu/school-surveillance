Thursday, March 21, 2019
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on March 21, 2019, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

March 21, 2019, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Business and Finance - Chief Financial Officer Joseph P. Heffron provided an overview of key financial issues and topics relevant to the sound fiscal operations of the school system.

 

2019 Science, Engineering, and Innovation Fair Report - Staff shared a report about the 2019 Science, Engineering,  and Innovation Fair, which was held on February 22, 2019.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff reviewed the status of education-related legislation in the current session of the Georgia General Assembly.
