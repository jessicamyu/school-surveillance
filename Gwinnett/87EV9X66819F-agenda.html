Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on October 11, 2007, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

October 11, 2007, Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

Year 3 SPLOST II Audit Report - A review of the highlights of the Year 3 SPLOST II Audit Report.

Legislative Priorities - A presentation of updated information on legislative priorities.

Achievement Update Part II - A presentation of Part II of the Achievement Update.

Redistricting Process Update - An update on the redistricting process.

Reform Governance in Action (RGA) Update - An update on recent and future activities related to the Board's participation in the RGA Institute.

Staffing Update - A monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).
