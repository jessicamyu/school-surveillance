Subject
1.03 Local Feedback on Proposed State Rules for Individuals with Disabilities Education Improvement Act
Meeting
Jan 11, 2007 - Monthly Work Session
Category
1. Work Session Discussion Topics and Presentations
Type
Information

The Georgia Department of Education (DOE) is seeking public input on revised state rules guiding special education, conducting sessions at 11 locations around the state, including one in Gwinnett to be held on January 9, 2007, at the Instructional Support Center. The changes are being made to put state rules in full compliance with 2004 revisions to the federal Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA). After the 2004 changes to IDEA, the United States DOE spent 18 months developing federal rules and guidance for states, forwarding that information to the state DOE in October. The process of creating state rules was begun. Dr. Cindy Loe, associate superintendent for Teaching and Learning, will share GCPS feedback which was provided to the Georgia DOE at the hearing.
