Thursday, January 19, 2017
Monthly Work Session 2:30 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on January 19, 2017, will begin at 2:30 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

January 19, 2017 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Instructional Resources Adoption Process for Career and Technical Education Courses - Staff updated the Board on proposed Instructional Resources Adoption for Career and Technical Education courses.

 

Update on eCLASS C & I Course Page Online Learning - Staff updated the Board on proposed eCLASS C&I course page outline learning to be accessed during fourth snow day.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff updated the Board on legislative issues relevant to the 2017 session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

New Life Academy of Excellence Charter School Amendment - Staff shared the recommendation to extend New Life Academy of Excellence Charter School's charter by two years.  This recommendation will be presented for a vote during the evening session.
