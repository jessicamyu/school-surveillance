Thursday, November 15, 2018
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on November 15, 2018, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

November 15, 2018, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Cross-Functional Action Team Report: On-Time Student Interventions - The findings for the On-Time Student Interventions Cross-Functional Action Team were presented to the Board. The team worked to analyze current models, practices, resources, and research around student intervention programs throughout various times in the year in an effort to effectively address student learning needs prior to the end of the year assessment. 

 

Human Resources and Talent Management Board Business Oversight Workshop - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing the management and operation of the school district, the Board held a Workshop on the Human Resources and Talent Management Division as part of its November 15, 2018, Monthly Work Session. Staff provided an overview of key issues and topics relevant to recruitment, employment, and development of Gwinnett County Public Schools' workforce. 

 

GCPS' Performance on the College and Career Readiness Performance Index - Staff provided a summary of the district and school level results on the College and Career Readiness Performance Index (CCRPI). 

 

GCPS' Performance on ACT, SAT, and Advanced Placement Exams -  Staff provided a summary of the district's 2017-18 school year performance on the ACT, SAT, and Advanced Placement Exams. 

 

Gwinnett Educational Management System (GEMS) Oversight Committee Report and Recommendations - Staff presented results of the November 1, 2018, GEMS Oversight Committee review of Academic Knowledge and Skills curriculum revisions and additions, and recommendations of the committee for Board approval.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

1st Quarter Discipline Report for SY2019 - Staff shared the 1st Quarter Discipline Report with the Board. 

 

2019 Legislative Program - Staff shared the 2019 Legislative Program with the Board for their consideration.

 

Division Information - Staff shared timely and pertinent information regarding their division's work.
