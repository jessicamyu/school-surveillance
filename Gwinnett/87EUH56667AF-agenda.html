February 14, 2008, Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

New Georgia Graduation Requirements - Discussion of Georgia's high school graduation requirement changes and the implications for Gwinnett County Public Schools.

School Nutrition Program Report - Report on all cafeteria programs, and participation and financial status of each school.

Reform Governance in Action Update - Report on the activities related to the Board's participation in the Reform Governance in Action Institute.

Reading Recovery and Reading Program Update- Update on Gwinnett County Public Schools reading instruction program with an emphasis on the Reading Recovery Program.

2008 Legislative Session Information - Review of the education-related legislation that is being considered in the 2008 session of the Georgia General Assembly.

Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (application, new hires, vacancies)

Quarterly Discipline Report - Report on student discipline statistics for the second quarter of the 2007-2008 school year.

Elementary School Retrofits - Update on retrofiting the elementary schools.

Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on February 14, 2008, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.
