Thursday, February 20, 2014
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on February 20, 2014, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

February 20, 2014 Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

ITBS Testing Achievement Update - Staff updated the Board on the latest ITBS testing achievement scores.

 

eCLASS Update on Curriculum and Instruction Tool - Staff updated the Board on the use and application of the Curriculum and Instruction tool.

 

Calendar Updates - Staff presented the 2014 summer school and four-day work week schedule.

 

AdvancED Accreditation Update - Staff presented an update on the AdvancED Accreditation Progress Report including schedule for completing the report.

 

Title I Update - Staff presented an update on the Title I program. The update included the number of schools served through Title I and the program's growth.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

2014 Legislative Update - Staff reviewed with the Board education-related legislation that is being considered in the 2014 session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

2nd Quarter Discipline Report - Staff presented the Board with the 2nd Quarter Discipline Report.
