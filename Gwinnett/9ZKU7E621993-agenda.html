Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on September 17, 2015, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

September 17, 2015 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Aspiring Leader Program Cohort 9 Graduation Recognition - The Aspiring Principal Program (APP), implemented through the Quality-Plus Leader Academy, prepares Assistant Principals to move into Principal positions.  The ninth cohort of 19 graduates was presented to the Board of Education for recognition of completion of a year-long course of study.  To date, 8 members of this cohort have been appointed as principal.  A total of 147 participants have been appointed to the principal positions from the first nine cohorts of the Aspiring Principal Program.

 

Naming New School - New Norcross Cluster ESâ€‹ - Staff reviewed with the Board the proposed name for the new Norcross Cluster elementary school, slated to open August 2016.

 

Advisement and Counseling Update - Staff provided the Board with an update of the Advisement and Counseling program.

 

Enhancing Student Engagement and Achievement - Staff provided an update to the Board on Project Based Learning (PBL), Literacy Design Collaborative (LDC), and Math Design Collaborative (MDC).

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

2016 Legislative Issues - Staff updated the Board on the timeline for the presentation of the 2016 Legislative Program.

 

403(b) / 457(b) Voluntary Retirement Plan Update - Staff provided the Board with information regarding the GCPS 403(b) / 457(b) Voluntary Retirement Plan.
