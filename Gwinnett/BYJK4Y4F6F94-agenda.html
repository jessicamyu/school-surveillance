Thursday, March 18, 2021
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on March 18, 2021, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

March 18, 2021, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Board Oversight Workshop - Business and Finance - Staff provided an overview of key financial issues and topics relevant to the sound fiscal operations of the school system.

 

Comprehensive Support and Improvement (CSI) Schools Update - Staff shared a Comprehensive Support and Improvement (CSI) schools update with the Board of Education.

 

North Metro Academy of Performing Arts One-Year Extension for SY2021-2022 - Staff shared the recommendation to extend North Metro Academy of Performing Arts' charter petition for one year. This recommendation will be presented to the Board for a vote during the evening session. 

 

2021-2022 School Year Update - Staff provided an update on the 2021-2022 school year.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff updated the Board on legislative issues relevant to the 2021 session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

Policy Development - Staff reviewed the policy development process used within Gwinnett County Public Schools.
