Monthly Work Session 2:30 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on September 14, 2017, will begin at 2:30 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

1.02 Aspiring Leader Program Cohort 8 Graduation Recognition

Category
1. Discussion Topics and Presentations

The Aspiring Leader Program (ALP), implemented through the Quality-Plus Leader Academy, prepares teachers to move into entry level assistant principal, administrative intern, and community school director positions. The 64 graduates of Cohort 8 are presented to the Board of Education for the recognition of completion of the six-month-long course of study, which concluded with a summer residency. To date, 57 members (89%) of this cohort have been appointed as assistant principals or administrative interns. A total of 342 graduates (88%) have been appointed to leadership positions from the eight cohorts of the Aspiring Leader Program.
