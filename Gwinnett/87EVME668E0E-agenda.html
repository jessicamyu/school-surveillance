August 9, 2007 Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 2:00 p.m. with a tour of Oakland Meadow School:

Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The Monthly Work Session on August 9, 2007, will begin at 2:00 p.m. with a tour of the Oakland Meadow School located at 590 Old Snellville Highway in Lawrenceville. After the completion of the Work Session at Oakland Meadow School, the Board will reconvene for Executive Session in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools' Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, in Suwanee.
