Subject
4.02 35 GCPS Schools - 2011 Georgia Single Statewide Accountability System Awards
Meeting
Feb 9, 2012 - Monthly Business Meeting - 7 p.m.
Category
4. BOARD RECOGNITIONS
Type
Information

Honorees:      35 Gwinnett County Public Schools 

 

Recognition:  2011 Georgia Single Statewide Accountability System Awards

 

Awarded by:   Georgia Department of Education and Governor’s Office of Student Achievement

 

Introduced by:     Dr. Gale Hey, Deputy Superintendent

 

Background:    The Governor’s Office of Student Achievement and the Georgia Department of Education recently announced that 35  Gwinnett County schools earned a 2011 Single Statewide Accountability System Award during the 2010-2011 school year. This award recognizes schools for their continuous improvement in raising student achievement and closing achievement gaps on state curriculum tests and Adequate Yearly Progress (AYP) status.

 

The schools were recognized in two categories:

1) Highest Performance, indicating the percentage of students meeting and exceeding academic standards; and

2) Greatest Gains, indicating the greatest gains in the percentage of students meeting and exceeding academic standards. 

 

In each category, the state recognizes four award levels: Platinum, Gold, Silver, and Bronze.

Meeting
Feb 9, 2012 - Monthly Business Meeting - 7 p.m.
Category
11. ADJOURNMENT
Type

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:15 p.m. Public Forum (open session) and 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session).  If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and student discipline appeals.
