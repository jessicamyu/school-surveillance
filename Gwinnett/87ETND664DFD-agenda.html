Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on June 19, 2008, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA. The first portion of the work session business will be the annual Elementary and Middle School Gateway Review Process, which will be conducted in executive (closed) session. The Board is expected to meet in open session beginning at approximately 4 p.m. for the remainder of the June work session.
