4.03 Berkmar HS Academic Decathlon Team, Georgia Academic Decathlon State Champions
Meeting
Jul 10, 2008 - Monthly Business Meeting
Category
4. BOARD RECOGNITION
Type
Information

Honoree(s): Berkmar HS Academic Decathlon Team - (Team members: Liana Nisimova, Agni Chandora, Anu Laxmi, Andrew Harazin, Joel Mathew, Allan Marin, Mai Himedan, and Fahad Chowdhury).

Recognition: Georgia Academic Decathlon State Champions

Awarded by: Professional Association of Georgia Educators - Georgia Academic Decathlon

Introduced by: Delores Hendrix, Area Superintendent

Background: The PAGE Georgia Academic Decathlon, which began in 1987, provides incentives for average students, underachieving students, above-average students as well as academically-talented students to become interested in intellectual activities. The competition events include seven tests of academic strength, three demonstrations of communication ability and the Super Quiz Oral relay– a team event held before a live audience. Seven comprehensive tests are given in the areas of economics, mathematics, language and literature, science (or social science), music, art and the Super Quiz topic. The three communication events include speech (prepared and impromptu), interview, and a written essay. The competition concludes with the Super Quiz Oral Relay. Berkmar HS competed and won its sixth straight state title February 22-23 this year, and competed at the national event in Garden Grove, California from April 29-May 2. The team placed 19th at the national competition.

Christopher Pae and Brian Bernhardt, decathlon coaches, will accompany the honorees.

11.01 Meeting Adjourned
Meeting
Jul 10, 2008 - Monthly Business Meeting
Category
11. ADJOURNMENT
Type

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:30 p.m. Public Forum and 7:00 p.m. Open Session. If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and student discipline appeals.
