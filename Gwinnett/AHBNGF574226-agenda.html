Thursday, February 16, 2017
Monthly Work Session 2:30 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on February 16, 2017, will begin at 2:30 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

February 16, 2017 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Summer 2017 - Staff will present a report on Summer 2017 schedules which will include Summer School and four-day work week information.

 

AdvancED Accreditation Update - Staff will present information regarding the upcoming AdvancED district accreditation visit.

 

Georgia Department of Education 2016 School Climate Star Ratings and Financial Star Rating - Staff will provide an update on the Georgia Department of Education release of the 2016 School Climate Star Ratings and the Financial Star Rating.

 

Staffing Update - Staff will update the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

2nd Quarter Discipline Report - Staff will present the discipline report for the 2nd quarter of SY2016-2017.

 

Legislative Update - Staff will review with the Board education-related legislation that is being considered in the 2017 session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

North Metro Academy of Performing Arts Charter School Amendment - Staff will share the recommendation to amend the North Metro Academy of Performing Arts Charter School's charter to change the grades served from Grades K-8 to Grades K-5. This recommendation will be presented for a vote during the evening session.

 

Performance-Based Teacher Compensation System Framework - Superintendent Wilbanks will review the proposed framework for a new teacher compensation system that is based on performance. The framework, which consists of two phases, will be presented for Board approval in the evening's Business Meeting.
