7.05  New Meadowcreek Cluster Theme HS, Construction Manager At Risk, Request for Qualifications/Request for Proposal (RFQ/FRP), Evaluation and Selection Approval

 

Prepared by:  Division of Facilities and Operations      

 

Presented by: Walt Martin, interim Chief Operations Officer      

 

Background:   On Monday, March 27, 2017, at 3:00 p.m., proposals were received from ­­­­­­­­­­­­six Construction Management at Risk firms for Construction Management at Risk Services for the upcoming New Meadowcreek Cluster Theme High School project. These proposals were solicited in a Request for Qualifications/Request for Proposal (RFQ/RFP) process where each firm submitted their qualifications demonstrating their ability to successfully complete a project like this on time and within budget. Their proposals included their project team, previous project experience on similar projects, and their fee on the total construction cost of the project. 

 

All six submitting firms were invited to present their credentials, project team, and approach to the selection committee.  After these presentations, the committee scored all firms and recommends Evergreen Construction of Atlanta, Georgia, as the most qualified Construction Manager at Risk.

 

Financial Impact:   The recommended Construction Manager at Risk will work with the design and construction team to complete this project for a fee on a Guaranteed Maximum Price (GMP). The total cost will be within the current budget of the 2017 Building Program.

 

Recommendation:  That the Board of Education adopt a motion to award the contract to Evergreen Construction of Atlanta, Georgia, as recommended.

14.01  Meeting Adjourned

 

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:15 p.m. Public Forum (open session) and 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session).  If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and student discipline appeals.
