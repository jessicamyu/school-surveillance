Subject
1.01 XFAT Team: Early Childhood Education and School Readiness
Meeting
Apr 16, 2009 - Monthly Work Session
Category
1. Work Session Discussion Topics and Presentations
Type
Information

Calvin Watts, Area 3 Superintendent and Team Facilitator; Dion Jones, Principal, Rockbridge ES; Mona Roberts, Principal, Knight ES; and Janice Warren, Assistant Principal, Annistown ES; will review the cross functional action team’s charge and the research and analysis which helped formulate the team’s recommendations.

Charge: The team will review and analyze current research on the correlation between quality early childhood education and school readiness for 4-year old students’ academic and social-emotional needs, prior to entering kindergarten.

Team Objectives/Goals:
    1. Establish pilot program model(s) in GCPS to educate the maximum number of 4-year-old pre-kindergarten students.
    2. Collaborate with Georgia Bright from the Start (GBFTS) to offer pre-kindergarten programs at existing GCPS sites based upon building level support, availability of space, and GBFTS grant funding.
    3. Develop and implement a high-quality curriculum that is standards-based, developmentally appropriate, and aligned with existing GCPS curriculum, assessment and instructional standards.
    4. Continue to recruit, hire, develop, and retain pre-kindergarten instructional and support staff in a manner that is consistent with existing GCPS staffing practices.
    5. Operate and manage the pre-k program so as not to further reduce financial resources currently allotted to the GCPS K-12 program.
