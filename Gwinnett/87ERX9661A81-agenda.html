Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on May 14, 2009, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

May 14, 2009, Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Transportation - An overview of key issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system's transportation department.

Curriculum Update: Gwinnett Online Campus - An update of GCPS' Online Campus Program.

Extended Learning Opportunities XFAT Report - An update on the work of the Extended Learning Opportunities Cross Functional Action Team.

2009 Legislative Update - A review of education-relation legislation submitted to the Governor during the 2009 Session of the Georgia General Assembly.

Charter Start-up Application - Aya Academy of Excellence - Staff will review the start-up application for the Aya Academy of Excellence.

Broad Prize Site Visit - Staff will review the recent visit by Broad representatives in conjunction with the Broad Prize.

End-of-School Processes and Checklist - A report on the various processes and checklist items associated with the end of the school year.

Construction Report - Staff will report on current construction projects including those planned for the summer.

Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

Third Quarter Discipline Report - The third quarter discipline report for school year 2008-2009 will be presented.

Review of 2009 Area Board Meetings - An overview of the 2009 Area Board Meetings that were held during the month of April.
