Thursday, August 21, 2014
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on August 21, 2014, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

Following the Work Session, the Board will hold a Public Hearing on the proposed renewal of the school district's Investing in Educational Excellence (IE2) Partnership Contract for Improving Student Achievement with the Georgia Board of Education. The hearing will be held at 6:00 p.m. in the Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center, 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

August 21, 2014  Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Aspiring Leader Program Cohort 5 Graduation - Staff reported on the Aspiring Leader Program (ALP) Cohort 5 graduation.

 

Review of Summer Leadership Conference - Staff presented a report on the 37th Annual Summer Leadership Conference held June 4-6, 2014, at the Instructional Support Center. The report included information on participants, distinguished speakers, and choice sessions. Conference evaluation data was shared.

 

Title IX Gender Equity Report - Staff presented the Board with the 2013-14 Title IX Gender Equity Report.

 

Final Discipline Report for School Year 2014 - Staff presented the Board with the 2013-2014 Final Discipline Report.

 

Summer School Report - Staff presented the Board with a report of the 2014 Summer School session. The report included the number of participants, pass rates following summer school, and online course participation.

 

CRCT/EOCT Achievement Results - Staff updated the Board on achievement results from Criterion Referenced Competency Test (CRCT), and the End-of-Course Testing (EOCT).

 

Summer Staff Development/New Teacher Orientation - Staff updated the Board on summer staff development and the 2014 New Teacher Orientation which took place on July 22, 2014.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

IE2 Presentation - Partnership Contract Renewal - Staff updated the Board on the IE2 Partnership Contract Renewal with the Georgia Board of Education. The contract would allow the school system to receive continued flexibility for its schools from specified state statutes and/or rules in exchange for greater accountability for student achievement. A public hearing was held at 6:00 p.m. prior to tonight's Business Meeting.  

 

Resolution Calling for E-SPLOST Renewal Study and Building Program Development - Superintendent Wilbanks reviewed with the Board a proposed resolution related to the need to study the district's ongoing capital improvement requirements and the need for continued collection of the current one-cent sales and use tax for educational purposes.

1.02 Aspiring Leader Program Cohort 5 Graduation
Meeting
Aug 21, 2014 - Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Category
1. Discussion Topics and Presentations
Type

The Aspiring Leader Program (ALP), implemented through the Quality-Plus Leader Academy, prepares teachers to move into entry level assistant principal and administrative intern positions. The fifth cohort of 71 graduates is presented to the Board of Education for recognition of completion of the 6-month long course of study concluding with a summer school residency. To date, 41 members of this cohort have been appointed as assistant principals or administrative interns. A total of 163 participants have been appointed to leadership positions from the first five cohorts of the Aspiring Leader Program. Staff will report on the Aspiring Leader Program Cohort 5 Graduation.
