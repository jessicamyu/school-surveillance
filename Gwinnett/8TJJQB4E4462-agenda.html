Thursday, May 17, 2012
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on May 17, 2012, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

May 17, 2012 Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.

 

Online Campus Update - Staff will provide the Board with an update on Online Campus and future plans.

 

Instructional Materials Adoption Schedule - Staff members will share the anticipated instructional materials adoption schedule for each content area.

 

Alternative School Options - Staff members will provide the board with information on alternative school options.

 

Gwinnett Effectiveness Initiative - Staff will share a progress report on the pilots of the new teacher and leader evaluation systems.

 

Review of 2012 Area Board Meetings - Staff provided an overview of the 2012 Area Board Meetings, which were held in each of the five School Board districts during the months of March and April.

 

 

Staff Reports:

 

Staffing Update - A monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

 

Quarterly Discipline Report - Staff updated the Board with the third quarter discipline report for the 2011-12 school year.

 

Summer Facilities Activity Report - Staff will provide the Board with information on facilities' activity occurring during summer 2012.
