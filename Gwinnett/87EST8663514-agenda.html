The Board will hold a public hearing for the purpose of receiving input on Gwinnett County Public Schools' proposed IE2 Partnership Contract with the Georgia Board of Education. The contract includes a district strategic plan and school plans for each school in the district. The contract would allow the district to receive flexibility for its schools from specified state statutes and/or rules in exchange for greater accountability for student achievement.
