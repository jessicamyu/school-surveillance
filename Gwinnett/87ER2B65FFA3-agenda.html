Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on November 12, 2009, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

November 12, 2009, Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

Human Resources Business Oversight Workshop - Overview of key issues and topics relevant to recruitment, employment, and development of the GCPS' workforce.

403(b) / 457(b) Voluntary Retirement Plan - Information regarding proposed improvements to GCPS' 403(b) / 457(b) Voluntary Retirement Plan.

Tax Allocation Districts (TADS) - Information regarding the five TAD's recently approved by the County.

2010 Legislative Program - Review of the Board's priorities in the areas of funding and governance, as well as the Board's K-12 Continuing Positions.

Revisions to 2009-2010 GCPS Calendar -The revised calendar for the 2009-2010 school year will be presented.




Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

First Quarter Discipline Report for 2009-2010 School Year - The discipline report for the first quarter of the 2009-2010 school year will be presented.

Open Enrollment - An overview of the 2010 Open Enrollment Program will be presented.

Redistricting Update - An update on the redistricting process will be presented.

Title I Public Hearing - An update on the Title I Public Hearing.
