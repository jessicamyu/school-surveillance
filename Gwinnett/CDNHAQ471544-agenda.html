Thursday, May 19, 2022
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on May 19, 2022, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

May 19, 2022, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Monthly Discipline Report - Staff shared the monthly discipline report for the 2021-2022 school year.

 

GCPS Literacy Plan - Staff shared the district's literacy plan for how teachers and administrators will be equipped with a deep understanding of the science of reading/structured literacy practices and shared details regarding the upcoming literacy pilot of instructional resources for K-5 teachers.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Final Legislative Update for 2022 - Staff provided a final review of all education-related legislation acted upon by the Governor of the State of Georgia.

 

Division Information - Staff shared timely and pertinent information regarding their division's work.

 

Policy BCBI - Public Participation at Board Meetings - Staff updated the Board on changes that have been made in accordance with policy guidance and/or mandates in state and/or federal law.

 

Policy JCD - Student Conduct Behavior - Staff updated the Board on changes that have been made in accordance with policy guidance and/or mandates in state and/or federal law.
