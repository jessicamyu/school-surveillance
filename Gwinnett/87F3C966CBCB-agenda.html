Subject
7.05 Adoption of Resolution Calling for Election
Meeting
Jul 13, 2006 - Regular Meeting
Category
7. SUPERINTENDENT’S REPORTS
Type
Action
Prepared by: J. Alvin Wlibanks, CEO/Superintendent

Presented by: J. Alvin Wilbanks, CEO/Superintendent

Background: The current one-cent sales tax for schools expires on June 30, 2007. Due to Gwinnett County Public Schools' continued enrollment growth over the past two decades, and the projected continuation of that growth for the foreseeable future, new schools and other capital improvements for the additional students are an ongoing, pressing need.

Recommendation: That the Board of Education adopt a resolution calling for an election to be held on November 7, 2006, for the purpose of asking Gwinnett County voters to approve extending the current special purpose local option sales tax for educational purposes for another five years. The SPLOST would be in effect from July 1, 2007 through June 30, 2012, if approved.

11.01 Meeting Adjourned
Meeting
Jul 13, 2006 - Regular Meeting
Category
11. ADJOURNMENT
Type
The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:30 p.m. Public Forum and 7:00 p.m. Open Session. If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and student discipline appeals.
