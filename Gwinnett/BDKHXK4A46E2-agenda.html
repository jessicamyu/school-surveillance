Thursday, July 18, 2019
Monthly Work Session 3:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on July 18, 2019, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

July 18, 2019, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Construction and Facilities Management Board Oversight Workshop - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board held a workshop on Construction and Facilities Management as part of its July 18, 2019, Monthly Work Session. CEO/Superintendent J. Alvin Wilbanks and Chief Operations Officer Walt Martin provided an overview of key issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system's construction and facilities management.

 

McClure Health Science High School Update - Staff provided an update on McClure Health Science High School.

 

Elementary Curriculum Directors Update - Staff provided a report on the role of elementary curriculum directors in their support of teaching and learning.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

3DE by Junior Achievement - Staff provided a report on 3DE by Junior Achievement.
