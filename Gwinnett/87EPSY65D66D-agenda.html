Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on July 15, 2010, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

July 15, 2010, Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Construction and Facilities Management - An overview of key issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system in the areas of construction and facilities management.

Summer Leadership Conference Report - A report on the 33rd Annual Summer Leadership Conference including participants, speakers, choice sessions, and evaluation data.

Annual Yearly Progress (AYP) Update - An update of recent developments and results relating to AYP.

Governor's Honors Program - An update on GCPS high school students' participation in the 2010 Governor's Honors Program.





Staffing Update - A monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).
