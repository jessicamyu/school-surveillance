Thursday, March 21, 2013
Monthly Work Session 2 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on March 21, 2013, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

March 21, 2013 Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Business and Finance -  The Department of Business and Finance held a workshop for Board members in conjunction with its responsibility for the Board overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems. CEO/Superintendent J. Alvin Wilbanks and Chief Financial Officer Rick Cost provided an overview of key financial issues and topics relevant to the sound fiscal operations of the school system. The overview focused on key financial information that is included in the June 30, 2012 Comprehensive Annual Financial Report (CAFR), the FY2012 SPLOST III Audit and Review Report and the 2012 Annual Investment Oversight Committee Report. 

 

GEMS Oversight Committee Recommendations -  Staff presented the recommendations of the GEMS Oversight Committee to the Board for their information and discussion, if needed.  The GEMS Oversight Committee currently meets once a year to review curriculum items for update/addition.

 

Board Members' Meeting with Teachers' Advisory Council (TAC) - Following their meetings with TAC representatives from schools in their district, Board members and TAC representatives gathered in the Board Room where Board members reported on the conversations just held with those representatives.

 

 

Staff Reports:

 

Staffing Update - A monthly report on personnel matters was presented to the Board.

 

Legislative Update - Staff updated the Board on the legislative issues relevant to the 2013 session of the Georgia General Assembly.
