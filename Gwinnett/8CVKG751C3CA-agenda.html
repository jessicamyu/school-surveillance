Monthly Work Session
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on March 17, 2011, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

March 17, 2011, Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 2:00 p.m. 

 

eCLASS Update - Staff will update the Board on the district's initiative called eCLASS.

 

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Business and Finance - Staff will provide an overview of key financial issues and topics relevant to the sound fiscal operations of the school system.

 

Board Members' Meeting with Teachers' Advisory Council (TAC) - Following their meetings with TAC representatives from schools in their district, Board members and TAC representatives will gather in the Board Room where Board members will report-out on conversations just held with those representatives.

 

 

 

Staff Reports:

 

Staffing Update - A monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

 

Legislative Update - Staff will review the status of education-related legislation in the current session of the Georgia General Assembly. 

 

GEMS Oversight Committee Report - Staff will present a report on the February 24, 2011 meeting of the GEMS Oversight Committee.

 

Vision for Public Education - Staff will update the Board on the district's activities in regard to this project.

In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board will hold a workshop on Finance as part of its March 17, 2011 Monthly Work Session which will begin at 2 p.m.  CEO/Superintendent J. Alvin Wilbanks and Chief Financial Officer Rick A. Cost will provide an overview of key financial issues and topics relevant to the sound fiscal operations of the school system.  The overview will be focused on key financial information included in the June 30, 2010 Comprehensive Annual Financial Report (CAFR), the FY2010 SPLOST III Audit and Review Report and the 2010 Annual Investment Oversight Committee Report.
