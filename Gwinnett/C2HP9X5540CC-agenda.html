Thursday, May 20, 2021
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on May 20, 2021, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

May 20, 2021, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Transportation - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board held a workshop on Transportation as part of its May 20, 2021, Monthly Work Session. CEO/Superintendent J. Alvin Wilbanks and Chief Operations Officer Walt Martin provided an overview of key issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system's transportation department. 

 

Summer Staff Development - Staff provided an overview of 2021 Summer Staff Development.

 

New Teacher Orientation - Staff provided an overview of 2021 New Teacher Orientation.

 

Cross-Functional Action Team (XFAT) Report - Post COVID-19 Learning Models - The XFAT team shared findings from their research on Post COVID-19 Learning Models. 

 

Cross-Functional Action Team (XFAT) Report - Human Resources and Talent Management Staffing - The XFAT team shared findings from their research on Human Resources and Talent Management Staffing. 

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislate Update - Staff reviewed the status of education-related legislation in the current session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

Charter School Update - Staff provided the Board with a Charter School update.

 

Return to Learning Update for School Year 2021-2022 - Staff presented a return to learning update for the 2021-2022 school year.  

 

3rd Quarter Discipline Report for SY2021 - Staff presented the 3rd quarter discipline report for the 2020-2021 school year.

1.05 Cross-Functional Action Team (XFAT) Report - Post COVID-19 Learning Models
Meeting
May 20, 2021 - Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Category
1. Discussion Topics and Presentations
Type
Information

Cross-Functional Action Teams, which are also known as XFAT teams, address issues of concern in the district which have been determined by the Superintendent, cabinet members, as well as other leaders within the district. XFAT teams meet for up to 90 days and are made up of a cross-section of district and school representatives. The team utilizes guiding concepts, principles, and practices as a way to create and sustain a culture committed to continuous improvement. This XFAT team will share findings on Post COVID-19 Learning Models.
