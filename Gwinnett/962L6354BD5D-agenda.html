Thursday, April 18, 2013
Monthly Work Session - 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on April 18, 2013, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

April 18, 2013 Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

XFAT Report: Initiating and Sustaining Innovative Instructional Programs - Staff presented the findings of a cross-functional action team regarding initiating and sustaining innovative instructional programs.

 

Grades 6-12 Mathematics Core Materials and Supplemental Materials Review - Staff provided the Board with an overview of instructional materials in view of adoption. In compliance with GCPS Policy IFAA and the Georgia Department of Education Rule 160-4-1.12, the instructional materials review was conducted for mathematics texts for grades 6-12 and nonfiction language arts texts for grades K-5 to align to Common Core.  Supplemental materials were approved the the Community Health Advisory Committee (CHEAC), as required.

 

 XFAT REPORT:  Middle School Promotion Criteria - As a component of the GCPS commitment to continuous quality improvement, this Cross Functional Action Team studied the current Middle School Promotion Criteria, and members of the team reported on the findings and recommendations.  Area Superintendent Nancy Martin presented the members of the Cross Functional Action Team to the Board.

 

Achievement Update for 2012 ITBS, PSAT, and GHSWT and 2013 8th Grade Writing Test - Staff provided the Board with an update of testing achievement for 2012 ITBS, PSAT, and GHSWT, as well as the 2013 8th grade writing test.

 

Review of 2013 Area Board Meetings - Staff provided an overview of the 2013 Area Board Meetings, which were held in each of the five School Board districts during the months of March and April.

 

 

 

Staff Reports:

 

Staffing Update - A monthly report on personnel matters was presented to the Board.

 

Legislative Update and Impact Report - Staff reviewed with the Board the status of education-related legislation in the current session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

Investing in Educational Excellence (IE2) Contract Amendment - Staff updated the Board on the Investing in Educational Excellence (IE2) partnership contract amendment.

 

 Testing Update - Staff provided the Board with information regarding the process used in GCPS to administer standardized tests.

 

Special Education Services and Organizational Partnerships - Staff updated the Board on the current partnerships between the GCPS Department of Special Education and various external agencies.
