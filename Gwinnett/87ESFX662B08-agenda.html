February 12, 2009 Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

Curriculum Update: Foreign Language - Staff briefed the Board on GCPS' Foreign Language Program.

Curriculum Update: Gifted and Talented Education - Staff reported to the Board on GCPS' Gifted and Talented Education Program.

Legislative Session Information - Staff reviewed the education-related legislation being considered in the 2009 Georgia General Assembly.

Update on Automated External Defibrillators (AEDs) - Staff updated the Board on the use of AEDs in Gwinnett schools.




Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

Quarterly Discipline Report - Staff presented the discipline report for the second quarter of the 2008-2009 school year.

Update on Naming New Schools - Staff updated the Board on the process now underway for naming new schools opening in 2010.

Redistricting Timeline - Staff presented the timeline for the school redistricting process that will begin in the fall of 2009.

High School Summer School Sites and Tuition - Staff announced the high school summer school sites and briefed the Board on the increase in tuition for the summer session.

Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on February 12, 2009 will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.
