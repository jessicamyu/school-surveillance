6.03 GCPSâ€™s Voluntary Retirement Savings Plans (403(b) and 457(b))
Meeting
Mar 17, 2011 - Monthly Business Meeting
Category
6. CONSENT AGENDA
Type
Action (Consent)

Prepared by:  Tom Daymude, Director, Gwinnett Retirement System

 

Presented by:  Rick A. Cost, Chief Financial Officer

 

Background:  The United States Department of Treasury Regulations governing the administration of GCPS’s voluntary retirement savings plans – the 403(b) and the 457(b) plans – allow for employers to automatically enroll new employees in the plans provided certain conditions are met.  The auto-enrollment option is becoming increasingly popular with both corporate and governmental employers as they seek ways to address the need for additional retirement savings, particularly among lower paid employees and those who retire early or work only part of their careers with the employer.  VALIC, as the GCPS plan administrator, would serve as the investment service provider under the auto-enrollment option.

 

The regulations also authorize GCPS to offer both Roth and non-Roth savings accounts under the plans, to enable participating employees to select from a full range of choices with regard to the tax treatment of both contributions and withdrawals from the plans.

 

Financial impact:  None.  All plans are funded by employee voluntary contributions. 

 

Recommendation:  That the Board approve amendments to the Plan Documents as follows:

  • GCPS 403(b) Plan Document:  amend to enable automatic enrollment of all new employees into the Plan and offering of Roth accounts effective July 1, 2011.
  • GCPS 457(b) Plan Document:  amend to enable offering of Roth accounts effective July 1, 2011.

Mar 17, 2011 - Monthly Business Meeting
Category
11. ADJOURNMENT
Type
Information

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:30 p.m. Public Forum (open session) and 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session). If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.
