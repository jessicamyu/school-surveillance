Monthly Work Session - 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on October 17, 2013, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

1.04 Dual Language Immersion Overview
Meeting
Oct 17, 2013 - Monthly Work Session - 3 p.m.
Category
1. Discussion Topics and Presentations
Type
Information

Jon Valentine, Director of Foreign Language will present information on the development of dual language programs. These innovative programs will support the growing need expressed by our partners in business and industry for a workforce possessing the advanced-level linguistic and cultural skills necessary to compete and lead in an increasingly global society. The GCPS Dual Language Immersion Program will utilize a fifty-fifty model, in which students spend half of their school day in the target language and the other half-day in English. Programs will begin in kindergarten utilizing a two-teacher model - one who instructs exclusively in the target language for half of the day and a second who teaches in English for the remainder of the day. In the 2014-2015 school year, three GCPS schools will implement the district's first dual language immersion programs.
