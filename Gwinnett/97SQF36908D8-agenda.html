Thursday, June 20, 2013
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on June 20, 2013, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

June 20, 2013 Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

Gwinnett Leader Effectiveness System - Staff presented to the Board an update on the Gwinnett Leader Effectiveness System.

 

36th Annual Summer Leadership Conference - Staff presented a report to the Board on the 36th Annual Summer Leadership Conference that was held June 5-7, 2013, at the Instructional Support Center. The report included information on participants, distinguished speakers, and choice sessions. Evaluation data on the conference was shared.

 

Community-Based Mentoring Program - Staff presented the Board with an update of the Community-Based Mentoring Program.

 

 

 

Staff Reports:

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with the current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Awarding Credit Rule Update - Staff presented the Board with an update on the awarding credit rule.
