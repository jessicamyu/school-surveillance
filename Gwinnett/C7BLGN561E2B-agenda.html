Thursday, October 21, 2021
Monthly Work Session 1:30 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on October 21, 2021, will begin at 1:30 p.m. with a tour of the division of Information Management and Technology, located in Building 200 at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia. The Board will return to the Executive Board Room for the remainder of the Work Session, which will begin at 2:00 p.m.

October 21, 2021, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Board Oversight Workshop - Information Management and Technology, and Information Security - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board will hold a workshop on Information Management and Technology as part of its October 21, 2021, Monthly Work Session. Chief Information Officer Frank Elmore will provide a tour of the Division of Information Management and Technology with an emphasis on technology use in the classroom. Information Security Officer Arden Peterkin will brief the Board on Information Security. 

 

Preview of Gwinnett Educational Management System (GEMS) Oversight Committee and Instructional Resource Process - Staff provided a preview of the Gwinnett Educational Management System (GEMS) Oversight Committee and the Instructional Resource Process.

 

Social Emotional Learning and Student Supports Update - Staff updated the Board on social-emotional learning and student supports that are in place for all Gwinnett County Public Schools students.

 

Monthly Discipline Report - Staff presented the monthly discipline report for the 2021-22 school year. 

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff presented the legislative priorities for 2022.

 

Calendar Development - Staff provided an overview of the school calendar development process.

 

Division Information - Staff shared timely and pertinent information regarding their division's work.

 

Board Discussion: After Action Review - The Board engaged in an after action review.
