Thursday, November 17, 2016
Monthly Work Session 2:30 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on November 17, 2016 will begin at 2:30 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

November 17, 2016 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Career Connections 2016 - Staff will provide an overview of Career Connections 2016, held at the Infinite Energy Center October 28, 2016, for eighth grade students of Academy feeder schools.

 

Fine Arts Programs Update - Staff will provide an update of programs and initiatives offered through the Fine Arts Department.

 

GEMS Report and Recommendations - Staff will present information from the November 3, 2016 GEMS Oversight Review, as well as recommended changes to the AKS from the GEMS Oversight Review Committee.

 

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

1st Quarter Discipline Report for SY2017 - Staff provided the first quarter Discipline Report for the Board of Education.

 

Legislative Program - Staff will present a draft of the 2017 Legislative Program for the Board's consideration.
