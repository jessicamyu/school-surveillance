Thursday, September 23, 2021
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on September 23, 2021, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

September 23, 2021, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

School Improvement and Operations Board Oversight Workshop  - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board will hold a workshop on School Improvement and Operations as part of its September 23, 2021, Monthly Work Session. Associate Superintendent, Dr. Al Taylor, will provide an overview of topics relevant to school improvement and operations within the district.  

 

Strategic Waivers School System (SWSS) Update - Add Schools to Contract - Amendment #5 - Staff shared the Strategic Waivers School System (SWSS) contract amendment #5 to add McClure Health Science High School and North Metro Academy of Performing Arts to the existing contract. 

 

New Life Academy of Excellence - Charter School Update - Staff provided a charter school update for New Life Academy of Excellence.

 

GCPS Tutoring Services Bid Process - Staff provided an overview of the Tutoring Services Bid Process.

 

GCPS COVID-19 Reporting Update - Staff provided an update regarding COVID-19 case reporting for the 2021-2022 school year. 

 

Reapportionment/Redistricting - Staff updated the Board on the local redistricting process as a result of the 2020 Census.  

 

Policy Update: Executive Session Guidelines - Staff updated the Board on Executive Session guidelines.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Division Information - Staff shared share timely and pertinent information regarding their division's work.

 

Board Discussion: Board Meeting After Action Review (August Board Meeting) - Board Members engaged in an after action review of the August Board Meeting.
