Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. 1.01 Improving Employee Attendance Cross-Functional Action Team
Meeting
Apr 17, 2008 - Monthly Work Session
Category
1. Work Session Discussion Topics and Presentations
Type
Information


Jorge Gomez, Team Facilitator, Joe Ahrens, Principal – Arcado ES, Barbara Desing, Media Specialist – Alcova ES, Dr. Charlotte Sadler, Data Administrator – Winn-Holt ES, and Christina Wimmer, Assistant Principal – Kanoheda ES, will review the cross functional action team’s charge and the research and analysis which helped formulate the team’s recommendations.

Charge: The team will examine employee attendance and develop strategies to motivate and increase the number of days present for all employees.

Team Objectives/Goals:

1. Recommend strategies and "best practices" that increase employee attendance.
2. Identify attendance practices that increase the number of days present by all employees and ensure the continuity and quality of instruction, minimizing any negative impact on the learning process.
3. Recommend improvements in the "culture" of the work environment to strengthen and develop an "employee attendance climate."

Apr 17, 2008 - Monthly Work Session
Category
1. Work Session Discussion Topics and Presentations

Since July 2006, the Gwinnett County Board of Education has been participating in a two-year, invitation-only program called "Reform Governance in Action." RGA is conducted by The Center for Reform of School Systems (CRSS) and is funded by The Broad Foundation. Its focus is on improving large, urban school districts through effective governance by the Board-Superintendent team. The two-year program will conclude in July 2008.

CRSS has extended an invitation to the Gwinnett County Board of Education to continue its work with RGA through July 2010 as a participant in the RGA Sustainability Program. Staff will brief the Board on the plans and expectations related to the continued participation in RGA.
