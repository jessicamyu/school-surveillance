1.02 eCLASS Update
Meeting
Feb 9, 2012 - Monthly Work Session - 2 p.m.
Category
1. Discussion Topics and Presentations
Type
Information

Prepared by:  Tricia Kennedy, Executive Director for eCLASS Transformation

 

Background: On November 8, 2011, Gwinnett voters approved the extension of the Special Purpose Local Option Sales Tax (SPLOST) for education.  With nearly 50% of the money collected from the SPLOST extension allocated for technology projects, the approval paved the way for the district's eCLASS initiative --  a digital content, learning, assessment and support system. eCLASS will bring together curriculum and instructional resources, assessment items, professional development, student information and gradebook functions in one place, making it easier for teachers to access tools that will help them improve student learning.  This integrated enterprise solution is a multi-year initiative.  The pilot is set to begin in August 2012 with schools in the archer, Berkmar, Duluth, North Gwinnett and Shiloh clusters.  

 

Staff will bring an update on the current status of the pilot preparation.
