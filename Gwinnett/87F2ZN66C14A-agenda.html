Subject
10.01 Naming of Brookwood HS Sports Facilities
Meeting
Oct 12, 2006 - Monthly Business Meeting
Category
10. NEW BUSINESS
Type

Prepared by: Jean Walker, Executive Director for Academic Achievement

Presented by: J. Alvin Wilbanks, CEO/Superintendent

Background: Acting on behalf of the school and community, Jane Stegall, principal of Brookwood HS, has requested that the sporting facilities on the campus of Brookwood HS be named "The Dave Hunter Athletic Complex." Dave Hunter has been the athletic director at Brookwood HS since 1987. He has submitted his retirement to take effect December 21, 2006. The Brookwood PTSA, Brookwood School Council, Brookwood Touchdown Club, Brookwood Soccer Club, Brookwood Dugout Club, Brookwood Track and Cross Country Club, Brookwood Youth Football Association, Brookwood Diamond Club, and Brookwood Band are unanimous in requesting the Board of Education’s approval of this name change to the facilities.

The purpose of the request is to dedicate the sporting facilities at Brookwood HS’s football stadium, baseball stadium and field house to be called “The Dave Hunter Athletic Complex.” Attached below is an outline of the specific signage for each of the athletic facilities. The entire sporting complex at Brookwood HS would be named in honor of Dave Hunter.

The dedication of the sports complex would be done during halftime at the Brookwood vs. South Gwinnett football game on Friday, October 20, 2006.

Financial impact: None

Recommendation: That the Board of Education name the sports facilities on the campus of Brookwood HS in honor of long-time employee Dave Hunter and be called "The Dave Hunter Athletic Complex."

Meeting
Oct 12, 2006 - Monthly Business Meeting
Category
11. ADJOURNMENT
Type

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:30 p.m. Public Forum and 7:00 p.m. Open Session. If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and student discipline appeals.
