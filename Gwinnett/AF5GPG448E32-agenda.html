Monthly Work Session 2:30 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on December 15, 2016, will begin at 2:30 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

December 15, 2016 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing the management and operation of the school district, on December 15, 2016, the Board held Business Oversight Workshops on:

  • Human Resources and Talent Management Division -Staff provided an overview of key issues and topics relevant to recruitment, employment, and development of Gwinnett County Public Schools' workforce.
  • School Nutrition Program - Staff provided the Board with information on the School Nutrition Program, which was held in order to keep the Board informed regarding the operations and financial status of the program.

 

GCPS' Performance on College and Career Readiness Performance Index (CCRPI) - Staff updated the Board with a summary of district and school level results on the College and Career Readiness Performance Index (CCRPI).

 

Governor's Office of Student Achievement (GOSA) Grants for STEM and Gear Up for Graduation - Staff provided an update on the Governor's Office of Student Achievement (GOSA) grants for STEM and Gear Up for Graduation.

 

Early Learning and School Readiness - Staff provided the Board with an overview of programs supporting early learning and school readiness.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Emergency Closings/Inclement Weather Procedures - Staff updated the Board on the process and procedures followed in regard to the cancelation of school or early dismissal due to inclement weather.
