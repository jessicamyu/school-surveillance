Thursday, October 15, 2020
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on October 15, 2020, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

October 15, 2020, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

2021 Employee Benefits Open Enrollment - Staff provided an overview of the 2021 Employee Benefits Open Enrollment Program which will begin October 19, 2020, and close November 6, 2020.

 

Redistricting Process Overview - Staff provided an overview of the district's redistricting process for establishing school attendance boundaries.

 

Health Response Teams Update - Staff provided an update on the district's Health Response Teams. 

 

Process for Second Semester Parent Choice of In-Person or Digital Instruction

 

School Nutrition Program Update

 

Assessment Update: 2019-2020 SAT and Advanced Placement Results - Staff provided SAT and Advanced Placement Results for the 2019-2020 school year.

 

Overview of ParentVUE Section of the New Parent Portal - Staff provided an overview of the ParentVUE section of the new Parent Portal. 

 

Cybersecurity Threat Landscape - Staff briefed the Board on recent cybersecurity issues impacting organizations, and the measures employed to reduce the risk of cyber attacks in Gwinnett County Public Schools. 

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update

 

2021-2022 School Year Calendar Development - Staff provided an overview of the 2021-2022 school year calendar development process.
