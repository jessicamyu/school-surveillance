Thursday, August 21, 2014
Executive Session
Executive Session will be held for the purpose of discussing or deliberating upon the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action or dismissal, or periodic evaluation or rating of a public employee or officer; considering student discipline appeals, discussing and considering threatened and/or potential litigation with the Board attorney; and/or discussing the future acquisition of real estate. The Board will recess Executive Session for the public forum and the Monthly Business Meeting. If there is remaining business to be conducted in Executive Session, the Board will reconvene in the Executive Board Room following the 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting.
