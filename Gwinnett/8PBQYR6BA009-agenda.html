Mar 15, 2012 - Monthly Business Meeting - 7 p.m.
Category
6. SUPERINTENDENT'S REPORTS
Type
Information

Prepared by:  Teaching and Learning Support

 

Presented by:  Dr. Gale Hey, Deputy Superintendent

 

Background:  The Junior Leadership Corps (JLC) program was launched in January 2011 at two Gwinnett schools in the Norcross Cluster, Summerour MS and Pinckneyville MS. The program is designed to develop successful, productive leaders, starting in the middle school years. Originally established through a partnership between the US Army and the National Association of State Boards of Education, JLC now is solely operated under the auspices of Gwinnett County Public Schools. Principals Dorothy Jarrett (Summerour MS) and Wanda Yeargin (Pinckneyville MS) and JLC cadets from their schools will brief the Board on the successful first year of the JLC program and its growing effectiveness and popularity in year two.

 

Financial impact:  None.

 

Recommendation:  Information item

11. ADJOURNMENT
Subject
11.01 Meeting Adjourned
Meeting
Mar 15, 2012 - Monthly Business Meeting - 7 p.m.
Category
11. ADJOURNMENT
Type

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:15 p.m. Public Forum (open session) and 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session).  If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and student discipline appeals.
