Thursday, February 19, 2015
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on February 19, 2015, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

February 19, 2015 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Advanced Placement U.S. History Update - CEO/Superintendent provided an update on Advanced Placement U.S. History.

 

Special Education Update - Staff provided an overview of special education services to the Board.

 

Instructional Materials/Textbook Adoption Update - Staff informed the Board on new instructional resources toward approval.

 

Summer 2015 Update -  Staff provided dates and information for summer 2015, including summer school and four day work week for local schools.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

2015 Legislative Update - Staff reviewed with the Board education-related legislation that is being considered in the 2015 session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

2nd Quarter Discipline Report - Staff provided the 2nd Quarter Discipline Report to the Board.

 

Facility Update - Staff updated the Board on facility naming and program information at 1221 Northbrook Parkway, Suwanee, GA 30024.

 

Future High School Plans - Staff briefed the Board on a proposal for two high schools in the Meadowcreek and Norcross clusters.
