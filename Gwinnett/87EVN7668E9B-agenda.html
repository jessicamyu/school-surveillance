4. BOARD RECOGNITION
Type
Information

Honorees: Kathy Jones, Camp Creek ES, and Dr. LaVern Watkins, Norcross ES (moving to another district for 2007-2008)

Recognition: High Performance Principals, State of Georgia

Awarded by: Governor Sonny Perdue and the Georgia Department of Education

Introduced by: Dr. Cindy Loe, Associate Superintendent for Teaching and Learning

Background: Governor Sonny Perdue and State Superintendent of Schools Kathy Cox have announced the names of individuals who are being identified as "High Performance Principals," as defined by Senate Bill 468, which was passed and funded during the 2006 General Assembly. According to the law, “the sole criteria for designating and selecting individuals as High Performance Principals shall be data-based evidence of the effectiveness of a proposed High Performance Principal in improving a low-performing school or in taking an average- or excellent-performing school to higher achievement within the last five years." This program provides a grant to encourage those recognized to become principals in secondary "Needs Improvement" schools. It also serves as an opportunity to recognize and honor the individual and collective achievements of these high-performing educators. These principals are two of the eight Gwinnett County Public Schools' high-performance principals named this year.

11.01 Meeting Adjourned
Meeting
Jul 12, 2007 - Monthly Business Meeting
Category
11. ADJOURNMENT
Type

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:30 p.m. Public Forum and 7:00 p.m. Open Session. If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and student discipline appeals.
