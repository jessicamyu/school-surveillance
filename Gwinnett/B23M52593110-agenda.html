Thursday, July 19, 2018
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on July 19, 2018, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

July 19, 2018, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Construction and Facilities Management Board Oversight Workshop - Staff provided an overview of key issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system's construction and facilities management.

 

2018 Summer Leadership Conference Report - Staff presented a report on the 2018 Summer Leadership Conference held June 6 - 8, 2018, at the Classic Center in Athens. 

 

International Transition Center and Paul Duke STEM High School Update - Staff presented updates on the International Transition Center and Paul Duke STEM High School.

 

Girls Flag Football Update - NFL FLAG Partnership - Staff provided an update on girls flag football, which included the NFL FLAG partnership.

 

On-Boarding Curriculum for New Gwinnett County Public Schools Teachers - Staff reviewed the on-boarding curriculum for new Gwinnett County Public Schools teachers.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Division Information - Staff shared pertinent information with the Board regarding their division's work.
