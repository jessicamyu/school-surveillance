Thursday, October 16, 2014
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on October 16, 2014, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

October 16, 2014 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Division of Information Management and Technology - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board held a workshop on Information Management and Technology as part of its October 16, 2014 Monthly Work Session. Staff provided an overview of key issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system.

 

Programs at the New Berkmar/Central Gwinnett Clusters Relief High School - The Board heard a report on the programs, in particular the entrepreneurship program, that will be components of the new high school opening in August 2015, to relieve the Berkmar and Central Gwinnett clusters.

 

Assessment Update - Staff updated the Board on assessment development and implementation.

 

Redistricting Update - Staff briefed the Board on the process for redistricting schools that began on October 6 and will conclude at the December Monthly Business Meeting of the Board.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Employee Benefits Open Enrollment - Staff reviewed changes to the 2015 Open Enrollment Program which will begin October 27, 2014, and close November 14, 2014.

 

2015 Legislative Program - Staff presented a draft of the 2015 Legislative Program for the Board's consideration.

 

Update on New IE2 Contract -  Staff updated the Board on the new IE2 contract.
