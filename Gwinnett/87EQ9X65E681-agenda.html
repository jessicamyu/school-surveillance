Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on April 15, 2010, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

1.01 XFAT Team: Academic Rigor
Meeting
Apr 15, 2010 - Monthly Work Session
Category
1. Work Session Discussion Topics and Presentations
Type
Information

Dr. Gale Hey, Area Superintendent and Facilitator; and team members will review the cross functional action team’s charge and the research and analysis which helped formulate the team’s recommendations. The team's charge was to review and analyze current research as it pertains to academic rigor and its relation to college preparedness and post academic success.

Team Objectives/Goals:
      1. Review current and relevant literature that defines academic rigor
      2. Determine current status of academic rigor in GCPS
      3. Provide recommendations to improve academic rigor in GCPS
