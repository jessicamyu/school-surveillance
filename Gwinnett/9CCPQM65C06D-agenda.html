Thursday, December 19, 2013
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on December 19, 2013, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

December 19, 2013 Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

District Leader Program Cohort One Graduation - The District Leader Program (DLP), implemented through the Quality-Plus Leader Academy, is designed to orient and instruct leaders in the knowledge, skills, and talents needed to become a Quality-Plus district level leader in Gwinnett County Public Schools, and to provide an opportunity for leaders to improve their performance and increase individual capacity. Staff presented the inaugural class of 12 graduates to the Board f Education for recognition of completion of a year-long course of study.

 

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Transportation - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board held a workshop on Transportation. Staff provided an overview of key issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system's transportation department.

 

Mathematics Instructional Materials Adoption - Staff provided the Board with an overview of mathematics instructional materials under consideration for adoption. In compliance with Gwinnett County Public Schools Policy IFAA and the Georgia Department of Education Rule 160-4-1.12, the instructional materials review was conducted for mathematics texts for grades K-5, Number Theory and Multivariable Calculus.

 

Georgia High School Writing Test Results - Staff updated the Board on the most recent student achievement statistics for the Georgia High School Writing Test.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Emergency Closings/Inclement Weather Procedures - Staff updated the Board on the process and procedures followed in regard to the cancellation of school or early dismissal due to inclement weather.
