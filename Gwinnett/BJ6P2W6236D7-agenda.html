The Aspiring Principal Program (APP), implemented through the Quality-Plus Leader Academy, prepares assistant principals to move into principal positions. The 13th cohort of 14 graduates is presented to the Board of Education and recognized for completion of a year-long course of study. To date, six members of this cohort have been appointed as principal, one was appointed Director of School Staffing for Middle Schools in the Human Resources and Talent Management division. A total of 215 participants have been appointed to leadership positions from the 13 cohorts of the Aspiring Principal Program.
