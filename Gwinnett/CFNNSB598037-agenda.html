4.08 Gwinnett Online Campus: Right of Way Acquisition and Permanent Easement

 

Prepared By: Division of Facilities and Operations    

 

Presented By: Walt Martin, Chief Operations Officer

 

Background: The Georgia Department of Transportation (GDOT) requires additional property to expand the Right of Way along Hi-Hope Road and Hurricane Shoals Road. This includes the easements on the property along

Hi-Hope Road at the Gwinnett Online Campus school site.

 

This property is needed by GDOT in order to construct a new extension of Hurricane Shoals Road to the west of Hi-Hope Road.

 

GDOT is requesting to acquire 3,703.89 square feet (0.085 acres) of property for the new Right of Way and 2,999.57 square feet (0.069 acres) for Temporary Easements for the construction and maintenance of slopes.

 

Financial Impact: Gwinnett County Public Schools will be compensated $50,400.00.

 

Recommendation: That the Board of Education adopt a motion to approve the signing and approval of the Permanent Utility Easement and the Temporary Construction Easements.

11.01 Meeting Adjourned OR Executive Session is Resumed

 

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session). If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and/or student discipline matters; and cybersecurity plans.
