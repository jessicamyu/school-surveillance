Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on October 20, 2022, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

October 20, 2022, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Multiple Pathways for All - Staff updated the Board on new developments in pathway opportunities for students in GCPS and provided an update on the district's Career and Technical Education programs.

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board on current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

Legislative Priorities - Staff presented the legislative priorities for 2023.

Policy KM - Visitors to Schools - Staff reviewed recommended changes to Policy KM - Visitors to Schools.

Policy GAEC - Elevating Employee Voice - Staff reviewed recommended changes to Policy GAEC - Elevating Employee Voice.

Governance Team Communications - Time designated for Governance Team Communications.
