Monthly Work Session
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on February 10, 2011, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

February 10, 2011, Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.

 

Georgia Mathematics Curriculum Changes - Staff will present a report on the proposed Georgia mathematics curriculum changes.

 

Gwinnett Regional Science and Engineering Fair - Staff will report on the Gwinnett Regional Science and Engineering Fair.

 

2011 Legislative Update - Staff will review education-related legislation being considered in the 2011 session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

Educational Trends and Issues - Staff will report on the latest educational trends and issues.

 

Vision Project - Staff will update the Board on the district's activities related to the Vision Project.

 

Staff Reports:

 

Staffing Update - A monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

 

Quarterly Discipline Report - Staff will present the discipline report for the second quarter of the 2010-2011 school year.

 

CRCT Testing Schedule - Staff will update the Board on calendar changes for CRCT administration in schools.

 

Special Education/English Language Learnings (ELL) Funding - Staff will provide information regarding Special Education/English Language Learners (ELL) funding.
