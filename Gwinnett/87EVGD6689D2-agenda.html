Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on September 13, 2007, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

September 13, 2007, Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Information Management - An overview of key technology issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system.

Achievement Update - An update on the achievement results for the district based on student assessments during the 2006-2007 school year.

Cross-Functional Action Team Final Report: Document Management - A review of the cross-functional action team’s charge and the research and analysis which helped formulate the team’s recommendations.

2008 Legislative Program - A presentation of the 2008 Legislative Program.

Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

Redistricting Process and Timeline - An update on the Redistricting Schedule and Boundary Committee Timeline for projects opening in August 2008, and August 2009.
