Prior to each monthly business meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a workshop for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The workshop typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled monthly business meeting.

The monthly workshop on September 14, 2006, will begin at 8 a.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

1.01 Reform Governance in Action Site Visit

Sep 14, 2006 - Monthly Board Workshop
Category
1. Work Session Discussion Topics and Presentations

Special Agenda Type

The Gwinnett County Board of Education was invited to participate in a two-year program on Reform Governance in Action (RGA) conducted by the Center for Reform of School Systems (CRSS) and funded by The Broad Foundation. The RGA program began in July 2006 and will conclude in the summer of 2008. Periodic on-site work with a CRSS consultant is one component of the RGA program. From 8 a.m. until 4 p.m. on September 14 and from 9 a.m. until 1:00 p.m. on September 15, the Board, Superintendent, and senior leadership staff will be involved in a workshop conducted by the CRSS consultant assigned to work with the Gwinnett Board/administration team. The focus of the workshop will be on the Board's Core Beliefs and Commitments, Board Meetings and Committees, and Constituent Service.
