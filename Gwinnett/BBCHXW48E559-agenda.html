Thursday, May 16, 2019
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session onMay 16, 2019, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

May 16, 2019, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Board Business Oversight Workshop: Transportation - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board held a workshop on Transportation as part of its May 16, 2019, Monthly Work Session. Staff provided an overview of key issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system's transportation department.

 

Reading Recovery Update - Staff provided an update on Reading Recovery.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff reviewed the status of education-related legislation in the current session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

3rd Quarter Discipline Report for SY2019 - Staff presented the discipline report for the 3rd Quarter of SY2018-2019.

 

Student Conduct Behavior Code Update - Staff updated the Board on the student conduct behavior code policy.

 

Division Information - Staff shared timely and pertinent information with the Board regarding their division work.
