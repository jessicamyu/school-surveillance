Subject
6.03 Price Increase for Adult Meals in School Cafeterias
Meeting
Jul 9, 2009 - Monthly Business Meeting
Category
6. CONSENT AGENDA
Type
Action (Consent)

Prepared by: Ken Yant, Director of School Nutrition Program

Presented by: Rick A. Cost, Chief Financial Officer

Background: Federal regulations state that adult meals prices should at least cover the full cost of producing the meal, including the value of USDA commodities. For the 2008-09 school year, the current adult meal price of $2.50 did not cover the annual average full cost of producing a meal; therefore, an increase in the adult meal price is required in order to remain in compliance with federal regulations.

Recommendation: That the Board of Education approve the increase in adult meal prices for the 2009-10 school year from $2.50 to $3.00.

Financial impact: Increase of $.50 per adult meal served beginning in the 2009-10 school year.

11.01 Meeting Adjourned
Meeting
Jul 9, 2009 - Monthly Business Meeting
Category
11. ADJOURNMENT
Type

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:30 p.m. Public Forum and 7:00 p.m. Open Session. If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and student discipline appeals.
