2.01 Adoption of Agenda (Amended)

 

The agenda for the Monthly Business Meeting is tentative and subject to change until it is adopted by the Board at this point in the meeting. Before the agenda is adopted, the Superintendent may recommend to the Board that items be added, deleted, or revised. The Board will vote on the Superintendent's recommendation(s) in adopting the evening's official agenda.

 

Board members receive supporting documentation for items on the Monthly Business Meeting agenda seven days prior to the meeting. Staff is available during this time to answer questions or provide additional information, if needed.

11.01 Adoption of Resolution Declaring Results of November 3rd Election

 

Prepared by: Victoria Sweeny, Board Attorney

 

Presented by: CEO/Superintendent J. Alvin Wilbanks

 

Background: On November 3, 2020, voters in Gwinnett County approved a five-year extension of the special purpose local option sales tax for educational purposes (E-SPLOST), which will benefit Gwinnett County Public Schools and Buford City Schools. The ballot question included approval of the issuance by Gwinnett County Public Schools of general obligation bonds to be repaid with sales tax proceeds. As required by law, the Board of Education must certify the results of the election associated with continuation of the sales and use tax and the issuance of the general obligation debt. 

 

Financial impact: None

 

Recommendation: That the Board adopt a resolution declaring the results of the election held on November 3, 2020, and submit a copy of the executed resolution to the Gwinnett County Board of Registrations and Elections.

14.01 Meeting Adjourned OR Executive Session is Resumed

 

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:15 p.m. Public Forum (open session) and 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session). If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and/or student discipline matters.
