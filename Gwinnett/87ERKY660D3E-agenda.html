Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on July 9, 2009, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

July 9, 2009, Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Construction and Facilities Management - An overview of key issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system.

Summer Leadership Conference Report - A brief report on the 32nd Annual Summer Leadership Conference held June 3-5, 2009.

Curriculum Update: Health and Physical Education Program - An update of Curriculum and Instruction's Health and Physical Education Program.

2009 Governor's Honors Program Update - An update on the number of GCPS high school students participating in the Governor's Honors Program during 2009.

Calendar Survey - FY2011 and FY2012 - An update on the calendar survey completed in May 2009 and sharing calendar options for FY2011 and FY2012.

Summer School Update - An update on the number of students involved in summer school this year.

Trailer Update - An update on the proposed of number of trailers in use for the FY2010 school year.



Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

College Scholarship Report - An update on the college scholarships received by GCPS students.

Community Schools Update - An update on GCPS' community schools.
