Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on December 16, 2010, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.


 

 

December 16, 2010, Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.

 

Quality-Plus Leader Academy (QPLA) Update and Graduation of 2010 Cohort IV - A QPLA update will be given, followed by the 2010 Cohort IV Graduates being presented to the Board.

 

Business Oversight Workshop on School Nutrition - Staff will provide the Board with information on all cafeteria programs. This report is provided to keep the Board informed regarding the participation and financial status of each school.

 

National Education Issues - The Board received information on a number of national education issues that have implications for Gwinnett County Public Schools, including Race to the Top.

 

 

Staff Reports:

 

Staffing Update -  A monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

 

Amended Start-Up Charter School Application: Gwinnett County Online Campus - Staff will update the Board on the amended start-up charter school application for Gwinnett County Online Campus. 

 

Emergency Closings/Inclement Weather Procedures - An update on the process and procedures followed due to cancellation of school or early dismissal.
