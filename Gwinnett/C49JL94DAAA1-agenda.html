7.06 Amendment to the Gwinnett County Board of Education’s 403(b) Plan and to the Gwinnett County Public Schools Deferred Compensation Plan

 

Prepared by: David Harris, Director of Retirement Services

 

Presented by: Joe Heffron, Chief Financial Officer

 

Background: Federal regulations allow for the automatic distribution of small account balances (i.e. less than $5,000) to terminated employees. Neither GCPS plan currently allows for such distributions. To alleviate the administrative burden of maintaining small account balances, GCPS is looking to amend each plan to allow for automatic distributions (for account balances less than $1,000) to participants who have been separated from service with GCPS for more than three years.

 

Financial impact: None. The Plans are 100% funded by employee voluntary contributions.

 

Recommendation: That the Board adopt the resolutions to amend both the Gwinnett County Board of Education’s 403(b) Plan and the Gwinnett County Public Schools Deferred Compensation Plan, and to authorize the Director of Retirement Services to execute the amendments.

14.01 Meeting Adjourned OR Executive Session is Resumed

 

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session). If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and/or student discipline matters.
