Monthly Work Session
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on July 21, 2011, will begin at 2:30 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

July 21, 2011, Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

State Requirements for School Board Training - A briefing on the new requirements for school boards as a result of Senate Bill 84.

 

eCLASS Report - A status report on the eCLASS project including the vision, goals, and components will be presented. 

 

Hope Scholarship Action Plan -  An update on this Plan will be presented.

 

Governor's Honors Program Update - An update on the Governor's Honors Program will be presented.

 

Charter School Update -  A charter school update will be presented.

 

Summer Leadership Conference Report - A report on the 34th Annual Conference held June 8-10, 2011 will be presented. 

 

 

 

 

Staff Reports:

 

Staffing Update - A monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

 

CRCT System Results -  A report on CRCT system results will be presented.

 

AYP Update - An update on system AYP results. 

 

Opening New School- A report on the opening of the new Moore MS.

 

IE2 Update - An update on IE2 will be presented.
