Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on August 14, 2008, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA. Prior to the work session, the Board will tour two new school facilities beginning at 12:30 p.m. The tour will include the new Lanier MS campus and Puckett's Mill ES. The Board will return to the ISC for its Monthly Work Session at 3:00 p.m.

TeachGwinnett – A Program to “Grow our Own Teachers” in the “Gwinnett Way”

TeachGwinnett is a recent teacher preparation initiative of Gwinnett County Public Schools. Under this innovative program, new teachers are prepared to serve the district in many critical fields, with special education as an emphasis this first year. Over thirty new teachers have been prepared in the first phase this summer, ready for Georgia certification, and ready to serve students throughout the district beginning August 1. TeachGwinnett preparation is tailored to Gwinnett’s standards of excellence and is fully approved by the Professional Standards Commission. Most of TeachGwinnett’s first teacher cohort were paraprofessionals with GCPS, guaranteeing these new teachers are mature practitioners, committed to helping all students achieve at high levels.
