Thursday, June 21, 2018
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on June 21, 2018, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

June 21, 2018, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

New Life Academy of Excellence and North Metro Academy of Performing Arts - Charter School Renewals - Staff presented charter school renewal information for New Life Academy of Excellence and North Metro Academy of Performing Arts.

 

End of Pathway Assessment Performance - Staff presented a briefing to the Board on GCPS' performance on End of Pathway assessments.

 

Gateway and Georgia Milestones End of Grade and End of Course Assessments Update - Staff provided the Board an update on the administration of the Gateway and the Georgia Milestones End of Grade and End of Course assessments.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Division Information - Staff shared information regarding planning and preparation for the upcoming school year.
