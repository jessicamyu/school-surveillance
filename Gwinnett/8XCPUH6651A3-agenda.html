Thursday, September 20, 2012
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on September 20, 2012, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

September 20, 2012 Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session which began at 3:00 p.m.

 

Quality-Plus Leader Academy Aspiring Principal Graduation Program - Staff will present the graduates of the Quality-Plus Leader Academy Aspiring Principal Program - Cohort 6.

 

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Information Management - Staff provided an overview of key technology issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system.

 

Advanced Placement Results for 2012 - Staff provided an update on Advanced Placement results from the 2012 school year.

 

Student Target Objective Report Instrument (STORI) - Staff updated the Board on the Student Target Objective Report Instrument (STORI), a web-based application which links IEP online with the Gwinnett GO portal.

 

QPLA District Leader Program  - Staff updated the Board on the launch of QPLA District Leader Program.

 

Race to the Top District Grant - Staff updated the Board on the progress of the Race to the Top District Grant application.

 

 

 

Staff Reports:

 

Staffing Update - A monthly report on personnel matters was presented to the Board.

 

2013 Legislative Issues - Staff updated the Board on the timeline for the presentation of the 2013 Legislative Program.
