Thursday, November 17, 2011
Monthly Work Session - 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on November 17, 2011, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

November 17, 2011, Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

Human Resources Business Oversight Workshop - An overview of key issues and topics relevant to human resources management and operation will be presented.

 

Emergency Response Report - A report on school safety and emergency response management will be presented. 

 

Common Core Georgia Performance Standards - Mathematics and English/Language Arts - A report on Common Core Performance Standards as they relate to Mathematics and English/Language Arts will be presented.

 

Adequate Yearly Progress (AYP) Update - The final AYP recalculations for school year 2011 will be shared. 

 

 

 

Staff Reports:

 

Staffing Update -  An update on current information related to staffing needs of GCPS will be presented.

 

2012 Legislative Program - A draft of the 2012 Legislative Program will be presented.

 

First Quarter Discipline Report for 2011- School Year - Staff will present the first quarter discipline report for the 2011-12 School Year.

 

Holiday Observances in Gwinnett County Public Schools -  An update on the Holiday Observances in GCPS will be presented.

In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing the management and operation of the school system, the Board will hold a Workshop on Human Resources Management as part of its November 17, 2011, Monthly Work Session.
