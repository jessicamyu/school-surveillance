Thursday, August 20, 2020
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on August 20, 2020, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

8/20/2020 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Back to School Update - Staff shared an update on Back-to-School for Gwinnett County Public Schools. 

 

Student, Teacher, and Parent Instructional Resources - Staff provided an update regarding student, teacher, and parent instructional resources.

 

Equity Teams Update - Staff updated the Board on the status of the district's equity teams. 

 

New Parent Portal - Staff provided an update on the new Parent Portal. 

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Final Discipline Report for 2019-2020 - Staff presented the final discipline report for the 2019–2020 school year.
