Thursday, October 20, 2011
Monthly Work Session - 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on October 20, 2011, will begin at 3 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

1.02 Aspiring Leader Program (ALP) Recognition
Meeting
Oct 20, 2011 - Monthly Work Session - 3 p.m.
Category
1. First Category
Type
Information

 

The Aspiring Leader Program (ALP), through the direction of the Quality-Plus Leader Academy, prepares teachers to move into entry-level assistant principal and administrative intern positions. The second cohort of 36 participants is presented to the Board of Education for recognition of completion of the 6-month long course of study concluding with a summer school residency. To date, 24 cohort members have been appointed assistant principal or administrative intern. A total of 50 participants have been appointed to leadership positions.
