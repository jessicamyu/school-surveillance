Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on July 10, 2008, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

July 10, 2008, Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Construction and Facilities Management - An overview of key issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system.

Summer School Update - An update on students involved in summer school this year at each school level.

Summer Leadership Conference Report - A brief report on the 31st Annual Summer Leadership Conference held June 4-6, 2008.






Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

AdaptedSPORTS Programs Report - A report on the American Association of AdaptedSPORTS Programs (AAASP).
