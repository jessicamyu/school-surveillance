10.01 Meeting Adjourned OR Executive Session is Resumed

 

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session). If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and/or student discipline matters; and cybersecurity plans.
