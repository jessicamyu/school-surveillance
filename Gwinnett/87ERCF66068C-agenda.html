Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on September 10, 2009, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

September 10, 2009, Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Information Management - An overview of key technology issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system.

Summer School Final Report - An update on the academic success of students involved in this year's summer school.

Title I Program Update - An update of the Title I Program will be given.




Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

Title IX Gender Equity Report - A Title IX Gender Equity Report for 2008-2009 will be presented.

Legislative Issues - An overview of anticipated issues related to the 2010 session of the Georgia General Assembly.

Center for Reform of School Systems (CRSS) Alumni Retreat - A review of presentations for the upcoming CRSS Alumni Retreat will be given.
