Monthly Work Session 3:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on July 16, 2020, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

July 16, 2020, Work Session Topics

 

Construction and Facilities Management Board Oversight Workshop  - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board held a workshop on Construction and Facilities Management as part of its July 16, 2020, Monthly Work Session. CEO/Superintendent J. Alvin Wilbanks and Chief Operations Officer Walt Martin provided an overview of key issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system's construction and facilities management. 

 

Simon Youth Foundation 2019-2020 School District Partner Report - Staff provided an update on the Simon Youth Foundation. 

 

Equity and Compliance Update - Staff presented current and future equity practices within our district.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff reviewed the status of education-related legislation in the current work session of the Georgia General Assembly.
