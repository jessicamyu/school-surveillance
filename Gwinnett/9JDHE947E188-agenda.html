Thursday, May 15, 2014
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on May 15, 2014, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

May 15, 2014 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

STEP Academies / Gear Up for Graduation - Staff updated the Board on middle school STEP Academies/Gear Up for Graduation programs. 

 

College and Career Ready Performance Index - Staff updated the Board on the College and Career Ready Performance Index (CCRPI).

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Quarterly Discipline Report - Staff shared the results of the 3rd Quarter Discipline Report with the Board.

 

Legislative Update - Staff reviewed with the Board the status of education-related legislation in the current session of the Georgia General Assembly.
