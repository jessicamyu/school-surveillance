Thursday, February 20, 2020
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on February 20, 2020, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

February 20, 2020, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Seckinger High School Update - Staff shared an update on Seckinger High School, which is scheduled to open August 2022 in the Mill Creek Cluster.

 

Comprehensive Support and Improvement (CSI) and Targeted Support and Improvement (TSI) Schools Update - Staff 

shared an update on CSI/TSI schools.

 

Summer 2020 - Staff shared an update on Summer 2020 activities within GCPS.

 

Reading Instruction in GCPS - Staff provided a summary of reading instruction in Gwinnett County Public Schools. 

 

Proposed Instructional Resources Review - Staff provided an overview of the proposed instructional resources for Language Arts (grades K-2, 6-8), Social Studies (grades 3-5, select 9-12), Career and Technical Education (select 9-12), and Math/ Dual Language Immersion

(select K-5).

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

2nd Quarter Discipline Report for SY2020 - Staff shared the discipline report for the 2nd quarter of SY2020.

 

 

Legislative Update - Staff reviewed education-related legislation that is being considered in the 2020 session of the Georgia General Assembly.
