Thursday, April 19, 2012
Monthly Work Session - 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on April 19, 2012, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

April 19, 2012, Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.

 

Georgia's Single Statewide Accountability Update - Staff will give an update on the Georgia Single Statewide Accountability System. The report will include information on the state's waiver for ESEA requirements and the new College and Career Readiness Performance Index.

 

8th Grade Writing Results - Staff will present an overview of the 8th Grade Writing Results.

 

Summer Schedule 2012 - Staff will present the Board with an update of the Summer 2012 schedule.                                                                               

                                                                                                                                                                                

 

Staff Reports:

 

Staffing Update - A monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

 

2012 Legislative Program - Staff will review with the Board the status of education-related legislation considered in the 2012 session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

Student Conduct Behavior Code Update - Staff will present the Board with an update on the Student Conduct Behavior Code.

 

Strategic Data Project Report - Staff will brief the Board on the findings from the district's involvement in the two-year Strategic Data Project done in conjunction with Harvard University's Center for Education Policy Research.

  

Gwinnett Retirement System and Teachers Retirement System Update - The Chief Financial Officer will present updates on the financial status, Plan highlights and funding requirements for the Gwinnett Retirement System and the Teachers Retirement System.
