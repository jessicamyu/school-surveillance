Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on January 20, 2021, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

January 20, 2021, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Responding to the Needs of Students with Dyslexia - Staff provided an update on how GCPS is responding to the needs of students with dyslexia. 

 

North Metro Academy of Performing Arts Update - Staff provided an update regarding the relocation of North Metro Academy of Performing Arts to Peachtree Elementary School, which is also in the Norcross Cluster. 

 

2022-2023 School Operating Plans for Digital Environment - Staff updated the Board on the 2022-2023 school operating plans for digital students. 

 

Monthly Discipline Report - Staff shared the monthly discipline report for the 2021-2022 school year.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff updated the Board on legislative issues relevant to the 2022 session of the Georgia General Assembly. 

 

Division Information - Staff shared timely and pertinent information regarding their division's work.

 

Board Policy Update: Policy BCBI-Public Participation in Board Meetings - The Board reviewed and discussed Policy BCBI.

 

GCPS Athletics - Theme Schools Update: Policy IDE - Competitive Interscholastic Activities Grades 6-12 - Staff provided an update on establishing a policy that will allow for students enrolled at schools without athletic programs (Gwinnett Online Campus, International Transition Center, Gwinnett School of Mathematices, Science, and Technolgy, McClure Health Science High School, and Paul Duke STEM High School) to participate in these programs at their home school. 

 

Annual Business Responsibilities - Staff reviewed the annual business responsibilities with the Board. 

 

Board Discussion: After Action Review - The Board engaged in an after action review of the previous month's Board Meeting. 

 

Cognia Institution Progress Report Review - The Board discussed and reviewed the Cognia Institution Progress Report.
