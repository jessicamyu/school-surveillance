Thursday, February 18, 2016
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on February 18, 2016, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

February 18, 2016 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Overview of Proposed Language Arts Teacher Resources for Adoption - Staff provided an overview of proposed language arts teacher resources recommended for adoption.

 

International Newcomers Center - Staff provided an update on the International Newcomers Center that included the purpose and function of the center.

 

eCLASS Instructional Update - Staff provided a progress report on eCLASS which included the evolution of how course pages are being used, increased availability of digital content, and examples of activities integrating technology that take learning to the next level.

 

G.I.V.E. South Proposal - Staff shared a proposal for alternative education opportunities for students residing in the southern region of Gwinnett County.

 

Summer 2016 - Staff presented a report on Summer 2016 which included Summer School and 4-day work week information.

 

Cross Functional Action Team Report on Post Secondary Credit -  Staff presented a report on this Cross-Functional Action team that focused on identifying best practices in implementing the Georgia Move On When Ready program in GCPS. 

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff reviewed with the Board education-related legislation that is being considered in the 2016 session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

2nd Quarter Discipline Report - Staff presented the discipline report for the 2nd quarter of SY2015-2016.

1.07 Cross Functional Action Team Report on Post Secondary Credit
Meeting
Feb 18, 2016 - Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Category
1. Discussion Topics and Presentations
Type
As a component of the GCPS commitment to continuous quality improvement, this Cross-Functional Action team focused on identifying best practices in implementing the Georgia Move On When Ready program in GCPS. Post-secondary credit at the high school level offers many opportunities for Gwinnett students, but also impacts curriculum alignment, instructional philosophy and scheduling. This XFAT reviewed the current state of offerings and requirements based on Georgia law and regulation, as well as current and best practices in Gwinnett, Georgia, and other districts in the country.
