Monthly Work Session - 2:00 p.m. Tour of New Northbrook MS; 3:00 p.m. Work Session
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The Monthly Work Session on July 17, 2014, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

 

The monthly Work Session on July 17, 2014, began at 2:00 p.m. with a tour of the new Northbrook Middle School, 1221 Northbrook Parkway, Suwanee, followed by the regular Work Session at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools' Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

 

 

July 17, 2014 Work Session Topics

 

Board Business Oversight Workshop, Construction and Facilities Management - Staff from the Department of Construction and Facilities Management presented a Board Business Oversight Workshop in conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems.

 

2014 GCPS HS Gateway Results - Staff updated the Board on 2014 HS Gateway achievement results.

 

Introduction to Georgia Milestones - Staff updated the Board on the new Georgia Department of Education assessment system: Georgia Milestones.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Status Report on New Berkmar/Central Relief Cluster High School - Staff along with architects from Smallwood, Reynolds, Stewart, Stewart & Associates updated the Board on the status of the new Berkmar/Central Relief Cluster High School.
