Thursday, January 18, 2018
Monthly Work Session 2:30 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on January 18, 2018, will begin at 2:30 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

January 18, 2018, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

 

Kindergarten Readiness Entry Profile and Play to Learn - Staff reviewed the Kindergarten Readiness Entry Profile and Play to Learn.

 

Robotics in GCPS - Staff shared an update on robotics in GCPS, specifically highlighting elementary robotics competitions.

 

Digital Learning Day Results and Impact - Staff reviewed the results and impact of the recent Digital Learning Day.

 

Staffing Update - Staff provided an update to the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update -  Staff provided an update to the Board on legislative issues relevant to the 2018 session of the Georgia General Assembly.
