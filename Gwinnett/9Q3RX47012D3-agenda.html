Thursday, November 20, 2014
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on November 20, 2014, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

November 20, 2014 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Business Oversight Workshop - Human Resources and Talent Management Division - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing the management and operation of the school district, the Board held a Workshop on Human Resources and Talent Management Division as part of its November 20, 2014 Monthly Work Session. Staff provided an overview of key issues and topics relevant to recruitment, employment, and development of Gwinnett County Public Schools' workforce.

 

GCPS Formative Instructional Practices - Staff updated the Board on Formative Instructional Practices implementation in GCPS.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

First Quarter Discipline Report - Staff provided the Board with the first quarter discipline report.

 

Positive Behavioral Interventions & Supports (PBIS) Update - Staff updated the Board on the Positive Behavioral Interventions and Supports program in Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

2015 Legislative Program - Staff presented a draft of the 2015 Legislative Program for the Board's consideration.

 

2015-2016 School Calendar - Staff reviewed the 2015-2016 school calendar.
