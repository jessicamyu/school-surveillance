Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on September 11, 2008, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

September 11, 2008, Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Information Management - An overview of key technology issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system.

Achievement and Demographics Update - An update on demographics and achievement results for the district based on student assessments during the 2007-2008 school year.

Year 4, SPLOST II Audit and Review of the 2006-07 School Year - A report on the SPLOST Audit and Review for the 2006-07 school year.

2009 Legislative Issues - An overview of the legislative issues for 2009.

Curriculum Update - An update on the curriculum in GCPS with an emphasis on mathematics and social studies.




Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

Chief Financial Officer Rick Cost will report to the Board that Gwinnett County Public Schools has been found to be in full compliance in all areas audited and reviewed for the 2006-07 school year. In November 2004, MGT of America, Inc. was awarded a contract to conduct an annual Special Purpose Local Option Sales Tax (SPLOST) Audit and Review. This report is presented in compliance with the O.C.G.A. 48-8-141, 48-8-121 and 21-2-491.
