2.01 Adoption of Agenda

 

The agenda for the Monthly Business Meeting is tentative and subject to change until it is adopted by the Board at this point in the meeting.  Before the agenda is adopted, the Superintendent may recommend to the Board that items be added, deleted, or revised.  The Board will vote on the Superintendent's recommendation(s) in adopting the evening's official agenda.

 

Board members receive supporting documentation for items on the Monthly Business Meeting agenda seven days prior to the meeting.  Staff is available during this time to answer questions or provide additional information, if needed.

11.01 Investing in Educational Excellence (IE2) Partnership Contract Amendment

 

Prepared by:     Dr. Steve Flynt, Associate Superintendent, School Leadership & Operations

 

Presented by:    Dr. Steve Flynt

 

Background:   Gwinnett's IE2Partnership Contract, approved in 2009, includes the strategic plan, all school plans and provides the local schools and school district with flexibility from13 laws and accompanying State Board rules. This flexibility is coupled with accountability measures which were identified for each school that call for schools to meet annual higher performance targets for the five years of the contract. Since the contract was approved, a number of factors have changed, including the addition of new schools and changes in the state graduation rule that call for the Georgia High School Graduation Tests to be replaced by End of Course Tests (EOCT).   GCPS has developed an amendment to the initial contract which will be submitted to the State Board of Education for approval. The amendment provides achievement targets for 15 new schools that have opened since the fall of 2009 and ensures the contracts are relevant based on changes to the state graduation rule and the transition to EOCTs.

   

 

  

Financial impact:  n/a

 

Recommendation: That the Board approve the IE2 Partnership Contract Amendment as presented.

14.01 Meeting Adjourned

 

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:15 p.m. Public Forum (open session) and 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session).  If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and student discipline appeals.
