Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on January 10, 2008, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

January 10, 2008, Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

American School Counselor Association Application - Representatives from Peachtree ES and Berkeley Lake ES will present their counseling programs as leaders in the implementation of the American School Counselor Association National Model.

2008 Legislative Issues and General Obligation Bond Referendum - Update on legislative issues and the General Obligation Bond Referendum that is on the ballot for the February 5, 2008, Presidential Preference Primary.

Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).
