Monthly Work Session
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on April 21, 2011, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

April 21, 2011, Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.

 

Redistricting Process Update - An update on the 2011-2012 redistricting process will be given.  

 

Gwinnett County Online Campus Update - An update on the Gwinnett County Online Campus will be provided.

 

Crossing Guard Update - An update on the Crossing Guard program will be provided. 

 

Instructional Materials Adoption - An update on the Instructional Materials Adoption will be given.

 

 

Staff Reports:

 

Staffing Update - A monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

 

Legislative Update -  Staff will update the Board on education-related legislation considered in the 2011 session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

Review of 2011 Area Board Meetings - An overview of the 2011 Area Board Meetings will be provided.
