7.06 Designation of Risk Management as Agent

 

Prepared by: Tom Daymude, Director, Retirement and Risk Management Services

 

Presented by:  Rick A. Cost, Chief Financial Officer

 

Background:  The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) requires the governing body of a public entity applying for and receiving federal assistance funds for disaster recovery to designate an agent to act on behalf of the entity for coordinating the application and payment processes.  The Gwinnett County School District applied for and received federal disaster relief funds based on flooding that occurred in Gwinnett County in 2009.  Since the original applications were submitted, there has been a change of the incumbent in the Risk Manager position, who coordinated the original applications.  In order to finalize the payments and documentation required, FEMA requires the Board to designate a new agent.

 

Financial Impact:  None

 

Recommendation:  That the Board of Education adopt a motion to designate the current incumbent in the Risk Manager position to act as the Board’s agent for applying for and receiving disaster relief funds from the Federal Emergency Management Agency, and execute the accompanying Designation of Applicant’s Agent form.

11.01 Naming of New Berkmar/Central Gwinnett Clusters Relief High School

 

Prepared by:   J. ALvin Wilbanks, CEO/Superintendent

 

Presented by:  Superintendent Wilbanks

 

Background:  A new high school will open at the beginning of the 2015-2016 school year to relieve overcrowding in primarily the Berkmar and Central Gwinnett clusters. The school will be the district's 19th cluster high school and will feature an entrepreneurship center, two Junior Achievement of Georgia programs for middle school students (JA BizTown and JA Finance Park), and other instructional innovations. A recommended name for the new high school will be presented by the Superintendent for the Board's consideration.

 

Financial impact:  None

 

Recommendation:  That the Board approve the Superintendent's recommended name for the new Berkmar/Central Gwinnett Clusters Relief HS.

14.01 Meeting Adjourned

 

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:15 p.m. Public Forum (open session) and 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session).  If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and student discipline appeals.
