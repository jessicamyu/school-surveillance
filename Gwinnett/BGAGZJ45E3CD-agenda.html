Thursday, October 17, 2019
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on October 17, 2019, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

October 17, 2019, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Social Emotional Learning in Gwinnett County Public Schools - Staff shared an update on social emotional learning.

 

Civic Engagement - Georgia 3R's Project - Staff gave an update regarding district initiatives on civic engagement and responsibilities including the partnership with the Georgia 3R's Project.

 

Georgia Department of Education STEM Certification - Staff recognized schools that have earned the Georgia Department of Education STEM Certification and schools that have earned the local benchmark standard toward state certification. 

 

GCPS 2018-2019 SAT and Advanced Placement Results - Staff shared the results of the SAT and Advancement Placement assessments for GCPS students.

 

Current Cyber Security Threat Landscape: Part II - Staff highlighted cyber security threats facing Gwinnett County Public Schools and the deliberate actions taken to mitigate these threats.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

2020 Legislative Program - Staff presented a draft of the 2020 Legislative Program for the Board's consideration.

 

Division Information - Staff shared timely and pertinent information regarding their division's work.
