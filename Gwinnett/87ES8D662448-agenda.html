10.04 Naming Brookwood HS Basketball Court
Meeting
Mar 12, 2009 - Monthly Business Meeting
Category
10. NEW BUSINESS
Type
Action

Prepared by: Delores Hendrix, Area I Superintendent

Presented by: J. Alvin Wilbanks, CEO/Superintendent

Background: Acting on behalf of the school and community, Principal Debbie Dees and the School Council at Brookwood HS, have requested that the basketball court in the main gym at Brookwood be named after retiring Coach Eddie Martin and be called “Martin Court.” The Brookwood HS School Council along with the PTSA, Tip-Off Club, and Athletic Department have submitted a proposal unanimously endorsing this recommendation.

Coach Martin served in the Gwinnett County School System for 29 years and recently retired. The Brookwood Community is requesting permission from the Board of Education to name the main gym basketball court at Brookwood High School “Martin Court” in honor of Coach Martin’s distinguished career as a high school player and coach in Gwinnett County.

Preparation of the floor to signify “Martin Court” would be completed during Summer 2009. A ceremony to honor Coach Martin would take place in Fall 2009.

Financial impact: None

Recommendation: That the Board of Education approve the naming of the basketball court in the main gym at Brookwood HS "Martin Court" in honor of retired Coach Eddie Martin.

11. ADJOURNMENT
Subject
11.01 Meeting Adjourned
Meeting
Mar 12, 2009 - Monthly Business Meeting
Category
11. ADJOURNMENT
Type

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:30 p.m. Public Forum and 7:00 p.m. Open Session. If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and student discipline appeals.
