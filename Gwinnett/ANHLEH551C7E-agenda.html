Thursday, July 27, 2017
Monthly Work Session 2:30 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on July 27, 2017, will begin at 2:30 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

July 27, 2017, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Department of Special Education and Psychological Services Update - Staff provided the Board with an update on the Department of Special Education and Psychological Services.

Strategic Waivers School System Contract Amendment #2 - Staff shared the Strategic Waivers School System contract amendment information.
