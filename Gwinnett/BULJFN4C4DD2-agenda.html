Thursday, November 19, 2020
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on November 19, 2020, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

November 19 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

GEMS Oversight Committee Report and Recommendations - Staff presented results of the November 5, 2020, Gwinnett Educational Management System (GEMS) Oversight Committee review of Academic Knowledge and Skills revisions, additions, and recommendations for Board approval. 

 

Staff Development Update - Math Boot Camp - Staff provided an update on Math Boot Camp.

 

Artificial Intelligence Update - Staff provided an update on Artificial Intelligence. 

 

Computer Science for All - Staff shared information regarding Computer Science for All  

 

Reassignment of Schools to New Cluster - Staff provided an update on school attendance boundaries for the new cluster feeding into Seckinger High School.  

 

Continuous Quality Improvement Update - Staff briefed the Board on the district's continuous quality improvement process. 

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Calendar Updates - Staff gave an update on school calendars. 

 

1st Quarter Discipline Report for SY2021 - Staff presented the first quarter Discipline Report for the 2020-2021 School Year. 

 

2021 Legislative Program - Staff presented a draft of the 2021 Legislative Program for the Board's consideration.
