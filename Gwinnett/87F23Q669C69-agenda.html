    6.18 Gwinnett Retirement System - Review of Claim
    Meeting
    Apr 12, 2007 - Monthly Business Meeting
    Category
    6. CONSENT AGENDA
    Type
    Action (Consent)

    Prepared by: Tom Daymude, Director of Retirement

    Presented by: Rick A. Cost, Chief Financial Officer

    Background: Within 60 days after notice of denial of a claim for benefits under the Gwinnett Retirement System (GRS) Plan, a participant may request a review of the denial by filing written notice with the GRS Plan Administrative Committee. The Committee received such a request on February 14, 2007. The claim involves a request for the Plan to pay out a lump sum benefit that exceeds the maximum amount allowable under the provisions of the Plan. The procedures outlined in Article 13 of the GRS Plan Document calls for the Committee to promptly notify the Board of Education of a request for a review. The Board then decides whether it or the Committee shall handle the review, and notifies the Committee and the claimant. Should the Board decide to allow the Committee to decide the claim, all such decisions are final, conclusive, and binding upon all parties. The claimant is advised of the disposition of the claim within 60 days after receipt of the request for review or within 120 days if a hearing is held.

    Financial impact: None

    Recommendation: That the Board of Education adopt a motion to allow the Gwinnett Retirement System Plan Administrative Committee to handle review of the request received by the Committee on February 14, 2007, regarding payment of a lump sum benefit.

    Type

    The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:30 p.m. Public Forum and 7:00 p.m. Open Session.
