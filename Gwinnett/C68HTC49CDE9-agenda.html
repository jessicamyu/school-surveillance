The agenda for the Monthly Business Meeting is tentative and subject to change until it is adopted by the Board at this point in the meeting. Before the agenda is adopted, the Superintendent may recommend to the Board that items be added, deleted, or revised. The Board will vote on the Superintendent's recommendation(s) in adopting the evening's official agenda.

4.01 Samad Hakani, Gwinnett School of Mathematics, Science, and Technology - 1st place in the category of Physical Science

 

Honoree: Samad Hakani, Gwinnett School of Mathematics, Science, and Technology

 

Recognition: 1st Place in the category of Physical Science   

 

Awarded by: National Junior Science and Humanities Symposium/National Science Teachers Association

 

Introduced by: Jessica Holden, Director of Science, 6-12

 

Background: The National Junior Science and Humanities Symposium (NJSHS) is a national science competition administered by the National Science Teachers Association (NSTA) in collaboration with the army, navy, and air force departments of the U.S. military. Each year, 250 of the most outstanding STEM students across the nation come together to share their independent project research and findings and compete for opportunities. Samad’s project "Observation of Kibble-Zurek Scaling on a Classical First-Order Phase Transition” focused on analyzing the scaling behavior of a pendulum phase transition and comparing it to a known scaling model for second-order phase transitions. He was awarded first place honors. The award comes with a $12,000 scholarship in addition to the $2,000 scholarship awarded for placing 1st overall at the Georgia Junior Science and Humanities Symposium.

14.01 Meeting Adjourned OR Executive Session is Resumed

 

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session). If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and/or student discipline matters.
