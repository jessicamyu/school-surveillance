Thursday, February 24, 2022
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on February 24, 2021, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

February 24, 2021, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Instructional Resources Adoption for SY2022 - Staff provided an overview of porposed instructional resources for Language Arts (grades 3-5, select 9-12), Social Studies (grades K-2, 6-8, select 9-12), Career and Technical Education (select 6-12), and Foreign Language/Dual Language Immersion (select K-12). 

 

GCPS Tutoring Program - Staff provided an overview of the district's tutoring program. 

 

Cognia Accreditation Update - Staff provided an accreditation update to the Board.

 

Monthly Discipline Report - Staff shared the monthly discipline report for the 2021-2022 school year.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff updated the Board on legislative issues relevant to the 2022 session of the Georgia General Assembly. 

 

Division Information - Staff shared timely and pertinent information regarding their division's work. 

 

Policy BCBI - Public Participation in Board Meetings - Staff provided an update on changes that have been made in accordance with policy guidance and/or mandates in state and/or federal law. 

 

Policy JBCB - Awarding Units of Credit and Acceptance of Transfer Credit - Staff updated the Board on changes that have been made in accordance with policy guidance and/or mandates in state and/or federal law. 

 

Policy JCD - Student Conduct Behavior - The Board engaged in a discussion regarding Policy JCD - Student Conduct Behavior. 

 

Board Discussion: After Action Review - The Board engaged in an after action review of the previous month's Board Meeting.
