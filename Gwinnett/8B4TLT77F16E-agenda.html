Thursday, January 20, 2011
Monthly Work Session - 3:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on January 20, 2011, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

January 20, 2011, Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon work session, which began at 3:00 p.m.

 

Summer School 2011 - Staff will provide information on Summer School for 2011.

 

Summer 2011 Four-Day Work Week - Staff will provide information on a four-day work week for Summer 2011.

 

Vision for Public Education in Georgia - Staff will report on the district's activities relating to its partnership with the Georgia School Boards Association and the Georgia School Superintendent's Association.

 

2011 Legislative Session - Staff will update the Board on legislative issues relevant to the 2011 session of the Georgia Legislative Assembly.

 

Staff Reports:

 

Staffing Update - A monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

 

Issues and Trends Affecting Public Education and GCPS - Staff will present a report on the issues and trends affecting public education and GCPS. 

 

Teacher Evaluation System Update - Staff will provide an update on the Teacher Evaluation System.
