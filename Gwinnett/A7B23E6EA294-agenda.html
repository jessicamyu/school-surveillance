Monthly Work Session 2 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on March 17, 2016, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

March 17, 2016 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Business and Finance Workshop Overview - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board will hold a workshop on Finance as part of its March 17, 2016 Monthly Work Session. Staff provided an overview of key financial issues and topics relevant to the sound fiscal operations of the school system. The overview was focused on key financial information included in the June 30, 2015 Comprehensive Annual Financial Report (CAFR), the FY2015 SPLOST IV Audit and Review Report, and the 2015 Annual Investment Oversight Committee Report.

 

Implementation of College and Career Academies and Update on Career and Technical Education in Middle and High School - Staff provided a brief update on the implementation of College and Career Academies at 7 GCPS high schools, including the current status of Career and Technical Education in the middle and high schools.

 

Board Members Meeting with Teachers' Advisory Council (TAC) - Board members met with the Teachers' Advisory Council (TAC) representatives from the schools in their Board electoral districts. Following the meetings, Board members and the TAC representatives gathered in the Board Room where Board members shared what was heard in their conversations with the TAC representatives.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff reviewed with the Board the status of education-related legislation in the current session of the Georgia General Assembly.

Subject
2.03 Transportation Service Waiver Resolution
Meeting
Mar 17, 2016 - Monthly Work Session 2 p.m.
Category
2. Staff Reports
Type

Gwinnett County Public Schools seeks the Georgia Board of Education’s approval of a request for a waiver from compliance with the specific vehicle provisions of “Georgia School Bus Specifications” (GADOE 2010). The district wishes to conduct a pilot using third-party transportation services in low-density (nine or fewer students) transport situations where requiring use of traditional school buses is not possible, practical, or cost effective. Use of third-party services in specific instances will provide for safe student transport and help mitigate the significant, rising costs associated with the growing requirements for this type of student transportation.
