Thursday, April 20, 2017
Monthly Work Session 2:30 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on November 21, 2013, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

1.01  April 20, 2017 Work Session Topics

 

Gwinnett Online Campus Report:  Representatives of Gwinnett Online Campus presented a report to the Board on the school's accomplishments, areas needing attention, and general student experience. The report is one in a series that are being presented by the school district's "special entity" schools that serve students district wide. 

 

Phoenix High School Report:  Representatives of Phoenix High School presented a report to the Board on the school's accomplishments, areas needing attention, and general student experience. The report is one in a series that are being presented by the school district's "special entity" schools that serve students district wide.

 

Milestones and GCPS District Assessments, Part I: Staff provided the Board an update on Milestones preparation and GCPS District Assessments.

 

Carl Perkins Compliance Five-Year Program Review Report:  Staff provided the Board a summary of the Carl Perkins Compliance Five-Year Program Review Report.

 

Staffing Update: Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update:  Staff reviewed with the Board the status of education-related legislation in the current session of the Georgia General Assembly.
