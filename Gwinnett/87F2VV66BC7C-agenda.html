Subject
1.06 Reform Governance in Action Update
Meeting
Nov 9, 2006 - Monthly Board Workshop
Category
1. Work Session Discussion Topics and Presentations
Type
Information

Presented by: J. Alvin Wilbanks, CEO/Superintendent

Background: The Gwinnett County Board of Education was selected as one of four large school systems in the nation to participate in a two-year Board-develoment program called Reform Governance in Action (RGA). Administered by the Center for Reform of School Systems, RGA is funded by The Broad Foundation and focuses on developing high-performance Board/Superintendent teams within large, urban school districts. The objective of RGA is to assist member districts in developing and implementing major reform policies that will directly improve student achievement and narrow the achievement gap. Periodic updates on the RGA work will be a regular feature of Board workshops throughout the two years of the program.
