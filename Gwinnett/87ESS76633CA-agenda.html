Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on December 11, 2008, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

CEO/Superintendent Alvin Wilbanks and Executive Director of Leadership Development Dr. Glenn Pethel will introduce members of the second Quality-Plus Leader Academy graduating class. The 27 members recently completed the one-year program of study. Sixteen members of the class currently are serving as Gwinnett principals. The Quality-Plus Leader Academy was established to ensure that Gwinnett County Public Schools has a pool of trained administrators/principals to meet future principalship needs.

Category
2. Staff Reports

Staff will update the Board on the IE2 Partnership Contract with the Georgia Board of Education. The contract would allow the school system to receive flexibility for its schools from specified state statutes and/or rules in exchange for greater accountability for student achievement.

A public hearing will be held at 6:30 p.m. prior to the Monthly Business Meeting. The public hearing was advertised in the Gwinnett Daily Post,the school district's official legal organ, on December 4 and 9, 2008.
