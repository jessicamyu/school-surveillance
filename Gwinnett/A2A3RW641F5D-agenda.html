Thursday, October 15, 2015
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on October 15, 2015, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

October 15, 2015 Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Division of Information Management and Technology - In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing management of the school system's business and operational systems, the Board held a workshop on Information Management and Technology as part of its October 15, 2015, Monthly Work Session. Chief Information Officer Frank Elmore provided an overview of key issues and topics relevant to the sound operation of the school system.

 

Title I Update - Staff updated the Board on the Title I program.  The Title I program provides support to students who are most at risk of not meeting state academic standards.

 

National Assessment Results - Staff summarized district SAT, ACT, and AP results.

 

Georgia Milestone Assessment Results - Staff reviewed preliminary results and summarized student reports.

 

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Employee Benefits Open Enrollment - Staff reviewed changes to the 2016 Open Enrollment Program which will begin October 19, 2015, and close November 6, 2015.

 

2016 Legislative Program - Staff presented a draft of the 2016 Legislative Program for the Board's consideration.
