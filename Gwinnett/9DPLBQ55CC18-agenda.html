Thursday, January 16, 2014
Monthly Work Session 3 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on January 16, 2014, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

January 16, 2014 Monthly Work Session Topics:

 

2014 Annual Martin Luther King Celebration - Staff provided an overview of this year's activities celebrating Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr.'s life and achievements in support and participation of the United Ebony Society's celebrations to be held throughout Gwinnett County on January 20, 2014.

 

Georgia Department of Education Determination Rubric for Special Education for FY14 - Staff provided the Board with an overview of the Georgia Department of Education Determination Rubric for Special Education for FY14, summarizing how the Georgia Department of Education will evaluate special education services delivered by the district.

 

Teach Gwinnett Alternative Certification Program Update - Staff updated the Board on the Teach Gwinnett alternative certification program.

 

JROTC Program Update - Staff provided an update on the district's JROTC program.

 

Growth and Redistricting Update - Staff provided a review of the upcoming plans for redistricting in the fall, and the projected growth for next year, based on the recent forecast.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update - Staff updated the Board on legislative issues relevant to the 2014 session of the Georgia General Assembly.
