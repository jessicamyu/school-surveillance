Thursday, February 8, 2018
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on February 8, 2018, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

February 8, 2018, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Summer 2018 - Staff presented a report on the Summer 2018 schedules for Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Georgia Department of Education 2017 School Climate Star Ratings and Financial Star Rating - Staff provided an update on the Georgia Department of Education release of the 2017 School Climate Star Ratings and Financial Star Rating. 

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update -  Staff reviewed education-related legislation that is being considered in the 2018 session of the Georgia General Assembly.

 

2nd Quarter Discipline Report for SY2017-2018 - Staff presented the discipline report for the 2nd quarter of SY2017-2018.
