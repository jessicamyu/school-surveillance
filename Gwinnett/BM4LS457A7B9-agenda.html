2.01  Adoption of Agenda

 

The agenda for the Monthly Business Meeting is tentative and subject to change until it is adopted by the Board at this point in the meeting. Before the agenda is adopted, the Superintendent may recommend to the Board that items be added, deleted, or revised. The Board will vote on the Superintendent's recommendation(s) in adopting the evening's official agenda.

7.03  GASB 84 Fiduciary Activities (and impact on GASB 54 Financial Statement)

 

Prepared by: Lawanda Hankins, Director Financial Reporting and Accounting

 

Presented By: Joe Heffron, Chief Financial Officer

 

Background: A new Governmental Accounting Standards Board (GASB) statement, Fiduciary Activities (statement number 84) is effective for the current year (FY2020) Financial Reporting. The GASB statement objective is to enhance the consistency and comparability of fiduciary activity reporting by state and local governments.  As a result, some funds classified as Fiduciary Funds in the past must now be reclassified.  We currently have one existing fund that will need to be reclassified to a Special Reserve Fund.  As a result, GASB statement 54 impacts this change as well, and the sources of funding must now be reported "committed".

 

Fund 510 - This revenue source would be committed as Local School Activity Funds.

 

Financial Impact: None

 

Recommendation: That the Board of Education adopt a resolution to "commit" the revenue sources for Fund 510 for the purposes outlined above. This revenue source would be reported as a Special Revenue Fund in compliance with GASB statement 84, and revenue sources reported in compliance with GASB54.

14.01  Meeting Adjourned OR Executive Session is Resumed

 

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:15 p.m. Public Forum (open session) and 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session).  If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and/or student discipline matters.
