Honorees: GCPS Schools Receiving the 2008 Georgia School Superintendent Distinguished Achievement Awards
Simpson ES, Alton C. Crews MS, Glenn C. Jones MS, Frank N. Osborne MS, Brookwood HS, Gwinnett County Online Campus; Gwinnett School of Mathematics, Science, and Technology, GIVE Center East and GIVE Center West

Recognition: 2008 Georgia School Superintendent’s Distinguished Achievement Awards

Awarded by: Georgia State Superintendent of Schools Kathy Cox

Introduced by: Dr. Steve Flynt, Chief Academic Officer

Background: Nine Gwinnett County schools made the state’s honor roll and were recognized by Georgia Superintendent of Schools Kathy Cox as schools that lead the state in student achievement and improvement.

The Superintendent's Distinguished Achievement Awards are based on the performance of students on the Criterion-Referenced Competency Tests (CRCT), the Georgia High School Graduation Tests (GHSGT) and the End-of-Course-Tests (EOCT). These awards acknowledge schools in two categories:
- IMPROVEMENT: The 10 schools in each honored subject area and grade that had the greatest improvement in the percentage of students meeting or exceeding standards.
- ACHIEVEMENT: The 10 schools in each honored subject area and grade that had the highest percentage of students score in the exceeds category.

Meeting
Oct 9, 2008 - Monthly Business Meeting
Category
11. ADJOURNMENT
Type

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 6:30 p.m. Public Forum and 7:00 p.m. Open Session. If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and student discipline appeals.
