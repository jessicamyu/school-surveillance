2.01 Adoption of Agenda

 

The agenda for the Monthly Business Meeting is tentative and subject to change until it is adopted by the Board at this point in the meeting. Before the agenda is adopted, the Superintendent may recommend to the Board that items be added, deleted, or revised. The Board will vote on the Superintendent's recommendation(s) in adopting the evening's official agenda.

 

Board members receive supporting documentation for items on the Monthly Business Meeting agenda seven days prior to the meeting. Staff is available during this time to answer questions or provide additional information, if needed.

8.02 Board Norms Adoption

 

Background: In an effort to enhance the Board's ability to focus on the business of the school district and to educate and communicate in a transparent manner, the Gwinnett County School Board identified the need for the development and adoption of norms that reflect shared agreements and guidelines. The establishment of norms is a best practice of highly effective Boards. The Board’s norms supplement its policies, which exist to provide a code of ethics, structure, operating guidelines, general meeting parameters, and the basic roles and responsibilities of the School Board. The agreed-upon norms also embrace the Standards for Effective Governance of Georgia School Systems, which are approved by the State Board of Education. Those standards address the following domains: Governance, Strategic Planning, Board and Community Relations, Policy Development, Board Meetings, Personnel, Financial Governance, and Ethics.

 

Financial Impact: None

 

Recommendation: That the Board of Education make a motion to adopt its Board Norms.

11.01 Meeting Adjourned OR Executive Session is Resumed

 

The Board meets in Executive (closed) Session prior to the 7:00 p.m. Monthly Business Meeting (open session). If Executive Session business remains to be conducted, the Board recesses Executive Session until the conclusion of the 7:00 p.m. meeting. The Board then reconvenes in the Executive Board Room to continue in Executive Session.

 

Executive Session may be held to discuss pending or threatened litigation with the Board attorney; the acquisition of real estate; the appointment, employment, compensation, hiring, disciplinary action, or periodic evaluation of personnel; and/or student discipline matters; and cyber security plans.
