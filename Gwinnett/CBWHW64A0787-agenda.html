Thursday, March 17, 2022
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on March 17, 2021, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

March 17, 2022, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Strategic Waivers School System (SWSS) Extension - Staff updated the Board on the Strategic Waivers School System (SWSS) contract with the Georgia Department of Education that is set to expire on June 30, 2022.

 

Monthly Discipline Report - Staff shared the monthly discipline report for the 2021-2022 school year.

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Legislative Update  - Staff updated the Board on legislative issues relevant to the 2022 session of the Geogia General Assembly.

 

Division Information - Staff shared timely and pertinent information regarding their division's work.

 

3:00 p.m.  Board Members' Meeting with their TAC Represenatives

 

Policy BCBI - Public Participation in Board Meetings - Staff provided an update on changes that have been made in accordance with policy guidance and/or mandates in state and/or federal law. 

 

Poicy JBCB - Awarding Units of Credit and Acceptance of Transfer Credit - Staff shared updates on changes that have been made in accordance with policy guidance and/or mandates in state and/or federal law. 

 

Policy JCD - Student Conduct Behavior - The Board engaged in a discussion regarding Policy JCD - Student Conduct Behavior. The Board will be developing this policy through the month of June at which time a recommendation for action will be brought forth. 

 

Board Discussion: After Action Review - The Board engaged in an after action review of the previous month's Board Meeting.
