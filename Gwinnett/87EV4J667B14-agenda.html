November 8, 2007, Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

Board Business Oversight Workshop - Human Resources - An overview of key issues and topics relevant to the Human Resources Division.

Shiloh HS Block Schedule Transition - Review of the work on the block schedule change.

Pandemic Planning - An update on Pandemic Planning

Redistricting Update - An update and timeline on the current redistricting process

2008 Legislative Program - An update on the 2008 Legislative Program

Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).

First Quarter Discipline Report - Presentation of the discipline report for the first quarter of the 2007-2008 school year.

In conjunction with its responsibility for overseeing the management and operation of the school system, the Board will hold a Workshop on Human Resources Management as part of its November 8, 2007, Monthly Work Session. Each year the Gwinnett County Board of Education communicates with the Gwinnett Delegation to the General Assembly those issues of critical importance to the funding and effective operation of Gwinnett County Public Schools. The 2008 Legislative Program summarizes the continuing positions and state funding improvement requests that the Board wishes to bring to the delegation for consideration in the upcoming session of the General Assembly.
