Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting.

The monthly Work Session on May 20, 2010, will begin at 3:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA.

May 20, 2010, Monthly Work Session Topics:

The following topics were on the agenda of the Board's afternoon Work Session, which began at 3:00 p.m.:

GCPS Foundation Fund, Inc. Update - Staff will update the Board on the status of the Foundation including funding opportunities and the Gwinnett County Sports Hall of Fame event.
Charter Schools Applications - Staff will update the Board on the Charter School Applications for Aya Academy of Excellence, and Gwinnett Online Campus.
Broad Team Site Visit - Staff will update the Board on the Broad team's recent site visit to GCPS.
Summer School 2010 - Staff will update the Board on the plans for summer school 2010.
End-of-Year Processes and Checklist - Staff will update the Board regarding end-of-year processes and checklist.


Staffing Update - Monthly report on personnel matters (applications, new hires, vacancies).
Quarterly Discipline Report - Staff will present the discipline report for the third quarter of the 2009-2010 school year.
Summary of 2010 General Assembly Session - Staff will review education-related legislation submitted to the Governor in the 2010 General Assembly Session.
Review of 2010 Area Board Meetings - Staff will review the 2010 Area Board Meetings that were held in April 2010.
