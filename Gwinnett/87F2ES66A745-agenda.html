1.00 Introduction to the March 8, 2007 Monthly Work Session of the Gwinnett County Board of Education
Meeting
Mar 8, 2007 - Monthly Work Session
Category
1. Work Session Discussion Topics and Presentations
Type
Information

Prior to each monthly business meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a work session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The work session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled monthly business meeting.

The monthly work session on March 8, 2007, will begin with a Board Workshop on Finance at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, GA. At 3:00 p.m. the Board will meet with all Teachers' Advisory Council (TAC) members by Board Districts. At 3:50 p.m. the Board will meet with all TAC members in the Board Room.

Each year the Board of Education members meet with members of the Teachers' Advisory Council (TAC) from the schools in their Board districts. The Board and TAC representatives will meet by district from 3 p.m. until 3:45 p.m. At 3:50 p.m. all TAC members and the Board will gather in the Board Room for a report out by each Board member on the conversations with the TAC representatives.
