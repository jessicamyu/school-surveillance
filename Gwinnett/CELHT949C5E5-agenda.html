Thursday, June 16, 2022
Monthly Work Session 2:00 p.m.
Prior to each Monthly Business Meeting, the Gwinnett County Board of Education holds a Work Session for the purpose of discussing a variety of topics related to student achievement and effective operations within the school system. The Work Session typically is held in the afternoon on the day of the School Board's regularly scheduled Monthly Business Meeting. The Monthly Work Session on June 16, 2022, will begin at 2:00 p.m. in the Executive Board Room at Gwinnett County Public Schools J. Alvin Wilbanks Instructional Support Center (ISC), 437 Old Peachtree Road, NW, Suwanee, Georgia.

June 16, 2022, Monthly Work Session Topics

 

Blueprint for the Future Overview - Staff provided an overview of the district's new strategic plan. 

 

Supporting the Blueprint Implementation - Staff shared how the implementation of the Blueprint for the Future would be supported. 

 

Staffing Update - Staff updated the Board with current information related to the staffing needs of Gwinnett County Public Schools.

 

Financial Update - Staff provided a financial update. 

 

Policy GAEB - Sexual Harassment/Discrimination (Employees) - Staff updated the Board on changes that have been made in accordance with policy guidance and/or mandates in state and/or federal law.

 

Policy JCD - Student Behavior Code Review - Staff reviewed the student conduct behavior code policy.
