Recommended Action
To acknowledge receipt of the superintendent's recommendation to pursue the termination of a teacher, ratify the teacher suspension with pay subject to the limitation of the Teacher Employment, Compensation, and Dismissal Act; and, to authorize the Secretary of the Board and legal counsel to send the teacher written notice of intent to dismiss together with the other matters required by the Act.
Goals
EL-3, Staff Treatment

estimated time: 9 a.m. (estimated duration: 2 minutes) 

 

PERTINENT FACTS:  

  1. Board executive limitation policy EL-3, Staff Treatment, provides that the superintendent shall make appropriate recommendations for staff employment terminations.
      
  2. Following a thorough review of the matter, the Superintendent has submitted his recommendation for the teacher dismissal, pursuant to the provisions of the Teacher Employment, Compensation, and Dismissal Act, C.R.S., Section 22-63-101 et seq.  
     
  3. The grounds for the superintendent's attached recommendation are neglect of duty, immorality, unsatisfactory performance, and other good or just cause as provided for in C.R.S., Section 22-63-301.  
     
  4. With approval of this item, the district will initiate the procedures for termination of this teacher as specified in C.R.S. Section 22-63-302.
