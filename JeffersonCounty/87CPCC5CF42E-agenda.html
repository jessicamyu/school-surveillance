Purpose:
For the Board of Education to receive feedback from its student advisors on the Board’s draft goal statements regarding student achievement.

Background:
At the August retreat and September 10 study session, the Board drafted new goals for student achievement in Jeffco Public Schools. The Board requested that its student advisors provide feedback on the Board goals.

This is the first meeting with student advisors for the 2009-2010 school year, the second year of the Board’s Student Advisor program. Throughout the year, the Board intends to meet with its student advisors to ask for feedback on various topics before the Board.

Discussion
Time: 6 p.m.
Location: 4th floor, Seminar Room, 1829 Denver West Drive, Bldg. 27, Golden

Presenting Staff:
none

Purpose:
For the Board of Education to develop questions it has for staff to address in a November report regarding the engagement of students in their learning in Jeffco Public Schools.

Background:
At its August retreat, the Board determined a schedule of topics to discuss with community members throughout the school year. As the stakeholders for public education in Jefferson County, discussions with community groups will help clarify the Board’s goals for student achievement in Jeffco Public Schools.

Student Engagement is the second topic to be developed by the Board for the 2009-2010 school year. The November staff report will provide background information specifically to address Board questions on the topic of student engagement so that the Board is prepared to meet with community groups later in the year on this topic.
