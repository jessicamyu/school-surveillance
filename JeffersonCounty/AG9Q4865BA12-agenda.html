EL-6, Financial Administration

PERTINENT FACTS: 

  1. In accordance with Board executive limitation policy, EL-6, Financial Administration, and pursuant to Colorado Statute C.R.S. 22-40-102(6), the Board of Education must certify its mill levies to the Board of County Commissioners of Jefferson and Broomfield by December 15, 2016.
     
  2. The required Certification Resolution for the General Fund and Bond Redemption Fund levies are based on the assessed valuation as certified by the Jefferson County Assessor on November 21, 2016, and as certified by the Broomfield County Assessor on December 1, 2016.  The counties have until December 10, 2016, to finalize the assessed value certification. 
     
  3. As presented to the Board of Education per the attached documents, the General Operating Mill Levy revenues will be levied in total of 40.191 mills against net valuation of assessment in the amount of $8,208,266,691 for all taxable property located within the boundaries of the School District.  The levy consists of 26.252 mills pursuant to the Public School Finance Act of 1994, 0.136 mills for abatements and refunds, and 13.803 mills in voter approved mill levy overrides. Revenues will flow to the District General Fund for the fiscal period beginning July 1, 2016, and ending June 30, 2017.
     
  4. The Bond Redemption Fund mill levy for 2016 taxes paid in 2017 is set at 5.750 mills.

     

  5. The counties have until December 10, 2016, for final assessed valuation numbers which are then sent to the district.  Preparing final documentation may require changes up until the Board of Education certification and approval on December 12, 2016.
