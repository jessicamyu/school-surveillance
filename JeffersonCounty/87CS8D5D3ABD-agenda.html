1. Preliminary

PRESENTING STAFF:
Lynn Setzer, executive director, Communications Services
Marlene Desmond, Manager of Communications

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to be informed of the new external website for Jeffco Public Schools.


BACKGROUND:
In (month/year), the Board of Education directed the superintendent to conduct a communications audit for the school district. One of the recommendations of the audit was to improve communications with the public through the district’s web presence.

The district will be launching a new external website in May. The Communications Services office wanted to provide a preview of the new site to the members of the Board of Education.
