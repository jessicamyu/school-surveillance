PRESENTING STAFF:
Jane Barnes, facilitator

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education and superintendent to discuss issues shared in prior comments with the facilitator regarding norms of behavior, Board priorities, academic goals and upcoming Board work for 2013-2014.

 

BACKGROUND:

At the November 21 special meeting, the Board of Education determined to meet in a work session on Saturday, December 14, 8 a.m. to 4 p.m., in the library at Wheat Ridge High School with facilitator Jane Barnes to discuss Board priorities.  The superintendent was requested to invite leaders of the three employee associations to participate in the early part of the day's session.
