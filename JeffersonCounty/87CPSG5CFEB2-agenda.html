Recommended Action
to adopt the resolution declaring a vacancy in Board of Education Director District 1 as required by C.R.S. 22-31-129(2); and, to discuss procedures for appointing a person to fill this vacancy.
PERTINENT FACTS:
1.The Board of Education is required by statute, C.R.S. 22-31-125, to declare a vacancy for the director seat when the duly elected director has submitted his or her written resignation.
2.On June 15, Scott D. Benefield submitted his written resignation, effective June 30, 2009, to the Jefferson County Board of Education for personal reasons.
3.C.R.S. 22-31-129(2) requires the Board of Education to appoint a person to fill the vacancy within 60 days after the vacancy has been declared, which is September 7, 2009.
