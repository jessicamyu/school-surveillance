2.01 Student Voice Report (GP-17)
Meeting
May 26, 2015 - Special Meeting
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion, Information

estimated time: 5:30 p.m. (estimated duration: 30 minutes) 
 

PRESENTING STAFF:

Dave Kollar, director, Student Engagement Office

Jason Firestone, manager, Student Engagement Office

Hillario Benzon, specialist, Diversity Student Engage, Student Engagement Office

Arianne Rivera, specialist, Diversity Student Engage, Student Engagement Office

High School Students from Jeffco Public Schools

 

PURPOSE:

To provide information to the Board of Education on the Student Voice program supporting high school students in Jeffco Schools. The group will provide background information on the organization and its goals for students in Jeffco, accomplishments during the 2014/2015 school year, and the value of the program for students as well as district staff.

 

BACKGROUND:
The Board of Education requested a presentation from a student organization.  Student Voice was selected as it represents students from across the entire district and has enjoyed consistent student engagement throughout the entire school year, providing valuable opportunities to gain insight from students. Jeffco Student Voice provides a diverse perspective to district leadership to promote positive and successful experiences for all students.   Students learn and are provided the opportunity to demonstrate leadership, advocacy and teamwork through the program.

Curtis Lee, director, Data Quality

Mary Beth Bazzanella, director, Educational Technology

Jeremy Felker, director, Instructional Data Reporting

Sunny Flynn, committee chair

 

PURPOSE:

To provide an update to the Board of Education on data privacy in the district, the work completed by the Technology and Data Privacy Advisory Committee (TDPAC) for the past year, and TDPAC goals for the upcoming school year.

 

BACKGROUND:

The Technology and Data Privacy Advisory Committee (TDPAC) is a 25-member committee consisting of both community members and Jeffco staff, and was created to advise the Board of Education on district technology strategies, systems and overall data governance.  The committee encourages continuous improvement of, and fosters adherence to, district policies, procedures and practices at all levels.  Reference: Board Policy GP-13
