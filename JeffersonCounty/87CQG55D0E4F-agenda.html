Pertinent Facts:

1.The Board executive limitation policy EL-11, Communication and Counsel to the Board, requires that the Board's positions on pending legislation and regulations be communicated to members of the Colorado General Assembly and the Executive Branch of state government. In establishing its work schedule for the 2008-2009 school year, the Board of Education directed the Superintendent to schedule special meetings to discuss pending legislation in the 2009 legislative session.
2.The 2009 legislative session will produce a number of bills of interest to Jefferson County Public Schools. The district contract lobbyist will bring legislation to the Board of Education for positions and direction.
3.The district’s contract lobbyist provides state legislators with the Board’s positions on legislation.
