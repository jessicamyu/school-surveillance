2.01 Student Advisor to the Board (Ends 1) 5:30 p.m.
Meeting
Mar 13, 2008 - Special Meeting-Study/Dialogue Session 5 p.m.
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion
PRESENTING STAFF:
Legal counsel

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to continue its discussion of how student advisors to the Board of Education would be helpful to its decisionmaking process. Board members have a draft application and program description to discuss.

BACKGROUND:
A Board of Education member was approached by a student who proposed that the Board needed a student advisor. On January 10, the Board received a written report and proposal from the student. Board members received information on a similar program in Alaska in the Board Run of January 18.

After discussion on February 21, the Board president offered to work on a draft program description and application process with the student who proposed the idea.
