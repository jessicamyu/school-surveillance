PURPOSE:

For Board members and legislators to discuss information regarding the successes and continuing challenges related to student achievement in Jefferson County.

 

BACKGROUND:

As part of its effective governance efforts, the Board of Education has begun to engage members of its community in discussions of issues important to the success of Jeffco students.  The Board invited members of the Jefferson County delegation to join in conversation regarding the important issues facing pre/k-12 public education in Colorado.

 

The following state legislators have confirmed they will be joining the Board of Education:
 

The Honorable State Senators Evie Hudak, Cheri Jahn, Andy Kerr and Jeanne Nicholson
 

The Honorable State Representatives Cheri Gerou, Tracy Kraft-Tharp, Brittany Pettersen, Sue Schafer, Libby Szabo, and Max Tyler

Subject
2.03 Policy Work: Compensation
Meeting
Jan 31, 2013 - Special Meeting-S/D Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion

estimated time: 6:40 p.m. (estimated duration: 30 minutes) 
 

PRESENTING STAFF:

Cindy Stevenson, superintendent
 

PURPOSE:

For Board members to review and discuss the draft Board policy on compensation.
 

BACKGROUND:

The Board of Education discussed its values for a compensation system in March and December 2012.  On December 13, 2012, the Board directed the superintendent to provide a draft policy for Board discussion in January before bringing revised policy language forward for adoption on February 7.
 

The Board has had staff presentations and ends discussions related to compensation redesign, student achievement and closing student growth gaps.  That information has informed board members’ understanding of the alignment of adult work to increased student achievement.
