Subject
2.01 Russell Elementary to Jefferson County Government
Meeting
Jul 8, 2010 - Special Meeting-Study/Dialogue Session, 5 p.m.
Category
2. Discussion Agenda
Type
Action
PERTINENT FACTS:
1.On May 13, 2010, the Board of Education declared Russell Elementary School surplus property, authorized the superintendent to negotiate the sale of the property; and, to execute the necessary documentation to finalize the sale of the property.
2.The district has the opportunity to serve Jefferson County, the city of Arvada, and the Russell neighborhood by selling this property to Jefferson County. This partnership benefits the children of the county as well as our community.
3.Jefferson County has offered to pay the appraised price of $2,000,000. The statement from the appraisal of value is “The final conclusion of Market Value as of March 31, 2010, therefore is $2,000,000 rounded.”
File Attachments

Motion & Voting

To endorse the sale of Russell Elementary School to Jefferson County; and, to endorse the district partnership with Jefferson County.

Motion by Laura Boggs, second by Paula E Noonan.
Final Resolution: Motion Carries
Yea: Dave J Thomas, Jane M Barnes, Laura Boggs, Paula E Noonan
