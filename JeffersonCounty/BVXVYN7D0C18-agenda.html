Recommended Action
to appoint Richard G. Rush, to the position of second vice president of the Jeffco Public Schools Board of Education.

estimated time: 3:25 p.m. (estimated duration: 1 minute) 
 

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. Board Governance Process policies GP-4, Board Job Description, and GP-5, President's Role, address the various Board member roles.  In following district policy BDA, Board Organizational Meeting, the following action is brought forward to the Board of Education.
     
  2. On December 8, following the interview of applicants, Board members voted to appoint Richard G. Rush to the school board for director district 5 after the resignation of Ron Mitchell and declaration of vacancy effective November 12, 2020.
     
  3. Upon election state law determines that the Board of Education elects president and vice president and either elects or appoints a treasurer and secretary upon election of new members. 
     
  4. As this vacant officer position is not due to election but appointment, and it has been the practice of the Board of Education to appoint a second vice president as a board officer, to avoid the reorganization of all board officers but president, the second vice president role will be maintained by the newly appointed member for director district 5.
