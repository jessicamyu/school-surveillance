Recommended Action

Goals
Ends 1 - Student Achievement

(estimated duration: 5 minutes)

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. The Rocky Mountain Deaf School (RMDS) is a duly organized and validly existing charter school and Colorado not-for-profit corporation chartered by the District.
     
  2. RMDS applied for and received approval for a Building Excellent Schools Today (BEST) lease purchase grant for the
    construction of a new school.
     
  3. Although RMDS was not able to execute a purchase agreement to obtain clear title to a project site by the established deadline of November 2, 2011, at its November 2011 meeting the Capital Construction Assistance Board (Assistance Board) voted to keep the RMDS project in the current December 2011 round of financing.
     
  4. The Assistance Board voted to allow RMDS one year to obtain clear title to a project site. Therefore, the Assistance Board is requiring the District, as the chartering authority for RMDS, to enter into a participation agreement, in lieu of the standard BEST documents, with RMDS and the State.  The participation agreement at this time, has yet to be prepared by the State.
     
  5. The attached resolution authorizes the RMDS project and, subject to review and approval by District counsel, authorizes District staff to execute, deliver and perform the participation agreement with such changes deemed necessary by District staff.
