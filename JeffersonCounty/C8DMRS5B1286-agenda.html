3.05 Assessment of Academic Functions/District Strategic Plan (EL-11)
Meeting
Dec 15, 2021 - Special Meeting-Study/Dialogue Session
Category
3. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion, Information

estimated time: 12:30 p.m. (estimated duration: 30 minutes)

PRESENTING STAFF:
Tracy Dorland, superintendent

Renee Nicothodes, chief of schools elementary

Jaime Aquino, Ph.D., consultant
 

PURPOSE: For the Board of Education to have a conversation with the superintendent on her planned next steps involving development of a new strategic plan for the district involving what was learned in the assessment of academic functions.


BACKGROUND: On November 10, Dr. Jaime Aquino shared his conclusions from the review of academic functions which occurred September and into November at the request of Superintendent Dorland with the goal that district central supports are organized so that Jeffco schools have the resources needed to ensure an excellent education for every student and a top-quality work environment for every staff member.

Superintendent Dorland’s key areas of focus for her first year’s work:

  • Focus on relationships, collaboration, and a positive climate and culture with stakeholders in our district, schools, and communities.
  • Explore how our district is organized to support schools with improved student achievement and social emotional well-being.
  • Find the just-right balance between system coherence and school-based autonomy.
  • Maximize the efficiency of our resources, aligned to the goal of improved student achievement - particularly focused on closing achievement gaps.
  • Support students with additional learning opportunities to make up for lost instructional time due to the pandemic.

This is the fourth monthly update on this work.
