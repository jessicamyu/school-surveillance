2.01 Development of the Strategic Plan
Meeting
Dec 1, 2017 - Study/Dialogue Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion

estimated time: 12:30 p.m. (estimated duration: 3.5 hours)

PRESENTING STAFF:
Jason E. Glass, superintendent

Tom McDermott, special assistant to the superintendent

 

PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education and Superintendent to review a draft strategic plan, aligning priorities, identifying strategies, tactics and performance metrics to ensure clear direction to employees and complete reports on the guiding work of the district to the community at future Board of Education meetings.


 

BACKGROUND:

On November 15, DeliverEd, an education consulting organization with expertise in strategy implementation, presented a Findings Report on the district’s capacity to focus resources needed to meet the strategies and tactics in the Jeffco Generations vision document released by Dr. Glass on October 11, 2017 as part of a strategic planning process.

 

At the August 2 Board retreat, Dr. Glass reviewed his plans for development of a district strategic plan.
