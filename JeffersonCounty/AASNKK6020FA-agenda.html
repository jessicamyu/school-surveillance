Recommended Action
To approve the intergovernmental agreements between the school district and the Jefferson County Clerk and Recorder and the City and County of Broomfield Clerk and Recorder for the mailing of notices for and conduct of the November 8, 2016 coordinated general election.

estimated time: 5:00 pm (estimated duration: 3 minutes) 

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. In accordance with Board governance process policy 15, Cost of Governance, “… costs will be prudently incurred…” the following is presented to the Board of Education for approval.
     
  2. The district and the County Clerk and Recorders have determined that it is in the best interests of the residents of Jefferson County and the City and County of Broomfield to cooperate and contract concerning the election to be held on November 8, 2016.
     
  3. State statutes provide that the County Clerk and Recorder and the district shall enter into an agreement for the administration of their respective duties concerning the conduct of the coordinated general election and mailing of notices relating to the election.
     
  4. District legal counsel has reviewed the proposed intergovernmental agreements prior to Board approval.

2.02 Resolution for Voter Support of Ballot Initiatives (GP-4)
Meeting
Aug 25, 2016 - Special Meeting: Study/Dialogue Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Action
Recommended Action
to adopt the Resolution for Voter Support of Ballot Initiatives as presented.

estimated time: 5:03 p.m. (estimated duration: 15 minutes) 
 

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. On June 16, the Board of Education reviewed proposed bond issue packages and a mill levy override proposal, settling on a $535 million bond amount to address identified facilities needs and a $33 million mill levy override to address general operational needs unmet through the level of state funding expected for Jeffco Public Schools in the immediate and future years. Specifically, Jeffco has not received approximately $490 million dollars which otherwise would have gone to educate and support Jeffco students since the implementation in 2009 of the negative factor in school financing.
     
  2. On August 2, the Board of Education took action to place two questions on the November 8, 2016 general election ballot for a $535 million bond issue and a $33 million mill levy override.
     
  3. The Board of Education will discuss the resolution below which provides the reasoning and rationale for supporting the ballot measures this November.
     
  4. The resolution is brought forward in accordance with Board governance process policies GP-1, Governance Commitment, and GP-4, Board Job Description, “the Board may, by motion or resolution, take a position of advocacy on national, statewide or local ballot issues, referred measures, legislation or rules and regulations.”
