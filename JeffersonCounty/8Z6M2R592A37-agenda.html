Subject
2.02 Board Ends Policies and Aligned Student Achievement Goals
Meeting
Oct 25, 2012 - Special Meeting-S/D Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion

Time:  5:10 p.m. (estimated duration: 1 hour)

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to review the district’s student achievement goals aligned with the Board’s Ends policies.

 

BACKGROUND:

Jeffco consistently analyzes student achievement data.  The analysis of data leads to the school and district student achievement goals.

 

The state requires the development of the Unified Improvement Plan (UIP) based on the results of the District Performance Framework (DPF).  The UIP includes student achievement goals based on data from the DPF. The district has additional student achievement goals included in the Call to Action.  Those goals emerge from data in the DPF as well as from data from other sources.  Those student achievement goals are reflected as targets in the Call to Action.

 

The student achievement goals all support the Board of Education Ends policies.

 

The Board of Education and district staff will discuss the student achievement goals based on data on student performance.
