PRESENTING GUEST:

Charlie Brown, director, Colorado Futures Center, Colorado State University

 

PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to learn more about the effect of TABOR (Taxpayers Bill of Rights) on Colorado and K-12 funding.

 

BACKGROUND:

 

As the Board of Education considers the priorities facing Jeffco Public Schools, both in facilities needs and general operations costs to support student achievement, more information was requested on the state of Colorado’s fiscal and constitutional mandates as they relate to anticipated resource support to Jeffco Public Schools from the state.

Through its development of the 2016/2017 Adopted Budget and the negotiations process with employees, the Board of Education knows of general operational needs unmet by the level of state funding expected for Jeffco Public Schools in the immediate and future years.
