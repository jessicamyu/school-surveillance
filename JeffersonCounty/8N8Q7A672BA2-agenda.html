2. Certification of the Canvass of the Regular Biennial School Board Election
Type
Action, Procedural
Recommended Action
to adopt the resolution declaring the results of the November 1, 2011 regular biennial school board election presented prior to the meeting.

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. Board Governance Process policy, GP-1, Governance Commitment, concerns governance commitment and the purpose of the school board.  On November 1, 2011, an election was held to elect two members to the school board.
     
  2. Per state law and the district's intergovernmental agreement with Jefferson and Broomfield counties, the canvass board has until November 18, 2011 to provide a certified official abstract of votes to the designated election official and the school district.
     
  3. As the final official abstract of votes cast for all candidates is available and results are not in question, a resolution indicating the newly elected board members has been prepared for the Board of Education prior to the meeting.

Recommended Action
to reorganize Board officers through the election of a president and vice president of the Board of Education; to appoint a second vice president, treasurer and secretary; and, to reappoint Lorie Gillis to the position of assistant treasurer and Helen Neal to the position of assistant secretary.

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. Board Governance Process policies GP-4, Board Job Description, and GP-5, President's Role, address the various Board member roles.  In following district policy BDA, Board Organizational Meeting, the following action is brought forward to the Board of Education.
     
  2. On November 1, 2011, an election was held to elect two members to the school board.
     
  3. State law determines that the Board of Education elects a president and vice president and appoints a treasurer and secretary upon election of new members.
     
  4. It has been the practice of the Board of Education to appoint a second vice president, treasurer, secretary and an assistant treasurer and assistant secretary at this time as well.
