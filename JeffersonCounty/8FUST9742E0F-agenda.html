2.02 Legislative Update (EL-11)
Meeting
Apr 21, 2011 - Special Meeting-S/D Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Action
Recommended Action
to receive an update on legislative activity and to take positions on pending legislation as needed.

Time: 5:30 p.m.

Location: 5th floor, Board Room
(estimated duration: 1 hour)

PRESENTING STAFF:
Cindy Stevenson, superintendent
Ed Bowditch, contract lobbyist, Tomlinson & Associates

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to provide direction to the contract lobbyist for the 2011 legislative session.

BACKGROUND:
Board executive limitation policy EL-11, Communication and Counsel to the Board, requires that the Board's positions on pending legislation and regulations be communicated to members of the Colorado General Assembly and the Executive Branch of state government. It has been the Board's practice to schedule special meetings to discuss pending legislation during the legislative session.  The Board receives weekly updates on legislative action from Mr. Bowditch.   

The 2011 legislative session has produced a number of bills of interest to Jefferson County Public Schools. The district contract lobbyist continues to bring legislation to the Board of Education for positions and direction, as needed. The district’s contract lobbyist provides state legislators with the Board’s positions on legislation.
