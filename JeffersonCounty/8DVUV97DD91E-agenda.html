Estimated Time:  8:00 p.m.

Location: Board room
 

PRESENTING STAFF:
Cindy Stevenson, superintendent

Helen Neal, manager of Administrative Services

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to review its budget reduction for 2011/2012 and its quarterly expense reports prepared in November 2010.

BACKGROUND:
In November 2010, members of the Board requested time to review the proposed reductions to its budget for 2011/2012.  On January 20, 2011, the Board postponed this discussion to February.

In September 2010, the Board agreed to review its current year expenses quarterly and committed to Board excellence through Ends 8, the Jeffco Board will be effective and responsible and will function as a highly effective team.

Board governance process policy GP-2, Governing Style & Philosophies, calls for the Board to “…informally debrief the Board’s process and performance at least quarterly. Self-monitoring will include comparisons of Board activity and discipline to policies in the Governance Process and Board/Staff Linkage categories. Additionally, GP-16, Monitoring Board Governance Process and Board/Staff Linkage policies, reminds board members that, “Monitoring will be as automatic as possible, using a minimum of Board time so that meetings can be used to create the future rather than to review the past.
