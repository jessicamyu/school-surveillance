2.01 Jeffco Online Enrollment Project (EL-11)
Meeting
Apr 16, 2018 - Study/Dialogue Session
Category
2. Discussion Agenda
Type
Discussion, Information

estimated time: Noon (estimated duration: 30 minutes) 

 

PRESENTING STAFF:

Kristopher Schuh, chief of schools, secondary

Shannon Fitzgerald, enrollment consultant

Diana Wilson, chief communications officer

Jeremy Felker, executive director, Student Data Privacy

Tim Matlick, achievement director

Matt Walsh, achievement director

Kristen Harkness, assistant director, Special Education

Sherri Fast, manager, Early Childhood SAE 

 

PURPOSE:

The Board of Education will receive information about Jeffco’s online enrollment project, including why it is happening, what work is being done, and expected impacts and timing. Board members will have the opportunity to ask questions and engage in dialogue about project plans, scope of the project, timing, policy implications, and other relevant topics.

 

BACKGROUND:

At the Board of Education meeting on November 16, 2017, Board members discussed a variety of challenges Jeffco currently faces with regard to multiple application and enrollment processes, the vast majority of which are paper-based. Board members concluded that solutions must be identified and implemented.

 

Since that time, Superintendent Dr. Glass has facilitated numerous conversations with a diverse group of stakeholders and summoned together a steering committee comprised of cross-functional district leaders to spearhead this work. Steering committee members have identified a set of solutions to implement in the 2018/19 school year and have contracted with an enrollment consultant to lead the initiative.

PRESENTING STAFF:

Amy Weber, chief human resources officer

Jim Branum, Caplan and Earnest, legal counsel

 

PURPOSE:

In accord with Board executive limitation policies EL-3, Staff Treatment; EL-4, Staff Compensation; and EL-11, Communication and Counsel to the Board, “… the superintendent shall not fail to give the Board as much information as necessary to allow Board members to be adequately informed and supported…” the district’s negotiations team leads will provide an update on the status of negotiations with the district employee associations.
 

BACKGROUND:
Under Colorado Law, school board discussions regarding employee contracts must be conducted in open session (except under specific circumstances). This session will provide Board members with an update on the status of negotiations with Jefferson County Education Association (JCEA) and Jeffco Education Support Professionals Association (JESPA).

The Board received a report on negotiations on January 25, 2018.
