PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to receive an update on the Colorado Department of Education Accreditation System beginning in the 2008-2009 school year.


BACKGROUND:
In order to track longitudinal growth, promote post-secondary readiness and align the three accountability systems at work in the Colorado K-12 public education system, the Colorado Department of Education is changing the state accreditation process. Board members requested the superintendent provide information on the proposed changes.

PERTINENT FACTS:

1.The Board executive limitation policy EL-11, Communication and Counsel to the Board, requires that the Board's positions on pending legislation and regulations be communicated to members of the Colorado General Assembly and the Executive Branch of state government. In establishing its work schedule for the 2007-2008 school year, the Board of Education directed the Superintendent to schedule special meetings in February, March and April to discuss pending legislation in the 2008 legislative session.
2.The 2008 legislative session will produce a number of bills of interest to Jefferson County Public Schools. The district contract lobbyist will bring legislation to the Board of Education for positions and direction.
3.The district’s contract lobbyist provides state legislators with the Board’s positions on legislation.
