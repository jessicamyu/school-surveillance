The Board of Education will receive training from legal counsel from Caplan and Earnest regarding policy governance and discuss its governance process, executive limitation, board/staff linkage and Ends policies as well as review next steps for community engagement.

The Board continues its orientation training begun in December to move forward on priority work topics noted at meetings in November and December 2015.
