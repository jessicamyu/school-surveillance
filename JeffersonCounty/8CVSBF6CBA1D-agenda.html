Estimated Time: 8:30 p.m.
 

PRESENTING STAFF:
Cindy Stevenson, superintendent
Debbie Backus, chief academic officer
 

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to provide direction to the members of the 2012-2015 Calendar Committee.
 

BACKGROUND:
The Board of Education approved membership for the 2012-2015 on January 6, 2011. At that time, the Board agreed to determine the charge for the committee before its first meeting on February 2, 2011.
 

The Calendar Committee determines the three year calendar cycle to present to the superintendent for adoption. This includes the 2012/2013, 2013/2014, and 2014/2015 school year calendars.
 

The committee is composed of individuals representing the three employee associations, Jeffco PTA, Strategic Planning and Advisory Council, County Student Council, two parent representatives from the elementary, middle and high school levels, a high school college counselor and a community superintendent. Additional information on the preparation of the district calendar can be found in Board executive limitation policy EL-14, School Year Calendar, and district policy IC/ICA, School Year/School Calendar.
