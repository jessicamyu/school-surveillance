PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to finalize the agenda for the Saturday, January 28 budget forums with the community.

 

 

BACKGROUND:

As part of the budget development process, the Board of Education planned for community budget forums in January/February 2012.  

The morning of Saturday, January 28, has been scheduled for the Board's five budget forums.  The budget forums will provide the opportunity for students, parents and community members to offer priorities for the Board as board members work to determine reductions for the 2012/2013 school year and the adoption of a balanced budget.  

At the immediately preceding January 12 Strategic Planning Advisory Council (SPAC) meeting, SPAC members modeled a feedback process which may be used by the Board members at the January 28 budget forums.
