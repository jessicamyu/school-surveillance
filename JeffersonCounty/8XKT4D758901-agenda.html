Meeting
Sep 27, 2012 - Special Meeting-S/D Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion

estimated time: 5:00 p.m. (estimated duration: 1 hour) 
 

PRESENTING STAFF:

none

 

PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to continue its implementation of effective governance practices focusing its time to increase student achievement in Jefferson County.

 

 

BACKGROUND:

The Board requested time to affirm or revise member priorities identified in March 2012 in order to move forward with a board work plan and monitoring schedule for 2012/2013. 

 

Additionally, the Board will review the changes to its agenda format for clarity among members, including the difference in how and when the Board monitors its Ends (goals) versus monitoring superintendent performance through executive limitation policy reports.

 

The work of advisory committees to the Board of Education will be discussed as requested in June by Board members.

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to review the resolutions directing the efforts of the CASB during the 2013 legislative session and provide guidance to the Board's delegate to the Fall Conference and Delegate Assembly on October 19-20, 2012.

BACKGROUND:
The Colorado Association of School Boards (CASB) holds its annual Fall Conference and Delegate Assembly in October in preparation for legislative work in Colorado on public education.

At the Delegate Assembly, positions are taken on resolutions which effect state school districts.  As the largest school district in the state, the work of CASB may impact the district's work.
