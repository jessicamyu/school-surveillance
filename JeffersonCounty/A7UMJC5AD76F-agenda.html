2.05 Stevenson Agreement
Meeting
Mar 17, 2016 - Special Meeting-Study/Dialogue Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion

estimated time: 7:40 p.m. (estimated duration: 10 minutes) 
 

PRESENTING STAFF:

Craig Hess, district legal counsel

 

PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to discuss paragraph #12 of the early release agreement with Dr. Cynthia Stevenson.
 

BACKGROUND:

On March 3, Board President Ron Mitchell requested the district bring forward information related to Dr. Stevenson's early release agreement and paragraph #12 barring her from volunteering or employment in Jeffco schools.

February 18, 2014, the Board of Education approved the agreement for Dr. Stevenson's early release. Paragraph #12, page 5 of 8 reads, "No continuing right of employment by the District. Dr. Stevenson agrees that she will not apply for or otherwise seek re-employment or seek to volunteer in the District, (emphasis added) including its charter schools until December 31, 2018, and that, in the event she does so, it will be a material breach of this Agreement, and the District and its charter schools will have no obligation to consider her application."
