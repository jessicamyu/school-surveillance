Recommended Action
To adopt the resolution to approve the Negotiated Agreement reached during contract negotiations between the District and Jefferson County Education Association (JCEA).
Goals
EL-3, Staff Treatment

estimated time: 5:30 p.m. (estimated duration: 15 minutes) 
 

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. In accordance with Board executive limitation policy EL-3, Staff Treatment, the following is presented to the Board of Education for approval.  
     
  2. The authorized bargaining representatives of the Jefferson County Education Association (JCEA) and Jefferson County Public Schools reached a tentative agreement on a draft of a successor agreement to the Negotiated Agreement that expires on August 31, 2015.
     
  3. This represents the first time in many years that the entire agreement has been rewritten and both teams worked diligently to craft an agreement for the 21st century that is one-third the length of the prior agreement.
     
  4. Positive features of the new agreement include an emphasis on school-level autonomy and decision-making; updates to the evaluation process to ensure compliance with state requirements; compensation increases tied to performance; and a continuation of the district and JCEA partnership at the school and district level. 
     
  5. The term of the agreement is September 1, 2015 through June 30, 2016.
     
  6. The JCEA will take the agreement to its membership for ratification and if ratified, and approved by the Board, the ratified agreement will be executed by the parties.
