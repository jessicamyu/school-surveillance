PRESENTING STAFF:
Jason E. Glass, superintendent
Helen Neal, chief of staff for the board and superintendent
Tom McDermott, special assistant to the superintendent

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education and superintendent to review specific purpose and goals for working together, including evaluation measures and communication efforts for the 2017/2018 school year.

 

BACKGROUND:

Dr. Jason Glass began his work as superintendent of Jeffco Public Schools effective July 1. The retreat is the first opportunity for the Board and superintendent to formally gather together to establish parameters, strategies and goals for the coming year.

The Board of Education meets annually in retreat session to ground its five members and superintendent in common understanding of the work ahead for the next school year. 
 

Please open the links, Ends Governing Statement, and the 2015-2017 Strategic Plan for the Jeffco 2020 Vision, for additional background material.

Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion

estimated time:  9:30 a.m. (estimated duration:  90 minutes) 

 

PRESENTING STAFF:
Jason E. Glass, superintendent
Helen Neal, chief of staff for the board and superintendent
Tom McDermott, special assistant to the superintendent

PURPOSE:
To clarify and discuss the district's current vision and strategic plan and identify the future direction for the district as well as reporting, budgeting, and accountability systems related to the strategic plan.

 

BACKGROUND:

The district's existing Ends statements, and the Jeffco 2020 Vision, establish some context for the district's current strategic direction. The Board and Dr. Glass will have the opportunity to review these current elements and discuss their importance going forward. Dr. Glass will also introduce the Deliverology system, and how that might work going forward as a mechanism to assure progress on elements of the district's strategic plan.

2.03 Superintendent Goals & Evaluation
Meeting
Aug 2, 2017 - Special Meeting-Study/Dialogue Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion

estimated time: 11:00 a.m. (estimated duration: 90 minutes ) 

 

PRESENTING STAFF:
Jason E. Glass, superintendent
Helen Neal, chief of staff for the board and superintendent
Tom McDermott, special assistant to the superintendent

PURPOSE:
Per the superintendent's contract, Dr. Glass and the Board will meet to discuss superintendent goals for the coming year and to establish a system for regular feedback and evaluation.

 

BACKGROUND:

Dr. Glass will present the model he used with the Board in Eagle. Members of the Board will discuss the process they used previously. The Board and the superintendent will establish a system of feedback and evaluation for the coming school year. In addition, the Board will work to define some of the major goals they wish the superintendent to be focused on for the coming year.

Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session

PRESENTING STAFF:
Jason E. Glass, superintendent
Helen Neal, chief of staff for the board and superintendent
Tom McDermott, special assistant to the superintendent

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education and superintendent to clarify and define communication procedures and expectations. 

 

BACKGROUND:

A critical element of Board/Superintendent success is clarity around communication procedures and expectations between the Board, the superintendent, and the rest of the organization. The Board will discuss models of this communication, discuss past practice, and clarify expectations going forward around Board/Superintendent communications.
