Category

Discussion

Location: Colorado Association of School Boards office, 1200 Grant Street, Denver


PRESENTING STAFF:
Cindy Stevenson, superintendent
Ken Delay, CASB facilitator
Randy Black, CASB facilitator

PURPOSE:
The Board of Education will review the principles of governance effectiveness, including the development of norms and ends policies, and the values that underlie the budget process.

BACKGROUND:
The Board of Education addressed some of these issues at an August retreat and work in September 2009. After the November 2009 election, two new members joined the Board of Education. The Board met on February 6 to discuss issues preliminary to a full discussion of the Board's norms, values and goals.
