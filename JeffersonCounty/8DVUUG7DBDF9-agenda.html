PRESENTING STAFF:
Cindy Stevenson, superintendent
Ed Bowditch, contract lobbyist, Tomlinson & Associates

Lorie Gillis, chief financial officer

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to discuss its legislative platform to provide direction to the contract lobbyist for the 2011 legislative session.

BACKGROUND:
Board executive limitation policy EL-11, Communication and Counsel to the Board, requires that the Board's positions on pending legislation and regulations be communicated to members of the Colorado General Assembly and the Executive Branch of state government. It has been the Board's practice to schedule special meetings to discuss pending legislation during the legislative session.  On September 17, those dates were set for January 20, February 24, March 17, April 7 and 21, 2011.  On February 3, the Board revised its meeting schedule to add a special meeting for February 17 to discuss pending legislation and the legislative platform.  On February 15, the Board added to the legislative discussion the topic of the impact to Jeffco of the Governor's proposed budget and reductions in k-12 funding for 2011/12. 

The 2011 legislative session will produce a number of bills of interest to Jefferson County Public Schools. The district contract lobbyist will bring legislation to the Board of Education for positions and direction. The district’s contract lobbyist provides state legislators with the Board’s positions on legislation.
