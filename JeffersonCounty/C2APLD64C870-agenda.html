The Board of Education will receive an update about the ongoing work related to the Pandemic Recovery Plan as outlined for how Jeffco Public Schools will address and monitor key areas of operation to ensure students are positioned to thrive in the years to come.

 

BACKGROUND:

The district is currently engaged in a process to develop and implement a Pandemic Recovery Plan.  Broad stakeholder engagement is underway and district leaders are examining stakeholder perception data as well as the newly released student performance data in order to construct a thoughtful and comprehensive plan for meeting the needs of all students.

Subject
2.02 Board Advisory Committee Appointments (GP-13)
Meeting
Jun 9, 2021 - Special Meeting-Study/Dialogue Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion

estimated time: 10:30 a.m.  (estimated duration: 30 minutes)
 

PRESENTING STAFF:

none
 

PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to review the membership recommendations and applications for openings in three Board advisory committees - Capital Asset Advisory Committee (CAAC), District Accountability Committee (DAC), and Technology Data Privacy Advisory Committee (TDPAC) - in order to make appointments.

 

BACKGROUND:

The role of Board of Education advisory committees is outlined in Board governance process policies GP-12, Board Committee Principles, and GP-13, Committee Structure.

 

Three committees generated vacancies in the 2020/21 school year. In order to be prepared for committee work for the 2021/22 school year, the annual online application process was available mid-March to April 26. Board members received membership applications in May for review prior to the Board’s June 3 discussion and appointments, and had an opportunity to meet interested applicants on May 25. 

Vacancies are as follows:

  • CAAC (2)
  • DAC (33) (thirty articulation area representatives, three direct Board appointments)
  • TDPAC (1)
