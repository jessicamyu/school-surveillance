PURPOSE:
To receive feedback from community leaders in the Strategic Planning and Advisory Council (SPAC) on the Facility Usage Committee draft recommendations as part of the November community feedback process.

BACKGROUND:
At its August retreat, the Board of Education outlined a series of topics to discuss with community members throughout the school year. The SPAC is one of the Board's advisory committees on topics impacting the education of students in Jeffco Public Schools.
