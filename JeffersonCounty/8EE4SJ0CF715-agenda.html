PRESENTING STAFF:             

Cindy Stevenson, superintendent

Lorie Gillis, chief financial officer

 
 

PURPOSE:  
To receive Board of Education direction for the 2011/2012 Proposed Budget Executive Summary, to plan for the public hearings, and to discuss the content of the community budget forums.

The Board of Education has met to discuss the budget development process and the information from the Employee Summit.  High level summary schedules for the draft Executive Summary of the 2011/2012 Budget will be presented in order to receive feedback and additional direction from the Board before the public hearings on April 5 and May 5, 2011.
