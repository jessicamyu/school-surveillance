2.02 Budget Update: Cabinet Recommendations for the 2019/2020 Budget Development (EL-5, 6)
Meeting
Mar 13, 2019 - Study/Dialogue Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion, Information

estimated time: 9:40 a.m. (estimated duration: 40 minutes) 
 

PRESENTING STAFF:

Kathleen Askelson, chief financial officer

Nicole Stewart, budget and treasury director


PURPOSE:

An opportunity for the Board of Education to further discuss the cabinet recommendations for funding requests for development of the 2019/2020 budget and funding requests for use of 5A mill levy override funds as presented at previous meetings.

 

BACKGROUND:

On March 7, the Board requested an opportunity to further discuss the 2019/20 funding requests at March 13 study session.

 

During the March 7 budget update to the Board of Education, members received an update on the current year budget and information for consideration of budget priorities to direct development of the 2019/2020 budget. Information shared included a financial update on the budget status for the current year; revenue assumptions per the state's fiscal outlook, school finance and estimated Jeffco funding, and other sources; and, expenditure assumptions and budget priorities as identified by Cabinet, staff and district leadership, and the community. Discussion focused on the "must do" Cabinet funding requests and the Board provided direction to move forward with those requests to begin building the 2019/2020 budget. 

 

Updates on development of the 2019/2020 budget began at the November 7, 2018, Board meeting and continued at the December 19, 2018, and January 10, January 16 and February 7, 2019, meetings. At those meetings, the Board received information on the timeline and objectives for development of the 2019/2020 budget, updates on the current year budget, assumptions per the Governor's proposed budget in November and its revision in January, and an overview of the community engagement plan.
