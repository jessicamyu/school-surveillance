This meeting was called by State Representative Sara Gagliardi and is posted by the Board of Education in accordance with C.R.S. 24-6-402, to afford public access for citizens to become fully informed on issues of public importance so that meaningful participation in the decision-making process may be achieved where three or more Jefferson County school board members may be involved in a discussion of issues to come to the Board for future action.

Rep. Gagliardi suggests attendees bring a non-perishable food item for the Arvada Food Bank donation purposes.
