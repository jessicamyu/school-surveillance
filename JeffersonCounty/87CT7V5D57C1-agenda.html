PRESENTING STAFF:
Cindy Stevenson, superintendent


PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to continue its discussion begun on October 25 regarding the revision of the district graduation requirements policy, IKF.


BACKGROUND:
Last fall, the Board of Education conducted community meetings to engage community members in a discussion of “what children need to succeed” in schools and how Jefferson County Public Schools is meeting the Board’s Ends policy goals in preparing all children for a successful future.

On October 5, 2006, the superintendent provided the Board of Education with an update on the work planned to update Jeffco’s graduation requirements. After a year of work, the superintendent and Cabinet will provide a recommendation to the Board of Education on graduation requirements for Jefferson County students.

PERTINENT FACTS:

1.Board Governance Process policy, GP-1, concerns governance commitment and the purpose of the school board. On November 6, 2007, an election was held to elect two members to the school board.
2.Per state law and the district’s intergovernmental agreement with Jefferson and Broomfield counties, the canvass board has until November 23, 2007 to provide a certified official abstract of votes to the designated election official and the school district.
3.As the unofficial abstract of votes cast for all candidates is available and results are not in question, a resolution indicating the newly elected board members and the is prepared for the Board of Education prior to the meeting.
4.The certification of the election will be given to the candidates on receipt of the official abstract of votes from Jefferson and Broomfield counties.
