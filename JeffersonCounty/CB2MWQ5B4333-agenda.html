PRESENTING STAFF:

Kym LeBlanc-Esparza, deputy superintendent

Lisa Relou, chief strategy & communication officer

 

PURPOSE:

To respond to the Board of Education request for a comprehensive plan for consolidation through review and discussion of a districtwide process for regional opportunities for thriving schools.

  

BACKGROUND:

At the study session on February 9, the Board received information on enrollment trends and school membership leading to implications for future needs and opportunities to address student needs.

 

Jeffco Public Schools is operating within a context of declining enrollment. We have space for 96,000 students in traditional schools and our enrollment is currently at 69,000 students enrolled in traditional schools. Significantly under-enrolled schools risk compromising the educational experience and Board Ends related to every student receiving the skills, support and educational opportunities needed to achieve his or her full potential.
