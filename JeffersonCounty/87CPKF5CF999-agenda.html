0.2 Board Discussion: Ends Policy 3
Meeting
Sep 10, 2009 - Special Meeting-Study/Dialogue Session, 5 p.m.
Category
0. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion
Time: 5 p.m.
Location: 4th floor, Seminar Room, 1829 Denver West Drive, Bldg. 27, Golden

PRESENTING STAFF:
Jim Weigel, facilitator, Colorado Association of School Boards (CASB)

PURPOSE:
To discuss revisions to its Ends 3 policy to clearly reflect and further support the goals and vision of the Jeffco Public Schools Board of Education as stated in policy Ends 1, "All students explore and develop their potential as productive global citizens in a 21st century environment."

BACKGROUND:
At its August retreat, the Board of Education outlined policy discussions it planned to have in the first half of the school year. As the Board's Ends policies state the Board of Education goals for the district's work, one of the first Board discussions is to review and clarify its Ends policy 3, "Students will possess personal characteristics and life skills to successfully meet life's challenges."

Category

Time: 5 p.m.

PRESENTING STAFF:
Jim Weigel, facilitator, Colorado Association of School Boards (CASB)
Cindy Stevenson, superintendent
Lorie Gillis, chief financial officer

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to continue its discussion of goals and priorities to increase achievement for students so that future Board decisions and district work are aligned with that vision.

BACKGROUND:
At its August retreat, the Board of Education outlined policy discussions it planned to have in the first half of the school year. In continuing its discussion of its vision and tradeoffs (for whom, at what cost) for increasing student achievement in Jeffco Public Schools, the Board of Education's goals provide parameters for the superintendent's development of the district budget.
