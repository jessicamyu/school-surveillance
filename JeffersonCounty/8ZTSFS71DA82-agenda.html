2.05 Board Values on Compensation
Meeting
Nov 15, 2012 - Special Meeting-S/D Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion

estimated time: 6:07 p.m. (estimated duration: 20 minutes) 
 

PRESENTING STAFF:

None

 

PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to revisit the discussion of March 23, 2012 with facilitator Jim Weigel related to its values for compensation work in the district to inform policy direction as Board policy is developed for future direction.

 

BACKGROUND:

The Board of Education worked throughout the winter/spring of 2012 on effective governance practices.  One discussion addressed how board members would identify ‘what is important to you about teacher compensation’ as a way to direct the work of the superintendent and district.

 

The Board agreed to work on shared definitions, all possible meanings and the range of interpretations used to define Board values prior to finalizing a direction for the superintendent.

 

At the March 23 study session, the Board identified the following values:

  • Attract and retain
  • Fair and accessible for all (i.e., art and math teachers)
  • Meaningful
  • Teachers feel valued
  • Reliable
  • Improves academics
  • Competitive salaries
  • Attract the best to Jeffco
  • Transparent system
  • Reward results
