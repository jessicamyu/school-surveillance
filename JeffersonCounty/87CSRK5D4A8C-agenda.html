Jan 24, 2008 - Regular Business Meeting 6 p.m.
Category
4. Public Agenda
Type
Information

At this time the Board listens to the community’s issues of concern. Board members or staff will respond during this meeting or at a later time. We listen with respect and ask those who address the Board to do the same. In order to respect all persons addressing the Board, we request that the audience not applaud individual speakers and that issues, rather than personalities, be addressed. Any resident or staff member of the district who has signed up on the public comment roster may speak. There is a three minute time limit per person.

If there are a number of speakers commenting on the same topic this evening, we ask that remarks be consolidated and new thoughts or concepts shared. It is not the number of people who address the Board that leads to better decision-making, rather the content of your presentation. So, we respectfully ask that you do not repeat the same message of an earlier speaker. If there are four or more people who wish to address the Board on the same side of the same topic, the Board will allow 10 minutes for that view to be heard. All those who sign up must accompany the speaker to the front of the Board room. We respectfully ask that you do not repeat the same message, but rather present varying concepts on the same theme. Thank you.

Subject
6.01 Report on Graduation Requirements Revisions (Ends 1)
Meeting
Jan 24, 2008 - Regular Business Meeting 6 p.m.
Category
6. Ends Development and Monitoring Agenda
Type
Action
PERTINENT FACTS:

1.The Superintendent began the revision process for Jeffco Public School graduation requirements in 2006 following changes to entrance requirements to Colorado state colleges and universities pursuant to Higher Education Admission Requirement (HEAR) guidelines.
2.Currently, Jefferson County Public Schools has the lowest required credits for graduation of any metropolitan school district, at 22. An increase of the required credits to 24 will align Jeffco Public Schools with metro school districts. The requirements will be closer to the HEAR expectations.
3.After a year of study and revision by a committee composed of district staff, district policy and regulation, IKF/-R, Graduation Requirements, includes the following major revisions:
  • 24 credits required for graduation from Jeffco Public Schools;
  • Graduation Plan for 7th-12th grades, signed by parents;
  • 2 years credits required in a world language;
  • Increase in credits required in mathematics (from 2 to 3 credits total); science (from 2 to 3 credits total); and, social studies;
  • Decrease in required elective credits from 10 to 8.5 credits;
  • 24 credits completed for graduation to participate in graduation ceremonies.
4.If approved, the new graduation requirements will begin with the 7th graders of the 2007-2008 school year.
5.The Board of Education has been informed of issues as the process proceeded through 2006 and 2007, in particular through public discussion in study sessions on October 25 and November 11, 2007 and January 17, 2008.
