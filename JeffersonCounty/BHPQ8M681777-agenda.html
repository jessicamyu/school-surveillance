PRESENTING STAFF:

Kathleen Askelson, chief financial officer

Dr. Stephanie Corbo, director of finance

Paul Niedermuller, independent auditor, CliftonLarsonAllen, LLP

 

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to receive information on the Comprehensive Annual Financial Report (CAFR) and Annual External Audit for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2019.   


BACKGROUND:

The Board of Education monitors the districtwide financial condition as outlined in Board executive limitation policies EL-6, Financial Administration and EL-5, Financial Planning/Budgeting. 

 

Colorado Revised Statute (C.R.S.) 29-1-606 (1)(b) requires that the Comprehensive Annual Financial Report be delivered to the Board of Education by November 30, 2019.  The Board received a copy of the CAFR on November 4, 2019. 

 

Colorado revised Statute 22-32-109 requires the district to prepare a comprehensive audited financial report. The financial report consists of financial information prepared by the district and audited by an independent firm and indicates the financial status of the district at the end of the reporting period. The CAFR also provides a starting point for the annual budget preparation process.

PURPOSE:

To provide the Board of Education with information on the Jeffco Innovation Acceleration Fund (JIAF) in its third year of implementation.


BACKGROUND:

On January 11, 2018 the Board of Education approved $1 million to fund the first Jeffco Innovation Acceleration Fund. Following that approval, staff facilitated an application process which resulted in the March 2018 identification and funding of 10 innovative ideas from Jeffco staff and the February 2019 identification and funding of 19 innovative ideas from Jeffco staff. On April 4, 2019, the Board of Education heard a presentation on modifying the Innovation Fund processes to allow for student participation. The Board of Education will hear a report on the current application process and a Jeffco Innovation Acceleration Fund Program Review.
