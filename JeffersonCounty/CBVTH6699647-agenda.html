Meeting

Recommended Action
to approve the request for a limited waiver of district policy to allow for the consumption of alcoholic beverages at the Miles for Smiles charity race event to benefit Two Angels Foundation on May 15, 2022, at the North Area Athletic Complex Stadium.

PERTINENT FACTS:
 

  1. In accordance with Board executive limitation policy EL-7, Asset Protection, “the superintendent shall not allow assets of the district to be unprotected, inadequately maintained, inappropriately used or unnecessarily risked…."
     
  2. Right Start Events manages a charity 5K run/walk race event for the non-profit Two Angels Foundation that was founded by Dawn and Roger Mohatt in memory of their daughters who were both diagnosed with a rare form of muscular dystrophy that ultimately took their lives. The foundation helps families with the financial burden of acquiring adaptive equipment for home and school thus facilitating a more normal life for special needs children.
     

  3. The Miles for Smiles 5K is a run/walk event followed by a 50-yard Adaptive Dash where children will use adaptive equipment to walk, roll or ride to the finish line. The race is typically held in May from 9:00 to 11:30 a.m. and draws approximately 250 participants, mostly families. Like last year, the race will begin and end in the stadium and utilize surrounding trails for the course. 
     

  4. The Miles for Smiles race event is in its 16th year. This is the fourth year the Miles for Smiles race event will be held at the North Area Athletic Complex (NAAC) Stadium.
     

  5. The organization receives donated beer that is provided as part of the race fee to participants 21 or older with valid identification at the event and limited to two beers maximum per qualified participant. In order to allow for such activity on district property, district administration requests that the Board of Education grant a limited waiver of district policies GBEC, Drug Free Workplace and KF-R, Community Use of School Facilities with respect to the prohibited use and possession of alcoholic beverages.
     

  6. Use of the NAAC Stadium and surrounding Jeffco facilities has been requested and approved through the Jeffco Facility Use Permit process.
     

  7. The event is contingent upon Right Start Events management acquiring all necessary permits and licenses from City of Arvada and appropriate insurance per district risk management. A copy of approved permits will be posted during the event.
     

  8. Approval of the limited waiver of these district policies at this time will allow the Right Start Events to finalize details of the events and to begin promoting these events to the community. 
     

  9. As per district policy KF, Jeffco Public Schools, events such as these promote community and business partnerships with stakeholders by making district buildings and facilities available to the community when not in use for school activities. The use of the NAAC Stadium and granting of a limited waiver to district policy allows for potential annual community events that celebrate and promote community spirit and citizen engagement.
     
  10. The Board of Education has approved similar limited waiver of district policy to allow for the consumption of alcoholic beverages on district property for this event and others since 2009.
