PERTINENT FACTS:

 

  1. In accordance with Board executive limitation policy 6, Financial Administration, and pursuant to Colorado Statute C.R.S. 22-41-102(6), the Board of Education must certify its mill levies to the Board of County Commissioners of Jefferson and Broomfield by December 15, 2011.  
     
  2. The required Certification Resolution for the General Fund and Bond Redemption Fund levies are based on the assessed valuation as certified by the Jefferson County Assessor on December 1, 2011 and as certified by the Broomfield County Assessor on December 1, 2011.  the counties have until December 10, 2011 to notify the district of the final assessed value certification.
     
  3. As reviewed with the Board of Education on December 1, 2011, the General Operating Mill Levy will be set at 26.252 mills with an additional 0.555 mills assessed for refunds and abatements and an additional 3.588 for the 1999 voter approved mill levy override and an additional 1.550 mills for the 1999 voter approved mill levy associated with the Performance Promise.  In addition, 5.526 mills are assessed for the 2004 voter approved mill levy override.  The total General Fund mill levy is 37.471 for 2011 taxes paid in 2012. 
     
  4. The bond Redemption Fund mill levy for 2011 taxes paid in 2012 is set at 11.250 mills.
     
  5. Since the counties have until December 10, 2011 for final assessed valuation numbers, preparing final documentation will require a special board meeting on December 14, 2011 to certify the final mill levy.
