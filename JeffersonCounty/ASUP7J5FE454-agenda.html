PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education and members of district Cabinet to receive the findings report of DeliverEd, an education consulting organization with expertise in strategy implementation, in preparation to updating the district's strategic plan.

 

BACKGROUND:

The team from DeliverEd will provide an analysis of the capacity review conducted on November 13 and 14, highlighting key strengths that can be developed and recommending action in areas where capacity needs to be built in order to focus resources to meet and report on the drivers in a strategic plan. The report will synthesize feedback gathered from the entire capacity review - including information gleaned during school visits, focus groups, and interviews with key staff. The team will present recommendations for strategic shifts that need to occur to see results for students.
