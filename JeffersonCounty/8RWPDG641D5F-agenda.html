Meeting
Mar 15, 2012 - Special Meeting-S/D Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion, Information

estimated time: 5:30 p.m. (estimated duration: 1 hour)

PRESENTING STAFF:
Dr. Cindy Stevenson, superintendent

Dr. Irene Griego, director, Office of Diversity and Inclusion

Student Representatives, Student Voices Council

Angelique Acevedo-Barron, assistant director, Office of Diversity and Inclusion

Griff Wirth, principal, Wheat Ridge High School

Eric Everding, executive director of School Management


PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to understand the process and actions taken in our district and schools to address issues of diversity affecting Jeffco students.

 

BACKGROUND:

On October 21, 2010, the Board of Education met with members of the Jeffco Community Diversity Advisory Council to gain an understanding of the role the Council, its members and the Office of Culture and Diversity play in supporting the district mission, ‘To provide a quality education that prepares all children for a successful future.”

 

This session will outline the strategies used in the district to engage students in appropriate behaviors and responses in preparation for a diverse world and global economy upon graduation.
