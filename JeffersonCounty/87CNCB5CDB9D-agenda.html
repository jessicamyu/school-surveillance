2.01 Board of Education Ends and Monitoring of District Performance
Meeting
Mar 27, 2010 - Special Meeting-Study/Dialogue Session, 8 a.m.
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion
Time: 8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.
Location: Colorado Association of School Boards office, 1200 Grant Street, Denver


PRESENTING STAFF:
Cindy Stevenson, superintendent
Ken Delay, CASB facilitator - pending
Randy Black, CASB facilitator - pending

PURPOSE:
The Board of Education will review its draft Ends statement to better refine and discuss how to monitor the district's performance in meeting the Board's goals.

BACKGROUND:
The Board of Education met on February 6, 15 and March 11 to discuss its goals and the work of the district related to Board Ends policies.

On March 11, 2010 in directing the superintendent on the Board values to guide budget development, a draft Ends statement was proposed, "Every child is provided a well prepared, well trained teacher in a school led by a well trained principal. Every child will demonstrate at least one year or more of academic growth each year."
