PRESENTING GUESTS:
Adam Franklin, general counsel, Colorado Public Employees’ Retirement Association (PERA)

Marcus Pennell, board member, PERA

PURPOSE:
The Board of Education will receive an overview of the current condition of PERA, the funding outlook and the PERA Board recommended package currently before state legislators.


BACKGROUND:
The Colorado Public Employees’ Retirement Association (PERA) was established in 1931 and is a substitute for social security for many groups of employees in Colorado, including public school employees, judicial, state and some local governments. PERA manages the largest pension fund in Colorado and the 24th largest public plan in the U.S. with 90 percent of PERA retirees living in Colorado.
