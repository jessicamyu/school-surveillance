PURPOSE:  

The Board of Education will hear from the superintendent on her plans for the year and board members will review their work together over the past year and prepare for the coming school year’s board work.

Agenda:

  • Welcome
  • BOE Self Assessment
  • Policy Updates
  • Jeffco Thrives 2025 & Board Ends
  • Reflections & Look Ahead

 

BACKGROUND:

The Board of Education meets in a retreat session in August to check its practices so that members and staff are responsive to changing needs, situations and resources. The superintendent supports this agenda item in accord with Board governance process policies GP 1-17 and board/staff linkage policies B/SL 1-4.
