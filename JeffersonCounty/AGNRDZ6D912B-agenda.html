PRESENTING STAFF:
Daniel M. McMinimee, superintendent

Kathleen Askelson, chief financial officer
Ed Bowditch, district lobbyist


PURPOSE:
The Board of Education will provide an update on the status of the Jeffco Schools budget, discuss the legislative staff uniform mill levy proposal and its impact on Jefferson County, changes to the state residential assessment rate (impact of Gallagher Amendment) and any other issues of mutual interest with Jefferson County legislators.

BACKGROUND:
The Board of Education offers the opportunity with Jefferson County state legislators to meet together during the legislative session to discuss pending legislation and other issues related to public education in Colorado.
