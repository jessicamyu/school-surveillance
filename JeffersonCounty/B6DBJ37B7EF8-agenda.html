Brian Kelso, managing director-Robert W. Baird & Co.
Yoon-Sook Moon, Robert W. Baird & Co.


PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to receive an update on the current bond market, timeline and structure for issuance of bonds.

 

BACKGROUND:

On November 6, 2018, voters approved a $567 million dollar bond package for school and district capital construction projects. The district has been working with the underwriting team on timeframe for the structure and sale of the bonds. Staff has been developing the investment plan for the bond proceeds.
