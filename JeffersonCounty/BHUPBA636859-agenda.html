Action
Recommended Action
to adopt the resolution declaring the results of the November 5, 2019 regular biennial school board and coordinated election presented prior to the meeting.

estimated time: 5 p.m. (estimated duration: 2 minutes) 
 

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. Board Governance Process policy, GP-1, Governance Commitment, concerns governance commitment and the purpose of the school board.  On November 5, 2019, an election was held to elect two members to the school board for districts 3 and 4.
     
  2. Per state law and the district's intergovernmental agreement with Jefferson and Broomfield counties, the canvass board has until November 27, 2019 to provide a certified official abstract of votes to the designated election official and the school district.
     
  3. As the final official abstract of votes cast for all candidates is available and results are not in question, a resolution indicating the newly elected board members has been prepared for the Board of Education prior to the meeting.

4. Board Reorganization and Election of Officers
Subject
4.01 Board Reorganization and Election of Officers (GP-4, 5)
Meeting
Dec 2, 2019 - Special Meeting-Oath of Office
Category
4. Board Reorganization and Election of Officers
Type
Action
Recommended Action
to reorganize Board officers through the election of a president and vice president of the Board of Education; to appoint a second vice president, treasurer and secretary; and, to appoint Kathleen Askelson to the position of assistant treasurer and Helen Neal to the position of assistant secretary.

estimated time: 5:41 p.m. (estimated duration: 10 minutes) 
 

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. Board Governance Process policies GP-4, Board Job Description, and GP-5, President's Role, address the various Board member roles.  In following district policy BDA, Board Organizational Meeting, the following action is brought forward to the Board of Education.
     
  2. On November 5, 2019, an election was held to elect two members to the school board for districts 3 and 4.
     
  3. State law determines that the Board of Education elects president and vice president and either elects or appoints a treasurer and secretary upon election of new members. District policy BDA, Board Organizational Meeting, specifies that the Board will appoint a treasurer and secretary.
     
  4. It has been the practice of the Board of Education to appoint a second vice president, treasurer, secretary, an assistant treasurer and assistant secretary at this time as well.
