Meeting
Apr 23, 2009 - Special Meeting-Study/Dialogue Session, 5 p.m.
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion
Estimated Time: 6 p.m.
Location: Seminar Room, 4th fl., Education Center, 1829 Denver West Dr., Golden, CO

Presenting Staff:
Cindy Stevenson, superintendent
Lorie Gillis, chief financial officer
Debbie Backus, chief instructional officer
Laura Munro, executive director, Grants and Federal Programs

Purpose:
For the Board of Education to receive the recommendations of Cabinet for the use of federal stimulus and Budgeting for Results funds to meet the Board of Education’s identified results for students in Jeffco Public Schools.

Background:
The Board of Education will adopt a budget for the 2009-2010 school year in June 2009. Due to the receipt of federal funds from the American Recovery and Reinvstment Act of 2009, the superintendent and her leadership Cabinet have reviewed how best to follow rules and regulations related to the expenditure of federal and district funds to meet President Obama’s and Board of Education goals.

In order to prepare a draft executive summary budget for public comment in May and June, the superintendent outlines her budget recommendations for Board discussion.
