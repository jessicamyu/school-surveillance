  • Every student will receive the skills, support and educational opportunities needed to achieve his or her full potential.

4.02 Public Comment (Agenda Related) via Zoom / Asunto de la agenda - Comentarios del público (Relacionados a la agenda)
Meeting
Feb 4, 2021 - Regular Meeting
Category
4. Public Agenda Part One
Type
Information

estimated time: 6:06 p.m. (estimated duration: up to 1 hour)

 

The Board listens to the community’s issues of concern in Public Comment on its agenda. Sign up online here to speak to the Board of Education.     
 

At this time, the Board listens to concerns on topics related to items on the agenda for up to one hour.  If your topic is unrelated to the meeting's agenda please submit written comments to board@jeffco.k12.co.us. We listen with respect and ask those who address the Board to do the same. In order to respect all persons addressing the Board, we request that issues, rather than personalities, be addressed. Any resident, staff member or parent of the district who has completed the requested information on the public comment roster and has the necessary equipment and internet access to join a virtual meeting via Zoom may speak. There is a three minute time limit per individual speaker. Additional guidelines for public comment.  Additional direction will be emailed to those who sign up for public comment (i.e., link to join the board meeting) prior to the meeting.

 

If there are a number of speakers commenting on the same topic, we ask that remarks be consolidated and new thoughts or concepts be shared. It is not the number of people who address the Board that leads to better decision-making, rather the content of your presentation. So, we respectfully ask that you do not repeat the same message of an earlier speaker. If there are four or more people who sign up to address the Board they will have 10 minutes for that group's view to be presented. All those who sign up as a group must be able to accompany the speaker virtually (see additional emailed direction). We respectfully ask that you not repeat the same message, but rather present varying concepts on the same theme.  If more than 20 individuals sign up to speak to the Board, individuals will have two minutes and groups will have five minutes for a collective message to the Board.

The Board will listen to Public Comment for up to one hour if necessary and, by a majority vote, may extend the time for high volume meetings.  Thank you.

 

Hora estimada: (time) (duración estimada: hasta 1 hora)
 

La Junta Directiva escucha los temas de interés de la comunidad en los Comentarios del Público de su agenda. Registrarse en línea aquí para dirigirse a la Junta Directiva de Educación. (estará disponible a las 10 a.m. del viernes)

Por el momento, la Junta atiende inquietudes sobre temas relacionados a items on the agenda hasta por una hora. Si su tema no está relacionado con la agenda de esta reunión, rogamos enviar sus comentarios por escrito a board@jeffco.k12.co.us. Escuchamos con respeto y pedimos lo mismo a los que se dirigen a la Junta Directiva. A fin de respetar a todas las personas que se dirigen a la Junta Directiva, pedimos que se enfoquen en los asuntos más que en las personalidades. Cualquier residente, miembro del personal o padre de familia del distrito que ha completado su información en la lista para Comentarios del público y tiene el equipo e internet necesarios para participar de una reunión virtual mediante Zoom puede hablar. Hay un límite de tres minutos por orador individual. Directrices adicionales para comentarios del público. Se enviarán instrucciones adicionales por correo electrónico a aquellos que se registraron para los Comentarios del público. (ej.: enlace para la participar de la reunión) antes de la reunión.

 

Si hay varios oradores con comentarios sobre el mismo tema, pedimos que se consoliden las observaciones y que se compartan nuevas ideas o conceptos. No es el número de personas que se dirigen a la Junta Directiva lo que guía en la mejor toma de decisiones, sino el contenido de su presentación. Por esta razón, pedimos respetuosamente no repetir el mismo mensaje de un orador anterior. Si hay cuatro personas que como grupo se registraron para dirigirse a la Junta Directiva, ellos tendrán 10 minutos para presentar su punto de vista. Todos aquellos que se registran como grupo deben acompañar de manera virtual al orador (ver instrucciones adicionales enviadas por correo electrónico). Respetuosamente pedimos que no repitan el mismo mensaje, más bien presenten conceptos distintos sobre el mismo tema. Si se registran más de 20 individuos para dirigirse a la Junta, los individuos tendrán dos minutos y los grupos tendrán cinco minutos para dar un mensaje colectivo hacia la Junta.

La Junta escuchará los comentarios del público hasta por una hora si es necesario y, por mayoría de votos, puede extender el tiempo para reuniones de alta participación. Gracias.

 

Rogamos abrir el anexo siguiente el día 11 de agosto después de las 8 a.m., para conocer el orden de oradores invitados a la reunión para el comentario público.

Recommended Action
To adopt the proclamation recognizing National School Counseling Week in Jeffco Public Schools, February 1-5, 2021.

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. In accordance with Board executive limitation, EL-11, Communication and Counsel to the Board, and governance process policy GP-4, Board Job Description, the Board of Education requested a proclamation be brought forward to recognize National School Counselor Week in Jeffco Public Schools, February 1-5, 2021.
     
  2. Jeffco Public Schools has approximately 200 school counselors who positively impact Jeffco students and families across the school district.
     
  3. Since the beginning of the counselors' work with students during the COVID19 pandemic, school counselors have continued to support the three domains of counseling: academics, career and college readiness, and social emotional learning.
        
  4. School counselors have also wholeheartedly jumped in and assisted in a wide variety of necessary COVID19 tasks.  They have adapted their typical duties to be performed in a virtual environment and have added new responsibilities to meet students' needs during the pandemic.
     
  5. Their dedication to student engagement and achievement is unwavering as evidenced by their commitment to support staff, students and their families in new and creative ways.
     
  6. School counselors are an integral part of the comprehensive mental health team in our schools and work daily toward maximizing student success, ensuring equitable outcomes, and promoting social emotional development.
     
  7. To this end, the Board of Education would like to recognize that February 1-5, 2021 is National School Counseling Week.

Type
Action
Recommended Action
to adopt the resolution conditionally approving a five-year renewal contract with Addenbrooke Classical Academy including the request for district and state waivers of law and policy.

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. In accordance with Board executive limitation policy, EL-13, Charter Schools Application and Monitoring, the superintendent shall not allow existing charter school contracts to continue if fiscal jeopardy or failure to make consistent progress towards their stated objectives is a likely outcome or is evident. In addition, the superintendent shall not allow existing charter schools to operate in a manner that would jeopardize the learning or well-being of their students.
     
  2. The charter school contract for Addenbrooke Classical Academy expires on June 30, 2021.
     
  3. District regulation, LBD-R, Charter Schools, states charter schools must submit applications for renewal no later than December 1 of the year prior to the year in which the charter expires. Addenbrooke Classical Academy submitted a renewal application by December 1, 2020.
     
  4. The Board of Education accepted the renewal application for study on December 9, 2020.
     
  5. In accordance with district regulation, LBD-R, Charter Schools, the Board of Education will rule on applications to renew no later than February 1, of the year the charter expires or a mutually agreed upon alternative date. By mutual agreement, the Board of Education is scheduled to make a decision on the renewal application at its February 4, 2021 regular business meeting.
     
  6. Jeffco Schools Cabinet members recommend adopting the resolution conditionally approving a five-year renewal charter contract with Addenbrooke Classical Academy.
     
  7. Materials regarding the evaluation of the renewal application by district staff will be provided to the Board of Education prior to the February 4, 2021 meeting.

  5. On January 19, 2021, competitive bids and unit prices were received and publicly read, from two pre-qualified bidders for the DW Mechanical 2021 project.
  6. The low bidder is US Engineering for the amount of $3,523,850.
  7. The project is over budget by $1,311,106 primarily due to the expanded scope of work at Thomson ES.  The cause of the overage was discussed at the January 21, 2021 Capital Asset Advisory Committee (CAAC) meeting.  During the pre-design assessment the consultant reviewed the condition of the 16 rooftop units at Thomson and determined they were at or approaching the end of life cycle (30 years).  The units were replaced in 1995, 26 years ago.  The scope review determined that a cascading failure of the units was likely and that instead of replacing them as they failed it would be more economical to replace them all at once.  If the units fail individually, building maintenance funds the replacement which can take a significant amount of time to locate and install a replacement unit.  The area in the school served by the replacement will be down, causing discomfort to the occupants since the unit provides both cooling and heating.
  8. On January 21, 2021 the CAAC voted to approve the scope and budget change.  Of seven CAAC members in attendance, six recommended approval, with the seventh not registering a vote due to technical issues.
  9. Funding for the project is included in the 2018 Capital Improvement Program.  US Engineering is a Kansas City, MO based mechanical contractor with an office in Westminster, CO.
  10. Project work will start as soon as the contract is fully executed. Project occupancy completion is scheduled for August 2021 with completion in October 2021.
