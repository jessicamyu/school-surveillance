PURPOSE: 
The Mayor and City Council of the City of Edgewater requested time to discuss issues of mutual interest with the Board of Education, Superintendent and leadership staff for schools in Edgewater.

BACKGROUND:
On February 8, the City Manager of Edgewater requested a meeting with the two board members whose director district areas encompass Edgewater schools and the district staff who work with Edgewater schools.  On Thursday, February 24, an Edgewater City Council member attended the Board of Education study session, talked to a Board member, found that through a miscommunication a requested change in the invitation to meet with the Edgewater Mayor and City Council to include the full Board of Education was in order.
