Subject
3.01 Director District 1 Applicant Interviews
Meeting
Aug 10, 2009 - Special Meeting, 5:30 p.m.
Category
3. Discussion Agenda
Type
Discussion
Time: 5:35 p.m.
Location: Seminar Room, 4th floor, Education Center, 1829 Denver West Dr., Golden

Presenting Staff:
Cindy Stevenson, superintendent

Purpose:
To conduct interviews with the applicants for the open Board of Education director district 1 seat.

Background:
On July 8, 2009 the Board of Education declared a vacancy in director district 1 upon the resignation of Board member Scott Benefield. The Board of Education requested eligible individuals apply for the opening with a letter of interest, resume and date of birth by August 3, 2009. By law, the Board has 60 days from the declared vacancy date of July 8, 2009 to appoint a resident for Jefferson County's school board director district 1 (September 7, 2009).

On July 8, the Board determined it would interview applicants on Monday, August 10 for 20 minute interviews. The Board plans to reach a consensus for appointment following the interviews.

The following individuals applied to fill the Board seat (letters of application and resumes are available through the links): Robin Johnson, Edward Duran, Tom Griego, Cody McNutt, and Harvey Burns.
