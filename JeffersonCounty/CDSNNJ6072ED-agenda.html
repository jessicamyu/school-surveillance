3.01 Opt Out: Colorado Family and Medical Leave Insurance (FAMLI) Program (EL-3, 4)
Meeting
Jun 8, 2022 - Special Meeting-Study/Dialogue Session
Category
3. Consent Agenda
Type
Action
Recommended Action
to formally select opt out of the Family and Medical Leave Insurance (FAMLI) Program, with an option to opt in at a later date

PERTINENT FACTS:

 

  1. On May 11, 2022, the Board of Education received and discussed information on the Colorado Family and Medical Leave Insurance (FAMLI) Program.
     
  2. The Colorado Family and Medical Leave Insurance (FAMLI) Program was approved by Colorado voters in November 2020. The FAMLI Program provides income protection for personal and family medical leaves, as allowed by new state statute.
     
  3. Local governments, including school districts, have a choice to opt in or opt out of their program, and if a local government chooses to opt out, must do so by July 1, 2022.
     
  4. The program goes into effect on January 1, 2023, with employer and employee contributions and payments to employees may begin on January 1, 2024. Total cost of participation is 0.9% of total payroll, funded by employer and employee in a percentage to be determined by the local government but no more than 50% by the employee.
     
  5. Given the Board’s May 11 discussion of the staff recommendation (to opt out of the FAMLI Program at this time, with the option to reconsider and opt in at a later date) the attached correspondence to the Colorado Department of Labor and Employment FAMLI Program is provided for Board action.
