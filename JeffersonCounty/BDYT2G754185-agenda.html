2.02 Strategic Plan & Response to Assessment Data (EL-11, GP-4)
Meeting
Aug 26, 2019 - Study/Dialogue Session-Retreat
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion

estimated time: 2 p.m. (estimated duration: 2 hours) 

PRESENTING STAFF:
Jason E. Glass, superintendent

Matt Flores, chief academic officer

Kristopher Schuh, chief of schools secondary

Renee Nicothodes, chief of schools elementary

Tammy Schiff, chief communications officer


PURPOSE:
The Board of Education will review Jeffco Public School’s strategic plan and look ahead to ensure the direction aligns with Board and community goals in response to recent state testing data.

 

BACKGROUND:
In 2017/18, Dr. Glass followed up the Jeffco 2020 strategic plan with the new Jeffco Generations strategic plan to increase student performance through three strategies of Learning, Conditions for Learning, and Readiness for Learning. This fall, the district received state testing data and has developed an action plan in response to that data.

Student achievement data will be formally presented to the Board of Education at the regular meeting of September 5, 2019.
 

The superintendent provides this information in accord with Board executive limitation policy EL-11, Communication and Counsel to the Board, and governance process policy GP-4, Board Job Description.
