Recommended Action
to reorganized Board officers through the election of a president and vice president of the Board of Education, to appoint a second vice president, treasurer and secretary; and, to reappoint Lorie Gillis to the position of assistant treasurer and Helen Neal to the position of assistant secretary.
PERTINENT FACTS:
1.Board Governance Process policies, GP-4, Board Job Description; and, GP-5, President’s Role, address the various Board member roles. On November 3, 2009 an election was held to elect three members to the school board.
2.State law determines that the Board of Education elects a president and vice president and appoints a treasurer and secretary upon election of new members.
3.It has been the practice of the Board of Education to appoint a second vice president and an assistant treasurer and assistant secretary at this time as well.
