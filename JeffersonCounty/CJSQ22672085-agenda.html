2.01 2022-23 Enrollment & Budget Update
Meeting
Nov 2, 2022 - Special Meeting-Study/Dialogue Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion, Information

estimated time:  3 p.m. (estimated duration: 45 minutes) 

 

 

PRESENTING STAFF:

Brenna Copeland, chief financial officer

Brian Sammons, director, budget and Treasury

Jeremy Felker, executive director, Student Data Privacy and Reporting

 

PURPOSE:

The Board of Education will receive information about the district’s preliminary student enrollment count and the estimated fiscal impacts of this enrollment.

 

BACKGROUND:

Each year, the district conducts a student count in early October, often called the October Count. The preliminary and unaudited results of this count will be shared with the board and the public. This same data will be a starting point for the multi-year projections that the district prepares in the coming months and typically releases.

 

The October count data drives most of the district’s revenue from public sources. The district included a forecast of enrollment in the budget that was adopted June 8, 2022 and subsequently amended September 15, 2022. The staff presentation will include a preliminary estimate for how the emerging enrollment data will impact revenue in the 2022-23 fiscal year. Additional data will emerge in the coming months (e.g., the count of at risk students) that will further refine the district’s revenue projection. The district plans to bring forward a revised budget in January 2023 for the 2022-23 fiscal year.

Lisa Relou, Chief of Communications & Strategy

Dr. Kym LeBlanc-Esparza, Deputy Superintendent

Renee Nicothodes, Chief Academic Officer

Julie Tolleson, Chief Legal Officer

 

PRESENTING GUEST:

Berrick Abramson, Keystone


PURPOSE:
To provide the Board with a final update on Regional Opportunities for Thriving Schools and give Board members an opportunity for dialogue prior to the vote on November 10, 2022. The update includes the following:

 

  • Overview of Board Materials for Review: 
    • Keystone Group report on community conversations and survey results
    • FCB committees and reports which will be published on 11/4
  • 2023-24 Pre-K Plan 
  • Student Success, Center Program Transition Plans
  • 2023-24 Transportation Fees & Reliability
  • Review of District’s recommendation for a vote of the Board on 11/10 and overview of 11/10 BOE meeting schedule

 

 

BACKGROUND:

On April 7, 2022, the Board of Education voted unanimously to direct the Superintendent to prepare a multi-phase consolidation plan to ensure thriving schools and extraordinary student experiences across the district. Regional Opportunities for Thriving Schools is an initiative that was devised in the Spring of 2022 to respond to the Board’s request for a comprehensive plan to ensure thriving schools with enrollment to support extraordinary student experiences across our district.

PRESENTING STAFF:

Brenna Copeland, chief financial officer

Christie Moss, controller


PRESENTING GUESTS:
Jessica Keene, chair, Financial Oversight Committee (FOC)

Christine Havlin, member, Financial Oversight Committee (FOC)

 

PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to review the districtwide financial condition through the fourth quarter of the 2021-22 school year. For the Board to dialogue with the Financial Oversight Committee (FOC) regarding the the district's financial wellbeing.

 

BACKGROUND:

Consistent with statutory requirements, the district prepares and publishes quarterly financial reports that are interim and unaudited financial statements. The reports are posted publicly on our financial transparency website, and reviewed with our Financial Oversight Committee (FOC).

 

Members of the Financial Oversight Committee (FOC) will share a set of conclusions and recommendations, based in part on their review of the quarterly financial report and in part on other meeting content and discussions.

 

The quarterly report and separate presentation prepared by staff are attached. Additionally, the memo of conclusions and recommendations from the Financial Oversight Committee (FOC) is attached.
