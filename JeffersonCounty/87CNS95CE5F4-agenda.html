PURPOSE:
Due to three board members who are new to the district budget and negotiations processes, the Board of Education requested an overview of both processes.

BACKGROUND:
The Board of Education directed the superintendent to proceed with budget development for the 2010/2011 school year in September. The Board must adopt a budget by June 30 and the negotiations process with the district employee associations is a critical component of budget development.
