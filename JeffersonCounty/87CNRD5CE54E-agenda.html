To receive feedback from McLain Community High School students on a Board goal of students being engaged in their learning.

At its August 2009 retreat, the Board of Education outlined a series of topics to discuss with community members throughout the school year. One of the topics identified by the Board was how Jeffco students are engaged in their learning and what the district does to keep students engaged.

On December 10, 2009 and January 7, 2010, the Board of Education finalized questions to address with constituent groups regarding this topic. The Board identified McLain students as a group of students with whom to discuss this topic.

Location: Library, 2nd fl., McLain Community High School, 13600 W. 2nd Pl., Lakewood 80228

PRESENTING STAFF:
Cindy Stevenson, superintendent
Lorie Gillis, chief financial officer


PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to discuss its goals and priorities to increase student achievement which will identify parameters for the superintendent in the development of an adopted 2010/2011 budget by June 30, 2010.

BACKGROUND:
At its August 2009 retreat and on September 10, 2009, the Board of Education discussed its vision and tradeoffs (for whom, at what cost) for increasing student achievement in Jeffco Public Schools. The Board of Education goals (Ends policies) provide parameters for the superintendent's development of the district budget.
