PRESENTING STAFF:

Dr. Syna Morgan, chief academic officer

Dr. Heather MacGillivary, director, Assessment and Research

Matt Flores, executive director, Curriculum and Instruction

Jennifer Steele, director, Curriculum and Instruction

 

PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to receive an update on Ends 1 and 2 in reading and writing.

 

BACKGROUND:

The Board has requested a presentation on Ends 1 and 2, the math update was presented on October 15, 2015. This presentation will provide an update on reading and writing.  Board Ends policy 1  states "every student will master the Colorado Content Standards at grade level," with reading and writing targets:  

 

  • The percentage of proficient/advanced third grade students in reading on TCAP will increase from 80% to 85% by August of 2015. 
  • The percentage of proficient/advanced students in writing on TCAP will increase by August 2014 to:
  • Elementary – 64%
  • Middle level – 66%
  • High school – 59%

 

Board Ends policy 2 states "every student will achieve one year's growth, or more as needed to 'catch up,' in each year of school and be ready for the next level."  The Board target states that the growth measures for the median growth percentile (MGP) will reach 50th MGP or growth measures over 50th MGP will increase by:


For Free/Reduced Lunch Eligible students 

  • in writing from 47 to 50
  • in reading from 48 to 50

​For students with disabilities

  • in writing from 45 to 50
  • in reading from 48 to 50

For English learners

  • in writing from 52 to 54
  • in reading from 54 to 56

For Advanced Learning Plan (ALP) students

  • in writing from 54 to 56
  • in reading from 57 to 59
