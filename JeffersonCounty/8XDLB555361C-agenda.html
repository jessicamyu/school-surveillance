PRESENTING STAFF:

Dr. Cindy Stevenson, superintendent

Dr. Heather Beck, chief academic officer

Dr. Carol Eaton, executive director, Educational Research and Design

 

PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to receive a presentation from the Department of Research and Design demonstrating the use of data driven decision making and problem solving for student achievement.

 

BACKGROUND:

The Board of Education receives its annual report on district achievement data on September 6.  That report is part of the monitoring of achievement of the Board’s Ends policies (outlined in Board Ends policies -1, -2, -3).

 

This study session demonstrates the importance of the use of data on student achievement in goal setting and in developing the Unified Improvement Plan (UIP) for the district and for all schools.

The presentation will be available on August 30, below.
