2.03 Board Director Boundaries (EL-11)
Meeting
Feb 28, 2013 - Special Meeting-S/D Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion

estimated time: 7:15 p.m. (estimated duration: 30 minutes) 
 

PRESENTING STAFF:

Cindy Stevenson, superintendent

Tim Reed, director, Facilities Planning

 

PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to review proposed revisions to Board of Education director district boundaries to comply with state statute C.R.S 22-31-110.

 

BACKGROUND:

State law requires school districts with director district plans of representation to determine the population in each director district at least every four years and prior to December 31.  If each director district does not contain substantially the same number of persons as each director district, new boundaries will be proposed to correct the imbalance.  C.R.S. 22-31-109 also requires districts be contiguous, compact, and as nearly equal in population as possible.

 

Director district boundaries have not been revised since January 2003.  With the current census data available, new boundaries are deemed necessary due to population shifts for director districts.

 

The Board of Education will be provided with proposed boundary changes for discussion prior to presentation of final boundary revisions for approval on March 7, 2013.
