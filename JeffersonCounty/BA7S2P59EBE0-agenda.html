PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to receive information on what it takes to grow and sustain the International Baccalaureate continuum, accessible to thousands of Jeffco Public Schools students, which equips learners for life in the 21st century.

 

BACKGROUND:

International Baccalaureate (IB) first came to Jeffco Public Schools at Lakewood High School in 1996. Since its beginnings in Jeffco, IB has expanded across the district to now include multiple primary years, middle years, and diploma programs, and has the only career-related program in the state of Colorado. These IB programs currently reside in Standley Lake High School in the north, Dakota Ridge High School in the south, and Lakewood High School and Alameda International Jr./Sr. High School in the central area of the district.  For 23 years, Jeffco students have benefited from the rigor and international recognition of IB programming. This link will provide a basic overview of the IB Program: https://vimeo.com/92725628.
