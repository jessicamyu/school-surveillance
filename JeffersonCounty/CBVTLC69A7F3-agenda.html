PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to reflect on and review its practices to date as a board which were created through its December 2021 retreat work.

 

BACKGROUND:

After seating three new members to the school board on November 29, 2021, the five member Board of Education met in a retreat session to discuss onboarding new members, priorities of the new board and how to maximize members’ time together.
 

Reflection questions have been shared with board members in preparation for this work to continue building on conditions for winning in board work together.

PRESENTING STAFF:

Dan Cohan, chief of schools-secondary, DAC liaison

PRESENTING GUESTS:

Carsten Engebretsen, chair, District Accountability Committee (DAC)

Laura Velapoldi, vice-chair, DAC; chair, Budget Subcommittee, DAC

Lorri Avery, co-chair, District Unified Improvement Plan (DUIP) Subcommittee, DAC

Jill Fellman, co-chair, District Unified Improvement Plan (DUIP) Subcommittee, DAC

Evie Hudak, chair, Family, School, Community Partnership (FSCP) Subcommittee, DAC

Jeff Baucum, chair, Charters Subcommittee, DAC

Therese Rednor, chair, Area Accountability Representatives, DAC

 

PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to dialogue with the District Accountability Committee leaders and receive updates on:

  • the SAC Survey, specifically the budget recommendations for FY23
  • the District Unified Improvement Plan (DUIP) process;
  • the Family, School, Community Partnership work; and,
  • an update on Charter School Reviews
  • any other DAC updates

 

BACKGROUND:

Following the work of the DAC budget subcommittee, the full District Accountability Committee (DAC) reached agreement on its 2022/23 budget recommendations at its 4/5/2022 meeting, which will be presented to the Board of Education, in compliance with state statute CRS § 22-11-302 (2016.)
 

In addition, the DAC subcommittees (DUIP, Budget, Family School Community Partnership, and Charter Review) will provide updates on their work.
 

The DUIP subcommittee provided its recommendations to the Board in Fall 2021 at the 10-21-2021  board meeting prior to the Board’s action on the DUIP in compliance with state statute CRS § 22-11-302 (2016.)
 

The DAC Executive Committee (Lorri Avery, Carsten Engebretsen, Jill Fellman, Evie Hudak, Therese Rednor, Jeff Baucum, Laura Velapoldi) welcomes any direction from the Board for future DAC work in the 2022/2023 school year.
