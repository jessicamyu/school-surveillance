Meeting

Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type

Goals
Ends 8 - Board Effectiveness

Estimated Time:  5:00 p.m.

Location:  5th Floor, Board Room

Estimated time:  (estimated duration: 1.5 hours )

 

PRESENTING STAFF: 

Cindy Stevenson, superintendent

Lorie Gillis, chief financial officer

 

PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to receive information on the Citizens' Budget Academy and the Budget Development Process for 2012-2013 and 2013-2014.

 

BACKGROUND:

In accordance with Board Ends policy 8, Effective and Responsible Teamwork, the “Jeffco Board will be effective and responsible and will function as a highly effective team and makes the following commitment to our community: Effective and transparent oversight of the fiscal condition of the district and of the allocation of funds to meet Ends policies.”
 

The Board of Education has requested a presentation and discussion on the Citizens' Budget Academy and the proposed 2012/2013 and 2013/2014 Budget Development Process.
 

Lorie Gillis, chief financial officer, will present preliminary budgetary information along with recommendations for an effective, communicative and transparent 2012/2013 and 2013/2014 Budget Development Process.

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. An important component of the Board of Education's governance model is GP-6, Annual Work Plan, which provides a schedule of monitoring for the work of the superintendent.
     
  2. The monitoring report and policy on Board executive limitation, EL-12, School Safety, are scheduled annually for review in June.  Due to other pressing Board matters, the monitoring report was postponed to August.
