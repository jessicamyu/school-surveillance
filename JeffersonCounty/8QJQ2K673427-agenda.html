2.02 Facilities Conditions Assessment Update (EL-8)
Meeting
Jan 26, 2012 - Special Meeting-S/D Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion

Estimated time: 7:00 pm (estimated duration: 30 minutes) 

 

PRESENTING STAFF:

Cynthia Stevenson, superintendent

Steven Bell, chief operating officer

Mike Moonan, director of capital programs, construction

Tim Reed, director of capital programs, facilities planning

 

PURPOSE:

The Board of Education will review the executive summary of the annual update of the Facilities Conditions Assessment Summary of Findings first presented to the Board in September of 2010.

 

BACKGROUND:

Board Executive Limitation EL-8 Facilities, states that, “the Superintendent may not operate without clear priorities when building, renovating, maintaining or repairing facilities within available resources.”
 

The Facilities Conditions Assessment Summary of Findings presented to the Board in September of 2010 consisted of a capacity analysis, an educational adequacy assessment, a building condition assessment, and a review of all site and building systems with a life cycle renewal forecast.
 

On an annual basis, staff updates the Summary of Findings data.  In addition to updated data on the physical condition of the buildings an enrollment forecast has been completed and is included in the Summary of Findings data.
 

An updated Facilities Conditions Assessment Summary of Findings dated January 2012 will be published after review by the Board.

The 2012 legislative session has produced a few bills of interest to Jefferson County Public Schools.
