PURPOSE:
To receive feedback from community leaders in the Strategic Planning and Advisory Council (SPAC) on charter schools and the charter school application process in Jefferson County.

Please open the attachment below to review the SPAC meeting agenda and questions to be discussed with the Board:


BACKGROUND:
At its August retreat, the Board of Education outlined a series of topics to discuss with community members throughout the school year. The first topic, charter schools, coincides with the Board's legal responsibility to accept and determine action on charter school applications for Jeffco Public Schools.

On September 3, the Board of Education will develop questions members have on the charter school application process. The SPAC is one of the Board's advisory committees on topics impacting the education of students in Jeffco Public Schools.
