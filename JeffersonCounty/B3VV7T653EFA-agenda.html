PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to hear from staff on the districtwide financial condition through the fourth quarter of the 2017/2018 school year in order to monitor district finances.

 

BACKGROUND:

Each quarter, in compliance with Board executive limitation policies EL-5, Financial Planning and Budgeting, and EL-6, Financial Administration, and as outlined in Board governance process policy GP-13, Committee Structure, the Board meets to review and monitor quarterly financial reports. Staff identifies the funds, departments and/or schools to monitor closely throughout the year. Members of the Financial Oversight Committee attend quarterly financial updates for the opportunity to provide a report to and dialogue with the Board of Education.

Oct 10, 2018 - Study/Dialogue Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion

estimated time: 10:20 a.m. (estimated duration: 45 minutes)

PRESENTING STAFF:
Dr. Jason Glass, superintendent
 

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to review the resolutions directing the efforts of the CASB during the 2019 legislative session and which provide guidance to the Board's selected delegate, Ron Mitchell, to the Fall Conference and Delegate Assembly on October 19-20, 2018 at the Cheyenne Mountain Resort in Colorado Springs.

BACKGROUND:
The Colorado Association of School Boards (CASB) holds its annual Fall Conference and Delegate Assembly in October in preparation for legislative work in Colorado on public education. There are proposed resolutions for the 2019 legislative session as well as standing resolutions on local government, finance and student achievement. 

At the Delegate Assembly, positions are taken on resolutions which affect state school districts.  As one of the largest school districts in the state, the work of CASB may impact the district's work.
 

Please open the link provided to review CASB 2018 proposed resolutions.
