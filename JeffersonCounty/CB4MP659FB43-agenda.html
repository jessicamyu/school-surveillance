PURPOSE:

Board members will revisit their work dating back to December 1, 2021, the findings of the recent academic review, and utilize pre-work to engage in a high-level discussion about the profile of a thriving school. Using this framework, the Board will work collaboratively to start to articulate a vision and mission, values, and ends.

 

BACKGROUND:

Board members and the superintendent work together to ensure board members receive information needed to effectively represent constituents and fulfill individual, Board of Education and superintendent goals.

 

Jeffco Public Schools is beginning the process of updating its strategy and launching a 2025 (3-year strategic plan).

 

Previous presentations on the superintendent’s academic review in the prior semester (September 9, October 20, November 10, December 15, 2021) previewed the need for revising Jeffco Generations. Superintendent Dorland shared with the Board who articulated an appetite to move quickly on a consistent strategic direction to which the organization can align.
 

On January 12, the board began the process of working toward the next iteration of a strategic plan for Jeffco Public Schools.
