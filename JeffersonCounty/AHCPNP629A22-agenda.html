6.02 Public Comment (Agenda Related)
Meeting
Feb 9, 2017 - Regular Meeting
Category
6. Public Agenda Part One
Type
Information

estimated time: 6:15 p.m. (estimated duration: up to two hours)

 

The Board listens to the community’s issues of concern in two Public Comment sections on its agenda. Sign up online here to speak to the Board of Education.   
 

Due to the high volume of individuals and groups who have signed up to address the Board of Education on February 9 in public comment, if the meeting rooms are full, those individuals will be allowed into the Board Room as their time to speak approaches.  Speakers will be called up by the Board President five individuals/groups at a time, to que up down the center aisle of the Board Room. One speaker will be asked to introduce all group members.
 

The list of speakers and groups signed up to speak to the Board of Education will be posted online to the meeting agenda, item 6.02, Public Agenda Part One, after 3:30 pm. The Board extended the usual amount of time reserved for public comment part one to two hours. At this point in time, individuals will receive one minute and groups will receive three minutes to address the Board of Education.


In Part One of Public Comment, the Board listens to concerns on topics related to items on this evening's agenda for up to two hours.  Part Two comments will be heard at the end of the meeting under Part Two Public Comment.  Board members or staff will respond during this meeting or at a later time. We listen with respect and ask those who address the Board to do the same. In order to respect all persons addressing the Board, we request that the audience not applaud individual speakers and that issues, rather than personalities, be addressed. Any resident or staff member of the district who has completed the requested information on the public comment roster may speak. Additional guidelines for public comment. 

 

If there are a number of speakers commenting on the same topic this evening, we ask that remarks be consolidated and new thoughts or concepts shared. It is not the number of people who address the Board that leads to better decision-making, rather the content of your presentation. So, we respectfully ask that you do not repeat the same message of an earlier speaker. If there are four or more people who wish to address the Board on the same side of the same topic then the Board will for that group's view to be heard. All those who sign up must accompany the speaker to the front of the Board room. We respectfully ask that you do not repeat the same message, but rather present varying concepts on the same theme.  

The Board will listen to Part One Public Comment for up to 90 minutes if necessary and, by a majority vote, may extend the time for high volume meetings.  Board members welcome Part Two Public Comments after the business items are completed.  Part Two Public Comment addresses topics unrelated to this evening's regular business. Thank you.

EL-3, Staff Treatment

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. Renewals for the Employee Benefit Plans for 2017/2018 were evaluated by the Benefits Advisory Committee. The Committee recommends Board action as follows:
     

    Renew all three Kaiser plans.  Increase in premiums of 2%.
     

    Discontinue offering the UnitedHealthCare (UHC) High Deductible Health Plan.
     

    Renew the contract with Delta Dental, our third party administrator responsible for adjudicating and paying dental claims.
