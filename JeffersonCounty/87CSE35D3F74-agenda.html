0.02 Community Engagement 6 p.m. - High School Students Former Dropouts (Ends 1)
Meeting
Mar 20, 2008 - Special Meeting-Community Engagement 5 p.m., Board Room
Category
0. Study/Dialogue Session 5 p.m.
Type
Discussion
PRESENTING STAFF:
Cindy Stevenson, superintendent
Peg Kastberg, community superintendent
Students from Brady Exploration School

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to meet with Jeffco Public School students who have dropped out of school and have returned to Brady Exploration School. The discussion will be framed through the Board’s Ends 1 policy, “All students explore and develop their potential as productive global citizens in a 21st century environment.”

The following questions will be posed for discussion:
What brought you back to school?
What skills will you and other students need for jobs we don't know will exist?
How can we tell if a student is a life-long learner?
What does a global perspective mean to you?

BACKGROUND:
Following their retreat discussion on February 28, the Board of Education requested time to discuss their work and the needs of students in Jeffco Public Schools with a variety of student, parent, staff and community groups.
