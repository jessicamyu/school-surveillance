PRESENTING STAFF:

Brett Miller, chief information officer

Chris Paschke, director, Information Security

T.O. Owens, committee member

 

PURPOSE:

To provide an update to the Board of Education on data privacy in the district and receive direction from the Board on the priorities for the Technology and Data Privacy Advisory Committee (TDPAC) for the second half of the school year and the upcoming 2016/2017 school year.

 

BACKGROUND:

The information security department has been protecting district data and providing oversight for the past 10 years. The Technology and Data Privacy Advisory Committee (TDPAC) is a 25-member committee consisting of both community members and Jeffco staff, and was created to advise the Board of Education on district technology strategies, systems and overall data governance.  The committee encourages continuous improvement of, and fosters adherence to, district policies, procedures and practices at all levels. Reference: Board Policy GP-13

2.04 Two Roads Online Education Program for K-5 (EL-13, 11)
Meeting
Jan 28, 2016 - Special Meeting-Study/Dialogue Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion

estimated time: 7:15 p.m. (estimated duration: 15 minutes) 

 

PRESENTING STAFF:

Terry Elliott, chief school effectiveness officer

Scott Allensworth, achievement director

Tim Matlick, achievement director, charter schools

 

PURPOSE:

Two Roads Charter School, in order to enable Jeffco Schools to continue providing a blended/ONLINE learning program for Jeffco families with students in grades kindergarten through 5th grade, is seeking approval to amend their contract.  

 

BACKGROUND:

Jeffco Schools over the past two years has built a blended learning/online elementary program as part of the Jefferson Virtual Academy (JVA) program.  The Board of Education received information on January 8th indicating the number of students attending the program has been significantly below projections and the program's funding does not support the continuation of the K-5 program at JVA.  Two Roads Charter would like to use the program developed by JVA to enable Jeffco Schools to continue the optional program for families seeking this educational environment.  The standard Jeffco Schools Charter contract prohibits charter schools from offering an online program.  The Board is being asked on February 4, 2016 to amend the Two Roads Charter contract to enable the offering of a K-5 blended/online program.
