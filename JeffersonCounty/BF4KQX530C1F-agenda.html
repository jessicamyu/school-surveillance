Recommended Action
to adopt the resolution to approve the tentative agreement and revised contract language as agreed to during contract negotiations between the District and Jefferson County Education Association (JCEA).

estimated time: 4 p.m. (estimated duration: 15 minutes)
 

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. In accordance with Board executive limitation policy EL-3, Staff Treatment, the following is presented to the Board of Education for approval.
     
  2. Throughout the budget process the Board emphasized competitive pay for employees as an on-going priority. The District and JCEA negotiating teams reached agreement on a Tentative Agreement (TA) on compensation for licensed educators for the 2019-2020 school year. The TA provides for steps and levels for teachers in accordance with Article 17 of the Master Agreement, a 2.5% cost-of-living increase, and allocated $3 million dollars to compensate those employees who incurred pay freezes during the recession.
     
  3. In addition to compensation, the bargaining teams could each bring two items for discussion as well any mutually agreed to items. Language changes were agreed to in Articles 1, 3, 5, 7, 8, 10, 13, and 16.
     
  4. Finally, the teams also agreed on a Memorandum of Understanding to develop mutually agreeable contract language with the Jeffco Education Support Professionals Association (JESPA) to bring the bargaining agreements into compliance with the Innovation School Act of 2008.
     
  5. The JCEA membership will have ratified the agreements reached by the negotiations teams before action is taken by the Board of Education.
