estimated time: 3 p.m. (estimated duration: 5 minutes)

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. In accordance with Board executive limitation policy, EL-6, Financial Administration, and pursuant to Colorado Statute C.R.S. 22-40-102(6), the Board of Education must certify its mill levies to the Board of County Commissioners of Jefferson and Broomfield by December 15, 2017.
     
  2. The required Certification Resolution for the general fund and bond redemption fund levies are based on the assessed valuation as certified by the Jefferson County and Broomfield County Assessors.  
     
  3. As presented to the Board of Education per the attached documents, the general operating mill levy revenues will be levied against net valuation of assessment for all taxable property located within the boundaries of the school district. The levy consists of mills pursuant to the Public School Finance Act of 1994 for abatements and refunds and for voter approved mill levy overrides. Revenues will flow to the district general fund for the fiscal period beginning July 1, 2017, and ending June 30, 2018.
     
  4. As presented to the Board of Education per the attached documents, the bond redemption fund mill levy for 2017 taxes paid in 2018 will be levied against net valuation of assessment for all taxable property located within the boundaries of the school district.
     
  5. The counties have until December 10, 2017, for final assessed valuation numbers which are then sent to the district.  Preparing final documentation may require changes up until the Board of Education certification and approval on December 13, 2017.
