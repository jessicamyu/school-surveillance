Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session

Discussion

estimated time: Noon (estimated duration: 1 hour)

PRESENTING STAFF:
none


PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to discuss how members envision board work engaging the community in decision making processes.

 

BACKGROUND:

To get a head start on their work together as a board prior to the next scheduled board meeting on December 10, new board members determined they needed additional time to meet on priority issues.

 

Since April 2013, the Board of Education has had a governance process policy, GP-17, Community Engagement. Board members requested time for this discussion as an opportunity to understand how individual members believe community engagement may be used in board work. Discussion may include but is not limited to school visits, groups to engage, special events and communications.
