Category
0. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion
PRESENTING STAFF:
Cindy Stevenson, superintendent
Members of Cabinet
Steve Welchert, Welchert & Britz

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to receive information related to the instructional and financial needs of the district in meeting the Board’s Ends 1 policy, “All students explore and develop their potential as productive global citizens in a 21st century environment.”

BACKGROUND:
With the beginning of the 2007-08 school year, the superintendent has met with school staff and parent community leaders regarding the work of the district in preparing students as directed by the Board of Education’s goal that all students be prepared for a 21st century environment. The reactions from parent leaders have been shared with the members of the Board on (date of weekly update).

On January 24, the Board of Education revised Jeffco Public Schools graduation requirements after a year of study and community input, raising the credits required for graduation to 24 from 22. Implementation of the graduation requirements for the class of 2013 will require a redirection of financial resources beginning with the 2008-09 school year.

While the 2008-2009 budget will allow both maintenance and enhancement of current educational reform efforts, beginning in 2009-2010 Jeffco Public Schools must begin budget reductions.
