2. Public Hearing Comment, Peck, 5 - 6 p.m.
Subject
2.01 Public Comment Re: The Recommendation Of Peck's Possible School Closure
Meeting
Oct 26, 2022 - Public Hearings: Peck, Molholm, and Glennon Heights
Category
2. Public Hearing Comment, Peck, 5 - 6 p.m.
Type
Information

estimated time: 5 - 6 p.m. (estimated duration: 60 minutes) 

 

Every school recommended for closure will have a one-hour public hearing with the Board of Education (BoE) prior to the vote focused exclusively on the recommendation for that particular school. This Public Hearing is for Peck Elementary School.

  • The order of speakers will be dictated by the date and time of sign-up.
  • Each speaker will have 3 minutes and it is estimated that time will allow for 15 speakers.
  • The first 7 spots on the speaking list will be reserved for current staff and families at the school recommended for closure.
  • The total time limit of each public hearing session is 1 hour
  • There will be live interpreters available so that people can speak and listen in their native language.
  • We encourage those who wish to speak to bring their comments in writing.
  • All written comments, regardless of whether they are shared verbally during the public comment session, will be shared with each BoE member.
  • Content of your comment should be focused exclusively on the recommendation for that particular school you signed up for.
  • This is an opportunity for the public to comment to the Board; during public comment the Board does not engage in discussion with, or respond to individuals.
  • The speaker shall refrain from using the names of any student and/or family that are not his/her own and from using defamatory or abusive statements.
