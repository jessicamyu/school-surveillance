Meeting

Category

PRESENTING STAFF:
Jason E. Glass, superintendent
Steve Bell, chief operating officer
Tim Reed, executive director, Facilities

PURPOSE:
The Board of Education will discuss the options for school funding that are possible for the November ballot, including Amendment 73/Great Schools Thriving Communities, to establish a clear understanding of bond program priority areas and amounts for August 23 board discussion and direction.

 

BACKGROUND:

With confirmation that Amendment 73 (formerly Initiative 93) will be on the statewide ballot in November, the Board will have the opportunity to continue its June 11 discussion of school funding in Jeffco Public Schools for construction (April 2017) and operations needs.

2.02 Strategic Planning and Monitoring (EL-11, GP-4)
Meeting
Aug 8, 2018 - Study/Dialogue Session-Retreat
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion, Information

estimated time: 1 p.m. (estimated duration: 2 hours) 

PRESENTING STAFF:
Jason Glass, superintendent

Tom McDermott, chief strategy officer

PURPOSE:
The Board of Education will review the first year of Jeffco’s strategic planning process and look ahead on new tactic reports to ensure the direction aligns with Board goals.

BACKGROUND:
In 2017/18, Dr. Glass brought a new ‘Deliverology’ method of monitoring district performance to the strategic plan, led by Dr. Tom McDermott with the assistance of tactic leaders and teams of staff to address 27 tactics to increase student performance. Last year’s focus began with three strategies (Learning, Conditions for Learning, Readiness for Learning).
 

The superintendent provides this information in accord with Board executive limitation policy, EL-11, Communication and Counsel to the Board, and governance process policy GP-4, Board Job Description.
