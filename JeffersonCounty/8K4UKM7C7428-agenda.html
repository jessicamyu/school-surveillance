2.02 Tax Anticipation Notes (TANs) Issuance (EL-6)
Meeting
Aug 25, 2011 - Special Meeting-S/D Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion
Goals
EL-6, Financial Administration

estimated time:  5:30 p.m. (estimated duration: 30 minutes) 

 

PRESENTING STAFF:

Lorie Gillis, chief financial officer

Steve Bell, chief operating officer

Ann Gifford, managing director, Robert W. Baird & Co.

 

PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to receive the Preliminary Official Statement (POS) for the school district’s pending tax anticipation note issuance to cover district cash flow needs prior to the receipt of 2012 property tax revenues.  The District’s investment bankers, Robert W. Baird & Co., have been monitoring the ratings from Moody’s Investors’ Services and Standard and Poor’s and the interest rates available and will provide an update.

 

BACKGROUND:

Board executive limitation policy 6, Financial Administration,  states that, “With respect to the actual, ongoing financial condition and activities of the district, the superintendent shall not cause nor allow fiscal jeopardy or any fiscal condition that is inconsistent with achieving the priorities established in Board's Ends policies."

 

Tax Anticipation Notes (TANs) are issued to provide funds to the General Fund to pay certain short-term expenses of the District.  Most expenses of the District are incurred during the traditional school year of September through May and most revenue is not received until the months of March through June.  Accordingly, the District typically experiences cash flow shortages during the fall and winter months.

 

The district has forecasted that a cash flow shortage will occur and would like to discuss the issuance of TANs with members of the Board of Education.
