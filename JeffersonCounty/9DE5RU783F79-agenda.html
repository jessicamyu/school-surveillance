2. Certification of the Canvass of the Regular Biennial School Board Election
Subject
2.01 November 5, 2013 Official Abstract Results (GP-1)
Meeting
Nov 21, 2013 - Special Meeting-Oath of Office
Category
2. Certification of the Canvass of the Regular Biennial School Board Election
Type
Action
Recommended Action
to adopt the resolution declaring the results of the November 5, 2013 regular biennial school board election presented prior to the meeting.

estimated time: 6 p.m. (estimated duration: 2 minutes)

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. Board Governance Process policy, GP-1, Governance Commitment, concerns governance commitment and the purpose of the school board.  On November 5, 2013, an election was held to elect three members to the school board.
     
  2. Per state law and the district's intergovernmental agreement with Jefferson and Broomfield counties, the canvass board has until November 22, 2013 to provide a certified official abstract of votes to the designated election official and the school district.
     
  3. As the final official abstract of votes cast for all candidates is available and results are not in question, a resolution indicating the newly elected board members has been prepared for the Board of Education prior to the meeting.


Please open the attachments below to review the draft resolution and final official result totals.

4. Board Reorganization and Election of Officers
Subject
4.01 Board Reorganization and Election of Officers
Meeting
Nov 21, 2013 - Special Meeting-Oath of Office
Category
4. Board Reorganization and Election of Officers
Type
Action
Recommended Action
to reorganize Board officers through the election of a president and vice president of the Board of Education; to appoint a second vice president, treasurer and secretary; and, to reappoint Lorie Gillis to the position of assistant treasurer and Helen Neal to the position of assistant secretary.

estimated time: 6:07 p.m. (estimated duration: 10 minutes)

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. Board Governance Process policies GP-4, Board Job Description, and GP-5, President's Role, address the various Board member roles.  In following district policy BDA, Board Organizational Meeting, the following action is brought forward to the Board of Education.
     
  2. On November 5, 2013, an election was held to elect three members to the school board.
     
  3. State law determines that the Board of Education elects a president and vice president and appoints a treasurer and secretary upon election of new members.
     
  4. It has been the practice of the Board of Education to appoint a second vice president, treasurer, secretary and an assistant treasurer and assistant secretary at this time as well.

5. Community Engagement
Subject
5.01 Public Comment
Meeting
Nov 21, 2013 - Special Meeting-Oath of Office
Category
5. Community Engagement
Type
Information

estimated time: 6:20 p.m. (estimated duration: 90 minutes) 

Board members will listen to public comment from community members who have signed up ahead of the special meeting to speak to the Board of Education.  Sign up will be available on November 21 in the Board of Education/Superintendent office and outside the Board room beginning at 4 p.m. until 5:45 p.m.


We listen with respect and ask those who address the Board to do the same. In order to respect all persons addressing the Board, we request that the audience not applaud individual speakers and that issues, rather than personalities, be addressed. Any resident or staff member of the district who has completed the requested information on the public comment roster may speak. There is a ONE minute time limit per person.

 

Thank you.
