3. Discussion Agenda
Subject
3.01 Capital Construction Update on Middle School Additions to Support 6-8 Grade Reconfiguration (EL-7, 8, 11)
Meeting
Apr 20, 2017 - Special Meeting-Study/Dialogue Session
Category
3. Discussion Agenda
Type
Discussion

estimated time: 6:30 p.m. (estimated duration: 30 minutes) 

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. On June 16, 2016 as part of finalizing the Facilities Master Plan, the Board approved that Jeffco Public Schools would move toward a K-5/6-8 elementary and middle school configuration generally, with exceptions. On February 9, 2017, the Board reaffirmed its commitment to the eventual implementation of the K-5/6-8 grade configuration for articulation areas with 7-8 middle school programs; and, that articulation areas would implement the structure in coordination with the surrounding area through community engagement and planning in the process.
     
  2. On January 26, the Board of Education was presented with an approach to address facilities and budgetary needs in Jeffco Public Schools to support the goals of the Jeffco 2020 Vision.
     
  3. The 6th grade movement to middle school results in additional student programming options, and academic and social emotional learning supports. Space for future pre-school expansion is possible for elementary sites in addition to better utilization of middle school facilities, and cost management to meet facility needs.
     
  4. On February 9, the district facilities staff presented, with prioritization, four middle school sites requiring building additions:  Drake, Dunstan, Ken Caryl and Creighton.
     
  5. The additions to Drake and Dunstan middle schools are critical to alleviating growth trends being experienced in the northwest corridor and central areas of Jeffco Public Schools.  Ken Caryl and Creighton middle school additions support the ongoing implementation of the 6-8 grade middle school model.
     
  6. Facility middle school addition cost estimates to date:
    1. Drake - $10.5 million
    2. Dunstan - $4.5 million
    3. Ken Caryl - $6.5 million
    4. Creighton - $4.5 million
