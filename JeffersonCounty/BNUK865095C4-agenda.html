estimated time: 8:30 a.m. (estimated duration: 2 hours)

 

PRESENTING STAFF:

Kathleen Askelson, chief financial officer

Nicole Stewart, director-budget and treasury

 

PURPOSE:

To provide the Board with additional information for consideration for development of the 2020/21 budget to address potential revenue adjustments, possible reductions, and use of reserves. 

BACKGROUND:

Discussion of the 2020/21 proposed budget to date has covered revenue assumptions per the state's fiscal outlook; impacts and updates at the state and district level on funding estimates for Jeffco; and, expenditure assumptions and budget priorities as identified by Cabinet, district leadership and the community including recommendations for funding requests and potential reductions for development of the 2020/21 proposed budget.

 

Due to the financial climate as a result of the COVID-19 pandemic, the Board will hear new information with regard to potential revenue reductions due to changes at the state and local level as well as several examples of changes to district budget assumptions to ensure continued financial stability for the district. 

Budget updates were presented to the Board of Education at the September 5, October 10 and December 12, 2019, and the February 5, March 5, March 11, April 2 and April 8, 2020, meetings.
