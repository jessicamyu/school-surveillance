2.05 Decision on Boundary Change: Bell Middle School (EL-11)
Meeting
Nov 13, 2019 - Special Study/Dialogue Meeting
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Action
Recommended Action
to approve the change in school boundary for Bell Middle School designating it as a neighborhood school for Maple Grove Elementary students and aligning enrollment with the historic high school pattern.

estimated time: 11:30 a.m. (estimated duration: 30 minutes)
 

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. The following is brought forward to the Board of Education in accord with Board executive limitation policy, EL-11, Communication and Counsel to the Board; and, district policies KCB, Cooperative Decision Making, and JC, School Attendance Area, “Generally, proposals for changing school boundaries shall be submitted to the superintendent and the Board in October for preliminary study and shall be reviewed and/or acted upon during November. However, exceptions to these deadlines may be appropriate when exigent circumstances require an early position be taken by the superintendent and the Board.”
     
  2. On November 7, the Board of Education received a briefing from Superintendent Dr. Jason Glass regarding a potential change to the boundary for Bell Middle School to allow priority enrollment for Maple Grove students. The Board requested additional information on transportation costs, enrollment projections for Bell MS including its wait list, and feedback from families in the Bell MS and Maple Grove ES communities. Community meetings with both school communities will occur on November 12.
     
  3. Historically, the Maple Grove Elementary School community has had priority enrollment into both Golden High School and Wheat Ridge High School. With this potential boundary change, the district will extend the historic high school configuration to their middle schools.
     
  4. On October 10 and September 5, the Board of Education reviewed an appeal of and subsequently affirmed the Superintendent’s decision on the enrollment pattern for Manning School. This boundary change to Bell Middle School will provide a short-term solution for the Maple Grove Elementary School community expressed during the September/October discussions with a long term solution begun to strengthen Everitt ​​Middle School focusing on first engaging the school community.
