Subject
2.02 Recommendation for Dismissal of a Teacher (EL-3)
Meeting
Jun 24, 2010 - Special Meeting-Study/Dialogue Session, 6:30 p.m.
Category
2. Consent Agenda
Type
Action

PERTINENT FACTS:

1. Board executive limitation policy EL-3, Staff Treatment, state that the superintendent shall not fail to make recommendations as appropriate for termination of employment.
2. Following a thorough review of the matter, the superintendent has submitted her recommendation for dismissal of a teacher, pursuant to the provisions of the Teacher Employment, Compensation, and Dismissal Act, C.R.S., Section 22-63-101 et seq.
3. The grounds for the recommendation are unsatisfactory performance, incompeteence, and insubordination, and other good and just cause as provided in C.R.S., Section 22-63-301 and as detailed in the superintendent's written charges. Upon receipt of the recommendation, the procedure for dismissal as specified in C.R.S., Section 22-63-302 will commence.

The Superintendent's Recommendation for Dismissal is available upon request.

File Attachments

Motion & Voting

To acknowledge receipt of the superintendent's recommendation for dismissal of the teacher, ratify the teacher's suspension with pay subject to the limitation of the Teacher Compensation and Employment Act; and, to authorize the Secretary of the Board and legal counsel to send the teacher written notice of intent to dismiss together with the other matters required by the Act.

Motion by Robin Johnson, second by Laura Boggs.
Final Resolution: Motion Carries
Yea: Dave J Thomas, Jane M Barnes, Laura Boggs, Robin Johnson
