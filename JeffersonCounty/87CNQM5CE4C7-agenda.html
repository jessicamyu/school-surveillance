Subject
2.05 Legislative Issues
Meeting
Jan 28, 2010 - Special Meeting-Study/Dialogue Session, 5 p.m.
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Action
Estimated Time: 9 p.m.
Location: 4th floor, Seminar Room, 1829 Denver West Drive, Bldg. 27, Golden

PRESENTING STAFF:
Cindy Stevenson, superintendent

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to discuss pending legislation for the 2010 legislative session.

BACKGROUND:
Board executive limitation policy EL-11, Communication and Counsel to the Board, requires that the Board's positions on pending legislation and regulations be communicated to members of the Colorado General Assembly and the Executive Branch of state government. It has been the Board's practice to schedule special meetings to discuss pending legislation during the legislative session.

At the request of the Board's representative to the district Legislative Forum, a coalition of Jeffco Public Schools agencies, the Board will review legislation discussed at Legislative Forum.

The 2010 legislative session will produce a number of bills of interest to Jefferson County Public Schools. On February 4, the Board has scheduled the district contract lobbyist to bring legislation to the Board of Education for positions and direction. The district’s contract lobbyist provides state legislators with the Board’s positions on legislation.
