Recommended Action
to determine whether to grant or deny the charter request by Global Outreach Charter Academy.

estimated time: 5:00 p.m. (estimated duration: 30 minutes) 

 

PERTINENT FACTS:
1. According to Board executive limitation policy EL-13, Charter Schools Application and Monitoring, the superintendent shall not allow charter school applications to be recommended if fiscal jeopardy or failure to make consistent progress towards their stated objectives is a likely outcome or is evident.
2. On August 15, 2011, Global Outreach Charter Academy submitted an application to the district for approval by the Board of Education to become a district charter school. The Board of Education accepted the application for study on September 1, 2011.
3. A public hearing was held on October 6, 2011.  The Board of Education requested additional information of the applicant.
4. According to Colorado Statute, the district must respond to the proposal within 75 days after receipt of the application.
   
To review the charter application and additional documents provided based on the request of the Board of Education, please open the link, provided here, the California school and Florida school links, and the attachments below.
