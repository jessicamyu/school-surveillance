PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to have a conversation with members of the Strategic and Sustainable Change Task Force concerning the work of the task force.

For the Board of Education to revisit the self-survey tool used in July 2010 for Board work on Ends 8, the Jeffco Board will be effective and responsible and will function as a highly effective team and makes the following commitments to our community:
