PRESENTING STAFF:
Cindy Stevenson, superintendent
Allen Taggart, executive director, Employee Relations/Legal Counsel
Amy Weber, executive director, Human Resources
Pati Montgomery, director, Strategic Compensation

 

Members of the Strategic and Sustainable Change Task Force
Kerrie Dallmann, president JCEA
Lisa Elliott, executive director JCEA

Edward W Wiley, Ph.D., Sequoia Strategy + Analytics, LLC 

 

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to hear a report on the work of the Strategic and Sustainable Change Task force on issues concerning SB191, Collaboration and Professionalism, and Compensation Structures.

 

BACKGROUND:
As the result of bargaining in Spring 2011, the district and the Jefferson County Education Association (JCEA) entered into a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) to create a Strategic and Sustainable Change Task Force. The Task Force was charged with addressing issues related to the implementation of Senate Bill (SB)-191, examining approaches to collaboration and professionalism, and studying alternative philosophies of compensating teachers and managing time. The Task Force and its respective subcommittees have met several times and will report on its progress.
