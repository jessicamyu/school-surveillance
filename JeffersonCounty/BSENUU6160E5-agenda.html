PURPOSE:
The Board of Education will work with Ken DeLay, facilitator and former executive director of the Colorado Association of School Boards, to review its work over the past year, continue its training on board leadership, and prepare for the coming school year’s board work.

Meeting
Aug 27, 2020 - Study/Dialogue Session-Retreat
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion

estimated time: 2 p.m. (estimated duration: 2 hours)

PRESENTING STAFF:
Kristopher Schuh, interim superintendent

Renee Nicothodes, chief of schools, elementary

Matt Flores, chief academic officer

Arianne Rivera, specialist, Equity and Diversity

Dr. Susan Leach, chief student success officer

Dr. Tom McDermott, chief strategy officer

PURPOSE:
The Board of Education will engage with identified priorities in the district strategic plan, and proposed revisions to their Board Ends policies in alignment with those priorities. Board engagement will involve an update on the planned and in-progress work aligned with the 2020/21 school year priorities, and the opportunity to provide direction on reporting structures for the identified strategic plan priorities.  

 

BACKGROUND:

The Board of Education discussed and approved the strategic plan and its system indicators February 1 and March 1, 2018, February 7, 2019, and January 30, 2020. On March 11, 2020, the Board of Education provided feedback on proposed revisions to its Ends policies aligned with work in the strategic plan. Additionally, on August 8, 2018, December 6, 2018, August 26, 2019, and December 6, 2019, the Board of Education engaged in strategic plan work during bi-annual retreats, identifying priorities and providing feedback on reporting structures.
