estimated time: 5 p.m. (estimated duration: up to 1 hour)

 

Sign up online here to speak to the Board of Education (opens 7 am Sunday, closes 5:30 pm Wednesday).   
 

At this time, the Board listens to concerns on topics related to items on the agenda for up to one hour.  If your topic is unrelated to the meeting's agenda please submit written comments to board@jeffco.k12.co.us. We listen with respect and ask those who address the Board to do the same. In order to respect all persons addressing the Board, we request that issues, rather than personalities, be addressed. Any resident, staff member or parent of the district who has completed the requested information on the public comment roster may address the Board. Public Comment will be taken in-person and virtually. 

 

There is a three* minute time limit per individual speaker. *Additional guidelines for public comment.  

 

If there are a number of speakers commenting on the same topic, we ask that remarks be consolidated and new thoughts or concepts be shared. It is not the number of people who address the Board that leads to better decision-making, rather the content of your presentation. So, we respectfully ask that you do not repeat the same message of an earlier speaker. If there are four or more people who sign up to address the Board they will have 10* minutes for that group's view to be presented. All those who sign up as a group must be able to accompany the speaker virtually (see additional emailed direction). We respectfully ask that you not repeat the same message, but rather present varying concepts on the same theme.  If more than 20 individuals sign up to speak to the Board, individuals will have two minutes and groups will have five minutes for a collective message to the Board.

The Board will listen to Public Comment for up to one hour if necessary and, by a majority vote, may extend the time for high volume meetings.  Thank you.

 

hora estimada: 5 p.m. (duración estimada: hasta 1 hora)

 

 

Inscríbase en línea aquí para hablar con la Junta Directiva de Educación (la inscripción se abrirá a las 7 a.m. del domingo y se cerrará a las 5:30 p.m. del miércoles).   

Por el momento, la Junta Directiva atiende inquietudes sobre temas relacionados con los puntos del orden del día hasta por una hora.  Si su tema no está relacionado con la agenda de esta sesión, rogamos enviar sus comentarios por escrito a board@jeffco.k12.co.us. Escuchamos con respeto y pedimos lo mismo a los que se dirigen a la Junta Directiva. A fin de respetar a todas las personas que se dirigen a la Junta Directiva, pedimos que se enfoquen en los asuntos más que en las personalidades. Cualquier residente, miembro del personal o padre de familia del Distrito que haya completado la información solicitada en la lista de comentarios públicos puede dirigirse a la Junta Directiva. El comentario público se recibirá en persona y de manera virtual. 

 

Hay un límite de tres* minutos por orador/a individual. *Directrices adicionales para comentarios del público.  

 

Si hay varios oradores con comentarios sobre el mismo tema, pedimos que se consoliden las observaciones y que se compartan nuevas ideas o conceptos. No es el número de personas que se dirigen a la Junta Directiva lo que guía en la mejor toma de decisiones, sino el contenido de su presentación. De esta manera, pedimos respetuosamente no repetir el mismo mensaje de un orador/a anterior. Si hay cuatro personas o más que como grupo se inscribieron para dirigirse a la Junta Directiva, ellos tendrán 10* minutos para presentar su punto de vista. Todos aquellos que se inscriban como grupo deben acompañar de manera virtual al orador/a (ver instrucciones adicionales enviadas por correo electrónico). Respetuosamente pedimos que no repitan el mismo mensaje, más bien presenten conceptos distintos sobre el mismo tema.  Si se inscriben más de 20 individuos para dirigirse a la Junta Directiva, los individuos tendrán dos minutos y los grupos tendrán cinco minutos para dar un mensaje colectivo a la Junta Directiva.

La Junta Directiva escuchará los comentarios del público hasta por una hora si es necesario y, por mayoría de votos, puede extender el tiempo para sesiones de alta participación.  Gracias.

Recommended Action
to adopt the resolution regarding Fitzmorris Elementary School as presented and entered into the record.

estimated time: 6 p.m. (estimated duration: 1 hour)

PERTINENT FACTS:

 

  1. In accordance with Board Ends policies 1-3, the Board of Education believes that every school will have effective learning systems and shared leadership that provide high quality core instructional practices, interventions and enrichments with the expectation that every student will receive the skills, support and educational opportunities needed to achieve their full potential.
     
  2. On January 6, 2022, members of the Fitzmorris Elementary School community signed up to address the Board on the challenges in delivering high quality core instructional practices, interventions, and enrichments at the school due to declining enrollment.
     
  3. On February 9, 2022, in accordance with Board Governance Process policy GP-17, Community Engagement, the Board invited members of the Fitzmorris Elementary School community to engage with the Board at a study session in connection with the Board’s work to review the District’s vision, mission and values.
     
  4. Based on these discussions and further engagement with the Fitzmorris Elementary School community, the Superintendent recommends that Jeffco Public Schools prepares to serve Fitzmorris students, staff members, and families at other district schools at the start of the 2022/2023 school year to ensure they have access to the best elementary school experience the district has to offer. The Board will have discussion about the recommendation and will take action on next steps to support Fitzmorris Elementary School students and families.
