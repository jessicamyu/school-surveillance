Type
Procedural

Estimated Time: 7:30 p.m.
Location: 3AB conference room, 3rd floor

PRESENTING STAFF:
Cindy Stevenson, superintendent

PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to schedule time for a special meeting to certify its mill levies to the Board of County Commissioners of Jefferson and Broomfield by December 15, 2010.

BACKGROUND:
Annually, the Board of Education must certify its mill levies to the county commissioners by December 15.  Because counties have until December 10 for final assessed valuation numbers, the final resolution to set the mill levy for 2010 with collections in 2011 will only be available after that date, necessitating a special meeting of the Board.

Subject
8.13 WALK-ON Certification: 2010 Mill Levies for Collection in 2011 (EL-6)
Meeting
Dec 9, 2010 - Special Meeting
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Action
Recommended Action
To adopt the certification resolution as presented prior to the Board meeting.

PERTINENT FACTS:

 

  1. In accordance with Board executive limitation policy 6, Financial Administration, and pursuant to Colorado Statute C.R.S. 22-41-102(6), the Board of Education must certify its mill levies to the Board of County Commissioners of Jefferson and Broomfield by December 15, 2010.
     
  2. The required Certification Resolution for the General Fund and Bond Redemption Fund levies are based on the assessed valuation as certified by the Jefferson County Assessor on November 30, 2010 and as certified by the Broomfield County Assessor on December 1, 2010. The counties have until December 10, 2010 to notify the district of the final assessed value certification.
     
  3. The General Operating Mill Levy will be set at 26.252 mills with an additional 0.543 mills assessed for refunds and abatements and an additional 3.420 for the 1999 voter approved mill levy override and an additional 1.445 mills for the 1999 voter approved mill levy override associated with the Performance Promise. In addition, 5.300 mills are assessed for the 2004 voter approved mill levy override. The total General Fund mill levy is 36.960 for 2010 taxes paid in 2011.
     
  4. The bond Redemption Fund mill levy for 2010 taxes paid in 2010 is set at 11.250 mills.
     
  5. The Certification Resolution included in background material may be available as a final resolution to set the mill levy for 2010 with collections in 2011 contingent upon final valuations being received by the counties by December 8, 2010.
     
  6. Since the counties have until December 10, 2010 for final assessed valuation numbers, preparing final documentation may require a special board meeting on December 15, 2010 to certify the final mill levy.
