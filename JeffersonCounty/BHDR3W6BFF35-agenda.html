PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to discuss issues of mutual interest related to public education with members of the Jefferson County state legislative delegation.

BACKGROUND:
The Board of Education offers Jefferson County state legislators the opportunity to meet with board members to discuss education issues prior to the legislative session while enjoying the culinary efforts of Warren Tech students, to be followed by a tour of other Warren Tech career/technical programs and dialogue with students.
