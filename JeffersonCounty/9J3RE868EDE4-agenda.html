Recommended Action
To move into executive session as authorized by the following provisions of the Colorado Open Meetings Law: C.R.S. § 24-6-402(4)(c) – to meet with applicants for the position of superintendent whose identity is required to be kept confidential by C.R.S. § 24-72-204(3)(a)(XI); C.R.S. § 24-6-402(4)(g) – consideration of documents submitted by applicants for the position of superintendent which are protected by the mandatory nondisclosure provisions of the Colorado Open Records Act, C.R.S. § 24-72-204(3)(a)(XI)(A); and, C.R.S. § 24-6-402(4)(e) – to determine positions relative to matters that may be subject to negotiations with finalists for the position of superintendent.

After the vote to move into executive session, this portion of the meeting is closed to the public.  The Board will reconvene in open session at approximately 9:30 p.m. in the Seminar Room (depending on the number of applicants brought forward after Board work on May 5). 

 

PRESENTING STAFF:

Dr. Bill Newman, Ray & Associates

Amy Weber, executive director, Human Resources

Brad Miller, Board attorney

 

PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to interview candidates for the position of superintendent, including discussion of documents submitted by the candidates.

 

BACKGROUND:

Ray & Associates was hired by the Board of Education to provide professional Superintendent Search services to Jeffco Schools. On May 5, the Board of Education reviewed candidate materials and selected candidates to be interviewed in executive session.
