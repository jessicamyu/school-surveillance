PURPOSE:
The Board of Education will receive an update on Jeffco’s Remote Learning and Work Plan covering areas of instruction, evaluation, communications, health/operations, and finances/staffing which has been in place since March 16. The update will also cover current plans regarding graduations.

 

BACKGROUND:

The district began preparing for the spread of COVID-19 at the end of February while monitoring next steps in partnership with the Jefferson County Public Health Department (JCPH).

 

On Friday, March 13, along with other metro area school districts, Jeffco Public Schools announced its closure to in-school instruction beginning Monday, March 16 through March 20. (The district was closed for Spring Break March 23-27.)  Jeffco’s Remote Learning and Work Plan began on March 16.

 

Governor Polis’ executive order closing school districts in Colorado through April 17 necessitated the continuation of Jeffco’s Remote Learning and Work Plan when students and staff returned from Spring Break on March 30. At that time, district staff and educators continued providing students with stability through learning, connections, and meals in uncertain times.

 

On April 1, Governor Polis continued school closures at least through the end of April. At that time, he also acknowledged it was highly unlikely schools would be returning for in-person learning for this school year.

 

After consultation with Jefferson County Public Health and in coordination with the 13 other school districts in the Denver metro area, on April 3 Jeffco Public Schools made the difficult decision to suspend in-person instruction for the remainder of the school year. Remote learning for students and remote work for staff continues for this 2019/20 school year. Key factors in this decision were:

  • clarity and a reduction of uncertainty moving forward for families and students; 
  • Governor’s announcement that the impact of COVID-19 is likely to peak from mid-April through possibly July; and 
  • the significant safety risk to our students, families and staff that this information provides.
