Meeting

Category
2. Certification of the Canvass of the Regular Biennial School Board Election
Type
Action
Recommended Action
to adopt the resolution declaring the results of the November 3, 2015 regular biennial school board and coordinated election presented prior to the meeting.

estimated time: 5:30 p.m. (estimated duration: 2 minutes)

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. Board Governance Process policy, GP-1, Governance Commitment, concerns governance commitment and the purpose of the school board.  On November 3, 2015, an election was held to elect two members to the school board for districts 3 and 4, and to recall three board members and select successor board members to complete the two year terms.
     
  2. Per state law and the district's intergovernmental agreement with Jefferson and Broomfield counties, the canvass board has until November 20, 2015 to provide a certified official abstract of votes to the designated election official and the school district.
     
  3. As the final official abstract of votes cast for all candidates is available and results are not in question, a resolution indicating the newly elected board members has been prepared for the Board of Education prior to the meeting.

4. Board Reorganization and Election of Officers
Subject
4.01 Board Reorganization and Election of Officers (GP-4, 5)
Meeting
Nov 19, 2015 - Special Meeting
Category
4. Board Reorganization and Election of Officers
Type
Action, Procedural
Recommended Action
to reorganize Board officers through the election of a president and vice president of the Board of Education; to appoint a second vice president, treasurer and secretary; and, to appoint Kathleen Askelson to the position of assistant treasurer and Helen Neal to the position of assistant secretary.

estimated time: 5:41 p.m. (estimated duration: 10 minutes) 
 

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. Board Governance Process policies GP-4, Board Job Description, and GP-5, President's Role, address the various Board member roles.  In following district policy BDA, Board Organizational Meeting, the following action is brought forward to the Board of Education.
     
  2. On November 3, 2015, an election was held to elect two members to the school board for districts 3 and 4; and a recall election to replace three school board members for districts 1, 2, and 5.
     
  3. State law determines that the Board of Education elects president and vice president and either elects or appoints a treasurer and secretary upon election of new members. Board policy BDA, specifies that the Board will appoint a treasurer and secretary.
     
  4. It has been the practice of the Board of Education to appoint a second vice president, treasurer, secretary, an assistant treasurer and assistant secretary at this time as well.

6. Public Comment
Subject
6.01 Public Comment
Meeting
Nov 19, 2015 - Special Meeting
Category
6. Public Comment
Type
Information

estimated time: 6:20 p.m. (estimated duration: 1 hour)

 

The Board listens to the community’s issues in the Public Comment section on its agenda. Sign up online here to speak to the Board of Education.
 

At this time, the Board listens to concerns on topics related to items on this evening's agenda  for up to one hour.  Board members or staff will respond during this meeting or at a later time. We listen with respect and ask those who address the Board to do the same. In order to respect all persons addressing the Board, we request that the audience not applaud individual speakers and that issues, rather than personalities, be addressed. Any resident or staff member of the district who has completed the requested information on the public comment roster may speak. There is a three minute time limit per person. Additional guidelines for public comment. 

 

If there are a number of speakers commenting on the same topic this evening, we ask that remarks be consolidated and new thoughts or concepts shared. It is not the number of people who address the Board that leads to better decision-making, rather the content of your presentation. So, we respectfully ask that you do not repeat the same message of an earlier speaker. If there are four or more people who wish to address the Board on the same side of the same topic then the Board will allow 10 minutes for that group's view to be heard. All those who sign up must accompany the speaker to the front of the Board room. We respectfully ask that you do not repeat the same message, but rather present varying concepts on the same theme.  If more than 20 individuals sign up to speak to the Board, individuals will have two minutes and groups will have five minutes for a collective message to the Board.

The Board will listen to Public Comment for up to one hour if necessary and, by a majority vote, may extend the time for high volume meetings. Thank you.
