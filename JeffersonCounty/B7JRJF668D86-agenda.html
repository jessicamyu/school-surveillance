Subject
2.03 Budget Update: 5A Funding Requests (EL-5, 11)
Meeting
Jan 16, 2019 - Study/Dialogue Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion

estimated time: 10:50 p.m. (estimated duration: 90 minutes) 

 

PRESENTING STAFF:
Kathleen Askelson, chief financial officer

Nicole Stewart, director-budget and treasury


PURPOSE:
As a follow-up to discussion at the January 10, 2019, Board of Education meeting, staff is requesting Board direction on the Cabinet recommendations for expenditure of 5A mill levy override funds in 2018/2019 which will be brought as a supplemental budget request on February 7, 2019, as well as initial direction on requests for use of 5A funds in 2019/2020 which will be adopted as part of the normal budget adoption process.

 

BACKGROUND:

Voters approved the 5A ballot issue for a $33 million mill levy override on November 6, 2018. On December 19, 2018, the Board of Education received information on the process for integrating the new funding from the 5A mill levy override ballot issue into the budget for the current year and as part of the planning process for development of the 2019/2020 budget. Funding requests were gathered by district leadership and submitted using the district's current budgeting for outcomes (BFO) budget process. The funding requests were prioritized by Cabinet based on the categories for use of 5A funds as promised in the ballot language and per current Board-directed expenditure percentages. The Financial Oversight Committee reviewed the 5A funding requests on January 8, 2019. Cabinet recommendations for 5A funding requests for 2018/2019 and 2019/2020 were presented to the Board on January 10, 2019. Additionally, at the January meeting, the Board was made aware that requests for use of 5A funds in 2018/2019 will be brought as a supplemental budget request on February 7, 2019, to allow for expenditure of the funds in the current year and that requests for use of 5A funds in 2019/2020 will be adopted as part of the normal budget adoption process.
