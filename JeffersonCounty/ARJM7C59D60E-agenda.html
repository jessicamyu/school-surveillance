PRESENTING STAFF:

Marcia Anker, coordinator, 6th grade transition

Kristopher Schuh, interim chief of schools-secondary

Karen Quanbeck, interim chief of schools-elementary

Kevin Carroll, chief student success officer

Matt Flores, chief academic officer

Amy Weber, chief human services officer

Tim Reed, executive director, Facilities and Planning

John White, principal of Wayne Carle Middle School

Michele DeAndrea, principal of Bell Middle School

Rob Hoover, principal of Deer Creek Middle School

Dawn Safranka, 6th grade teacher, Meiklejohn Elementary

 

PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to receive an update on the 6th grade move of students from elementary to middle schools across the district including the transition of teachers, instruction supports, and facilities.

 

BACKGROUND:

 

On June 16, 2016; February 9 and May 4, 2017 the Board discussed and approved that Jeffco Public Schools would systemically move to a K-5/6-8 elementary and middle school configuration generally, with exceptions and engagement of the community. District staff have been working with school principals and community members to implement this transition for the 2018/19 school year for the majority of articulation areas.

 

The Board of Education requested an update on the status of the move-up of 6th graders to middle school.
