Subject
2.01 Effective and Responsible Teamwork: Session 2 (Ends 8)
Meeting
Sep 16, 2010 - Special Meeting 5:00 pm
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion

PRESENTING STAFF:
Matt Van Auken, facilitator

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to continue its discussion to develop strategies to meet its Ends statement, the Jeffco Board will be effective and responsible and will function as a highly effective team and makes the following commitments to our community:
• Effective and transparent oversight of the fiscal condition of the district and of the allocation of funds to meet Ends policies.
• Communication about the constantly evolving and innovative work of Jeffco Schools.
• High expectations for staff and student performance.

On September 2, the Board unanimously approved a motion to discuss its governance process policies 12 through 14 and 16, Board Committee Principles, Committee Structure, Process for Addressing Board Member Violations, and Monitoring Board Governance Process and Board/Staff Linkage Policies, respectively. Additionally, the Board requested time to determine how it will accept for study and approve the superintendent’s monitoring reports.

BACKGROUND:
On July 22, the Board of Education discussed working with facilitator Matt Van Auken. Mr. Van Auken interviewed each board member and on August 20 held the first work session with the Board of Education. At that meeting, the Board committed to two more work sessions with Mr. Van Auken on September 16 and 17, 2010.

On August 20, the Board met several of its desired outcomes toward being a more effective and responsible team, including communication agreements on parent/student advocacy; communications with board members and superintendent on issues, students and advice; meeting structure agreements including timing of and member participation in discussion topics, arrival time for regular meetings, and presiding member role in the absence of the Board President; and, review of governance process policies, including agreement on action related to Board Member Conflict of Interest (GP-9) and the Financial Oversight Committee.
