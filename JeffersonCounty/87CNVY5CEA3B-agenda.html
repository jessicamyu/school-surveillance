PURPOSE:
The Board of Education requested time to discuss its decision-making process related to the Facility Usage Committee’s work.

BACKGROUND:
In November 2008 during the budget development process, the Board of Education directed the superintendent to create a Facilities Usage Committee to study current facilities based on capacity, enrollment, future enrollment, needs of the district and our unique communities, special programs, future direction, educational specifications and financial stewardship.

A project plan and timeline were shared with the Board of Education in January 2009 with committee recommendations expected to include, but not be limited to, school closures, boundary changes, and/or re-purposing of buildings. The Board discussed the plan and timeline with committee leadership, facilitators and staff in March 2009.

In September and November 2009, the Board met with the Facility Usage Committee leadership, facilitators, and staff to review the September interim report, community forum process, and November draft recommended options for public feedback. The Facility Usage Committee will provide to the Board options for consideration in its final report on January 14, 2010.
