PRESENTING STAFF:

Dr. Tom McDermott, chief strategy officer

Dave Kollar, director, Student Engagement Office

Arianne Rivera, specialist on Equity and Diversity, Student Engagement Office


PURPOSE:
To better align with the district strategic plan, leadership has drafted proposed revisions to the current Board of Education Ends policy statements. This presentation and dialogue will offer the opportunity for feedback on the proposed revisions as well as update the Board of Education on work specific to elements identified in the proposed revisions.

 

BACKGROUND:

In December 2019, the Board reviewed its Ends Statements and directed staff to draft proposed revisions in alignment with the district strategic plan and system indicators. This presentation and supporting materials bring forth the requested revisions and the alignment to the strategic plan and system indicators. The Board of Education discussed and approved the strategic plan and its system indicators on February 1, March 1, 2018, February 7, 2019, and January 30, 2020.

estimated time: 10 a.m. (estimated duration: 1 hour)

 

PRESENTING STAFF:

Kathleen Askelson, chief financial officer

Nicole Stewart, director-budget and treasury

 

PURPOSE:

To provide a continuation of the March 5 2020/21 budget discussion if initial direction from the Board on priorities and placeholders is still needed for development of the proposed budget for 2020/2021. 

BACKGROUND:

The March 5 2020/21 budget discussion covered revenue assumptions per the state's fiscal outlook; impacts and updates at the state and district level on funding estimates for Jeffco, and expenditure assumptions and budget priorities as identified by Cabinet, district leadership and the community including recommendations for funding requests and potential reductions for development of the 2020/21 proposed budget. 

Budget updates were presented to the Board of Education at the September 5, October 10 and December 12, 2019, February 5, and March 5, 2020 meetings.

Recommended Action
to determine action on the arbitrator's advisory opinion through resolution provided to the Board of Education prior to the board meeting.

estimated time: Noon (estimated duration: 5 minutes) 

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. In accordance with Board executive limitation policy EL-3, Staff Treatment, the following is presented to the Board of Education after discussion of the matter in executive session. 
     
  2. Grievance #846 was filed by Jeffco Education Support Professionals Association (JESPA) on behalf of Casey Robinson, alleging that the district did not have just cause to terminate the grievant and that he was discriminated against on the basis of race and retaliated against. 
       
  3. An arbitration hearing was held and following the hearing and post-hearing briefing by the parties, the arbitrator, Kathryn E. Miller, provided a written Advisory Opinion and Award.
     
  4. Pursuant to the JESPA Agreement, the Board of Education is required to take action on the arbitrator's Advisory Opinion and Award.
