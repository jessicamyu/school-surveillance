Matt Flores, chief academic officer

PURPOSE:

To review and discuss with the Board of Education an overview of the survey findings from the 2017/2018 surveys administered in Jeffco Public Schools:  Family School Partnership (FSP) survey, Make Your Voice Heard (MYVH) student survey, and Teaching Learning Conditions Colorado (TLCC) staff survey.

 

BACKGROUND:
Every other year Jeffco Public Schools conducts stakeholder surveys to gather feedback from Jeffco Public Schools students, parents, and employees.  Each of these surveys inform the district and schools about implementation of Board Ends, the Strategic Plan, District and School Unified Improvement Plans, and other areas of continuous improvement.  The Board of Education last reviewed these surveys administered in 2015/2016.

3.04 Fiber Optic Project (EL-11)
Meeting
Jun 11, 2018 - Special Meeting-Study/Dialogue Session
Category
3. Study/Dialogue Session, 9 am
Type
Discussion

estimated time: 11:25 a.m. (estimated duration: 20 minutes) 
 

PRESENTING STAFF:

Brett Miller, chief information officer

 

PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to receive an update regarding a proof of concept for school circuit upgrades in the northwest Arvada articulation area. This is part of a three-phase project to provide adequate bandwidth for student learning in our schools

 

BACKGROUND:

This is the final phase of a three-phase project increasing network capacity to meet minimum requirements in delivering bandwidth for online assessments, digital content and 1-1 device usage. In 2010, the Board of Education approved $4.5 million of ongoing funding to sustain the wireless and routing infrastructure in our schools for phase one of this project. In 2016, phase two was completed with the district partnering with the Bi-State Optical Network (BISON) to provide increased internet capacity at a $135,000 annual cost reduction.
