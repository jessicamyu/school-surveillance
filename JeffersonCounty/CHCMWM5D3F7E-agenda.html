Recommended Action
to adopt the resolution to approve the tentative agreement and revised contract language as agreed to during contract negotiations between the District and Jefferson County Education Association (JCEA).
Goals
EL-4, Staff Compensation
EL-3, Staff Treatment

estimated time:  3:10 p.m.

 

PERTINENT FACTS:

 

  1. In accordance with Board executive limitation policy EL-3, Staff Treatment, and EL-4, Staff Compensation, the following is presented to the Board of Education for approval.
     
  2. The District and JCEA negotiating teams reached a Tentative Agreement (TA) on compensation for licensed educators for the 2022-2023 school year.  The TA creates a new salary schedule with a beginning educator salary of $50,000 and maximum salary of $100,209; an additional lane 6 for BA+90 graduate credits with an MA; and all educators will receive a minimum of a $3,000 salary increase.  The TA includes a new salary schedule for Career Technical Educators and a 1% stipend for educators assigned to Title I schools. All agreed upon changes are in accordance with Article 17 of the Master Agreement.
     
  3. In addition to compensation, the bargaining teams conducted negotiations within several identified articles reaching tentative agreement on many areas as noted in the attachments below. Language changes were agreed to in Articles 5,7,8,11,14,16,17 and 26, and Memos of Understanding were created related to staff and student physical and psychological safety and concerning engagement and commitment to a collaborative relationship and bargaining process.  
     
  4. The JCEA Membership will have ratified the agreements reached by the negotiations teams before action is taken by the Board of Education.
