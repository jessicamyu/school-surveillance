2.01 Superintendent Search
Meeting
Jan 9, 2014 - Special Meeting-S/D Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion

estimated time: 5:30 p.m. (estimated duration: 1 hour) 

 

PRESENTING STAFF:
Lorie Gillis, chief financial officer
Amy Weber, executive director of human resources

Betty Standley, director of purchasing

 

PURPOSE:
Discuss with the Board of Education the Request for Proposal (RFP) for hiring a superintendent search firm and the overall process for the hiring of a superintendent. Staff will address questions, receive direction, and support the Board’s planning for the hiring process. Staff would like to discuss and clarify the following:

  1. Timeline for RFP
  2. Members of the RFP evaluation committee
  3. Evaluation criteria and weightings
  4. Scope of Work
  5. Proposal submission requirements


BACKGROUND:
The Board of Education requested information about the district’s Request for Proposal process at the December 14 Board of Education retreat.  In preparation for discussion on January 9, a transmittal and preliminary draft RFP were submitted to the Board in the December 19 Weekly Update. The transmittal covered information on the district’s purchasing/contracting processes, applicable district policies, a tentative schedule for RFP activities, and items for Board discussion, clarification and direction.
