Category

Discussion

estimated time: 6:00 pm (estimated duration: 40 minutes)

 

PRESENTING STAFF:

Cynthia Stevenson, superintendent

Ed Bowditch, district contract lobbyist, Tomlinson & Associates

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to receive an update on the 2012 legislative session and to provide direction to the contract lobbyist as needed.

BACKGROUND:
It has been the Board's practice to schedule special meetings to discuss pending legislation during the legislative session.  The Board receives weekly updates on legislative action from Mr. Bowditch.   

The 2012 legislative session has produced a few bills of interest to Jefferson County Public Schools. The district contract lobbyist continues to bring legislation to the Board of Education for positions and direction, as needed. The district’s contract lobbyist provides state legislators with the Board’s positions on legislation.

PURPOSE:
For the Board of Education to review the agenda for the Saturday, April 28 community forums as finalized on April 5.

 

 

BACKGROUND:

As follow-up in the budget development process, the Board of Education determined to hold more community forums.  

On the morning of Saturday, April 28, the Board of Education will host five community forums.  The community forums will provide the opportunity for students, parents and community members to begin discussions on what the future of Jeffco Public Schools will be given the expected budget for 2013/2014 and into the future.
