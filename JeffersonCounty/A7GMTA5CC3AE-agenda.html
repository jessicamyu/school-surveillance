For the Board of Education to discuss and review the District Unified Improvement Plan (UIP).

On March 17, the Board of Education received and discussed the UIP recommendation from the District Accountability Committee. 

 

The Colorado Department of Education (CDE) requires all public school districts and schools complete an annual Unified Improvement Plan (UIP). These improvement plans are designed to identify priority challenges and develop major improvement strategies to meet these needs. While Jeffco is a high performing school district that consistently meets state accreditation expectations, the CDE expects the district’s UIP to focus on areas for improvement.

6. Public Agenda Part One

Information

estimated time: 6:50 p.m. (estimated duration: 1 hour)

 

The Board listens to the community’s issues of concern in two Public Comment section on its agenda.  Sign up online here to speak to the Board of Education.   
 

At this time, in Part One of Public Comment, the Board listens to concerns on topics related to items on this evening's agenda for up to one hour.  Part Two comments will be heard at the end of the meeting under Part Two Public Comment.  Board members or staff will respond during this meeting or at a later time. We listen with respect and ask those who address the Board to do the same. In order to respect all persons addressing the Board, we request that the audience not applaud individual speakers and that issues, rather than personalities, be addressed. Any resident or staff member of the district who has completed the requested information on the public comment roster may speak. There is a three minute time limit per person. Additional guidelines for public comment. 

 

If there are a number of speakers commenting on the same topic this evening, we ask that remarks be consolidated and new thoughts or concepts shared. It is not the number of people who address the Board that leads to better decision-making, rather the content of your presentation. So, we respectfully ask that you do not repeat the same message of an earlier speaker. If there are four or more people who wish to address the Board on the same side of the same topic then the Board will allow 10 minutes for that group's view to be heard. All those who sign up must accompany the speaker to the front of the Board room.
