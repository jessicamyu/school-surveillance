estimated time: 4 p.m. (estimated duration: up to 1 hour)

 

The Board listens to the community’s issues of concern in Public Comment sections on its agenda. Sign up online here to speak to the Board of Education.     
 

At this time in Public Comment, the Board listens to concerns on topics related to items on this evening's agenda for up to one hour.  We listen with respect and ask those who address the Board to do the same. In order to respect all persons addressing the Board, we request that the audience not applaud individual speakers and that issues, rather than personalities, be addressed. Any resident or staff member of the district who has completed the requested information on the public comment roster may speak. There is a one-to-three minute time limit per person depending on the number of speakers who sign up. Additional guidelines for public comment. 

 

If there are a number of speakers commenting on the same topic this evening, we ask that remarks be consolidated and new thoughts or concepts shared. It is not the number of people who address the Board that leads to better decision-making, rather the content of your presentation. So, we respectfully ask that you do not repeat the same message of an earlier speaker. If there are four or more people who wish to address the Board on the same side of the same topic then the Board will allow three-to-ten minutes depending on the number of speakers who sign up for that group's view to be heard. All those who sign up must accompany the speaker to the front of the Board room. We respectfully ask that you do not repeat the same message, but rather present varying concepts on the same theme.  If more than 20 individuals sign up to speak to the Board, individuals will have two minutes and groups will have five minutes for a collective message to the Board.

Thank you.

Recommended Action
To appoint Dr. Jason E. Glass as superintendent of schools effective July 1, 2017; and, to approve the contract of employment with Dr. Glass as presented.

estimated time: 5 p.m. (estimated duration: 30 minutes)

PERTINENT FACTS:

  1. On January 12, 2017, the Board of Education decided to undertake a superintendent search process and authorized staff to take the necessary and appropriate steps to find a new superintendent for Jeffco Public Schools.
     
  2. On January 26, the Board secured the services of Ray and Associates to conduct the executive search which began with community input on the characteristics sought in a superintendent through an online survey and community meetings during the month of February.
     
  3. In early March the Board finalized the superintendent profile and accepted applicants through the deadline of April 10, 2017.
     
  4. At the meetings of April 20, 26 and 28, 2017, the Board of Education narrowed its search. The law requires that the finalist(s) be made public fourteen days prior to appointing or employing one of the finalists.
     
  5. On May 1, the Board of Education in a unanimous vote, announced it sole superintendent finalist as Dr. Jason Glass and directed the chief human resources officer and legal counsel to negotiate the terms of employment with Dr. Glass.
