COMMITTEE MEMBERS:

Gordon Callahan, owner, Calahan Construction

George Latuda, retired director, Construction Management, Jeffco Public Schools and Denver Public Schools

Tom Murray, president, Lakewood Brick & Tile Co.

M.L. Richardson, regional manager, Asphalt Paving Company

Brittany Warga, architectural graduate, construction project manager
Jeff Wilhite, financial services partner, Wilhite2025, LLC
 

 

PURPOSE:

The Board of Education meets annually with members of its advisory committees to discuss issues related to the charge of the advisory committee.

 

BACKGROUND:

In 2010, the Board of Education authorized the establishment of the Capital Asset Advisory Committee in accordance with policies EL-8, Facilities, and FB, Facilities Planning, both of which deal with facility condition and long range facility planning.  The committee was formed from members of the 2005 Capital Improvement Program Oversight Committee and the 2009 Facilities Usage Committee. The purpose of the committee is to monitor the planning of capital needs and the implementation of capital programs, which includes oversight of the 2018 Bond program.
 

Members have a working familiarity with facility design and/or construction practices; business management expertise with organization(s) of comparable size to the district, and are independent and free from any relationship that would interfere with independent judgment.  Preference is given to Jefferson County residents. The committee meets monthly throughout the year.

Subject
2.03 Jeffco Deeper Learning Model & MAP Update (EL-11)
Meeting
Jan 15, 2020 - Special Meeting-Study/Dialogue Session
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion, Information

estimated time: 10 a.m. (estimated duration: 30 minutes)

PRESENTING STAFF:
Dr. Tom McDermott, chief strategy officer

Kristopher Schuh, chief of schools secondary

Renee Nicothodes, chief of schools elementary

Matt Flores, chief academic officer


PURPOSE:
The Board of Education requested frequent updates on the implementation progress of the Jeffco Deeper Learning Model and its impact to date on student achievement.

 

BACKGROUND:

To support schools and educators in providing students with opportunities to develop knowledge and skills aligned to Colorado Academic Standards, district leadership developed tools and resources within the Jeffco Deeper Learning Model. The Jeffco Deeper Learning Model advances the district strategic plan and responds to needs that were elevated in 2019 academic performance results.

The Board of Education discussed Spring 2019 district academic performance results on November 13, 2019, September 5, 2019 and August 26, 2019.
