PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to receive information regarding our work to support our District Unified Improvement Plan (DUIP) improvement strategies.

 

BACKGROUND:

Board Ends 3 states, "Every school and the district will have effective learning systems and shared leadership that:

  • Utilize continuous improvement processes that incorporate problem-solving approaches to reduce or eliminate root causes of student performance challenges."

Since July, leaders from throughout the Jeffco Schools system (Educational Research and Design, School Innovation and Effectiveness Team, and the Student Success Office) have been working to use continuous improvement processes to address the major improvement strategies in the DUIP.  Representatives from each team will provide a second update to the Board of Education on the work that has been done to impact improvement in the areas of Algebraic Thinking, Early Literacy, Student Plans, Continuous Improvement, and Learning Pathways.

For the Board of Education to monitor the districtwide financial condition through the second quarter financial report. 

 

BACKGROUND:

Each quarter, in compliance with Board executive limitation policies EL-5, Financial Planning and Budgeting and EL-6, Financial Administration and as outlined in Board governance process policy GP-13, Committee Structure, the Board meets to review and monitor quarterly financial reports.
