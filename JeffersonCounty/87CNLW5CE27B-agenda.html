Category

Discussion

Location: Colorado Association of School Boards office, 1200 Grant Street, Denver


PRESENTING STAFF:
Cindy Stevenson, superintendent
Ken Delay, CASB facilitator

PURPOSE:
The Board of Education will review the principles of policy governance, the development of ends policies, and the values that underlie the budget process.

BACKGROUND:
The Board of Education addressed some of these issues at an August retreat and work in September 2009. After the November 2009 election, two new members joined the Board of Education. The Board has not had the opportunity to fully address the norms, values and goals of the new board.
