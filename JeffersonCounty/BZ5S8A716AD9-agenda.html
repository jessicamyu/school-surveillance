Category
2. Discussion Agenda

Action
Recommended Action
To review the hearing officer's findings of fact and recommendation and enter a written order on the teacher's employment taking one of the following three actions: that the teacher be dismissed, the teacher be retained, or the teacher be placed on a one-year probation.
Goals
EL-3, Staff Treatment

estimated time: 4 p.m. (estimated duration: 5 minutes) 

 

PERTINENT FACTS:  

  1. In accordance with Board executive limitation policies EL-3, Staff Treatment, the following is brought forward to the Board of Education.  
     
  2. On September 3, 2020, the superintendent submitted to the Board of Education a recommendation for dismissal of a teacher, Mr. Wiley Cruse, pursuant to the provisions of the Teacher Employment, Compensation, and Dismissal Act of 1990, C.R.S., Section 22-63-101 et seq.  ("the Act").  
     
  3. Proper written notice of intent to dismiss was provided to the teacher.  
     
  4. The teacher requested a hearing in accordance with the Act.  
     
  5. A virtual hearing was held on February 3, 4, and 5, 2021 before Hearing Officer Tanya T. Light in accordance with the Act.  
     
  6. On March 8, 2021, Judge Light issued a detailed written finding of fact and concluded the District had established by a preponderance of the evidence that Mr. Cruse was insubordinate warranting dismissal under The Act.   
     
  7. Pursuant to provisions of the Act, the Board of Education shall review the Hearing Officer's findings of fact and recommended decision and enter a written order within 20 days after issuance of the Hearing Officer's findings and recommendation.  
     
  8. District staff have reviewed the Hearing Officer's written decision and proposes the Board of Education accept the Hearing Officer's findings of fact and recommendation to dismiss Mr. Cruse.  
     
  9. The Board of Education has been provided three proposed orders on this teacher's employment: dismissal, retention and probation.  
File Attachments

Motion & Voting

to adopt the following Order of Dismissal of Wiley Cruse, as read into the record.

The Board of Education of the Jefferson County School District No. R-1, acting pursuant to the Colorado Teacher Employment, Compensation, and Dismissal Act of 1990, C.R.S. §22- 63-302(9), enters the following written order in the matter of the teacher dismissal proceeding of Wiley Cruse (the teacher):

1. The Board of Education has received and reviewed the Findings of Fact and Recommendation dated March 8, 2021, submitted by Hearing Officer Tanya T. Light, in the matter of the Wiley Cruse dismissal proceeding.

2. The Board of Education adopts and incorporates by reference herein the Hearing Officer’s evidentiary Findings of Fact and notes that the Hearing Officer found that the grounds for dismissal were proved and recommended that the teacher be dismissed.

3. The Board of Education concludes that the Findings of Fact support the conclusion that the teacher was in violation of school district policies. The reasons supporting this conclusion include the following: a) Insubordination, as supported by the findings of the ALJ on March 8, 2021.

4. Based upon the foregoing, the Board of Education orders that the teacher, Wiley Cruse, be dismissed.

5. The Board of Education directs the secretary of the board to cause a copy of this order to be given immediately to the teacher and a copy to be entered into the teacher’s local file.

Motion by Stephanie D Schooley, second by Charles B Rupert.
Final Resolution: Motion Carries
Yea: Susan L Harmon, Susan L Miller, Charles B Rupert, Richard G Rush, Stephanie D Schooley
