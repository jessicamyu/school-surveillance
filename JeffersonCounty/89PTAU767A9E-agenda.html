Subject
2.01 Budget Forums (EL-5)
Meeting
Oct 26, 2010 - Special Meeting 5:00 pm
Category
2. Study/Dialogue Session
Type
Discussion

Estimated Time:  8:00 p.m.
 

 

PRESENTING STAFF:

Cindy Stevenson, superintendent
Lorie Gillis, chief financial officer
Lynn Setzer, executive director, Communications Services

 

PURPOSE:
To provide the Board an opportunity to discuss how community budget forums will be facilitated by members of the Board and will take place on Saturday, November 13 at various school locations. The community budget forums will be held to provide current budgetary information including preliminary plans for the development of the 2011/2012 budget. The budget forums will provide an opportunity to lead, communicate and solicit feedback from community and staff.

 

BACKGROUND:

On October 21, the Board revised its October 26 agenda to continue this discussion.

 

On September 17, the Board set aside time to work together on the community budget forums.
 

On October 7, the Board of Education received information on the Budget Development Process for the 2011/2012 school year from Lorie Gillis.  As a result of the discussion, Board members have a better understanding of the information to be shared at community budget forums.

A revised presentation for the budget forums will be given to the Board prior to the meeting.
