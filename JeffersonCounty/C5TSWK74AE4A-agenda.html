PRESENTING STAFF:
Tracy Dorland, superintendent

Helen Neal, chief of staff for Board and Superintendent


PURPOSE:
The Board of Education will hear from the superintendent on her plans for the year and board members will review their work together over the past year and prepare for the coming school year’s board work.

 

BACKGROUND:

The Board of Education has met in retreat session in August to check its practices so that members and staff are responsive to changing needs, situations and resources. The superintendent supports this agenda item in accord with Board governance process policies GP 1-17 and board/staff linkage policies B/SL 1-4.
