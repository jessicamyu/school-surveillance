Type

estimated time: 9 a.m. (estimated duration: 1 hour) 
 

PRESENTING STAFF:

Matt Flores, chief academic officer

Carol Eaton, executive director of Instruction Data Services

 

PURPOSE:

Members of the Board of Education will have opportunity to discuss and consider approaches to measure the recently adopted Ends. 

 

BACKGROUND:

In January 2016, the Board of Education decided to host February community forums on the development of its Ends (goals) policies. 

 

The Board discussed the community feedback and its goals on February 18 and March 7 in preparation for approving its new Ends policies on April 7.

Discussion

estimated time: 11 a.m. (estimated duration: 30 minutes) 
 

PRESENTING STAFF:

Dan McMinimee, superintendent

Helen Neal, chief of staff for the Board and Superintendent

 

PURPOSE:

For the Board of Education to discuss which members will represent the Board of Education on committees requesting a member of the Board.

 

BACKGROUND:

Annually, the Board of Education looks to confirm and/or assign board member participation on committees requested by organizations in Jefferson County or state-level Board involvement in its work. Board member involvement facilitates communication to the full Board of Education on the work of the participating organization.
