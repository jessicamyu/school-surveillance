Staff Contact:          Dean A. Tistadt, chief operating officer, Facilities and Transportation Services

 

Meeting Category:  December 13, 2012 - Work Session No. 58

 

Subject:                  Facilities Planning Advisory Council (FPAC) Quarterly Report

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion

 

Background:

 

The Facilities Planning Advisory Council reports on its activities to the School Board on a quarterly basis as required by its charter.  For this quarterly report, FPAC reports on their current and planned activities and submits several recommendations to the School Board for consideration.

 

Key Points:

 

FPAC acknowledges the approval of the Strategic Facilities Plan by the School Board on November 26, 2012, which provides guidelines for FPAC recommendations on facilities and staff proposals.

 

From September through December, 2012, six public meetings were held at which FPAC undertook review of multiple topics: Operations and Maintenance, the Capital Improvement Program, a “Boundary Lite” process, Proposed Advanced Academic Changes, and Renovation Criteria.  FPAC also requested the preparation of a staff work program to address the issues identified in the FPAC 2011-2012 Annual Report to the School Board.  The quarterly report includes several recommendations (with attached documents) on these issues and outlines plans for FPAC activities in the coming months. 

 

Recommendation:

 

That the School Board accept the Facilities Planning Advisory Council Quarterly Report dated December 13, 2012.
