Meeting Category:  June 29, 2021-Work Session

 

School Board Action Required: Discussion

 

Related To: Policy 1710, Citizen Advisory Committees to the School Board

 

Summary/Background (Key Points):

 

The AAPAC charge for the 2020-21 school year was:

“The external program review of equity in identification practices for AAP from the 2019-20 school year provided several recommendations that will require stakeholder input in a timely manner. AAPAC stakeholder representatives will receive updates and provide timely feedback on progress of recommended changes.”


AAPAC will present four topical recommendations around communicating program goals, the Young Scholars Model, Local Level IV programs, and screening processes and two overarching recommendations around staffing for Advanced Academic Resource Teachers (AARTs) and partnership with the Equity office in supporting equitable implementation of programs.

 

Recommendation:

The committee will address the School Board Charge and present recommendations as documented in the attachments.
