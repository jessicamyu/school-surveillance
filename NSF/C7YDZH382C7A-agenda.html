Subject:               2022 FCSB State and Federal Legislative Program

 

Staff Contact:      Michael Molloy, Director of Government Relations

 

Meeting Category:        Work Session

 

School Board Action Required: Discussion

 

Ignite Link:          Goal 4, Resource Stewardship

 

 

Problem Statement: 

Fairfax County Public Schools is both directly and indirectly impacted by policy and budget decisions made by local, state, and federal officials. As such, it is critical that FCPS provide input to policymakers as they debate various policy options, sharing the possible programmatic and financial impacts of legislative and policy decisions on Fairfax students, teachers, staff and the broader community.

 

Summary/Background (Key Points): 

The School Board annually adopts a legislative program and legislative priorities to inform local, state and federal public officials and others of its positions on legislative policy and budget issues of potential importance to Fairfax County Public School students, teachers, staff, and community.  The program contains the School Board’s positions on state and federal issues of major and current significance.

 

The Fairfax County School Board 2022 State and Federal Legislative Program will include additions, deletions, and formatting changes (if applicable) from the Board’s 2020 legislative positions.  These changes and updates are intended to adjust position statements to reflect actions taken over the past year by state and federal governments; to reflect the Board’s current approaches to policy debates and issues; and to clarify and simplify existing position language where appropriate.

 

Full strikethrough/underline draft text with all staff proposed changes, as well as a table detailing each staff proposed change will be provided prior to the October 25th Work Session.  Final Action on this agenda item is scheduled for later in November.

 

Recommendation:

That the Board adopt the 2022 Fairfax County School Board State and Federal Legislative Program.
