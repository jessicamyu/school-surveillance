Summary/Background (Key Points):

 

This Presentation will provide an overview of the School Improvements and Support (DSIS) budget which support the Division in:

  • Providing support for all schools to be fully accredited with the Virginia Department of Education
  • To develop best practices and support of teachers and school teams in strategies for closing achievement gaps through Region Support Teams
  • Provide professional development and support in the areas of MTSS to ensure consistent practices through Region Support Teams
  • Centralize practices for Assessment and Reporting and ensure compliance with VDOE policy
  • Provide central guidance and support for the successful implementation and operation of VHSL Athletics and Activities and ensure a robust and inclusive clubs program for all students
  • Ensure opportunities and access for students to participate in alternative pathways to graduation through our Nontraditional programs
