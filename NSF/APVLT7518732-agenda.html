In order to comply with Section 2.2-3712 (D) of the Code of Virginia, it is necessary for the Committee to certify that since the Audit Committee of the Fairfax County School Board convened a closed meeting on October 25, 2017, to the best of each member’s knowledge, only public business matters lawfully exempted from open meeting requirements and only such public business matters as were identified in the motion convening the closed meeting were heard, discussed, or considered by the Committee during the closed meeting.
