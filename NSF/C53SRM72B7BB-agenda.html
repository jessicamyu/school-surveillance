Citizens who want to address the Board at a Regular meeting, may sign up online beginning at 6 a.m. four (4) business days prior to the meeting, or by calling the School Board office at 571-423-1075 during normal business hours. For more information, please visit the FCPS Website.

 

A total of 16 in-person, live virtual, and prerecorded video speaker slots are available per meeting. Thirteen slots will be reserved for in-person and live virtual speakers.  Three (3) slots will be reserved for students and three (3) slots will be reserved for those speakers who wish to submit a prerecorded video. The speakers list closes at 48 hours before the meeting. After the registration closes, speakers will be determined by a random lottery process.  After the random process, speakers will be notified if they have received a speaking or prerecorded video spot.   After the first 13 in-person or live virtual speakers are determined, the next ten (10) names as determined by the same random process will be chosen as the waitlist and notified that they are on the waitlist to speak.  Video submissions will not be permitted for the waitlist.  Waitlist speakers must be present at the meeting to speak in the event of a no show.  Speaker substitutions are not permitted.  A speaker may not yield their time to another individual before or during their remarks.

 

The School Board will not hear statements involving issues that have been scheduled for public hearings (such as Capital Improvement Program, budget, and boundaries), or personal attacks on any person. Complaints regarding individual students or school-based employees should not be raised at public meetings. Any such concerns should be directed to the appropriate school principal or other school official. Citizens are encouraged to write the School Board on any school-related topic.

Subject:    Appointment of “individual Trustee” to the Board of Trustees of the Educational Employees’ Supplementary Retirement System of Fairfax County (ERFC)

 

Staff Contact:  Eliazer Martinez, ERFC Executive Director/CIO, ERFC

 

Meeting Category:     June 30, 2022– Regular Meeting

 

School Board Action Required:   Information

 

Related To:  Legal Requirement

 

Key Points:

 

The Fairfax County Ordinance, Ch. 3, Art. 4, Section 3-4-3 (the “Enabling Ordinance”), provides that the ERFC Board of Trustees (“ERFC Board”) shall consist of seven people, one of whom shall be the “individual Trustee.”  The individual Trustee may not be affiliated with or employed by: Fairfax County, the Fairfax County School Board, or any union or similar organization which represents teachers or other employees in Fairfax County.  The individual Trustee is chosen by unanimous vote or consent of the other six Trustees on the ERFC Board, subject to the approval of the Fairfax County School Board.  Under the Enabling Ordinance, the ERFC Board recommendation must be based on a determination that the “individual Trustee possesses competent financial and/or investment counseling skills.”

 

The ERFC Board unanimously voted to recommend Adam McConagha for re-appointment as the individual Trustee. 

 

Mr. McConagha serves as Managing Director and Co-lead of Origination for Private Equity and Venture Capital at the U.S. International Development Finance Corporation (“DFC”), the U.S.’s development finance institution.  At DFC, Mr. McConagha is responsible for overseeing underwriting and strategic initiatives related to DFC’s equity funds and co-investment programs.  Prior to joining DFC, Mr. McConagha worked in Asian capital markets as a banker, equity analyst, and trader, and covered the region for nearly two decades at banks in New York and Hong Kong.  Mr. McConagha earned B.S. and M.S. degrees from Georgetown University’s School of Foreign Service and an MBA from Georgetown’s School of Business.  Mr. McConagha meets all the requirements of the position.

 

Recommendation:

That the School Board approve the re-appointment of Adam McConagha to the ERFC Board of Trustees as the individual Trustee for the one-year term beginning July 1, 2022.
