Meeting Category:  February 29, 2016 – Worksession No. 75

 

Subject: Consideration of options for potential redesign of Board’s Standing and Advisory committees

 

Action Required:  Discussion

 

Key Points:

At its January 11, 2016 worksession, the Board agreed upon a timeline for discussing the redesign of the Board’s Standing and Advisory Committees.  According to the timeline (attached):

 

February 27 Board Retreat: Prior to the retreat, the school board liaisons to touch base with their advisory committee chairpersons and find out what is working, what is not working and what could be improved and report at retreat.  The superintendent will research what is being done in other districts and incorporate this research into proposed options for redesign for discussion at February retreat.  The board will discuss restructuring of the board’s standing committees and advisory committees and come up with potential options. (The February retreat has been replaced with the February 25 worksession.) 

 

Recommendation:
That the committee consider the feedback from the advisory committee chairpersons and the superintendent’s research on the options and decide on potential options to take back to the advisory committees and the Board to share for further feedback.
