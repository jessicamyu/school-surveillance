Comprehensive Planning Development Committee (CPDC)

 

Charge: To design a recommendation for a comprehensive planning process, monitor that process, and suggest improvements to the Board.

 

Members: Three School Board members, one of whom shall be appointed chairman, and one member of the Fairfax City School Board.

 

Staff Liaison: Assistant Superintendent of Facilities and Transportation Services

 

Reporting Schedule: Following each meeting of the committee


Appointment: Annually in July
