Summary/Background (Key Points):

 

As part of the FY 2020 Advertised Budget, consideration of all budget amendments were deferred to the April 29 and May, 2 2019 budget work sessions.  Below is a summary of budget amendments and follow-on motions.

 

FY 2020 Proposed School Board Amendments

  1. Get2Green Program
  2. AART - Title 1 Schools Only
  3. Living Wage $15.27
  4. World Language Expansion
  5. MSA 1% instead of Bonus
  6. Custodial Staffing
  7. Facilities Trades Staffing
  8. Movable Walls Safety
  9. Behavior Intervention Teachers
  10. Restraint and Seclusion Placeholder

 

FY 2020 Proposed School Board Follow-Ons

  1. Teacher Recruitment, Retention & Salary Budget Guidance
  2. Title 1 Multi-year Plan
  3. Community Schools and Health Support
  4. Equity of Opportunity for Enrichment & Afterschool Activities
  5. Staffing Standards
  6. Instructional Assistant Salary Restoration -1/2 Teacher BA
  7. Living Wage Review
  8. Custodial/Trades Staffing Standard & Review
