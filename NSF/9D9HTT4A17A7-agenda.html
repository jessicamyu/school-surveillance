1. Closed Meeting - 5:00 p.m.

 The Board will now make a motion to go into closed meeting:

  1.  to discuss and consider disciplinary matters concerning students pursuant to Section 2.2-3711(A)(2) of the Code of Virginia;
  2.  to discuss and consider the employment and performance of a specific employee pursuant to Section 2.2-3711(A)(1) of the Code of Virginia;
  3.  to protect the privacy of individuals in personal matters not related to public business pursuant to Section 2.2-3711(A)(2) of the Code of Virginia;
  4.  to discuss and consider the acquisition (by purchase contract or by statutory process) of real property for location of an elementary school, where discussion in an open meeting would adversely affect the bargaining position or negotiating strategy of the School Board, and
  5.  to consult with legal counsel and to consider briefings by staff members on the same topic, where such consultation and briefing in open meeting would adversely affect the negotiating or litigating posture of the School Board, pursuant to Sections 2.2-3711(A)(3) and 2.2-3711(A)(7) of the Code of Virginia.

2.02 Certification of Closed Meeting Compliance
Meeting
Nov 18, 2013 - WS No. 50 - Forum
Category
2. Forum on Board Topics - 6 p.m.
Type
Action
Recommended Action
Certify closed meeting compliance

In order to comply with Section 2.2-3712 (D) of the Code of Virginia, it is necessary for the Board to certify that since the Fairfax County School Board convened a closed meeting on November 18, 2013, to the best of each member’s knowledge, only public business matters lawfully exempted from open meeting requirements and only such public business matters as were identified in the motion convening the closed meeting were heard, discussed, or considered by the Board during the closed meeting.


The foregoing also shall be deemed to include the certification of each School Board member who served on a student disciplinary committee that those closed committee meetings are lawfully exempted from open meeting requirements by Section 2.2-3711(A)(2) of the Code of Virginia, and that only student disciplinary matters were heard, discussed, or considered in those closed committee meetings.
