Meeting Category: June 24, 2019 – Work Session

 

School Board Action Required: Discussion

 

Ignite Link: Goal 2: Caring Culture

 

Problem Statement: Regulation 2601, Student Rights and Responsibilities, needs to be updated to reflect legislative changes and to address concerns raised by principals and the community.

 

Outcome:  Provide input regarding recommended changes and presentation materials.

 

Summary/Background (Key Points):

 

A number of changes are being proposed to the SR&R (Attachment A) based on requests from Board members at the June 17, 2019, work session. These are highlighted on the attachment and include:

 

  • Additional language was added to clarify:
    • FCPS works to meet unique needs of students (p. 1)
    • Parents will be notified of search (p. 8)
    • Parents may contact the Ombudsman’s office if seeking more information about  how to make a complaint (p. 11)
    • All staff are mandated reporters of allegations of discrimination and Title IX violations (p. 11)
    • Definition for substantial disruption (p. 58)
  • Charts (pp. 13-22) were updated, and a violation code key was added
  • Removed absenteeism as a discipline violation (p. 26)
  • Adjusted consequences for first time tobacco and vaping to 2 day in-school suspension and interventions (p. 27)
  • Temporary removal from student activities (pp. 28, 31, 35) was clarified, including an exception for participation in convocation and graduation ceremonies, except for specified violations
    • Footnote added to indicate which violations would result in removal from graduation ceremonies (pp. 28-29)
  • Parent notification requirements were updated (p. 38)
  • Academic services available during appeal were enumerated (p. 46)
  • Guidance for Parents of Students with Disabilities was updated (pp. 49-50)

 

Attachment C is a proposed revision to Regulation 6410, Acceptable Use Policy for Student Network Access, which is included in the SR&R Guide for Families as Appendix A.

 

Recommendation: 

 

That the School Board adopt Regulation 2601.33P, Student Rights and Responsibilities, as detailed in the agenda item.
