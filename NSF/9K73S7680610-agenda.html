Staff Contact:          Dan Parris, Interim Deputy Superintendent

 

Meeting Category:  June 12, 2014 – Work Session No. 123       

 

Subject:                  Strategic Planning - Update from ECRA Consultants

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion                      

 

Key Points:

 

The ECRA group was selected to partner with Fairfax County Public Schools in the development of a division-wide strategic plan. The proposal outlines a three-phase process that includes conducting research, developing the strategic plan and identifying steps for implementation. 

 

During this work session, ECRA will engage and focus the School Board on the tasks described below: 

  • Review the strategic plan process
  • Review the number of surveys administered
  • Review survey questions
  • Engage FCPS School Board and Board of Supervisors in discussion around shared priorities
  • Discuss next steps for the July School Board Retreat
