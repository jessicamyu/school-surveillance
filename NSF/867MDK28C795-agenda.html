Agenda Item: FY 2007 – 2011 FCPS Capital Improvement Program


Staff Contact: Dean A. Tistadt


Background:

The Superintendent has proposed the Capital Improvement Program (CIP) for the years 2007 – 2011. This program reflects updated project cost estimates while maintaining the project schedules of past programs. This program also has been updated to reflect current enrollment projections that have an impact on scheduled capacity enhancement projects. The net result of these actions is a program that requires funding in excess of the annual bond authority amount provided by the Board of Supervisors.


Key Issues:

The School Board is being asked to adopt a CIP that exceeds the funding currently provided by the Board of Supervisors, to consider staff recommendations for boundary studies to address school capacity challenges, and to approve project changes based upon current enrollment projections.



School Board Discussion/Decision: See Key Issues


Staff Recommendation:

To adopt the Capital Improvement Program as proposed by the Superintendent and to give direction to staff regarding boundary studies as outlined in the attached document.


Options:

The School Board could adopt a program based upon the current Board of Supervisors approved annual funding amount of $130 million. Such a program would have a negative impact on the timing of projects.
