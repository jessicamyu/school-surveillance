Subject: Consideration of Renaming Robert E. Lee High School

 

School Board Action Required: Action

 

Key Points: FCPS held a community meeting on March 11 to collect community input on changing the name of the school. Feedback from this meeting is posted online and has been shared with the School Board and the Superintendent. A School Board Public Hearing was also held on Monday, June 22, 2020.

As outlined in Regulation 8170.8: Procedures for Naming School Facilities and Dedicating Areas of School Facilities or Grounds, “…a vote will be taken by the Board, through its regular meeting agenda process, to proceed with the name change request.”

 

If the Board votes to proceed with the name change, the one month public comment period will begin on June 24. On July 23, the Superintendent will recommend to the Board a new name for the school, and the Board will take vote on the new name. If the Board does not vote to proceed with renaming the school at the June 23 meeting, the process will end.


Recommendation: That the Board vote to proceed with the name change for Robert E. Lee High School.
