Meeting Category: October 19, 2021-Work Session           

 

School Board Action Required: Discussion

 

Summary/Background:

On May 4, 2021, the Board directed staff to initiate a Program Review to ensure FCPS Program Offerings are aligned to 21st Century best practices and Portrait of a Graduate Program outcomes.

 

The Board requested a review of 10 non-core programs, including information on program locations, associated costs, demographics and enrollment. The Board directed the program review to be conducted in 3 phrases.

 

Phase 1: May 2021-June 2021--Review of Current Non-core programs

  • Staff provided an inventory of current non-core program offerings. This inventory included program descriptions and addressed program differences and availability across schools, program locations, associated costs, demographics, and enrollment by school.

 

Phase 2: October 19, 2021-- School Board work session

 

  • At today’s work session staff will present a summary of Phase 1 findings. School Board members will determine the final scope of the programs to be analyzed and the analysis process for Phase 3. Per the May 4 Forum guidelines, the Phase 3 review will include only offerings presented in Phase 1.

 

Phase 3:

  • Programs selected by the Board for further review will be analyzed for their alignment to research-based practices and Portrait of a Graduate outcomes. The Phase 3 review will provide the Board with recommendations for program improvements, expansion, additions, or elimination.
