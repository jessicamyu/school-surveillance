Summary/Background (Key Points):

On February 6, 2020, the School Board adopted the FY21 Advertised Budget. Due to the changing economic conditions resulting from the Coronavirus Pandemic (COVID-19), it is necessary to revise the FY21 Advertised Budget.

 

On March 31, 2020, the Board of Supervisors held a Budget Committee meeting to discuss potential changes to the County Executive’s FY21 Advertised Budget Plan due to the impact on County revenue sources resulting from the health crisis.

 

On April 7, 2020, the County Executive will submit and add-on package to the Board of Supervisors with a revised budget proposal.

 

An overview will be provided on the estimated impact of COVID-19 to the FY20 and FY21 budgets. The FY21 overview will include the revised County transfer, estimated state revenue impact, and potential expenditure revisions.
