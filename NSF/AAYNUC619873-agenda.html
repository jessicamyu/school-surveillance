8:00 AM – 2:45 PM

 

 Arrival                                                                                                      8:00

 

Board Member Roles and Assigned Responsibilities                                 8:30 – 10:00

  • Board Member
  • Meeting Manager
  • Committee Chair
  • Committee Liaison

 

Questions to Consider - Please focus on the role rather than the individual.

  • Are the responsibilities of the role clearly defined and understood?
  • Are the responsibilities of the role defined in a way to make the role meaningful and helpful?
  • Is the role needed?
  • What improvements need to be made to make the role more effective?

 

Board Chair and Vice Chair

  • Are the purpose and the authority of the Chair and the Vice Chair clearly understood by all board members?
  • What changes in the role and the authority of the Chair might be considered?
