Subject:                       FY 2016 Advertised Budget

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion                                                     

 

Key Points:

 

On January 8, 2015, Superintendent Karen Garza presented the FY 2016 Proposed Budget.  Superintendent Garza’s presentation and the FY 2016 Proposed Budget book are available at http://www.fcps.edu/news/fy2016.shtml

 

The FY 2016 Proposed Budget for the School Operating Fund totals $2.6 billion. This is an increase of $64.0 million, or 2.6 percent, from the FY 2015 Approved Budget.

 

The increase results primarily from several significant expenditure requirements which are offset by budget reductions totaling $55.4 million:

  • Enrollment Growth and Student Demographic Adjustments - $18.6 million
  • Step Increase for All Eligible Employees - $39.7 million
  • One Percent Market Scale Adjustment for All Employees - $19.7 million
  • Full-Year Impact of Step from FY 2015 - $10.4 million
  • Full-Day Mondays - $4.9 million
  • Later High School Start Times - $4.9 million
  • Placeholder to Address Large Class Sizes in Elementary Schools - $3.1 million

 

In addition to addressing questions regarding the FY 2016 Proposed Budget, information for using the placeholder to address large elementary classes will be discussed.

None.
