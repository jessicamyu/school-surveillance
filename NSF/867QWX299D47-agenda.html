DRAFT

Policy 7325.1
School Board



GENERAL SUPPORT—FACILITIES AND TRANSPORTATION SERVICES

Security, Safety, and Emergency Procedures

External Video Surveillance

This policy supersedes Policy 7325.


I. PURPOSE

To protect the safety of students, employees, visitors, and property.

II. PHILOSOPHY

The school system may install video cameras and record video images of
building exteriors and of interior rooms and closets used for storage of
valuable assets. It also may make video and sound recordings on school buses.
