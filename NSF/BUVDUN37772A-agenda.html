Subject: Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology (TJHSST) Admissions

 

Staff Contact: Scott Brabrand, Superintendent, Fairfax County Public Schools

 

Other Staff Present: Marty Smith, chief operating officer

          Fabio Zuluaga, assistant superintendent, Region 2

          Ann Bonitatibus, principal, TJHSST

          Jeremy Shughart, director of admissions, TJHSST

 

Meeting Category: November 17, 2020 – Work Session

 

School Board Action Required: Information

 

Key Points: The School Board reviewed options for revising the TJHSST admissions process in September and October 2020. On September 5, 2020 the School Board considered a Merit Lottery process for admissions. On October 6 and 8, 2020, the School Board discussed a Revised Merit Lottery, which incorporated a holistic review into the Merit Lottery. This work session will provide final options for School Board consideration.
