RESOLUTION OF COMMENDATION
FOR
MICHELLE OHANIAN


WHEREAS, Michelle Ohanian, English for speakers of other languages teacher at Mountain View Alternative High School, is an inspiring instructor, an exemplary teacher, and a tireless advocate for students in Fairfax County; and

WHEREAS, she provides a supportive learning environment and community of trust for her studentsÂ¯building their confidence and encouraging them to set goals and envision a bright future; and

WHEREAS, Ms. Ohanian is highly qualified and knowledgeable in her field and is a leader who contributes greatly to the professional development and mentoring of other teachers; and

WHEREAS, she is congratulated for her collaboration within the larger teaching community as an active teacher-consultant for the Northern Virginia Writing Project and chairperson of the National Writing Project’s English Language Learner Network; and

WHEREAS, Ms. Ohanian is a passionate and devoted educator who motivates others with her energy, creative approach, and enthusiasm for teaching and learning and is “…a teacher who consistently demonstrates dedication, professionalism, caring, and a commitment to excellence”;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that the School Board of Fairfax County, Virginia, honors Michelle Ohanian as the 2008 Fairfax County Public Schools Teacher of the Year.
