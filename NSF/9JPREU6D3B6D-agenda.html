Meeting Category:      May 29, 2014 - Work Session No. 117

 

Subject:                      Human Relations Advisory Committee (HRAC) Annual Report

 

School Board Action Required: Discussion

 

Related to:                 Policy 1710, Citizen Advisory Committees to the School Board

 

Key Points:

 

The School Board charged the HRAC to “Examine FCPS’s consideration of the diverse needs of its diverse student populations.” 

 

In response to the School Board's charge, the committee focused in on three areas: 21st Century Learning, Diverse Needs, and Communication.  The committee had several speakers attend meetings over the course of the year to provide information about the various FCPS programs that appeared to be in alignment with the charge.  The committee considers their efforts a beginning step and plans to recommend continuing this work as the charge for the upcoming year.  If approved, the HRAC would take a closer examination of one or more of the three areas they addressed this year.
