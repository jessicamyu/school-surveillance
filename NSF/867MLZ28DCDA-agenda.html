Agenda Item: CTEAC Annual Report

Staff Contact: Ann Monday, Assistant Superintendent, Instructional Services

Background:
The Career and Technical Education Advisory Committee (CTEAC), formerly known as the Professional Technical Studies Advisory Committee (PTSAC) has eight meetings per year. The annual report presents the issues discussed by the Committee, recommendations to the School Board, and future goals of the Committee. The CTEAC is composed of members appointed by the School Board and members recommended by a CTEAC selection committee.

Key Issues:
Members of the CTEAC are interested in the following:
路 Researching solutions to the academy transportation and bell-schedule alignment challenges
路 Collaborating in the planning for the implementation of the ‘High Schools of the Future’ initiative
路 Continued funding for industry certification tests for students
路 Promoting awareness of CTE programs to students, parents, and guidance counselors

School Board Discussion/Decision:
For information only

Staff Recommendation:
The CTE staff supports the work and the annual report of the Career and Technical Education Advisory Committee.
