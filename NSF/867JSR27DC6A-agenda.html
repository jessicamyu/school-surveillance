SCHOOL BOARD WORK SESSION
November 6, 2006

After reviewing this agenda item, board members are requested to e-mail any questions or issues about this agenda item to the staff contact person listed below prior to the work session. Those issues and questions will be used as the framework for the staff presentation at the board work session.
Agenda Item: Adult and Community Education (ACE) Financial Recovery Plan

Staff Contact: Ann Monday, Assistant Superintendent, Instructional Services

Background:
The Board has requested an update on ACE’s financial situation, since having awarded a $500,000 budget transfer from the operating fund to the ACE Fund in third quarter of FY 2006. ACE’s Financial Recovery Plan, and the associated review conducted by the FCPS Internal Audit Office, will be described and discussed.

Key Issues:
ACE’s expenditures have outpaced its revenue over the past five years, thus eroding reserve funds. Key issues to be covered in the presentation are the measures being taken by ACE to restore financial stability, as well as the recommendations made in the publication, Financial Issues Facing the Office of Adult and Community Education, that was authored by the FCPS Internal Audit Office.

School Board Discussion/Decision:
A key issue for Board discussion is the interrelationship between ACE and the FCPS operating funds, as described in the audit report.

Staff Recommendation:
Recommendations of the Internal Audit Office, with staff responses by the ACE Director, are included in the audit report.

Options:
None recommended at this time.
