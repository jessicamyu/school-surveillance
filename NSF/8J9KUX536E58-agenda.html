Staff Contact:         Dean A. Tistadt, chief operating officer, Department of Facilities and Transportation

 

Meeting Category:  March 12, 2012 - Work Session No. 94

 

Subject:                  Facilities Planning Advisory Council (FPAC) Quarterly Report

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion

 

Background:

 

The Facilities Planning Advisory Council reports on its activities to the School Board on a quarterly basis as required by its charter.

 

Key Points:

 

The attached quarterly report summarizes the community outreach ‘Listening Tours’ which were undertaken for each school cluster and completed in December 2011.  A power point presentation on the Listening Tour activity is also attached.

 

The report also includes information on the FPAC retreat held in January 2012 and FPAC’s work assignment from the School Board which is:

 

Develop and update annually a long-term strategic plan for FCPS facility needs for presentation to and approval by the School

Board.  The strategic plan should reflect input from all interested stakeholders and constituencies.

 

A second power point entitled “FPAC Background Information” is also attached to provide additional information on the formation and mission for FPAC.

 

Recommendation:

 

That the School Board accept the Facilities Planning Advisory Council March 2012 Quarterly Report.
