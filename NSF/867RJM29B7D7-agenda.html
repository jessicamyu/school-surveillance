DRAFT
School Board/Staff Communication Process


Level I – Information
      Requests: Routine, readily available information, quick response, copy of report that has already been generated, constituent need/information

      Process: Request may be made directly to the appropriate Leadership Team member. If the request is made by e-mail, please copy the Superintendent and Pam Goddard.

Level II – Research
      Requests: Prepare talking points, reports or other correspondence; any request for analysis (i.e., hiring process, discipline data or test scores; updates on programs, initiatives, etc.); or any other request that requires staff time to generate a new document, as well as request for staff support to attend meetings scheduled by board members.

      Process: Request must be made directly to the Superintendent who will coordinate with the appropriate Leadership Team member taking into consideration prioritization with normal duties, establishing reasonable response time, and linking with Strategic Targets, etc.


Level III – Policy Research/Recommendation
      Requests: Analysis of current/future policy or issue requiring School Board attention

      Process: To School Board for review and appropriate direction to Superintendent.
