Meeting Category:  October 5, 2021 – Work session


School Board Action Required:   Discussion         

 

Key Points:    

 

Presentation will begin with remarks from Dr Emily Solari, from University of Virginia.  Remarks will include information about the science of reading.

 

Data will be shared to demonstrate the current state of reading achievement, including the significant and persistent gaps between student groups.

 

Information about both the immediate changes that have been made to PreK-2 literacy instruction for this school year and the development of a PreK-12 Equitable Access to Literacy Plan will be detailed.

 

Immediate changes include professional development, basal resource adoption process, required assessment, and curriculum development in phonological awareness and phonics. 

 

The PreK-12 Equitable Access to Literacy Plan will be focused on the success of all learners and include outcomes and actions related to core instruction and intervention, assessment, professional development, communication, leadership, and accountability.   Plan will be created in collaboration with diverse stakeholder group input.   

 

FCPS strives to ensure students achieve proficiency in literacy regardless of race, gender, zip code, ability, socioeconomic status, or languages spoken at home.
