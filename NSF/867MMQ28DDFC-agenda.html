SCHOOL BOARD WORK SESSION
December 5, 2005


After reviewing this agenda item, board members are requested to e-mail any questions or issues about this agenda item to the staff contact person listed below prior to the work session. Those issues and questions will be used as the framework for the staff presentation at the board work session.

Agenda Item: FY 2007 Budget Priorities


Staff Contact: Jack Dale or Deirdra McLaughlin


Background:

The Office of Budget Services is developing the FY 2007 proposed budget. A major part of the budget development process is the identification of new resources. The attached list of new resources represents priority items submitted by the Leadership Team. The items are categorized by school-based and nonschool-based and are sorted by department. Details on each of the items are attached.

Key Issues:

Five of the new resource items are identified as required.


School Board Discussion/Decision:

Prior to finalizing the FY 2007 Proposed Budget, the Superintendent is seeking input from the School Board on the new resource items that most accurately reflect the School Board priorities.
