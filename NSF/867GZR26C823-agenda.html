    RESOLUTION
    NAMING THE 100 WING COURTYARD AT
    HUTCHISON ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
    “THE JoANN DeMARIA MEMORIAL GARDEN”

    WHEREAS, JoAnn DeMaria was a beloved teacher and friend to students and staff members at Hutchison Elementary School for 27 years; and

    WHEREAS, her belief in the ability of each child to succeed and grow created an environment of caring and warmth in her classroom; and

    WHEREAS, Ms. DeMaria fostered a love of learning in her students and continued to nurture them throughout her years of teaching; and

    WHEREAS, Ms. DeMaria shared her love of science with students, staff members, and the community in many ways throughout the years, and she used her love and knowledge to help students understand the world in which they live; and

    WHEREAS, because she devoted her life to helping students, staff members, and community members in many ways, it is fitting to create a living science garden to continue the love of science she so passionately believed in;

    NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Fairfax County School Board names the 100 wing courtyard at Hutchison Elementary School “The JoAnn DeMaria Memorial Garden,” in recognition of her love and support of children, her commitment to student achievement, and her valued service to her community.

    6.03 CTEMS Annual Plan and 2007-2008 Application for Career Technical Education Funds - Recommendation to approve the CTEMS Annual Plan and 2007-2008 Application for Career and Technical Education Funds (IS; action scheduled 5/24/07)
    Meeting
    May 10, 2007 - Regular Meeting No. 19
    Category
    6. New Business
    Type
    Information



    Staff Contact: Ann Monday , Assistant Superintendent, Instructional Services

    SUBJECT: CTEMS Annual Plan and 2007-2008 Application for Career and Technical Education Funds

    RECOMMENDATION:
        That the School Board approve the Career and Technical Education Management Systems (CTEMS) Annual Plan and 2007-2008 Application for Career and Technical Education funds, as detailed in the agenda item.


    EXPLANATION:
        The Career and Technical Education Management Systems (CTEMS) document provides the Virginia Department of Education with each local education agency’s (LEA) plan for providing and implementing career and technical education instruction to both public school and adult students. The CTEMS document specifies all of the activities and programs available to students as a result of local, state, and federal funds. The CTEMS document is prepared in the preset uniform format (including assurances, charts, and schedules) as provided by the Virginia Department of Education.

    Changes to the previous 2006-2007 CTEMS document have been bolded throughout.

        Federal funding from the Carl D. Perkins Career and Technical Education Act of 2006 (Perkins IV), which is a continuing grant provided to all local education agencies (LEA) in the Commonwealth of Virginia, is available to assist school districts in providing elective career and technical programs for middle and high school students. Formal application for federal moneys is one component of the annual CTEMS document to the Virginia Department of Education.Schedule 2-B, page 47, of the CTEMS document specifies the expenditures of the Perkins federal grant in improving career and technical education programs according to the “Appropriate Use of Federal Funds” on page 61 of the document.

        Focusing on current priority needs for this school division, the CTEMS local plan continues a cycle in identifying the goals, objectives, and strategies for locally providing career and technical education programs.
