Meeting Category:  September 8, 2014 – Work Session No. 16

 

Subject:                    FY 2016 Fiscal Forecast     

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion                                              

 

Key Points:

 

In order to begin the FY 2016 budget planning process, the fiscal forecast for the School Operating Fund will be presented. The forecast includes five years of projections for all key financial areas that drive the budget development process, such as revenue assumptions and expenditure assumptions, including employee salaries and benefits, student membership, and other requirements. 

 

Based on projections for revenue and required expenditures and after including a three percent transfer increase as included in the County’s budget guidance, FCPS is forecasting a deficit for FY 2016. A discussion of the fiscal forecast, including revenue and expenditure projections, will begin the process of providing input regarding considerations for the FY 2016 proposed budget development.

Staff Contact:         Theresa Weatherman, school board auditor, Internal Audit                                                                                                             

 

Meeting Category:  September 8, 2014 - Work Session No. 18                       

 

Subject:                    Audit Committee Composition and Office Structure

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion

 

Key Points:

 

The Board has had much discussion over the past several years about the structure of the Audit Committee (AC), the organization of the School Board’s Internal Audit Office, and the scope of the office's activities and plans.  At the FY 2014 mid-year budget review, the AC recommended and Board approved adding two new auditor positions.  The AC is recommending additional changes to the Audit Committee, the School Board Internal Audit Office, and the scope of auditing activities as follows:

  • Audit Committee Structure
    • Increase the number of School Board members from three to four
    • Add two positions for citizen members whose role and responsibilities would be the same as all other committee members, with specific qualifications required
    • Set a two-year term for committee members
  • Internal Audit Office Structure
    • Create an Auditor General (AG) position
    • Separate the office into two areas, both reporting to the AG

The Board is scheduled to consider these recommendations as new business on September 18, with action scheduled October 9, 2014.
