Fairfax County Public School Board Retreat
Saturday, November 7, 2020
11:00 – 4:00 pm

Retreat Goals
• strengthen interpersonal dynamics and build trust among board members;
• arrive to consensus regarding work procedures;
• review essential Robert’s Rules in support of efficient board operations;
• evaluate school board priorities; and
• identify opportunities for continued board reflection and improvement.
