Subject:  FY 2020 Budget

 

Key Points:

 

On January 10, 2019, the Superintendent presented the FY 2020 Proposed Budget that focuses on employee compensation. The FY 2020 Proposed Budget for the School Operating Fund totals $3 billion and is an increase of $117.4 million, or 4.1 percent, over the FY 2019 Approved Budget. 

            

During the work session, questions regarding the FY 2020 Proposed Budget and potential amendments to the main motion for the advertised budget will be discussed.  The draft motion states that the School Board adopt the FY 2020 Advertised Budget as detailed in the Superintendent’s Proposed Budget.

 

The FY 2020 Proposed Budget presentation and document are available at https://www.fcps.edu/about-fcps/budget/budget-documents.
