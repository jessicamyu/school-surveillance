The Board will now make a motion to go into closed meeting to: 1) discuss and consider personnel matters, specifically the performance, demotion, salaries, resignation, termination and/or disciplining of specific public officers and specific public employees pursuant to Section 2.2-3711(A)(1) of the Code of Virginia; 2) consult with legal counsel regarding specific legal matters requiring the provision of legal advice by such counsel pursuant to Section 2.2-3711(A)(7) and (A)(8) of the Code of Virginia, specifically personnel issues, breach of contract and related matters; and 3) dispense with the attendance of the Superintendent or his designee pursuant to Va.
