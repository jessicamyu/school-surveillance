The following topics will be discussed:

 

1. Board Statement to support or elaborate on BOS Statement/Resolution on Diversity and Inclusion - The Board of Supervisors plans to consider a Resolution on Diversity and Inclusion at its April 4 regular Board meeting, which includes statements about our school system being committed to maintaining a safe, inclusive and welcoming learning environment for all children and valuing the richly diverse backgrounds of our students and families. The draft Resolved statement reads:

 

Therefore, be it resolved that the Fairfax County Board of Supervisors does hereby affirm our strong commitment to maintaining a community culture that values and celebrates the differences among our neighbors.  

 

The School Board can 1) take no action and let the BOS statement stand on its own, 2) vote on a simple motion to support the BOS statement or 3) Issue a  statement of our own on this issue.

To be timely and relevant, a placeholder agenda item has been placed on the April 4 SB agenda. The Forum discussion is to determine what motion if any will be considered. (Evans)

 

2.  Contract signing authority and board notification - Review the existing policy and consider revising the contract amount requiring Board notification and/or signature, discuss reporting timeline with consideration of increasing the frequency of reports. (Corbett Sanders)
