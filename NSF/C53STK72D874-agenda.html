Subject: Comprehensive Special Education Review Interim report

Staff Contact: Esther Ko, auditor general

Other:  Allison Gandhi, American Institutes for Research (AIR), vice president of learning supports    
             Lindsey Hayes, American Institutes for Research (AIR), senior researcher

Meeting category: September 21, 2021 – Work Session

School Board Action Required: Discussion

 

Summary: AIR and the auditor general will present the Comprehensive Special Education Review – First Year Report.  This review was conducted at the request of the School Board, focusing on below four objectives: (1) to evaluate the design, structure and established processes of special education services, degree of fidelity of implementation and continuous monitoring; (2) to evaluate the adequacy of human capital resources; (3) to analyze alignment with evidence-based practices; and (4) to evaluate the effectiveness of communication strategies.

 

Recommendation: AIR and the auditor general will present outcomes as documented in the attachments and address the School Board members’ comments.
