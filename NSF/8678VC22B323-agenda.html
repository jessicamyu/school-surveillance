Other Staff Present: Thomas Jenkinson, chairman; Celeste Peterson, vice chairman;
          Diane Pruner, director, Career and Technical Education

Meeting Category: May 27, 2009 - Work Session No. 114
Subject: Career and Technical Education Advisory Committee Annual Report

School Board Action Required: Information

Related To: Student Achievement Goal 1.3

Key Points:
      Members of the CTEAC are interested in the following:
        · Integration of academic and CTE educational goals and objectives.
        · Improvements in all CTE program areas.
        · Additional outreach programs (marketing) for the promotion of CTE for students, parents, faculty and staff.
        · Explore current and future possibilities for public/private partnerships to present meaningful recommendations and implementation models.
        · Continue to research methods and approaches for including CTE students in the important fields of science, technology, engineering and mathematics.
        · Monitor metrics of CTE performance identified locally and by the VDOE and determine the budget impact of increasing performance on such measures.
Recommendation:

That the School Board accept the Career and Technical Education Advisory Committee annual report.
