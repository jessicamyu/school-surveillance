4. TECHNOLOGY: The Superintendent will use technology to support teaching, learning, and the Board’s operational expectations and goals. The Superintendent will:
      1. Provide a comprehensive and functional technology infrastructure.
      2. Provide technology capabilities that are useful for staff members and students.
