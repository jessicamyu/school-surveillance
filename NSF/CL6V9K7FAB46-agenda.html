Meeting Category: November 15, 2022 – School Board Work Session

 

School Board Action Required:  Information

Summary/Background:

In response to a request from the School Board, an external review of FCPS’ special education program was conducted by the American Institute for Research (AIR) which began December 2020 with the final report being presented at the October 4, 2022 School Board Work Session.

The purpose of today’s presentation is to share the proposed process for developing the three-year special education enhancement plan. As outlined in the presentation, the proposed process includes collaboration with internal and external stakeholders with opportunities for stakeholder feedback throughout the plan’s development.

 

Recommendation:  The administration requests that the School Board support the Special Education Enhancement Planning Process as presented.
