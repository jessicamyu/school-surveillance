Subject:                 Ad Hoc Community Committee on Student Rights and Responsibilities

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion                                      

 

Related To:                Student Rights and Responsibilities, Regulation 2601

 

Key Points:

 

At the June 7, 2012, regular School Board meeting, the Board unanimously approved the following motion: 

 

“That the School Board establish a special committee of the Board to perform a comprehensive review of the Fairfax County Public Schools’ Student Rights and Responsibilities (SR&R), and those manuals of other school districts, to recommend changes to the School Board by March 2013 that would simplify the language and substantially reduce the size of SR&R and ensure consistency with our community’s values, School Board policies, and Virginia laws.” 

 

At the September 20, 2012, regular School Board meeting, the Board unanimously approved the composition of the committee, with 40 members. The Chairman was elected by the School Board community appointees. The Deputy Superintendent and the Assistant Superintendent for Special Services served as non-voting advisors to the Committee.

 

Committee Recommendations:

 

The Ad Hoc Committee will make 52 recommendations to the School Board in the following areas:

  • SR&R Document
  • Students—Prevention, Intervention & Range of Responses
  • Students With Disabilities
  • Parent Involvement & Parent Rights
  • Discipline Process

The Chairman of the Ad Hoc Committee will present these recommendations to the School Board.
