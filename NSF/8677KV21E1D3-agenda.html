Staff Contact: Dean Tistadt, chief operating officer, Department of Facilities and Transportation Services

Meeting Type: October 19, 2009 - School Board Work Session No. 28

Subject: Facilities Planning Advisory Council

School Board Action Required: Decision

Key Points:
      On January 22, 2009, the School Board established the Comprehensive Plan Development Committee (CPDC) with a charge to design a recommendation for a comprehensive planning process for Board approval. The Committee began monthly meetings in March 2009 and a two-step process was presented to the School Board in September 2009. During the meeting the CPDC presented a detailed Step 2 of the process, known as the Ad Hoc Community Engagement Committee. Step 2 was developed to respond to the timely enrollment and capacity concerns outlined by staff in the southwestern region of the county. It was purposely developed in such a manner so that it could be modified and used for future studies when deemed appropriate within an overall comprehensive planning process.

      The CPDC indicated to the Board that the creation of an “umbrella planning group” was envisioned which could provide comprehensive planning oversight and guidance for the entire school district and which could also recommend the development of Ad Hoc Committees, when needed, for the School Board’s approval. The establishment of such an umbrella planning group is envisioned as Step 1 of this process.
      To that end, the CPDC presents the attached recommendation which outlines the formation of a district-wide planning group; the function and process for comprehensive school facilities planning; criteria and expectations for appointees; and work deliverables for the Board’s consideration.

Options:
      This is a new comprehensive planning initiative and process; therefore, the CPDC seeks School Board comments and guidance on the recommendation in order to continue moving forward.

Recommendation:
      That the School Board direct staff to initiate a charter for the establishment of a Facilities Planning Advisory Council as generally outlined in Attachments A and B.
