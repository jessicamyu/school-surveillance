Subject:                      Fairfax County School Board 2011 Draft Legislative Program

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion

 

Related To:                 Legislation

 

Key Points:

 

The School Board annually adopts a legislative program to inform public officials and others of its positions on legislative policy issues of importance to the Fairfax County Public Schools.  The program contains the School Board’s positions on state and federal issues of major and current significance.

 

The attached 2011 FCSB Draft Legislative Program includes additions, subtractions, and formatting changes. These changes are intended to reflect actions taken over the past year by state and federal government, to reflect the current policy climate, to incorporate staff recommendations, and to update, simplify and streamline existing position language.

 

A detailed list of recommended changes is included in the attached Summary of Staff Recommendations

 

Options:        

 

Modify as desired.

 

Recommendation:

 

That the School Board accept staff recommendations for the Fairfax County School Board draft 2011 Legislative Program.
