1. WS No. 121 Instruction and Facilities - 7 p.m.
Type
Discussion

Staff Contact:           Jeffrey Platenberg, assistant superintendent, Facilities and Transportation Services

 

Meeting Category:  June 4, 2015 – Work Session No. 121

 

Subject:                     Facilities Planning Advisory Council 2014-15 Annual Report to the School Board

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion

 

Background:

 

On September 23, 2010, the School Board approved the formation of a Facilities Planning Advisory Council (FPAC) with the mission to advise and inform the staff and School Board in the development of comprehensive, long term plans for facilities needs in the most effective and efficient way.  FPAC is made up of 13 appointed members – one from each Fairfax County magisterial district, one from the City of Fairfax, and three at-large members.

 

Key Points:

 

Consistent with the School Board guidelines for advisory committees, FPAC submits its annual report to the School Board, including an executive summary and recommendations, list of meeting dates, attendance reports, and voting records.
