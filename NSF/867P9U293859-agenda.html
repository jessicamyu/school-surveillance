RESOLUTION OF COMMENDATION
FOR
STEPHEN R. SCHOLLA

WHEREAS, Stephen Scholla, physics teacher at Oakton High School, is an exemplary teacher whose enthusiasm, creativity, and passion for teaching inspire all who are privileged to work with him; and

WHEREAS, he demonstrates a unique capacity to instill a love of physics and an awareness of its many facets in students of all academic abilities, and he takes difficult concepts, breaks those concepts into manageable lessons, reminds students what they already know, and helps students re-create experiments that demonstrate the concepts; and

WHEREAS, he maintains high expectations for himself and his students and consistently models intellectual curiosity both in the classroom and outside the classroom as he interacts with colleagues, staff members, students, and parents; and

WHEREAS, Mr. Scholla gives of his time selflessly and sponsors both the Rocketry Team and Crew Team, works with robotics, and serves as a liaison between the students and a NASA rocketry contract they were awarded; and

WHEREAS, parents express gratitude for the significant impact he has had on their children’s intellectual and moral development, and students appreciate Mr. Scholla’s humorous stories that actually connect to the lessons, his ability to understand the needs of each student, and his unending desire to “create an environment in which thought and intellect are rewarded and respected”;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the School Board of Fairfax County, Virginia, honors Stephen R. Scholla as the 2005 Fairfax County Public Schools Teacher of the Year.
