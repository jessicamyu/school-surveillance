RESOLUTION OF COMMENDATION
FOR
FRANCIS PAYNE



WHEREAS, Francis Payne, U.S. and Virginia History teacher and Advancement via Individual Determination (AVID) coordinator at Falls Church High School, is a respected and admired educator who empowers students in Fairfax County by helping them believe in themselves and find success by taking responsibility for their own learning; and

WHEREAS, he connects with students and understands their individual needs, which has a lasting impact on their lives, and he helps them build confidence and challenges them to set goals and envision a bright future; and

WHEREAS, Mr. Payne’s spirit and drive motivate his colleagues, and they laud his collegiality and dedication to the profession; and

WHEREAS, under his leadership, Falls Church High School’s AVID program has not only grown but has received the distinctive honor of being a national demonstration site, and Mr. Payne is congratulated on his membership on the AVID national training team; and

WHEREAS, Mr. Payne is commended for his selfless dedication to his students, his leadership within the school community, and his commitment to the mission of Falls Church High School;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED, that the School Board of Fairfax County, Virginia, honors Francis Payne as the 2009 Fairfax County Public Schools Teacher of the Year. The School Board expresses its appreciation to Mr. Payne for his many contributions to FCPS students and staff members and for his outstanding service to others and bestows on him its highest commendation for having achieved these distinctions.
