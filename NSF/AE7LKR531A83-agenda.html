Staff Contact:  Susan Quinn, Chief Operating Officer

                                    Michelle Pratt, coordinator, Contract and Procurement

 

Meeting Category:  October 6, 2016 – Work Session

 

Subject:  Process for Request for Proposal (RFP) Superintendent Search

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion

 

Key Points:

 

On September 19, 2016, Dr. Karen Garza announced her resignation as Division Superintendent to the School Board, effective on or before December 16, 2016.  The School Board will conduct a search for a new division superintendent during the 2016-2017 school year.

 

To facilitate the development of the Request for Proposal (RFP) for an independent search firm, the following documents have been provided:

  • RFP from the last Superintendent search
  • Sample weighted vendor score sheet
  • Proposed vendor selection timeline
  • SAC guidelines for evaluating proposals

Once approved by the Board, an RFP will be issued by the Office of Procurement Services.

 

Recommendation:

 

That the School Board authorize staff to issue a Request for Proposal related to the superintendent search.
