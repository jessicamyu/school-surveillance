Subject: FY 2019 Budget

 

School Board Action Required: Discussion

 

Key Points:

 

On January 11, 2018, the Superintendent presented the FY 2019 Proposed Budget that focuses on employee compensation. The FY 2019 Proposed Budget for the School Operating Fund totals $2.9 billion and is an increase of $118.7 million, or 4.3 percent, over the FY 2018 Approved Budget.

 

During the work session, questions regarding the FY 2019 Proposed Budget and potential amendments to the main motion for the advertised budget will be discussed. The draft motion states that the School Board adopt of FY 2019 Advertised Budget as detailed in the Superintendent’s Proposed Budget. 

 

 

The presentation from January 22, 2018, has been modified to include next steps regarding the FY19 teacher scale investment:

 

  • Slide 6 has been added to show total compensation for FY18.
  • Slide 7 has been modified to show the total compensation comparisons for FY18.
  • Slide 8 has been modified and slide 9 has been added to include the number of teachers per lane and step and their FY19 pay increases.
  • Slide 10 has been added to provide cost estimates for grandfathered and maximum salary employees, as well as the costs included for teacher salary investments in FY19.

 

Those slides have been marked with a header stating, “Updated for February 1, 2018, Work Session.”

 

The next steps regarding the BA lanes will be provided in the presentation for the March 12, 2018, work session regarding the BA lane options.

 

The FY 2019 Proposed Budget presentation and document are available at https://www.fcps.edu/about-fcps/budget/fy2019.
