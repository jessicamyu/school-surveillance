Staff Contact: Dean Tistadt, Chief Operating Officer, Department of Facilities and Transportation Services

SUBJECT: Adjustments to School Attendance Areas

RECOMMENDATION:
        1. That the School Board approve adjustments to school attendance areas involving South County Secondary School and Hayfield Secondary School, effective for the 2007-2008 school year.

        NOTE: Tonight's discussion will only deal with the recommendation involving South County and Hayfield Secondary Schools.

EXPLANATION:
      It should be noted that the recommendations include a plan to phase-in the changes over a period of years. In order to provide capacity relief more quickly, and in order to minimize the impact of phasing on transportation, the School Board may want to consider a phasing plan that is shorter in duration than that included in the current recommendation. Alternatively, the School Board might want to consider allowing students to remain at their existing schools as indicated in the recommended phasing plan but without school bus service.

      Attached is a narrative broadly describing the boundary adjustment recommendations with details provided in the Appendixes.
