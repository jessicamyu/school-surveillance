Audit Committee Meeting Agenda

January 24, 2013

 

  • Status Report.

This report provides a summary of projects completed by the Office of Internal Audit since the last report, dated November 19, 2012.  In addition, this report includes the status of ongoing projects, a summary of the assistance the office has provided to others, a hotline update, and the results of the latest customer satisfaction survey.  The report, in its entirety, will be provided at the meeting.

  • Athletic Fund & Ticket Management Audits
  • Herndon High School

The fund appeared to be in compliance with Virginia High School League (VHSL) policies, where applicable, and with related FCPS regulations and guidelines. There were no significant issues noted during the audit and minor issues, deemed not reportable, were discussed with appropriate personnel prior to report issuance.

  • Westfield High School (tentative, awaiting management response)

The fund appeared to be in compliance with Virginia High School League (VHSL) policies, where applicable, and with related FCPS regulations and guidelines. However, we noted items purchased for the school were shipped to employees’ home addresses.
