                                    Dede Bailer, coordinator, Psychology Services

 

Meeting Category:  February 16, 2017 - Work Session

 

Subject:  SCYPT Update

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion

 

Related To:  Strategic Plan: Caring Culture     

 

Key Points:

 

The Successful Children and Youth Policy Team (SCYPT) serves as the team guiding and coordinating efforts to ensure that children and youth are healthy and successful across Fairfax. It helps to identify priorities, provide strategic direction for action, marshal resources, and make recommendations on policy and resource allocation. SCYPT members include high-level leaders from the County Government, Fairfax County Public Schools, and the community.

 

In October 2016 the Fairfax County Board of Supervisors and the Fairfax County School Board asked SCYPT to present a work plan that would allow both Boards to address identified priorities related to children, youth, and families, beginning with the FY 2018 budget. Discussion on the following topics will provide an update on work by the SCYPT:

 

  • Response to the Joint Boards
  • Update on One Fairfax Policy Development
  • Recommendations for Funding Endorsed by the SCYPT
