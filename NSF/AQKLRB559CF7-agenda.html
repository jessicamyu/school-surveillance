Meeting Category:  November 2, 2017 – School Board Work Session  

 

Subject:  Strategic Plan Update

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion

 

Related To:            Strategic Plan Goals 1-4

 

Key Points:

 

The Board will review the 2016-17 Strategic Plan Update with estimated costs for actions in each goal area:

Goal 1 – Student Success

Goal 2 – Caring Culture

Goal 3 – Premier Workforce

Goal 4 – Resource Stewardship

 

Staff members will be available to answer questions about the estimated cost of actions in the strategic plan. At the conclusion of the question/answer period, the School Board will prioritize desired outcomes within each goal area of Ignite.
