[updated 5/28/14, added information on coversheet]

Staff Contact:               Phyllis Pajardo, assistant superintendent, Department of Human Resources

 

Meeting Category:       May 28, 2014 - WS No. 116 - Human Resources

 

Subject:                       Standard School Year Calendar

 

School Board Action Required: Decision

 

Related To:                  Legal Requirement

 

Key Points: 

 

Approve the modification in the makeup day schedule for the 2014-2015 Standard school Year Calendar, which provides FCPS more flexibility around emergency school closings.

 

2014-2015 Standard School Year Calendar Makeup day schedule: 

 

 

 

Days Missed                                       Makeup Days                                                     Notes

 

         4*                                             January 29                                                         Staff Development Day/Student Holiday

         5                                               February 16                                                       Washington's Birthday and Presidents' Day

         6                                               No makeup required                                          

         7                                               April 17                                                             Teacher Workday

         8                                               No makeup required                            

         9**                                           June 22                                                             Teacher Workday (extends last day of school)

        10                                              No makeup required                                          

        11                                              March 30                                                           Spring Break (first day)

        12                                              No makeup required                                          

        13                                              March 31                                                           Spring Break (second day)

        14                                              No makeup required    

 

*January 29 will become a (banked)makeup day if 2+school days are missed.

 

**Make up days 9+ may extend the last day of school or add time to day. Other makeup days may include March 30 & 31 during Spring Break.

 

 

 

Recommendation: 

 

That the Board (1) approve the information in the key points regarding the 2014-2015 Standard School Year Calendar modified makeup day schedule.
