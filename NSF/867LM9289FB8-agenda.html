PRO’S and CON’S of 2006 SOUTH COUNTY BOUNDARY STUDY vs. 2007 STUDY

PRO’S:

· Avoids extending the flex schedule at South County one additional year.

· Less likely that capital improvements will be needed to accommodate excess enrollment.

· Reduces time community has to become “attached” to their school assignment.

· Avoids prolonged angst in the community regarding a boundary study.



CON’S:

· Does not meet the desired three-year “moratorium” on boundary changes identified in School Board Policy 8130.5.

· Depending on the phasing plan adopted could move some students twice during their secondary school years.
