RESOLUTION OF COMMENDATION
FOR
BRIGITTE LAVEY


WHEREAS, Brigitte Lavey, AP World History, Geography, and World Studies 2 social studies teacher at Langley High School, has diligently served Fairfax County Public Schools for 35 years by doing whatever it takes to reach all students―by engaging their minds and encouraging them to think; and

WHEREAS, she is a respected, admired, and passionate educator who supports students and fosters their self-esteem by setting high standards and instilling in them a desire to learn; and

WHEREAS, Mrs. Lavey has mentored many new teachers and is always willing to give guidance and advice to colleagues when asked, is a role model for teachers and students in the school and citizens in the Langley community, and is a devoted professional who motivates others with her energy, expertise, and enthusiasm for teaching and learning; and

WHEREAS, she acts as a change agent by influencing the development of Langley’s interdisciplinary humanities academic program; and

WHEREAS, Mrs. Lavey is commended for her dedication to her students, her leadership within the school community, and her commitment to the mission of Langley High School and to that of Fairfax County Public Schools;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the School Board of Fairfax County, Virginia, honors Brigitte Lavey as the 2010 Fairfax County Public Schools Teacher of the Year.
