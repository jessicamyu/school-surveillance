Staff Contact: Jeffrey K. Platenberg, assistant superintendent, Facilities and Transportation Services

 

 

Other Staff Present:  Kevin Sneed, special projects administrator, Capital Improvement & Planning

                                  Jessica Gillis, director, Facilities Services Planning

                                  Tracy Strunk, director, Zoning Evaluation Division, Fairfax County Department of Planning and Zoning

                                  Marianne Gardner, director, Planning Division, Fairfax County Department of Planning and Zoning

 

 

Meeting Category: November 5, 2018 – Work Session

 

 

School Board Action Required: Discussion

 

 

Ignite Link: Goal 4: Resource Stewardship https://www.fcps.edu/node/32800

 

 

Outcome:

 

Both Fairfax County staff and Fairfax County Public Schools (FCPS) staff discuss changes to planned land use and development which may affect our facilities throughout the entire development review process. Staff from both the County and FCPS will discuss developments within the County to ensure  that FCPS is well positioned to accommodate student growth.

 

 

Summary/Background:

 

A number of new development projects have been proposed as part of planning initiatives across Fairfax County, some of which may impact enrollment within Fairfax County Public Schools. It is important to note that several of these projects will take many years until the development is actually completed.
