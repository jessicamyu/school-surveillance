Subject: Fairfax County School Board 2016 Draft Legislative Program

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion

 

Related To:  Legislation 

 

Key Points:

The School Board annually adopts a legislative program to inform public officials and others of its positions on legislative policy issues of importance to the Fairfax County Public Schools.  The program contains the School Board’s positions on state and federal issues of major and current significance.

 

The attached 2016 Fairfax County School Board Draft Legislative Program includes additions, subtractions and formatting changes. These changes are intended to reflect actions taken over the past year by state and federal government, to reflect the current policy climate, to incorporate staff recommendations, and to update, simplify and streamline existing position language.

 

Options:

Recommendation:

That the School Board accept staff recommendations for the Fairfax County School Board draft 2016 Legislative Program.

Subject: 2016-2017 School Calendar

 

School Board Action Required:  INFORMATION

 

Key Points:

In developing the Standard School Calendar 2016-2017, staff sought feedback from various stakeholder groups, including the Superintendent’s Teacher Advisory Council, the teacher associations, Fairfax County Council of PTAs, the principal associations, and the support employee associations.  The agreed upon calendar includes an 11-Day Winter Break and Friday, June 23, 2016, as the last day of school for students.  In addition, we are posting a calendar that highlights what a Pre-Labor Day start would look like in the event of FCPS qualifying for a Pre-Labor Day start date from the Virginia Department of Education (VDOE).
