    Staff Contact: Susan Quinn, Chief Financial Officer

    Meeting Type: October 31, 2008 - Work Session No. 39

    SUBJECT: Program Review

    TYPE: Discussion

    BACKGROUND:
        The FY 2009 Midyear Budget will be presented with an update to the fiscal forecast for FY 2010. The revised forecast provides a reduction target for the development of the FY 2010 budget. Since membership growth accounts for unavoidable increases in FCPS’ budget, a summary of enrollment trends will be discussed. To address the projected deficit, the Superintendent and the Leadership Team have reviewed more than 175 individual programs and developed two tiers of preliminary reduction options. Additional information, including County reductions presented to the Board of Supervisors that will impact FCPS, current projects, transportation considerations, recent program growth, and other School Board recommendations will be discussed.

        Employee and community feedback will be discussed at the work session on November 10, 2008.


    AGENDA ITEM CONTENT:

    1. FY 2009 Midyear Budget Review
    2. Revised Fiscal Forecast
    3. Enrollment Trends
    4. Leadership Team Program Review Overview
    5. County Lines of Business Review Reductions that Impact FCPS
