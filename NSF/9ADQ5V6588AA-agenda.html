Subject:                        FY 2015 Budget

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion                                             

 

Key Points:

 

In order to begin the FY 2015 budget planning process, the fiscal forecast for the School Operating Fund was presented to the School Board at the September 23, 2013, work session.  The forecast included five years of projections for all key financial areas that drive the budget development process, such as revenue assumptions and expenditure assumptions, including employee salaries and benefits, student membership, and other identified program needs. 

 

Based on projections for revenue and required expenditures, FCPS is forecasting a significant deficit for FY 2015.  Prior to any County transfer increase or employee salary increase, FCPS faces a shortfall of $140.7 million.  Without additional funding from the State or County in FY 2015, FCPS will need to reduce programs and/or services to eliminate the projected shortfall while making every effort to minimize the impact of budget reductions on instruction.

 

The following items will be reviewed prior to discussing potential reductions to balance the FY 2015 budget:

  • an overview of the County transfer over time
  • a comparison of the projected deficit for the upcoming year as presented in the fiscal forecast in September for fiscal years 2014 and 2015
  • the amount of reductions needed based on potential requested increases in the County transfer
  • how to read the FY 2014 Program Budget which is available at http://www.fcps.edu/fs/budget/documents/approved/FY14/ProgramBudgetFY2014.pdf
