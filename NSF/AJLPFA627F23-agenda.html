School Board Action Required:  Guidance for Reduction Planning                   

 

Key Points:

 

On February 9, 2017, the School Board adopted the Advertised Budget, and the FY 2018 budget documents are available at www.fcps.edu/budget.

 

This work session will continue the potential reductions and fees discussion that began at the January 23, 2017, work session and continued through the February 13, 2017, work session where the Superintendent presented a list of potential reductions and fees for the School Board’s consideration.

 

In January, the School Board provided guidance by indicating if planning should begin for potential reductions and fees to ensure the least disruptive implementation for next school year. At this work session, the Board will be asked to provide guidance on the items on the Superintendent’s list that have a decision date of February, and the critical dates were added to the document.
