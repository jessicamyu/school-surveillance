Agenda Item: Fairfax County School Board 2007 Draft Legislative Program

Staff Contact: Michael Molloy, Director, Government Relations

Background: The School Board annually adopts a legislative program to inform public officials and others of its positions on legislative policy issues of importance to the Fairfax County Public Schools.

The attached draft 2007 Legislative Program is based on previous year’s programs as well as proposals and changes submitted this year. The program contains the School Board’s positions on state and federal issues of major and current significance.

Key Issues: The draft program has been updated based on state and federal actions over the past year. Three positions—“Distribution of State Elementary and Secondary Education Funding,” “Educational Technology Funding: Innovative Approaches,” and “Student Substance Abuse”—have been deleted, with the bulk of their contents shifting to other positions. “Teachers and Instructional Personnel” has been expanded and moved into the first section. “Post-Retirement Employment Options” has been renamed “Retirement/VRS” and expanded. Wording changes, clarifications, and data updates have been made throughout the draft. A complete chart detailing recommended changes is attached.

School Board Discussion/Decision: Review proposed additions/deletions/changes to the School Board’s legislative positions.

Staff Recommendation: That the Legislation, Policy & Future Issues Committee accept the attached recommendations, which will then be forwarded to the entire School Board for consideration.

Options: Modify as desired.
