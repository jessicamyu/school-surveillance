School Board Action Required:  Discussion                                                         

 

Key Points:

 

On February 9, 2012, the School Board adopted the FY 2013 Advertised Budget.  The state budget was not adopted prior to the Board of Supervisors Budget Work Session with the School Board.  As a result, FCPS included the impact of the state budget within a response to FY 2013 Budget Question # 56 (http://www.fcps.edu/fs/budget/questions.shtml).  On April 24, 2012, the County Board of Supervisors included in the markup of their FY 2013 Adopted Budget an increase in the transfer to the School Operating Fund of 4.5 percent above the FY 2012 approved budget. 

 

Based on the available revenue for FY 2013, and in response to the state adopted mandate regarding employee contributions to the Virginia Retirement System, the following items will be presented and discussed:

  1. Virginia Retirement System
    1. Options for Implementing Required Changes
    2. Impact to FCPS Employees
  2. Framework for Budget Recommendations - School Board Priorities
  3. Recommendations to Balance the Budget for FY 2013
  4. Looking to the Future
    1. Unfunded Needs
    2. Challenges Ahead
