Meeting Category:  June 23, 2020 – Work Session

 

School Board Action Required: Information                         

 

Key Points:

 

The June 15, 2020 School Board work session provided draft options for reopening of schools under the following scenarios-- virtual start, return with social distancing, or reopen with some students and staff unable to return.

 

Today’s presentation will provide the Board with responses to information requested from the June 15 work session, timeline for key decision points, budget needs, and a recommendation for reopening of schools to ensure safe and flexible learning and working environments for students and staff.

 

 

Recommendation:                           

That the School Board give consensus for the Superintendent to move forward with the reopening of schools plan.
