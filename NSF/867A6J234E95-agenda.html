Staff Contact: Dean Tistadt, chief operating officer, Facilities and Transportation Services

SUBJECT: Proposed 2009-2010 Program/Attendance Area Adjustment

TYPE: Discussion

BACKGROUND:

In September 2008, the School Board authorized and directed staff to undertake two boundary studies: 1) Adjustments to school attendance areas to establish school boundaries for a new elementary school at the Coppermine site which includes Floris, Herndon, Hutchison, McNair and Oak Hill Elementary Schools and, 2) Adjustments to school attendance areas to establish school boundaries for a new elementary school at the Laurel Hill site which includes Gunston, Halley, Lorton Station, Newington Forest and Silverbrook Elementary Schools.

On January 8, 2009, staff presented boundary recommendations to the School Board for both study areas. On the January 12, 2009, work session with the Board, staff summarized the basis and rationale for each recommendation, focusing on the purpose of the studies – to establish attendance areas for new elementary schools at the Coppermine and Laurel Hill sites, both of which are under construction and are scheduled to open in fall 2009 and relieve overcrowding.

KEY POINTS:

Staff is responding to questions and concerns which were raised at the work session regarding the Coppermine boundary study. (see Attachment 1)
