Staff Contact:  Marty K. Smith, Chief of Staff

 

Meeting Category:  October 19, 2017 – School Board Retreat  

 

Subject:  Strategic Plan Update

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion

 

Related To:            Strategic Plan Goals 1-4

 

Key Points:

 

2016-17 Strategic Plan Update with Implementation Status of Actions:

Goal 1 – Student Success

Goal 2 – Caring Culture

Goal 3 – Premier Workforce

Goal 4 – Resource Stewardship

 

Staff members will be available to answer questions about the implementation of the 2016-2017 strategic plan. At the end of the question/answer period, staff will outline the process the School Board will use to prioritize the strategic plan for the FY19 budget cycle.  On November 2, the School Board will work through a process to prioritize the Strategic Plan: Ignite. The related budget information will be made available prior to the November 2 work session.
