Audit Committee Meeting Agenda

Monday, November 19, 2012, 9:30 a.m.

Gatehouse Administration Building, Room 1600

 

  • Status Report - 9:30 a.m.  

This report provides a summary of projects completed by the Office of Internal Audit since the last report, dated July 19, 2012.  In addition, this report includes the status of ongoing projects, a summary of the assistance the office has provided to others, a hotline update, and the results of the latest customer satisfaction survey.  The report, in its entirety, will be provided at the meeting.

 

  • State Efficiency Review Overview – 10:00 a.m.

The Audit Committee will receive an overview of the State Efficiency Review program and process.  John Ringer, Associate Director, and Cari Corr, Management & Evaluation Analyst, from the Commonwealth of Virginia, Department of Planning and Budget, Best Management Practices Division, will be in attendance.
