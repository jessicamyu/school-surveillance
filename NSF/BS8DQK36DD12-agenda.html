Meeting Category: August 18, 2020 – Work Session

 

School Board Action Required: Information

 

Key Points:

 

The June 15 and June 23, 2020 School Board work sessions provided draft options for reopening of schools under the following scenarios-- virtual start, return with social distancing, or reopen with some students and staff unable to return.

 

The July 13 work session provided the Board with a roundtable opportunity to discuss the hybrid and in-person Return to School (RTS) options and ask questions of staff.

 

The July 21 work session provided the Board with updates related to health conditions, the impact of those conditions on operations, technology readiness, telework, and the use of face coverings. 

 

Today’s presentation will provide an update on preparation for the September 8th virtual start, focusing on technology work (e.g. computer purchases, readiness of infrastructure, laptop distribution, etc.), teacher professional development, and parent/student technology support.  We will also review the 17 recommendations from the Technology Advisory Council and provide our responses. In addition, we will present Special Education updates.  Finally, we will share a draft of the metrics and a phase in plan for in-person learning that is still under development.
