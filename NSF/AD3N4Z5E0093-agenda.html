Subject:  Facilities Planning Advisory Council SY2016-17 Annual Report to the School Board

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion

 

Background:

 

On September 23, 2010, the School Board approved the formation of a Facilities Planning Advisory Council (FPAC) with the mission to advise and inform the staff and School Board in the development of comprehensive, long term plans for facilities needs in the most effective and efficient way.  FPAC is made up of 13 appointed members – one from each Fairfax County magisterial district, one from the City of Fairfax, and three at-large members.  

 

Key Points:

 

Consistent with the School Board guidelines for advisory committees, FPAC submits its annual report to the School Board, including an executive summary and recommendations, list of meeting dates, attendance reports, and voting records.

Subject:  Principal Selection Process

 

School Board Action Required:  Information

 

Key Points:

 

The principal selection process is being revised to incorporate a candidate pool approach for the beginning of the process. These enhancements to the process will increase and diversify the candidate pool, while providing individual schools greater access to candidates from across FCPS (and candidates external to the organization). The revised process is the first stage in the development of a systematic leadership pipeline that will be coupled with more robust and personalized leadership development for future building and system leaders. The information is provided for discussion with the Board; no Board action is required.
