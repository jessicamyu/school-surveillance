May 28, 2015 – Work Session No. 119

 

 

Subject:

Human Relations Advisory Committee (HRAC) Annual Report

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion

 

Related To:                 Legal Requirement

 

Key Points: 

 

The School Board charged “The HRAC to work with the Office of Equity and Employee Relations in providing evaluation of and feedback of FCPS efforts related to peer-to-peer behaviors including bullying, harassment, and sexual misconduct.  The committee’s areas of focus will be on definitions, applications, reporting, training, monitoring, addressing the areas of concern, and education." 

 

In response to the School Board’s charge, the committee focused on three areas in regard to FCPS efforts related to preventing adverse peer-to peer behaviors to include bullying, harassment, and sexual misconduct: 1) Reporting and monitoring of incidents; 2) Training and education of students, staff and administrators; and 3) Application which was focusing on review of the current FCPS peer-to-peer behavior evaluation system.

Subject:

School Health Advisory Committee (SHAC) Annual Report

 

School Board Action Required: Discussion

 

Related To:                 Policy 1710, Citizen Advisory Committees to the School Board

 

Key Point:

 

SHAC Charge 2014-2015: SHAC will use the Coordinated School Health model to complete the FCPS wellness policy regulations that were presented to the School Board in the 2013-2014 final report; and continue to act as a point of convergence for community input regarding the FCPS wellness policy and regulations.  Wellness policy regulations to be completed will address matters of family and community involvement; food and nutrition; health promotion for staff; healthy and safe school environments; and - as a priority - mental health.
