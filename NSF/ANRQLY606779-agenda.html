Staff Contact:  Kristen Michael, assistant superintendent, Department of Financial Services

 

Other Staff Present: Alice Wigington, director, Office of Budget Services

 

Meeting Category:  December 11, 2017 – Wok Session

 

Subject:  FY 2018 Midyear Budget Review   

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion

 

Key Points: 

 

In the School Operating Fund, funds available are increasing by $5.5 million. The FY 2018 beginning balance is increasing by $3.8 million due to final year-end adjustments made after the FY 2017 Final Budget Review. The adjustments include an increase of $0.9 million in sales tax revenue based on actual receipts; an increase of $3.1 million due to insurance proceeds related to 2016 winter storms; an increase of $0.1 million for state revenue; offset by an expenditure increase of $0.4 million. In addition to the beginning balance adjustment, other FY 2018 revenue adjustments include an increase of $0.2 million for City of Fairfax tuition based on actual expenditures for FY 2017; and a net increase of $1.5 million in federal grant revenue. 

 

Recommended FY 2018 expenditure adjustments reflect a net increase of $5.5 million resulting primarily from a set aside of $4.0 million to support FCPSOn and $1.5 million in federal grant expenditures; an increase of $0.3 million for a masters cohort tuition sharing and a tuition reimbursement program; and a placeholder of $1.1 million to address teacher salaries on the BA lanes. These increases are offset by the elimination of the assistant superintendent position and the chief of staff position resulting in savings of $0.4 million; and additional savings of $1.0 million is identified from the set-aside for the public safety radio system. 

 

Expenditure adjustments offset revenue adjustments, resulting in no change to the set aside of the FY 2019 beginning balance of $24.2 million. 

 

Changes to other School Board funds and an enrollment update for FY 2018 are detailed in the attachment. Also attached is an update on the status of grants in FY 2017 and FY 2018.

 

 

Recommendation: 

 

That the School Board approve revenue and expenditure changes reflected in the FY 2018 Midyear Budget Review as detailed in the agenda item.

Meeting Category: December 11, 2017 - Work Session

 

Subject: Implementation Plan, Justice High School

 

School Board Action Required: Decision

 

Key Points:

 

The Fairfax County School Board voted to change the name of J.E.B. Stuart High School to Justice High School.  The Superintendent was directed to provide the Board with a name change implementation plan by December 14, 2017.  The Superintendent charged staff with producing an implementation plan that:           

a. Captures costs for required items to be changed or replaced in an efficient and equitable manner,

b. Identifies systemic implications of a name change, and                                                                                                

c. Includes consideration for how information can be effectively communicated and shared with students, staff, and the school community.

The implementation plan recommends the name change taking effect in July of 2018.  Timelines and any associated costs for materials, equipment, systemics and infrastructure, communication, and student and community involvement have been established.

 

Recommendation:

 

That the School Board authorize the Superintendent to access funds from the flexibility reserve for the implementation plan for Justice High School as detailed in the agenda item.
