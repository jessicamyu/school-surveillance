Staff Contact: Michael Molloy, Director, Government Relations

SUBJECT: Legislative Recommendations for the 2006 Policies and Resolutions of the Virginia School Boards Association (VSBA)

RECOMMENDATION:
      That the School Board approve the attached recommendations for presentation to the VSBA Legislative, Policies, and Resolutions Committee, as reviewed and discussed by the Legislation, Policy and Future Issues Committee.

EXPLANATION:
      Each spring the VSBA Legislative, Policies, and Resolutions Committee seeks recommendations from local school boards concerning new or revised legislative policies and resolutions to be considered for VSBA approval. The Committee reviews the local school boards' recommendations along with other suggestions for changes to the existing policies and resolutions. The VSBA takes action on the Committee's report at the annual fall convention.
      This process allows the School Board to make recommendations for the VSBA legislative program and to receive statewide support for some of its own legislative positions. The Fairfax County School Board 2006 Legislative Program will be developed for adoption in the fall.
