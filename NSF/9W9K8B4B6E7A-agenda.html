Staff Contact:        Kristen Michael, assistant superintendent, Department of Financial Services

 

Meeting Category:  May 14, 2015 – Work Session No. 115

 

Subject:                  FY 2016 Approved Budget       

 

School Board Action Required:   Discussion                                                   

 

Key Points:

 

On February 5, 2015, the School Board adopted the FY 2016 Advertised Budget. The advertised budget was presented to and discussed by the School Board and County Board of Supervisors at a joint budget work session on February 27, 2015.

 

On April 28, 2015, the County Board of Supervisors adopted their FY 2016 budget that included an increase in the transfer to the School Operating Fund of 3.2 percent above the FY 2015 Approved Budget. The increase of $56.7 million is $14.0 million less than requested in FCPS’ advertised budget. The Virginia General Assembly adopted its 2014-2016 budget, as amended on April 15, 2015, which provides an increase of $3.9 million in revenue and a decrease in expenditures of $6.0 million as compared to the estimate included in FCPS’ FY 2016 Advertised Budget.

 

Revenue:

In total, revenue in the School Operating Fund reflects a net reduction of $10.1 million as compared to the FY 2016 Advertised Budget.  Funding from the county is $14.0 million less than requested, and this reduction is offset by a projected increase in state revenue of $3.9 million.

 

Expenditures:

In total, expenditures in the School Operating Fund reflect a net reduction of $10.1 million as compared to the FY 2016 Advertised Budget. This includes $6.0 million in savings due to a decrease in the employer contribution rate for the Virginia Retirement System (VRS) from 14.50 percent to the rate of 14.06 percent for FY 2016 and a reduction placeholder of $7.6 million. These reductions are offset by an increase of $3.5 million for the cost of enrollment growth and changes in student demographics and $0.1 million for School Board Member salary increases. In order to balance the budget, the School Board will need to identify reductions of $7.6 million prior to the budget being adopted on May 21, 2015. To meet the required level of expenditure reductions, the Superintendent has recommended a reduction totaling $7.6 million, which is detailed on page three of the agenda item.

 

Changes to other School Board funds are detailed in the attachment.

 

Recommendation:

 

That the School Board adopt the FY 2016 Approved Budget, as detailed in the FY 2016 Advertised Budget, modified by the adjustments included in the agenda item.
