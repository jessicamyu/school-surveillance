Staff Contact: Dean Tistadt, chief operating officer, Facilities and Transportation Services

Other Staff Present: Denise James, director, Office of Facilities Planning

Meeting Category: September 14, 2009 – Work Session No. 15
Subject: Southwestern County Community Engagement Process and Ad Hoc Planning Studies, Process Guidelines for School Board Members

School Board Action Required: Decision

Key Points:
      Feasibility studies have been conducted by Facilities and Transportation Services on the Clifton Elementary School renovation and on overcrowding in several elementary schools in the southwestern portion of the county

      The scope and complexity of the issues suggest that a community based comprehensive planning study should be undertaken to address the renovation challenges at Clifton Elementary School and the capacity deficit challenges in this area of the county. The study process is designed to accomplish three primary objectives through community engagement:1) to review and validate/determine the issues; 2) to determine the most complete list of the possible solutions to the issue(s) identified; and 3) to document all advantages and disadvantages of each potential solution.

      The School Board’s Comprehensive Plan Development Committee (CPDC) drafted the community engagement process. As it is likely that the proposed process would begin in fall 2009 with a goal to conclude in spring 2010, it is important that the School Board give direction to staff at the conclusion of the September work session.

      To support the work of this proposed Southwestern County Ad Hoc Planning study, Process Guidelines for School Board Members have also been developed for review and discussion.
Options:
      Since we have never conducted such a process before, the School Board is asked to provide its thoughts about the proposed process at the September 14, 2009, work session so that the guidance can be given to staff in order to allow the process to begin immediately.

Recommendation:
      That the School Board direct staff to initiate the Southwestern County Community Engagement Process for Clifton Elementary School renovation and overcrowded schools as presented by the School Board’s Comprehensive Planning Development Committee.
