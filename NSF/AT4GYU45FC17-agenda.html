Subject: Strategic Plan – Prioritize Outcomes

 

School Board Action Required: Discussion

 

Key Points:

 

The Board will review the School Board’s and Leadership Teams’ collated list of priorities in each goal area, toward the aim of reaching a consensus with the Superintendent about the strategic priorities for FY 2018 and FY 2019:

 

Goal 1 – Student Success

Goal 2 – Caring Culture

Goal 3 – Premier Workforce

Goal 4 – Resource Stewardship 

 

The School Board will discuss potential metrics and goal statements staff propose to report on to the School Board regarding progress on the Strategic Plan. Staff members will be available to answer questions about the potential metrics and goal statements. At the conclusion of the question/answer period, the School Board will accept or request changes to the metrics and goal statements within each goal area of Ignite.
