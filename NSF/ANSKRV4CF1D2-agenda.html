Subject:  Governance Committee Proposed Revisions to Policy 8335 - Wireless Telecommunication Facilities

 

Meeting Category:  May 14, 2018 - Work Session

 

Problem Statement: At the Board's December 15, 2016 forum, Ms. Hynes and Mr. Wilson requested School Board review of Policy 8335, which was inherited by the current Board, to ensure the policy reflects the wishes of the Board and takes into account any new relevant factors or technological advancements that may have been made in the almost ten years since the policy's adoption.

 

Summary/Background (Key Points):

 

At the December 15, 2016 forum, the Board requested that the Governance Committee review Policy 8335 - Wireless Telecommunication Facilities and make a recommendation to the Board.  The Governance Committee discussed the policy and regulation during several committee meetings attended by interested community members. 

 

The Committee recommends revisions to the policy to provide earlier involvement and increased input from the community and School Board and to ensure that any proposed changes are in compliance with applicable Federal, State and local laws and regulations, as detailed in the agenda item.  The revisions recommend adding language to reserve the right of the School Board to review and approve/disapprove  any application for review prior to any government approval, and ensure FCPS sites only be considered after the magisterial district(s) and at-large School Board member(s) has been informed about the site application and briefed on placement.

 

Recommendation:

 

 

That the School Board approve proposed revisions to Policy 8335 - Wireless Telecommunication Facilities at a future meeting.
