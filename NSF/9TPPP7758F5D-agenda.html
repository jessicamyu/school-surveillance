Fairfax County School Board

Retreat

 

Gatehouse Administration Center

Gatehouse Cafe

8115 Gatehouse Road

Falls Church, VA

Friday and Saturday, February 20-21, 2015

 

 

AGENDA

 

Friday, February 20, 2015

 

3-5:30 p.m.      Discussion of the Strategic Plan:  Goals, Overarching Strategies, Desired Outcomes, and Products, Processes and Initiatives.  Each Goal champion will present a brief overview of the Goal area and thinking behind the proposed action areas.  The purpose of the following discussion is for the Board to affirm that the Goal area is moving in the right direction and/or provide input on what might need to change.

 

            3-3:30 p.m.                  Review of planning to date

3:30-4 p.m.                  Goal One:  Student Success

4-4:30 p.m.                  Goal Two:  Caring Culture

4:30-5 p.m.                  Goal Three:  Premier Workforce

5-5:30 p.m.                  Goal Four:  Resource Stewardship

 

CRITICAL QUESTION:  Are the overarching strategies, desired outcomes, and actions moving in the right direction to accomplish what the Board envisions around the four Goal areas?

 

5:30-5:45 p.m. – Break

 

5:45-7 p.m.      Share the Strategic Plan survey results

 

Saturday, February 21, 2015

 

8 a.m. – Coffee

 

8:30-10:20 a.m.           Discussion of measures associated with goal areas:  The purpose of the discussion is to identify the most critical measurements for each Goal area that Board members feel are essential in monitoring the implementation of the Strategic Plan.

 

            8:30-9 a.m.                  Questions about the Strategic Plan survey results

9-9:20 a.m.                  Student Success

9:20–9:40 a.m.             Caring Culture

9:40-10 a.m.                Premier Workforce

10-10:20 a.m.              Resource Stewardship

 

10:20–10:30 a.m. – Break

 

10:30-11:30 a.m.                     Discussion and development of a reporting structure and schedule for monitoring the progress of the Strategic Plan

 

11:30 a.m.–noon          Next steps in the strategic planning process

 

Noon – Lunch

 

Adjournment time will be dependent on the weather conditions at lunchtime.
