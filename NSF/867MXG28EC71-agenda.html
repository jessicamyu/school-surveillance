Agenda Item: Teacher Leadership Initiative

Staff Contact: Jack Dale

Background

In April 2005 a team from FCPS met in Chicago with teams from 8 other school systems across the nation to begin a discussion and to research best practices and strategies for developing teacher leaders with the ultimate goal of improving student achievement. Since that time, a “Teacher Leadership Task Force” with representatives from the School Board, teacher and principal associations, and other key stakeholders have been meeting to develop a model for FCPS. Over the past few days, that model has been presented to all principal groups as well as the Superintendent’s Teacher Advisory Council. An implementation model has been designed based on suggestions and feedback received from those meetings.

Key Issues

· Determine the right model and future direction for FCPS
· Determine appropriate multi-year phase-in
· Obtain necessary resources to fund teacher leadership model
    School Board Discussion/Decision

    Information and discussion only.

    Staff Recommendation

    Continue to refine teacher leadership model and include in budget recommendations for FY 07 and beyond.

    Options

    None
