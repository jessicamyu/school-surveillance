Staff Contact:            Karen K. Garza, Division Superintendent

 

Other Staff Present:  Marty K. Smith, Chief of Staff

 

Meeting Category:     November 13, 2014 - Work Session No. 47

 

Subject:                     Refinement of the Strategic Plan

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion

 

Key Points:

 

The Strategic Plan represents the cooperative work of the School Board and Leadership Team to create a long term strategic plan for Fairfax County Public Schools. The School Board approved the four goal areas at the September 18, 2014, Board meeting.

 

The School Board provided input on desired outcomes, objectives and sample strategies during the October 6, 2014, work session.  Staff acted upon those recommendations to create a draft Strategic Plan for School Board review.

 

Recommendation:

 

That the School Board provide feedback on the direction of the Strategic Plan.
