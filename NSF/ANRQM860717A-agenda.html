Subject: Impact of Mobility on Individual Student Achievement and on School Accreditation Measures

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion

 

Related To:  Strategic Plan Goal 1: Student Success

 

Key Points: 

  • Student mobility is a complicating factor that can impact students' academic and social development. Further, schools with high mobility face additional challenges in meeting state accreditation outcomes.
  • Mobility is a locally monitored metric and is not reported to the Virginia Department of Education. As such, we are unable to compare our mobility rates to those in other divisions.
  • FCPS mobility calculations include all withdrawals and enrollments, including those from extended absence or dropouts.
  • In general, our highest mobility schools have remained consistently so over a number of years. Further, we see a correlation between mobility rates and economic disadvantage.
  • Schools have in place specific strategies and actions to help mitigate the challenges of mobility for individual students. Central offices offer resources to support this work.
  • With the help of central offices, schools have in place specific strategies and approaches to help staff develop the skills they need to support transient students.

Subject: Strategic Plan Goal 3: Premier Workforce

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion

 

Related To:  Strategic Plan Goal 3: Premier Workforce

 

Key Points: 

This year’s Goal 3 Premier Workforce report discusses major Strategic Plan activities taking place during the current year and reflects data that has been utilized in making the decisions driving the work underlining Goal 3. In addition, this presentation and report will provide an update on the monitoring metrics and current work that support Goal 3, Premier Workforce. A report on the work accomplished for the implementation of the class size regulation will also be presented.

 

The following are the two overarching strategies and the desired outcomes for Goal 3, Premier Workforce:

 

Overarching Strategy 8

Recruit and retain high quality employees who are valued, respected, and rewarded for their efforts

 

Desired Outcomes

  • Recruit, hire, and, retain employees to maintain a premier workforce
  • Achieve and preserve a culture of equity and fairness for all FCPS employees
  • Employees will benefit from competitive compensation packages

 

Overarching Strategy 9

Invest in growth and leadership opportunities for all employees by providing meaningful professional development and quality feedback on performance

 

Desired Outcomes

  • Dynamic professional development opportunities will be available for all employees
  • FCPS will develop and maintain a performance evaluation process that is meaningful to all employees

 

Recommendation:  That the School Board approve the Strategic Plan Goal 3: Premier Workforce report as detailed in the agenda item.
