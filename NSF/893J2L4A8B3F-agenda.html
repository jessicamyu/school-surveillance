Meeting Category:      September 13, 2010 - Work Session No. 18

 

Subject:                      Instructional Program and Treatment of Students Operational Expectations Monitoring Report – Updated Operational Expectations 4 and 5

 

School Board Action Required:  Decision                            

 

Related To:                Operational Expectations: Instructional Program and Treatment of Students

 

Key Points:

 

At the May 10, 2010, work session, the Instructional Program and Treatment of Students Operational Expectations Monitoring Report was approved with the exception of operational expectations 4 and 5.  The Board deferred operational expectations 4 and 5  until the September 2010 work session because data was not yet available for the School Climate Survey and the Fairfax County Youth Survey. The updated operational expectations 4 and 5 prove evidence that all school staff, parents, and community members constantly work toward improving the school environment, culture, and conditions so that students’ learning is improved.

 

School Board Discussions/Decisions:

 

  1. Does the Board need any clarification on the information in the updated report?
  2. Does the report meet the Board’s need to determine progress toward meeting operational expectations 4 and 5?
  3. Does the report fulfill the need to report to the public the progress toward meeting operational expectations 4 and 5?

 

Recommendation:

 

That the School Board accept the updated operational expectations 4 and 5, as part of the previously approved Instructional Program and Treatment of Students Operational Expectations Monitoring Report.
