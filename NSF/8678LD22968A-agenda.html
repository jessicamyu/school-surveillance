124. Governance - 8:30 a.m.

Information
Staff Contact: Kevin North, Assistant Superintendent, Human Resources

Meeting Type: June 22, 2009 – Work Session No. 124

Subject: Revised Reasonable Interpretation and Indicators for Human Resources

School Board Action Required: Decision

Related to: Human Resources Operational Expectations

Key Points: In February 2009, the Governance Committee requested Human Resources to:

Add a specific question to the Working Conditions Survey for current indicator A.3.b.

Status will be provided.

Revise the language of B.2.b.

Current Indicator B.2.b.:
More than 90 percent of all employees perceive FCPS as a quality workplace.

      Revised Indicator:
      FCPS will exceed national benchmarks related to workplace satisfaction and demonstrate continuous improvement.

Define a “living wage.”
      The Minimum Living Wage is defined as the Living Minimum Wage paid by the Fairfax County Government. This amount is derived from the highest living wage paid by either Arlington County Government or the City of Alexandria.

Revise the reasonable interpretation and indicators under D. Staff Compensation.
      Revised Reasonable Interpretation:
      Compensation and benefit plans must be developed within budgetary guidelines and School Board funding priorities. Fair and equitable compensation plans are defined as those supported by a well-maintained classification structure, and competitive market pay, and living wage level.

      Revised Indicator D.2.:
      Complete, analyze, and report results of external, and internal, and living wage compensation and benefits surveys to ensure equity and market competitiveness and create salary scales, classification levels, and benefit programs that reflect this.

Recommendation: That the School Board approve the revised indicator for Operational Expectation B.2.b., the definition of the minimum living wage, and the revised reasonable interpretation and indicator for Operational Expectation D., as detailed in the agenda item.
