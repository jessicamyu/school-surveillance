Recommended Action
Certify closed meeting compliance

In order to comply with Section 2.2-3712 (D) of the Code of Virginia, it is necessary for the Board to certify that since the Fairfax County School Board convened a closed meeting on July 18, 2013, to the best of each member’s knowledge, only public business matters lawfully exempted from open meeting requirements and only such public business matters as were identified in the motion convening the closed meeting were heard, discussed, or considered by the Board during the closed meeting.


The foregoing also shall be deemed to include the certification of each School Board member who served on a student disciplinary committee that those closed committee meetings are lawfully exempted from open meeting requirements by Section 2.2-3711(A)(2) of the Code of Virginia, and that only student disciplinary matters were heard, discussed, or considered in those closed committee meetings.

Subject:                   Teacher Workload

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion

 

Background: 

 

In two April 2013 work sessions, Human Resources (HR) staff provided a follow up to the School Board on the 2012 Working Conditions Survey (WCS) and how FCPS could begin addressing findings and recommendations from the 2012 WCS.

 

At the April 8 Work Session, the School Board supported staff’s three recommendations.

 

At the April 27 School Board retreat, the School Board received an update and requested a follow up in July 2013.
 

Key Points:

 

Provide the School Board with a requested update of actions taken to date and to discuss future actions regarding workload related to: leadership oversight, assessment, teacher evaluation, and planning time.
