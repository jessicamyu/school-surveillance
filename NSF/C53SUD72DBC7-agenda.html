Summary/Background (Key Points)

In November 2021, the School Board Governance Committee, in collaboration with FCPS Division Counsel, developed an initial draft of a Trust Policy, to align with the Fairfax County Trust and Confidentiality Policy, with the goal of ensuring FCPS families feel comfortable that their personal information will not be shared with immigration officials. Since that time, the draft policy has been revised and updated through the Governance Committee processes, and has been assigned to the Office of Professional Learning and Family Engagement, to align with Strategic Plan Goal #2-Caring Culture.
