2. Description of the work / nature of new HRAC:

 

Form a Human Resources Advisory Committee aligned with Goal 3 of the FCPS Strategic Plan: Premier Workforce. The purpose of this new committee will be to assist the School Board to carry out its commitment to invest in FCPS employees, encourage innovation and celebrate success. The work of this Committee will begin in September 2017. 

 

The Committee will be composed of 13 members, appointed by the 12 School Board members and the Student Representative.  Appointees should be citizens with expertise and experience in the field of human resources and/or representatives of community organizations related to human resources. Members will be appointed at the first June 2017 Board meeting so they may begin planning the transition for work to begin in September 2017.

 

Between July 2016 and June 2017, realign the work of current Human Relations Advisory Committee (HRAC) into other Committees, including MSAOC.  HRAC will lead the effort to combine the work of the two Advisory Committees and develop a charge for the new group. Gather feedback from members of each of these committees on suggestions for implementation and share proposal with the committee members when developed.

 

Current Human Relations Advisory Committee Purpose:  The committee assists the School Board in fulfilling its commitment to equal opportunity and non-discrimination as essential components of educational excellence.

 

Current Minority Student Achievement Oversight Purpose:  This Committee advocates for minority student achievement. In that capacity, it identifies, reviews, and assesses issues affecting minority student academic achievement within the policies, curriculum, practices and procedures of FCPS. The Committee’s responsibilities also include:  making appropriate recommendations to the School Board; reviewing strategies and proposing solutions for closing the achievement gap; evaluating progress towards goals set by the School Board for closing the achievement gap; and ensuring outreach to parents/guardians about resources available through FCPS.
