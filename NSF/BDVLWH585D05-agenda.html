Subject:  Proposed Scope Amendment to the Glen Forest Elementary School Boundary Study

 

 

Staff Contact:  Jeffrey Platenberg, assistant superintendent, Facilities and Transportation Services

 

 

Other Staff Present:  Marty Smith, chief operating officer

                                  Jessica Gillis, special project administrator, Facilities and Transportation Services

 

 

Meeting Category:  February 24, 2020 – Work Session

 

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion

 

 

Summary/Background:

 

The School Board discussed the proposed scope of boundary adjustments at the work session on May 13, 2019. Glen Forest Elementary School (ES) was included in the discussion for a boundary adjustment and the School Board provided concurrence on a scope to include the following elementary schools: Bailey's ES, Bailey's Upper ES, Glen Forest ES, and Parklawn ES. A boundary scope meeting was held with the community on June 10, 2019, to further inform the Glen Forest ES boundary study.

 

 

Discussion:

 

As a result of community feedback, and in accordance with School Board Regulation 8130.9 on Local School Boundaries, Program Assignments, and School Closings, an amendment to the scope of study is being presented to the School Board for approval. The amendment to the scope will allow staff to develop possible options based on input gathered for presentation to the community at a boundary study meeting.

 

The boundary study meeting with the community will gather feedback on the options presented and staff will make a recommendation to the School Board. The School Board will schedule a public hearing before taking action on the recommendation to adjust the boundary.
