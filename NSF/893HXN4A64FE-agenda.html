Staff Contact:      Terri Breeden, Assistant Superintendent, Professional Learning and Accountability


Meeting Type:      September 13, 2010 - Work Session No.17


Subject:                Professional Learning and Training Operational Expectations Monitoring Report


School Board Action Required:   Decision

 

Key Points:

 

Currently, the annual Professional Learning and Training (PLT) Operational Expectations Monitoring Report is scheduled for presentation in September.

 

In 2009, the mergers of PLT and Accountability into Professional Learning and Accountability resulted in that department being responsible for three School Board reports--Passages in June, PLT Operational Expectations Monitoring Report in September, and Accountability and Audit Operational Expectations Monitoring Report in October.  Additionally,  report data for the PLT monitoring report is not available until July 1.

 

Staff is recommending that the annual Professional Learning and Training Operational Expectations Monitoring Report be moved from September to March.

 

Recommendation:


That the School Board approve a new reporting date of March for the Professional Learning and Training Operational Expectations Monitoring Report, as detailed in the agenda item.

 



Date Approved by School Board:  March 2011
