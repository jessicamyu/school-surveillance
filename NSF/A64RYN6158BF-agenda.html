Staff Contact: Kristen Michael, assistant superintendent, Department of Financial Services

 

Meeting Category:  May 19, 2016 – Budget Work Session No. 115

 

Subject:  FY 2017 Approved Budget      

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion                                    

 

Key Points:

 

On February 4, 2016, the School Board adopted the FY 2017 Advertised Budget. The advertised budget was presented to and discussed by the School Board and County Board of Supervisors at a joint budget work session on February 26, 2016.

 

Impact of Final County and State Budgets:

On April 26, 2016, the County Board of Supervisors adopted their FY 2017 budget that included an increase in the transfer to the School Operating Fund of 4.8 percent above the FY 2016 Approved Budget. The increase of $88.4 million is $34.3 million less than requested in FCPS’ advertised budget. The Virginia General Assembly adopted its 2016-2018 budget, as amended on March 16, 2016, which provides an increase of $16.8 million in revenue and an increase in expenditures of $1.2 million as compared to FCPS’ FY 2017 Advertised Budget.

 

Starting with the FY 2017 Advertised Budget, following are the required adjustments in millions:

 

Required Revenue Adjustments:

State Aid and Sales Tax                             $16.8     

County Transfer                                      ($34.3)

Fairfax City                                                $0.9

 

Required Expenditure Adjustments:

Enrollment and Demographic Adjustments  ($3.8)   

VRS State Life Insurance                             $1.2

Health Insurance                                       ($2.6)

Fuel                                                          ($2.0)

 

After the required adjustments, the remaining gap totals $9.3 million. To balance the budget, the Superintendent has recommended the following revenue and expenditure adjustments in millions: 

 

Recommended Revenue Adjustments:

Beginning Balance                                      $5.3

Community Use Fees                                   $0.2

 

Recommended Expenditure Adjustments:

Substitute Pay Rates                                  ($2.3)

Living Wage Charged to FNS Fund              ($1.1)

Reduction from Central Department            ($0.5)

   Operating Accounts                                    

 

Changes to other School Board funds are detailed in the attachment.

 

Recommendation:

 

That the School Board adopt the FY 2017 Approved Budget as detailed in the FY 2017 Advertised Budget, modified by the adjustments included in this agenda item.
