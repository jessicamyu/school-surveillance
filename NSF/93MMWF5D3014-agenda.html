Staff Contact:            Barbara Hunter, assistant superintendent, Department of Communications and Community Outreach

 

Other Staff Present:  Jay Garant, coordinator, Business and Community Partnerships, Department of Communications and Community Outreach

 

Meeting Category:     January 17, 2013 - Work Session No. 68

 

Subject:                     Alumni Working Group

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion                                         

 

Key Points:

 

The School Board directed the Superintendent to establish a working group to explore creating an alumni networking website, taking account of the potential for corporate sponsors and alumni involvement in its creation.  The working group will seek to facilitate simpler organization of class reunions, more comprehensive tracking of the college and career paths of graduates, and a more streamlined donation process.  The report contains:

  • Overview of School Board Goals
  • Inventory of Alumni Relations Best Practices
  • Observations about Current FCPS Alumni Relations
  • Potential Four Phase Recommendations for FCPS Alumni Relations

Staff Contact:           Michael Molloy, Director, Government Relations                 

 

Meeting Category:  January 17, 2013 -  Work Session No. 71

 

Subject:                 Proposed Legislation from 2013 General Assembly Session

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion                                        

 

Key Points:

 

The General Assembly convened its 2013 Session on Wednesday, January 9th.  As of January 11th, there were already over 1,700 pieces of legislation filed for consideration during the 45 day session, many of which have a potential impact on public education.

 

In addition, Governor Bob McDonnell has released a three phase education plan which focuses on teachers, accountability and choice; components of which will also be considered during the General Assembly session.

 

The Fairfax County School Board annually adopts a Legislative Program, which addresses many of the Board’s general and specific policy priorities and positions, the most recent copy of which can be found at:  http://www.fcps.edu/supt/govtrelations/documents/2013FCSBLPFinalActionApproved.pdf
