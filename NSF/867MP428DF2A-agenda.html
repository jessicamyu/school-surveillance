OPERATIONS BELIEFS AND VALUES

Transportation
· Transport all students in a safe and timely manner to instructional programs for which they are eligible OR meet their needs.

Facilities Management
· Provide and maintain safe, healthy, and modern classrooms and work environments that support the instructional program.

Capital Improvement and Infrastructure
· Maintain a renovation and new construction cycle which provides classroom capacity consistent with instructional programs and community/ neighborhood needs.
· Provide the necessary infrastructure to support instruction and operations.
    Human Resources
    · Recruit, select, and retain a high quality and diverse workforce that enables FCPS to deliver a world-class education to every student.

    Budget
    · Develop a balanced, clearly presented, needs-based budget that meets the community’s values and the School Board’s goals.

    Financial Management
    · Manage financial affairs in a fiscally accountable, transparent manner consistent with School Board budget and goals.
    · Maximize effectiveness and conserve resources

    Accountability/Audit
    · Maintain a system that tracks, measures, and evaluates our effectiveness in realizing student achievement and business management goals.
    · Such a system should include both benefits and costs and should be done in a timely manner.

    Technology
    · Technology is a powerful tool which should be embedded throughout all the instructional and operational venues.

    Staff Development
    · The quality of our workforce is key to the quality of Fairfax County Public Schools.
    · Staff development is fundamental to the continued excellence of the entire workforce.

    ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES OF THE SCHOOL BOARD


    The role of the Fairfax County School Board is to direct Fairfax County Public Schools and focus the greater Fairfax County community toward ensuring the successful education of students, consistent with the vision and goals adopted by the School Board. To carry out that role, the School Board will:

    1. Establish expectations for student achievement, provide resources to enable students to meet those expectations, and hold Fairfax County Public Schools accountable for student achievement.
      a. Develop goals and rigorously monitor performance against approved benchmarks and toward identified goals.
      b. Hire and regularly evaluate the performance of the superintendent of schools. Does the Board want to include a self-evaluation component here?]
      c. Adopt operating and capital budgets that support the Board’s identified goals.

      2. Represent the interests of the citizens of Fairfax County.

      a. Engage the community to solicit input about expectations for student achievement and preparation for the future.
      b. Report regularly to the community on student achievement, operational goals and challenges, and initiatives to improve achievement and effectiveness of division operations.
      c. Exercise stewardship of resources by monitoring the use of resources to ensure that operating and capital funds are spent wisely and effectively.
      d. Ensure that Fairfax County Public Schools responds respectfully and timely to issues raised by members of the community.

      3. Build coalitions with parents and guardians, and with groups representing the diverse elements of the Fairfax County community.
        4. Advocate for the needs of Fairfax County Public Schools, and the children it serves, with local, state and federal elected and appointed officials, and with the citizens of Fairfax County.
