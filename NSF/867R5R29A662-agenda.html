14.02 Discussion - FY 2006 Budget
Meeting
Oct 21, 2004 - Work Sessions
Category
14. Resource Management & Evaluation
Type
Information
Presenters: Deirdra McLaughlin, Chief Financial Officer
Andy Hawkins, Director, Office of Budget Services

In response to the School Board’s request for long-term planning and involvement in the budget process, the attached list was developed for your consideration. It represents ideas from the clusters, departments, and principal associations. This list will be the starting point for a discussion of budget priorities for the FY 2006 budget and beyond at the October 21, 2004, resource management and evaluation work session. At that time, the School Board will have the opportunity to add or eliminate priorities. At the November 8, 2004, resource management and evaluation work session, the School Board will prioritize these initiatives to guide the development of next year’s budget.
