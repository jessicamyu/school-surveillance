Meeting Category:  June 15, 2020 – Work Session

 

School Board Action Required: Information                         

 

Key Points:

Following the May 11, 2020 School Board work session, where the Board considered initial fall reopening challenges, the superintendent and deputy established a new task force structure to prioritize and expedite information gathering and development of recommendations for reopening school in the fall.  The task force was formed to address six key action areas: Facilities and School Operations, Instruction, Technology, Communication, Community Partnerships, and Human Resources.  

 

Today’s presentation will provide options for reopening of schools under the following scenarios-- virtual start, return with social distancing, or reopen with some students and staff unable to return.

 

Recommendation:                           

The recommendation is the that the School Board give consensus for the Superintendent to move forward with the reopening of schools plan.
