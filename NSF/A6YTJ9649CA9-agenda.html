Fairfax County Public Schools

School Board

Audit Committee Agenda

April 27, 2016

Gatehouse Administration Center

Conference Room 1610

4:30 p.m. – 7:00 p.m.

 

  1. Consent of minutes of March 30, 2016 audit committee meeting.  [10 minutes]
  2. FY17 Annual Audit Plan discussion: AG describes risk-based audit plan, and seeks input for auditable units, and audit plan risk assessment survey. [60 minutes]
  3. Auditor General update
    1. OAG Metrics: AG has defined a set of metrics and the strategy for how the metrics will facilitate performance of OAG.  Seeking additional input for the data-driven performance measurements that will be deployed. [30 minutes]
    2. OAG Strategy Achievements, October – March 30: AG is informing the committee of the status of projects initiated in the OAG Change Management Program (circa October 2015). Q&A. [15 minutes]

follow up on AG Recommendations from Bond Audit (circa February 2016). [ 5 minutes]

 

  1. Internal Audit Charter, R1420, review and discussion: AG is proposing minor changes to the charter.   [30 minutes]

 

  1. Auditor General Authority Policy, discussion:  As a result of the audit committee meeting on March 30, 2016 the Auditor General made revisions to this proposed policy.  During today’s committee meeting, any agreed upon edits of the proposed policy can be made. Further discussion will carry over to the next audit committee meeting.  [10 minutes]

 

  1. Status of current audit work: Informing the committee of the status of current audit engagements. Q&A. [5 minutes]

 

  1. Report out on Business Process Turnover Audits for Lees Corner Elementary School and Sangster Elementary School [5 minutes]

 

  1. Pending topics

 

  1. Review and affirmation of action items from today’s meeting. [5 minutes]

 

  1. Closed session

Next meeting:

FY17 Annual Audit Plan discussion; Auditor General Authority Policy; Report out on Claims Management Audit; Report out on Business Process Turnover Audit for Cameron ES; Carryover items from today’s meeting
