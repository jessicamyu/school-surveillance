1.  Revisions to the Strategic Governance Manual regarding Citizen Participation Procedures

     a.  Substitutions will be permitted at the meeting, not during the week prior to the meeting.  Suggested language:

          Speaker substitutions are discouraged, however, in the event that a substitute is necessary, it will be done in the

          following manner:  must be face to face with the Clerk of the Board on the day of the meeting. 

     b.  Modify language around Board Chair discretion regarding who may speak at a meeting.

     c.  Wordsmith to include the notion of a lottery:

          1.  Speaker sign up window to open 3 days prior to the meeting (at a time to be decided). 

          2.  Window will remain open until a set time. 

          3.  10 speaker names will be randomly selected and posted. 

          4.   If the 5 video spots are not taken, the remaining slots may become speaker slots.

     d.  PEC to consider setting aside 3 additional speaking slots at each Regular meeting for the 3 employee "unions"
