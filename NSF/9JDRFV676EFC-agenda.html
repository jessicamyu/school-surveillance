Meeting Category:     April 28, 2014 – Work Session No. 105

 

Subject:                      Budget

 

School Board Action Required:  Discussion                                                          

 

Key Points:

 

On February 6, 2014, the School Board adopted the FY 2015 Advertised Budget.  The advertised budget was presented to and discussed by the School Board and County Board of Supervisors at a joint budget work session on March 18, 2014. 

 

On April 22, 2014, the County Board of Supervisors included in the markup of their FY 2015 budget an increase in the transfer to the School Operating Fund of 3.0 percent above the FY 2014 Approved Budget.  The increase of $51.5 million is $46.6 million less than requested in FCPS’ advertised budget.  While the State has not yet adopted their budget, FCPS is projecting an increase of $30.0 million over the estimate included in FCPS’ FY 2015 Advertised Budget based on the current state budget proposals available.  It appears very possible that the State will not adopt their budget prior to FCPS’ approved budget adoption.

 

Based on the available revenue from the County and the projected revenue from the State for FY 2015, the following items will be presented and discussed:

  1. A summary of the required and recommended adjustments to the advertised budget
  2. Employee compensation options
  3. Planning Information for FY 2016
  4. Key information shared with the BOS on March 18, 2014

 

Following is the calendar of upcoming budget work sessions and public hearings for the FY 2015 Approved Budget:

May 8

School Board FY 2015 Approved Budget presented for new business

May 13, 14  

School Board Budget Public Hearings (Jackson Middle School)

May 15 

School Board Budget Work Session (Jackson Middle School)

School Board Adopts FY 2015 Approved Budget
