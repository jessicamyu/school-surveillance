Meeting Category: July 13, 2020 – Work Session

 

School Board Action Required: Discussion

 

Ignite Link: Caring Culture

 

Problem Statement: Regulation 2601, Student Rights and Responsibilities, needs to be updated to reflect legislative changes and to address concerns raised by principals and the community.

 

Outcome: Provide input regarding recommended changes and presentation materials.

 

Summary/Background (Key Points):

 

As a follow-up to the SR&R presentation on April 23, 2020, additional information is being presented to guide the conversation to support the recommended changes outlined previously. Topics will include:

 

Required Training and Professional Development to Support SR&R

Office of Professional Learning and Cultural Responsiveness Work

New Data Tools

Quarterly Data Reviews

Continued Implementation of MTSS

Additional recommended edits based upon Title IX federal guidance that was released after the April 23rd presentation

 

 

 

  1. Regulation 2610 has been renamed 2602.

 

  1. Section IV, new distribution protocols are outlined.

 

  1. Chapter 1.A., clarifying attendance language was added.

 

  1. Chapter 1.C., Dress Code language updated to reflect state code.

 

  1. Chapter 1.J., updated footnote 3 to include Title IX considerations.

 

  1. Chapter 1.J., added Title IX contact information for questions or concerns about reporting sexual harassment.

 

  1. Chapter I.K.4., deleted Ombudsman language as discrimination complaints should be addressed to the Title IX coordinator.

 

  1. Chapter II, Introduction, language was added to include social emotional learning, multi-tiered system of support, restorative practices and the term unbiased.

 

  1. Chapter II, Administrative Response Guide, clarifying language was added and charts were moved below Leveled Responses to Student Behaviors Charts.

 

  1. Chapter II, Leveled Responses to Student Behaviors Charts, updated to align with new state codes, footnotes 4 and 5 added, and charts moved above Administrative Response Guide.

 

  1. Chapter II, DV Code Key, removed due to changes in state codes.

 

  1. Chapter II.A., added the phrase as outlined in this regulation in three locations to clarify when certain sanctions can be imposed.

 

  1. Chapter II.A., footnote 10, added the word consecutive.

 

  1. Chapter II.A., removed the words sexual assault.

 

  • Chapter II.A., threat assessment language was changed from may to will.

 

  1. Chapter II.A.1.a.(5)., added Title IX considerations to footnote 11, updated should to shall and deleted last sentence.

 

  1. Chapter II.A.1.b, removed sexual assault and battery and moved physical assault of a staff member into the paragraph.

 

  1. Chapter II.A.2., Restorative Behavior Intervention Specialist updated to Restorative Justice Practitioner.

 

  1. Chapter II.A.2.a.(8)., added footnote to address self-defense.

 

  1. Chapter II.A.2.b.(1-5), renumbered footnotes, updated should to shall in footnote 14, and aligned language to meet Title IX considerations.

 

  1. Chapter II.A.3.a.(1)., updated parent AOD information.

 

  1. Chapter II.A.3.b and e., deleted last sentence of second paragraph.

 

  1. Chapter II.A.3.b.(1).(a), removed the word sexual assault from footnote 15.

 

  1. Chapter II.A.3.e.(1).(b), added in lieu of a five day out of school suspension.

 

  1. Chapter II.A.3.e.(1).(b).(ii), changed to shall result in an out of school suspension for three days.

 

  1. Chapter II.A.3.e.(2).(a)., added or imitation drugs.

 

  1. Chapter II.B.1., updated to reflect mandatory and discretionary reporting to police.

 

  1. Chapter II.B.6., Updated parent notification to reflect reportable incidents as outlined in section B.

 

  1. Chapter II.C.3., added words consecutive and for a violation in the first sentence.

 

  1. Chapter II.D.5., clarified IEP team language.

 

  1. Glossary, updated the Alcohol and Other Drug Intervention Seminar to reflect current Alcohol and Other Drug Intervention practices.

 

  1. Glossary, updated the Discriminatory Harassment definition.

 

  1. Glossary, updated the Sexual Harassment definition to reflect Sexual Misconduct.

 

  1. Glossary, removed the Stalking definition.

 

  1. Glossary, removed Restorative Behavior Intervention (RBI) definition.
