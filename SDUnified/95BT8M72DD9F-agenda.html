  1. Request that the San Diego City Council adjust the boundary to remove the baseball field portion from the existing joint use agreement;
  2. Begin maintaining the field once it is removed from the joint use agreement;
  3. Continue discussion with David Wells and the Point Loma Baseball Boosters regarding fundraising for certain elements of the proposed improvements;
  4. Form an ad hoc field usage task force including community sports stakeholders to develop a protocol to ensure equitable field usage by schools, youth leagues, and community sports leagues for the Dana field.

PRIOR YEAR FISCAL IMPACT: None.

BACKGROUND:  At the November 6, 2012 regular meeting, the board approved a motion by Trustee Barnett to request that the San Diego City Council amend the existing joint use agreement to remove the Dana baseball field.  Approval of the motion included consideration of a private donation to fund a new baseball field and maintain it in perpetuity at a higher standard than currently provided by the City or could be provided by the district. 

 

This direction was executed by staff and the San Diego City Council was scheduled to consider the removal of the field from the joint use agreement on October 22, 2013.  However, on October 21, 2013, the donor informed Point Loma High School staff that he would not be able to commit funds to the renovation or maintenance of the baseball field at this time.  Consequently, district staff requested that the City Council agenda item be continued to a later date.

 

Since that time, a preliminary cost estimate and proposal for improving the field has been discussed by staff and Proposition Z is being suggested as the funding source for the improvements only.
