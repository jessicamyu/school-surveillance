3. Public Hearing to Consider the General Waiver Request of Gompers Preparatory Academy
Meeting
Aug 31, 2010 - Special Meeting, 5:00 P.M.
Type
Public Hearing

RECOMMENDATION: Conduct a public hearing to consider the general waiver request of Gompers Preparatory Academy.

 

FISCAL IMPACT: None

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None

PRIOR YEAR EXPENSE HISTORY: None

BACKGROUND: Pursuant to Education Code section 33050, a school district, after a public hearing, may request on behalf of one of its charter schools, that the State Board of Education waive the application of the Education Code.  Gompers Preparatory Academy (“Gompers”) is adding grade levels in 2010-2011 and is seeking the approval of the Governing Board of the San Diego Unified School District (“District”) to request a general waiver from the State Board of Education.  Gompers is seeking to waive the application of the second sentence of Education Code section 47652(b) regarding the calculation of its advance apportionment on the basis of prior year average daily attendance.  There is no fiscal impact to the District.

7. Implications of Conditional Budget Approval from the San Diego County Office of Education
Meeting
Aug 31, 2010 - Special Meeting, 5:00 P.M.
Type
Information

RECOMMENDATION:  To discuss implications of San Diego County Office of Education (SDCOE) Conditionally approving the district’s adopted budget. 

 

FISCAL IMPACT:  The Board needs to discuss various options to revise the 2010/11 budget.  These options could include a reduction to our Reserve for Economic Uncertainty previously required at 2% down to the minimal allowed of .67%.  Another option would be to reduce budgeted expenditures in order to maintain the recommended 2% reserve.

 

PRIOR YEAR EXPENSE HISTORY: None

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None

 

BACKGROUND:  On August 16th, the District received a Conditional Budget Approval from the SDCOE.  The Conditional approval was based on the inclusion of a revenue assumption of $9M per year for Special Disabilities Adjustment for 2009/10, 2010/11 budget and two out-years.  In addition, the district included a COLA assumption in 2011/12 for $12M based on the School Services Dartboard. The SDCOE is requiring the District to reverse these assumptions and submit a revised budget by September 8th, 2010.  The Board must also adopt a resolution identifying the reductions to address the deficits for 2011/2 and 2012/13.  By 1st Interim, the District must identify the reductions and provide a timeline for implementation to SDCOE.
