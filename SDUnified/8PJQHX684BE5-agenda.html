RECOMMENDATION:  1. Approval of the preliminary proposals of facilities (Attachment 1) to eligible charters that have made appropriate applications for facilities, without waiver of the deficiencies in each of their requests.  The preliminary proposals are subject to the terms and conditions set forth in this report. 2.  Adoption of this report as the supporting statement of reasons for board's decisions.

 

FISCAL IMPACT:  No fiscal impact.

 

PRIOR YEAR FISCAL IMPACT:  None.

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING:  None.

 

CONSULTATION WITH BOARD ADVISORY COMMITTEE:  Not applicable.

 

BACKGROUND:  Education Code section 47614 and the California Code of Regulations, Title Sections 11969 et seq. ("Regulations"), require:

  • Districts offer facilities, to the extent required by law, to a charter that submit a legally complete application;
  • A charter school wishing to occupy district facilities in the 2012-13 year must submit a complete application for facilities by November 1, 2011;
  • Districts analyze each charter school's request in conformity with the law and raise concerns to the charter school regarding the charter school's state in-district classroom ADA projections, prior to developing the district's preliminary proposal in response to the application;
  • On or before February 1, districts must submit preliminary proposals to eligible charter schools, and on or before March 1, charter schools must respond with any concerns regarding the preliminary proposals;
  • On or before April 2, districts must submit final offers of facilities to eligible charter schools, and on or before May 1, charter schools must respond to the final offers if they are to receive district facilities for the upcoming year. 

The preliminary proposals are only the first step in the process of developing final offers of facilities to charter schools.  Board approval of the preliminary proposals is required in order to continue this process, including discussion of the preliminary proposal with the charter schools, district schools impacted by the proposals, and to meet the statutory requirements to present these proposals to charter schools by February 1, 2012.
