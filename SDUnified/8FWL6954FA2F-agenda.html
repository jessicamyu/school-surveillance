3. Development of Five-Year Strategic Plan for Neighborhood Schools
Meeting
May 24, 2011 - Special Meeting, 9:30 A.M.
Type
Information

The Superintendent will provide an update to the board on the status of the preparation of a five year strategic plan for the district.  The Superintendent will report on the work done by the leadership team at the Panasonic Leadership Associates conference held on May 5-7, 2011.  The leadership team developed a draft framework for a Strategic Process to Create A Quality School in Every Neighborhood, consistent with the board Vision 2020 and the board direction regarding the development of a strategic plan.  The Superintendent will seek feedback and further direction from the board on the elements of the Strategic Process and timeline for its development and stakeholder engagement.
