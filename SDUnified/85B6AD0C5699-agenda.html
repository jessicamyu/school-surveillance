C. STUDENT INSTRUCTIONAL MATTERS
Subject
C.01. Public Hearing to Consider the New Charter Petition for San Diego Global Vision Academy Charter School; BUDGETED: N/A (OE-2)
Meeting
Jan 19, 2010 - - Special Meeting, 5:00 P.M.
Type
Public Hearing

RECOMMENDATION: Conduct a public hearing for the new Charter Petition for San Diego Global Vision Academy Charter School.

FISCAL IMPACT: None.

PRIOR YEAR EXPENSE HISTORY: None.

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None.

BACKGROUND: Public hearing pursuant to Education Code sections 47605 and 47607 to consider the level of district teacher, other district employee and parent support for the new charter petition submitted by the San Diego Global Vision Academy (SDGVA) Charter School. Board action is not required at this time. This matter will be brought back to the board at a future date for action on the charter petition. The attached exhibits include the proposed charter and budget information. The charter petition is being reviewed by district staff to determine whether the proposed charter meets all operational and legal requirements for adoption.
