C. Operational Matters Reserved for the Board
Subject
1. Ratification and Approval of Agreements with San Diego Education Association (SDEA) related to the COVID-19 Pandemic
Meeting
Apr 6, 2021 - Special Meeting, 5:00 p.m.
Type
Action

RECOMMENDATION: Ratify and approve the agreements between the District and San Diego Education Association (SDEA) regarding the identified impacts and effects of the COVID-19 pandemic. 

 

FISCAL IMPACT: No budget adjustments are required for this agreement as existing budget for leave and visiting teachers covers any fiscal impact.

 

PRIOR YEAR FISCAL IMPACT: None

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: N/A

 

CONSULTATION WITH BOARD ADVISORY COMMITTEE: Not applicable.

 

BACKGROUND: In order to promote public health, the safety of employees, and to ensure the continuity of learning for students, the District continues to meet with each of the bargaining units to discuss the COVID-19 Pandemic. After bargaining in good faith, the District reached the additional agreements listed below with SDEA on specific terms related to the impacts and effects of the COVID-19 Pandemic.

  • COVID-19 Leave
  • Reasonable Accommodations
  • Health and Safety for Onsite Instruction
  • Onsite Instruction for the 2020-2021 School Year

 

The District continues to meet with each of the bargaining units to reach agreements related to the impacts and effects of the COVID-19 pandemic and will continue to bring completed agreements for Board approval.
