C. STUDENT INSTRUCTIONAL MATTERS
Subject
C.01. Briefing on DataDirector (Board Governance Policy: OE-8; OE-10)
Meeting
Sep 22, 2008 - Special Meeting
Type
Information
In the spring of 2008, staff in the Research and Evaluation Division, in collaboration with staff from Information Technology, began working with Achieve Data Solutions to implement use of DataDirector, an online data management and reporting tool. DataDirector provides a user-friendly means for school staffs (principals, vice principals, counselors, teachers, etc.) and central office staff to monitor student achievement with easily accessible reporting tools that permit flexible grouping and sorting of students. Additionally, reports provide full drill down capability to identify specific students who may need additional, targeted instruction.

Board members will be briefed on the selection process for the software, apprised of the implementation plan for the rollout to appropriate staff, and shown a demonstration of typical uses of the software. [Originator/Contact: Karen Bachofer, Executive Director, Research and Evaluation, Office of the Chief Curriculum and Staff Development Officer, 619.725.7167, kbachofer@sandi.net]
