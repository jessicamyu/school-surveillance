BACKGROUND:  The Board of Education has annually adopted a resolution for issuance of TRANS for the upcoming fiscal year, most recently at the June 14, 2011 meeting for the 2011-12 fiscal year.

RECOMMENDATION:  Approve the Resolution Certifying the Final Negative Declaration Report for the Millennial Tech Middle School Three-Story Classroom Building Project and certify that:

  1. An Initial Study was conducted and it was determined that the proposed project would not result in a significant effect on the environment;
  2. the Final Negative Declaration Report (Final ND) for the construction and operation of the Millennial Tech Middle School (SCH 2011121029) has been prepared in compliance with applicable laws and regulations to evaluate the potential for significant adverse environmental impacts, and finds on the basis of the whole record before it (including the Initial Study and any comments received, which were none) that there is no substantial evidence that the project will have a significant effect on the environment; and
  3. the Final ND (Attachment A) was presented to the Board of Education, which reviewed and considered the information contained in the record prior to approving the project; and
  4. the Final ND reflects the district’s independent judgment and analysis.  A copy is available at the Physical Plant Operations Annex, Room 5, 4860 Ruffner Street; and
  5. Authorize staff to file the Notice of Determination for the Millennial Tech Middle Three-Story Classroom Building Project (SCH No. 2011121029) with the Recorder/County Clerk of San Diego County, California (Attachment B). 
  6. Authorize that the project be carried out as defined in the Final ND for the proposed Millennial Tech Middle School Three-Story Classroom Building Project.

FISCAL IMPACT:  Certification of the Final Negative Declaration Report by the board will require the district to file a Notice of Determination with the County Clerk of San Diego County with associated filing and processing fees totaling of $50.00.  The filing and processing fee will be paid out of the State School Facilities Fund (SSFF); budget string: 9341-77100-00-5853-8500-0000-35000-0000.

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING:  There is no impact to district staffing levels associated with certification of the Final ND.

BACKGROUND:  The district proposes to construct and operate the Millennial Tech Middle School, three-story cassroom building within the Encanto Community of the City of San Diego.  The proposed project includes the following components:

  • Construction and operation of the new three-story classroom building;

The proposed new three-story classroom building would support the enrollment needs in the Encanto Community and help the District achieve the enrollment standards set forth in its Long Range Facilities Master Plan. 

 

On December 9, 2011, the district filed a Draft Negative Declaration out for 30-day public review period.  A Notice of Intent to adopt the Negative Declaration was published in the Union-Tribune and La Prensa, and filed at the County of San Diego and California State Clearinghouse.  Copies of the Draft Negative Declaration were sent to the State Clearinghouse, state, and local agencies, and community entities/organizations.  The Draft Negative Declaration is available at the district’s Physical Plant Operations Annex; the City of San Diego Library; Valencia Park/Malcolm X Branch Library; and online at the Environmental Review/Studies page at: www.sandi.net/facilities. 

 

The Draft Negative Declaration evaluated the following environmental issues: aesthetics, agriculture and forestry resources, air quality, greenhouse gas emissions, biological resources, cultural resources, geology and soils, hazards and hazardous materials, hydrology and water quality, land use and planning, mineral resources, transportation/circulation, noise, population and housing, recreation, public services and utilities and service systems. 

 

No revisions to the Draft Negative Declaration were made in the Final Negative Declaration in response to comments received.

 

The Final Negative Declaration was prepared in accordance with the California Environmental Quality Act (CEQA) and the Guidelines for Implementation of CEQA.  A copy of the Final Negative Declaration and Technical Appendices for the Millennial Tech Middle School Three-Story Building Project has been provided to the board for review prior to the public meeting and is located in Millennial Tech Middle School, 1110 Carolina Lane, San Diego, CA 92101.  Additional copies of the Final ND are available for review in the Physical Plant Operations Annex, Room 5; and the Valencia Park/Malcolm X Branch Library.  The Final Negative Declaration, without Technical Appendices, is also available online at the Environmental Reviews/Studies page at www.sandi.net/facilities.
