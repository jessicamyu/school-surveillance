2. Public Hearing to Consider New Charter Petition for Downtown Charter High; BUDGETED: N/A (OE-2)
Meeting
Oct 5, 2010 - Regular Charter Meeting, 5:00 P.M.
Type
Public Hearing

RECOMMENDATION: Conduct a public hearing for the new charter petition for Downtown Charter High.

 

FISCAL IMPACT: None.

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None.

PRIOR YEAR EXPENSE HISTORY: None.

BACKGROUND: Public hearing pursuant to Education Code sections 47605 and 47607 to consider the level of district teacher, other district employee and parent support for the new charter petition submitted by Downtown Charter High petitioners. Board action is not required at this time. This matter will be brought back to the board at a future date for action on the charter petition. The attached exhibits include the proposed charter and budget information.  The charter petition is being reviewed by district staff to determine whether the proposed charter meets all operational and legal requirements for adoption.

 

The school is proposing to a new charter to operate a ninth through twelvth grade education program model and to be located at a new central library to be built in Downtown San Diego.  Staff will prepare a report with staff analysis when the charter petition returns for board action.
