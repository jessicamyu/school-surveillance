Meeting
Apr 19, 2016 - Special Joint Meeting of the San Diego Unified School District and the San Diego Community College District Boards, 5:00 P.M.
Type
Information

Create an accountability strategy that includes:

  1. A clear message to students, parents, and the community that college and career readiness is the measure of accountability for San Diego Unified
  2. Professional development for K-12 teachers to focus on college and career preparedness
  3. A plan to work with “contributor” organizations, such as PQE and CBET, to promote college readiness to parents and communities
