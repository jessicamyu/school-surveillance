A.03. Operational Expectations OE-6, Financial Planning, Interpretations and Indicators; BUDGETED: N/A (GC-6)
Meeting
Apr 27, 2010 - - Special Meeting, 11:00 A.M.
Type
Action
Fiscal Impact
No
RECOMMENDATION: Approve staff interpretations and indicators for Operational Expectations OE-6, Financial Planning and direct staff to submit a monitoring report to the board in June 2010. The monitoring report will include evidence that the district is in compliance, or compliance with exceptions, with the board's policy values concerning the Operational Expectations for Financial Planning.

FISCAL IMPACT: None.

PRIOR YEAR EXPENSE HISTORY: None.

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None.

BACKGROUND: On September 9, 2008, the board adopted, and on September 29, 2009, the board re-adopted, revised Board Governance Policies that include Operational Expectations. As requested by the board, beginning in 2010, a two-step process was implemented for the monitoring of Board Governance Policy Statements. Step 1 is to provide proposed interpretations and indicators for board review and approval. Step 2 is the presentation of the monitoring reports of each policy statement to the board throughout the year in accordance with the annual work plan calendar.
