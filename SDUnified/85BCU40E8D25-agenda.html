C. STUDENT INSTRUCTIONAL MATTERS
Subject
C.01. Magnet and Innovation Schools Plan and Changes to Enrollment Options Application Procedures (Board Governance Policy: OE-10)
Meeting
Oct 13, 2008 - Special Meeting
Type
Information
This briefing is to provide the board with the status of Magnet and Innovation programs, the evaluation process and Enrollment Options procedures. It is also to request board feedback regarding the issues presented. Following the board’s feedback, the Office of the Chief School Innovation and Choice Officer will bring forward at the October 28 board meeting and at future board meetings as appropriate, changes to the district’s primary goals and characteristics and fair and equitable recommendations for approved schools that will open, remain or exit as magnet or innovation schools. It is the intent of the Office of the Chief School Innovation and School Choice Officer to publish the 2009-2010 Enrollment Options application on November 1 and the required 2009-2010 Enrollment Options catalog in mid-November. [Originator/Contact: Rich Cansdale, Chief School Innovation and Choice Officer, 858.627.7408, rcansdale@sandi.net; Chuck Morris, Deputy Superintendent, 619.725.7103, cmorris@sandi.net]
