C. LOCAL CONTROL AND ACCOUNTABILITY PLAN (LCAP)
Subject
1. Opening Comments/Overview
Meeting
Jun 8, 2022 - Special Meeting, 12:00 pm, Local Control and Accountability Plan (LCAP) Workshop
Type
Information

RECOMMENDATION: The Superintendent and staff will provide information to the board on the development of Year 2 of the 2021-2023 Local Control and Accountability Plan (LCAP). Each LCAP Goal will be reviewed, and plans for implementation of each goal for the 2022-23 school year will be presented.  Budget Expenditures will be included in the LCAP document at the June 21, 2022 First Reading.

 

FISCAL IMPACT: None. 

 

PRIOR YEAR FISCAL IMPACT: None. 

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None. 

 

CONSULTATION WITH BOARD ADVISORY COMMITTEE: Not applicable.

 

BACKGROUND: This workshop will provide board members and the public with the opportunity to learn about the district's plans for implementation of Year 2 of the the 2021-23 LCAP.   

 

[Originator/Contact: Lamont Jackson, Superintendent of Public Education, 619.725.5506, superintendent@sandi.net]
