Legal Services provides legal advice and representation to the Board of Education, administration, and other district staff. Services are provided through eight staff members: a General Counsel, one Deputy General Counsel, two Assistant General Counsel, one Legal Secretary II, and three Legal Secretary I. We are involved either directly or indirectly in all legal issues affecting the district. We emphasize a service orientation and mission.
