3. Local Educational Agency (LEA) Plan, First Reading
Meeting
Apr 10, 2012 - Special Meeting, 10:00 A.M.
Type
Information, First Reading

The Superintendent and Ron Rode, Executive Director, Office of Accountability, will provide the board with an update on the process to revise and submit the district's revised Local Educational Agency (LEA) Plan.

 

In November 2011, the State Board of Education designated San Diego Unified a Year 3 Program Improvement district. As a result, the district was required to enter into an agreement with a technical assistance provider to assist with conducting a needs assessment and revising the district's Local Educational Agency (LEA) Plan. The presentation and discussion during this first reading will focus on the needs assessment conducted by WestEd, the district's technical assistance provider, and a review of the draft LEA Plan, including the allocation of the $9.7 million that is awarded to the district in support of LEA Plan implementation.
