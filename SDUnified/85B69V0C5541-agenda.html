RECOMMENDATION: Approve staff interpretations and indicators for Goals for Student Achievement-2 (GSA-2), Academics, (excluding sections 2.3.3, 2.3.4, 2.4, and 2.5) and direct staff to submit a monitoring report to the board in October 2010. The monitoring report will include evidence that the district is in compliance, or compliance with exceptions, with the board’s policy values concerning the GSA-2.

Staff will provide interpretations and indicators for the excluded sections during the 2010-11 school year, upon board establishment of academic priorities, and reflective of budget and staffing considerations.

FISCAL IMPACT: None.

PRIOR YEAR EXPENSE HISTORY: None.

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None.

BACKGROUND: On September 9, 2008, the board adopted, and on September 29, 2009, the board re-adopted, revised Board Governance Policies that include Goals for Student Achievement (GSA). GSA-2 specifies the board's policies for Academics. The board's Annual Work Plan Calendar for 2010-11 calls for Interpretations and Indicators to be submitted to the board in January 2010.
