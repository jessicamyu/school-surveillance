3. Goals for Student Achievement (GSA)-2 Academics, Interpretations and Indicators for Section 2.5 (Fine Arts); BUDGETED: N/A (GSA-2) (ROOM 2226)
Meeting
Oct 25, 2011 - Special Meeting, 9:30 A.M.
Type
Action

RECOMMENDATION:  Approve interpretation and indicators for GSA-2 Academics, Section 2.5, Fine Arts. (Section 2.5 also includes practical arts, which will be presented separately).

 

FISCAL IMPACT:  None.

 

PRIOR YEAR FISCAL IMPACT:  None.

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING:  A workgroup consisting of approximately 30 people has been working since spring 2010 on developing the GSA-2 Interpretations and Indicators.  This group consists of teachers, administrators, area superintendents, instructional support services staff, and community members.  Meetings are generally held after school hours to ensure that classroom staff and community members have the opportunity to participate.  Substitutes are provided for any meetings/workshops held during school hours.

 

CONSULTATION WITH BOARD ADVISORY COMMITTEE:  Not applicable.

 

BACKGROUND:  In fall 2009, under the guidance of the Aspen Group, a team comprised of central office leaders was created to address the interpretations of the board's academic goals and develop indicators that show the district's progress in meeting the goals.  In spring 2010, as a result of board feedback on the proposed interpretations and indicators, a new workgroup was created that began building upon and expanding the work of the original team.

 

The second workgroup identified four components in the Overarching Goal:  (1) how to encourage and measure critical thinking; (2) how to help students reach their full academic potential; (3) how to measure one year of academic growth; and (4) development of social skills for success in academics and the workplace.  Interpretations and indicators of the Overarching Goal were presented to the board and approved in summer 2010.  The board then directed the workgroup to develop interpretations and indicators for the Sections 2.1 through 2.6.  Interpretations and indicators for Sections 2.1, 2.2, 2.3, 2.4, and 2.6 were presented to and approved by the board during the 2010-11 school year.
