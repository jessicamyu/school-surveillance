4. Proposal to Utilize Revenue from Mesa Fund to Restore Visual and Performing Arts (VAPA) in 2012-13 Budget (Beiser and Jackson)

Jan 24, 2012 - Special Meeting, 9:30 A.M.

Action

RECOMMENDATION: Board Trustee Kevin Beiser proposes that the board direct the superintendent to utilize revenue from the Mesa Fund to restore Visual and Performing Arts (VAPA) in 2012-13 budget.

 

BACKGROUND: In the 2012-13 Budget Solutions and First Interim Financial Report approved by the board on December 13, 2011, the board approved a 50 percent reduction to the VAPA program in the amount of $1.58 million, 16 FTE.  The Mesa Fund currently has a balance of $2 million which is sufficient to fund the restoration of the 50 percent reduction of $1.58 million.
