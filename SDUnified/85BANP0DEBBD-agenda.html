Subject
A.02. Proposition S Board Priorities for Project Planning
Meeting
Mar 24, 2009 - 9:00 A.M. Special Meeting - Room 2226
Type
Information
This meeting will provide an opportunity for the board members to offer Proposition S project planning guidance to district Facilities Planning and Construction staff members.

General items of discussion will include general project prioritization, site selection priorities, business outreach planning, and discussion pertaining to the district staff’s proposed three year Proposition S planning and construction plan.

Specific items of discussion may include:
  • Program Alignment: The alignment of Proposition S funds with Board of Education program priorities. This discussion will give board members the opportunity to provide district staff with their insights into how best to link the execution of Proposition S projects to specific educational programmatic goals.
  • Sustainability: This discussion will afford board members the opportunity to provide district staff with leadership pertaining to issues of sustainability and compliance with California’s Collaboration for High Performance Schools.
  • Collaborative Planning: This discussion will provide opportunity for board members to give planning assistance pertaining to district collaborative planning, community development, and the use of the design task force planning model as well as the site master planning process.
  • Site Selection and Prioritization: This discussion will provide opportunity to discuss the district’s site prioritization algorithm, and the proposed three-year work plan.
  • Performance Metrics: This discussion will present the opportunity for board members to provide district staff with direction pertaining to progress reporting expectations and program performance metrics.
  • Outreach and Community Relations: This discussion will provide board members the opportunity to discuss Community Outreach Expectations and Community Relations priorities with district staff members.
