RECOMMENDATION: Approve the renewal of the charter of KIPP Adelante Preparatory Academy, a Charter School, for a five-year term from July 1, 2013, to June 30, 2018.

 

FISCAL IMPACT: None.

 

PRIOR YEAR FISCAL IMPACT: None.

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None.

 

CONSULTATION WITH BOARD ADVISORY COMMITTEE: Not applicable.

BACKGROUND: KIPP Adelante Preparatory Academy is a charter school serving Grades 5-8. The charter school is eligible for renewal based on its 37-point API growth from 767 to 804.  KIPP Adelante Preparatory Academy has been deemed as being on sound financial standing.  For the 2012-13 school year, the school is projecting a financial reserve of $3.1 million.  The charter school manages its resources prudently and is not at risk of not meeting its ongoing financial obligations.  Staff recommends the board approve the renewal of the charter of KIPP Adelante Preparatory Academy for a five-year term, from July 1, 2013, to June 30, 2018.

PRIOR YEAR FISCAL IMPACT: None.

BACKGROUND: The Preuss School UCSD is a charter school serving Grades 6-12.  Preuss is eligible for renewal based on obtaining a statewide rank of 10 and similar schools rank of 10 in the prior year.  Based on the projected enrollment, revised projected budget, expenses, and cash flow submitted in the renewal petition, Preuss has been found to be financially viable.  In addition, Preuss has maintained an adequate reserve for economic uncertainty of 3 percent or $50,000.  Staff recommends the Board of Education approve the renewal of the charter of Preuss for a five-year term, from July 1, 2013, to June 30, 2018.
