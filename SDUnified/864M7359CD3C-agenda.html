BACKGROUND: Resolutions in the matter of elimination of vacant classified positions: Office-Technical and Business Services (OTBS) Bargaining Unit; Operational Support Services (OSS) Bargaining Unit; and Paraeducators (PARA) Bargaining Unit; School Police Services (POA) Bargaining Unit; Confidentials (CONF); and Administrators Association of San Diego Classified Supervisors (SUPER) and Management (MGRS) Bargaining Unit are the result of budget reductions throughout the district.


Like other school districts and public agencies in our state, the district is faced with addressing reductions in financial resources and increasing costs. It follows that schools and central offices are impacted by dwindling revenue streams and have to make budget and staffing adjustments as necessary. It is extremely difficult for schools and central offices to make the necessary hard decisions concerning eliminating vacant positions. If the board does not approve these eliminations, approximately $3.7 million will need to be reinstated and these vacancies will be filled by employees that were affected on the April 27, 2010, resolution to eliminate filled positions.
