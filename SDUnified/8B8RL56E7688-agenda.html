RECOMMENDATION:  This item will provide the Board of Education with an update on the implementation of the Cluster Governance Model at each of the 16 clusters.

 

FISCAL IMPACT:  None.

 

PRIOR YEAR EXPENSE HISTORY:  None.

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING:  None.

 

BACKGROUND:  For the 2010-11 school year, the board approved the establishment of Area Superintendents to provide oversight and guidance to the 16 established K-12 clusters (Clairemont, Crawford, Henry, Hoover, Kearny, La Jolla, Lincoln, Madison, Mira Mesa, Mission Bay, Morse, Point Loma, San Diego, Scripps Ranch, Serra, and University City).  In 2010-11, the district adopted a Community-Based Reform Model to improve student achievement by empowering teachers, site leaders, students, and parents within each cluster to identify specific cluster-needs and develop strategies to address those needs.  This report provides an in-depth follow-up to the brief update presented to the board on November 30, 2010, on the work-to-date to develop, nurture, and increase leadership within each cluster. This report also includes results of the brainstorming sessions held by the San Diego Education Association (SDEA) as we partner to develop community schools
