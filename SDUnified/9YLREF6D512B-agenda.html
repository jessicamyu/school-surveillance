RECOMMENDATION: Recognize 2016 Classified Employee of the Year Honorees and Classified Employee of the Year Winner and Runner-Up.

 

BACKGROUND: The California Legislature and the Governor approved legislation in 1986 setting aside the third full week in May of each year as Classified School Employees Week. This year the week of May 15-21, 2016, has been designated Classified School Employees Week.

 

During this week, San Diego Unified celebrates its Classified Employees of the Year with a special event hosted off-site by the Communications and Human Resource Departments.

 

At this ceremony, all 2016 Classified Employee of the Year honorees are recognized, along with the Classified Employee of the Year winner and runner-up.
