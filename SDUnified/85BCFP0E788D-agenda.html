As part of Board Governance Policy GC-6, Governance Culture, Annual Work Plan, the board will continually monitor and review all of its policies. Operational Expectation OE-3, Treatment of Stakeholders, states that the superintendent shall maintain an organizational culture that treats all people, including members of the board, parents, community members, and staff, with respect, dignity, and courtesy.

We recommend approval of the revisions to Appendix 1 and authorization for the revised Appendix 1 to be sent to the San Diego County Board of Supervisors for their further review and consideration.

Under California Government Code section 87300 and following, local governmental agencies such as cities, counties and school districts are required to adopt and periodically update a Conflict of Interest Code which requires disclosure of certain financial interests on FPPC Form 700. The Board of Supervisors for each county serves as the code-reviewing body, and works with local agencies to ensure that the agency’s Conflict of Interest Code, the list of Designated Positions (Appendix 1 to the Code), and the Categories of Reportable Economic Interests (Appendix 2 to the Code) are reviewed and updated as necessary biennially during even-numbered years.

Since the last revision of Appendix 1 two years ago, a number of position changes have been made by the district. Attached is a draft proposed Appendix 1 which lists positions to be added to the list of required filers in bold and underlined, and designated positions to be deleted as lined out.

Actions to accept, budget, and spend income, in excess of that included in the adopted budget, are authorized by Education Code Section 42602. The counselors will consult and collaborate with teachers, administrators and families to assist all cohort students in their personal and academic success and to formalize post-secondary plans for each student. The mission of GEAR UP is to significantly increase the number of low-income students who are prepared to enter and succeed in post-secondary education.
