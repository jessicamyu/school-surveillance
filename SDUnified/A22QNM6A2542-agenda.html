3. Vision 2020 Quality Schools in Every Neighborhood: 2016-19 Local Control and Accountability Plan (LCAP) — Overview - 9:00 A.M.
Meeting
Jun 14, 2016 - Special Meeting, 9:00 A.M. Local Control Accountabiliy Plan (LCAP) 2016-19 Workshop
Type
Information

RECOMMENDATION: The superintendent and staff will provide information to the board on the implementation of Vision 2020 Quality Schools in Every Neighborhood and alignment with the proposed 2016-19 Local Control and Accountability Plan (LCAP). Each LCAP Goal will be reviewed, and plans for implementation of each goal in the 2016-17 school year will be presented.   

 

FISCAL IMPACT: None. 

 

PRIOR YEAR FISCAL IMPACT: None. 

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None. 

 

CONSULTATION WITH BOARD ADVISORY COMMITTEE: Not applicable.

 

BACKGROUND:  This workshop will include a review of Vision 2020 and LCAP implementation in the 2015-16 school year, and will provide board members and the public with the opportunity to learn about the district's plans for implementation of Vision 2020 and LCAP in the 2016-17 school year.
