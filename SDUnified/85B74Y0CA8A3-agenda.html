A. OPENING CEREMONY - AUDITORIUM
Subject
A.02. Hearing on San Diego State University Enrollment Policy Changes for Local San Diego Unified Students; BUDGETED: N/A
Meeting
Nov 9, 2009 - - Special Meeting, 4:00 P.M.
Type
Information, Public Hearing
RECOMMENDATION: Conduct hearing on San Diego State University enrollment policy changes affecting current San Diego Unified seniors.

FISCAL IMPACT: None.

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None.

BACKGROUND: Due to unprecedented budget cuts, San Diego State University has decreased their enrollment for the 2010-11 academic year by more than 4,500 students which will limit admission for students who reside within the local service area in order to maintain a ratio of half service area and half out-of-service area students on their campus. The board is urging San Diego State University to reconsider the changes to their admissions policy.
