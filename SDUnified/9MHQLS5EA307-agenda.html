3. ITEM WITHDRAWN: Presentation of 2015-16 Future Educators Scholarships, Mashin Scholarships, and Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math (STEM) Scholarships

May 5, 2015 - Special Meeting, Day of the Teacher, 5:00 P.M. (Reception at 4:15 P.M.)
Type
Information

RECOMMENDATION: Recognize the 2015-16 scholarship recipients—Future Educators Scholars and Mashin/Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math (STEM) Scholars.

 

BACKGROUND: Future Educator Scholars—The Future Educator Scholars Program is the major project of the San Diego Education fund, which was founded in 1954. Future Educator Scholars are aspiring teachers who receive five-year scholarships, along with mentoring and counseling, in exchange for agreeing to teach in San Diego Unified. Scholars are usually the first in their families to attend college and reflect the diversity of district schools.

 

Mashin and STEM Scholarships—Mashin scholarships are awarded in math and science while STEM scholarships are awarded to students planning to major in science, technology, engineering or mathematics, and attend a college or university in San Diego County. In addition to the scholarship funds, scholarship recipients receive counseling and mentoring from the San Diego Education Fund. The scholarships are generously provided through the Jack and Virginia Mashin Endowment.

4. Resolution in the Matter of Recognizing May 5, 2015, as San Diego Unified School District Day of the Teacher

RECOMMENDATION: Approve the resolution in the matter of recognizing May 5, 2015, as San Diego Unified School District Day of the Teacher.

 

BACKGROUND: Day of the Teacher/Teacher of the Year is an annual, San Diego Unified special event hosted offsite by the Communications Department, which is within the office of the Chief Public Information Officer, in collaboration with the San Diego Education Association (SDEA).

 

At this ceremony, all 2015 site Teacher of the Year honorees, district Teacher of the Year candidates, and district Teacher of the Year winners and runners-up will be recognized, along with this year's Future Educator Scholars, selected by the San Diego Education Fund, STEM Scholarship winners, and the district's recent National Board Certified teacher.
