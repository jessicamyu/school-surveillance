Approve the establishment of the Virtual High School as an online diploma program in 2008-09, with the intention of transitioning from a program to a separate high school entity in 2009-10, as outlined in this report. 

Approve a Second FirstAmendment to the district Agreement with APEX Learning to provide curriculum and instruction for enrolled virtual high school students in 2008-09, train district teachers to serve as online course instructors, and provide associated professional development.

Approve the adoption of the APEX course offerings as listed on Attachment 2 into the district Course of Study for use in the Virtual High School program and online learning.

The report on this item outlines a three-year road map for the establishment of the Virtual High School (VHS), a district online high school.  The VHS will offer an educational option for students who are not served in a traditional high school setting, are unable to attend school regularly due to extenuating circumstances, are home-schooled and need coursework their parents are not able to teach, need credit recovery, or want to accelerate their learning. The goal is to provide a rigorous, college-preparatory online school program that meets the needs of 21st century learners to expand post-secondary education options, and prepare graduates for successful careers in a competitive global marketplace.

A hallmark of this full-service program is that it not only provides online curriculum for a virtual high school but also serves students with independent study and/or credit recovery needs.  All enrolled students will be provided a portable learning device and broadband connectivity, ensuring equity of access to online learning.
