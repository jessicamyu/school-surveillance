RECOMMENDATION: Information only. This workshop provides an overview of and an opportunity to learn about the Local Control Funding Formula (LCFF), the state’s new school district funding method, within the San Diego Unified context.  Information and discussion will address how the district LCFF allocation from the state is determined and how the district funds can be used to achieve equity and excellence.
