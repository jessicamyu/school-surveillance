A.03. Update on 2009-10 Budget and Continuation of Board Discussion on the Development of the 2010-11 District Budget; BUDGETED: YES, ALL FUNDS (OE-7; OE-11)

Type
Action, Information
Fiscal Impact
Yes
RECOMMENDATION: Board members and the Superintendent will continue their February 16, 2010 discussion of the update on 2009-10 budget and development of the 2010-11 district budget. It is expected that the board at their regular meeting later today, February 23, 2010, will take actions to address the budget deficit and balance the 2010-11 district budget. The budget action item, H.03., will be heard at 5:00 p.m.; the board will hear public testimony at that time.

FISCAL IMPACT: None at this time. The final decisions and actions of the board to adopt a balanced budget for FY 2010-11 will have a significant impact.

PRIOR YEAR EXPENSE HISTORY: None.

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: To be determined based on final decisions and actions of the board.

BACKGROUND: On December 16, 2009, the board approved the priority based budgeting process for the development of the district's 2010-11 budget. On January 5, 2010, at a special workshop session, the board developed a list of proposed priorities for board consideration and action. At the January 12, 2010 regular board meeting, the board approved six budget priority areas. On February 9, 2010 and February 16, 2010, staff presented the Priority-Based Budgeting recommendations to the board.
