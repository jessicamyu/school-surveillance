3. Vision 2020 Quality Schools in Every Neighborhood: 2017-2020 Local Control and Accountability Plan (LCAP) — Overview - 9:00 A.M.
Meeting
Jun 7, 2018 - Special Meeting, 9:00 A.M. Local Control Accountability Plan (LCAP) Workshop
Type
Information

RECOMMENDATION: The superintendent and staff will provide information to the board on the implementation of Vision 2020 Quality Schools in Every Neighborhood and alignment with the proposed 2017-2020 Local Control and Accountability Plan (LCAP). Each LCAP Goal will be reviewed, and plans for implementation of each goal in the 2018-19 school year will be presented.  Budget Expenditures will be included in the LCAP document at the June 19, 2018 First Reading.

 

FISCAL IMPACT: None. 

 

PRIOR YEAR FISCAL IMPACT: None. 

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None. 

 

CONSULTATION WITH BOARD ADVISORY COMMITTEE: Not applicable.

 

BACKGROUND:  This workshop will provide board members and the public with the opportunity to learn about the district's plans for implementation of Vision 2020 and LCAP in the 2018-19 school year.  

 

[Originator/Contact:  Cindy Marten, Superintendent of Public Education, 619.725.5506]

 

*PowerPoint presentation and LCAP report will be available Monday, June 4, in the morning.
