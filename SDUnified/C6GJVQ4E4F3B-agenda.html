RECOMMENDATION: The Trustees will discuss the following Governance topics: 

 

  • Board Reports (Superintendent-Board Best Practices #7)
  • Code of Civil Discourse (Board Bylaw 1014)
  • Student Trustee Election Process (Board Bylaw 1035-F)
  • Composition of Advisory Committees (Governance document, GC-5)
  • Board Member Initiatives and Priorities (Governance document, B/SR-2, B/SR-4)
  • Trustee support services

 

FISCAL IMPACT: None. 

 

PRIOR YEAR FISCAL IMPACT: None.

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None.

 

CONSULTATION WITH BOARD ADVISORY COMMITTEE: Any recommended revisions to Advisory Committees will be shared with the Advisory Committee Chairpersons.

 

BACKGROUND: The Board meets semi-annually to discuss matters pertaining to Governance.
