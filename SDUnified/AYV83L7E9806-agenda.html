3. Vision 2020 Quality Schools in Every Neighborhood: 2017-2020 Local Control and Accountability Plan (LCAP) — Opening Comments/Overview - 12:00 P.M.
Meeting
Jun 12, 2019 - Special Meeting, 12:00 P.M. Local Control Accountability Plan (LCAP) Workshop
Type
Information

RECOMMENDATION: The superintendent and staff will provide information to the board on the implementation of Vision 2020 Quality Schools in Every Neighborhood and alignment with the proposed 2017-2020 Local Control and Accountability Plan (LCAP). Each LCAP Goal will be reviewed, and plans for implementation of each goal in the 2019-20 school year will be presented.  Budget Expenditures will be included in the LCAP document at the June 18, 2019 First Reading.

 

FISCAL IMPACT: None. 

 

PRIOR YEAR FISCAL IMPACT: None. 

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None. 

 

CONSULTATION WITH BOARD ADVISORY COMMITTEE: Not applicable.

 

BACKGROUND:  This workshop will provide board members and the public with the opportunity to learn about the district's plans for implementation of Vision 2020 and LCAP in the 2019-2020 school year.  

 

[Originator/Contact:  Cindy Marten, Superintendent of Public Education, 619.725.5506]
