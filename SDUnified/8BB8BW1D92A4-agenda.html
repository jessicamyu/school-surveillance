Meeting
Jan 4, 2011 - Regular Charter Meeting, 5:00 P.M.
Type
Information, Public Hearing

RECOMMENDATION: Conduct a public hearing for the renewal charter petition for King-Chavez Academy of Excellence Charter School.

 

FISCAL IMPACT: None.

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None.

PRIOR YEAR EXPENSE HISTORY: None.

BACKGROUND: Public hearing pursuant to Education Code sections 47605 and 47607 to consider the level of district teacher, other district employee, and parent/guardian support for the renewal charter petition submitted by King-Chavez Academy of Excellence Charter School petitioners. The attached include the charter petition and budget information.  The charter petition is being reviewed by district staff to determine whether it meets all operational and legal requirements for approval.

 

Staff will prepare a report with its analysis when the charter petition returns for board action.

C. Student Instructional Matters
Subject
3. Charter School Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) with O’Farrell Community School; BUDGETED: N/A (OE-2)
Meeting
Jan 4, 2011 - Regular Charter Meeting, 5:00 P.M.
Type
Action

RECOMMENDATION: Approve and adopt Charter School Memorandum of Understanding with O’Farrell Community School.

 

FISCAL IMPACT: None.

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None.

PRIOR YEAR EXPENSE HISTORY: None.

BACKGROUND:  On August 5, 2008, the Board of Education approved a template Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) setting forth the terms and conditions under which the district and district-authorized charter schools will interact with each other. The template MOU, developed with the cooperation of the charter schools, clarifies and supplements operational terms set forth in the charters of each of the charter schools.  The most recent charter school that signed the MOU is O’Farrell Community School.

 

The Office of Charter Schools recommends that the board approve entering into the MOU with O’Farrell Community School.
