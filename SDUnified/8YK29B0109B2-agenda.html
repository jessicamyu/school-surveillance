RECOMMENDATION:  Information only. This Spotlight on Success workshop will provide an overview of "what's working" at 13 district schools, and serve as the annual monitoring reports for GSA-2, Academics; GSA-3, Personal Development; GSA-4, Citizenship Development; and OE-2, Instructional Program.  The presentation brings together tenets of the Strategic Process and Local Education Agency (LEA) Plan; focuses on the academic priorities of Quality Teaching, Quality Leadership, Professional Learning for All Staff, and Access to Broad and Challenging Curriculum, and the district priorities of Professional Learning Communities, College and Career Readiness, and Parent and Community Engagement; highlights Academic Performance Index (API) growth; and the shares the best practices that are contributing to student success.
