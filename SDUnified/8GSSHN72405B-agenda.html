6. Board Direction to Staff to Conduct Districtwide Audit of All Certificated and Classified Positions (Evans)
Meeting
Jun 21, 2011 - REVISED Special Budget Meeting, 3:00 P.M.
Type
Action

Board Trustee John Evans will propose for board consideration and action direction to staff to conduct a districtwide audit of certificated and classified positions.

 

In tight budget times every position in the district must be carefully scrutinized.  It is proposed that early in the 2011-12 school year that the Superintendent conduct an audit of all certificated and classified positions in the district. This would be both a paper audit and an on-site physical audit.

 

On the certificated side higher priority is placed upon those employees who work directly with the students.  Secondary priority is to staff who indirectly aid student achievement through assisting other certificated staff.  Every position would be reviewed for its impact on student achievement.  The Superintendent and Deputy Superintendent Academics could assign the Area Superintendents, or other designees they so choose, to visit each school site to compare the position on paper with the actual functioning of each certificated employee at that school site. The most basic question is whether the employee is teaching students in the classroom, and if not, why not?

 

A similar audit on the classified side would be conducted by the Superintendent and Deputy Superintendent for Business and the senior managers that they designate to conduct the audit.

 

This report would be submitted to the board by October 31, 2011.
