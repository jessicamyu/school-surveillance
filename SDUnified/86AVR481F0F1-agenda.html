RECOMMENDATION: This report provides an update to the board on the development of Interpretations and Indicators for Goals for Student Achievement (GSA)-2, Academics. In early May, the Interim Deputy Superintendent convened a new GSA-2 workgroup that includes community representatives, elementary and secondary classroom teachers, resource teachers, elementary and secondary School Improvement Officers, and Chiefs. The workgroup has focused on the Interpretation of and Indicators for GSA-2's overarching goal, and has begun work on the Interpretations and Indicators for Sections 2.1-2.6. An update of the work to date is attached for board review.

 

The next steps in developing interpretations and indicators for GSA-2 are:

  • Collecting feedback from the board workshop
  • Continuing to meet with the new GSA-2 team on a regular basis (ongoing)
  • Continue to meet with Instructional Learning Teams at school sites to gather feedback on work in progress (ongoing)
  • Creating interpretations and indicators for GSA-2 sections 2.1 - 2.6 (June - July 2010)
  • Preparing updates as appropriate for the board and Superintendent (July 2010)

The new GSA-2 team will also be preparing the GSA-2, Academics and OE-2, Instructional Program monitoring reports for presentation to the board in October 2010.

 

FISCAL IMPACT: None.

 

PRIOR YEAR EXPENSE HISTORY: None.

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None.

 

BACKGROUND: Proposed Interpretations and Indicators for GSA-2 were submitted for board approval on January 19, 2010 (item A.03.), March 2, 2010 (item A.05.), and March 16, 2010 (item A.07.). The board directed staff to continue making revisions to the proposed document.
