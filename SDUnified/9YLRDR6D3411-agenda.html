RECOMMENDATION: Recognize the 2016-17 scholarship recipients—Future Educators Scholars and Mashin/Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math (STEM) Scholars.

 

BACKGROUND: Future Educator Scholars—The Future Educator Scholars Program is the major project of the San Diego Education fund, which was founded in 1954. Future Educator Scholars are aspiring teachers who receive five-year scholarships, along with mentoring and counseling, in exchange for agreeing to teach in San Diego Unified. Scholars are usually the first in their families to attend college and reflect the diversity of district schools.

 

Mashin and STEM Scholarships—Mashin scholarships are awarded in math and science while STEM scholarships are awarded to students planning to major in science, technology, engineering or mathematics, and attend a college or university in San Diego County. RECOMMENDATION: Recognize 2016 Site Teacher of the Year Honorees, Teacher of the Year Candidates, and Teacher of the Year Winners and Runners-Up.

 

BACKGROUND: On April 26, 2016, the board approved a resolution recognizing May 3, 2016, as San Diego Unified School District Day of the Teacher.

 

Day of the Teacher/Teacher of the Year is an annual, San Diego Unified special event hosted offsite by the Communications Department, which is within the office of the Chief Public Information Officer, in collaboration with the San Diego Education Association (SDEA).

 

At this ceremony, all 2016 site Teacher of the Year honorees, district Teacher of the Year candidates, and district Teacher of the Year winners and runners-up will be recognized, along with this year's Future Educator Scholars, selected by the San Diego Education Fund and STEM Scholarship winners.
