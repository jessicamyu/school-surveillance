RECOMMENDATION:  Approve interpretations and indicators for remaining sections of GSA-2 Academics (Section 2.2 - Achievement; Section 2.3.2 - Math; Section 2.3.3 - Science; Section 2.4 - Two Languages; and Section 2.6 - Technology). 

 

FISCAL IMPACT:  None.

 

PRIOR YEAR FISCAL IMPACT:  None.

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: A workgroup consisting of approximately 30 people has been working since spring 2010 on developing the GSA-2 Interpretations and Indicators.  This group consists of teachers, administrators, area superintendents, instructional support services staff, and community members.  Meetings are generally held after school hours to ensure that classroom staff and community members have the opportunity to participate.  Substitutes are provided for any meetings/workshops held during school hours.   

 

CONSULTATION WITH BOARD ADVISORY COMMITTEE:  Not applicable.

 

BACKGROUND:  In fall 2009, under the guidance of the Aspen Group, a team comprised of central office leaders was created to address the interpretations of the board's academic goals and develop Indicators that show the district's progress in meeting the goals.  In spring 2010, as a result of board feedback on the proposed interpretations and indicators, a new workgroup was created that began building upon and expanding the work of the original team.  

 

The new workgroup identified four components in the Overarching Goal (1) how to encourage and measure critical thinking; (2) how to help students reach their full academic potential; (3) how to measure one year of academic growth; and (4) development of social skills for success in academics and the workplace.  Interpretations and Indicators of the Overarching Goal were presented to the board and approved in summer 2010.  The board then directed the workgroup to develop interpretations and indicators for English/Language Arts (Section 2.3.1).  A subgroup met during fall 2010 to develop the English/Language Arts Interpretations and Indicators, which were approved by the board in November 2010.  The board then authorized the workgroup to continue preparing interpretations and indicators for additional sections.  Interpretations and indicators for Section 2.1 - High Expectations and Section 2.3.4 - Social Studies were presented to and approved by the board on February 22, 2011.  The workgroup has continued its subgroup work throughout spring 2011 to develop Interpretations and Indicators for the remaining GSA-2 sections, which are presented to the board for review and approval today.

 

Interpretations and indicators for Section 2.5 (Fine and Practical Arts) will be presented to the board for approval in fall 2011.

RECOMMENDATION:  Informational item on the plan for development of Interpretations and Indicators for Goals for Student Achievement (GSA)-3 Personal Development, and Goals for Student Achievement (GSA)-4 Citizenship Development.   In summer 2011, workgroups will be established to develop interpretations and indicators for GSA-3 and GSA-4.  In accordance with the board's annual work plan schedule, interpretations and indicators for GSA-3 will be presented to the board for review and approval in November 2011, and interpretations and indicators for GSA-4 will be presented to the board for review and approval in October 2011. 

 

FISCAL IMPACT:  None.

 

PRIOR YEAR FISCAL IMPACT:  None.

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING:  None.

 

CONSULTATION WITH BOARD ADVISORY COMMITTEE:  Not applicable.

 

BACKGROUND:  The Board Governance Policies adopted September 9, 2008, and re-adopted September 29, 2009, and March 1, 2011, include Goals for Student Achievement (GSA)-3 Personal Development, and Goals for Student Achievement (GSA)-4 Citizenship Development.  Preliminary work relating to both GSA-3 and GSA-4 has begun as a result of work on the GSA-2 overarching goal.  This work was presented to the board for information on February 22, 2011, in the form of the "7 Steps to Personal and Social Success," "7 Steps to Career and College Success," and "Student Success Model Dashboard" for elementary and secondary (all documents attached).
