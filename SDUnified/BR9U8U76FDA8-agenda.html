INFORMATION: LCAP Goal 3 — Accelerating Learning with High Expectations for All.  

 

RECOMMENDATION: Approve the Expanded Learning Opportunity (ELO) Grant Plan to support summer programming and extended school day services.

 

FISCAL IMPACT: Total estimated revenue amount: $77,753,383.00. 

Revenue Budget String:

0000-74250-00-8590-0000-0000-01000-0000, $70,684,945.00

0000-74260-00-8590-0000-0000-01000-0000, $7,068,438.00

 

PRIOR YEAR FISCAL IMPACT: None. 

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None. 

 

CONSULTATION WITH BOARD ADVISORY COMMITTEE: Not applicable.

 

BACKGROUND:Per State reporting requirements, districts must indicate how Expanded Learning Opportunities (ELO) funds will be utilized to support student learning starting in June 2021 through August 2022. San Diego Unified plans to use ELO funds on opportunities that accelerate learning, enhance connections to the school community, and reignite passion for learning through the following programs and services:

  1. Expanded summer programs encompassing both academics and enrichment for K-12

  2. Learning acceleration extended day classes

  3. Multilingual Tutoring programs

  4. Summer bridging programs for transition years

Priority placement into ELO funded programs will be based on academic need, multilingual learner English proficiency level, IEP goals, and homeless and foster youth status. Special education staff will be provided for students with IEPs based on needs to ensure access and support during expanded learning programs.
