RECOMMENDATION: Staff recommends the board non-renew the renewal charter petition for Nubia Leadership Academy Charter School (Nubia).

 

Alternatively, based on board direction at the May 4, 2012, board meeting, Nubia agreed to submit a revised charter petition to reflect the commitments they are making in the following areas:

  • Demonstrate academic growth via the API score through meeting the API Target Growth.  This will be analyzed annually for the next three years (2012-13, 2013-14, and 2014-15);
  • Convert to 501c3 status; and
  • Modify the governance procedure to include a democratically-elected board process.

FISCAL IMPACT: None.

 

PRIOR YEAR FISCAL IMPACT: None.

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING:  None.

 

CONSULTATION WITH BOARD ADVISORY COMMITTEE: Not applicable.

 

BACKGROUND: The meeting on Tuesday, May 1, 2012, to consider the renewal of the charter petition for Nubia Leadership Academy Charter School, was adjourned and will be re-considered at a special meeting on May 4, 2012.
