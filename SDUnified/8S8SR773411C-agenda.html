RECOMMENDATION:  Staff will provide an update to the board on the 2012-13 planned implementation of the Common Core State Standards adopted by the State of California in 2010.  The Common Core State Standards have been adopted by 45 states as a set of shared national standards to ensure that students nationwide are held to the same level of expectation, are able to compete with students in the highest performing countries, are prepared for post-secondary education, and are prepared for the global arena. Information only.

 

Inclusive of rigorous content and applied knowledge, as well as research and evidence-based, the Common Core State Standards are aligned with the district's college and career readiness goals, Vision 2020, and the Strategic Process. The update also includes sample "next generation" assessment questions.
