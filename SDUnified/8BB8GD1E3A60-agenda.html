RECOMMENDATION: Approve the charter petition for City Heights Prep Charter School (City Heights Prep) for a five-year school term beginning on July 1, 2011, and expiring on June 30, 2016.   The Office of Charter Schools recommends the adoption of this staff report as findings in support of the board’s decision to grant the charter petition.  City Heights Prep petitioners submitted their petition for review to the Office of Charter Schools on November 5, 2010.  A public hearing for the petition was held on December 7, 2010.  The approval of the charter petition shall become effective July 1, 2011, and shall expire on June 30, 2016.  

 

If the Board of Education desires additional options beyond approval, these options could be:

 

- Approve the charter petition with conditions. In essence, the petition is approved with conditions set by the Board of Education.

 

- Deny the charter petition.  The board would need to identify written findings to support a decision to deny the charter petition.

 

FISCAL IMPACT: None.

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None.

 

PRIOR YEAR EXPENSE HISTORY: None.

 

 

BACKGROUND: Charter Petition Review Summary: The proposed mission of the school is the following, “to provide an excellent neighborhood based and college preparatory education for middle and high school students in City Heights”  Based on a departmental staff and legal review of the original charter petition submitted on November 5, 2010, deficiencies and concerns were identified and district staff met with the petitioners to provide feedback and share deficiencies and concerns with charter petitioners.  The approved petition is effective July 1, 2011 and expires on June 30, 2016. 

 

The City Heights Prep charter petition proposes to operate a charter school serving Grades 6 through 12 in the City Heights neighborhood of San Diego. The charter petition indicates the school will be part of the district's Special Education Plan Area (SELPA), but reserves the right to join another SELPA later in the life of the charter.
