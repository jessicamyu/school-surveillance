RECOMMENDATION: Approve the Purchase and Sale Agreement with high bidder Edward and Barbara Malone, under declaration of trust (UDT) dated December 14, 1972, for the sale of Hale Junior High, district-owned property.  Direct staff to enter into a negotiation/escrow period with the selected bidder.  ITEM REQUIRES 4 VOTES.

 

FISCAL IMPACT: Upon close of escrow, total potential income received by the district will be $23,520,000.00, less title and escrow.

 

PRIOR YEAR FISCAL IMPACT: $1,480,076.60 in annual lease income and $166,626.15 in annual maintenance expense, which includes $128,662.17, the cost of a major infrastructure plumbing upgrade to replace collapsed orange berg pipes.

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None.

 

CONSULTATION WITH BOARD ADVISORY COMMITTEE: The Advisory Committee on Utilization of Excess School Property was consulted on May 23, 2013, and supports their recommendations for each property as delineated in the Property Management Plan Exhibit C, approved on June 18, 2013.  Exhibit C is attached.

 

BACKGROUND: On March 26, 2013, the board approved a resolution of intent to sell Hale Junior High School.  District staff first offered the property to public agencies and other entities as required by Education Code Section 17464.  On March 6, 2013, Horizon Christian Fellowship submitted a letter of interest in acquiring the site.  Negotiations did not result in a sale of the property.  On October 8, 2013, the board approved a revised resolution of intent to sell Hale Junior High.  On October 24, 2013, a bid opening occurred.  The bidder with the highest bid and terms most beneficial to the district was determined to be Edward and Barbara Malone, UDT dated December 14, 1972. 

 

Approval of this agenda item will authorize the Director, Special Projects, or her designee, to execute any documents in connection herewith.
