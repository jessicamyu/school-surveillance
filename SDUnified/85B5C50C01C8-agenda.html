A.03. Demonstration of "EXCEED" Student Achievement Applications Software for Management of Special Education Processes: BUDGETED: N/A (OE-2)
Meeting
Mar 30, 2010 - - Board Workshop, 11:00 A.M.
Type
Information
RECOMMENDATION: Overview and demonstration of Spectrum K-12 "EXCEED" Student Achievement Management System to replace the outdated "Encore" Suite.

The contract for Board consideration and action is on the Board's regular meeting agenda today, March 30, 2010, item I.16., Agreement with Sprectrum K-12 School Solutions, Inc. for Purchase of "EXCEED" Student Achievement Management Software Suite

FISCAL IMPACT: None. This item at the workshop is for information only. Contract is on the regular meeting agenda at March 30, 2010, 5:00 session.

PRIOR YEAR EXPENSE HISTORY: None.

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None.

BACKGROUND: EXCEED IEP (Individual Education Plan/Special Education) is designed to replace the outdated "Encore" Suite and will drive the day-to-day activities, timelines, tasks, rules, forms, and the reporting necessary to manage the special education process. The districtwide deployment of EXCEED IEP will assist in meeting the requirements for student evaluations and assessments for which special education and related services are provided or considered. EXCEED IEP will manage user caseloads, student profiles, student IEP summaries, student IEP histories, placements evaluations, assessments, contacts, testing, and progress monitoring to enable IEP teams to make data-driven decisions regarding student goals and objectives. EXCEED IEP also will use codes and forms that are automatically mapped to meet the requirements for state reporting.

EXCEED RTI (Response to Intervention) will be a new addition to the district's student achievement plan and will drive the day-to-day instructional activities, timelines, tasks, rules, forms and reporting. In addition, EXCEED RTI will guide instructional interventions as well as the response to intervention process in general education settings. EXCEED RTI will monitor academic and behavioral progress and uncover academic concern gaps at the student, grade, classroom, group and/or district levels. EXCEED RTI enables district staff and service providers to make data-driven decisions with district-approved strategies and recommendations based on student needs. Additionally, EXCEED RTI can monitor the interventions, progress, supervision, and overall effectiveness of programs and strategies for closing the achievement gap. EXCEED RTI also can provide information to students and parents so they are continually aware of the intervention process and are able to monitor the effects of academic and behavioral interventions.
