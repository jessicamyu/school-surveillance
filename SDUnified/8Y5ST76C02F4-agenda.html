RECOMMENDATION: Adoption of the Tentative Agreement with Administrators Association San Diego City Schools (AASD) Certificated Bargaining Unit, pending review by the San Diego County Office of Education (SDCOE), regarding the 2012-13 AASD Certificated layoffs, in accordance with Section 3547.5 of the California Government Code and Section 1031 of the Bylaws of the Board of Education.

 

FISCAL IMPACT: Report to be provided in the format adopted under Government Code section 3547.5(a).

 

PRIOR YEAR FISCAL IMPACT: None.

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: Rescind notice of layoffs and reduction in hours of the AASD Certificated Bargaining Unit members.

 

CONSULTATION WITH BOARD ADVISORY COMMITTEE: Not applicable.

 

BACKGROUND: The AASD Certificated Bargaining Unit and the district met on many dates to bargain the 2012-13 layoffs of AASD Certificated Bargaining Unit members. AASD and the district bargained and reached agreement during the course of bargaining on specific items. The Tentative Agreement reached was forwarded to the San Diego County Office of Education (SDCOE) for review. SDCOE has since reviewed and accepted the AASD Tentative Agreement.
