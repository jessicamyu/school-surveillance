RECOMMENDATION:  This workshop will provide the board members with an update on the work to date on developing Interpretations and Indicators for GSA-2, Academics.  It is recommended that the board approve the Interpretations and Indicators presented in Section 2.3.1, English Language Arts.

 

FISCAL IMPACT:  None.

 

PRIOR YEAR EXPENSE HISTORY:  None.

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING:  A workgroup consisting of 30 people has been working since spring 2010 on developing the GSA-2 Interpretations and Indicators.  This group consists of teachers, administrators, area superintendents, curriculum and instruction staff, students and community members.  Meetings have generally been held after school hours to ensure that students, classroom staff and community members have the opportunity to participate.  Substitutes have been provided for any meetings/workshops held during school hours.  A roster of the workgroup members is attached.

 

BACKGROUND:   In fall 2009, under the guidance of the Aspen Group, a team comprised of curriculum leaders was created to address the Interpretations of the board's academic goals and develop Indicators that show the district's progress in meeting the goals.  In spring 2010, as a result of board feedback on the proposed Interpretations and Indicators, a new workgroup was created that began building upon and expanding the work of the original team.  

 

The new workgroup identified four components in the Overarching Goal:  (1) how to encourage and measure critical thinking; (2) how to reach full academic potential; (3) how to measure one year of academic growth; and (4) development of social skills for success in academics and the workplace.  Interpretations and Indicators of the Overarching Goal were presented to the board in workshops held during June and July, 2010. The board gave direction to the workgroup to continue on this path and develop Interpretations and Indicators for English/Language Arts (Section 2.3).  A subgroup met during August and September to develop the English/Language Arts Interpretations and Indicators, and presented a draft to the full workgroup on October 6, 2010.  The subgroup gathered feedback and suggestions from the full workgroup and revised the Interpretations and Indicators that are being presented to the board for review and approval in this workshop.
