RECOMMENDATION: Approve the resolution recognizing May 18-22, 2015, as San Diego Unified School District Classified School Employees Week.

 

FISCAL IMPACT: None.

 

PRIOR YEAR FISCAL IMPACT: None. 

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None. 

 

CONSULTATION WITH BOARD ADVISORY COMMITTEE: Not applicable.

 

BACKGROUND: The California Legislature and the Governor approved legislation in 1986 setting aside the third full week in May of each year as Classified School Employees Week.

 

During this week, San Diego Unified celebrates its Classified Employees of the Year with a special event hosted off-site by the Communications and Human Resource Departments.

 

At this ceremony, all 2015 Classified Employee of the Year honorees are recognized, along with the Classified Employee of the Year winner and runner-up.
