1. Charter Renewal Petition for Einstein Academy Charter Middle School; BUDGETED: N/A (OE-2)
Meeting
May 3, 2011 - Regular Charter Meeting, 2:00 P.M.
Type
Action
Fiscal Impact
No

RECOMMENDATION: Approve the petition to renew the Albert Einstein Academy Charter Middle School (Einstein Middle) for a five-year term from July 1, 2011 to June 30, 2016.

 

If the board desires additional options beyond approval, these options include:

  • Approve the petition with conditions.
  • Deny the petition. The board must adopt written findings to support a decision to deny the petition.

FISCAL IMPACT: None.

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None.

 

PRIOR YEAR EXPENSE HISTORY: None.

 

BACKGROUND: Einstein Middle opened in September 2006, and currently serves approximately 280 students in Grades 6-8. Einstein Middle is co-located on a district site with another charter school, Einstein Elementary at 3035 Ash Street, San Diego, California, 92102, through a facilities agreement.  To be eligible for renewal, a charter school must meet at least one of the following criteria:

  1. Attained its Academic Performance Index (API) growth target in the prior year or in two of the last three years, or in the aggregate for the prior three years.
  2. Attained a statewide rank of 4 or higher in the prior year or in two of the last three years.
  3. Attained a similar school rank of 4 or higher in the prior year or in two of the last three years.
  4. The academic performance of the charter school is at least equal to the performance of the public schools that the charter school pupils would otherwise have been required to attend, as well as the district schools in which the charter school is located.
  5. Has qualified for an alternative accountability system.  

Einstein Middle met the academic performance criteria to be eligible for renewal.  In 2009-10, Einstein Middle exceeded its growth target with a 62-point increase in its API from 793 to 855 and attained a statewide rank of 7.  Einstein Middle is not in Program Improvement (PI) status.  A more detailed description of Einstein Middle's academic performance is included in the attached board report.

 

Staff recommends the board approve the Einstein Middle petition for renewal for a five-year term from July 1, 2011 to June 30, 2016.

Subject
1. Formation of Ad-Hoc Committee on Charter Policies and Procedures (Barnett and Barrera)
Meeting
May 3, 2011 - Regular Charter Meeting, 2:00 P.M.
Type
Action
Fiscal Impact
No

RECOMMENDATION: Direct the superintendent to form an Ad Hoc Committee to review charter policies and procedures.

The Ad Hoc Committee would include two members of the Board of Education (Barrera and Barnett), selected members of the charter school community including the Charter Principals Advisory group, and district staff including area superintendent(s) from districts with a large number of charter schools.

The committee should return to the board with recommendations by November 2011.

The Ad Hoc committee will examine issues, including but not limited to:

 

·        New charters, including term of new charters and criteria for approval

·        Charter renewals

·        Charter oversight

·        District resources for administering charters

·        Charters on excess district property, rent, and co-location issues

·        Dispute resolution

·        Financial viability

·        Academic plans

·        Other
 

BACKGROUND: The last several months have demonstrated the need to review existing charter policies and analyze various issues to ensure not only compliance with Prop 39, but develop procedures and policies to ensure the greatest likelihood of the success of charter schools.
