In accordance with Administrative Procedure 3105 and the Board of Education (BOE) directive on February 24, 2004, the Property Inventory and Property Management Plan (PMP) for Selected Schools is reviewed annually by district staff and the Advisory Committee on the Utilization of Excess School Property (ACUESP) to determine if any revisions are necessary. The PMP was last amended and approved by the board on January 22, 2008. The proposed and unmodified Property Inventory and Property Management Plan for Selected School Sites is provided as Exhibit “A”. Exhibit “B” is a map showing the Board of Education districts and the excess school properties within each district. Exhibit “C” is a summary of ACUESP’s thoughts and comments gained from committee discussions and site visits. The PMP and Summary of Comments (SOC) were recently reviewed by the ACUESP, which took into account demographic and other information provided by staff. Each site was evaluated and discussed to determine if the current designations were still appropriate. ACUESP members toured each site location and the SOC was updated as necessary. As part of the review, teams of committee members visited the sites and the surrounding neighborhoods and presented their findings and recommendations to the all ACUESP members. The members, in turn, discussed the findings and recommendations for each site and ultimately there were no recommendations to change any of the site designations to the PMP, however many of the comments on the SOC, were updated to reflect the current use and to reflect updated information shared by staff. All committee deliberations were conducted at properly noticed public meetings.
