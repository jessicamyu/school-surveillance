RECOMMENDATION: Hold a public hearing regarding the renewal of the charter of Albert Einstein Academy Charter Middle School, including grade level changes and a name change. The school is seeking a renewal of its charter for a five-year term beginning July 1, 2021, and ending June 30, 2026

 

FISCAL IMPACT: None. 

 

PRIOR YEAR FISCAL IMPACT: None. 

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None.

 

CONSULTATION WITH BOARD ADVISORY COMMITTEE: Not applicable.

 

BACKGROUND: Albert Einstein Academy Charter Middle School (“AEACMS”) is operated by the nonprofit public benefit corporation, Albert Einstein Academies (“AEA”), pursuant to Education Code section 47604. AEA also operates Albert Einstein Academy Charter Elementary School. As part of this renewal, AEA is seeking to “merge” its elementary school into its middle school, and change the name of the charter school to Albert Einstein Academies. In addition, a material revision to add grades 9-12 to the charter has been submitted concurrently with the charter renewal. The school is seeking a renewal of its charter for a five-year term beginning July 1, 2021, and ending June 30, 2026. The public hearing is to consider the level of support for the school. RECOMMENDATION: Review the Implementation Plan for Assembly Bill 1505 regarding charter schools.

 

FISCAL IMPACT: None. 

 

PRIOR YEAR FISCAL IMPACT: None. 

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None. 

 

CONSULTATION WITH BOARD ADVISORY COMMITTEE: Not applicable.

 

BACKGROUND: Several new laws affecting charter schools and charter school authorizers were signed by Governor Newsom in 2019, including Senate Bill (“SB”) 75, SB 126, Assembly Bill (“AB”) 1505 and AB 1507. These new laws, particularly AB 1505, represent some of the most significant and comprehensive changes to charter school legislation since the original Charter Schools Act of 1992.

 

In response to these legislative changes, on July 28, 2020, the San Diego Unified School District Board of Education adopted a resolution requesting that the Superintendent bring forward an AB 1505 Implementation Plan within 90 days.  The attached document is a first draft of the Implementation Plan.
