C. STUDENT INSTRUCTIONAL MATTERS
Subject
C.01. Charter School Oversight; BUDGETED: N/A (OE-2; OE-7)
Meeting
Nov 10, 2009 - - Special Meeting, 8:30 A.M.
Type
Information
RECOMMENDATION: The Office of Charter Schools is seeking guidance from the Board of Education for their vision of charter school oversight.  There are currently 37 operational charter schools in the San Diego Unified School District and eight charter schools will seek renewal in the 2009-10 school year.  The Office of Charter Schools will present a synopsis of the academic and financial performance of all 37 charter schools, present innovative components of our charter schools, present a summary of oversight activities, including new and renewal petition review, and present a summary of major accomplishments and collaboration between the district and charter schools.

FISCAL IMPACT: None, information only; no action required.  

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None.

BACKGROUND: The Office of Charter Schools will present eight charter petition renewals to the Board of Education in 2009-10.  Prior to presenting these items to the board, the Office of Charter Schools wishes to share information pertaining to the oversight and performance of all charter schools.

Subject
C.02. Resolution in Support of Innovations Academy Charter School's Intent to File Application for School Facility Program Project Funds; BUDGETED: N/A (OE-9)
Meeting
Nov 10, 2009 - - Special Meeting, 8:30 A.M.
Type
Action
Fiscal Impact
No
RECOMMENDATION: Adopt Resolution in the Matter of Innovations Academy Charter School, a charter school residing within San Diego Unified School District boundaries, who is requesting funding from the School Facility Program Project

FISCAL IMPACT: This resolution has no fiscal impact. The charter school identified is seeking funds from the state to participate in a facility program independent of the district.

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING:

BACKGROUND: On August 27, 2009 the Office of Charter Schools received a request to file an updated Form SAB 50-01 and a district board-adopted resolution certifying the number of district, unhoused pupils that this project will house from Innovations Academy. The Office of Charter Schools has collaborated with the Director of Planning in the Facilities, Planning, and Construction Office to provide this school with the requested documentation and certifies that there are no unhoused pupils in the Kearny High School area. The adoption of this resolution fulfills the district's requirement for the request made from Innovations Academy.
