C. Board Workshop: Freedom Summer 2020: Building Anti-Racist School Communities
Subject
1. Board Discussion as Outlined in the June 23, 2020 Resolution
Meeting
Jul 21, 2020 - Special Board Workshop, 3:30 p.m.
Type
Action, Information

BACKGROUND:  On June 23, 2020 the Board of Education adopted a Resolution in the Matter of Supporting a Call for Freedom Summer 2020 and focus on eliminating those barriers that prevent our students of color from receiving an equitable education. Under the direction of the Superintendent, led by the Division specifically charged with advocating for our students of color, the Department of Youth Advocacy will collaborate with students to plan for a Board workshop during the month of July on the issue of racial justice and equity. The focus of the workshop will be on reviewing and strengthening the district's key strategies in achieving racial justice and equity, with an emphasis on student voice as we build anti-racist and restorative school communities. The Freedom Summer workshop will include a review of relevant data reflecting our progress at eliminating education barriers based on race with an emphasis on student voices and action steps moving forward. The topics to be reviewed should include:

  • Staff Diversity
  • Implementation of Ethnic Studies for All Students
  • Interrupting Discriminatory Grading Practices
  • Dismantling Discriminatory Discipline Policies and Practices
  • Building Anti-Racist and Restorative School Communities
