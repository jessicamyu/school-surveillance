RECOMMENDATION:  Establish an Operations and Efficiency Ad Hoc Committee and appoint Board members Scott Barnett and Kevin Beiser to the ad hoc committee.

 

The ad hoc committee will be charged with the task of exploring all opportunities to increase efficiency and budgetary savings in district operations and programs.  The ad hoc committee will work with district department managers and staff, employees, stakeholders and will seek input from the public.  The ad hoc committee will provide periodic reports with recommendations to the Board and Superintendent until the review is complete.  

 

FISCAL IMPACT:  None at this time.  The intent of the ad hoc committee will be to bring forward recommendations that will provide budgetary savings in district operations and programs.

 

PRIOR YEAR EXPENSE HISTORY:  None

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING:  None at this time
