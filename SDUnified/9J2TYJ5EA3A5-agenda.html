BACKGROUND: Groundwork San Diego-Chollas Creek (Groundwork) is a non-profit environmental organization formed to implement environmental, social, and economic development objectives as described in the Chollas Creek Enhancement Program adopted by the City of San Diego in 2002.

 

Educational Partnership

 

Since 2007, Groundwork has worked with the district and Millennial Tech Middle School with the goal of creating a fully-functioning, outdoor learning lab as the center of academic and community watershed education in the Lincoln cluster. The EarthLab currently serves more than 1,000 third through twelvth grade students per year, and seeks to grow to operate seven days a week, serving educators, students, and families in Subdistrict E and ultimately, the region.

 

EarthLab’s informal science learning is a vital component of Common Core and Next Generation Science Standards, and integrates with formal classroom instruction. Continuity of learning between grades is encouraged as students from Lincoln cluster elementary, middle, and high schools all study at the EarthLab. EarthLab also encourages the empowerment of students and families to direct their own learning, and to create a dynamic school culture. EarthLab supports a number of Vision 2020 goals.

 

License Agreement

 

On September 14, 2010, Groundwork and the district entered into a Memorandum of Understanding to master plan the open space parcel adjacent to Millennial Tech Middle School to support environmental education programs for its students and faculty. On April 4, 2012, the board approved a license agreement with Groundwork to provide for the development, maintenance, and other terms for the project and educational programs.  This proposed License Agreement supersedes the previous agreements of the Parties and establishes a long-term partnership between the district and Groundwork for development and operation of the EarthLab.  At this point, the parties have developed a preliminary site master plan.  This License Agreement establishes a framework for continued design development and eventual construction of the EarthLab, subject to further agreement of the parties. 

 

Site Master Plan

 

Coordinated master planning for the Earth Lab and Millennial Tech Middle based on stakeholder involvement and community engagement has resulted in a vision for shared facilities, state-of-the-art demonstration technologies, and united district/industry/higher education resources to bring 21st century STEAM learning to students.
