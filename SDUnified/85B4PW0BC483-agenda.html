RECOMMENDATION: Approve the resolution in the matter of the recognition of May 4, 2010, as San Diego Unified School District's Day of the Teacher.

FISCAL IMPACT: None.

PRIOR YEAR EXPENSE HISTORY: None.

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING: None.

BACKGROUND: Day of the Teacher/Teacher of the Year is an annual San Diego Unified School District Special Event hosted offsite by the Communications Department of the District Relations Division, in collaboration with the San Diego Education Association (SDEA).

At this ceremony, all site nominees, Teacher of the Year Candidates, and district Teacher of the Year winners will be recognized. This year's Future Educator Scholars selected by the San Diego Education Fund, and recent National Board Certified Teachers will also be recognized.
