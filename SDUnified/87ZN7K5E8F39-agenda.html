RECOMMENDATION: Adoption of the resolutions in the matter of the reduction or elimination of classified services, in the Office- Technical and Business Services (OTBS) and Paraeducators (PARA) Bargaining Units, effective end of day September 30, 2010.

 

FISCAL IMPACT: The Finance Division has calculated an estimated savings of $736,396 $940,795 for salary and benefits:

 

CBET                         $36,938

SANDAPP                $903,857

Total Savings          $940,795

 

However, if employees are bumped into lower level positions, their current salary will be in place for 18 months which will delay the savings. Actual savings cannot be determined until bumping is complete and layoffs have taken place.

 

IMPACT TO DISTRICT STAFFING:These resolutions reduce and eliminate vacant and filled positions that were identified by school sites and departments. The positions are as follows:

BACKGROUND: Resolutions in the matter of reduction and elimination of classified positions in the Office–Technical and Business Services (OTBS) and Paraeducators (PARA) Bargaining Units are the results of the Board approved reduction of services in the Community Based English Tutoring Program (CBET), and the loss of funding for the San Diego Adolescent Pregnancy and Parenting Program (SANDAPP).

 

Funding for SANDAPP from the First Five Commission of San Diego County has not yet been granted. Should the district receive an award the employees will be recalled in seniority order.

 

Overall, these reductions/eliminations are a result of reductions in funding for public education across the state and nation. The board has had to make difficult decisions regarding program changes and reorganizations. The CBET reduction is a result of a board-approved budget reduction for 2010-11. These recommendations will complete the classified employee changes for the beginning of 2010-11
