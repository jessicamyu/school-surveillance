EFMP Virtual Public Hearing
Wednesday, April 28 • 7 p.m.
Prince George’s County Public Schools (PGCPS) invites students, families and community members to call in and provide testimony about the FY2022 Educational Facilities Master Plan (EFMP). The EFMP is used to develop the annual and six-year Capital Improvement Plans (CIP).
To participate in the hearing and/or provide testimony, call 844-734-8766. To comment during the meeting, dial 0. (Speakers are limited to three minutes each.) Written comments can also be emailed to the Board of Education at testimony@pgcps.org.
For more information about the FY2022 EFMP, visit the Capital Programs EFMP website or submit questions to capital.programs@pgcps.org.
