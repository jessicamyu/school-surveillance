Motion & Voting


Ms. Fletcher read the following statement:
Mr. Secretary (Dr. Deasy), thank you, I want to take this opportunity to enthusiastically nominate Verjeana Jacobs as Chair of the Prince George's County Board of Education…If I can Mr. Secretary (Dr. Deasy), I would like to remind my colleagues and share with the viewing public, some of the overwhelming reasons why Verjeana Jacobs will serve us well as Chair of the Board of Education: Ms. Jacobs is a graduate of our school system (Bladensburg High School), she cares about the success of our school District; Her broad range of experiences as an attorney, an educator, public safety professional and elected official, demonstrates her ability to achieve excellence; She is a former union president – she understands and appreciates the value of our employees; She has experience serving our school system – she presided as the Hearing Officer for three years; I personally know that she cares about the success of our children; Ms. Jacobs has proven her ability to build consensus, which is extremely necessary for the work we have to do for our school district; She has demonstrated exceptional leadership in her capacity as Vice Chair for the past year, which is reflective of her experience as a leader in the workplace and in the community It is with great pleasure and enthusiasm that I nominate Verjeana Jacobs to serve as Chair and I urge my colleagues to support this nomination…Mr. Secretary, (Dr. Deasy), thank you.



that the Prince George's County Board of Education hereby agrees to elect Verjeana M. Jacobs, Esq., as the Chair to the Board of Education for the period of December 3, 2007 - December 1, 2008.

Motion by Pat J Fletcher, second by Ron L Watson.
Final Resolution: Motion Carries
Aye: Verjeana M Jacobs, Ron L Watson, Donna H Beck, Pat J Fletcher, Heather H Iliff, R Owen Johnson, Rosalind A Johnson, Linda T Thomas, Amber P Waller, Haywood Perry
