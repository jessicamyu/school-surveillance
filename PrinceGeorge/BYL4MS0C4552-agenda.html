After further review by the Chief Financial Officer, the following changes were in the revised memo:

  • STEAM Program: The Committee voted on a proposal for $210,000  shown in this link. The letter states that it is $500,000.
  • TAWS Program:  This is included in the letter as Budget Neutral. However, it appears that the FY 2022 cost would be $2.1 million per the attached link. We would be happy to further discuss the potential savings detailed in the memo, though it appears that the grant savings are not available and the other revenue would not be achieved until FY 2023 at the earliest.
  • Free In-Person Summer School: This is already included in the CEO's Proposed Budget so no amendment is required. 
  • In letter C, for the $57 million, there is a reference to the ESSA Grant. This Should be the "ESSER II" Grant.
