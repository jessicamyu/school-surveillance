OPENING COMMENTS
Verjeana M. Jacobs, Esq.
Public Hearing – For the FY2010 Proposed Operating Budget


Good evening and welcome to the January 5, 2009 Fiscal Year 2010 Proposed Operating Budget Public Hearing of the Prince George’s County Board of Education.

Board Members, Dr. Watson, Mr. Johnson, Mrs. Thomas, Edward Burroughs, and Ms. Waller will not be in attendance tonight.

I would like to ask everyone to turn off any wireless communication devices, such as cell phones, Blackberrys, Treos, etc. as these devices interfere with the microphones and taping of the meeting. Thank you.

The Board of Education has convened this evening for the sole purpose of receiving public commentary on the Fiscal Year 2010 Proposed Operating Budget.

Tonight, Dr. William Hite, Interim Superintendent will provide a brief overview of the Fiscal Year 2010 Proposed Operating Budget. Following the overview, the Board and staff will hear comments from registered speakers.

At this time I will yield the floor to Dr. Hite and the Administration for a brief overview.

Public Comment
PUBLIC PARTICIPATION
Colleagues we will now begin with our registered speakers list. We have 2 registered speakers for Public Comment who will speak for three minutes in a public comment forum where the Board will listen to your comments.

Our first speaker for tonight is Ms. Susan Peterson. Ms. Peterson, you may proceed.



After the registered speakers list is exhausted, please announce the following:

Our next scheduled Public Hearings are January 12, 2009 at Forestville Military Academy and January 15, 2009 at Oxon Hill High School at 7:00 p.m.

This concludes tonight’s Public Hearing.

Ms. Beck, may I have a motion to adjourn? May I have a second? It has been properly moved and seconded that the January 5, 2009 Public Hearing on the Fiscal Year 2010 Proposed Operating Budget be adjourned. All those in favor, please signify by saying aye. Opposed? The ayes have it and the meeting is adjourned. Thank you.
