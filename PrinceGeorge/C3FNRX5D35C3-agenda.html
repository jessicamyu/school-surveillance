CANCELLED: Special Board Meeting - 5:00 P.M.
With apologies extended, today's Special Board meeting is being canceled due to a lack of Board members confirming their attendance for which a quorum is needed.

The purpose of this meeting was to vote for an interim legal counsel service for the Board of Education.

Therefore, by the authority vested in the Chair (to facilitate the board's work), the BOE has entered into a legitimate and fully vetted contract for interim legal services with the Kapprinsky Cornbrook & Karp law firm, effective May 24, 2021.

Again, apologies are extended for any inconvenience this cancellation may have caused.
