In accordance with Board Policy 9361 which states the following:

The Board of Education shall hold an annual meeting on the first Monday in December to elect a Chair and a Vice-Chair from among the members.

The Board shall schedule regular meetings at least once a month during the months of July, August, November and December and twice a month at all other times. All meetings of the Board are open to other public, except for meetings in executive session.

The Board will have its meetings at Upper Marlboro in the Board meeting room, unless changed to another location for a specific meeting. Two weeks public notice must be given if the meeting is changed or held elsewhere.

Legal Reference: MD. Ann.Code, Educ. Art. 3-1004.
