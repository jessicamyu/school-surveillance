    Nov 12, 2007 - Board Meeting - 7:05 P.M.
    Category
    7.0 NewsBreak
    Type
    Information
      Staff from Television Resources Department presented a video on Entrepreneur for A Day and National Hunger and Homelessness Awareness Week with the Student Board Member Mr. Haywood Perry, III.
              Prince George’s County Board of Education student member Haywood Perry III along with the Prince George’s Regional Association of Student Governments (PGRASG) will take part in a nationwide effort to bring greater awareness to the problems of hunger and homelessness during the week of November 11-17.
              The National Coalition for the Homeless and the National Student Campaign Against Hunger and Homelessness co-sponsors National Hunger and Homelessness Awareness Week. The extremity of those without a home can be both lonely and rigid. Therefore, the purpose of National Hunger and Homelessness Awareness Week is to bring a greater harmony and compassion to the struggles of the homeless.

              “We hope to raise awareness that not only resonates throughout this one week, but that also transcends into an on-going effort to assist those in need,” said Perry, a senior at Oxon Hill High School.
